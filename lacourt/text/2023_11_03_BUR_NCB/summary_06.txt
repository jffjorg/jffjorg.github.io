Summary:

This Tentative Ruling is regarding a motion to compel a vehicle inspection of a 2019 Honda Accord owned by Plaintiff Juan Chavarria. The vehicle was allegedly manufactured and/or distributed by Defendant American Honda Motor Company, Inc. Defendant seeks to inspect the vehicle to investigate claims of defects. Plaintiff's counsel initially agreed to inspection dates but later claimed summer vacation plans and did not provide a response to the demand for inspection. The Court notes that the motion is technically untimely but will consider the substantive merits. The Court grants the motion and orders the parties to meet and confer to schedule a mutually agreeable date for the inspection.
