This is a tentative ruling on a pro hac vice application by Paul D. Root to appear as counsel in a motor vehicle personal injury action. The case involves a vehicle collision that resulted in the wrongful death of Juan Cordova. The Plaintiffs filed a complaint alleging four causes of action, including statutory liability, negligence, premises liability, and survival action. The Defendant, 46205 Division Realty LLC, filed an application for Paul D. Root to appear as counsel pro hac vice. The Court notes that the State Bar of California did not approve Root's application, but rather processed it internally. The Court finds that Root has met the requirements for pro hac vice admission and is inclined to grant the application.
