The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the motion for attorney fees and costs brought by Plaintiffs Iliana Garcia and Umbrella Professional Services against Defendants Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC and House of Imports. The Court's ruling is based on the principles of the Song-Beverly Act and Civil Code section 1794, subdivision (d), which allow prevailing parties to recover reasonable attorney fees and costs. The Court also considers the reasonableness of the attorney's fees and costs incurred by Plaintiffs' counsel, The Lemon Pros, LLP.
