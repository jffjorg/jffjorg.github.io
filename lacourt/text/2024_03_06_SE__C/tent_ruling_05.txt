 Case Number:  22NWCV00745    Hearing Date:   March 6, 2024    Dept:  C Perez
v. American Honda Motor Company, Inc., Case No. 22NWCV00745
 
This
is a Song-Beverly action. Plaintiff applies ex parte to advance the hearing on
Plaintiff’s motion to compel deposition of defendant’s PMK and request for
sanctions. It is currently set for September 18, 2024.  Trial is currently
set for April 3, 2024.  
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Plaintiff’s
ex parte application is GRANTED in part.  The hearing date is ADVANCED
from Wednesday, September 18, 2024, to today’s date and CONTINUED to Wednesday,
June 12, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C. The parties are not foreclosed from
seeking a trial continuance in Dept. SE-F.
