And here is the English-text summary:

The Judge has granted Plaintiff BMB Commercial Corp.'s motion for a protective order, directing that the Plaintiff need not respond to the special interrogatories and requests for production of documents propounded by Defendant Applied Soil Technology, Inc. dba Applied Earth Sciences. The Judge has also granted the Plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the reduced amount of $1,750.00, payable by the Defendant and its counsel to the Plaintiff and its counsel within 30 days of the entry of this order. The Judge's ruling is based on the fact that the Defendant did not provide sufficient basis for additional discovery and that the discovery propounded was duplicative and unwarranted.
