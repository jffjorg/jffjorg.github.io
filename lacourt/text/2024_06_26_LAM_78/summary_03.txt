The Judge's tentative ruling is in response to a Petition for Approval of Transfer of Structured Settlement Payment Rights filed by J.G. WENTWORTH ORIGINATIONS, LLC. The Petitioner seeks to transfer structured settlement payments from C.T., the Payee, to the Petitioner in exchange for a lump payment. The Court finds that the transfer agreement generally complies with statutory requirements, but requires more information about Payee's financial situation before approving the petition. The Court is concerned that Payee transferred away his original annuity without sufficient understanding that he lost a significant portion of his original settlement from previous transactions. The Judge is requiring the Petitioner to file additional information, including copies of the four previous petitions and supporting declarations, and a supplemental declaration from Payee explaining how the $154,066 was spent.
