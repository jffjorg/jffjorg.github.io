This is a tentative ruling on Plaintiff Frances Falco's motion to tax costs. The Court grants the motion in part and will tax a total of $9,917.93. The Court finds that the filing and motion fees were reasonably necessary to the conduct of litigation and will not be taxed. The Court also finds that the jury fees were reasonably incurred and will not be taxed. The Court finds that the deposition costs were reasonably necessary to the conduct of litigation and will not be taxed. The Court further finds that the court reporter's fees were expressly allowable under Gov. Code, section 68086 and Cal. Rules of Court, Rule 2.956. The Court exercises its discretion and allows the costs for producing and photocopying trial binders and exhibit binders.
