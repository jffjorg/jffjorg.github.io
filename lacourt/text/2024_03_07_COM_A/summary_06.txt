This tentative ruling concerns the petition for approval of a minor's compromise of claims, filed by Annamarie Sierra, et al. against Irma Rodriguez. The case arises from injuries caused by the defendant's alleged failure to maintain the plaintiffs' residence in a habitable condition. The minor, Annamarie Gonzales, sustained emotional distress but has fully recovered from her injuries without seeking medical treatment. The claimant settled her claim for $500 and proposes that the settlement be distributed to her guardian ad litem. The court is inclined to grant the petition, subject to a hearing, and notes that the petitioner and claimant must appear at the hearing unless good cause is shown to excuse their appearance.
