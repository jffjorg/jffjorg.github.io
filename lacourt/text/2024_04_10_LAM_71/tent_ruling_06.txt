The Court notes
that Plaintiff has obtained a Default against Defendant.  The Court therefore discharges the Order to
Show Cause regarding same set for hearing on April 10, 2024.  
The Court further notes that Plaintiff has submitted a partial request
for entry of default judgment.  The Court
denies Plaintiff’s default judgment packet. 

The Court sets a hearing on an order to show cause why the complaint
should not be dismissed and/or Plaintiff’s counsel sanctioned $250 for failing
to enter default judgment (California Rule of Court, rule 3.110(h)) on May 14,
2024, at 8:30 AM in Department 71 at Stanley Mosk Courthouse for the following
reasons:

1.     No Proposed Judgment Form (JUD-100).
 
2.     No Request for Court Judgment on the mandatory
Judicial Council form CIV-100.
 
3.     No declaration of Plaintiff or declarant with
percipient knowledge competent to testify and authenticate the evidence to
support the damages claimed.  No
declaration submitted and signed under penalty of perjury.  (C.C.P. §2015.5.)
