Gohar seeks to compel further responses to her requests for production, set one, nos. 1, 14, 19, 32, 37, 40–45, 54, 56, 71, 88, 90, 98 and 99 from Jila.The Motion is denied as moot because Jila served supplemental responses after the Motion was filed; this took place on February 29, 2024.Second, the Motion is denied because Gohar failed to meet and confer in good faith.  Jila asked for a written meet and confer correspondence in June 2023.  Gohar provided this on August 3, 2023, at 5:26 PM when she knew that Jila’s counsel was leaving for vacation and not available for over two weeks.  Jila’s counsel was already on an airplane at this time and did not have an internet connection.Also, the separate statement is deficient because it provides incorrect numbers for the requests at issue.The Court declines to award sanctions.Moving party to give notice.
