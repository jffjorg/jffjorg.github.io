The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel discovery responses from the plaintiff, Michael Adam Cadena, to special interrogatories (Set One) served by the defendants, Sharon Ibara and Randy Ibara. The defendants sought sanctions against the plaintiff for failing to provide timely responses despite extensions. The Judge granted the motion to compel discovery responses, but denied it as moot since responses had already been served. The Judge did grant the defendants' request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $330, to be paid within 20 days. The Judge noted that the plaintiff's failure to provide discovery responses waived any objections to the request, including those based on privilege or the protection of attorney work product.
