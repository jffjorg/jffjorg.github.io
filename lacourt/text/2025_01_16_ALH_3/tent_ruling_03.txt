On September 5, 2024, plaintiffs
Sceneries Entertainment Corporation dba Cinema Libre Studio and Cinema Libre
Diversity, LLC (“Plaintiffs”) filed this action for breach of contract, common
counts, and fraud against defendant Nicole Canale (“Canale”), among others,
arising from the marketing and production of a movie called “I am Gitmo.” On
November 7, 2024, Canale filed this motion to quash the summons and complaint
for lack of personal jurisdiction and to dismiss her from this action. 
On January 14, 2025, Plaintiffs filed a
request to dismiss Canale. The dismissal was entered on January 15, 2025.
Accordingly, this motion is MOOT. 
Moving party to give notice.
