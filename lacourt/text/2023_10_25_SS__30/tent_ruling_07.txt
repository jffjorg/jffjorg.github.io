 Case Number:  23STCV07280    Hearing Date:   October 25, 2023    Dept:  30 PATRICIA PETERS vs PAINTCRAFT SUPPLY & HARDWARE CO. INC., et al.Motion to Compel Plaintiff's Responses to Special InterrogatoriesTENTATIVEDefendant’s Motion for an Order Compelling Plaintiff Patricia Peters’ Responses to Special Interrogatories, Set One is granted. Plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses, without objections, within 20 days. Request for sanctions is denied.  Moving party is ordered to give notice.DISCUSSIONDefendant served Special Interrogatories, Set One, on Plaintiff on May 29, 2023. (Babaian Decl. ¶ 2, Ex. A.) To date, Defendant has not received responses. (Id. at ¶ 6.) A party waives its objections to a discovery request when it does not serve a timely response to the request. (Code Civ. Proc. 2030.290(a)) The Court declines to award sanctions because the motion is unopposed.
