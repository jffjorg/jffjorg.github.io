 Case Number:  22STCV30628    Hearing Date:   October 25, 2023    Dept:  30 PEDRO SERRANO-TRUJILLO, et al. vs SYSTEMS SOURCE, INC., et al.Motion to Compel Responses to Form InterrogatoriesMotion to Compel Responses to Special InterrogatoriesMotion to Compel Responses to Request for Production of DocumentsTENTATIVEThe Court DENIES AS MOOT Plaintiff’s: (1) motion to compel Defendant Alejandro Cadena’s (“Defendant”) verified responses, without objection, to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories, Set One; (2) motion to compel Defendant’s verified responses, without objection, to Plaintiff’s Special Interrogatories, Set One; and (3) motion to compel Defendant’s verified responses, without objection, to Plaintiff’s Demand for Production, Set One.  Requests for sanctions are denied.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONPlaintiff does not dispute that verified and code-compliant responses were served prior to the hearing on the motions. The motions are moot. Sanctions are denied.  The Court finds that imposition of sanctions is not necessary to curtail future violations of discovery given the reasons proffered by responding party.  As such, sanctions are not warranted as they are not meant to be punishment.
