 Case Number:  21STCV39972    Hearing Date:   October 25, 2023    Dept:  30 MID-CENTURY INSURANCE COMPANY vs HECTOR M. PALOMARESMotion to Set Aside / Vacate DismissalRuling:  Plaintiff's Motion to Set Aside / Vacate the Dismissal is granted due to extrinsic mistake.  The court clerk erred in dismissing the case for failure to appear at trial, after the default judgment already was entered against defendant. The dismissal order is vacated.  Moving party to give notice.
