  Case Number:  20STCV11776    Hearing Date:   April 23, 2024    Dept:  30 KARINA GOMEZ, et al. vs CITY OF INGLEWOOD, et al.TENTATIVEThe Court excuses the personal appearance of the claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.The Court has reviewed the Petition to Approve Compromise of Disputed Claim of a Minor (L.A., age 6). The Court finds the settlement is reasonable, however, the following deficiencies preclude the granting of the petition at this time:(1) There is no reduction agreement for medical expenses from Beverly Hills Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Institute.(2) The proposed order to deposit in blocked account incorrectly states $12,640.82 is to be deposited when the petition states it is $16,390.82.As a result, the petition is CONTINUED. Counsel is ordered to file and serve the reduction agreement, and correct the order for the blocked account no later than six court days prior to the next hearing.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
