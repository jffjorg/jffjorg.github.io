And here is the English summary:

This is a tentative ruling by Judge Robert B. Broadbelt III regarding a motion for sanctions filed by Plaintiff Eric Jones against Counsel Herbert Hayden and Harris & Hayden. The Plaintiff alleged that Counsel made misrepresentations in an expert witness declaration under penalty of perjury. The court grants Plaintiff's request for judicial notice. However, the court denies Plaintiff's motion for sanctions, including monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,000, as well as attorney's fees and costs. The court also denies Plaintiff's request to direct Counsel to pay the increased deposition fees sought by Twomey. The court notes that Twomey will likely be compensated for his time testifying at deposition based on his hourly fee.
