 Case Number:  19STCV28264    Hearing Date:   November 6, 2023    Dept:  30 RODY CASTROLL, AN INDIVIDUAL vs CALIFORNIA POLICE ATHLETIC FEDERATION, et al.Motion to Deem Request for Admissions AdmittedTENTATIVEDefendant Los Angeles Sheriff’s Athletic Association’s motion to have matters in requests for admissions deemed admitted is GRANTED. The truth of the matters in Defendant’s request for admissions, set two, served on Plaintiff is deemed admitted.  Defendant’s request for sanctions is GRANTED. Plaintiff Rody Castroll is ordered to pay monetary sanctions in the amount of $280, payable to Defendant, within 30 days of this order.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONOn November 21, 2022, Defendant served upon Plaintiff, Request for Admissions, Set One. (Metros Decl., Exh. A.) Responses were due by December 23, 2022. (Id., ¶ 3.) To date, no responses to the discovery were provided. (Id.)As Defendant properly served the discovery request and Plaintiff failed to provide any responses, the Court finds Defendant is entitled to an order establishing the truth of the matters in Defendant’s request for admissions served on Defendant. Therefore, the motion is granted.As the motion is granted, Defendant’s request for sanctions is also granted as sanctions are mandatory in connection with this motion. However, the amount in sanctions will be reduced due to the simplicity of this motion. As such, the Court imposes sanctions against Plaintiff in the amount of $280 ($280 per hour, for one hour), to be paid within 30 days of this order.
