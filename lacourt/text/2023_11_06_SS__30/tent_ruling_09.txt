 Case Number:  22STCV19959    Hearing Date:   November 6, 2023    Dept:  30 BENJAMIN SEDAGHAT vs MIKHAIL TSIPENYUK, et al.Motion to Continue TrialTENTATIVEThe motion to continue trial is GRANTED. Judicial Assistant is directed to calendar a trial on May 18, 2024, or a date convenient to Court and parties.  All trial related deadlines are continued in accordance with the new trial date.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONGood cause exists to continue trial because Defendants have had an excused inability to obtain discovery despite diligent efforts and trial is quickly approaching. Additionally, this would be the first continuance. The motion to continue trial is granted. This regularly noticed motion was unopposed.
