 Case Number:  21STCV46904    Hearing Date:   November 6, 2023    Dept:  30 MARIE CARINGAL, et al. vs JUAN VICTORINO VELASQUEZ LOPEZ, et al.Motion to Continue TrialTENTATIVEThe motion to continue trial is GRANTED. Judicial Assistant is directed to calendar the trial and final status conference to a date in July 2024.  All trial related deadlines are continued in accordance with the new trial date. Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONGood causes exists to continue trial because Plaintiffs’ counsel needs time to conduct discovery. Additionally, Plaintiffs’ counsel will be in trial during the time that Plaintiffs’ trial is scheduled to begin. This would be the second continuance in this matter. The motion to continue trial is granted.  This regularly noticed motion is unopposed.
