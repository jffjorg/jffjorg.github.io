 Case Number:  20STCV03496    Hearing Date:   November 6, 2023    Dept:  30 ARMANDO RUIZ CARLOS, AN INDIVIDUAL vs HD SUPPLY CONSTRUCTION & INDUSTRIAL WHITE CAP, et al.Defendant's Motion to Compel Supplemental Request for Production of DocumentsTENTATIVEDefendant’s Motion to Compel Supplemental Request for Production is DENIED as MOOT. Defendant’s request for sanctions is DENIED. Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONOn May 15, 2023, Defendant propounded on Plaintiff supplemental request for production fo documents. (Rivera Decl., ¶ 2; Exh. A.) By August 18, 2023, Plaintiff had not provided any responses. Defendant’s counsel contacted Plaintiff’s counsel, who acknowledged that no responses had been served. However, as of the date of the motion, no responses have been provided. (Rivera Decl., ¶ 5.)Plaintiff has filed a late opposition. The Court exercises its discretion and considers the opposition, despite being untimely.Plaintiff argues that on October 30, 2023, Plaintiff responded to the Supplemental Request for Production of Documents and produced verified responses, without objections. (Exh. A.)As Plaintiff has produced verified responses, the motion to compel is moot.Defendant requests sanctions. However, as the motion was not unsuccessfully opposed, sanctions are unwarranted.
