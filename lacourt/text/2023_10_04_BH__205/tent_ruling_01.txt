  Case Number:  20SMCV01985    Hearing Date:   October 4, 2023    Dept:  205    Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles – West District  Beverly Hills Courthouse / Department 205   WCO SPECTRUM, LLC,    Plaintiff, v.  SIGNAL FLARE CAPITAL, INC. et al.,    Defendants.    Case No.:  20SMCV01985    Hearing Date:  October 4, 2023   [TENTATIVE] ORDER RE:   PLAINTIFF WCO SPECTRUM LLC’S    MOTION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT OR    IN THE ALTERNATIVE, FOR SUMMARY    ADJUDICATION    Plaintiff WCO Spectrum, LLC’s Separate Statement fails to comply with Cal. R. Ct. 3.1350(d)(3).  Under that rule, a separate statement must state undisputed material facts, including “reference to the¿exhibit, title, page and line numbers.”  This rule is designed to aid the Court in quickly identifying the evidence supporting each alleged undisputed material fact.  Plaintiff’s Separate Statement fails to identify the exhibit numbers supporting each of its claimed undisputed material facts.     Accordingly, the Court DENIES WITHOUT PREJUDICE Plaintiff’s motion for summary judgment or in the alternative, for summary adjudication.    IT IS SO ORDERED.  DATED: October 4, 2023    ___________________________ Edward B. Moreton, Jr. Judge of the Superior Court
