And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has sustained the demurrer filed by the State of California, acting through the Department of Transportation, to the third cause of action for dangerous condition of public property in Plaintiff Raymond Joshua Rios's first amended complaint. The judge finds that Plaintiff has not pleaded facts establishing a dangerous condition of public property, as required by Government Code sections 835 and 835.2. The judge also finds that Government Code sections 830.4 and 830.8 provide immunity to the State in this case, as Plaintiff's first amended complaint relies on allegations involving inadequate traffic control devices. The judge grants Plaintiff leave to amend the complaint to plead the additional factors alleged in the complaint with the required particularity.
