The judge has granted Lyft's motion for leave to conduct an additional medical examination of the plaintiff. The examination will be conducted by Dr. Joshua Prager on June 6, 2024, and will include a general physical examination, neurological examination, myofascial examination, medical history, and interrogation of the spinal cord simulator system. The judge has also set forth several conditions for the examination, including limiting the scope and duration of the examination and prohibiting Dr. Prager from questioning the plaintiff about any discussions between the plaintiff and his counsel. The judge has also ordered the parties to comply with relevant California Code of Civil Procedure sections regarding the conduct of the examination and the production of reports.
