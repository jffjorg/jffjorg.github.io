 Case Number:  22NWCV01760    Hearing Date:   March 15, 2024    Dept:  C Haynie v. American Honda Motor Co., Inc. 22NWCV01760
Defendant's Ex Parte Application for Relief Pursuant to CCP
473(b) to Permit Defendant to File an Untimely Opposition to Plaintiffs' Motion
to Compel Further Responses to Plaintiff's Requests for Production of Documents
(set one) is GRANTED in the interests of judicial efficiency and to ensure
adjudication on the merits.  However, in order to ensure that Plaintiffs
are not unfairly prejudiced by the untimely filing of Defendant's Opposition,
Plaintiffs' Motion to Compel Further Responses to Request for Production of
Documents (set one) is ADVANCED from Wednesday, March 20, 2024 to
today's date and CONTINUED to Wednesday, May 22, 2024 at 10:30 a.m.
in Dept. SE-C.  Defendant is ORDERED to file and serve it's
Opposition to Plaintiff's Requests for Production of Documents (set one) per
Code in accordance with the new hearing date. The Reply is also to be filed and
served per Code in accordance with the new hearing date. Moving Party to give
notice.
