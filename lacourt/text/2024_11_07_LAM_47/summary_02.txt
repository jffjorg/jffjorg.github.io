This tentative ruling addresses a motion by Defendant Narinder Pallan, acting in pro per, to file a compulsory cross-complaint against Plaintiff E on Los Angeles, LLC. The case involves a breach of lease, with Defendant alleging that Plaintiff failed to pay rent and left the premises in disrepair. Defendant claims that Plaintiff allowed Defendant's property to sustain water damage during heavy rains in December 2023 by failing to make repairs to the roof of the premises. The court grants Defendant's motion, holding that the proposed crossclaims are compulsory crossclaims and that Defendant acted in good faith in seeking leave to file the cross-complaint. The court orders Defendant to serve and file a clean standalone copy of the proposed cross-complaint within 10 days of the order.
