And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has issued a tentative ruling on American Honda Motor Company, Inc.'s motion to compel depositions and request for sanctions. The motion is granted in part, requiring Plaintiffs Juan Aguilar Maldonado, et al. to appear for their depositions on a date and time to be agreed upon by the parties. If no agreement is reached, the deponents are ordered to appear at a reasonable date and time to be determined by the moving party. However, the request for sanctions is denied due to the failure of the notice of motion to identify every person, party, and attorney against whom the sanctions are sought.
