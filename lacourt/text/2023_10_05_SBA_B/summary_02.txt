Judge Gary Y. Tanaka has issued a tentative ruling in the case of South Bay Aviation, Inc. v. Santa Monica Propeller, et al. The defendant, Santa Monica Propeller Service, Inc., has moved to compel responses to its Form Interrogatories, Set One, and Request for Production of Documents, Set One. The court has granted these motions, ordering the plaintiff, South Bay Aviation, Inc., to serve verified responses within 10 days. Additionally, the court has sanctioned the plaintiff and its counsel in the amount of $1,470.00 for failing to respond to the discovery requests and for making false claims about receiving the requests. The court has also ordered the plaintiff to pay this amount within 30 days.
