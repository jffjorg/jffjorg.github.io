After consideration and review of
the file herein, Defendant Rutan

Defendant is

STATEMENT OF THE CASE
This action arises out of alleged violations of
Government Code section 87100 et seq. Plaintiff James M. Belna (“Plaintiff”)
brings suit against Defendant Rutan & Tucker, LLP (“Defendant”) alleging
the following cause of action:
1.      Violation of California Government
Code Section 87100 pursuant to Section 91005(b)).
On July 28, 2023, the Court sustained Defendant’s
Demurrer to the Complaint, without leave to amend.
On December 28, 2023, the Court denied Plaintiff’s Motion
For Reconsideration Of Order Sustaining Demurrer of Defendant Rutan &
Tucker LLP Without Leave To Amend Pursuant to Code of Civil Procedure Section
1008 and entered Judgment in favor of Defendant.
Defendant filed the instant Motion For Prevailing-Party
Attorneys’ Fees (the “Motion”).
Pursuant to Government Code
section 91012, Defendant moves for and award of $40,000.00 in attorney’s fees
on the ground that it is the prevailing party in the action. 
 
This hearing officer did not have
the opportunity to hear the underlying motion and argument by counsel.  Further, the matter was taken under
submission by Judge Bowick and was continued for submission of courtesy copies,
argument and subsequent review and ruling by the Court.
Accordingly, the motion is CONTINUED
to April 9, 2025, 8:30 a.m., Department 19 of the Stanley Mosk Courthouse. 
 
Defendant is ordered to give
notice.
