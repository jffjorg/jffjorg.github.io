 Case Number:  23PSCV01620    Hearing Date:   September 30, 2024    Dept:  H Towne Ranch LLC v.
Kelly Ferm, Inc.,
Case No. 23PSCV01620
ORDER ON
DEFAULT JUDGMENT APPLICATION
Background
This
is an unlawful detainer regarding the commercial premises located at 619 W.
Foothill Boulevard, Claremont, CA 91711.
On May
31, 2023, Plaintiff Towne Ranch LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed a “Complaint for
Unlawful Detainer for Non-Residential Premises for Non-Payment of Rent” against
Kelly Ferm, Inc. (“Defendant”) and Does 1-10.
On
September 8, 2023, Defendant’s default was entered.
On
September 11, 2023, a clerk’s default judgment for possession only was entered.
An
Order to Show Cause Re: Default Judgment is set for September 30, 2024.
 
Discussion
Plaintiff’s
Application for Default Judgment is granted, effective upon Plaintiff
filing an amended declaration from Sajara Garland identical in all respects to
her prior declaration filed with the Court on April 17, 2024, except correcting
Paragraph 1 therein (i.e., Garland erroneously identifies herself as “Towne
Ranch LLC”) and upon Plaintiff furnishing a corrected proposed judgment
to the Court reducing holdover damages from $28,611.90 to $28,611.76 (i.e.,
$205.84 (x) 139 days) and reducing attorney’s fees from $1753.38 to $1753.37,
for a total judgment of $48,024.00.
