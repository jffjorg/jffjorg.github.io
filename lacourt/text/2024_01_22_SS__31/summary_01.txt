The Judge's tentative ruling is in response to a motion to compel production of raw data from a neuropsychological evaluation of the Plaintiff, Elizabeth Amezquita, conducted by defense expert Dr. Kyle B. Boone. The Plaintiff seeks production of the raw data to assess the basis of Dr. Boone's expert opinions and to challenge his opinions. The Defendant does not oppose the motion. The Judge grants the motion, subject to the parties meeting and conferencing to draft a stipulated protective order to confine the scope of disclosure and use of the raw test data of the examination. The protective order should include instructions to destroy the material at the conclusion of the matter.
