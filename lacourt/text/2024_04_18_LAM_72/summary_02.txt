And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court issued a tentative ruling in a landlord-tenant dispute between Plaintiffs Milagro Paz Umanzor, et al. and Defendants Sylvester Malone, et al. The case involves allegations of violations of Business and Professions Code, sections 17200, et seq., and Civil Code, section 1942.4. The Court overruled the demurrer to the fifth cause of action, which alleged a violation of the Unfair Competition Law (UCL). The Court also overruled the demurrer to the ninth cause of action, which alleged a violation of Civil Code, section 1942.4, which requires a landlord to maintain a habitable dwelling. The Court found that the UCL claim was not time-barred and that the Civil Code, section 1942.4 claim was not time-barred for periods occurring after October 19, 2020.
