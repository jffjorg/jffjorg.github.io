This tentative ruling concerns a lawsuit filed by Fire Guard Corporation (Plaintiff) against Stanford and 12th Street, LP, Shu C. Kou, and Shan C. Kou (Defendants) alleging breaches of contract, open book account, account stated, quantum meruit, and foreclosure of mechanic's lien. The Cross-Defendant, Mayer Separzadeh, filed motions to compel responses to requests for production and requests for sanctions for $3,310.
