The Court has granted the motion to set aside the dismissal of the entire action with prejudice, which was entered on April 18, 2024. The case is reinstated as to defendants Global Transport Enterprise and Jose Fernando Castro, but remains dismissed as against defendant Avis Budget Car Rental LLC.
