 Case Number:  24STCV01414    Hearing Date:   May 29, 2024    Dept:  61 ESTATE OF ELSEE SPENCER vs TZIPPY CARE, LLC, et al.TENTATIVEThe present motions pertain to attorneys John Carpenter and Asa O. Eaton of the firm Zuckerman & Rowley, counsel for Plaintiff Estate of Elsee Spencer. The motion states that irreconcilable differences exist between the personal representative of the estate and counsel. The motion complies with the above requirements. The Court finds that a compelling reason exists to grant the motion and that Plaintiff will not be unduly prejudiced as no trial date has been set.John Carpenter and Asa O. Eaton’s motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff Estate of Elsee Spencer is GRANTED, effective upon moving counsel filing with the court proof of service of a copy of the signed order on the client, as set forth in Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1362(e).Moving party to give notice.
