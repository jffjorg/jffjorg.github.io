 Case Number:  21STCV21874    Hearing Date:   January 24, 2024    Dept:  31 DEPT:  31 HEARING DATE: 01/24/2024 CASE NAME/NUMBER: 21STCV21874 LUNA KURDY, et al. vs TANNER JOHN HAYNES, et al. PETITION TO APPROVE COMPROMISE OF PENDING ACTION OF A MINOR Abigail Bostadjian, Age 9 RECOMMENDATION: CONDITIONAL GRANT  TENTATIVE¿ ¿ The Court excuses the personal appearance of Claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.¿¿  The Court has reviewed the Petition to Approve Compromise of Pending Action of a Minor Abigail Bostadjian (“Claimant”), Age 9, filed on January 19, 2024.  This matter settled with cross-defendant Hamazasb Aghajaniani for the policy limits of $100,000 and with defendants Tanner and Alan Haynes for the policy limits of $250,000, for the total sum of of $350,000. If the settlement is approved, $1,284.06 will be used for medical expenses, $8,838.07 will be used for expenses other than medical, and $87,500 for attorney’s fees. The net balance of $252,377.87 will be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity.   The Court has reviewed the settlement and finds it fair and reasonable.   Petitioner requests 25% of Claimant’s settlement to be used for attorney’s fees. However, Item 13(a) requires an Attachment 13a with a declaration from the attorney explaining the basis for the request for attorney fees, including a discussion of applicable factors listed in rule 7.955(b) of the Cal. Rules of Court. No such attorney declaration was provided.   The Court conditionally approves and grants the petition upon Counsel filing a declaration meeting the requirements described in Item 13(a).   Moving party is ordered to give notice.
