**Facts**
```
  - The subject property is a vacant property in West Hollywood that has been neglected and poses a threat to public health and safety.
  - The property has been the subject of numerous code violations and notices.
  - Unhoused individuals have been frequenting the property, and neighbors have reported issues with the property.
  - Fred Behfarin is a prospective purchaser of the property and has three recorded liens on the property.
  - DH, Sergey, Lucy, and Larisa have been unable to maintain the property and have been ordered to abate the nuisance, but have failed to do so.
```
**Disputes**
```
  - Plaintiffs seek to appoint a receiver to manage and oversee the rehabilitation or demolition of the subject property.
  - Behfarin opposes the motion, arguing that he is willing and able to cure the violations and secure the property, but is unable to do so due to the lack of permits and the ongoing litigation over ownership of the property.
```

**Intentions**

**Acts**
