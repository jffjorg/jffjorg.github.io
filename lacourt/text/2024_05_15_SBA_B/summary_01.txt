This tentative ruling concerns a motion to compel plaintiff to respond without objections to defendant's discovery requests and to deem admitted the requests for admission. The defendant served the discovery requests on January 10, 2024, but the plaintiff failed to respond. The court finds that the defendant properly served the discovery requests and that the plaintiff failed to respond. The court grants the motions and orders the plaintiff to respond without objections to the defendant's discovery requests. The court also orders the plaintiff to pay a monetary sanction in the amount of $1230 to the defendant. Additionally, the court precludes the plaintiff from giving evidence as to its causes of action for common counts due to the plaintiff's failure to provide a bill of particulars.
