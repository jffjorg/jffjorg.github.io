This tentative ruling is issued in the case of Miguel Mora v. Paet Real Estate, LLC, Herc Rentals, Inc., et al. (Case No. 21STCV15616). The court considered the motion for determination of good faith settlement filed by Defendant Paet Real Estate, LLC. The motion was uncontested, and the court granted the motion. The settlement agreement between Plaintiff Miguel Mora and Defendant Paet Real Estate, LLC was reached on December 20, 2023, and provides for payment of $5,000.00 to Plaintiff, with no admission of fault, liability, or wrongdoing. The court held that the settlement was made in good faith, considering the allegations, defenses, and legal exposure of the parties. The court noted that the settlement was a fair approximation of Defendant Paet's proportionate liability, and that Defendant Paet reasonably believed its liability to be zero. The court also noted that the parties were represented by counsel and that the settlement was the result of lengthy arms-length negotiations.
