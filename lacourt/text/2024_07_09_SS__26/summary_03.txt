The California Department of Public Health (Defendant) has moved to dismiss the action brought by Blythe/Windsor Country Park Healthcare Center, LLC (Plaintiff) challenging a citation issued by the Defendant. The Court has reviewed the pleadings and finds that the Plaintiff failed to file a case management statement within six months of the Defendant's answer, as required by California Health & Safety Code section 1428, subdivision (b). The Court concludes that this failure mandates dismissal of the action, and accordingly, the Defendant's Motion to Dismiss is GRANTED. The Court also rejects the Plaintiff's alternative request for relief from its failure to file a case management statement under Code of Civil Procedure section 473, subdivision (b).
