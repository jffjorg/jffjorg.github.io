The Judge has granted summary judgment in favor of the Defendants, Randall Pomeroy and Janell Pomeroy, on their Motion for Summary Judgment. The Plaintiff, Ronald Ortiz, was injured while performing roofing work on the residence of the Defendants. The Plaintiff argues that the Defendants are liable for his injury because they hired an unlicensed contractor, Icon Remodeling, Inc., and failed to take action to prevent the injury. However, the Court finds that there are no triable issues of fact and that the Plaintiff's arguments are unfounded. The Court also finds that the Plaintiff's injury was caused by his own actions and that the Defendants are not liable for his injuries.
