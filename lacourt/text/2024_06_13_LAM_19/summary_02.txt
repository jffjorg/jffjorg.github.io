And here is the English-text summary of the judge's tentative ruling:

The court has granted the unopposed motions to compel filed by both Guadalupe Pharmacy, Inc. and San Marcos Pharmacy, Inc. against Santa Maria Pharmacy, Inc. The defendant is ordered to serve verified responses to the discovery requests without objections within 30 days. Additionally, the court has granted the motions to establish admissions against the defendant, which means the truth of the matters sought will be deemed admitted. The court has also awarded monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,680.00 to be paid within 30 days through the plaintiff's counsel for record. The court finds that the defendant has engaged in an abuse of the discovery process, resulting in the plaintiff incurring reasonable expenses.
