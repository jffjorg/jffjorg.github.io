And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Defendant Housing Authority of the City of Los Angeles has moved for summary judgment or alternative motion for summary adjudication. The Plaintiff The Hardell Group, LLC has opposed the motion. The Court has considered the evidence and arguments presented and finds that the Defendant's motion is denied.

The Court has analyzed the claims and defenses presented and finds that there are triable issues of material fact as to the 2023 abatement of rent. The Defendant failed to provide notice of the failed inspection in 2023, which is a required notice under the Housing Assistance Payments Contract. The Court finds that the Defendant has not met its burden to demonstrate that there is no material triable issue of fact as to this cause of action.

Therefore, the Defendant's Motion for Summary Judgment is denied. The Court will conduct a hearing on December 3, 2024, and all interested parties are invited to submit on the tentative ruling by contacting the courtroom via email by 4:00 p.m. the day before the hearing.
