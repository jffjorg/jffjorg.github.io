The court has considered the plaintiff's ex parte application for entry of default judgment or order setting hearing on default judgment. The court finds that the default of JJL Machine Shop, Inc., d/b/a Alameda Motors was improperly entered and that the court should not have approved the amendment to the complaint filed by the plaintiff. The court denies the plaintiff's application and sets an order to show cause to set aside its previous order approving the amendment to the complaint and to set aside and strike the default of JJL Machine Shop, Inc., dba Alameda Motors. The order to show cause will be heard on December 11, 2024.
