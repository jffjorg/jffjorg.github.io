This is a tentative ruling in the case of John Roman vs. Pacific Beach House, LLC, case number 21STCV26863. The defendant has filed a motion to quash a deposition subpoena for the production of business records of Sedgwick Claims Management Services, Inc. The subpoena seeks a wide range of documents, including insurance records, medical records, and correspondence between Pacific Beach House and various individuals. The defendant argues that these records are irrelevant to the case and that the subpoena is overbroad and constitutes a "fishing expedition." The court has granted the motion to quash the subpoena, finding that the plaintiff has failed to show good cause for the requests and that the records are not relevant to the case.
