The tentative ruling states that the demurrer filed by defendants Carol W. Kwan and Ki Tak P. Leung to the Complaint filed by plaintiff Wenjia Wang is overruled. The court finds that the complaint does not clearly disclose a bar to recovery based on the statute of limitations.
