Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court grants Plaintiff Justin Ybarra's motion to compel Defendant Greystar California, Inc.'s responses to Plaintiff's request for production of documents and form interrogatories. The Court orders Greystar to provide verified, code-compliant responses to the request for production of documents and form interrogatories without objections by April 3, 2024. The Court also grants Plaintiff's request for sanctions and orders Greystar and its counsel to pay Plaintiff $1,123.30 by April 3, 2024.
