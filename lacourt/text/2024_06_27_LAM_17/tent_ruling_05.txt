Defendant’s motion to compel initial responses to Form
Interrogatories is GRANTED. Plaintiff is sanctioned, jointly and severally with
counsel, $700.
 
           On 11/21/2023, Plaintiff Roberto
Perez (Plaintiff) filed suit against Mireya Perez, David Jorge, and Bank of
America, alleging quiet title. 
 
           On 5/23/2024, Defendant Mireya Perez
(Defendant) moved to compel initial responses to her Form Interrogatories.
Defendant also seeks monetary sanctions.
 
           The motion is unopposed. 
 
Discussion

           
           On 4/9/2024, Defendant served
Plaintiff via e-mail and U.S mail with Form Interrogatories. Despite responses
being due on or before 5/14/2024, Plaintiff has not responded nor did Plaintiff
oppose this motion.
 
           Having failed to set forth any
justification for the failure to respond, Plaintiff is sanctioned, jointly and
severally with counsel, $700.00. ($350/hr x 2.)
 
           Based on the foregoing, Defendant’s
motion to compel initial responses to Form Interrogatories is granted.
Plaintiff is sanctioned, jointly and severally with counsel, $700.
 
It is so
ordered.
