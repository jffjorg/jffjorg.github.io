This tentative ruling is a decision by Judge Kerry Bensinger in a wage and hour lawsuit filed by Irma Gonzalez against Martini Associates Development, LLC. The plaintiff seeks to vacate the dismissal of her Private Attorney General Act (PAGA) claim, which was dismissed by the court on December 5, 2023. The plaintiff's counsel inadvertently included a request for dismissal of the PAGA cause of action without prejudice in the default judgment package. The court finds that the plaintiff is entitled to mandatory relief under Code of Civil Procedure section 473, subdivision (b) and grants the motion to vacate. The PAGA cause of action is reinstated, and the plaintiff is directed to re-serve the defendants with the summons and complaint.
