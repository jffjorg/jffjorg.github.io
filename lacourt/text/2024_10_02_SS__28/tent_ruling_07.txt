 Case Number:  23STCV30422    Hearing Date:   October 2, 2024    Dept:  28 On its own motion, the  Court vacates the portion of its October 1, 2024 order addressing the motion of  Plaintiff Myung Shin (“Plaintiff”) to compel  the responses of Elizabeth Yang as Trustee of the Elizabeth Yang Trust  (“Trustee”) to special interrogatories, set one, and the accompanying sanctions  request.  The  Court continues the hearings on Plaintiff’s motion to compel responses to  special interrogatories (set for October 1, 2024) and Plaintiff’s motions to  compel responses to form interrogatories and requests for production of  documents (set for October 2, 2024) to a date to be provided at the October 2,  2024 hearing.  No  later than five court days before the continued hearing, Plaintiff and Trustee  may file and serve supplemental briefs addressing Sinaiko Healthcare  Consulting, Inc. v. Pacific Healthcare Consultants (2007) 148 Cal.App.4th  390, 405-410, and its application to the issues raised by Plaintiff’s motions to compel discovery responses and Trustee's untimely discovery responses.  (See also L. Edmon & C. Karnow, Cal.  Practice Guide: Civil Procedure Before Trial (Rutter 2024) ¶ 8:1137, pp. 8F-58  to 8F-59.)Moving party is ordered to give notice.
