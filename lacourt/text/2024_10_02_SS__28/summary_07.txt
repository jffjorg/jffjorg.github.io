The Court has issued a tentative ruling vacating the portion of its previous order regarding the motion to compel responses to special interrogatories filed by Plaintiff Myung Shin. The Court has continued the hearings on Plaintiff's motions to compel responses to special interrogatories, form interrogatories, and requests for production of documents, which were previously set for October 1 and October 2, 2024. The parties are ordered to file and serve supplemental briefs addressing the application of Sinaiko Healthcare Consulting, Inc. v. Pacific Healthcare Consultants to the issues raised by Plaintiff's motions. The Court has also ordered the moving party to give notice of this tentative ruling.
