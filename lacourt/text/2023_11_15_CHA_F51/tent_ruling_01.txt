  Case Number:  21CHCV00570    Hearing Date:   November 15, 2023    Dept:  F51 MOTION TO COMPEL

Los Angeles Superior Court Case # 21CHCV00570

 
Motion filed: 10/19/23
 
MOVING PARTY: Defendant Micro Matic USA, Inc. (“Micro
Matic”)
RESPONDING PARTY: N/A
NOTICE: Okay
 
RELIEF REQUESTED: Compel Discovery
 
TENTATIVE RULING: DENIED for improperly combining
three separate discovery requests into one motion. 
 
BACKGROUND: 
 
Defendant moves for an order compelling Plaintiff Tony
Laurita (“Plaintiff”) to respond, without objections, to 1) Micro Matic’s
Special Interrogatories, Set One; 2) Form Interrogatories, Set One, and 3)
Requests for Production, Set One. Defendant further seeks an order awarding monetary
sanctions in the amount of $2,460 against Plaintiff.
 
No opposition was filed. 
 
ANALYSIS
 
Defendant improperly combines three separate discovery
requests into one motion. (Motion, p. 2.) Defendant must file a separate motion
to compel for each discovery request (three separate motions). 
 
Before filing additional discovery motions, the parties must
meet and confer on the phone or in person. 

 
CONCLUSION
 
Plaintiff’s motion to compel is DENIED.
