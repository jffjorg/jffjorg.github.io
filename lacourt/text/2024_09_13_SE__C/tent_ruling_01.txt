  Case Number:  20STCV49554    Hearing Date:   September 13, 2024    Dept:  C Munoz
vs. Maggie’s Pub, et al., Case No. 20STCV49554
 
This
is about a bar fight. Defendant moves ex parte to advance the hearing to
authorize the deposition of prisoner defendants. It is currently scheduled for
May 1, 2025, and the trial is scheduled for February 14, 2025. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based
on the above, the motion is GRANTED. The motion is ADVANCED from Thursday, May
1, 2025 to today and CONTINUED to Thursday, September 19, 2024 at 10:30 A.M. in
Dept. SE-C. Plaintiff is to submit a proposed order. 
 
Moving
party to give notice.
