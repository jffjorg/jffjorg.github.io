This is a tentative ruling on a petition for release of a mechanic's lien filed by Felix, LLC against Global Plumbing & Fire Supply, et al. The petition was filed on March 30, 2023, and an amended petition was filed on August 21, 2023. Despite no response being filed by the respondent, the court ordered the petitioner to file and serve an amended petition that included a legal description of the property subject to the lien. The petitioner has satisfied the requirements of Civil Code section 8484, which permits a property owner to petition for an order to release the property from the claim of lien if the claimant has not commenced an action to enforce the lien within the time provided in section 8460. The court has ordered the petitioner to file proof of service of the instant order and notice of continued hearing date by November 8, 2023, and the petition will be continued to November 29, 2023. The petitioner is also entitled to attorney's fees of $2,028.00 and costs of $610.00.
