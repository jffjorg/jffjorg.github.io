The judge has granted the defendant's motion to compel the plaintiff to respond to special interrogatories without objection, and has also granted the defendant's motion to deem the plaintiff's responses to requests for admission admitted. The plaintiff was served with the special interrogatories and request for admissions on May 23, 2024, and was required to respond by June 23, 2024. However, no responses have been received. The judge has ordered the plaintiff to serve code-compliant responses to the special interrogatories without objections within 30 days. The judge has also deemed the plaintiff's responses to the request for admissions admitted as of the date of entry of this order.

The judge has imposed a monetary sanction of $1,250 on the plaintiff for failing to comply with the discovery requests. The sanction represents five hours of work spent preparing and appearing at the hearing on the motions, at a rate of $250 per hour. The plaintiff is required to pay the sanction to the defendant and defense counsel within 30 days of entry of this order.
