This is a tentative ruling by Judge Robert B. Broadbelt III in a civil case (Case Number: 23STCV21848) regarding a motion by the Plaintiff, Immigrant Rights Defense Council, LLC, to deem admissions admitted and for sanctions. The Plaintiff seeks to admit certain requests for admission served on the Defendant, Laura E. Vaca, et al., and to award monetary sanctions in the amount of $3,992.15. The Defendant had filed a motion to quash service of summons, which was denied by the court. The Defendant also filed petitions for writ of mandate, which were denied by the Court of Appeal and the California Supreme Court. The court finds that the Plaintiff has properly served the Defendant with the motion and that the Defendant has failed to serve timely responses to the requests for admission. Therefore, the court grants the Plaintiff's motion to deem admissions admitted and awards monetary sanctions to the Plaintiff in the amount of $860.
