Here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a Tentative Ruling in the case of Pedro Santos versus UMA Enterprises, Inc. and Victor Sierra. The case involves allegations of battery, assault, sexual battery, and other forms of mistreatment by Defendant Sierra, a supervisor at UMA Enterprises, Inc. where Plaintiff Santos worked from May 2020 to November 2020. The Defendant has demurred to certain causes of action, arguing that the claims are barred by the statute of limitations. The Court has found that the Plaintiff's Complaint does not sufficiently plead employer liability and has overruled the demurrer to the first, second, and third causes of action. Additionally, the Court has determined that the statute of limitations does not apply due to the nature of the alleged sexual abuse. The demurrer is overruled, and the Defendant has 20 days to answer.
