The judge grants the unopposed motion for attorneys' fees filed by Petitioners Heng Li, Jie Li, and Ning Jiang in the total amount of $26,580.00. The motion is based on the Petitioners' successful litigation against Respondents FMB Development, LLC, and FMB Consulting LLC, which resulted in a Judgment in favor of the Petitioners. The Judgment includes an attorneys' fees provision, and the Petitioners' counsel has submitted a declaration detailing the reasonable hourly rates and billed hours incurred in the litigation. The judge finds that the Petitioners are the prevailing party and are entitled to an award of their reasonable attorneys' fees. The judge also finds that the hourly rates and billed hours submitted by the Petitioners' counsel are reasonable and appropriate given the attorneys' experience and qualifications. The motion is granted, and the Petitioners are awarded attorneys' fees in the amount of $26,580.00.
