The judge has issued a tentative ruling in a case involving a breach of lease agreement. The plaintiff, Willowbrook Home LLC, is seeking to compel the defendants, David Wohlman and Felicia Edelman, to respond to requests for production of documents and for monetary sanctions. The defendants have failed to respond to the discovery requests, and the plaintiff has filed a motion to compel their responses. The judge has granted the motion and ordered the defendants to provide verified, complete, and code-compliant responses to the requests for production of documents within 30 days. The judge has also granted the plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $660.00, which is to be paid by the defendants and their counsel of record, Marc Lafer, jointly and severally, within 30 days.
