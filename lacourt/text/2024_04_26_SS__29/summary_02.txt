The Court grants Plaintiff's motion to compel the deposition of Defendant's person most qualified. The Court finds that Defendant did not serve a valid objection to the deposition notice and failed to produce the person most qualified to take the deposition. The Court orders Defendant to produce a person most qualified to answer questions on the topics set forth in the deposition notice served on February 12, 2024. The Court also grants Plaintiff's request for sanctions in the amount of $1,185.
