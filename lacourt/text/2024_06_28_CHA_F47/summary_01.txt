The English text that summarizes this judge's tentative ruling is:

The judge grants the defendant's motion to set aside the default and allows the defendant to file an answer to the amended complaint. The default was entered against the defendant on November 27, 2023, after the defendant was served by substituted service at 17603 Sunburst Street, Northridge, CA 91325 on August 18, 2023. The judge finds that the defendant did not have actual notice of the lawsuit in time to defend and that the default was entered as a result of mistake, inadvertence, surprise and/or excusable neglect. The judge also finds that the defendant failed to discover the documents had been mailed to her until late January/early February 2024 as a result of excusable neglect. The judge orders the defendant to separately file her answer to the amended complaint on or before July 1, 2024.
