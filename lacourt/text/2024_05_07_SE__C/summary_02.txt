The defendant, Estes Terminals of California, LLC, has demurred to the third cause of action in the plaintiff's Fifth Amended Complaint, alleging that it fails to state facts sufficient to establish a valid cause of action. The third cause of action alleges that the defendant violated California Code of Regulations, Title 8, § 1592, by failing to equip vehicles with safety measures.
