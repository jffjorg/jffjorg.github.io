And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has ruled that the motion to compel arbitration against Westlake Services, LLC dba Westlake Financial Services is granted. The court finds that a valid arbitration agreement exists between the parties and that there is no defense to its enforcement. The matter is to be arbitrated before the American Arbitration Association (AAA) or, in the alternative, JAMS or another reputable arbitration organization.

The court has also denied the plaintiffs' request for costs without prejudice. The court has stayed all proceedings pending the outcome of the arbitration and has scheduled a post-arbitration status conference for July 31, 2025. Counsel for the plaintiff is ordered to serve and electronically file a joint report on the status of the arbitration proceedings five court days prior to the status conference.

The court has also taken off calendar the trial date of September 12, 2025, and the order to show cause date of March 19, 2027.
