  Case Number:  21STCV35518    Hearing Date:   April 15, 2024    Dept:  9 On April 15, 2024, Counsel for the parties
participated in an informal discovery conference with the Court, at the
conclusion of which the parties agreed as follows:
No later than April 26, 2024, Defendant COMPLETELY
CONCRETE STRUCTURE will serve verified responses to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories
Set One (“FROG”), Special Interrogatories Set One (“SROG”), and Requests for Production
of Documents Set One (“RPD”), without objections.
No later than May 10, 2024, the parties
will file a Joint Statement that: (1) reports whether Defendant has served verified
responses to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories Set One (“FROG”), Special Interrogatories
Set One (“SROG”), and Requests for Production of Documents Set One (“RPD”),
without objections, (2) advises whether the hearing on Plaintiff’s Motion for Issue
and Evidentiary Sanctions remains set for June 27, 2024 or whether it has been
completely mooted, (3) identifies what issues remain unresolved if Plaintiff’s
Motion for Issue and Evidentiary Sanctions set for June 27, 2024 has not been
completely mooted, (4) sets forth the parties’ respective positions as to any
issues that remain unresolved and have not been mooted, and (5) the parties’
meet and confer efforts to schedule depositions of all witnesses necessary for
Plaintiff to file the motion for class certification.
The Court hereby sets a non-appearance
case review re filing of the Joint Statement for May 17, 2024 at 8:30 am.
Plaintiff’s Motion for Sanctions remains
set for June 27, 2024 at 10:00 am.
In light of the ongoing discovery issues, all
parties stipulated today to extend the deadline for filing Plaintiff’s motion
for certification to September 16, 2024. 
Before filing the motion for certification, Plaintiff must contact
Department 9 to obtain a hearing date.  A
non-appearance case review re filing of Plaintiff’s motion for certification is
set for September 23, 2024 at 8:30 am.
It is so ordered.
