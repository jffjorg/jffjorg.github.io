The judge has issued a tentative ruling in favor of the plaintiff, Assil, who is seeking punitive damages against Whole Foods Market California, Inc., Sol-Ti, Inc., and Berlin Packaging LLC for injuries sustained when a bottle of "SuperAde" beverage exploded in her hand.
