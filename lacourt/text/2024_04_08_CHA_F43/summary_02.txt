And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel responses to special interrogatories. The plaintiff had served the special interrogatories on January 4, 2024, and they were due on or before February 8, 2024. The defendant failed to respond, and the plaintiff served a meet and confer letter on February 13, 2024. Despite this, no responses were received by the plaintiff. The plaintiff has requested sanctions in the amount of $2,315 against the defendant for having to bring this motion. The judge has granted the plaintiff's request for sanctions in the amount of $665, which includes the time spent by the plaintiff's counsel preparing the motion and the filing fee. The defendant is ordered to serve responses to the special interrogatories within 20 days and to pay the sanctions within 20 days.
