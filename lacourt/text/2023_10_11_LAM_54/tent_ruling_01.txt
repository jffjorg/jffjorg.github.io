The Court considers the moving papers. No opposition has been received.
 
Plaintiff moves for an order granting approval of the proposed
settlement of claims brought pursuant to the Private Attorneys general Act of
2004 (“PAGA”), Cal. Lab Code § 2698, et seq.  Pursuant to the proposed settlement,
Defendants have agreed to pay $100,000.00 total, $75,000.00 of which is
allocated for the representative PAGA claims. The PAGA settlement will be
distributed as follows: (1) $16,000.00 in attorney’s fees and $2,825.00 in
costs; (2) $56,250.00 to be paid to LWDA and $18,750.00 to be paid to the
aggrieved employees and (4) $5,085.50 settlement administration fee.
 
The Court finds that
the settlement is reasonable and meets the requirements of PAGA. Defendant has
not opposed. The motion is GRANTED.
