Plaintiff Circa 1200, LLC’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On August 8, 2024, Circa
1200, LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed its operative Complaint against Defendants Raphael
Camren Rodney aka Raphael C. Rodney and Creashia Paulann Keith aka Creashia P.
Keith aka Creashia Keith for breach of contract and common counts.
On October 7, 2024, the clerk
entered default against Creashia Paulann Keith aka Creashia P. Keith aka
Creashia Keith. On October 31, 2024, the clerk entered default against Raphael
Camren Rodney aka Raphael C. Rodney.  On December
16, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be heard on January 10,
2025.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendants and award Plaintiff a judgment of $100,983.25
consisting of $84,112.90 in damages; $15,670.35 in prejudgment interest at a
rate of 10%; $697.00 in attorneys’ fees; and $503.00 in costs. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted a
completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments. The Court
finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants Plaintiff Circa
1200, LLC’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application for default
judgment filed on December 16, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $100,983.25 is
entered against Defendants Raphael Camren Rodney aka Raphael C. Rodney and Creashia
Paulann Keith aka Creashia P. Keith aka Creashia Keith.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
