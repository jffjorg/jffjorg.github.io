  Case Number:  21STCV42831    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2023    Dept:  19 Defendant  Lyft's unopposed Motions to Compel: 1)  Plaintiff's Responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One; 2)  Plaintiff Responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One; and 3) Plaintiff's Responses  to Special Interrogatories, Set One are all GRANTED.  Plaintiff is ordered to serve verified  responses, without objections, and produce responsive documents to all of the above-listed discovery requests within 30 days. (Code of Civil Procedure sections 2031.300, 2031.310,  2030.290, 2030.300). Defendant's request for sanctions is granted in  part. The Court finds that Plaintiff’s conduct constitutes an abuse of the  discovery process.  Defendant has been denied a full opportunity to  conduct discovery in which to defend this lawsuit and has been severely  prejudiced by Plaintiff’s failure to respond to valid written discovery. The Court also notes that Plaintiff has failed to file an  opposition to these motions.  Accordingly, the Court grants Defendant's  motion and request for sanctions. The Court imposes a monetary sanction against  Plaintiff in the amount of $850.00 for each motion filed for reasonable expenses incurred by  Defendant in filing each of the above motions, for a total of $2,550.00 in monetary sanctions, payable within 30 days through  Defendant's counsel of record.  (Code of Civil Procedure Sections  2023.010(d) and 2023.030(a).)  The Court takes into consideration that no opposition was filed.Orders signed this date.In addition, the Court, on its own motion, advances and specially sets Defendant's Motion to Deem Requests for Admissions, Set One, Admitted from 4/09/2024 to 01/05/2024, at 9:00 a.m. Moving  party to to give notice.
