This tentative ruling is about a motion to compel arbitration and stay judicial proceedings pending completion of arbitration. The case involves a motor vehicle accident that occurred on August 31, 2020. The plaintiff, Jesus Vera, filed a complaint against defendants Lyft, Inc., Luis Rivera Alvarado, Diaz Mirian, and Does 1 to 50 for negligence. Lyft, Inc. moves to compel arbitration based on the agreement to arbitrate in its Terms of Service. The court finds that there was a valid agreement to arbitrate between the parties and that the agreement covers the present controversy. The court grants the motion to compel arbitration and stays all judicial proceedings pending completion of arbitration. The case is set for an Order to Show Cause Re Dismissal Due to Completion of Arbitration on November 22, 2024.
