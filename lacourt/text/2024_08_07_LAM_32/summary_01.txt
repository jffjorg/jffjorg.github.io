The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Liana Gabrielyan et al. v. BMW of North America, LLC, et al. The case involves a dispute over whether the Song-Beverly Act applies to a used vehicle with a remaining manufacturer's warranty. The Judge has stayed the case in its entirety pending the resolution of a Supreme Court review of the Rodriguez case, which also involves the interpretation of the Song-Beverly Act. The Judge has also continued BMW NA's motion for summary judgment or adjudication until February 7, 2025, and vacated the current trial date of October 29, 2024.
