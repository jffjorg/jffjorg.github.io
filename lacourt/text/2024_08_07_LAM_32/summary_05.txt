And here is the English-text summary:

The tentative ruling concerns a motion by plaintiffs Lexi Burrows and Brooklynn Lundberg to compel defendant Touchstone Golf, LLC to comply with requests for production (RFP) Set Three. The court finds that Touchstone failed to produce electronically stored information (ESI) in native format, as agreed, and failed to produce complete documents. The court grants the motion, ordering Touchstone to produce all non-privileged responsive material in its possession and produce ESI in native format within 20 days. The court also sanctions Touchstone and its counsel in the amount of $1,860, to be paid within 30 days.
