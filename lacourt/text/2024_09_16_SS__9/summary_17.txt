This tentative ruling is related to a petition for a name change. The Petitioner is unable to be identified and the Clerk's office has requested a Live Scan for a complete California Criminal History. The Judge orders the Petitioner to contact the Name Change Clerk to obtain instructions on how to have their fingerprints taken for a California Record Review Live Scan. The Petitioner is also required to provide sufficient information to run a criminal history assessment prior to the next hearing date. If the Petitioner fails to comply, the petition will be denied and dismissed without prejudice. The Name Change Clerk is ordered to run and complete the criminal history assessment prior to the continued hearing, and the Court Clerk is ordered to give notice. The petition is continued to November 25, 2024 at 9:30 am to enable the Live Scan to be run.
