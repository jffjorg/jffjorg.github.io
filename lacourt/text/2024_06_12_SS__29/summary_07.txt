And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court grants the plaintiff's motions to compel the defendant to respond to form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production. The court orders the defendant to serve code-compliant, verified, written responses to the discovery requests within 15 days of notice. The court also grants the plaintiff's motion for a deemed-admitted order, ordering the defendant to be deemed to have admitted the matters specified in the requests for admission. The court denies the plaintiff's requests for sanctions on the three motions to compel but grants the request for sanctions on the motion for a deemed-admitted order, ordering the defendant and its counsel to pay a monetary sanction of $750 to the plaintiff within 30 days of notice.
