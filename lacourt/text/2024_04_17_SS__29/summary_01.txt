### Facts

* The plaintiff, Maria Velasco Rodas, filed a complaint against the defendant, Costco Wholesale Corporation, and others, alleging causes of action for negligence and premises liability.
* The incident occurred on November 18, 2018, when the plaintiff slipped and fell on a sausage on the floor of a Costco store on Century Boulevard in Inglewood.
* The defendant filed a motion for summary judgment or, in the alternative, for summary adjudication.
* The plaintiff objects to some of the defendant's evidence.

### Intentions

* The defendant is seeking to establish that it did not have actual or constructive notice of the dangerous condition on the floor.
* The plaintiff is seeking to establish that the defendant was negligent in its inspection and maintenance of the store.

### Disputes

* The main dispute is whether the defendant had actual or constructive notice of the dangerous condition on the floor.
* The defendant argues that it did not have notice of the condition, while the plaintiff argues that the defendant was negligent in its inspection and maintenance of the store.

### Acts

* The defendant had an employee perform an hourly walk/safety inspection of the store on the date of the accident.
* The inspector did not observe any slipping hazards during the inspection.
* The accident occurred approximately 41 minutes after the inspection.
