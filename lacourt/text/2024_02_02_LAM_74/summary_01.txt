The court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Stephanie Salto Soto v. Liberty Global, LLC, et al. Plaintiff Salto Soto is seeking a protective order to limit the dissemination of her medical records, which were agreed to be produced in discovery. The court grants the motion for a protective order, finding that Defendants' arguments against the order are unpersuasive.

Additionally, the court grants Plaintiff's counsel's request for sanctions in full, awarding $2,324.77 in attorney fees to Plaintiff's counsel. The court finds that Plaintiff's counsel is the prevailing party and that Defendants lack substantial justification for opposing the protective order. The court orders Defendants' counsel to pay Plaintiff's counsel the awarded amount by February 12, 2024.
