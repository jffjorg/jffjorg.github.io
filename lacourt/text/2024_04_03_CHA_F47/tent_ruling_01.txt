and Yana Miraghaffari
RESPONDING PARTY: Plaintiff GGP Northridge Fashion
Center, L.P.
 
RULING: The motion is placed off calendar.
 
On 2/6/24, after a court trial, Judgment was entered in
favor of Plaintiff GGP Northridge Fashion Center, L.P. (Plaintiff) and against Defendants
Alibaba Express Mediterranean Grill, Inc.; Shamdan Restaurant, Inc.; Ali
Mirghaffari and Yana Miraghaffari (Defendants).
 
On 2/20/24, Defendants filed and served a Notice of
Intention to Move for New Trial (Motion). 
On 2/21/24, the Court set the hearing on the Motion for 4/3/24.  After the execution of several stipulations
and orders to extend the time due for Defendants’ filing of a memorandum in
support of the Motion, the last extension extended the deadline to 3/22/24.  (See Stipulation and Order filed
3/19/24).
 
Defendants failed to file a memorandum in support of the
motion by 3/22/24 and there is no further stipulation and order to continue the
deadline for such filing.  Therefore, the
hearing on the Motion is placed off calendar.
