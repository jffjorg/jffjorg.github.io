 Case Number:  SC128193    Hearing Date:   September 10, 2024    Dept:  P Tentative Ruling
Craig Blair v. Lauren Mathers White, et
al., Case No. SC128193 
Hearing Date: September 10, 2024 
Defendants Hannaford and Margolin’s
Petition to Confirm Arbitration Award (UNOPPOSED)
 
Blair sued, alleging defendants’ home was not
as advertised. The parties were ordered to binding arbitration on July 29,
2019. Defendants Hannaford and Margolin move to confirm the award by arbitrator
Judy Lam. 
 
Defendants submitted a copy of the
arbitration award entered April 29, 2024. Unless a petition or response seeking
to correct or vacate an arbitration award was filed properly based on valid
grounds or the proceeding is dismissed, courts must enter judgment in
conformity with the award. Code Civ. Proc. sec. 1286; Valsan Partners Ltd.
Partnership v. Calcor Space Facility (1994) 25 Cal.App.4th 809, 818.  
 
Blair did not file a petition to correct
or vacate or file a response/opposition to the petition. GRANTED.
