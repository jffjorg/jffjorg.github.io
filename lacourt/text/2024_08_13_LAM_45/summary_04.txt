Summary:

The tentative ruling grants the motion of Plaintiffs Realtek Semiconductor Corp. and Steven Baik to propound discovery in anticipation of the anti-SLAPP motion filed by Defendants Winston & Strawn LLP and David P. Enzminger. The Plaintiffs seek to discover information related to the scope of the representation, the extent to which there is a substantial relationship between the adverse representation and the prior work for Realtek, and the acquisition of confidential Realtek information. The Court finds that the discovery sought is narrowly tailored to the issues raised in the anti-SLAPP motion and grants the motion.
