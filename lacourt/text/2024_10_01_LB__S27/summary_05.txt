**Significant Statements**

* The court finds that the defendant adequately showed that the claim is barred by the statute of limitations, and in the absence of opposition on this ground, the demurrer is sustained.
* The court sustains the demurrer to the cause of action for private nuisance because Plaintiffs fail to show that the moving pest control defendant did or did not do anything that constitutes a nuisance.
* The court sustains the demurrer to the cause of action for public nuisance because Plaintiffs fail to show how the bed bugs at issue in this lawsuit affected an entire community or neighborhood, or any considerable number of persons.

**Tentative Ruling**

The defendant's demurrer is sustained without leave to amend.
