Summary in English:

This tentative ruling is related to a hearing on December 15, 2023, in the Superior Court of California, County of Los Angeles, Department 50. The case involves BRENT EVANS, et al. (Plaintiffs) and SOPHIA JIN, et al. (Defendants). Colorish, LLC, a defendant, moves for orders compelling responses to discovery requests served on June 2, 2023. The requests include requests for production and form interrogatories. The court finds that the plaintiffs failed to timely respond to the discovery requests and grants Colorish's motions. The court orders the plaintiffs to serve complete responses to the discovery requests within 30 days and denies Colorish's requests for monetary sanctions.
