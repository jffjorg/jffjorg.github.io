The tentative ruling grants two motions to compel further responses to special interrogatories from each plaintiff, Neuro Diagnostic Center, Inc. and Shushanik Devine. The motions were brought by the defendant, A & D Fire Sprinklers, Inc., arguing that the plaintiffs' responses are insufficient and do not comply with the court's previous order. The court finds that the responses include evasive responses and continue to include language in disobedience of the court's prior order. The court orders the plaintiffs to serve further verified responses to special interrogatories, which must provide all information requested and fully comply with the requirements under the Discovery Act. The court also awards monetary sanctions of $2,560 to the defendant for the work performed in connection with the pending motion.
