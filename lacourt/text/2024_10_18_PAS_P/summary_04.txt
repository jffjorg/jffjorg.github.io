And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling granting the Plaintiff's motion to sever the claims against Clean Initiative, LLC from the action. The Plaintiff, SRS Distribution, Inc. dba JB Wholesale Roofing and Building Supply, initiated this action against Clean Initiative and Juan Pablo Quintero alleging Breach of Contract – Promissory Note and Common Counts – Account Stated. The action arises from a promissory note executed on February 9, 2024, with a principal amount of $166,816.82. Clean Initiative failed to respond to the complaint, resulting in an entry of default, while Quintero filed for Chapter 13 bankruptcy protection, triggering an automatic stay of proceedings. The Plaintiff seeks to sever Clean Initiative from the action to pursue a default judgment without being prejudiced by the automatic stay affecting Quintero. The Judge agrees that severance is appropriate under these circumstances, as it will allow the Plaintiff to obtain a separate judgment against Clean Initiative while maintaining the stay as to Quintero, without prejudicing any of the parties or promoting judicial economy.
