 Case Number:  22STCV06147    Hearing Date:   September 28, 2023    Dept:  31 DEPT:   31 OSC DATE:  09/28/2023 CASE NAME/NUMBER:  22STCV06147 CRUSADER INSURANCE COMPANY vs TYSON COREY BAKER REQUEST FOR COURT JUDGMENT AGAINST [DEFAULTING PARTY]:  TYSON COREY BAKER RECOMMENDATION:  GRANT in the following amounts: Principal damages: $111,708.46 Costs: $519.00 TOTAL: $112,227.46   TENTATIVE  Plaintiff seeks reimbursement for the amount it paid for its insured’s damages in the principal amount of $111,708.46 in this subrogation claim. Plaintiff also seeks costs in the amount of $519.00. Plaintiff has properly dismissed all other parties in this action aside from Defendant Tyson Corey Baker.     Plaintiff seeks damages in the principal sum of $111,708.46, in accordance with amount sought in Plaintiff’s Complaint. Plaintiff, through its custodian of records, substantiates the principal amount sought by providing proof of property damage costs and payments, and payments to Plaintiff’s insured arising from the incident.    Plaintiff further seeks costs of $519.00, comprised of clerk filing fees and process server’s fees, which are allowable under CCP §1033.5.   The application for default judgment filed on August 1, 2023 is GRANTED in the total amount of $112,227.46 against Defendant Tyson Corey Baker.     Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
