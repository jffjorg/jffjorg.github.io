Demurrer to the Answer
 
RULING: Demurrer is moot.
 
SUMMARY OF ACTION 
This action was filed by Plaintiff Kemyia Thomas (Plaintiff)
on March 18, 2024. On April 22, 2024, Defendant Bogardus Clewis (Defendant)
filed his answer to Plaintiff’s complaint. On May 6, 2024, Plaintiff filed a
demurrer to Defendant’s answer. Rather than file an opposition, Defendant timely
filed an amended answer pursuant to CCP § 472 on May 15, 2024. 
 
The filing of the amended answer moots Plaintiff’s demurrer.

 
Moving party to give notice to all parties.
