This tentative ruling grants the petition filed by Paige Wood, a minor, to approve the compromise of her claim for personal injuries sustained in a golf cart accident on August 26, 2023. The accident was caused by Defendant Jayden Timmins, who was operating the golf cart with Paige Wood as a passenger. The minor suffered abrasion and skin lesions on her left shoulder and arm, left-sized neck spasm and pain, and right foot pain, and received treatment for her injuries. The settlement agreement provides for Defendant to pay Claimant $75,000.00, with $3,011.50 to be paid or reimbursed for medical expenses, $18,750.00 to be paid for attorney fees, and $530.51 for other costs and expenses. The amount to be paid to the minor is $52,707.89, which will be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the court. The Court will not request oral argument on this matter, and notice of intent to appear is required to argue.
