And here is a summary in English text:

The tentative ruling grants in part and denies in part the plaintiff Forex Express Corp., d/b/a WireCash's request for a preliminary injunction. The court denies the request against defendants Inter & Co. Payments, Inc., Inter & Co., Inc., and Fernando Fayzano, but grants it against defendant Eliran Grushkowsky. The court orders that Grushkowsky shall preserve and is enjoined from deleting, concealing, altering, or destroying evidence related to the use of the MacBook Pro laptop. The court also orders that Grushkowsky shall not access or dispose of the laptop or access, alter, delete, or destroy files or other data on the device. The ruling is based on the court's finding that the plaintiff has shown a likelihood of prevailing on the merits of its second cause of action for breach of contract against Grushkowsky, and that Grushkowsky has waived the bond requirement set forth in California Code of Civil Procedure section 529.
