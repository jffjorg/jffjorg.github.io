 Case Number:  21STCV13094    Hearing Date:   February 9, 2024    Dept:  39 Ramiro Mata Moya
v. Chipmasters Manufacturing, Inc.
Case No. 21STCV13094
Motion for Preliminary
Approval of Settlement
 
            Plaintiff
moves for preliminary approval of the parties’ settlement.  Good cause having been shown, the Court
orders as follows:
 
            1.         The Court grants the parties’
stipulation to file a second amended complaint. 
Plaintiff’s counsel shall file that complaint forthwith.
 
            2.         The Court grants Plaintiff’s motion for
preliminary approval of the settlement.
 
            3.         The Court sets the following dates:
 
                        a.         Defendants shall provide the settlement
administrator with electronic database concerning class member contact
information and data necessary to calculate individual settlement shares on or
before February 23, 2024.
 
                        b.         Settlement administrator will mail notices
and opt-out forms on or before March 8, 2024.
 
                        c.
        The deadline for receipt of
opt-out forms shall be April 8, 2024.
 
                        d.         The deadline for the motion for final
approval and related motions shall be May 10, 2024.
 
                        e.         The Court shall hold a hearing on final
approval and related motions on June 10, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.
 
            4.         The Court continues the Order to Show Cause
re: Dismissal (Settlement) to June 10, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.
 
            5.         Plaintiff’s counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
