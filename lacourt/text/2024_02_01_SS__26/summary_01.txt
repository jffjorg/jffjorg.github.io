The Judge grants Plaintiff State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company's motion to vacate the dismissal, enforce the settlement agreement, and enter judgment. The Judge finds that the settlement agreement is enforceable and that the request for retention of jurisdiction is proper under Code of Civil Procedure section 664.6.
