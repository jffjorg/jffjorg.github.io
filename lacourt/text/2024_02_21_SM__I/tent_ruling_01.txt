  Case Number:  24SMCV00034    Hearing Date:   February 21, 2024    Dept:  I The arbitrator’s award in petitioner’s favor in the net
amount of $1,309,930.38, inclusive of interest, is affirmed.  Fees and costs of $150,083.04 were also
