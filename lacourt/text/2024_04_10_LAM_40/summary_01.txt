The Tentative Ruling is about a motion filed by Jilbert Tahmazian, Esq. and Tahmazian Law Firm, P.C. to be relieved as counsel for Defendant T-1 Lighting, Inc. The motion is unopposed and complies with California Rules of Court, rule 3.1362. However, the court notes that the motion was not served on the Plaintiff, Shanghai Hongbao Lighting Co., Ltd. The court grants the motion, conditioned on proof of service of the order on the Plaintiff or non-opposition to withdrawal at the hearing. The Defendant is advised to find a substitute attorney to represent it, as a company cannot represent itself in court.
