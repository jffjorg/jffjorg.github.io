**Tentative Ruling:**

The Court has reviewed the motion by Defendants Gregory Royston, Yuko Royston, and YGR Living Trust for an award of attorney's fees and costs related to the successful motion to expunge the lis pendens. The Court finds that the motion is granted in part.

**Award:**

Defendants are awarded $13,750.00 in attorney's fees and $1,223.12 in costs. The Court finds that the hourly rate of $550 per hour is reasonable, but the time spent by counsel appears excessive, and the Court reduces the award to $13,750.00 based on a Lodestar approach.

**Facts:**

The lawsuit was brought by Plaintiff Vipan Bhola against the Defendants for alleged violation of the Uniform Fraudulent Transfers Act. The Defendants successfully moved to expunge the lis pendens, and Plaintiff did not file an opposition.

**Analysis:**

The Court finds that the Defendants are entitled to an award of attorney's fees and costs under California Code of Civil Procedure § 405.38 and Labor Code sections 218.5, 226, and 2699. The Court grants the motion in part, awarding the Defendants the costs and fees as specified.
