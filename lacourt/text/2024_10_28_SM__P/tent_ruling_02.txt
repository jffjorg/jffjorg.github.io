 Case Number:  23SMCV00610    Hearing Date:   October 28, 2024    Dept:  P Tentative Ruling
Ramirez v. Velazquez, Case no. 23SMCV00610
Hearing date October 28, 2024
Plaintiffs’
Counsel’s Motions to be Relieved
Plaintiffs
Doraldina Ramirez, Consuelo Foronda and Jose Luis Navarro sue defendants Jaime
Zurita Velazquez, Berger Transfer & Storage and Penske Truck Leasing for
motor vehicle and general negligence claims arising from a motor vehicle
accident. Trial was set for 9/30/2024. Plaintiffs’ counsel Mark David Ruszecki
moved ex parte 9/17/2024 to be relieved as counsel. Plaintiffs argued
they did not receive notice of counsel’s motion to be relieved. The court noted
that no proofs of service had been filed. Min. Order 9/20/2024. The court
vacated the 9/30/2024 trial date and ordered Ruszecki to serve the motion to be
relieved on plaintiffs. Id. Ruszecki moves to be relieved as counsel for
all plaintiffs, citing a breakdown in the attorney-client relationship. Decl.
Ruszecki para. 2.
An
application to be relieved must be made on judicial counsel form MC-051 (Notice
of Motion and Motion) per Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(a), MC-052
(Declaration) per CRC rule 3.1362(c), and MC-053 (Proposed Order) per CRC rule
3.1362(e).¿ The proposed order must specify all hearing dates scheduled in the
action or proceeding, including the date of trial, if known. CRC rule
3.1362(e). Plaintiffs’ counsel has met all requirements under Cal. Rules of
Court 3.1362 and has filed proofs of service as to all plaintiffs.
Trial
is not set in this action. There is no risk of prejudice to plaintiffs.
GRANTED.
