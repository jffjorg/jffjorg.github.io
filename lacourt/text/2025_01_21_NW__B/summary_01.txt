The judge has granted the defendant's motion to compel the plaintiff's responses to discovery requests. The plaintiff failed to provide any responses to the defendant's discovery requests, which were propounded on May 23, 2024. As a result, the defendant is ordered to pay an additional $120.00 in filing fees. The plaintiff and their counsel are also ordered to pay $260.00 in sanctions to the defense counsel. The plaintiff is ordered to serve the defendant with verified, objection-free responses to the discovery requests within 20 days of the hearing.
