The court has issued a tentative ruling in response to Defendant Sam Tucker's motions to compel further responses from Plaintiff Noah Cvengros. The Plaintiff failed to provide complete and coherent responses to Defendant's discovery requests, including Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, and Requests for Admissions. The court has granted Defendant's motions and ordered the Plaintiff to provide verified, complete, and code-compliant responses to the discovery requests within 20 days. Additionally, the court has awarded Defendant monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,845.00 for the Plaintiff's misuse of the discovery process.
