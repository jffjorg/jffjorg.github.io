 Case Number:  23PSCV02122    Hearing Date:   March 19, 2024    Dept:  O Tentative Ruling
 
DEFENDANT
MERCEDES-BENZ USA, LLC’S MOTION TO COMPEL BINDING ARBITRATION is CONTINUED. 
 
Background
 
This is a
lemon law case. 
 
On July 14,
2023, Plaintiffs NELSON REYES and EVELYN REYES filed suit against Defendant
MERCEDES-BENZ USA, LLC for the following three causes of action (COAs): 1.     VIOLATION OF SONG-BEVERLY ACT - BREACH
OF EXPRESS WARRANTY 2.     VIOLATION OF SONG-BEVERLY ACT - BREACH
OF IMPLIED WARRANTY 3.     VIOLATION OF THE SONGBEVERLY ACT
SECTION 1793.2(b)
 
On November
7, 2023, Defendant filed its answer. 
 
On November
13, 2023, Defendant filed the instant motion. 
 
On March 6,
2024, Plaintiffs filed their opposition. 
 
On March 13,
2024, Defendant filed its reply. 
 
Discussion
 
As the Ford
Motor Warranty Cases (2023) 89 Cal.App.5th 1324 (“Ochoa”) is on appeal
to the California Supreme Court and the state supreme Court is expected to
address Felisilda v. FCA USA LLC (2020) 53 Cal.App.5th 486 regarding a
nonsignatory’s ability to enforce an arbitration agreement against a signatory
consumer, the court continues the motion pending a determination by the California
Supreme Court.
 
 
Conclusion
 
Based on the
foregoing, the motion to compel arbitration is continued.
