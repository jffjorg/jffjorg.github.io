MOTION
 
This case arises from an alleged motor vehicle accident.  Defendant Adolfo Barrera (“Defendant”) has
moved to strike from the Complaint the prayer for punitive damages.  
 
Plaintiffs Jesse Pastor-Lawrence and Axel Navarro-Lawrence
(collectively, “Plaintiffs”) have filed a notice of non-opposition, indicating
Plaintiffs agree to strike from the Complaint the prayer for punitive damages.
 
CONCLUSION AND ORDER
 
Therefore, the Court grants Defendant’s unopposed Motion to Strike the
prayer for  punitive damages from the
Complaint.
 
Further, the Court orders Defendant to file and serve an Answer to the
complaint on or before February 28, 2024.
 
Defendant shall provide notice of the Court’s ruling and file a proof
of service regarding the same.
