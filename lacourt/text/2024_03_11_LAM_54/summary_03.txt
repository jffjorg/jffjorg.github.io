And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling granting the plaintiff's motion to compel the defendant's person most knowledgeable (PMK) deposition and further responses to requests for production. The motion was filed by the plaintiff, Natalie G. Aghaei, seeking to compel the defendant, Jaguar Land Rover North America, LLC, to produce documents and testimony related to repairs to a subject vehicle, the defendant's investigation in response to the plaintiff's repurchase request, and warranty coverage of the repairs. The judge has ordered the defendant to produce the requested documents and to appear for deposition on April 9, 2024. The parties have agreed to this date. The judge has also noted that the defendant need not produce documents that have already been produced. The judge's ruling is based on California Code of Civil Procedure sections 2025.410 and 2025.450, which govern the production of documents and testimony in discovery.
