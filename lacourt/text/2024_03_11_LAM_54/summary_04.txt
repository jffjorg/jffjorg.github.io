The Tentative Ruling grants the petition for approval of the transfer of structured settlement payment rights. The petition was filed by J.G. Wentworth Originations, LLC, seeking approval of the transfer of a lump sum payment from Jennifer Ruiz, the payee. The court finds that the transfer is in the best interest of the payee, complies with the requirements of Insurance Code section 10139.5, and is fair and reasonable. The transfer involves a lump sum payment of $110,000.00 due on 9/27/2026, which the payee is exchanging for a present value of $76,000.00. The court notes that the payee has received independent professional advice and has waived the right to cancel the transfer agreement.
