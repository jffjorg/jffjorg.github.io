This is a tentative ruling by Judge Bruce G. Iwasaki on a petition to approve a compromise of a pending action on behalf of a minor, Kayden Diego Vicente. The case is a habitability case, in which the Plaintiffs, Rosa Maria Vicente Xec, Desideria Xec Tecum, and Kayden Diego Vicente, sued the Defendants, 310 South Alvarado St. LLC and Rampart Property Management, Inc., for breach of warranty of habitability, negligence, breach of implied covenant of quiet enjoyment, and breach of contract. The Plaintiffs have settled the case for $85,000, and are seeking approval of the compromise of Kayden Diego Vicente's claim. The Court finds that the petition is properly filed, and that the allocation of settlement funds is reasonable. The Court also finds that the attorney's fees of $1,250 are reasonable. The petition is granted, and the funds will be deposited with Rosa Maria Vicente Xec, the custodial parent, to be held in trust until Kayden Diego Vicente reaches majority.
