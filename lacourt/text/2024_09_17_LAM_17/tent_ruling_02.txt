DANIEL ELIEZER CORRAL PULIDO 
                           
         vs.
 
BMW OF NORTH AMERICA, LLC, et al.

 


 Case No.: 
  23STCV07048 
 
 
 
 Hearing Date:  September 17, 2024
 



 
 
            Plaintiff’s
motion for sanctions is MOOT.
 
            On
3/30/2023, Plaintiff Daniel Eliezer Corral Pulido (Plaintiff) filed suit
against BMW of North America, LLC, and Del Montell Motors, LTD dba Santa Monica
BMW, alleging violations of the Song-Beverly Act.
 
            On
7/24/2024, Plaintiff moved for sanctions against Defendant for its failure to
comply with the Court’s 5/1/2024 Order.
 
            On
8/8/2024, Plaintiff filed a Joint Statement re: Status of Mediation indicating
that the parties reached a settlement in principle, and that a Notice of
Settlement will be filed with the Court after a signed release is fully
executed. 
 
            Accordingly,
the Court considers this motion to be moot. 
 
It is so ordered.
