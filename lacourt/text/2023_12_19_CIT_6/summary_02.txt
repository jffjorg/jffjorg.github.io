The Judge has granted the motion to be relieved as counsel for Defendant Alicia Rodriguez, filed by Nicholas L. D'Amico of Blake Law Firm. The motion was unopposed and the Judge has discretion to allow an attorney to withdraw, as long as there is no prejudice to the client and the orderly process of justice is not disrupted. The Judge finds that there has been a breakdown in the attorney-client relationship and a material term of the underlying engagement such that representation cannot continue. The Judge orders Counsel to give notice of this ruling and file proof of service of same within five calendar days of this order, and to serve the signed Order upon Rodriguez and file proof of service of same within five calendar days thereof.
