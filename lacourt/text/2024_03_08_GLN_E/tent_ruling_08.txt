TENTATIVE RULING 
On
01/17/2024, Defendant Hagop Bardakjian, filed a motion to strike pursuant to
CCP §425.16, for an order striking the Second and Third Supplemental causes of
action of Plaintiff’s Verified First Supplemental Complaint, that was to be
heard on 03/08/2024. On 02/02/2024, attorney for Plaintiffs Arman Yegiyants and
10415 Commerce LLC and attorney for Defendant Hagop Bardakjian filed a stipulation
taking the instant motion off calendar as moot. On 02/02/2024, Judge Chowdhury
signed the stipulation. On 02/05/2024, attorney for Plaintiffs Arman Yegiyants
et al filed a notice of entry of order on the anti-SLAPP motion that contained
the stipulation taking the instant special motion to strike off calendar as
moot.
The instant motion is DENIED as MOOT and taken off
calendar.
