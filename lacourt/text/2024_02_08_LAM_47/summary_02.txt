The Court has issued a tentative ruling in a fraud case filed by Edward D. Fagan against Zhejiang Sunmarr International Transportation Co. Ltd. and others. The Court has struck the proofs of service on Kenneth and Paul Corkum as invalid because they lack supporting declarations from process servers. Therefore, the Court has denied the plaintiff's motion for an order issuing letters rogatory without prejudice. The Court has also denied the plaintiff's motion for an order to show cause re: proof of service because the plaintiff has failed to demonstrate that service was properly made on the Corkum defendants in compliance with the Hague Service Convention.
