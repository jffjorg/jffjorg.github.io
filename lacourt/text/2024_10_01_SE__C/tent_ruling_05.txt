#1 
TENTATIVE RULING
Plaintiff Maria Ramirez’s Motions to Compel
Further Responses to Request for Production of Documents (Set One) and Special
Interrogatories (Set One) are OFF-CALENDAR
Plaintiff to give notice.
 
This lemon law action was filed by Plaintiff MARIA RAMIREZ
(“Plaintiff”) against Defendants CERRITOS FORD, INC. (“Cerritos”); and FORD
MOTOR COMPANY (“Ford”) on October 7, 2022.
On August 6, 2024, this Court CONTINUED Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Further Responses to Request
for Production of Documents (Set One) and Motion to Compel Further Response to
Special Interrogatories (Set One) and ordered the parties to make further
efforts to resolve the issues presented. 
 
On August 29, 2024, the Court granted in part Ford’s motion
for a protective order.  
On September 23, 2024, counsel for both parties filed a
joint statement indicating that through further meet and confer efforts the
remaining discovery issues have been resolved. 
Accordingly, the motions are taken OFF-CALENDAR.
