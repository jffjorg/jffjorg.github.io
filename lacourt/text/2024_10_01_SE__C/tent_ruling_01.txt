discovery referee; advance the hearing on cross-complainants’ motion for

part.  Cross-Complainants’ motion for terminating sanctions is ADVANCED
from Thursday, July 10, 2025 to today and CONTINUED to Thursday, October 24,
2024 at 10:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.  That issue is fully briefed.  The
motion to vacate the April 17, 2024 stipulation and order appointing the
discovery referee and to compel Saadia to pay outstanding discovery referee
fees is specially set on October 24, 2024 at 10:390 a.m. in Dept. SE-C. 
The ex parte application is deemed to be the moving papers.  Opposition
and Reply briefs to be filed and served in compliance with the hearing
date.  Moving party to give notice.
