 Case Number:  23AHCV01818    Hearing Date:   August 6, 2024    Dept:  26   
 Palmer v. Torres, et al.MOTION TO SUBSTITUTE SUCCESSOR IN
INTEREST
(CCP § 377.10, et seq.)
TENTATIVE
RULING
 
Non-party Bobby Marcellus Gould’s Motion to Substitute Bobby Marcellus Gould, an Individual and
as Successor In Interest To Decedent Simone Palmer, is PLACED OFF CALENDAR. THE
FIRST AMENDED COMPLAINT IS TO BE FILED AND SERVED WITHIN 20 DAYS OF THIS ORDER.
 
 
ANALYSIS:
 
The
instant action was commenced by Plaintiff Simone Palmer (“Palmer”), Ahnesti
Johnson, and Knowledge Johnson (“minor Plaintiffs”) on August 10, 2023. The
action for motor vehicle negligence is brought against Defendants Hortencia
Reyes Torres and Samantha Reyes Torres (“Defendants”). On May 9, 2024, Moving
Party Bobby Marcus Gould (“Gould”) filed the instant Motion to Substitute Bobby
Marcellus Gould, an Individual and as Successor In Interest To Decedent Simone
Palmer. The Motion was initially heard on June 12, 2024 and continued to allow
Gould to file supplemental papers. (Minute Order, 06/12/24.) At the second
hearing on July 2, 2024, Plaintiff represented to the Court that a stipulation
was signed to amend the Complaint. (Minute Order, 07/02/24.) 
 
The signed
stipulation and proposed order to allow Plaintiff to file a First Amended
Complaint was filed on July 30, 2024. Accordingly, the Court signs the proposed
order on this date.  
 
Non-party Bobby Marcellus Gould’s Motion to Substitute Bobby Marcellus Gould, an Individual and
as Successor In Interest To Decedent Simone Palmer, is PLACED OFF CALENDAR. THE
FIRST AMENDED COMPLAINT IS TO BE FILED AND SERVED WITHIN 20 DAYS OF THIS ORDER.
 
 
Court
clerk to give notice.
