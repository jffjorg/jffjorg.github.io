The Judge issues a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further discovery responses to Form Interrogatories and Requests for Production of Documents in a personal injury action. The Plaintiff, Vertis Bayne, a minor, alleges that he was run over by a golf cart at Palmdale High School on May 12, 2023. The Defendant, Antelope Valley Union High School District, claims that it cannot release information about student witnesses due to privacy concerns and California Education Code sections. The Judge grants the Plaintiff's motions, ordering the Defendant to provide further discovery responses, subject to limitations discussed in the ruling. The Judge notes that the Defendant's actions have justification as it appears that the goal was to protect the privacy of minor students.
