RULING: The demurrer is placed off calendar.  
 
On 8/16/23, Plaintiff Maximilian I Webb (Plaintiff) filed
this action against Defendants Ford Motor Company (Ford) and Autonation Ford
Valencia (Autonation): (1) Violation of Civil Code 1793.2(d); (2) Violation of
Civil Code 1793.2(b); (3) Violation of Civil Code 1793.2(a)(3); 
(4) Breach of Implied Warranty of Merchantability (Civil
Code 1791.1, 1794, 1795.5); 
(5) Fraudulent Inducement – Concealment and (6) Negligent
Repair (not alleged against Ford).
 
On 9/18/23, Ford filed and served the instant demurer to
the 1st – 5th causes of action in the complaint which is
set for hearing on 2/2/24.  On 1/22/24,
nine court days before the hearing, Plaintiff filed and served a First Amended
Complaint which renders the demurrer moot. 
See CCP 472(a); CCP 1005(b).
