 Case Number:  24STCV07320    Hearing Date:   July 8, 2024    Dept:  61 ROSS LEVINSOHN, AN INDIVIDUAL vs THE ARENA GROUP HOLDINGS, INC., A DELAWARE CORPORATION, et al.TENTATIVEDefendants Simplify Inventions, LLC and Manoj Bhargava (Defendants) move to admit attorneys Lauren J. Walas and Eric J. Pelton as counsel pro hac vice. Both attorneys are not residents of California, and are not regularly employed in California. (Walas Decl. ¶¶ 5–6; Pelton Decl. ¶¶ 5–6.) Both are residents of Michigan, with that state as their primary place of employment. (Walas Decl. ¶¶ 5, 7; Pelton Decl. ¶¶ 5, 7.) Walas is licensed to practice in Maryland and the District of Columbia (Walas Decl. ¶¶ 9–10), while Pelton is licensed to practice law in Michigan. (Pelton Decl. ¶ 9.) Both are members in good standing of their respective states, neither has been suspended or disbarred, and neither have applied for pro hac vice admission in California in the previous two years. (Walas Decl. ¶¶ 10–12; Pelton Decl. ¶¶ 10–12.) The California State Bar has been served with the applications, and the $50 fee has been paid. (Walas Decl. ¶ 5; Pelton Decl. ¶ 5.)The applications are GRANTED.
