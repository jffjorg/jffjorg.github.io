The Judge has granted the petition to enforce the writ of mandate filed by Tiffany Abraham, the plaintiff. The writ was personally served on the respondents, City of Los Angeles and Los Angeles World Airports, on August 14, 2024. The respondents have failed to comply with the writ and have not filed a return or produced the responsive documents as ordered. The Judge has ordered the respondents to make a return of the writ, produce a list detailing the number of documents and emails located using the search terms, and state the basis upon which they are withholding any documents. The Judge has also ordered the respondents to produce a privilege log for any documents withheld due to claims of privilege or exemption. The Judge has given the respondents until October 24, 2024, to comply with the order.
