The Plaintiffs have moved to compel the deposition of Mike Nijjar, the president and general partner of Group V, which owns the subject premises. Group V has filed a motion for a protective order to prevent the deposition, arguing that it is an apex deposition. The Judge has granted the Plaintiffs' motion to compel the deposition of Mike Nijjar and has set a deadline for the deposition to occur within 30 days.
