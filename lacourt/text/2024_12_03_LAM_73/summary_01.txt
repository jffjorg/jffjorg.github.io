The Judge, Hon. Rolf Treu, has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Gatti v. CBS Broadcasting, Inc., et al. (19STCV03674). The case involves a petition to confirm an arbitration award in favor of the plaintiff, Gwendolyn Gatti, who was awarded $100,000 on her false imprisonment claim. The defendant, CBS Broadcasting, Inc., et al., did not oppose the petition. The Judge finds that the petition complies with the requirements of Code of Civil Procedure section 1285.4 and that there is no reason to correct the award, vacate the award, or dismiss the proceedings. As a result, the petition to confirm the arbitration award is GRANTED. The plaintiff is ordered to lodge a proposed judgment consistent with the arbitration award within 10 days, and the moving party is required to give notice.
