The court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Gerald Meehan and Lois Meehan vs. Cliffside Malibu 1. The plaintiffs moved to compel the defendant to respond to special interrogatories, set one, and to impose sanctions on the defendant and its counsel for failing to timely respond. The court grants the motion and orders the defendant to serve responses without objections to the special interrogatories within 15 days. Additionally, the court orders the defendant and its counsel to pay the plaintiffs $3,085 in sanctions within 15 days. The court finds that the defendant failed to act in good faith and that the sanctions are justified under the circumstances.
