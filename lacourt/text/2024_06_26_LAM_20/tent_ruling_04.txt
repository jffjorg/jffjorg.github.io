On December 20, 2022, Plaintiffs George Lamas and Claudia Lamas filed the operative Complaint against Defendants Accurate Rate Insurance Services, Palomares Escrow, Inc., and CIT Bank, N.A. for (1) breach of contract, (2) breach of fiduciary duty, (3) negligence, (4) negligence, (5) breach of contract, (6) breach of fiduciary duty, (7) negligence, (8) breach of contract, and (9) breach of fiduciary duty.  Plaintiffs allege that their newly purchased home was damaged in a fire, but that they did not receive insurance coverage for the damage.  Plaintiffs allege that Defendants received a notice that Plaintiffs had not paid for their insurance, but Defendants did not take any action thereafter.  Defendant Palomares Escrow, Inc. now seeks to compel initial responses from Plaintiffs to its form interrogatories, set one, and special interrogatories, set one.  Palomares Escrow, Inc. also seeks to deem admitted its requests for admission, sets one and two, propounded on Plaintiffs.Because responses have apparently not been provided, the Motions are granted.  The requests for admission are deemed admitted.  Responses, without objections, are to be provided to the interrogatories within 30 days.The Court awards reduced sanctions in the total amount of $1,250.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
