  Case Number:  22NWCV01133    Hearing Date:   August 16, 2024    Dept:  C Erik Clarke vs Ford Motor Company, et al., Case No.:
22NWCV01133
This is a breach of contract matter. Defendant Ford Motor
Company moves ex parte for an Order advancing the hearing date on its Motion to
Compel Plaintiff’s Deposition and Request for Monetary Sanctions; and Motion to
Compel Plaintiff’s Further Responses to Special Interrogatories, Set Two and
Request for Monetary Sanctions.  Defendant contends that it will be harmed
by not having its discovery motions heard prior to the motion for summary
judgment cutoff date, which are on or around October 17, 2024. Currently, the
discovery motions are set to be heard on December 3, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. 
The ex parte application is DENIED.  The Court is unable to accommodate
Defendant’s request.  Defendant is not foreclosed from seeking to continue
the January 30, 2025 trial date.  Defendant to give notice.
