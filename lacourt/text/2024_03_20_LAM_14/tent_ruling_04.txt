 Case Number:  23STCV24247    Hearing Date:   March 20, 2024    Dept:  14 Benjamin v Los Angeles Water and PowerCase Background
 
            Plaintiff
alleges that Defendants’ failure to properly repair their pipe caused damage to
his property.
 
            On October 5,
2023, Plaintiff filed his Complaint for (1) Conspiracy, (2) Defamation, (3) Trespass,
(4) Property Damage, and (5) Violation of 42 U.S.C. § 1983 against
Defendants Los Angeles Department of Water and Power (“LADWP”), Jeffrey Furnace
(“Furnace”), Cesar Cuevas (“Cuevas”), and Marty Adams (“Adams”).
 
            No trial
date has yet been set.
 
Instant Motion
 
            Plaintiff
now moves this court for an order dismissing his complaint, without prejudice. 
 
Decision
 
            The motion
is GRANTED. Code of Civil Procedure § 581(c) provides that a plaintiff may
dismiss their complaint, with or without prejudice, prior to the actual
commencement of trial.
