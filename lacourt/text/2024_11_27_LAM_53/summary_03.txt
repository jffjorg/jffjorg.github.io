The judge has granted the defendant's motion to compel responses to form interrogatories and for sanctions. The defendant, California Department of Social Services, served the plaintiff, Antoinette Morris, with form interrogatories on June 22, 2023, but the plaintiff failed to serve timely responses. The judge found that the plaintiff did not file opposition papers or evidence to establish that responses were served, and therefore granted the motion. The judge also ordered the plaintiff to pay monetary sanctions to the defendant in the amount of $440. The judge ordered the plaintiff to serve full and complete verified answers to the interrogatories within 20 days and to pay the sanctions within 30 days.
