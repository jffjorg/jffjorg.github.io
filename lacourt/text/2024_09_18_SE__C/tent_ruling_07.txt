TENTATIVE RULING
 
Plaintiff Diyana Foster’s
Motion for Leave to File First Amended Complaint is GRANTED.  Plaintiff to file and serve First Amended
Complaint within 5 days. 
 
Moving party to give Notice.
 
On October 17, 2023, Plaintiff
Diyana Foster filed a complaint against Defendant 10030 Alondra LLC alleging premises
liability and negligence.   
 
Plaintiff requests leave to
file and serve a first amended complaint which adds new facts.  Trial is scheduled for October 14, 2025.  Defendant does not argue prejudice.  
 
“A
court may, in furtherance of justice, and on any terms as may be proper, allow
a party to amend any pleading.”  (Code
Civ. Proc., § 473, subd. (a)(1).)  Such
amendment may occur “at any time before or after commencement of trial.”  (Code Civ. Proc., § 576.)  The court’s discretion will usually be
exercised liberally to permit amendments of the pleadings.  (Nestle v. City of Santa Monica (1972)
6 Cal.3d 920, 939.)¿
 
Plaintiff’s Motion for Leave
to File First Amended Complaint is GRANTED. 
Plaintiff to file and serve First Amended Complaint within 5 days.
