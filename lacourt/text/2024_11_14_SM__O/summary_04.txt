Here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Court grants Defendant/Cross-Complainant Western Surety Company's Motion to Deposit Bond Funds, Exoneration and dismissal of Western Surety, and for Attorneys' Fees and Costs. Western Surety Company has incurred reasonable attorneys' fees and costs in the amount of $13,554.66 in connection with the Interpleader action. The Court orders Western Surety Company to deposit the remaining balance of the Bond, $11,445.34, with the Los Angeles County Superior Court Clerk within 30 days of the entry of this Order. The Bond is exonerated and Western Surety Company is fully and forever released from any and all liability arising from the Bond. The Court also awards Western Surety Company its costs and reasonable attorney fees from the amount in dispute, which has been deposited with the court.
