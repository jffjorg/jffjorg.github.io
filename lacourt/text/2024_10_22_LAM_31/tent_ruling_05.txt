This is a Song-Beverly action.  On September 24, 2024, defendant Kia America,
Inc. (Kia) filed these motions to compel plaintiff Brenda Rowan to provide
further responses to Kia’s Special Interrogatories, Set One, and Request for
Production of Documents, Set One.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of this motion.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Kia’s motions to January 228, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31.  The parties are to contact
the judicial assistant to obtain a copy of the court’s tentative Song-Beverly
discovery order.  Thereafter the parties
are to contact the judicial assistant to schedule an IDC date and time.
