  Case Number:  22STCV08223    Hearing Date:   December 6, 2023    Dept:  57 The demurrer of Defendant Brock Real Estate to the cross-complaint of Piotr Kuczynski and Emily Kuczynski based on the proposition that the fraud-based claims against Brock Real Estate are barred by the applicable three-year statute of limitations is overruled.  In the Court's view,  the allegations in the cross-complaint fall within the protective ambit of the discovery rule and thus stave off application of the statute of limitations.The motion to strike of Defendant Brock Harris is denied.  In the Court's view, the allegations in the cross-complaint support an award of damages for emotional distress, punitive damages, and attorney's fees.
