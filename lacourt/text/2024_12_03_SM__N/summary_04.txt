And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has granted the motions of Courtney M. Serrato and Olivia Zorayan of Bremer Whyte Brown & O'Meara, LLP to withdraw as counsel for Defendants Hesam Dean Yazdanpanah aka D. Hess Panah and Law Offices of D. Hess Panah & Associates. The Judge found that the attorneys have a valid reason for withdrawal due to a breakdown of the attorney-client relationship caused by terms of representation not being honored. The attorneys have provided the required forms and served the client and opposing parties with the same. The ruling will become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon the defendants. Additionally, the Judge reminded the defendant corporation, Law Offices of D. Hess Panah & Associates, that it cannot represent itself in litigation and must be represented by licensed counsel.
