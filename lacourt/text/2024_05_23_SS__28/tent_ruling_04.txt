 Case Number:  23STCV02078    Hearing Date:   May 23, 2024    Dept:  28 On  April 4, 2024, the Court issued an order stating:  “The Court has reviewed the Expedited Petitions to  Approve Minor’s Compromise (Sandra P., age 15; and Ussain Efrain P. B., age 8).  The Court declines to approve the Petitions as requested. Pursuant to Cal Rules  of Court 7.950.5(c)(3), the Court elects to schedule a hearing date to permit  counsel to correct the following defects: (1) Defendant has not been served  with the petition. (2) There is no declaration of written assurance for  Ussain’s settlement proceeds. Counsel is ordered to file a declaration of written  assurance for Ussain, and serve Defendant with the petitions and proposed  orders.”  The  Court continued the hearing to May 23, 2024.  On  May 6, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a Declaration of Written  Assurance by Sandra Patricia Bonilla Sanchez  and proof of service on Defendant.  The  Court therefore grants the petitions for expedited approval of minor’s  compromises filed on March 4, 2024 on behalf of Claimants Sandra Perez and  Ussain Efrain Perez Bonilla.  The  Court orders that the net settlement amount of $913.43 be paid or delivered to Sandra  Patricia Bonilla Sanchez, mother of Claimant Ussain Efrain Perez Bonilla, 1505  Quartz Hill Drive, Bakersfield, CA, 93307, on the terms and under the  conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without bond.  The  Court orders that the net settlement amount of $16,022.53 for Claimant Sandra Perez be deposited in  an insured account at Bank of America, 11138  Main Street, El Monte, California 91731, (626) 527-9619, subject to withdrawal  only on authorization of the Court.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement on July 22, 2024, at 8:30  a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on July 22, 2024, at 8:30  a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  Petitioner  is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
