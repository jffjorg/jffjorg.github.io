Summary:

The court has granted the application for a right to attach order against Michael Sullivan & Associates, LLP in the amount of $996,807.16, which is the unpaid rent owed by the defendant for the period from August 1, 2023, through July 31, 2024. The defendant had argued that it was unable to pay the rent due to the COVID-19 pandemic, but the court found that the defendant had failed to meet its burden of proof to establish a valid defense. The court also found that CG Owner, LLC had made reasonable attempts to mitigate its damages and that the attachment was sought for a proper purpose. The court ordered that the plaintiff post a $10,000 undertaking before the right to attach order is issued.
