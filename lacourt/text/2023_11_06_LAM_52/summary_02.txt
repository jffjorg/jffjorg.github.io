The judge has made a tentative ruling in favor of the plaintiffs, Gerald Meehan and Lois Meehan, on their motion to compel depositions of witnesses Gal Batzri, Nitzan Ben-Nun, and Neta Golani. The judge finds that the motions are moot as to compelling the depositions, but the plaintiffs seek monetary sanctions. The judge also grants the plaintiffs' motion to compel defendant Pacific Horizon Holdings, LLC to respond to special interrogatories, set two. The judge orders the defendant to serve further responses without objections to the special interrogatories within 20 days.

Furthermore, the judge orders the defendant and its counsel, Rouben Varozian and David Beitchman, to pay the plaintiffs $3,085 in sanctions within 20 days. The judge finds that the defendant's delay necessitated this motion and its untimely responses are insufficient.
