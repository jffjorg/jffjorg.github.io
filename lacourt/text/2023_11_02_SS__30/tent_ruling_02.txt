 Case Number:  22STCV17284    Hearing Date:   November 2, 2023    Dept:  30 NICHOLAS MORENO vs JAMES ABRAHAMMotion to Compel Second Independent Medical Evaluation TENTATIVEDefendant James Abrahams’ motion to compel a second independent medical examination of Plaintiff Nicholas Moreno is GRANTED.  Plaintiff is ordered to submit to second independent medical examination pursuant to a duly noticed request. Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONAs a preliminary matter, defense counsel has attempted to meet and confer, but Plaintiff’s counsel has not responded to the requests. (Declaration of Gabriel H. Wainfeld, ¶5; Ex. “B.”)Plaintiff is alleging both orthopedic and neurological injuries because his medical records and deposition show he received both orthopedic and neurological treatment for his injuries. Because Plaintiff is seeking damages for both orthopedic and neurological injuries, the Court finds good cause exists for Defendant to complete a neurosurgical examination of Plaintiff. Further, the Court finds Defendant has complied with the specificity requirements enumerated within Code of Civil Procedure section 2032.310, subdivision (b). (Wainfeld Decl., ¶¶ 6-7; Ex. “C” and “D.”)Based on the foregoing, the Court grants Defendant’s unopposed regularly noticed motion.
