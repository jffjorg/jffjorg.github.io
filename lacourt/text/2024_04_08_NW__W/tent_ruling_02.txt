FACTUAL
BACKGROUND
 
On May 17, 2023, Plaintiff Neil
Buenaventura filed a complaint against Defendants Wetzel's Pretzels, LLC,
Westfield Topanga, Westfield Topanga Owner LLC, and West Valley Owner LLC for
negligence and premises liability. Plaintiff alleges that he slipped and fell
when he came into contact with a dangerous condition (blue liquid on the
ground) on the subject property. On October 4, 2023, Plaintiff dismissed
Westfield Topanga. 
 
TENTATIVE
RULING: DENY/CONTINUE
 
Counsel
states there has been an irreparable breakdown of the attorney client communications,
such that continued representation of this Plaintiff is not possible (Form
MC-051).
 
Counsel
did not file the mandatory MC-052 or MC-053 form. These forms are mandatory and
must be filed and served prior to the court granting the motion to be relieved
as counsel. Please see California Rules of Court, Rule 3.1362.
