The Judge, Honorable Gary Y. Tanaka, issued a tentative ruling on a motion for post-judgment attorneys' fees and costs filed by Carlos Esqueda, et al. against Creation Builders, Inc., et al. The case involves a home improvement project with alleged construction defects, which was taken to arbitration and resulted in a judgment entered on December 10, 2020. The motion seeks to recover reasonable attorneys' fees and costs incurred in enforcing the judgment.

The Judge granted the motion, finding that the judgment creditor is entitled to reasonable and necessary costs of enforcing the judgment, including attorneys' fees and costs. The Judge used the "lodestar" method to determine the reasonable amount of attorneys' fees, based on the number of hours spent by attorneys and paralegals and their reasonable hourly rates. The Judge awarded post-judgment attorneys' fees in the amount of $76,680.67 and costs in the amount of $25,645.76, for a total of $102,326.43. The ruling is pending further review.
