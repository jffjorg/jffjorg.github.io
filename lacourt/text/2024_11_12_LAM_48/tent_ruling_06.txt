[TENTATIVE] ORDER DENYING MOTION TO STAY
  AS MOOT 
 
Dept. 48
8:30 a.m.
November 12, 2024



 
On October 11, 2024, Defendant
Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. filed a motion to stay all proceedings pending
the Supreme Court’s decision in Everardo Rodriguez et al., v. FCA US, LLC.  On October 31, 2024, the Court issued its opinion.  The request for a stay is now moot.
In
its Reply, Defendant acknowledges that its motion is moot, and it a requests a trial
continuance to enable it to file a dispositive motion.  This is an improper new request on reply.
The
motion to stay is DENIED AS MOOT.

party to give notice.
