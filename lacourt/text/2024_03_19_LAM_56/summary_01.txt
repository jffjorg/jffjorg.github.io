Summary in English:

The tentative ruling is a decision made by Judge Holly J. Fujie on March 19, 2024, regarding a motion to compel further responses to discovery requests filed by Defendant Cuil Investments LLC. The motion was filed on November 17, 2023, and the responsive party, Plaintiffs Hans H. Kim and Aqua Wetclean, LLC, opposed the motion.

The Court found that Cuil LLC had shown good cause to compel further responses to discovery requests, and granted the motion. The Court ordered Plaintiffs to provide further responses to discovery requests within thirty (30) days of the date of this Order.

The Court also imposed sanctions against Plaintiff Aqua Wetclean and its counsel in the amount of $2,700.00, payable within twenty (20) days of the date of this Order. The Court based its decision on the facts that Plaintiffs had failed to provide sufficient responses to discovery requests, and that Cuil LLC had shown that it had acted with substantial justification in filing the motion.
