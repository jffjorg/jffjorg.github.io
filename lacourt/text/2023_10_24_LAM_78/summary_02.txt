This is a tentative ruling regarding a motion to compel a defendant to provide further responses to requests for production. The plaintiff, Alejandro Banuelos, is seeking to compel the defendant, Patriot Hyundai of El Monte, to provide further responses to requests for production, set two, numbers 33-36. The court finds that the defendant failed to serve supplemental responses on the correct address and that its objections to the requests for production are invalid. The court grants the motion to compel and orders the defendant to serve verified further responses without objections within five days after the date of this order. The court also imposes sanctions in the amount of $400 jointly and severally on the defendant and its counsel of record, payable within 30 days. The court finds that the defendant was not substantially justified in making this motion and that the plaintiff's motion to compel was granted.
