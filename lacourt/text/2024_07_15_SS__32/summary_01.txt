And here is the English-text summary:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to correct an error in a minute order entered on April 29, 2024. The motion was filed by the Defendant, County of Los Angeles, Deputies George Hanley and William Warner, who argue that the minute order is incorrect because it dismissed Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department and Unidentified Deputies without prejudice. The Court concludes that the second amended complaint, originally filed while the case was pending in federal court, is the operative pleading. Therefore, the Court vacates the April 29, 2024 order and sets aside the dismissal as to Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department and Unidentified Deputies in the First Amended Complaint. The Court also orders Plaintiffs to confirm that they are moving to dismiss Does 1 through 10 at the hearing on this motion.
