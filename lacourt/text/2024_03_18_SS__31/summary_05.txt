This is a tentative ruling by Judge Michelle C. Kim in the case of Dwayne Flowers vs. Jasmine Webb et al. The case number is 21STCV09808. The defendant, Jasmine Webb, has propounded a demand for inspection of documents and special interrogatories, and the plaintiff, Dwayne Flowers, has failed to serve timely responses. The defendant is seeking an order compelling the plaintiff to respond without objections and is also seeking sanctions.

The court has found that the plaintiff was properly served with the discovery requests and failed to timely respond. As a result, any objections have been waived. The court is granting the defendant's unopposed motion to compel the plaintiff to respond to the outstanding discovery requests.

The court is also imposing sanctions on the plaintiff for failing to serve timely responses. The sanctions will be in the amount of $370, which includes $250 in attorney's fees and $120 in costs. The plaintiff is ordered to pay the sanctions within 20 days.

The court has also awarded the defendant two motion filing fees of $60 each, for a total of $120. The defendant is ordered to give notice of the ruling.
