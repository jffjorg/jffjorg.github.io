And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court has denied, without prejudice, the motion by plaintiffs AWI Builders, Inc., Construction Contractors Corporation, Zhirayr "Robert" Mekikyan, and Anna Mekikyan to substitute the estate of Susan Nakagama in place of the deceased defendant Susan Nakagama. The court found that the plaintiffs have not presented sufficient authority or evidence to support the substitution of the estate, and have not complied with the Probate Code requirements for substituting the defendant's personal representative. The court has ordered the plaintiffs to file a new motion that complies with Code of Civil Procedure sections 377.40 and 377.41.
