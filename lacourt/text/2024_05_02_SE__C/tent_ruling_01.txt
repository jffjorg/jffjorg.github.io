  Case Number:  22NWCV00275    Hearing Date:   May 2, 2024    Dept:  C Cargo
Express Group, Inc., et al. vs Nexxgen Enterprises, Inc., et al.
This is a
breach of contract action.  Plaintiffs Cargo Express Group, Inc. and Eagle
Freight LLC apply ex parte for appointment of a discovery referee to hear
Defendants Milestone Equipment Company LLC and Milestone Chassis Company LLC’s
Motion to Quash Deposition and Motion for Protective Order.  The motions
are currently scheduled to be heard on September 24, 2024.  No trial date
has been set.  Plaintiffs have not demonstrated the need for a ruling on
an ex parte basis, nor have they demonstrated the need for a discovery referee
to hear the motion.  Therefore, Plaintiffs’ ex parte application for
appointment of a discovery referee is DENIED.  On the Court’s own motion,
Defendants’ Motion to Quash Deposition and Motion for Protective Order is ADVANCED
from September 24, 2024 to today’s date and CONTINUED to July 11, 2024 at 9:30
a.m. in Dept. SE-C.  Opposition and Reply briefs to be filed and served in
compliance with the new hearing date.  
Moving party to
give notice.
