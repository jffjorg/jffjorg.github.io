The tentative ruling is regarding a wage-claim action filed by Plaintiffs David Espinoza, Oscar Aparicio, and Ramon Gonzalez against Defendant Omni Logistics, LLC. The Plaintiffs allege that Defendant misclassified them as independent contractors, failed to pay wages owed, failed to provide accurate wage statements, and failed to reimburse for necessary business expenses. The Defendant demurred to the third and fourth causes of action. The Court sustained the demurrer to the third cause of action, which alleges failure to reimburse for necessary business expenses, due to a lack of factual allegations. However, the Court overruled the demurrer to the fourth cause of action, which alleges violation of Business and Professions Code §17200. The Court granted 30 days' leave to amend in part.
