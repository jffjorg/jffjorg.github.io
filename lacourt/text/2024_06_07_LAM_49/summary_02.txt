And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

**Demurrer to the Complaint**

The Judge rules that the Complaint does not state a cause of action for elder abuse and neglect. The Judge finds that the Complaint does not plead specific facts demonstrating a custodial relationship and that the Defendants' alleged conduct was reckless, oppressive, or malicious.

**Demurrer to the Second Cause of Action for Negligence/Willful Misconduct**

The Judge rules that the Complaint does state a cause of action for negligence. The Judge finds that the Complaint alleges that the Defendants, as hired caretakers, owed a duty of care to the decedent and breached that duty by failing to care for or treat the decedent's pressure sores.

**Motion to Strike**

The Judge grants the motion to strike the Complaint's demand for punitive damages. The Judge finds that the Complaint does not plead specific facts to support an award of punitive damages. The Judge rules that the Complaint does not allege facts entitling the Plaintiffs to punitive damages.

**Order**

The Judge orders that the demurrer to the Complaint be sustained, and that the motion to strike be granted. The Judge grants leave to amend the Complaint to plead specific facts demonstrating a reasonable possibility of a successful amendment.
