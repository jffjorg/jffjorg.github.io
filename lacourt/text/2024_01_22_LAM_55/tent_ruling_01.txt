  Case Number:  22STCV25895    Hearing Date:   January 22, 2024    Dept:  55 NATURE OF PROCEEDINGS:  Plaintiffs’ Motion
For Order Admitting Elaine S. Kusel As Counsel Pro Hac Vice.Plaintiffs filed a motion requesting an order admitting
Elaine S. Kusel as Counsel pro hac vice. Defendant did not file an opposition.

The motion sufficiently complies with California Rules
of Court, Rule 9.40, including the applicant’s declaration addressing the rule
elements.  The Court therefore grants the
motion and attorney Kusel may appear as counsel pro hac vice.
