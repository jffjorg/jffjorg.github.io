Clerk to give NOTICE. 
 
 
This is a partition action. Plaintiff Mary
Lou Kramer, Trustee of the Kramer Family Trust, sues defendants Ricardo Orosco
Martinez, Trustee of the Martinez Family Revocable Living Trust and Marcus
Martinez for the following: (1) partition by sale; (2) accounting; and (3)
ejectment. 
 
Defendant Ricardo Orosco Martinez moves to
compel Marcus Martinez to provide further responses to Form Interrogatories,
Set One and verified responses to Requests for Admissions, Set One. 
 
On July 17, 2024, Plaintiff filed a request
for dismissal as to Defendant Ricardo Orosco Martinez, Trustee of the Martinez
Family Revocable Living Trust dated June 18, 2018. 
 
Thus, defendants’ motions are MOOT.
