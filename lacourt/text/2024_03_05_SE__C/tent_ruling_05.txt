 Case Number:  23NWCV00379    Hearing Date:   March 5, 2024    Dept:  C Peter Gonzalez v. City
of Los Angeles, et al., Case No. 23NWCV00379
 
This is a trip and fall that
occurred on a public sidewalk in the City of Whittier. Defendant LaRue Living
Trust owned the residence adjacent to the public sidewalk. It moves ex
parte for an order to advance or specially set the hearing on its Motion
to Compel Deposition of Plaintiff Petra Gonzalez and Impose Monetary Sanctions,
which is currently set for November 19, 2024.
 
A party may seek ex
parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable harm,
immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Defendant La Rue Living Trust
contends that Plaintiff has failed to appear for and participate in her
deposition or answer questions related to her allegations despite being served
with timely and properly served deposition notices. (Déziel Decl., ¶¶ 4-11.)
The ex parte application is GRANTED.  Defendant La Rue Living Trust’s
Motion to Compel Deposition of Plaintiff Petra Gonzalez is ADVANCED from
November 19, 2024 to today’s date and CONTINUED to April 18, 2024 at 9:30 a.m.
in Dept. SE-C.  
 
Moving party to give notice.
