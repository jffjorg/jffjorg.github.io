  Case Number:  23STCV19069    Hearing Date:   July 8, 2024    Dept:  74 The People
of the State of California v. Ultimate Host, LLC, et al.
Plaintiff People of the State of
California’s Motion to Compel Defendant Ultimate Host, LLC’s Responses to Form
Interrogatories (Set One) and Special Interrogatories (Set One).
 
The
court grants Plaintiff’s unopposed motions filed March 22, 2024, to compel Defendant
Ultimate Host, LLC’s responses to form interrogatories (set one) and special
interrogatories (set one).  Defendant Ultimate
Host, LLC is ordered to serve without objection Code compliant responses to form
interrogatories (set one) and special interrogatories (set one) within 10 days.
The court orders Defendant to pay $1,140 in attorney’s fees (2 x $720) to
Plaintiff within 30 days. 
Plaintiff shall give notice.
