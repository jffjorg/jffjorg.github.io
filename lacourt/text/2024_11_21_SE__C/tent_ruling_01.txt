  Case Number:  18NWCV00147    Hearing Date:   November 21, 2024    Dept:  C Makridis v. Boulos 18NWCV00147
 
This
action was filed on December 10, 2018. A Judgment, after jury trial, was
entered on June 5, 2024 for Plaintiff George Makridis and against Defendants
William Watson; Rescue One Ambulance; and Andrew Ayad Boulos in the total
amount of $1,133,500.00. On August 5, 2024, Defendants Boulos and Watson filed
a Notice of Appeal. 
 
The
following motions are currently scheduled to be heard on December 17, 2024 by
the Honorable Julian Recana in Dept, SE-L: Hearing on Claim of
Exemption/Third-Party Claim; and Motion to Stay Enforcement of Judgment for
Appeal. 
 
There
is a Hearing on Application for Order for Appearance and Examination of Andrew
A. Boulos set for hearing in Dept. SE-F on January 14, 2025. 
 
Defendant
Boulos' Motion to Quash Service of Application and Order for Appearance and
Examination is scheduled to be heard on March 27, 2025 in Dept. SE-C. 
 
Defendant
Boulos now moves ex parte to advance the hearing date on Defendant Boulos'
Motion to Quash Service of Application and Order for Appearance and Examination
("Motion to Quash") to be heard on December 17, 2024, in conjunction
with the other two motions set for hearing on that same date. Boulos argues
that the Motion to Quash should be heard prior to the Debtor's Exam. Defendant
Boulos also requests that the Motion to Quash be transferred to Dept. L to be
heard by Judge Recana. 
 
Defendant
Boulos' Ex Parte Application to Advance Defendant's Motion to Quash Service of
Application and Order for Appearance and Examination; and for an Order to
Transfer the Motion Quash to Dept. L is GRANTED. Defendant's Motion to
Quash Service of Application and Order for Appearance and Examination is ADVANCED
from March 27, 2025 to today's date and CONTINUED to December 17,
2024 at 10:30 a.m.  in Dept. SE-L. Opposition and Reply papers to be
filed and served per Code in accordance with the new hearing date. Moving Party
to give notice.
