ANALYSIS:  
 
On March 9, 2023,
Plaintiff Creditors Adjudgment Bureau, Inc. (“Judgment Creditor”)
filed this action against Defendants Olympus ADHC, Inc. Dba National Adult Day
Care adba Olympus Adult Day Health Center adba National Adult Day Health
Center; and RJR Care, Inc. dba National Adult Day Care adba Olympus Adult Day
Health Center (“Judgment Debtor). On July 21, 2023, this Court entered default
judgment against Judgment Debtors for the total amount of $17,269.40.

 
Judgment Creditor filed the instant Motion for an Assignment
Order on March 27, 2024. The Motion came for hearing on July 1, 2024, at which
time it was continued for further briefing evidencing payments to be made to
Judgment Debtors by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services and Noridian
Healthcare Solutions, LLC. (Minute Order, 07/01/24.) Judgment Creditor filed a
supplemental affidavit on August 26, 2024 declaring that subpoenas for the
production of documents have been served to the custodian of records of Medicare
and  Medicaid Services and to the
custodian of records of Noridian Healthcare Solutions, LLC. (Motion, Supp.
Frischer Decl., ¶3.) Responses to those subpoenas are currently pending. (Ibid.)

 
Accordingly, the Court again continues the hearing on
Judgment Creditor’s Motion for Assignment Order.
