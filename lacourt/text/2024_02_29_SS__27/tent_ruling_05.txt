RULING:


Deny



 
TENTATIVE 
The Court excuses the personal
appearance of Claimants and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by
telephone or video conference call.  
The Court has reviewed the
Petition to Approve Compromise of Pending Action of Alex Nikolai,14. The
application is denied for the following reasons: Under 11(b)(3) and 11(b)(4),
the petition states that petitioner LILIT KHACHATRYAN is a plaintiff in the
same action with the claimant, and she would receive money under the proposed
settlement, but she is not a party to the action. No breakdown of the cost is
provided. Requesting party need to provide reasons for the apportionment of the
settlement payments between the claimant and the other Plaintiff in Attachment
11b(6).
The Court has reviewed the
Petition to Approve Compromise of Pending Action of Aaron Alchian, 14. The
application is denied for the following reasons: Attachment 11b(3) needs to be
filed describing the circumstances and the effect your claim and its
disposition has on the proposed compromise of the claim or action described in
this petition. Requesting party needs to provide reasons for the apportionment
of the settlement payments between the claimant and the other Plaintiff in
Attachment 11b(6). No breakdown of the cost is provided. Furthrer, on MC-351,
the Claimant is listed as Alex Nikolai and not Aaron Alchian.
CLAIMANTS ARE ORDERED TO
CORRECT THE DEFICIENCIES NOTED ABOVE. 
FAILURE TO DO SO MAY RESULT IN SANCTIONS.  THE COURT SETS AN OSC RE MINOR COMPROMISE ON
MARCH 29, 2024, IN DEPARTMENT 27 AT 8:30 AM. NO APPEARANCE REQUIRED IF RECEIPT
IS FILED IN ADVANCE.
Moving party is ordered to give
notice.
