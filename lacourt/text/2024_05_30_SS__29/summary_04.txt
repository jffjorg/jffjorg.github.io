The Court will consider two discovery disputes between City and NR Development, Inc. (NR) on May 30, 2024. City filed motions to compel NR to respond to Form Interrogatories and for an order deeming NR to have admitted as true the matters specified in Requests for Admission. NR failed to provide timely responses to these discovery requests. City made false statements in its moving papers regarding NR's failure to provide verifications. The Court will hear from counsel regarding whether these false statements were intentional or accidental. The motions are continued for at least 21 days.
