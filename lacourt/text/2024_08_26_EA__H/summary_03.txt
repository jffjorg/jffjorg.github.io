The court has issued a tentative ruling on the plaintiff's application for default judgment against the defendant, White Mountain Trucking, Inc. The plaintiff, Mediterranean Shipping Company (USA) Inc., as agent for MSC Mediterranean Shipping Company, S.A., Geneva, alleges that the defendant failed to return intermodal equipment and pay sums due under a Uniform Intermodel Interchange and Facilities Access Agreement. The court has denied the plaintiff's application for default judgment without prejudice, citing several defects in the plaintiff's submission, including the lack of documentary evidence of the relationship between the plaintiff and MSC Mediterranean Shipping Company, S.A., Geneva, and the lack of signature pages to the agreements attached to the declaration. The court has directed the plaintiff to submit a new default judgment packet that addresses these issues and provides additional documentation, including copies of invoices summarized in Exhibit 3 to the declaration.
