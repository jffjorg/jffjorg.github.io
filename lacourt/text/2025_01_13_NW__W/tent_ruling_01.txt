On August 9, 2023, Plaintiff Commercial Scaffolding of
California, Inc. filed a complaint against Ice Rink Rentals, LLC, GKT Warner Center
Mall CPR, L.L.C., GKT Warner Center Mall Katy, L.L.C., GKT Warner Center Mall GKT,
L.L.C., GKT Warner Center Mall JTCC, L.L.C., GKT Warner Center Mall WTCC, L.L.C. for (1) monies due; (2) quantum
meruit; (3) breach of contract; (4) account stated; (5) foreclosure of
mechanic’s lien; and (6) declaratory relief. Plaintiff alleges Defendants
became indebted to Plaintiff in the sum of $59,860.00 plus further amounts for
labor, services and materials rendered by Plaintiff to Defendants. 
 
Plaintiff filed a Notice of Lis Pendens on May 22,
2023. Plaintiff withdrew its Notice of Pendency Action on October 28, 2024. 
 
GKT Warner Center Mall Katy, L.L.C. filed a
cross-complaint against Ice Rink Rentals LLC for breach of contract, express
and implied indemnity, equitable indemnity and declaratory relief on November
3, 2023. 
 
On February 9, 2024, Commercial Scaffolding of
California’s request for entry of default was entered. Commercial Scaffolding
dismissed the remaining Defendants on October 28, 2024. 
 
On March 7, 2024, GKT Warner Center Mall Katy, L.L.C
dismissed their Cross-Complaint against Ice Rink Rentals LLC.
