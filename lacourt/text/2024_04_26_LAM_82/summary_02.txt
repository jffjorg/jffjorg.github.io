This is a tentative ruling by Judge Beckloff on a motion for an order finding that communications between Petitioner Mabel Frias and her counsel are not privileged. The motion is granted in part and denied in part. The court finds that communications between Petitioner and her counsel transmitted through Luna Magic's server on or after April 12, 2022, are not privileged under Holmes v. Petrovich Development Co., LLC. The court also finds that Petitioner agreed to be bound by the conditions of the employee handbook and the relevant provisions of the technology policy. The court's ruling is based on the evidence presented, including the employee handbook and the testimony of the parties.
