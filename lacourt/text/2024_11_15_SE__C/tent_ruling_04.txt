 Case Number:  24NWCV01115    Hearing Date:   November 15, 2024    Dept:  C Sonterra Capital vs Hyung Do Min, et
al., Case No.: 24NWCV01115
This is an unlawful detainer action.
Plaintiff Sonterra Capital applies ex parte for an application to unseal record
and enforcement judgment and enter default. The ex parte application is DENIED
without prejudice.  Plaintiff does not provide adequate evidence that the
stipulated settlement has allowed the Court to retain jurisdiction. Further,
Plaintiff does not include the alleged stipulated settlement for the Court’s
review. Moving party to give notice.
