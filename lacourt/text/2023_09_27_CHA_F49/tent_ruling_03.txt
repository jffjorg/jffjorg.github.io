SUMMARY
OF ACTION
On
February 14, 2023, the Court of Appel reversed the default judgment and
postjudgment order conveying title to real property from Road Runner to Dewelt,
and remanded the case. Road Runner was awarded costs on appeal. Remittitur was
filed on April 20, 2023. On June 23, 2023, Department 47 accepted the 170.6
peremptory challenge filed by defendants, and the action was assigned to
Department 49.
 
Meanwhile,
on May 26, 2023, Dorene Robinson, in pro per, filed a memorandum of costs on
behalf of Road Runner Land & Homes, LLC listing $1,022.04 in costs and
seeking $20,000 in attorney fees. 
 
RULING: Granted.
Plaintiff
Dewelt, LLC moves to tax the May 26, 2023, filed memorandum of costs filed by
Dorene Robinson, in pro per, on behalf of Road Runner Land & Homes, LLC
listing $1,022.04 in costs and $20,000 in attorney fees. Plaintiff moves to tax
on grounds that a party in pro per cannot recover fees and costs on behalf of a
corporate entity. 
 
The
court electronic filing system shows no opposition or reply at the time of the
tentative ruling publication cutoff. Plaintiff filed a notice of non-opposition
as well.
 
California Rule of
Court, Rule 8.278 allows for a motion to tax costs to be filed in the same
manner as a challenge under California Rules of Court, rule 3.1700. (Cal. Rules
Ct., rule 8.278(c)(2).) The memorandum of costs was filed on May 26, 2023; the
instant motion filed on June 2, 2023. The motion is timely. The appellate court
only awarded costs, no attorney fees, but a prevailing party may still seek
attorney fees as an item of costs. (Cal. Rules Ct., rule 8.278(d)(2).) The
court docket shows no motion to recover attorney fees filed.
 
The
Court of Appeal only awarded the recovery of fees and costs to Road Runner Land
& Homes, LLC. Road Runner Land & Homes, LLC, as a corporate entity may not may not appear
in this action without counsel. (Merco
Constr. Engineers, Inc. v. Municipal Court (1978) 21 Cal.3d 724, 731.)
Dorene Robinson, a party appearing in pro per may not appear on behalf of Road
Runner Land & Homes, LLC.
 
The
motion to tax is granted.
