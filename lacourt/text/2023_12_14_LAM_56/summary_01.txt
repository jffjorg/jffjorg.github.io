And here is the English text summary:

The tentative ruling is in response to a demurrer filed by the defendants, Move Sales, Inc., against the plaintiffs, Patience and Fortitude, Inc. and Wander and Ponder. The demurrer argues that the claims against the defendants, Kalie Caldwell and Harold Hildebrand, are time-barred and do not allege a basis for imposing alter-ego liability. The court has considered the moving and opposition papers and has found that the complaint includes sufficient information regarding the lack of corporate formalities to allege a unity of interest to support alter-ego allegations against Caldwell and Hildebrand with respect to PFI and WP. The court has overruled the demurrer with respect to the first, second, and fourth causes of action. However, the court has sustained the demurrer to the third cause of action, which alleges an account stated, with 20 days leave to amend. The court has ordered the moving party to give notice of this ruling and has specified the procedures for submitting on this tentative.
