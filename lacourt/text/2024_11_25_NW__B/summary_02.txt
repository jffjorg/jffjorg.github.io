The Judge has issued a tentative ruling granting the application of Lina R. Powell, Esq. to appear pro hac vice on behalf of Defendants Savage Services Corporation and Gaston Scranton in the case of Erbent Geobany Martinez, Johnarcadio Martinez Ramirez, and Stephanie Martineth Nineth Martinez Zepeda v. Savage Services Corporation, Gaston Scranton, and DOES 1 to 100. The application was filed on October 29, 2024, and was unopposed. The Judge found that the application conforms with California Rules of Court Rule 9.40, which allows an attorney in good standing from another jurisdiction to appear pro hac vice in California. The Judge noted that the application included all required information, including the attorney's residence and office addresses, the courts to which the attorney has been admitted, and representation that the attorney is in good standing. The Judge also observed that notice of the hearing was served on the State Bar of California, along with the required $50.00 fee. As a result, the Judge granted the application.
