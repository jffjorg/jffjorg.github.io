**Facts**

* Respondent Daniel Reisbord filed a claim with Petitioner California Automobile Insurance Company for a loss to his 1977 Air Stream Trailer

* Petitioner has initiated an appraisal proceeding and has identified Universal Appraisal Company (UAC) as their appraisal firm

* Petitioner seeks an order appointing an umpire and compelling an appraisal to resolve the dispute over the value of the loss
* Respondent has not opposed the petition

* The Court has denied Petitioner's instant petition without prejudice
