 Case Number:  24NWCV00031    Hearing Date:   April 30, 2024    Dept:  C Station
11 LLC vs Arayna Hedgepeth, Case No. 24NWCV00031
This is an
unlawful detainer action.  Plaintiff Station 11 LLC applies ex parte to
Enforce the Stipulated Agreement.  The Agreement calls for Defendant to
vacate the premises and pay reduced damages in the amount of $5,562.00. 
Defendant has failed to make the agreed upon payments of $900 per month. 
However, Defendant has vacated the premises, and Defendant’s only outstanding
obligation is payment of money damages.  Thus, Plaintiff has failed to
demonstrate irreparable harm or immediate danger. (Cal. Rule of Court, Rule
3.1202(c).)  The ex parte application is DENIED.  Plaintiff is not
foreclosed from filing a noticed motion.  
Moving party to
give notice.
