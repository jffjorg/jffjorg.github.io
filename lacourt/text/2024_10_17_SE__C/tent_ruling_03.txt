 Case Number:  22NWCV00320    Hearing Date:   October 17, 2024    Dept:  C The Pacific Southwest District of The
Lutheran Church-Missouri Synod vs Irene Jensen, et al, Case No.: 22NWCV00320
This is a real property action.
Defendant and Cross Complainant PEACE LUTHERAN CHURCH, INCORPORATED, OF SOUTH
GATE, CALIFORNIA (“PLC”) applies ex parte to advance the hearing date for its
Motion For Summary Judgement, or In the alternative Summary Adjudication,
currently scheduled for March 5, 2025.  This date is 27 days before trial,
which is set for March 19, 2025.  A motion for summary judgment shall be
heard no later than 30 days before trial, unless the court for good cause
orders otherwise. (CCP §437c(a)(3).) Defendant’s ex parte application is
DENIED.  The Court is unable to accommodate the request due to its
congested calendar.  The Court exercises its discretion to hear the motion
on the date currently set.  Moving party to give notice.
