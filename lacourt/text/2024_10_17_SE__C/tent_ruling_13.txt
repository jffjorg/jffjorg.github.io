 Case Number:  24NWCV03161    Hearing Date:   October 17, 2024    Dept:  C LISA MARIE VERNOLA-SALAS, SUCCESSOR
TRUSTEE OF THE VERNOLA FAMILY TRUST vs URBAN SOCCER 5 CENTER, LLC, Case No.:
24NWCV03161
This is an unlawful detainer action.
Plaintiff Lisa Marie Vernola-Salas applies ex parte for a ruling on the
demurrer to the Complaint or, in the alternative, for an order shortening time
for hearing on the demurrer. Defendant’s demurrer is currently set for July 15,
2025.  Because an unlawful detainer action is an expedited proceeding, the
Court finds good cause to advance the demurrer. Accordingly, Plaintiff’s ex
parte application is GRANTED in part.  Plaintiff’s Demurrer is ADVANCED
from Tuesday, July 15, 2025 to this date and CONTINUED to Tuesday, November 19,
2024 at 10:30 a.m. in Department SE-C.  Opposition and Reply briefs to be
filed in compliance with the new hearing date.  Moving party to give
notice.
