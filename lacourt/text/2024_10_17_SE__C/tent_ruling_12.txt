 Case Number:  24NWCV02382    Hearing Date:   October 17, 2024    Dept:  C 3301 Leonis, LLC vs Furniture Delivery
Solutions, Inc., Case No.: 24NWCV02382
This is an unlawful detainer action.
Plaintiff 3301 Leonis, LLC (”Plaintiff”) applies ex parte for an order
shortening time to hear Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Attendance at Deposition.
The Motion is currently scheduled for hearing on Wednesday, July 16, 2025.
Because an unlawful detainer action is an expedited proceeding, the Court finds
good cause to advance the motion. Accordingly, Plaintiff’s ex parte application
is GRANTED. Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Attendance at Deposition is ADVANCED
to this date and CONTINUED to November 21, 2024 at 10:30 a.m. in Det. SE-C.
Opposition and Reply briefs to be filed in compliance with the new hearing
date.  Moving party to give notice.
