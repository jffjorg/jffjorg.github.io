Plaintiff’s unopposed Motion to Compel Deposition Attendance
of Defendant AMERICAN HONDA MOTOR CO., INC.’s PMQ and Custodian of Records is GRANTED.

 
Moving Party to give notice.
 
No Opposition filed as of October 14, 2024. Due by October
4, 2024. (CCP §1005(b).)  
 
This lemon law action arising out of Plaintiffs’ purchase of a 2023
Honda HR-V Vehicle was filed on July 12, 2023.  
 
Plaintiff moves to compel the deposition of Defendant American Honda
Motor Co., Inc.’s (“Defendant”) PMQ and Custodian of Records, with production
of documents. 
 
“Pursuant to CCP §2025.450, if after service of a deposition
notice, a party to the action, without having served a valid objection, fails
to appear for examination, or proceed with it, or to produce for inspection any
document, the party giving notice may move for an order compelling the
deponent’s attendance and testimony, and the production for inspection of any
document. (CCP §2025.450(a).) 
 
The unopposed Motion is GRANTED. Defendant is ORDERED to
produce its PMQ and Custodian of Records to appear for a deposition by no later
than 30 calendar days from the date of the Court’s issuance of this Order. This
date may be extended by agreement of the parties. 
 
Reasonable sanctions in the amount of $1,357.00 is GRANTED.
Defendant and its Counsel of Record are ORDERED to pay Plaintiffs and their
Counsel of Record sanctions in the amount of $1,357.00 within 30 days from the
date of the Court’s issuance of this Order. This date may be extended by
agreement of the parties.
