 Case Number:  21STCV47154    Hearing Date:   June 10, 2024    Dept:  28 Having  considered the petitioning and supplemental papers, the Court rules as follows.  BACKGROUND  On  December 28, 2021, Plaintiff Saghar Yadegar, by and through her guardian ad  litem Samira Yadegar (“Plaintiff”), filed this action against Defendants Nestor  R. Gonzalez, M.D. (“Gonzalez”), Zachary Barnard, M.D. (“Barnard”), Cedars-Sinai  Health System (“Cedars- Sinai”), and Does 1-50 for medical negligence.  On  May 17, 2022, Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint.  On  June 20, 2022, Gonzalez and Cedars-Sinai filed an answer.  On  June 21, 2022, the Court dismissed Barnard with prejudice at Plaintiff’s  request. On December 28, 2023, the Court dismissed Gonzalez with prejudice at  Plaintiff’s request.  On  August 28, 2023, Plaintiff filed a notice of settlement.  On  January 19, 2024, Petitioner Samira Yadegar (Petitioner) filed a revised  petition to approve the compromise of a claim of a person with a disability,  set for February 8, 2024.  On  February 8, 2024, the Court heard argument and continued the matter to March  18, 2024.  On  February 29 and March 13, 2024, Petitioner filed supplements to the revised  petition.  On  March 18, 2024, the Court continued the hearing on the revised petition to  April 19, 2024. The Court ordered Petitioner’s counsel to file a supplemental  declaration concerning the outcome of a Probate Court hearing scheduled for  April 12, 2024 in Plaintiff’s conservatorship case.  On  April 19, 2024, the Court continued the hearing to June 10, 2024 and ordered Petitioner  to file a supplemental declaration or pleading describing the Probate Court’s  rulings in the conservatorship case.  STATUS OF CONSERVATORSHIP  CASE  On  June 4, 2024, Petitioner filed a Fourth Verified Supplement to the Revised  Petition for Approval of Compromise of Claim filed on January 19, 2024 (the  “Revised Compromise Petition”).  Petitioner stated that on May 31, 2024, a hearing was  held before the Honorable Stephanie C. Santoro in Saghar Yadegar’s  conservatorship case, Los Angeles Superior Court Case No. 21STPB11784,  regarding Petitioner’s (1) Petition to Authorize Conservator to Marshal Assets  Resulting From Compromise of Medical Malpractice Settlement, (2) Petition for  Order to File Bond for Conservator, (3) Petition to Invest in Mutual Funds and  U.S. Government Bonds with Maturity Dates Later than Five Years, and (4)  Petition to Set Review Hearing Date for Filing of the First and Current Account  of Conservator (“Petition to Marshal Assets”).  Following the hearing, the Probate Court  issued an order authorizing Samira Yadegar, as Conservator of the Estate, to  receive and marshal all settlement funds (cash and annuity payments) from this medical  negligence litigation.  The Probate  Court’s May 31, 2024 minute order grants the Petition - Authority  (Conservatorship/Guardianship) filed on 2/29/2024 by Petitioner(s) Samira  Yadegar.  The Probate Court made the  following orders:  ·       The Probate Court orders and authorizes Samira  Yadegar, as Conservator of the Estate, to receive and marshal all settlement  funds (cash and annuity payments) from the litigation entitled Saghar Yadegar,  by and through her Guardian ad Litem, Samira Yadegar, Plaintiff v. Nestor R.  Gonzalez, M.D. et al., Defendants, filed on December 28, 2021 in the Los  Angeles County Superior Court, Case No. 21STCV47154.·       Samira Yadegar as Conservator of the Estate for the  Conservatorship of Saghar Sasha Yadegar shall file bond in the amount of  $2,000,000 (rounded and not as prayed). ·       The Conservator of the Estate is authorized to invest  in mutual funds and to invest in government bonds with maturity dates of  greater than five (5) years. ·       The Probate Court sets a Review Hearing for the filing of the  Conservator's First and Current account to be filed no later than 4/14/2025 and  OSC set for 6/12/2025 (not as prayed). ·       The Court Appointed Counsel remains and is authorized  to perform an additional 10 hours of service. ·       The Probate Court orders the Conservator(s) to file an  accounting no later than Monday, April 14, 2025. ·       The Probate Court sets an Order to Show Cause Hearing on  Thursday, June 12, 2025 at 8:30 AM in [the Probate Court]. ·       Attorney for Petitioner is ordered to prepare the  Order After Hearing.  PAYMENT TO CONSERVATOR  The  Probate Court “determine[d] that the payment request of $250,000.00 to Samira  Yadegar as set forth in the Revised Compromise Petition filed on January 19,  2024 in Case No. 21 STCV47154 may proceed in the Civil Department.”  The  Court grants the payment request and orders that Petitioner Samira Yadegar will  receive $250,000.00 of the settlement proceeds.  ANNUITIES  Petitioner  has provided updated payment information for the annuities.  DISCUSSION            Petitioner has submitted a completed application for  approval of the compromise of Plaintiff’s claims.  The Court grants the application. The Court grants the payment request and orders that Petitioner Samira Yadegar will receive $250,000.00 of the settlement proceeds.  CONCLUSION  The  Court GRANTS Petitioner Samira Yadegar’s Revised Petition for Approval of  Compromise of Claim filed on January 19, 2024, as supplemented.   The net settlement funds will be distributed as set forth in Section 18a of the revised petition filed on January 19, 2024, as supplemented by Petitioner's June 4, 2024 Fourth Verified Supplement to the Revised Petition for Approval of Compromise of Claim filed on January 19, 2024. The Court grants the payment request and orders that Petitioner Samira Yadegar will receive $250,000.00 of the settlement proceeds.   Petitioner  is ordered to give notice of this ruling.  Petitioner  is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within  five days.
