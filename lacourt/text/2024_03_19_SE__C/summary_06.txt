And here is the English-text summary:

The court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Ani Khachikyan, et al. vs Platinum Transportation Group, Inc., Worldwide Transportation Group, Inc. (Case Number: 22STCV29276). The defendants have applied ex parte for an order appointing a discovery referee to monitor depositions and stay depositions until a referee is appointed. The court has granted the application in part, appointing a discovery referee for the limited purpose of monitoring depositions where antagonism between the parties may prolong the proceedings and frustrate discovery. The appointment is further limited to the depositions of defendants' principals and employees and defendant Miguel Pacheco. The court has denied the request to conduct depositions remotely and has stayed the depositions of the aforementioned individuals pending the selection of a discovery referee. The parties are required to stipulate to a referee or file and serve their respective nominees by March 21, 2024.
