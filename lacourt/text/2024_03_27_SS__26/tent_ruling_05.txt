 Case Number:  23STLC06273    Hearing Date:   March 27, 2024    Dept:  26   
KMC Landscaping Services, Inc. v. Age, et al.DEMURRER
(CCP § 430.10)
TENTATIVE RULING:
 
Defendants Future
Age Enterprises, Inc. dba Ace Realty and Cedric Age’s Demurrer to the Complaint is PLACED
OFF CALENDAR.
 
 
ANALYSIS:
 
Plaintiff KMC Landscaping, Inc. (“Plaintiff”), in propria
persona, filed the instant action against Defendants Future Age Enterprises, Inc. dba Ace Realty (erroneously sued as Ace
Realty) and Cedric Age (“Defendants”) on September 29, 2023. Defendants
filed the instant Demurrer to the Complaint on November 21, 2023. Defendants
filed a notice of non-opposition on December 26, 2023. The Demurrer initially
came for hearing on January 4, 2024, at which time the Court noted Plaintiff, a
corporation, improperly filed the action in propria persona, through a
non-lawyer. (Minute Order, 01/04/24.) The court continued the hearing on the
Demurrer and ordered Plaintiff to file a substitution of attorney demonstrating
it is represented by a licensed California attorney by March 6, 2024. (Ibid.)
The Court also set an order to show cause regarding striking Plaintiff’s Complaint as improper for March
27, 2024. (Ibid.) To date, Plaintiff has not filed a substitution of
attorney as ordered, nor filed any other papers. 
 
Therefore, Defendants
Future Age Enterprises, Inc. dba Ace
Realty and Cedric Age’s Demurrer to the Complaint is PLACED OFF CALENDAR. 
 
 
Court clerk to give notice.
