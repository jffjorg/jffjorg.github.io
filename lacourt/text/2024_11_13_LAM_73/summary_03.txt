The court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of PCAM, LLC v. SOCAL MAINTENANCE PRO X, LLC, granting the petition for release of property from mechanics lien. PCAM, LLC (Petitioner) had contracted with property owners to provide parking services and with Respondent to perform construction work on the properties. Respondent recorded a claim of lien against the properties, but failed to bring an action to enforce it within 90 days. Petitioner gave notice and demand letter to Respondent, but Respondent refused to release the mechanics lien. The court finds that Petitioner has complied with the requirements of Civil Code section 8484 and grants the petition, releasing the property from the mechanics lien and awarding Petitioner attorney fees and costs in the amount of $2,085.
