English-text summary:

This tentative ruling addresses a motion for summary judgment filed by the City of Los Angeles (Defendant) in a lawsuit brought by Miguel Mendoza (Plaintiff) who was struck by a vehicle while riding his bicycle on Plummer Street near Gloria Avenue. The Plaintiff alleges that the City of Los Angeles was negligent in maintaining a safe bikeway and that the accident was caused by the City's failure to provide adequate warning signs and striping. The City of Los Angeles argues that it is entitled to design immunity under Government Code section 830.6 and that the Plaintiff has failed to present triable issues of material fact. The Court finds in favor of the City of Los Angeles, granting its motion for summary judgment and dismissing the Plaintiff's claims.
