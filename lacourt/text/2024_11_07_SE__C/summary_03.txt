The Defendant has filed unopposed motions to compel responses to discovery requests from the Plaintiffs, GABRIEL HERRARTE, RAIDER MUNIZ, RAYLEENA MUNIZ, and FARIBA SANTOS. The Judge finds that the discovery requests were properly served on the Plaintiffs, and that the deadlines to respond have expired. Since no responses have been provided, the Judge grants the motions to compel and orders the Plaintiffs to provide verified responses and documents within 30 days.

The Judge also grants sanctions in the amount of $1,200.00 against the Plaintiffs and their counsel for failing to respond to the discovery requests. Additionally, the Judge grants a motion to compel the deposition of Plaintiff FARIBA SANTOS and orders her to appear for examination. Sanctions in the amount of $400.00 are also granted against Plaintiff FARIBA SANTOS and her counsel for failing to appear for the deposition.
