TENTATIVE  
  
The Court excuses the personal
appearance of Claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by
telephone or video conference call.   
  
The Court has reviewed the Petition to
Approve Compromise of Pending Action of ATARAH ROBINSON, 15.
The application is denied for the
following reasons: Plaintiff filed an expedited petition; however, the total
settlement in this case exceeds $50,000.00. Claimant is to receive $15,000.00
and petitioner is to receive $38,000.00. Claimant needs to file a petition for
minor compromise (MC-350).
 
Claimant is hereby ordered
to file a petition for minors comprise (MC-350) and other required documents no
less than 5 days prior to the new court date set below. Failure to do so may
result in sanctions. 
  
THE COURT SETS AN OSC RE MINOR
COMPROMISE ON April 12, 2024 IN DEPARTMENT 27 AT 8:30 AM. NO APPEARANCE
REQUIRED IF RECEIPT IS FILED IN ADVANCE. 
  
Moving party is ordered to give
notice.
