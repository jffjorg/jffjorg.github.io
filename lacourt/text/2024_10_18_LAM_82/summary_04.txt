The court has granted the plaintiff's application for a writ of attachment against the defendant, United Escrow Co., Inc. et al., in the amount of $3,300,000.00. The plaintiff, Pureland Hospitality LLC, is seeking to secure its claim for breach of contract against the defendant. The defendant had agreed to hold escrow funds in anticipation of a hotel purchase, but failed to refund the plaintiff's deposit despite a breach of contract. The court finds that the plaintiff has established probable validity of its claims and that the writ of attachment is not being sought for an improper purpose. The court also orders an undertaking of $10,000.
