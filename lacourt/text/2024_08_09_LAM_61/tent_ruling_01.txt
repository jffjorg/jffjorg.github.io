  Case Number:  23STCV00224    Hearing Date:   August 9, 2024    Dept:  61 BIANCA NICOLE BASILIO, et al. vs USA EXPRESS LEGAL & INVESTIGATIVE SERVICES, INC., et al.TENTATIVEThe present motions pertain to attorney Young K. Park, of the firm Justice for Workers, P.C., counsel for Plaintiffs Bianca Nicole Basilio, Lydia Fuller, and Isela Linares. The declaration of Park accompanying the motion states there has been a breakdown of the attorney-client relationship and a breach of the attorney-client agreement. The motions are accompanied by proofs of service upon the clients and other parties to this action. The proposed order lists the upcoming hearings in this action, but does not list the upcoming hearing on the motion to correct clerical error in a prior minute order, which was filed after the present motions. Trial in this matter is set to begin on May 6, 2025.Young K. Park’s Motions to be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiffs Bianca Nicole Basilio, Lydia Fuller, and Isela Linares are GRANTED, effective upon moving counsel filing with the court proof of service of a copy of the signed order — amended to reflect the hearing date on the upcoming motion to correct clerical error, now set for September 30, 2024 — on the clients, as set forth in Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1362(e).Moving Counsel to give notice.
