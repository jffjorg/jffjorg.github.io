 Case Number:  24STCV09841    Hearing Date:   April 24, 2024    Dept:  39 L&R Auto Parks, Inc. v. Ocean Blue Investments,
LLC, et al.Case No. 24STCV09841The court
issued the following order on April 23, 2024:
Plaintiff
L and R Auto Parks, Inc. (“Plaintiff”) filed this action against Ocean Blue
Investments, LLC and Illusion Holdings, LLC (collectively, “Defendants”) on
April 19, 2024, and immediately filed an ex parte application for a temporary
restraining order and order to show cause why a preliminary injunction should
not issue.  Plaintiff alleges that it holds a possessory interest in a
property and exclusive parking management rights under an agreement with
Defendants, and that Defendants unlawfully locked Plaintiff out of the Property
and hired a replacement manager.  Plaintiff seeks a temporary restraining
order and preliminary injunction to restore Plaintiff to its prior role.
 
The
court advances and continues the hearing on the ex parte application from
Wednesday, April 24, 2024, at 8:30 a.m., to Friday, April 26, 2024, at 8:30
a.m.  Any opposition shall be filed on or before Wednesday, April 24,
2024, at 4:00 p.m.  Defendants shall email a courtesy copy of any
opposition to the court on or before Wednesday, April 24, 2024, at 4:00
p.m.  The court’s email address is as follows: WRDept82@LACourt.org.
 
Plaintiff’s
counsel shall provide notice via email forthwith and shall file proof of
service with the court.
