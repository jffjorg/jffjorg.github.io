The tentative ruling is regarding a motion for an order that the truth of matters specified in Plaintiff's Request for Admissions be deemed admitted against Defendant Byoung Lee. The Plaintiff, National Commercial Recovery, Inc. d/b/a Blair Smith and Associates, filed a complaint on July 31, 2023, against various defendants, including Byoung Lee. The Plaintiff served requests for admission on December 6, 2023, but the Defendant failed to respond. The Plaintiff then filed a motion for an order deeming the requests for admission admitted and seeking monetary sanctions. The Court finds that the Defendant did not timely serve a response to the requests for admission and therefore grants the Plaintiff's motion. The Court orders that the matters in Plaintiff's Request for Admission of Facts for Defendant Byoung Don Lee; Set One be deemed admitted. The Court also awards the Plaintiff monetary sanctions in the amount of $610.00.
