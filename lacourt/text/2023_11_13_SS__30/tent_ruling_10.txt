 Case Number:  22STCV28124    Hearing Date:   November 13, 2023    Dept:  30 TRINIDAD ELIZALDE, et al. vs ALIDA PONTIFESMotion to Be Relieved as CounselTENTATIVEThe unopposed motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiffs, Trinidad Elizalde and Emma Castillo, is GRANTED, effective upon filing proof of service of the signed order on the clients. Counsel for Plaintiff’s counsel has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053), and has complied with CRC Rule 3.1362. Trial is set for February of 2024, so the clients have sufficient time to obtain new counsel, and thus will not be prejudiced by counsel’s withdrawal of representation. The Court delays entry of the order pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1362l(e). Counsesl will remain counsel of record until compliance which must be made within 5 days.  Moving party is ordered to give notice.
