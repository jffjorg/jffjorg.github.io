 Case Number:  23NWCV02824    Hearing Date:   August 15, 2024    Dept:  C Passport
Foods USA, LLC vs Passport Foods (SVC), LLC, et al., Case No.: 23NWCV02824
This is a fraud
action. Plaintiff Passport Foods USA, LLC moves ex parte for an order to set
aside the dismissal entered on August 9, 2024 after Plaintiff’s counsel failed
to appear for the case management conference scheduled for that same day. 
The ex parte application is DENIED.  In the alternative, the Court
specially sets a hearing for Plaintiff’s motion on Wednesday, October 16, 2024
at 9:30 a.m. in Department SE-C.  The ex parte application is not deemed
to be the moving papers.  Plaintiff has not attached a copy of the
proposed First Amended Complaint as required by CCP § 473, subd. (b).  The
moving papers, opposition and reply briefs to be filed in compliance with the
hearing date.  Moving party is advised that the Court typically requires
litigants to file noticed motions after first making a reservation on the
Court’s reservation system.  A party may then seek ex parte relief if
necessary.  Moving party to give notice.
