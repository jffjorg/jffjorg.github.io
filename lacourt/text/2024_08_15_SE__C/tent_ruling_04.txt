 Case Number:  22NWCV01461    Hearing Date:   August 15, 2024    Dept:  C Maria
Carmen Escalante vs Luz Elena Alvarez Vasquez, et al., Case No.: 22NWCV01461
This is an
action to quiet title.  Plaintiff Maria Carmen Escalante applies for ex
parte relief regarding the terms of a settlement agreement between the
parties.  On July 29, 2024, a mandatory settlement conference was held
before Judge Ann H. Park.  The court ordered Defense counsel to submit a
Stipulation for Settlement before August 29, 2024.  
A party may
seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable
harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)
The ex parte
application is DENIED.  Plaintiff fails to demonstrate irreparable harm or
immediate danger.  The matter remains on calendar for August 29, 2024 at
9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-P.  Moving party to give notice.
