Padilla vs Hermenegildo Espinoza Mendoza, et al., Case No.: 21NWCV00291
This is a
breach of contract action. On January 18, 2023, the Court stayed the instant
action pending institution and final determination of this action in the
alternative forum of Mexico.  Plaintiff Marcel P. Padilla now applies ex
parte for an order shortening time to allow the Court to hear Plaintiff’s
Renewed Motion to Terminate Stay of Action.  The ex parte application is
DENIED.  On March 20, 2024, the Court ordered Plaintiff to show cause on
September 11, 2024 why the action should not be dismissed for lack of
prosecution.  The March 20, 2024 order remains in effect, including the
briefing schedule set forth therein.  Moving party to give notice.
