Here is a brief summary in English text:

The tentative ruling addresses a demurrer to a cross-complaint filed by Octopus Restaurant Long Beach, Inc. against 200 Pine Avenue, LLC and Automac Parking, Inc. The cross-complaint seeks indemnity, contribution, and breach of contract. The demurrer argues that the cross-complaint fails to state a cause of action and that Octopus Restaurant Long Beach, Inc. is collaterally estopped from bringing a claim for equitable indemnity and contribution against 200 Pine Avenue, LLC. The court finds that the demurrer to the first cause of action is sustained with leave to amend, and the demurrer to the second cause of action is sustained with leave to amend. The court also grants Octopus Restaurant Long Beach, Inc.'s request for judicial notice of certain documents.
