Ruling: MOTION TO CONTINUE TRIAL IS DENIED
 
On July 12, 2024,
Plaintiff filed a motion to continue the trial for at least 120 days from the
then trial date of September 18, 2024. On July 15, 2024, Plaintiff submitted an
ex parte application to continue trial, making the same argument and request
as in the present motion. The Court granted the ex parte application and
continued the FSC and trial date by 120 days to the current trial date of
January 16, 2025. Thus, the present motion is moot and therefore denied.
