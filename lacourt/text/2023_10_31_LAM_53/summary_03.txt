The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in a case brought by Shai Harris, as successor trustee of the H. McClenon Trust and as trustee of the ADH Harris Trust, against Teresa Gorman Martinez, Ruben Martinez, Lil Wave Financial Inc., Superior Loan Servicing, and Funding Rush Inc. The Plaintiffs allege that the Defendants administered an unconscionable contract or clause under Uniform Commercial Code section 2-302, but the Judge has sustained the Defendants' demurrer to the complaint, finding that the allegations do not state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action. The Judge has also dismissed the Defendants with prejudice from the case.
