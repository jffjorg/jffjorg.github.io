 Case Number:  22STCV04240    Hearing Date:   January 6, 2025    Dept:  B Plaintiff Arturo Sandoval’s Request for Default JudgmentDefendant: Michael Scott Hurtado GomezTENTATIVE RULINGPlaintiff Arturo Sandoval’s Request for Default Judgment against Defendant Michael Scott Hurtado Gomez is GRANTED.BACKGROUNDThis action arises from a motor vehicle accident. On February 3, 2022, Plaintiff Arturo Sandoval (“Plaintiff”) filed a complaint against Defendants Michael Scott Hurtado Gomez (“Gomez”), Jesus Edgar Salazar (“Salazar”), and Does 1 to 15 (collectively “Defendants”), alleging two causes of action: (1) motor vehicle negligence; and (2) general negligence.On June 6, 2024, the case was reassigned to the Honorable Karen Moskowitz in Department B at Van Nuys Courthouse East, effective June 24, 2024.On January 2, 2025, Plaintiffs filed the instant request for default judgment.LEGAL STANDARDCal. Code of Civil Procedure § 585 permits entry of a default judgment after a party has failed to timely respond or appear. (CCP § 585.) A party seeking judgment on the default by the court must file a Request for Court Judgment, and: (1) a brief summary of the case; (2) declarations or other admissible evidence in support of the judgment requested; (3) interest computations as necessary; (4) a memorandum of costs and disbursements; (5) declaration of nonmilitary status; (6) a proposed form of judgment; (7) a dismissal of all parties against whom judgment is not sought or an application for separate judgment under CCP § 579, supported by a showing of grounds for each judgment; (8) exhibits as necessary; and (9) a request for attorney’s fees if allowed by statute or by the agreement of the parties. (Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1800.)ANALYSISPlaintiff seeks damages in the total amount of $680,560.82, representing $180,060.82 in special damages, $500,000.00 in general damages, and $500.00 in costs.Plaintiff’s request for default judgment against Defendant Michael Scott Hurtado Gomez isdenied for the following deficiencies:Defendant has failed to timely respond or appear. Plaintiff’s default packet meets all statutory requirements under CCP § 585. Therefore, Plaintiff Arturo Sandoval’s request for default judgment against Defendant Michael Scott Hurtado Gomez is GRANTED
