A MINOR, BY AND THROUGH HIS GUARDIAN AD LITEM, CRISTYN MARTIN vs THE HELP GROUP WEST, et al.TENTATIVEThe Court excuses the personal appearance of the claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.On November 29, 2023, the Court reviewed the Petition to Approve Compromise of Disputed Claim of a Minor (L.W., Age 13), and held certain trust issues precluded the granting of the petition.Now, Plaintiff has filed a supplemental declaration stating that the CPT trust documents submitted to the Court in the Amended Petition for Minors Compromise filed on November 6, 2023 addresses each of the issues raised in the Court’s tentative ruling dated November 29, 2023. However, there is no amended petition filed on November 6, 2023. The only amended petition was filed on October 27, 2023. In addition, the Court identified issues with the proposed order, and Plaintiff has not filed a corrected proposed order.Plaintiff is ordered to file the amended petition with the corrections and a proposed order in accordance with the Court’s 11/29/2023 ruling. Accordingly, the petition is CONTINUED for 60 days in Department 30 at Spring Street.  Judicial Assistant to calendar.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
