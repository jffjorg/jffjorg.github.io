Tracy v. Treat, Case no. 24SMCV00547
Hearing date October 15, 2024
Plaintiff’s
Counsel’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel
Plaintiff
sues for damages arising out of a motor vehicle accident. Shaun J. Bauman,
plaintiff’s counsel, seeks to be relieved as counsel, citing communication
issues (see Bauman decl, para 2). 
An
application to be relieved must be made on judicial counsel form MC-051 (Notice
of Motion and Motion) per Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(a), MC-052
(Declaration) per CRC rule 3.1362(c), and MC-053 (Proposed Order) per CRC rule
3.1362(e).¿ The proposed order must specify all hearing dates scheduled in the
action or proceeding, including the date of trial, if known. CRC rule
3.1362(e). Plaintiff’s counsel has met all obligations under Cal. Rules of
Court 3.1362. 
Trial
is set for 2/18/25, so plaintiff has ample opportunity to find new counsel, so
there is no prejudice to plaintiff via granting this motion. GRANTED.
