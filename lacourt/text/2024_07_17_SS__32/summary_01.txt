The tentative ruling grants the motion to substitute Lourdes Cabriales, Fermin Javier Cabriales, Jesus Jose Cabriales, Jose Federico Cabriales, Maria Zoila Diaz Cabriales, and Hector Hugo Cabriales as successors in interest to Maria Cabriales, deceased. The motion is based on California Code of Civil Procedure Section 377.31, which allows the decedent's personal representative or successor in interest to continue a decedent's pending action. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's children have filed declarations under penalty of perjury stating that they are the Plaintiff's only children and successor in interest to the Plaintiff's interest in this action. Therefore, the motion to substitute is granted.
