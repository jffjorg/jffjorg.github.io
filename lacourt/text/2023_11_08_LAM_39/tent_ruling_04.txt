Motions to be
Relieved
 
            Counsel for
Defendants Advanced Surgical Institute, APC, and Mohamad Afraciab moves to be
relieved.  The Court orders as follows:
 
            1.         Good cause having been shown, the
motions are granted on the condition that counsel complies with this order.
            
            2.         The Court issues an Order to Show Cause
why the Court should not strike the answer of Advanced Surgical Institute, APC,
because this defendant cannot appear without counsel.  The OSC hearing shall be held on January 23,
2024, at 8:30 a.m. at the following location:
 
            Stanley
Mosk Courthouse
            111 North
Hill Street
            Department
#39 (Goorvitch, J.)
            Los
Angeles, California 90012
 
The Court provides notice: If this defendant does not have
counsel by the upcoming hearing date, or if a prospective attorney does not
specially appear at the hearing, absent good cause, the Court will strike the
answer of this defendant, which would permit Plaintiff to seek entry of default
and default judgment.
 
            3.         The Court orders counsel to serve a
copy of BOTH this minute order AND the Court’s signed order on Form MC-053 upon
the clients.  
 
            4.         The Court orders counsel to file a
proof of service making clear that BOTH this minute order AND the Court’s
signed order on Form MC-053 were served upon the clients.  
 
            5.         Once counsel complies with this order,
she shall be automatically relieved without further order of the Court.
 
            6.         Counsel shall provide notice and file
proof of such with the Court.
