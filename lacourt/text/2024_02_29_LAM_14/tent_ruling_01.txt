  Case Number:  21STCV40537    Hearing Date:   February 29, 2024    Dept:  14 Mourad vs TeslaCase Background
 
Lemon law involving Plaintiff’s 2018
Tesla Model 3.
 
On November 3, 2021, Plaintiff filed
his Complaint for (1) Breach of Implied Warranty – Song-Beverly; (2) Breach of Express
Warranty – Song-Beverly; (3) Breach of Express Warranty – Magnuson-Moss; (4)
Breach of Implied Warranty – Magnuson-Moss; and (5) Negligence against
Defendants Tesla Inc. fka Tesla Motors Inc. (“Tesla”) and DOES 1-100. 
 
On May 19, 2022, Defendant Tesla
filed its Answer.
 
On July 12, 2023, this court
construed Defendant Tesla’s Motion for Summary Judgment as a motion for
judgment on the pleadings and granted it, with leave to amend.
 
On July 31, 2023, Plaintiff filed
his First Amended Complaint for (1) Breach of Express Warranty – Commercial Code,
(2) Negligence, (3) Breach of Warranty – Magnuson-Moss, (4) Breach of Implied
Warranty – Song-Beverly, and (5) Breach of Express Warranty – Song-Beverly
against Defendants Tesla and DOES 1-100.
 
On August 30, 2023, Defendant Tesla
filed its Answer.
 
Jury Trial is currently set for May
6, 2024.
 
Instant Motions
 
Defendant Tesla now moves this
court for an order compelling Plaintiff to (1) sit for deposition, and (2)
produce the vehicle for inspection.
 
Decision
 
The motions are GRANTED. Plaintiff
is to sit for deposition on or before March 29, 2024. Plaintiff is to produce
the vehicle for inspection on or before April 12, 2024.
 
The motions are unopposed. The lack
of an opposition operates as a
concession that the motion has merit. See California Rules of Court Rule
3.1113(a); see also Rule 3.1320(f); Herzberg v. County of Plumas (2005)
133 Cal.App.4th 1, 20.
