And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge grants in part the motion to compel Defendant Rolls-Royce Motor Cars of Beverly Hills to provide verified responses to Plaintiff's Form Interrogatories, Set One, and Special Interrogatories, Set One. The judge finds that Defendant has waived any rights to exercise the option to produce writings under Section 2030.230 and any objections to the interrogatories. The judge also grants in part the request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,316.75, which includes 2.5 hours of attorney time and $66.75 in motion filing fees. The judge orders Defendant Rolls-Royce Motor Cars of Beverly Hills to provide verified responses to the discovery requests within 20 days of receipt of the order and to pay the awarded sanctions.
