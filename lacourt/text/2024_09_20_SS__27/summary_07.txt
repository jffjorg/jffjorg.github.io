This is a tentative ruling by Judge Lee S. Arian in the case of Edwin Zepeda vs. City of Los Angeles, et al. (Case No. 23STCV13167). The ruling concerns a motion to deem admitted and requests for sanctions. The defendant, County of Los Angeles, moved the court to deem the contents of its Requests for Admissions, Set One, admitted after the plaintiff, Edwin Zepeda, failed to comply with a court order to provide further responses. The court granted the motion, deeming the contents of the Requests for Admissions admitted. The court also ordered sanctions in the amount of $875 against the plaintiff, to be paid to the defendant within 30 days. The court found that the plaintiff did not act with substantial justification and that the sanctions were reasonable.
