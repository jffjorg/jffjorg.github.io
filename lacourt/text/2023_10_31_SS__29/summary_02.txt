Here is a summary in English text:

This is a tentative ruling by a judge in a premises liability case arising from a train incident in Griffith Park, Los Angeles. The plaintiff, Malvina Mkhchyan, Ovsanna Mkhchian, and Adam Bersigian, filed a complaint against the City of Los Angeles, Travel Town Transportation Museum, and City of Los Angeles Department of Parks and Recreation. The defendant, City of Los Angeles, filed a motion to compel the plaintiff to respond to special interrogatories and a request for sanctions. The plaintiff failed to respond to the interrogatories and did not file an opposition to the motion. The court granted the motion to compel and awarded sanctions in the amount of $525 against the plaintiff and their counsel, K & L Associates. The court ordered the plaintiff to provide written responses to the interrogatories within 21 days and to pay the sanctions within the same period.
