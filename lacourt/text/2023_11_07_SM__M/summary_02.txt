And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has considered the motions to seal records and dismiss/stay the action brought by Irwin Naturals, Inc. The Judge finds that the motion to seal is granted, as the records contain confidential, proprietary, and highly sensitive business information, and the public has an interest in protecting the private, sensitive, and/or medical information of non-party consumers.

Regarding the motion to dismiss/stay, the Judge finds that California is not a seriously inconvenient forum. Although there are some private factors that weigh in favor of Colorado, such as the site of injury and the location of witnesses, the public factors do not support a transfer. California has a clear interest in regulating the product, which was designed, manufactured, and supplied in California, and the local community may be rightfully concerned about this action. The Judge denies the motion to dismiss/stay.
