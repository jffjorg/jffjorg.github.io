 Case Number:  23NWCV00135    Hearing Date:   December 4, 2024    Dept:  SEC Thomas v. BV Lakewood Carson SE LLC 23NWCV00135 This wrongful eviction action was filed on January 13, 2023. Plaintiff moves ex parte to request Permission to Electronically File Motions and Accommodations. Plaintiff states her status is “disabled” under the Social Security Administration. Plaintiff contends she faces significant challenges in physically filing court documents and participating courtroom proceedings due to her disability. Plaintiff’s request for Permission to Electronically File Motions is GRANTED pursuant to California Rules of Court, rule 1.100 which governs requests for accommodations by persons with disabilities. Plaintiff may electronically file motions and court documents. Plaintiff’s request for Accommodations is DENIED. The Court requires additional information regarding Plaintiff’s request for “additional communication assistance.” (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 1.100(c)(2) (“[r]equests for accommodations must include a description of the accommodation sought along with a statement of the medical condition that necessitates the accommodation.”).)
