TENTATIVE ORDER
 
Defendant Rosario Pasillas’s demurrer to the first
amended complaint is MOOT.
 
Moving party to give notice. 
 
Defendant Rosario Pasillas demurs to Plaintiff Jose
Villalobos’s first amended complaint.
 
Discussion
 
This is an unlawful detainer action. On May 22, 2024, Jose
Villalobos (Plaintiff) filed a complaint against Rosario Pasillas (Defendant) for
unlawful detainer. Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint on June 20, 2024,
reclassifying the case to a limited jurisdiction case. On July 3, 2024, Defendant
filed a demurrer to Plaintiff’s first amended complaint.
 
During the case management conference on November 26, 2024, pursuant
to oral stipulation, the Court advanced and vacated Defendant’s demurrer.
Accordingly, Defendant’s demurrer to the first amended complaint is MOOT.
