 Case Number:  21STCV12948    Hearing Date:   March 15, 2024    Dept:  30 TINNA JONSDOTTIR vs HONGSHI SUN, et al.TENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Defendant, Hongshi Sun, is DENIED without prejudice. On January 25, 2024, the Court noted that counsel for Defendant has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). However, the proof of service does not indicate that the papers have been served on the client. (See CRC Rule 3.1362(d).) The motion was continued to allow counsel to correct the defect, which counsel has not.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
