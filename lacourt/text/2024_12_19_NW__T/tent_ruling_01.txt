  Case Number:  23VECV03786    Hearing Date:   December 19, 2024    Dept:  T   23VECV03786 Sweet Streets v S/R Laboratories Tentative ruling:  While empathetic to the medical issues of counsel, the court is unwilling to continue the trial for 8 months without a guarantee that counsel will be able to handle the case.  Therefore, the court will continue the trial for 90 days and then will obtain more up to date information about counsel's availability.
