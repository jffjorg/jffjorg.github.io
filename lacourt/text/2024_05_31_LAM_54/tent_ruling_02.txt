PETITIONER TO NOTICE.
 
If the parties wish to submit on the
tentative, please email the courtroom at SMCdept54@lacourt.org with notice to opposing counsel

The Court considers the moving
papers.  No opposition has been
received.  
 
Any party to an arbitration in which an award has been made may petition
the court to confirm, correct or vacate the award.  (CCP § 1285.) 
Petitioner has complied with CCP § 1285.4 as it has set forth the name
of the arbitrator (Hon. Rosalyn Chapman (Ret.)), attached documents evidencing
an agreement to arbitrate, and attached a copy of the arbitration award.
Respondents were served with the petition by personal service.
 
No response to the petition has been
filed by Respondents.  (See CCP §
1290.6 [“A response shall be served and filed within 10 days after service of
the petition”].)  As Respondents have not
filed a timely response, the allegations of the petition are deemed admitted by
Respondents.  (CCP § 1290.) The petition
is GRANTED.
