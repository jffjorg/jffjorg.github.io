 Case Number:  22NWCV00987    Hearing Date:   June 18, 2024    Dept:  C Woolsey,
et al. v. Security Paving Company, Inc., Case No. 22NWCV00987
 
This
is a products liability case that resulted in a death. Defendants Security
Paving Company, Inc.; Recycled Materials, Inc.; and Sully Miller Contracting
Company move ex parte to advance defendants’ motion to sever special defenses
and trifurcation of trial. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Defendants
filed the motion to be heard on March 18, 2025, which was the earliest
available reservation date. Trial is currently set for July 24, 2024.  The motion shall be heard by the trial
court.  
 
Accordingly,
the Court grants the ex parte motion. The motion to sever special defenses and
trifurcation of trial is ADVANCED to June 18, 2024, and CONTINUED to Tuesday,
July 24, 2024, at 9:30 A.M. in Dept. SE-F. 
 
Moving
party to give notice.
