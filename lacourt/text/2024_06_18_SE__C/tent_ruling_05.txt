 Case Number:  22STCV14356    Hearing Date:   June 18, 2024    Dept:  C Sanchez,
et al. v. Life Care Centers of America, Inc., Case No. 22STCV14356
 
This
is an elder abuse case against a convalescent and rehabilitation center.
Plaintiffs move ex parte to amend the complaint. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Plaintiffs
argue that plaintiff Gary Sanchez passed away on October 1, 2023, and his
daughter, Alexandra Rae Sanchez survives him. Plaintiffs seek to substitute
Alexandra Rae Sanchez as Gary Sanchez’s successor in interest. The parties have
stipulated to the fact. 
 
Based
on the above, the motion is GRANTED. Plaintiffs are to file the second amended
complaint within five court days.
