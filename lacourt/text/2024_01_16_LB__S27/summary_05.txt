The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer to the Complaint filed by the Defendant, Long Beach Healthcare Center, LLC, et al. against the Plaintiffs, Magnolia Lopez, et al. The Plaintiffs are seeking damages for elder abuse, professional negligence, wrongful death, NIED, and violation of patients' rights arising from the care and treatment of the Decedent, Jose Perez. The Defendant has filed a demurrer seeking dismissal of the entire complaint, but failed to meet and confer with the Plaintiffs prior to filing the demurrer. The Judge is inclined to continue the hearing on the demurrer to require Counsel to meaningfully meet and confer in connection with each issue presented by way of the demurrer and motion to strike. The parties are also reminded of a Case Management Conference (CMC) scheduled concurrently with the hearing on the demurrer and motion to strike.
