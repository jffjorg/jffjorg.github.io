Defendants’
demurrer is MOOT. Defendants’ motion to strike is MOOT. 
 
            On
11/18/2024, Plaintiff Obadiah Swaffer (Plaintiff) filed suit against Biomat
USA, Inc., Grifols USA, Inc., Grifols USA, LLC, and Grifols Worldwide
Operations USA (collectively, Defendants), alleging: (1) negligence; (2)
negligent misrepresentation; (3) defamation; (4) tortious interference; (5)
breach of contract; (6) violation of privacy rights; (7) declaratory judgment;
(8) violation of Business and Professions Code section 17200.
 
            On
12/18/2024, Defendants demurred to Plaintiff’s first, second, fourth, fifth,
sixth, seventh, and eighth causes of action. 
Defendants also moved to strike portions of Plaintiff’s Complaint. 
 
            On
1/10/2025, Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint (FAC) lieu of an
opposition. Accordingly, Defendants’ demurrer is moot, as is the motion to
strike. 
 
It is so ordered.
