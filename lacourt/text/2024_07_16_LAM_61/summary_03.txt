The tentative ruling grants Plaintiff Yoshitaka Takeuchi's motion to approve a PAGA settlement with Defendant Select 7, LLC. The settlement provides for a gross payment of $270,000, with $90,000 going to Plaintiff's counsel as attorney fees, and the remaining amount to be paid in penalties to the Labor Workforce Development Agency (LWDA) and aggrieved employees. The court finds the settlement to be fair, reasonable, and adequate in view of PAGA's purposes to remediate labor law violations and deter future ones. The court also finds the attorney fee award to be reasonable in light of a proposed lodestar calculation presented by Plaintiff's counsel.
