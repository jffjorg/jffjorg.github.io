The Court considers the moving papers.
No opposition has been received. 
 
 When timely responses to interrogatories are
not received, “[t]he party propounding the interrogatories may move for an
order compelling response to the interrogatories.”  (CCP § 2030.290(b).) 
 
Defendant moves to compel verifications
to Plaintiff’s responses to SIs, sets one and two. Plaintiff served unverified
responses to the subject discovery on February 13, 2023, April 19, 2023, and
May 15, 2023. Defendant requested that Plaintiff serve verifications, but
Plaintiff has failed to do so. Plaintiff has not opposed this motion to show
verifications have been served. Defendant’s motion is GRANTED.
 
Defendant requests sanctions but does
not state the amount of sanctions requested in the notice of motion or points
and authorities. The Court cannot grant this request.
