Discussion
Aragon and BTS move the court for an order consolidating
Case Nos. 22STCV02707, 22STCV08818, 22STCV10726, 22STCV12485, 22STCV12608,
22STCV12787, 22STCV13620, 22STCV15084 and 22STCV16434 for all purposes,
including trial.
The motion is denied without prejudice, based upon
the notice deficiency set forth in the above footnote.
Any re-filing of the instant motion must comply with
California Rules of Court rule 3.350, subdivision (a)(1)(C).
