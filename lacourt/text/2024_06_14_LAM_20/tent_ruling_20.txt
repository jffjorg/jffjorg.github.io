Plaintiffs Ronald Courey and Beverley Courey seek to compel Defendants Spinning Gold, LLC, Timothy Bogart, and Jessica Martins to provide initial responses to Plaintiffs’ requests for production, set one, form interrogatories, set one, special interrogatories, set one, and requests for admission, set one.Because responses were apparently not provided, the Motions are granted.  Responses, without objections, are to be served within 30 days.Plaintiffs seek $1,500 in sanctions for each Motion.  The Court awards reduced sanctions in the amount of $500 per Motion.  Moving parties to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
