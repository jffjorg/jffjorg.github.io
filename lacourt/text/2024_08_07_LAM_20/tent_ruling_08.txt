Plaintiff Haya Nemtzov seeks to strike the Answer of Defendant ACTCA, A Member of the Alliance, Inc. because this entity does not have legal counsel. ACTCA has not had counsel since December 2023.Because a corporate entity cannot represent itself, the Motion to Strike is granted.  The Court enters a default for ACTCA.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
