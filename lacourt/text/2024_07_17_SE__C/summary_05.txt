The judge has granted the plaintiffs' motion to compel further responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One, in part. The judge ordered the defendant to comply with the requirements of Code of Civil Procedure, Section 2031.280, which requires that documents produced in response to a demand for inspection be identified with the specific request number to which the documents respond.
