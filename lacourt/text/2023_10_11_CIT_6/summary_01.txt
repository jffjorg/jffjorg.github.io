Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Bin Xu, et al. v. TMD Shops LLC, et al. The Court sustains the demurrer to the First and Second Causes of Action with leave to amend, and overrules the demurrer in all other respects. The First Cause of Action for conversion is dismissed due to insufficient pleading, and the Second Cause of Action for breach of fiduciary duty is dismissed due to lack of standing. The Court also sustains the demurrer to the Fourth Cause of Action for member's right of inspection and accounting, but overrules the demurrer to the Seventh Cause of Action for common law trademark infringement. The Court orders plaintiffs to give notice of the ruling within five days.
