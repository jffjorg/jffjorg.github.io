 Case Number:  20STCV45139    Hearing Date:   January 4, 2024    Dept:  76             Plaintiff
alleges that he suffered retaliation for voicing safety concerns at various
worksites involving the operation of heavy machinery.  
            Defendant
City of Los Angeles doing business by and through its Department of Water and
Power moves to compel responses to requests for production of documents, set
two and requests sanctions.
TENTATIVE RULING
Defendant City of Los Angeles
doing business by and through its Department of Water and Power’s motion to
compel responses to Requests For Production, Set Two  is MOOT.
After this motion as filed, Plaintiff
served verified responses on December 19, 2023. If Defendant deems these responses
insufficient, Defendant may bring a motion to compel further responses.
Defendant’s request for sanctions
against Plaintiff and his counsel, jointly and severally is GRANTED[1] in
the reduced amount of $760 (2 hours at $350/hour plus $60 filing fee).
Sanctions are to be paid to Defendant’s counsel within 20 days.
