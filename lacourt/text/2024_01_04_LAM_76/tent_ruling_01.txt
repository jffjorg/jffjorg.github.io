  Case Number:  19STCV39673    Hearing Date:   January 4, 2024    Dept:  76             Plaintiff alleges that Defendants
committed legal malpractice in connection with a disciplinary matter pending
before the State Bar of California.
Petitioners/Defendants Century Law
Group LLP, Edward O. Lear, and Rizza Gonzales (erroneously sued as Rezza
Gonzales) move for an order confirming the arbitration award.
TENTATIVE RULING
Petitioners/Defendants served Plaintiff/Respondent in pro per via email
only. However, Plaintiff’s counsel passed away and there is no indication that
Plaintiff agreed to accept service by email. A pro per is not required to
accept electronic service. (Civ. Proc. Code
§ 1010.6(f)(2).)
The hearing on Petitioners/Defendants Century Law Group LLP, Edward
Lear, and Rizza Gonzalez’s motion to confirm arbitration award is CONTINUED to February
9, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. Petitioners/Defendants are to serve the petition and
notice of this continuance by proper means.
