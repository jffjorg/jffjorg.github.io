And here is a summary in English text:

This tentative ruling grants a motion for attorney's fees and costs filed by Plaintiff Harutyun Shatarevyan in a lemon law action against FCA US, LLC (FCA) and Does 1 through 30. The Plaintiff leased a 2020 Jeep Wrangler and sued FCA for breach of warranties and lemon law violations. The Court granted the motion for attorney's fees in the amount of $79,521 and costs in the amount of $5,449.10. The fees were calculated using the lodestar method and a 1.2 multiplier enhancement due to the contingency nature of the representation. The Court also found the costs to be reasonable and granted the motion for costs.
