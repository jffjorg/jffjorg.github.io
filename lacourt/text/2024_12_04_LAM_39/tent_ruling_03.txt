


[1]Moreover,
pursuant to Code of Civil Procedure section 1987, subdivisions (b) and (c), a
party can serve on another party a notice to appear in lieu of a subpoena at least
20 days before the time required for attendance at trial that includes a
request for that party to bring books, documents, and other information to the
trial.  Therefore, section 3295,
subdivision (c) and Code of Civil Procedure section 1987 provide appropriate mechanisms
for a party to obtain the information necessary to establish a defendant’s
profits and financial condition to support a claim for punitive damages.
