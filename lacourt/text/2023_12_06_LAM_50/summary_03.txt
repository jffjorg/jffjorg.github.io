This tentative ruling addresses three motions filed by the Plaintiff, IMMIGRANT RIGHTS DEFENSE COUNCIL, LLC: (1) a motion to deem admissions admitted and request for sanctions, (2) a motion to compel response to request for production of documents, and (3) a motion to compel response to form interrogatories. The Plaintiff seeks monetary sanctions against the Defendant, ANGELICA CENDEJAS, et al., for their failure to respond to the requests. The Court has decided to continue the motions to a new date and orders the parties to participate in an Informal Discovery Conference (IDC) to resolve their discovery disputes. The Plaintiff must make a prompt reservation for the IDC using the Court's online reservation system and file a one-page IDC form in the department seven days prior to the conference. The parties are ordered to meet and confer in person or via telephone to resolve their discovery disputes before the IDC date.
