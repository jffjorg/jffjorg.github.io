 Case Number:  22NWCV00532    Hearing Date:   May 22, 2024    Dept:  C T. M. vs
Whittier City School District, Case No. 22NWCV00532
This is a
sexual misconduct case. There are two separate ex partes filed before the
Court. First, Defendant Whitter City School District filed an ex parte
applicationto advance the hearing date on its Motion for Stay of Proceedings.
Secondly, Plaintiff T.M. filed an ex parte application for an order to advance
the hearing date on Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Further Response. 
A party may
seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable
harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)
Defendant
contends the first available hearing date on the Defendant District's Motion is
February 4, 2025, over five months after the September 16, 2024 trial date. 
 As to
Plaintiff’s ex parte application, Plaintiff contends Plaintiff filed the
Motions on May 15, 2024, and at that time, the earliest available hearing dates
February 6, 2025, and February 26, 2025. 

Both ex parte
applications are GRANTED in part.  Defendant
District’s Motion to Stay Proceedings is advanced from Tuesday, February 4,
2025 to this date and CONTINUED to Tuesday, August 27, 2024 in Department SE-C
at 9:30 a.m. Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Further Discovery is advanced from
Thursday, February 6, 2025 and February 25, 2025 to this date and CONTINUED to
September 5, 2024 in Department SE-C at 10:30 a.m.  The parties are not foreclosed from seeking a
trial continuance in Dept. SE-F.
Defendant
District to give notice.
