#6
TENTATIVE
RULING
 
Defendant
American Honda Motor Company, Inc.’s motion to compel the depositions of Plaintiffs
is MOOT. 
 
Moving
Party to give NOTICE.
 
 
This is a Song-Beverly action. Defendant American
Honda Motor Company, Inc. moves to compel the depositions of Plaintiffs Maria Avina
Jacobo and Alfredo Lopez Gomez. 
 
Discussion
 
According to the
opposing and reply papers, Defendant deposed Plaintiff Maria Avina Jacobo on
April 30, 2024, and started deposing Plaintiff Alfredo Lopez Gomez on May 7,
2024. The parties have agreed to continue Gomez’s deposition on June 18, 2024.  Because the depositions have already begun,
the motion to compel is MOOT.
