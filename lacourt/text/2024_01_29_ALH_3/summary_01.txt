This tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel answers to form interrogatories and a request for sanctions in a lawsuit filed by Ray Gomez against Benjamin Pena arising from a motor vehicle collision. The defendant served form interrogatories on the plaintiff, but no responses were received. The defendant filed a motion to compel responses and requested sanctions against the plaintiff. The plaintiff did not oppose the motion, and the court granted the motion, ordering the plaintiff to serve verified responses without objections to the defendant's form interrogatories within 20 days. The court also granted the defendant's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $235, which includes 1 hour of defense counsel's time and $60 in filing fees, to be paid within 20 days.
