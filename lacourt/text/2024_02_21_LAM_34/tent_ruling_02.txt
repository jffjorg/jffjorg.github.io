 Case Number:  23STCV08986    Hearing Date:   February 21, 2024    Dept:  34 SUBJECT:        Motion to
Compel Further Responses to Plaintiff’s Request for Production of Documents,
Set One
 
Moving Party: Plaintiff
Reyad Samir Alzir
Resp. Party:    None 
 
 
The Motion is TAKEN OFF CALENDAR.
 
BACKGROUND:
 
On April 24,
2023, Plaintiff Reyad Samir Alzir filed a Complaint against Defendant FCA US
LLC on causes of action for violations of the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty
Act. 
 
On May 25,
2023, Defendant filed its Answer to the Complaint. 
 
On November
29, 2023, Plaintiff filed Motion to Compel Further Responses to Plaintiff’s
Request for Production of Documents, Set One. In support of the Motion,
Plaintiff concurrently filed: (1) Declaration of Noreelie Panhwar; (2) Separate
Statement; and (3) Proposed Order. 
 
No informal
discovery conference was held on the Motion. 
 
On February
16, 2024, Plaintiff filed Judicial Council Form CM-200, Notice of Settlement of
Entire Case. 
 
ANALYSIS:
 
I.         
Discussion
 
This Court’s Trial Orders require that Counsel attend
an informal discovery conference before any discovery motion will be heard in a
Lemon Law case. (See Court’s Trial Orders, § VIII.) That has not occurred
regarding this Motion. 
 
 
II.      
Conclusion 
 
The Motion is TAKEN OFF CALENDAR.
