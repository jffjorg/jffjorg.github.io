The judge has denied the motion of the defendants, Pomona Community Health Center dba Parktree Community Health and Ranjit Theofile Shenoy, M.D., to stay the proceedings or to extend the time to file a response. The motion was unopposed by the plaintiff, Armen Sarafian, who is a self-represented litigant. The court found that the motion was not properly served on the plaintiff, as it was served electronically despite the plaintiff not having consented to receiving documents electronically. Additionally, the motion contained the wrong address for the court. The court denied the motion without prejudice, meaning the defendants can refile the motion if they can provide proper service and address the other issues. The defendants are ordered to notify the court of the ruling within five days.
