The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motions to compel discovery responses and requests for sanctions filed by the defendants, Aiten Levy and Ingeborg Levy. The defendants had requested that the plaintiff, 6824 Ben Avenue, LLC, provide responses to their discovery requests, which included form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production of documents. The defendants also requested that the requests for admissions be deemed admitted.

The judge granted the defendants' motions to compel responses to the discovery requests, ordering the plaintiff to provide verified responses without objections within 20 days of notice of the order. The judge also granted the defendants' motion for an order deeming the requests for admissions admitted.

Additionally, the judge granted the defendants' requests for sanctions against the plaintiff and its counsel, ordering them to pay a total of $1,820 in sanctions. The judge ordered the plaintiff and its counsel to pay the sanctions within 20 days of notice of the order.
