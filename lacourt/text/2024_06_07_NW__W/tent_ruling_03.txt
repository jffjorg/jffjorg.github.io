FACTUAL
BACKGROUND
 
On September 5, 2023, Plaintiffs Ralph Hitchcock, Jeanie Cootz,
and Robert Duecker filed a complaint against Francisco Relles for motor vehicle
negligence, and general negligence. 
 
TENTATIVE
RULING: GRANT
 
Counsel contends they have reached out to Plaintiff Robert Duecker
and Jeanie Cootz on several occasions, including attempting to contact clients
via an address produced by skip trace. They have been unable to locate them or
speak with them. As for Plaintiff Hitchcock, Counsel spoke with Cootz who
advised Counsel that Plaintiff Hitchcock was in hospice and later became known
to Counsel that Plaintiff Hitchcock passed away. Mr. Hitchcock died intestate
with no assets or heirs. 
 
Counsel has complied with the requirements of CRC Rule 3.1362 by
filing forms MC-051 and MC-052 and lodging with the court a copy of the
proposed order on form MC-053. 
 
Counsel has been unable to confirm any of the client’s addresses.   
 
Trial is not yet set. The next hearing is a Trial Setting
Conference for October 2, 2024.
