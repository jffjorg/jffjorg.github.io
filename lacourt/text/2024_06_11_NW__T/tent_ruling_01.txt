Introduction 
Plaintiff State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company
(Plaintiff) moved to deem requests for admissions (RFAs) admitted against
Defendant Mariana Diaz-Zamora (Defendant). 
Plaintiff
further requested monetary sanctions of $460.00 against
Defendant.  Defendant did not file an
opposition.  
Discussion 
 A party that has not
received timely responses to RFAs is entitled to an order deeming the matters
specified in the requests as admitted. 
(Code Civ. Proc., sec. 2033.280(b).) 
Plaintiff provided that RFAs were served upon Defendant.  Defendant did not serve responses to the
RFAs.  (Tristan Espinosa Decl., pars. 1-3.)  The motion is persuasive.  
Sanctions under section Code of Civil Procedure section
2033.280(c) are mandatory.  Plaintiff’s
request for a total of $460.00 against Defendant is reasonable.  The requested sanctions is merited.
The motion to deem RFAs admitted and for monetary sanctions
is GRANTED.  
IT IS
SO ORDERED, MOVING PARTY TO GIVE VERBATIM NOTICE.
