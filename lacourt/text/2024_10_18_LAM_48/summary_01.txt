The Court has tentatively granted the motion for determination of good faith settlement submitted by Defendant County of Los Angeles. The settlement was reached on September 19, 2024, and provides for Defendant to pay Plaintiffs $550,000.00, which is 57% of the total known recoverable damages available to Plaintiffs. Defendant denies liability, but believes the settlement amount is within the reasonable range. The Court has found that the settlement was made in good faith and has ordered that any other joint tortfeasor or co-obligor is barred from asserting further claims against Defendant County of Los Angeles for equitable comparative contribution, or partial or comparative indemnity, based on comparative negligence or comparative fault.
