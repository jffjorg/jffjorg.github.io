Here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

This is a tentative ruling on a motion to be relieved as counsel of record for Defendant Victory Bistro Cafe. The motion was filed by Rafi Ourfalian and Benjamin Aydindzhyan, counsel for the Defendant, on October 19, 2023. The motion is unopposed and seeks to be relieved as counsel due to the deteriorated attorney-client relationship. The client has ceased all communications with the attorney office and has failed to make payments for attorney services. The court finds that the motion complies with California Rules of Court, rule 3.1362, and grants the motion. The relief is effective upon the filing of the proof of service of this signed order upon the Defendant.
