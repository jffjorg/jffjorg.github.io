This is a tentative ruling in a case where the defendants, Jason Torres, N.P. and Medcove Urgent Care, APC, move to compel the plaintiff, Bella Rose Lopez, to serve verified responses to their discovery requests, including a demand for production of documents, special interrogatories, and form interrogatories. The defendants also request monetary sanctions against the plaintiff. The plaintiff has failed to serve any responses, and the court grants the motions to compel. The court awards the defendants $1,170 in monetary sanctions, which is reduced from the requested amount of $720. The court orders the plaintiff to provide verified responses to the discovery requests within 10 days and to pay the monetary sanctions within 30 days of the date of the order.
