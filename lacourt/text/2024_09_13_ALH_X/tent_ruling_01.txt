  Case Number:  23GDCV02066    Hearing Date:   September 13, 2024    Dept:  X Plaintiff's Motion to Compel Defendant's PMK Deposition The hearing on Plaintiff Katrina Ramos’ Motion to Compel the Deposition of Defendant Ford Motor Company’s Person Most Knowledgeable and Production of Documents is continued to September 25, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. in Department X of the Alhambra Courthouse. The parties are ordered to proceed with the deposition of Defendant FMC’s PMK on or before September 20, 2024. Defendant FMC is to produce a witness responsive to the categories identified in the deposition notice and produce responsive documents.  Defendant FMC has preserved its objections to the PMK categories and document requests. Following completion of the deposition on or before September 20, 2024, and to the extent that Plaintiff Ramos believes that Defendant FMC has failed to produce a knowledgeable witness on some or all of the categories identified or responsive documents, the parties are ordered to further meet and confer after the deposition.  Any deposition issues which remain outstanding or unresolved will be addressed at the continued hearing on September 25, 2024.  Any supplemental declaration concerning the categories and production demands at issue must be filed and served by September 23, 2024. Trial and the final status conference dates remain on calendar for October 3 and October 8, 2024, respectively.
