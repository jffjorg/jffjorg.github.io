Summary:

This is a tentative ruling on an application for a writ of attachment in a breach of contract case. The Plaintiff, ARF Financial LLC f/k/a Advance Restaurant Finance, LLC, is seeking to attach the assets of the Defendant, We Waffle, LLC dba The Waffle Experience, and its owner, Michael Donoho, to secure a debt of $396,426.10. The Court finds that the Plaintiff has established the probable validity of its breach of contract claims and grants the application for a writ of attachment. The Court also orders the Plaintiff to post an undertaking in the amount of $10,000.00 prior to the issuance of the writ of attachment.
