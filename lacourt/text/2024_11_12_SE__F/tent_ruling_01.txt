Plaintiff
ERMANNA SANTORO’s motion for trial preference is DENIED without prejudice. CCP §36(c)(1). 
 
Opposing
Party to Give notice.  
 
This
action for premises liability was filed by Plaintiff ERMANNA SANTORO on July
10, 2023. 
 
Plaintiff
moves for mandatory trial preference under CCP §36(a). 
 
CCP
§36(c)(1) states, “A party may file and serve a motion for preference supported
by a declaration of the moving party that all essential parties have been
served with process or have appeared.” (Id.) 
 
The
Court has reviewed Plaintiffs’ Moving Papers, and there is no declaration
attached indicating that all essential parties have been served with process or
appeared. Upon the Court’s review of this case file, the following parties have
not yet been served with process or appeared in this action: SOCA CONTRACTORS
CORPORATION; and GOLCHEH DEVELOPMENTS AND INVESTMENTS, LLC. 
 
Plaintiff’s
 motion is denied without prejudice.
