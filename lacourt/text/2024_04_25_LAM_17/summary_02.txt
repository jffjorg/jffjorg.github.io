And here is the English summary of the judge's tentative ruling:

The plaintiff, Immigrant Rights Defense Council, LLC, filed a lawsuit against Guadalupe Avitia-Dominguez and Income Tax Solutions, seeking injunctive relief for violation of the Immigration Consultant Act. The plaintiff served the defendant with requests for production (RFPs) on November 7, 2023, and responses were due by December 12, 2023. The defendant failed to respond to the RFPs, and the plaintiff moved to compel responses. The defendant did not oppose the motion, and the judge granted the motion. The defendant is sanctioned $700, jointly and severally with counsel, for failing to respond to the RFPs.
