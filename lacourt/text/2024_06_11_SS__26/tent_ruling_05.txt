State Farm v. Montes, et al.PETITION
FOR CASE NUMBER AND FOR COURT ORDERS COMPELLING FURTHER RESPONSES TO
INTERROGATORIES 
 (Ins. Code § 11580.2(f); CCP § 2030.290)
TENTATIVE RULING: 
 
Petitioner State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company’s Petition
for Case Number and Resolution of Uninsured Motorist Discovery Disputes is
PLACED OFF CALENDAR AS MOOT. The Petition asks the Court to issue a case number
in connection with Petitioner’s requests for discovery orders in an uninsured
motorist proceeding. (Pet., pp. 1-2.) It is not necessary to hold a hearing to
obtain the relief sought; a case number was assigned automatically upon the
filing of the Petition and discovery orders have already been issued. (See
Minute Orders, 04/08/24 and 04/09/24.)
 
 
Court clerk to give notice.
