The defendant, Cappo Management XLI LLC dba Ocean Honda of Whittier, filed a motion to compel responses to the plaintiff's request for production of documents and a request for sanctions. The plaintiff, Martha Delia Anda, failed to serve initial responses to the discovery requests despite a meet and confer effort. The court granted the motion to compel and ordered the plaintiff to serve verified responses to the discovery requests without objections within 20 days. The court also awarded sanctions of $273.00 to the defense counsel.
