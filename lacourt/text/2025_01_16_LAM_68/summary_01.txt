The court has granted the unopposed motion for approval of a settlement of claims under the Private Attorney General Act (PAGA) between Plaintiff Bruce William Ballinger, individually and on behalf of aggrieved parties, and Defendant B&B Transport, Inc. The settlement amount is $145,000, with $25,000 going to attorney costs, $9,000 to settlement administration costs, and $48,333.33 to attorney fees. The remaining amount will be distributed to employees and the Labor and Workforce Development Agency. The agreement provides for 18 installment payments over 30 days, with the first payment being made within 30 days of the full execution of the settlement agreement. The court acknowledges the request for retention or jurisdiction under Code of Civil Procedure section 664.6.
