The judge grants Plaintiff Mesa Underwriters Specialty Ins. Co.'s motion to be discharged from liability in the interpleader action. The court determines that Plaintiff has disclaimed any interest in the policy limits and will pay whatever sums Defendant Bardales is obligated to pay as damages up to the aggregate limit. The court awards Plaintiff $10,000 from the disputed funds in attorney's fees, reducing the fee request by 50% to account for overestimation in Plaintiff's billing. The court declines to award costs.
