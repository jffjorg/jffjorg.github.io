Here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to quash subpoenas for production of business records and medical records filed by Plaintiff John Peter Gawronski. The Plaintiff seeks to quash the subpoenas, which were issued by Defendant Ralphs Grocery Company, and argues that they are too broad and invade his right of privacy. The Judge has granted the motion in part and denied it in part. The Judge has limited the subpoenas in scope of time to 10 years prior to the injury to the present and has modified the subpoenas to limit the disclosure of medical records regarding mental health and substance abuse to documents generated in the last two years. The Judge has also ordered the parties to enter into a protective order and lodge the protective order for the Court's signing. The Judge has declined to award sanctions to the Plaintiff.
