The Judge's tentative ruling is regarding a case where Plaintiff Dawn Marie-Larkin Souther filed an action against Defendants Realty Center Management, Inc. and Americana La Crescenta LLC for general negligence and premises liability. The Defendants filed an answer and later filed a motion to continue the trial and related dates. The Court granted part of the motion and denied the rest, continuing the trial from December 22, 2023 to June 24, 2024.

The Defendants requested that the Court reopen discovery to trail the new trial date, citing the need for additional discovery to prepare for trial. The Court found that the motion satisfied the requirements of Code of Civil Procedure section 2024.050 and granted the request to reopen discovery.

The Court ordered that discovery be reopened and will trail the June 24, 2024 trial date. The moving parties are ordered to give notice of this ruling and file proof of service with the Court within five days.
