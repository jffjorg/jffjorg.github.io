 Case Number:  20STCV35340    Hearing Date:   March 28, 2024    Dept:  28 On March 11, 2024, the Court continued the hearing on Defendant Shay Rose Bolton's motion for an order requiring Plaintiff Katheryn Drexler to post an undertaking to give Plaintiff an opportunity to demonstrate her financial inability to post an undertaking.  The Court continued the hearing to March 28, 2024 and ordered that Plaintiff could file a supplemental declaration providing any additional information demonstrating her inability to obtain sufficient sureties for purposes of Code of Civil Procedure section 995.240 no later than five days before the continued hearing.  Plaintiff has not filed a supplemental declaration or asked for additional time to provide information about her financial inability to post an undertaking.  The Court grants the motion.The Court orders Plaintiff to file the undertaking not later than 30 days after service of the court’s order.The Court sets an order to show cause re: posting of an undertaking on May 28, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.Moving party is to give notice.Moving party is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling within five days.
