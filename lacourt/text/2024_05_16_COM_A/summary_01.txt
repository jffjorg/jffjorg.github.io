And here is the English summary:

The tentative ruling grants a motion for monetary sanctions against Cross-Complainants, Ansari, RHDM Oil, and their counsel, Siddiqui Law, APC. The Court finds that Cross-Complainants abused the discovery process by unilaterally cancelling Ansari's deposition and making an ex parte application to continue trial and reopen discovery. The Court awards sanctions of $5,580.00 to the Plaintiff and $4,500.00 to the Cross-Defendants for legal expenses incurred to move to compel Ansari's deposition and to oppose Cross-Complainants' ex parte application. The Court also awards costs incurred for filing fees and court reporter fees.
