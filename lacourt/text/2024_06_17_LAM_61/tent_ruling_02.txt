 Case Number:  22STCV26253    Hearing Date:   June 17, 2024    Dept:  61 VIEWPOINT AMBULANCE, INC. vs AMBULNZ HEALTH, LLC, A DELAWARE LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANYTENTATIVE     The present motion pertains to attorney Nathan D. Wirtschafter of the firm Nathan D. Wirtschafter Corp., counsel for Defendant Ambulnz Health, LLC. The motion is supported by the declaration of Wirtschafter, who states that Defendant is no longer conducting business, and the State of California has issued a certificate of cancellation for entity, meaning that Wirtschafter no longer has any expectation of being paid to conduct the defense. The motion includes a schedule of upcoming hearings and a proof of service. Trial is set for October 8, 2024. No opposition has been filed.Nathan D. Wirtschafter’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel for Defendant Ambulnz Health, LLC is GRANTED, effective upon the filing of a proof of service of a copy of the signed order upon Ambulnz Health, LLC, pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1362.Counsel Wirtschafter to give notice.
