 Case Number:  24STCV01929    Hearing Date:   June 17, 2024    Dept:  61 ANDIE JANE DE LA TORRE vs PAUL LINCOLN DAVIDGETENTATIVE    The present motion pertains to attorneys Anthony Ellis, Julian R. Contreras, and Daniel Kim, of the firm Ellis & Bakh LLP, counsel for Plaintiff Andie Jane de la Torre. The motion is supported by the declaration of Contreras, who states there has been a significant breakdown in attorney-client communication, impeding counsel’s ability to prosecute this case and represent the client. The proposed order lists the upcoming case management conference. However, no proof of service accompanies the motion, indicating service “on the client and on all other parties who have appeared in the case.” (CRC Rule 3.1362, subd. (d).)Anthony Ellis, Julian B. Contreras, and Daniel Kim’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff Andie Jane de la Torre is therefore DENIED.Moving attorneys to give notice.
