  Case Number:  20STCV42421    Hearing Date:   September 25, 2023    Dept:  30 OLGA CROCKER-MAY vs NALLELY ALVAREZMotion to Deem Request for Admissions Admitted, or in the Alternative, an Order Compelling Answers without Objection.  Ruling: Plaintiff’s motion to deem RFAs admitted is denied. The Court grants Plaintiff's alternative motion to compel responses to request for admissions, without objections, is granted. Defendant is to serve responses, without objection, within 20 days of notice of ruling. The request for sanctions is granted.  Defendant is ordered  to pay Plaintiff $ 961.65 in monetary sanctions within 30 days. Moving party is to  give notice of this ruling.Discussion:    Plaintiff’s counsel testifies  that he propounded RFAs on Defendant on September 16, 2023. Defendant served responses to the RFAs on August 4, 2023, well after the deadline. CCP section 2033.280 provides that when a party to whom requests for admissions are directed fails to serve a timely response, the party waives any objections to the requests. (Id.)Sanctions with respect to motions to deem requests for admissions admitted are mandatory if a party to whom the requests for admissions have been directed failed to serve a timely response to the request for admission. (Code Civ. Proc., §2033.280(c).) Accordingly, the Court awards sanctions. The Court awards Plaintiff $961.65 in monetary sanctions which represents three hours of attorney time at a rate of $300, plus $61.65 in filing fee.
