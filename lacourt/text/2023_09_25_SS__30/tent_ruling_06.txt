 Case Number:  22STCV03665    Hearing Date:   September 25, 2023    Dept:  30 GABRIELLA TRANCHINA, AN INDIVIDUAL; , vs UBER TECHNOLOGIES, INC., A CORPORATION;, et al.Motion to Continue Trial DateRuling:  Defendant's motion to continue the trial date is GRANTED. Trial is continued to June 17, 2024, at 8:30 am, in Dept. 30. Final Status Conference is continued to June 3, 2024, at 10:00 am. All trial-related deadlines are continued in correspondence with the new trial date. Moving party to give notice.Discussion:  Each party has the right to have a timely filed motion for summary judgment heard before trial. Defendant filed its motion for summary judgment timely for the November 16 trial date. The hearing date for the summary judgment motion is May 2, 2024. Given the Court's congestion, age of the case, and number of pending motions already filed, the Court declines to advance the hearing on the summary judgment motion.  Good cause appearing, and no prejudice shown, the Court grants the motion to continue.
