 Case Number:  21STCV04196    Hearing Date:   September 25, 2023    Dept:  30 JUAN PEDRO SALAZAR-RODRIGUEZ, et al. vs FEDEX GROUND PACKAGE SYSTEM, INC., A CORPORATION, et al.Motion to Compel DepositionRuling: Plaintifff's Motion to Compel Deposition is granted. Defendant FEDEX GROUND PACKAGE SYSTEM, INC., A CORPORATION is ordered to produce its person(s) most qualified for deposition for all topics identified in the deposition notice, provide code-compliant verified responses without
objections to all requests for production within plaintiffs' notice, and produce all responsive
documents to those request not less than three days before the deposition. The scheduled date of deposition is ordered within 30 days or a date agreed to, in writing, by the parties, of the notice of ruling. Moving party to give notice.Discussion:   Good cause appearing and no prejudice shown, the Court grants plaintiffs' motion to compel deposition. Plaintiffs timely served a deposition notice on defendant. (Ex. 1.) Defendant
never objected to the deposition notice. (Owen Dec. at ¶ 3.) Defendant then refused to appear.
(Ex. 2 at pp. 4-5.) Defendant then refused to provide alternative dates for the deposition. (Owen
Dec. at ¶ 6, Ex. 2 at p. 1.) As a result, the motion to compel deposition is granted. No opposition was filed against this regularly noticed motion.
