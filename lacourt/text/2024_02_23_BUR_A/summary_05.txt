And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has reviewed the motion brought by NHP/PMB Burbank Medical Plaza II, LLC (Plaintiff) to deem matters in their Request for Admissions (RFA) admitted. The Plaintiff alleges that Salem Neurological Associates (Defendant) failed to pay rent pursuant to a commercial lease agreement between the parties and seeks damages in the amount of $168,913.64. The Plaintiff served the RFA on the Defendant on September 27, 2023, but the Defendant failed to respond in a timely manner. The Plaintiff filed a motion to deem RFA matters admitted, and the Defendant did not oppose the motion.

The Court finds that the Plaintiff's motion must be granted, as the Defendant failed to respond to the RFA and the Plaintiff has incurred expenses in preparing and filing the motion. The Court awards sanctions in the amount of $700 against the Defendant and Defendant's counsel jointly and severally.
