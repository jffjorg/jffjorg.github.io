 Case Number:  23VECV04115    Hearing Date:   January 11, 2024    Dept:  T 23VECV04115
McHale v Ghalari
Tentative
ruling: Motion for relief from default – Denied.  There is a failure to show excusable
neglect.  The court can take judicial
notice that the clerk’s office is posted with a sign that says clerk cannot
give legal advice.  Also, it is patently
implausible that the defendant was told not to answer the complaint until the
case management conference.  In light of
these factors, the court denies the application.  Clerk of the court to release the writ of
possession.

give notice.
