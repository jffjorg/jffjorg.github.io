The court is considering a motion for a protective order regarding discovery filed by the City of Montebello. The City is seeking to quash the discovery requests propounded by the plaintiff, Awilda Perez, or require her to propound the discovery again in reasonable amounts. The plaintiff argues that the discovery requests are not unduly burdensome and seek relevant information. The court has reviewed the discovery requests and finds that they are not unduly burdensome, but grants the City 30 more days to respond to the requests. The court also denies the City's requests to quash the discovery requests in their entirety and to require the plaintiff to propound the discovery again. The court denies the plaintiff's request for sanctions. The motion is granted in part and denied in part.
