The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Jayesh Kumar vs. The City of Los Angeles and Morley Construction Company. Morley Construction Company has moved to compel Jayesh Kumar to serve further responses to form interrogatories, which were served on October 9, 2023. The Court has decided to continue the motion to a new date and has ordered the parties to participate in a meet and confer (Informal Discovery Conference, IDC) to resolve their discovery disputes. The parties are required to meet and confer in person or via telephone before the IDC date and are ordered to have any necessary materials with them. If the parties resolve their discovery disputes before the IDC date, Morley Construction Company is ordered to take both the IDC and the motion off calendar as soon as possible. The Court has also ordered Morley Construction Company to provide notice of this Order.
