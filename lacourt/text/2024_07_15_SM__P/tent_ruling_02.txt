Technologies US, Inc., et al., Case No. 23SMCV03092
Hearing date July 15,
2024
Plaintiffs Syed
and Kundra’s Request for Entry of Default Judmgent
 
On July 10, 2023,
plaintiffs filed this case for declaratory relief and costs of suit against
defendant. Defendant defaulted on January 8, 2024. Plaintiffs now request entry
of default judgment.
 
Plaintiffs request
costs of $950.46 which is justified by the evidence. Plaintiffs request
declaratory relief, with a finding that plaintiff Syed is entitled to 30%
ownership and plaintiff Kundra is entitled to 35” ownership of defendant Swyft
Technologies US, Inc.'s outstanding equity. Plaintiffs failed to provide any
declaration or other evidence in support of this requested finding. 
 
The matter may be
continued for such evidence to be presented.
