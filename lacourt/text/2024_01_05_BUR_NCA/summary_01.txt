The Plaintiff alleges that Defendant negligently administered derma filler injections, resulting in personal injury. Defendant has filed four motions: to compel further responses to RFPD Set One, to compel initial responses to RFPD Set Two and Special Interrogatories Set Two, and to amend its Answer to add affirmative defenses. Plaintiff has rendered no opposition to these motions.

The Court has granted Defendant's motions to compel further responses to RFPD Set One and to compel initial responses to RFPD Set Two and Special Interrogatories Set Two. The Court has also awarded sanctions to Defendant in the amount of $882.50 for Plaintiff's failure to timely respond to discovery requests. Additionally, the Court has granted Defendant's motion to amend its Answer to add affirmative defenses.

The Court has ordered Plaintiff to provide further responses to RFPD Set One within 30 days and to provide initial responses to RFPD Set Two and Special Interrogatories Set Two within 30 days. Defendant is to file the amended Answer within 10 days. The case is set for trial setting conference on April 16, 2024.
