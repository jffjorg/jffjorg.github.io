 Case Number:  23STLC03950    Hearing Date:   August 8, 2024    Dept:  26   
Tri-Tech Restoration and Construction, Co., Inc. v.
Fan, et al.MOTION TO RESET TRIAL DATE
(CCP § 128(a)(3))
TENTATIVE RULING: 
 
Plaintiff / Cross-Complainant
Tri-Tech Restoration and Construction, Co., Inc.’s Motion to Re-Set Trial Date
to December 19, 2024 is GRANTED. TRIAL IS SET FOR DECEMBER 19, 2024 AT 8:30 AM
IN DEPARTMENT 26 IN THE SPRING STREET COURTHOUSE. MOTION AND DISCOVERY CUTOFFS
TO FOLLOW THE NEW TRIAL DATE.
 
 
ANALYSIS:
 
On June 22, 2023, Plaintiff Tri-Tech Restoration and
Construction, Co., Inc. (“Plaintiff”) filed the instant action for breach of
contract and common counts against Defendant Wenxuan Fan (“Defendant”).
Following Defendant’s failure to file a responsive pleading, the Court entered
their default on October 11, 2023. Default Judgment was entered on November 28,
2023.
 
On April 9, 2024, the Court granted Defendant’s Motion to
Vacate Default and Default Judgment and ordered Defendant to file their
responsive pleading. (Minute Order, 04/09/24.) On April 10, 2024, Defendant
filed and served its answer and cross-complaint. Plaintiff answered the
cross-complaint on May 10, 2024. 
 
Plaintiff filed the instant Motion to Re-Set Trial Date to
December 19, 2024, on May 15, 2024. No opposition has been filed to date. 
 
Discussion
 
The Motion is brought pursuant to the Court’s inherent
authority to provide for the orderly conduct of proceedings before it under
Code of Civil Procedure section 128, subdivision (a), which states that “Every
court shall have the power to do all of the following: . . . (3) To provide for
the orderly conduct of proceedings before it, or its officers.” (Code Civ.
Proc., § 128, subd. (a)(3).) The action is now at issue following the filing
and service of the parties’ pleadings and responses. Accordingly, the Court
resets the trial date for December 19, 2024 at 8:30 am in Department 26 in the
Spring Street Courthouse. 
 
Conclusion
 
Plaintiff / Cross-Complainant Tri-Tech Restoration and Construction,
Co.’s Motion to Re-Set Trial Date to December 19, 2024 is GRANTED. TRIAL IS SET
FOR DECEMBER 19, 2024 AT 8:30 AM IN DEPARTMENT 26 IN THE SPRING STREET
COURTHOUSE. MOTION AND DISCOVERY CUTOFFS TO FOLLOW THE NEW TRIAL DATE.
