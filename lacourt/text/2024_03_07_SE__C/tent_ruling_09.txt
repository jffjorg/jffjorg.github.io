Defendant JUAN GONZALES’s Demurrer to Plaintiff’s Complaint
is OVERRULED. Defendant is ORDERED to Answer within 5 days. 
 
Opposing Party to give Notice. 
 
This action for unlawful detainer concerning real property
located at 7404 Florence Ave., Downey CA, 90240 (“Subject Property”) was filed
by Plaintiff DEL RIO 7444, LLC (“Plaintiff”) on January 9, 2024. 
 
Defendant (in pro per) JUAN GONZALES (“Defendant”) demurs to
the sole cause of action for unlawful detainer. 
 
Defendants argues that Plaintiff’s claim for unlawful
detainer is subject to demurrer because the form Complaint is improperly
verified. 
 
“In all cases of a verification of a pleading, the affidavit
of the party shal state that the same is true of his own knowledge… and where a
pleading is verified, it shall be by the affidavit of  party, unless the parties are absent from the
county where the attorney has his or her office, or from some cause unable to
verify it, or the facts are within the knowledge of his or her attorney or
other person verifying the same.” (CCP §446(a).) 
 
The demurrer is OVERRULED. The Court finds that Plaintiff’s
verification meets the requirements set forth in CCP §446(a).
