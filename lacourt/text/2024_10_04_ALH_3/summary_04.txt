Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by defendant Kenny Wan, Consolidated Disposal Service, LLC, et al. to the complaint filed by plaintiff Antonio Guachiac Carac. The demurrer alleges that the complaint fails to state sufficient facts to support the cause of action for violation of Labor Code section 3706, which requires employers to carry workers' compensation insurance. The judge has overruled the demurrer, finding that the complaint adequately alleges the facts necessary to state a cause of action under Labor Code section 3600. The judge also rejects the defendant's argument that the complaint is uncertain or ambiguous, finding that any ambiguities can be clarified through discovery. The defendant is ordered to file an answer within 20 days of the date of the order.
