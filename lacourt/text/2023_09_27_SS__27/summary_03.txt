And here is the English summary:

Judge Kerry Bensinger has issued a tentative ruling granting the Motions to be Relieved as Counsel filed by Ira N. Katz, counsel for Defendants HK Logistics Corporation, HK Trans LLC, and Miguel Angel Roque. The Motions were filed due to a breakdown in communications between Ira N. Katz and his clients, resulting in a personality clash and a difference in opinion over legal strategy. The Judge found that the Motions comply with California Rules of Court, rule 3.1362, and that absent a showing of resulting prejudice, an attorney's request for withdrawal should be granted. The Motions are granted and will take effect upon the filing of proof of service of the signed orders upon the Defendants.
