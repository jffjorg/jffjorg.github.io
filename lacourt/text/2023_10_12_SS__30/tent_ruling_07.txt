 Case Number:  22STCV01400    Hearing Date:   October 12, 2023    Dept:  30 MARIA VILLALOBOS vs RYDER TRUCK RENTAL, INC., et al.Re: Motions to be Admitted Pro Hac ViceTENTATIVEThe Court has reviewed Defendants’ motions and finds that Defendants’ applications for William J. Madden IV to be admitted pro hac vice are CONTINUED for Defendants to file proof of service of the application on the State Bar, proof of payment of fee, and to address Madden IV’s prior repeated appearances under this rule.  Judicial Assistant to calendar.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONThe Court finds that Defendants have failed to file proof of service of the application on the State Bar of California, as required by California Rules of Court rule 9.40(c). Additionally, the Court notes that Madden IV has appeared in California under this rule in three separate instances in the past year, which is prohibited by California Rules of Court rule 9.40(b) “absent special circumstances.”
