 Case Number:  21STCV45993    Hearing Date:   October 12, 2023    Dept:  30 MIGUEL ANGEL RODRIGUEZ MARROQUIN vs ANN JIHAE SINRe: Motion to Compel Discovery (not "Further Discovery")TENTATIVEDefendant Ann Jihae Sin’s motion to compel responses is GRANTED. Plaintiff is ORDERED to serve responses, without objection, to Defendant’s Form Interrogatories, Set One, within 20 days of the issuance of this order. Request for Sanctions is DENIED.  Moving party is to give notice of this ruling.¿¿DISCUSSIONDefendant moves to compel Plaintiff to provide responses to the Form Interrogatories. On March 1, 2022, Defendant propounded Form Interrogatories on Plaintiff, but Plaintiff has failed to provide verified responses. (Motion 5: 9-12.) Plaintiff has failed to provide responses to the form interrogatories that were filed over a year ago in March 2022.  ¿Failure to timely respond waives all objections, including privilege and work product.¿ (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.290, subd. (a).) The Court declines to award sanctions because these motions are unopposed.
