The judge has issued a tentative ruling granting Defendant Perfect Parking, Inc.'s motions to compel further responses from Plaintiff Andres Aguilar-Maldonado to Requests for Admissions and Request for Production of Documents. The motions were filed due to Plaintiff's failure to provide substantive responses to the requests. The parties had previously agreed to additional extensions, but Plaintiff's counsel notified Defendant that substantive responses were not served due to inability to confer with Plaintiff. The judge awarded monetary sanctions in the amount of $860 to counsel for Defendant, to be paid by Plaintiff within 30 days. The judge also ordered Plaintiff to pay the sanctions and directed Defendant to provide notice and file a proof of service.
