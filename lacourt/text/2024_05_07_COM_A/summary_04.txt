The tentative ruling grants the Defendant's three motions to compel the Plaintiff's responses to form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and request for production of documents. The Court also imposes sanctions of $760.00 on the Plaintiff, Reginald Hackett, payable to the defense counsel, for failing to respond to discovery requests in a timely manner.
