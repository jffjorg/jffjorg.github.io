The court has issued a tentative ruling granting defendants' motion to compel arbitration. The defendants, including Joe Ko, Louisa Zhou, Predicate Holdings, Inc., Studio 88 Holdings, Inc., and J. Adams Fashion, LLC, moved to compel arbitration of the claims alleged in plaintiff Zooshoo, Inc.'s complaint. The plaintiff signed an Amended and Restated Services Agreement (Agreement) with Predicate Holdings, Inc. on February 17, 2021, which includes an arbitration provision stating that any controversy or claim relating to the Agreement shall be settled by arbitration. The court finds that the plaintiff has not met its burden to show that the Agreement is invalid due to fraud in the execution. The court also finds that the arbitration clause in the Agreement applies to the causes of action alleged in the plaintiff's complaint. The court orders the parties to arbitrate the claims alleged in the plaintiff's complaint and stays the action until arbitration is completed.
