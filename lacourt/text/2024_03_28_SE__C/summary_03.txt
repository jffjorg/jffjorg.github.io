The court has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents. The plaintiff, Jose Sanchez Orejel and Susana Sanchez, filed a suit against American Honda Motor Co. in December 2022, alleging a lemon law case. The plaintiff filed a motion on October 25, 2023, seeking to compel further responses from the defendant regarding specific requests for production of documents. The court has granted the motion in part, ordering the defendant to provide further responses to requests Nos. 45 and 46, which seek documents related to warranty repairs to 2019 Honda Civic vehicles sold in California. The court has denied the plaintiff's request to compel further responses to requests Nos. 20, 22, 23, 26, 27, 28, 29, 30, and 32, finding that the defendant's responses were adequate. The court has also imposed a monetary sanction against the plaintiff for the unsuccessful motion.
