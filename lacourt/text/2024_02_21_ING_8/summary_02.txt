And here is the English summary:

This tentative ruling by the judge addresses the motions to compel responses filed by Defendant Providence Medical Institute Manhattan Beach Urgent Care to Plaintiff Edwin J. Ulloa's failure to respond to discovery requests. The Plaintiff had filed a complaint against multiple defendants, including Providence, alleging medical malpractice and wrongful death. Providence propounded written discovery requests, including interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production of documents, to which the Plaintiff failed to respond. Despite meet and confer letters, no responses were received. The judge grants the motions to compel responses, ordering the Plaintiff to submit verified responses to each discovery request without objection by March 19, 2024.
