  Case Number:  22STCV22921    Hearing Date:   February 1, 2024    Dept:  52 Defendants Morruth Holdings, LLC and
RMK Holdings, LLC’s Motion to Continue Trial and Related Dates
Defendants Morruth Holdings,
LLC and RMK Holdings, LLC move to continue the trial and pretrial deadlines.  “The
court may grant a continuance only on an affirmative showing of good cause
requiring” it.  (Rule 3.1332(c).)  “A party’s excused inability to obtain
essential testimony, documents, or other material evidence despite diligent
efforts” indicates good cause.  (Rule
3.1332(c)(6).)
Defendants show
good cause to continue the trial.  They
have faced delay in receiving documents from third parties (White Decl., ¶¶ 6,
11, 14), and plaintiff recently raised new issues of lead and asbestos exposure
(id., ¶ 7).  
Disposition
            Defendants Morruth
Holdings, LLC and RMK Holdings, LLC’s motion to continue trial is granted.  The court hereby continues the final status
conference from February 28, 2024, to June 17, 2024, at 9:00 a.m.  The court hereby continues the order to show
cause re: dismissal of cross-complaint from February 28, 2024, to June 17,
2024, at 9:00 a.m.  The court hereby
continues the jury trial from March 6, 2024, to July 3, 2024, at 10:00 a.m.  All pretrial deadlines are continued to
follow the new trial date.
