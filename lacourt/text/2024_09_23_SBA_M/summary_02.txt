The Honorable Gary Y. Tanaka, Judge of the Los Angeles Superior Court, Southwest District, has issued a tentative ruling in the case Jose Erick Alvarado v. The Court also finds that the attorney has filed the required declaration and has shown sufficient reasons for withdrawal.
