And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by the City of Los Angeles to the first amended complaint filed by Abet Security Services, Inc. The City challenges the breach of contract claim, arguing that the complaint improperly includes terms not agreed upon, specifically the provision of "Field Supervisors". The judge has sustained the demurrer with 30 days leave to amend, allowing Abet Security Services, Inc. to allege terms within the legally operative scope of the incorporated contract. The judge has also denied the motion to strike and has granted leave to amend, subject to certain conditions.
