Plaintiff CE Haley seeks to compel initial responses to her form interrogatories and requests for production from Defendant Sherrell Walker. On July 17, 2024, Defendant Walker filed a declaration stating that she did not receive the Motion. Defendant has had six months to obtain the Motion or provide responses.  It is not apparent that responses have ever been provided.  Thus, the Motion is granted.  Responses, without objections, are to be served within 30 days. The Court declines to award sanctions.Moving party to give notice.
