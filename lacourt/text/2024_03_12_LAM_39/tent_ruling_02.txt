 Case Number:  23STCP02938    Hearing Date:   March 12, 2024    Dept:  39 Bristol Farms v.
Heritage Two LLC, et al.
Case No.
23STCP02938
Motion for
Attorneys’ Fees
 
            Petitioner
Bristol Farms (“Petitioner”) prevailed in arbitration against Respondents
Heritage Two LLC, Jeffrey B. Panosian, and Claire B. Panosian (“Respondents”).  The Court entered judgment per the
arbitration award on Petitioner’s request. 
Now, Petitioner seeks attorneys’ fees pursuant
to the underlying leases.  The motion is
unopposed. 
 
            Based
upon the foregoing, the Court orders as follows:
 
            1.         Petitioner’s motion for attorneys’ fees
is granted.
 
            2.         The Court orders Respondents to pay
attorneys’ fees in the amount of 18,192.75 within thirty (30) days.
 
            3.         Petitioner’s counsel may lodge an
amended judgment if necessary.  
 
            4.         Petitioner’s counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
