This is a tentative ruling on the motion of Simon P. Etehad, counsel for defendants Shahin S. Zackary and Stanford & 12th Street, LP, to be relieved as counsel of record. The motion is granted, effective upon proof of service of this order. The court has considered the moving papers and declares that there has been a breakdown of the working relationship between Counsel and the clients, and that Counsel has been unable to communicate with the clients, preventing further representation. The court notes that the clients did not file an opposition to the motion. The court also notes that the next hearing is scheduled for August 14, 2024, but the proposed order lists the next hearing as August 6, 2024. The court grants the motion and orders that Counsel be relieved as counsel effective upon proof of service of this order.
