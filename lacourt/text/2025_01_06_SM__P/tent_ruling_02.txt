Inabu vs. City of Culver City, Case
no. 23SMCV06014
Hearing date January 6, 2025
Defendant’s
Motion for Leave to File a Cross-Complaint
Plaintiff
alleges she sustained injury after tripping and falling due to tree roots,
which causing an uplift on a Culver City sidewalk. Plaintiff sued the City,
which seeks to file a cross-complaint against West Coast Arborists, which is
contractually obligated to report uplifts to the City, as well as adjacent
property owner Cherry on Top FSMDR. 
As
this involves the same incident, the cross-complaint is compulsory. There is no
opposition filed. The motion is GRANTED. City to file the cross-complaint
within 5 court days.
