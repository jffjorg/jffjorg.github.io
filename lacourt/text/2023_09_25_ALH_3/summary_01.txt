The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the motions filed by the Defendants, Richard Dale Gentile, D.D.S., et al., seeking to compel the Plaintiff, Rosevianney C. Ogumsi, to serve responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Requests for Admission (Set Two), as well as seeking sanctions against the Plaintiff for failing to do so. The Court has granted the motion to compel responses, ordering the Plaintiff to serve verified responses, without objections, to the Defendants' Form Interrogatories (Set Two) within 20 days of the date of this Order. The Court has also granted the motion to deem admitted the truth of the matters in the Requests for Admission (Set Two). As for the request for sanctions, the Court has imposed sanctions on the Plaintiff in the amount of $870, consisting of 2 hours at defense counsel's hourly rate of $375 and $120 in filing fees. The Court has also ordered the Plaintiff to serve the responses to the Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Requests for Admission (Set Two) within 20 days of the date of this Order.
