The court also finds that the allegations in the Complaint do not state facts sufficient to constitute causes of action for violation of Civil Code section 789.3(b) and 1942.5. The court orders the Defendants to file an Answer within 10 days.

In case 24STCV05884, the court sustains the demurrer as to the second cause of action in Plaintiff Coastline's Complaint. The court finds that the allegations in the Complaint do not state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action for breach of contract and negligent misrepresentation. The court also finds that the allegations in the Complaint do not state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action for fraud. The court orders the Plaintiff to file an amended Complaint within 20 days.

The court has also granted leave to amend the Complaint in case 23STCV30193 to the extent that the Plaintiff seeks relief under Civil Code section 789.3(b) and 1942.5. The court has also granted leave to amend the Complaint in case 24STCV05884 to the extent that the Plaintiff seeks relief under the Federal Fair Housing Act and the California Housing Discrimination Law.
