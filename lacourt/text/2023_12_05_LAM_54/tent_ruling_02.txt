The Court considers the moving papers.
No opposition has been received. 
 
“If parties to pending litigation
stipulate, in a writing signed by the parties outside the presence of the court
or orally before the court, for settlement of the case, or part thereof, the
court, upon motion, may enter judgment pursuant to the terms of the
settlement.”  (CCP § 664.6.) 
 
Defendant Yozura moves to enforce the
settlement executed by Plaintiff and Defendant December 23, 2022 and enter
judgment according to its terms. The settlement provides that Plaintiff will
pay Defendant $37,000.00. The Court retained jurisdiction to enforce the
settlement. Defendant represents that Plaintiff has not complied with
agreement. 
 
The motion is GRANTED. Defendant also
is awarded attorneys’
fees and costs in the amount of $13,622.25 and $61.65, respectively.
