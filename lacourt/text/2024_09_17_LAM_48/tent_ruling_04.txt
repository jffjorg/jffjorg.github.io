On
February 10, 2023, Plaintiff Mehrnaz Hadian filed this action against Defendant
Irene Chen, M.D. and others.
On
May 21, 2024, Defendant served Special Interrogatories, Set Two on Plaintiff.  Plaintiff did not provide any responses.
On
August 22, 2024, Defendant filed a motion to compel responses.
Where
a party fails to serve timely responses to discovery requests, the court may make
an order compelling responses.  (Code Civ.
Proc., §§ 2030.290, 2031.300; Healthcare Consulting,
Inc. v. Pacific Healthcare Consultants (2007) 148 Cal.App.4th 390, 403.)   A party that fails to serve timely responses
waives any objections to the request, including ones based on privilege or the protection
of attorney work product.  (Code Civ. Proc.,
§§ 2030.290, subd. (a), 2031.300, subd. (a).)
Plaintiff
filed no opposition to this motion and did not serve timely responses.  Accordingly, the motion is GRANTED.  Plaintiff is ordered to provide verified responses,
without objections Special Interrogatories, Set Two within 10 days.
Moving
party to give notice.

in the case submit on the tentative ruling, no appearances before the Court are
required unless a companion hearing (for example, a Case Management Conference)
