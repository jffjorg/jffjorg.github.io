The court has reviewed the demurrer to the complaint and motion to strike filed by the defendant, AIDS Healthcare Foundation. The court has overruled the demurrer to the first and second causes of action for breach of the warranty of habitability under Civil Code § 1941.1 and Health & Safety Code § 7920.3. The court has also overruled the demurrer to the third cause of action for violation of Civil Code § 1942.4. The court has overruled the demurrer to the fifth cause of action for nuisance and the sixth cause of action for intentional infliction of emotional distress. The court has overruled the demurrer to the ninth cause of action for fraudulent concealment. The court has denied the motion to strike the prayers for punitive damages and attorney's fees. The defendant is ordered to file and serve an answer to the complaint within 30 days of notice of this ruling.
