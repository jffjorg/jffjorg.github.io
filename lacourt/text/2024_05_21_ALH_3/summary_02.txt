This tentative ruling is about a dispute between Gerardo Martinez, Jr. and Leila Martinez (plaintiffs) and the City of Pasadena (defendant) regarding a fight that occurred at a soccer match. The defendant served the plaintiffs with Form Interrogatories, Set One, but the plaintiffs failed to serve any responses. The defendant filed a motion to compel the plaintiffs to serve verified responses without objections. The court granted the motion and ordered the plaintiffs to serve the responses within 20 days. The defendant also requested monetary sanctions, which were granted in the reduced amount of $285. The court imposed this amount on the plaintiffs for the defendant's defense counsel's hourly rate and filing fees.
