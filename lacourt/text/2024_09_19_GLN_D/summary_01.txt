As for the English-text summary:

The tentative ruling grants the motion of Dixieline Builders Fund Control, Inc. and Probuild Company, LLC for leave to file a compulsory cross-complaint against Nieves and King Cobra. The cross-complaint seeks to add allegations to the July 9, 2024, inspectors' cross-complaint, including claims for contractual indemnity and breach of duty to defend. The court finds that the interests of justice favor the judicial efficiency of determining all liability and indemnity issues between the parties in a single proceeding. The motion is granted in the interest of justice, and the moving parties are ordered to e-file their signed First Amended Cross-Complaint by the close of business this date.
