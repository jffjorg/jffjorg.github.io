  STREET LENDING CORPORATION’S DEMURRER TO PLAINTIFF’S COMPLAINT



 
Plaintiff Denise Wheeler (“Plaintiff”) filed
this action on September 21, 2023 against Defendant Center Street Lending
Corporation (“Defendant”). The Complaint alleges causes of action for (1) violation
of Civil Code section 2923.55 and (2) violation of Business and Professions Code section 17200 et seq. 
Defendant
demurs to both causes of action of the Complaint. No opposition to the demurrer
was filed. However, the Court notes that on January 8, 2024, Plaintiff filed a
First Amended Complaint.  
Accordingly,
Defendant’s demurrer to the Complaint is moot.
