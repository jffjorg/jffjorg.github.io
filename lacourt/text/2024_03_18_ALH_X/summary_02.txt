This tentative ruling is in regards to a motion to compel further responses and a motion to compel deposition testimony in a lemon law case involving a 2020 Chevrolet Silverado 1500. The plaintiff, Steven Jackson, has filed a complaint alleging breach of express warranty and implied warranty. The defendant, General Motors, LLC, has objected to the deposition notice and failed to appear for the deposition. The court grants the plaintiff's motion to compel deposition testimony of the defendant's person most knowledgeable, but denies the motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents. The court notes that the parties must meet and confer regarding the deposition date for the defendant's person most knowledgeable. The court also denies the plaintiff's requests for sanctions.
