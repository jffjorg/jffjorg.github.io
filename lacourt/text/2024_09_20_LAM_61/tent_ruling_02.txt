 Case Number:  24STCV12209    Hearing Date:   September 20, 2024    Dept:  61 TRUCONNECT COMMUNICATIONS, INC., A DELAWARE CORPORATION vs TRUE CONNECTIONS LLC, AN OHIO LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANYTENTATIVEThe present application pertains to Kevin Terrazas, to be admitted as counsel for Defendant True Connections, LLC (Defendant). Terrazas is a resident of Texas and licensed to practice law in Texas, in good standing, with his office address in Austin. (Terrazas Decl. ¶¶ 1–2, 5.) He has not applied to act as pro hac vice counsel in California within the past two years. (Terrazas Decl. ¶¶ 4–6.) The present application has been sent to the State Bar of California along with the requisite fee. (Simon Decl. ¶¶ 5–6.)The application is GRANTED.Defendant to give notice.
