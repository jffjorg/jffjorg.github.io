 Case Number:  23STCV00865    Hearing Date:   October 24, 2024    Dept:  74 Murray v
Southern California Permanente Medical Group, Inc.
Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Further
Responses to Special Interrogatories, set One.
 
            This
motion arises out of a disability discrimination and employment dispute.
            On
January 13, 2023, plaintiff Sylvia Monique Murray (Plaintiff) filed her suit
against defendant Southern California Permanente Medical Group, Inc.
(Defendant). On November 14, 2023, Plaintiff propounded Special
Interrogatories, set One. Defendant served verified responses and objections on
January 12, 2024. On February 27, 2024, Plaintiff filed her motion to compel. Defendant
served supplemental responses on August 1, 2024, making Plaintiff’s motion
moot.  
            Sanctions
may be awarded even if supplemental responses have been provided.  (Sinaiko Healthcare Consulting, Inc. v.
Pacific Healthcare Consultants (2007) 148 Cal.App.4th 390, 409.) Plaintiff
requests $3,910.00 in sanctions for 8 hours for the motion, 3 hours preparing a
reply and appearing at the hearing at $350.00 per hour. Additionally, Plaintiff
requests $60.00 in costs.  The Court
finds this reasonable and awards $3,910.00 in sanctions. 
 
CONCLUSION 
Plaintiff’s
motion to compel further responses to Special Interrogatories, Set 1 is moot.  The court grants Plaintiff’s request for
sanctions in the amount of $3,910.00.
Plaintiff
shall give notice.
