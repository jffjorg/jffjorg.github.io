Case Number: 22CHCV00474

Department: F47
Case Type: Civil
Plaintiff: Sam Ostayan
Defendants: Prestige Default Services, LLC, Center Street Lending VIII SPE, LLC, Soldiers of America (SOA), SoCal Innovative Group, LLC
Facts:
  - The case arises from a non-judicial foreclosure sale of a residential property located at 10711 Sunnybrae Avenue, Chatsworth, California 91311.
  - The foreclosure sale was conducted by Prestige Default Services, LLC, as trustee for ABS Trust VI Select Portfolio Servicing, Inc.
  - The winning bidder at the foreclosure sale was U.S. Bank Trust National Association, as Trustee for ABS Trust VI Select Portfolio Servicing, Inc.
  - Plaintiff Sam Ostayan filed a lawsuit against Prestige, Center Street, SOA, and SoCal, alleging that Prestige failed to comply with Civil Code 2924m when conducting the foreclosure sale.
  - Plaintiff claims that SOA's bid was not permitted as it was submitted outside of the 45-day statutory deadline provided in Civil Code 2924m(c)(4), making the sale void.
  - Plaintiff alleges that SOA then sold the property to SoCal for approximately $1,010,000.00, thereby realizing a substantial profit from its alleged improper purchase of the property.
  - Plaintiff claims that SoCal was not a bona fide purchaser of the property because the TDUS in favor of SOA was void, as is the legal title of SoCal, a defaulted entity.
  - Plaintiff seeks a judicial declaration of the rights and obligations of the parties concerning the rights, title to, and ownership of the subject property.

  - Plaintiff filed this action on June 28, 2022.
