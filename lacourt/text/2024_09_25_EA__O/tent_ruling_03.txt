Background
 
This is a lemon law case. 
 
On May 3, 2024, Plaintiff Donta Rhea filed suit against
General Motors, LLC for SBA violations arising from his purchase of a 2018
Chevrolet Camaro. 
 
On July 15, 2024, Defendant filed the instant demurrer and
motion to strike (as to the 5th COA for fraudulent concealment cause
of action). 
 
On September 12, 2024, Plaintiff filed its first amended
complaint (FAC) (reasserting the same COAs).
 
Discussion
 
As an amended complaint has been filed, the demurrer and MTS
are moot.
