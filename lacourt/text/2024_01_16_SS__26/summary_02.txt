**English Text Summary**

The Judge has granted Petitioner Gemstone, LLC's Petition to Declare Mobilehome Abandoned. The Judge finds that the mobilehome is abandoned as it is unoccupied, a reasonable person would believe it to be abandoned, and it is not permanently affixed to the land. The Judge also finds that Petitioner has satisfied the requirements of Civil Code Section 798.61, including posting a Notice of Belief of Abandonment and serving it on Respondent. The Judge awards Petitioner rent due on the mobilehome site, attorney's fees, and costs in the amount of $14,869.44, $836.08, and $564.57, respectively. The Judge sets an Order to Show Cause hearing for April 16, 2024 to determine the sale/disposal of the mobilehome and submission of accounting.
