The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion filed by Plaintiff Lisa Carson to compel Defendant FCA US, Inc. to further respond to requests for production of documents. The motion was filed on June 17, 2024, and Defendant has not filed an opposition. The judge finds that Defendant has already responded to the requests, and therefore, the motion to compel further responses is denied. The judge notes that Plaintiff's remedy is to offer Defendant's allegedly incomplete response as an admission or as a prior inconsistent statement to impeach Defendant at trial, if Defendant seeks to introduce evidence it did not disclose in discovery. The judge orders Plaintiff to give notice of this order and to file proof of service of same.
