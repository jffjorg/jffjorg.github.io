 Case Number:  22STCV09840    Hearing Date:   December 7, 2023    Dept:  29 Plaintiff’s counsel
has filed a motion to be relieved as counsel. 

Counsel has
filed the requisite forms pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1362. Counsel states they
confirmed current mailing address within the past 30 days oral with their
client, confirmed delivery with priority mail tracking, and have served
documents in the past to this address with the client confirming their receipt.
(MC-052, no. 3b(1)(d).) Counsel declares that the attorney-client relationship
between all Plaintiffs and counsel has broken down.
(MC-052, no. 2.)  
While Counsel
has included the OSC re Dismissal for failure to file proof of service on
December 6, 2023, Counsel has failed to include the hearing date for the OCS re
Dismissal scheduled for March 17, 2025, per Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(d). (MC-052,
no. 5; MC-053, no. 8.)
Accordingly, the
motion is DENIED without prejudice.
Moving party to give notice.
