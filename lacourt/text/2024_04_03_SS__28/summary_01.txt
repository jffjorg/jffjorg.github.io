The judge has considered the petitioning papers and rules as follows. The plaintiffs, Emmanuel Vasquez, Yahtsiry Vasquez, Dayana Villanueva, Alaia Vasquez, Jacob Vasquez, and Josue Vasquez, filed this action against defendants Curtis Deondis Morse and Does 1-50 for motor vehicle tort, general negligence, and wrongful death. The plaintiffs have allocated the global settlement amount of $15,000.00 equally among themselves, and each of the six plaintiffs will receive $1,855.05, which is their equal share of the global settlement amount with reductions for attorney's fees and costs. The court grants the petitions for approval of the compromise of the pending actions of plaintiff Jacob Vasquez and plaintiff Alaia Vasquez against defendant Curtis Deondis Morse. The court sets an order to show cause re: distribution of settlement proceeds on May 15, 2024, at 8:30 a.m. in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. The court also sets an order to show cause re: dismissal (settlement) on May 15, 2024, at 8:30 a.m. in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.
