The Judge's tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel a second medical examination of the Plaintiff, Otoniel Martinez, by Defendant Sumitomo Rubber North America and others. The Plaintiff claims traumatic brain injury and has previously undergone a medical examination with the Defendants' orthopedic specialist. The Defendants seek a second examination to investigate the Plaintiff's claim for traumatic brain injury. The Plaintiff opposes the motion, citing procedural defects in the Defendants' motion. The Judge finds that the Defendants have cured the procedural defects and grants the motion to compel the second examination. The Plaintiff is ordered to appear for the examination on October 30, 2023, at 1:00 p.m. at Regus (California Riverside Turner Riverwalk), located at 11801 Pierce Street, Suite 200, Riverside, California.
