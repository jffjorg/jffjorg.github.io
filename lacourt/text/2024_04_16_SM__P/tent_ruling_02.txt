Jane
Doe v. Curtis Olson et al., Case No. SC126806
Hearing
date April 16, 2024
Plaintiff’s
Attorney of Record’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel  
 
On
February 21, 2024 plaintiff’s counsel Gloria P. Martinez-Senftner filed the instant motion to be
relieved as counsel of record due to her deteriorating physical health.
Martinez-Senftner Decl. ¶ 2. Plaintiff’s counsel states plaintiff does not
oppose the motion but requests time to find new counsel. Id. at ¶ 3. No
opposition has been filed.  There is no prejudice. 
 
California Rule of Court, rule
3.1362 requires a proposed order relieving counsel. Once plaintiff’s counsel
submits the MC-053 proposed order, the motion may be GRANTED.
