And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to compel report filed by Plaintiff Iris Carmina Escobar Alba. The Plaintiff sought to compel Defendants Rexdeo Properties LTD dba Rexdeo Apartments, ET AL. to produce a complete defense medical examination report from Dr. Eric S. Millstein. The report was served, but Plaintiff contends it does not comply with CCP §2032.610 because it does not include Dr. Millstein's diagnoses, prognoses, and conclusions.

The Court agrees that the report is statutorily compliant in part, but does not contain any prognoses. The Court orders Defendants to produce an amended report within 30 days addressing Dr. Millstein's prognoses. The Court also declines to award monetary sanctions to any party.
