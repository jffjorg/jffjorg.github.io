


[1] The official comments of the
California Law Revision Commission “are declarative of the intent not only of
the draftsman of the code but also of the legislators who subsequently enacted
it.” (People v. Williams (1976) 16 Cal.3d 663, 667-668.) Commission
materials are entitled to great weight in construing statutes.  (Hale
v. Southern Cal. IPA Med. Group. Inc. (2001) 86 Cal.App.4th 919, 927.) 
They are subject to judicial notice.  (Barkley v. City of Blue Lake,
(1993) 18 Cal.App.4th 1745, 1751 n.3.)
