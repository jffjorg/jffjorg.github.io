The judge has granted the motion to compel defendant Dairyon Dayvon Thompson West to appear for a deposition, as well as the request for sanctions. The defendant failed to appear for a previously scheduled deposition and did not communicate with the plaintiff's counsel regarding outstanding discovery. The plaintiff has demonstrated that the meet and confer requirement is satisfied and that there was no good cause for the defendant's failure to attend the deposition. As a result, the plaintiff is entitled to an order compelling the defendant's appearance. The judge has ordered the defendant to pay sanctions of $1,081.00 to the plaintiff's counsel within 20 days of the order.
