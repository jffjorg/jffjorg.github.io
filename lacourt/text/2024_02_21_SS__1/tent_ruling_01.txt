  Case Number:  22STCV16420    Hearing Date:   February 21, 2024    Dept:  1 The Court hereby GRANTS final approval and awards/approves
the following: 
(1) $148,333.33 for attorney fees to Class Counsel,
Mooradian Law, APC; (2) $12,259.72 for attorney costs to Class Counsel; (3)
$15,000 (75% of $20,000 PAGA penalty) to the LWDA; and (5) $11,800 for
settlement administration costs to CPT Group, Inc. 
Plaintiff’s counsel’s amended proposed Order and Judgment
should include a statement that no class members requested exclusion and
ensures that distribution from the Gross Settlement Funds for attorney fees
will occur after mailing of checks to Class Members. The order should also
include a paragraph reflecting the gross settlement amount, all funds to be
deducted from the GSA and the Net Settlement Amount to be distributed to the
class per the settlement agreement.  
The Court orders class counsel to file a final report
summarizing all distributions made pursuant to the approved settlement,
supported by declaration. The Court will set a non-appearance date for
submission of a final report for December 20, 2024.
Finally, the Plaintiff is ordered to file a dismissal with
prejudice of Case No. 19STCV45338 forthwith. A non- appearance will be set for
February 26, 2024 to confirm this has occurred.
