And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has issued a tentative ruling on a demurrer filed by defendants Salt International Co., LTD, Jea Hung Lyoo, Gyumyeong Chae, Jihyun Seo, Daeyoung Kim, and One Road USA, Inc. to the First Amended Complaint filed by plaintiff Sky Express World Courier, Inc.

The court sustained the demurrer to the first cause of action for breach of fiduciary duty, finding that plaintiff has failed to state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action. However, the court granted leave to amend, allowing plaintiff to re-assert the claim as one for breach of the duty of loyalty.

The court overruled the demurrer to the fifth cause of action for violation of Uniform Trade Secrets Act, finding that plaintiff has stated the ultimate facts necessary to maintain the claim.

The court ordered plaintiff to file a Second Amended Complaint within 30 days, consistent with this ruling.

The court also addressed the demurrer to the fourth cause of action for economic interference, sustaining it and granting leave to amend.
