This is a tentative ruling by Judge William A. Crowfoot regarding a motion to compel arbitration in the case of Shaina Smith vs. Inter-Con Security Systems, Inc. (Case No. 23AHCV01897). The case involves allegations of wrongful termination in violation of public policy, retaliation, violations of the Labor Code, and unfair competition. The defendant, Inter-Con Security Systems, Inc., filed a motion to compel arbitration, which the plaintiff, Shaina Smith, disputes.

The judge finds that the preponderance of the evidence shows that Shaina Smith signed the arbitration agreement electronically through Taleo's secure website. Although Shaina Smith claims that she does not recall signing the agreement, the judge finds no grounds for invalidating the agreement. The judge grants the motion to compel arbitration and stays the action pending the completion of arbitration. A status conference regarding arbitration is set for June 4, 2024.
