And here is the English-text summary:

The Judge's tentative ruling grants the Petition to Declare Mobilehome Abandoned filed by Garden West Estates against Mizell Jones and the City of Gardena. The Petition seeks a court order deeming the mobilehome located at 17700 S Western Avenue, Space 66, Gardena, California to be abandoned and awarding rent due on the mobilehome site from June 5, 2023 to the present. The Judge finds that the mobilehome has not been occupied since June 2023, no rent has been paid for the premises, and a reasonable person would believe the mobilehome to be abandoned. The Judge awards damages in the amount of $6,628.68 principal and $225.00 costs to Garden West Estates. The Judge orders Garden West Estates to file and serve a proposed judgment within 20 days of this order.
