This is an unlawful detainer action brought by 1615 Westgate LLC against Austin Grant and Jonah Brustein, seeking to recover possession of the Premises located at 1615 S. Westgate Ave #2, Los Angeles, California. The defendants demurred to the complaint on the ground that it was not verified. The court finds that the complaint is verified by Diana Mandel, the managing member for the plaintiff, and therefore, there is no basis for the demurrer. The court overrules the demurrer and allows the complaint to proceed.
