TENTATIVE 
 
The Court excuses the
personal appearance of Claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear
by telephone or video conference call. 
 
Plaintiffs agreed to settle
their claims against Defendants for the total amount of $85,000, with $5,000
being apportioned to minor claimant Emily Pineda
(“Claimant”). If the settlement is approved, the entirety of the $5,000 will be
delivered to Genesis Garcia, the parent and guardian ad litem of Claimant.
 
The Court reviews the
settlement and finds it fair and reasonable. Based on the foregoing, the
petition is GRANTED.
 
Moving party is ordered to give
notice.
