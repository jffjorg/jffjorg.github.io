Plaintiff Sofgia Zermoglio seeks to compel further responses to her special interrogatories, set one, from Defendant LA Opinion, LP. Defendant contends it is willing to supplement its responses and would like to continue to meet and confer.At this time it seems that an order to compel responses would be premature.  The Motion to Compel is, thus, denied without prejudice.  The Court declines to award sanctions at this time.  The parties are to continue meeting and conferring. Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
