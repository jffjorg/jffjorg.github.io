The tentative ruling is in response to a demurrer and a motion to strike portions of the first amended complaint filed by defendants AQP Property Management and Station 11, LLC. The motion challenges the sufficiency of the allegations in the complaint, specifically regarding habitability violations, discrimination, and punitive damages. The court holds that the defendants' demurrer to the fifth cause of action, alleging a violation of the Rosenthal Fair Debt Collection Practices Act, is sustained with leave to amend. The court also grants the motion to strike the punitive damages allegations with leave to amend, finding that the plaintiffs failed to provide specific facts to support their claims. The court orders the plaintiffs to file an amended complaint within 10 days and requires the defendants to provide notice of the order and file proof of service.
