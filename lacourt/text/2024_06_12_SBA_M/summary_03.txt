This tentative ruling is related to a partition action involving two properties owned by Plaintiff and Defendants Benedict Cockburn and Elizabeth Wood. The Plaintiff seeks dissolution and declaratory relief with respect to Greene Family Partners LLC. The Defendant demurred to the First Amended Complaint, arguing that the causes of action fail to state facts sufficient to state a cause of action. The Judge sustained the demurrer in part, and sustained with 20 days leave to amend in part. The Judge also granted the Defendant's request for judicial notice and denied the Defendant's Motion to Strike Portions of First Amended Complaint. The Judge ordered the Defendant to give notice of this ruling.
