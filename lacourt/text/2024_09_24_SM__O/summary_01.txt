The Judge has made a tentative ruling in the case of Gagliano v. The Beverly-Westwood Condo Homeowners Assoc., et al. The Defendants have filed a demurrer with motion to strike the Plaintiff's Statement of Claim (SAC). The Judge has sustained the demurrer with leave to amend as to the first four causes of action, which seek to enforce the Defendant HOA's current duties to the members of the Association. However, the Judge has overruled the demurrer as to the fifth, sixth, seventh, eighth, and ninth causes of action, which seek monetary damages for claims that accrued while the Plaintiff was an owner and member of the HOA, including claims of negligence, private nuisance, breach of settlement agreement, and declaratory relief.
