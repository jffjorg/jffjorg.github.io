  Case Number:  23PSCV02887    Hearing Date:   March 12, 2024    Dept:  O Tentative Ruling
 
(1)  
GENERAL MOTORS LLC’S DEMURRER TO PLAINTIFF’S COMPLAINT is MOOT
because a FAC was filed. 
(2)  
GENERAL MOTORS LLC’S MOTION TO STRIKE PUNITIVE DAMAGES FROM PLAINTIFF’S
COMPLAINT is MOOT. 
 
Background
 
This is a lemon law case. 
 
On September 19, 2023, Plaintiff filed suit. 
 
On November 27, 2023, Defendant filed the instant demurrer
and MTS. 
 
On February 28, 2024, Plaintiff filed a first amended
complaint (FAC).
 
Discussion
 
As a FAC was filed, the demurrer and MTS are moot.
