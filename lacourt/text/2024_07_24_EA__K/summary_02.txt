Summary:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer to the second amended cross-complaint filed by Intelligent Express Inc. and Xiaojun Xu against Wanda Moving, Inc. and 2Cube Global Inc. The demurrer was sustained as to the first and fourth causes of action, which alleged breach of contract and breach of implied covenant of good faith and fair dealing, respectively. The court found that the allegations did not sufficiently plead the existence of written and/or oral contracts.

The court also granted the motion to strike certain portions of the second amended cross-complaint, including allegations of oppressive, fraudulent, or malicious conduct. The court found that the allegations did not support a claim of oppressive, fraudulent, or malicious conduct.

The next hearing is scheduled for July 24, 2024.
