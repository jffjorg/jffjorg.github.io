Background
This
action for partition of real property was filed by Plaintiffs Vanessa Leal and
Raul Gomez on October 4, 2021. On January 6, 2023, Defendant/Cross Complainant
Guadalupe Maria Corona filed the subject Second Amended Cross-Complaint.

Interest to Defendant on December 7, 2023. Thus, this Motion shall be continued
to that hearing so that the Court may first determine whether Laura Corona is
the appropriate successor in interest.
