The court has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel a third-party accounting firm, Green Accounting and Consulting (GAC), to comply with a deposition subpoena for production of business records. The court also denies the defendants' request for monetary sanctions against GAC. The court orders the defendants to give notice of this order.
