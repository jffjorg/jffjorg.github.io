 Case Number:  23CHCV02247    Hearing Date:   November 12, 2024    Dept:  F51  NOVEMBER 8,  2024    MINOR’S COMPROMISE  Los Angeles Superior Court Case # 23CHCV02247    Petition filed: 10/17/24    MINOR: Ameenah Richards (“Claimant”) GAL: Gloria Muniz DEFENDANT: KW Santa Clarita Townhomes, LLC; and  Greystar California, Inc. (collectively, “Defendants”)   RELIEF REQUESTED: Approval of the compromise  of the claim of the minor.¿    TENTATIVE RULING: The petition is granted.    SUMMARY OF ACTION: Plaintiff/Claimant Ameenah  Richards is a minor, bringing this personal injury action against Defendants  through her parent and guardian ad litem Gloria Muniz. Claimant was injured by  a bedbug infestation at Defendants’ apartment complex, discovered in June 2022.   Claimant suffered bedbug bites, and received no treatment. (Pet. ¶¶ 6–7.) Claimant has recovered completely from her  injuries, with no permanent injuries. (Id. at ¶ 8.)   SETTLEMENT: Defendants to pay Claimant $4,000.00.   MEDICAL EXPENSES: None.   ATTORNEY FEES: $1,000.00 to be paid from settlement  proceeds.   OTHER COSTS/EXPENSES: None.   AMOUNT TO BE PAID TO MINOR: $3,000.00, to be deposited  in insured accounts in one or more financial institutions in this state,  subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the court.   CONCLUSION: The petition is granted.
