  Case Number:  21STCV26276    Hearing Date:   November 18, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On July
16, 2021, Plaintiff Rima Wright filed this action against Defendants William
McDaniels (“McDaniels”) and Does 1-20 for motor vehicle tort and general
negligence. 
On
February 3, 2023, Plaintiffs Rima Wright, Kianna Pamperin by and through her
guardian ad litem Adrianna Pamperin, Archille
Pamperin by and through his guardian ad litem Adrianna Pamperin, and Michael
Pamperin by and through his guardian ad litem Adrianna Pamperin filed a first
amended complaint against McDaniels and Does 1-20 for negligence. 
On February
9, 2023, the Court appointed Adrianna Pamperin to serve as Plaintiff Kianna Pamperin’s guardian ad litem. 
On
February 14, 2023, the Court appointed Adrianna
Pamperin to serve as guardian ad litem for Plaintiffs Archille Pamperin and
Michael Pamperin. 
On
May 9, 2023, McDaniels filed an answer. 
On
May 1, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On
August 30, 2024, Petitioner Adrianna Pamperin (“Petitioner”) filed petitions
for expedited approval of the compromises of the claims of minor Plaintiffs
Kianna Pamperin, Archille Pamperin, and Michael Pamperin. 
PETITIONER’S REQUESTS 
Petitioner
asks the Court for expedited approval of the compromises of the claims of minor
Plaintiffs Kianna Pamperin, Archille Pamperin, and Michael Pamperin. 
DISCUSSION 
The
petitions contain the following information for each Plaintiff: 
Gross
settlement amount:                    $1,500.00
Total
medical expenses:                       $0
Medical
expenses paid:                        $0
Reductions:                                          $0
To
be paid from proceeds:              $0
Liens:                                                   $0
Attorney’s
fees:                                   $375.00
Costs:                                                   $155.76
Net
balance:                                         $969.24
 Petitioner
has submitted complete petitions with all required information.  The Court grants the petitions.  For each Plaintiff, a check for $969.24 is to
be paid or delivered to Petitioner at 13523 Norton Avenue, Chino, California,
91710, on the terms and under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections
3401-3402, without bond. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor
Plaintiff Kianna Pamperin’s action filed by Petitioner Adrianna Pamperin on
August 30, 2024.  A check for $969.24 is
to be paid or delivered to Petitioner Adrianna
Pamperin at 13523 Norton Avenue, Chino, California, 91710, on the terms and
under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without
bond. 
The

Plaintiff Archille Pamperin’s action filed by Petitioner Adrianna Pamperin on
August 30, 2024.  A check for $969.24 is
