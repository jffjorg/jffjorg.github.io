 Case Number:  21STCV29381    Hearing Date:   February 29, 2024    Dept:  30 MADDOX MANN vs RAMON JARAMILLO, et al.TENTATIVEThe Court excuses the personal appearance of the claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.The Court has reviewed the Petition to Approve Compromise of Disputed Claim of a Minor (Maddox Mann, Age 9). The Court finds the settlement is reasonable, however the following issues preclude the granting of the petition at this time:(1) The Court has considered the Declaration in support of the fee request. The Court finds that on the current record, and in consideration of the factors set forth in Cal Rules of Court, Rule 7.955(b), a fee of 25% of the gross settlement is reasonable.(2) Not all medical expenses are listed on the proposed order and $673.94 is not listed on the petition as a medical expense.Accordingly, the petition is CONTINUED. Counsel is ordered to file a new proposed order correcting the defects.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
