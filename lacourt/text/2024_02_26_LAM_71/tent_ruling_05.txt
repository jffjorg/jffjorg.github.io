Plaintiff Cargo
Express Freight Corp.’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents
submitted in support of a default judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On November 28,
2022, Cargo Express Freight Corp. (“Plaintiff”) filed this action against
Defendant Next Generation Logistics, Inc. dba ABC Depot Logistics (“Defendant”)
for breach of contract, quantum meruit, open book account, account stated, and
common counts. 
On October 17,
2023, the clerk entered default against Defendant. On January 25, 2024,
Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be heard on February 26, 2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendant and award Plaintiff $56,986.41 consisting of
$47,562.91 as the demand of the complaint, $6,737.95 in prejudgment interest at
the annul rate of 10%, $1,816.86 in attorney fees, and $868.69 in costs.
 
Discussion
Plaintiff
submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments.
The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants
Plaintiff’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application
for default judgment filed on January 25, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $56,986.41 is entered
against Defendant.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
