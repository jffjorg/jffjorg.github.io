And here is the English text summarizing the judge's tentative ruling:

The plaintiff, Leticia Martinez, filed a motion to compel the defendant, Bollywood Bites, Inc., to respond to special interrogatories and requested sanctions for the defendant's failure to timely respond to discovery requests. The court finds that the defendant failed to timely respond to the propounded discovery requests and that the plaintiff is entitled to an order compelling the defendant to serve verified responses to the discovery requests without objections. The court awards sanctions of $500.00 against the defendant and counsel for their misuse of the discovery process. The defendant is ordered to serve verified responses to the special interrogatories within 20 days of service of this order, and is jointly and severally liable to pay the sanctions to the plaintiff's counsel within 20 days.
