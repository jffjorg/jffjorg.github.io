 Case Number:  23STLC02679    Hearing Date:   May 16, 2024    Dept:  26 TENTATIVE RULING:
 
            
Plaintiff Richard Heng Taing dba
USA Donuts’ Motion to Compel Attendance at Deposition and Request for Sanctions
is PLACED OFF CALENDAR AS MOOT.
 
  
Taing v. Worldwide ATM, Inc., et al.MOTION TO
COMPEL DEPOSITION; MOTION TO DEEM RFAS ADMITTED
(CCP §§ 2025.450, 2033.280)
ANALYSIS:
 
Richard Heng Taing dba USA Donuts (“Plaintiff”) filed the
instant action against Defendant Worldwide ATM, Inc. (“Defendant”) on April 24,
2023. On March 5, 2024, the Court granted defense counsel’s motion to be
relieved as counsel. (Minute Order, 03/05/24.) 
 
On April 17, 2024, Plaintiff filed the instant Motion to
Compel Attendance at Deposition and Request for Sanctions. On May 7, 2024, at
the order to show cause regarding legal representation for Defendant, there was
appearance by Defendant and the Court struck its answer. (Minute Order,
05/07/24.) The Court Order to Show Cause Re: Default/Default Judgment for
November 5, 2024. Accordingly, the instant Motion is placed off calendar as
moot.
