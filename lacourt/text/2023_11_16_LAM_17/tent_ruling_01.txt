Plaintiff’s
motion is DENIED.
 
On
12/15/2020, Leo Cono (Plaintiff) initiated this Complaint against eighteen
various individuals and entities. 
 
            Now,
Plaintiff moves for an entry of default and service by publication and/or
substitute service.
 
            The
Court already denied Plaintiff’s motion for an order of entry of default and
service by publication and/or substitute service on 1/12/2022. Moreover, on
12/15/2022, the Court dismissed this action pursuant to its discretion under
CCP section 583.420. 
 
Based on the
foregoing, Plaintiff’s motion is denied.
 
 
It is so ordered.
