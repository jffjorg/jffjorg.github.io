And here is the English-text summary:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Casillas v. Merrilee's, Inc. dba Merrilee's Swimwear. The Defendant's demurrer to the Complaint has been sustained with 20 days' leave to amend. The Complaint alleges a single violation of the Unruh Civil Rights Act, claiming that the Defendant's website is not fully accessible to screen-reading technology and that the Plaintiff was deterred from transacting business as a result. The Judge has found that the Complaint fails to allege sufficient facts regarding the nexus between the website and physical locations, and that it does not specify the deficiencies in the website that impeded the Plaintiff's access. The Plaintiff has been granted leave to amend the Complaint to address these deficiencies.
