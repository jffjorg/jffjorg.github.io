The court has issued a tentative ruling on Defendant Rosa Y. Jou's motion to compel further responses to discovery. The court grants Defendant's motion to compel Plaintiffs' further responses to Defendant's Requests for Production of Documents and Special Interrogatories. Specifically, the court orders Plaintiffs Nicolitsa Pitsos and Georgios Pitsos to serve further, full and complete answers to Defendant's Special Interrogatories and to produce all documents and things in their possession, custody, or control that are responsive to Defendant's Requests for Production of Documents. The court also grants Defendant's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $4,875.
