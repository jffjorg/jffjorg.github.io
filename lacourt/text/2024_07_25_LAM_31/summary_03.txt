* Defendants filed a Motion to Quash Service of Summons, alleging that service was not proper

**Legal Standard**

* Personal service may be accomplished by personally delivering a copy of the summons and complaint to the person to be served
* Substitute service may be effected by leaving a copy of the summons and complaint at the person's dwelling house, usual place of abode, usual place of business, or usual mailing address
* A summons in an action for unlawful detainer of real property may be served by posting if the party to be served cannot be served in any other manner
