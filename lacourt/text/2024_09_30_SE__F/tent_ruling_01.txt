  Case Number:  21NWCV00501    Hearing Date:   September 30, 2024    Dept:  F Gutierrez v. Nekola 21NWCV00501
 
This
construction defect action was filed on August 3, 2021. Trial is currently
scheduled for October 24, 2024. Plaintiff moves ex parte for a 90 day
continuance of trial to a date on or after February 2025, and for this Court to
re-set an MSC date. 
 
Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Application to continue trial is GRANTED pursuant to CRC Rule
3.1332(c)(1) - the unavailability of an essential lay or expert witness because
of death, illness, or other excusable circumstances. A 90 day continuance,
depending on the Court and parties' availabilities, is reasonable. Discovery and
related deadlines will be tied to the new trial date. The Court also finds good
cause to re-set the MSC date. The Court will confer with the parties to select
new MSC, FSC, and Trial dates. The Court notes that the parties may seek to
advance the hearing dates on the Motion for Protective Order and Motion to
Compel Mediation in Dept. SE-C, if necessary. Moving Party to give
notice.
