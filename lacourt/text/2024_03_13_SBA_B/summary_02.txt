This is a tentative ruling issued by a Superior Court Judge in a case between plaintiffs Ostralia J. Mathews and A'Myah Jackson, and defendants Coastline Real Estate Advisors, Inc., et al. The plaintiffs are seeking to authorize substituted service on defendant Realty Holdings PSJC LLC, a California limited liability company, by delivering process to the California Secretary of State. The court has considered the moving papers and found that the plaintiffs have shown reasonable diligence in attempting to personally serve the defendant, having made 12 attempts between August 24, 2023 and December 22, 2023. The court has granted the motion and authorized substituted service on the defendant.
