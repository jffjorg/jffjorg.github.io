The plaintiff, Fox Holdings LLC, is seeking a default judgment against the defendant, Triller Inc., for a total of $181,031.47. The court has reviewed the evidence presented and has found that the plaintiff has not properly complied with the requirements for a default judgment. Specifically, the plaintiff has failed to provide a declaration of reasonable diligence or declaration of mailing with the proof of service, and has not stated the statutory or contractual basis for the attorneys' fees claimed. The court has denied the plaintiff's request for default judgment without prejudice. The matter will proceed to a hearing on October 26, 2023.
