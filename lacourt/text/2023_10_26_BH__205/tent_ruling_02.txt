 Case Number:  22SMCV01934    Hearing Date:   October 26, 2023    Dept:  205    Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles – West District  Beverly Hills Courthouse / Department 205  LEO DAVID,    Plaintiff, v.  AURICE VELOSO,   Defendant.    Case No.:  22SMCV01934     Hearing Date:  October 26, 2023   [TENTATIVE] ORDER RE:   PLAINTIFF LEO DAVID’S MOTION TO    COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO    FORM INTERROGATORIES, SPECIAL    INTERROGATORIES, REQUESTS FOR    ADMISSIONS AND REQEUSTS FOR    PRODUCTION OF DOCUMENTS AND    REQUESTS FOR SANCTIONS     AURICE VELOSO,    Cross-Complainant, v.  LEO DAVID, et al.,   Defendants.            This Department’s rules require that before a discovery motion is filed, the parties are to attend an Informal Discovery Conference (IDC).  No IDC was held on Plaintiff Leo David’s Motion to Compel Further Responses to Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, Requests for Production and Requests for Admissions.  Plaintiff asserts that Defendant Aurice Veloso would not provide dates for an IDC.  Defendant is admonished to cooperate with Plaintiff in scheduling an IDC.  Defendant must provide available dates and times for an IDC within 5 days of this Order.  The Court denies Plaintiff’s motions to compel without prejudice.  DATED: October 26, 2023    ___________________________ Edward B. Moreton, Jr. Judge of the Superior Court
