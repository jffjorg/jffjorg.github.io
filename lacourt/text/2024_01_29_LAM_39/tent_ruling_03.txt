 Case Number:  21STCV39136    Hearing Date:   January 29, 2024    Dept:  39 Chagit Yamini, et  al. v. Inner City Skyline, Inc., et al. Case No.  21STCV39136 Motion to Compel  Plaintiff Limor Rad’s Deposition  THIS HEARING IS BEING CONTINUED.  THE PARTIES SHOULD NOT APPEAR ON MONDAY.               Plaintiffs  Chagit Yamini and Limor Rad filed this employment case against Inner City  Skyline, Inc., Novel Remodeling, Inc., and David Partiel.  Defendants move to compel the deposition of  Limor Rad, which is set for hearing on January 29, 2024.  Defendants also move to compel the deposition  of Chagit Yamini, which is set for hearing on April 4, 2024.  The Court orders as follows:               1.         The hearing on Defendants’ motion to  compel the deposition of Chagit Yamini is continued to April 3, 2024, at 8:30  a.m.  The Court will hear both motions at  once if necessary.                 2.         The Court orders the parties to  meet-and-confer via telephone (not email) and to schedule the depositions of  Plaintiffs.               3.         The Court orders the parties to file a  joint statement on or before February 9, 2024, informing the Court when the  depositions of each plaintiff will occur.               4.         The Court orders Defendants’ counsel to  take the motions off-calendar or file a notice on or before March 8, 2024,  informing the Court what issues the Court must resolve on April 3, 2024.  If Defendants’ counsel does not comply with  this order, the Court will take the motions off-calendar.                   5.         The Court’s clerk shall provide  notice.
