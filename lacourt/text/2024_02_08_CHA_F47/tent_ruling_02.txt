MOTION TO
COMPEL
(Requests for
Production of Documents, Set 1)
 
Motion filed on 9/25/23.
 
MOVING PARTY: Defendant J.C.I. Construction, Inc.
RESPONDING PARTY: Plaintiff Sherine Hanna
NOTICE: ok
 
RELIEF REQUESTED: An order compelling Plaintiff
Sherine Hanna to provide responses to Defendant 
J.C.I.’s Request for Production of Documents, Set 1, within 10 days
after the entry of the order granting this motion.  Additionally, J.C.I. requests sanctions
against Plaintiff and Plaintiff’s counsel, Karimian Law Group, in the amount of
$1,497.50.
 
RULING: The motion is granted as set forth
below.  
 
On 3/28/23, Defendant J.C.I. Construction, Inc. (J.C.I.)
served Requests for Production of Documents, Set 1, on Plaintiff making
responses due on 5/2/23.  (Gaines Decl.,
Ex.1).  On 4/26/23, J.C.I. granted
Plaintiff a 60-day extension to respond to the discovery.  Id. 
Plaintiff failed to provide responses. 
Id.  After further meet and
confer efforts between counsel for the parties, Plaintiff still failed to
provide responses to the discovery.  Id.
 
Due to Plaintiff’s failure to provide timely responses to
the subject document requests, J.C.I. is entitled to an order compelling
responses without objections.  CCP
2031.300(a), (b).  Additionally,
Defendant is entitled to sanctions against Plaintiff and her attorney for their
failure to comply with their discovery obligations.  CCP 2023.030(a); CCP 2031.300(c).  J.C.I. is awarded $922.50 in sanctions for 1
hour to prepare the motion + ½ hour to prepare for and appear at the hearing at
$575/hour + $60 filing fee.
 
CONCLUSION
 
The motion is granted. 
Responses are due and sanctions are payable within 15 days.
