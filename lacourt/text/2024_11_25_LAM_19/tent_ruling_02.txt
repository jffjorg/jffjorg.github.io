LLC v. L.V.
No. 24STCP033161
 
TENTATIVE
RULING 
On the
Court’s own motion, the Hearing on Petition Notice of Hearing on Petition for
Approval for Transfer of Payment Rights is CONTINUED to February 14, 2025, at
8:30 a.m., in Department 19 of the Stanley Mosk Courthouse. 
The Court has
reviewed the First Amended Verified Petition For Approval For Transfer Of
Payment Rights and First Amended Declaration Of Payee, filed on October 29,
2024, but does not make a ruling at this time because a Notice of Related Case
has been filed in Case Nos. 20BBC00367 and 21BBCP00373 seeking to relate Case
Nos. 20BBC00367 and 21BBCP00373 with the instant matter and with each other. 
However, the
Notice of Related Case was not filed in the instant matter as required by
California Rules of Court, rule 3.300, subdivision (d). 
The Court
continues the hearing to give Department B the opportunity to rule on the
Notice of Related Case. Plaintiff should follow up regarding the outstanding
Notice of Related Case.  
In the
meantime, the Court orders counsel for Petitioner to file the Notice of Related
Case in the instant action within 5 court days. 
The Court
sets a Non-Appearance Case Review Re: Notice of Related Case, Case Nos.
20BBC00367 and 21BBCP00373 for December 13, 2024, at 10:30 A.M. in Department
19 of the Stanley Mosk Courthouse. 
Counsel for
Petitioner J.G. Wentworth Originations, LLC to give notice to all interested
parties.
