            The Court
posts this tentative order on Friday, February 2, 2024.  The Court provides notice: If Defendants’
counsel does not request a hearing by emailing the Court’s clerk, Roberto
Mendoza, on or before Monday, February 5, 2024, at 8:30 a.m., the Court will
take the hearing off-calendar and will grant the motion.  The Court’s clerk may be reached at:
SMCDept39@LACourt.org.  
 
            Plaintiffs
MHJ Group, Inc. and Kai Hou Liang obtained a judgment against Defendants Ji Li
and AA Sunrise, Inc. in the total amount of $7.7 million.  Now, Plaintiffs seek a charging order against
Defendant AA Sunrise, Inc.’s interest in Yorba Linda Gardens, LLC.  Defendants filed no opposition to this
motion, and the Court finds no basis in the record to deny this motion.  Therefore, good cause having been shown, the
Court grants the motion and signs the proposed order.  Plaintiffs’ counsel shall provide notice and
file proof of such with the Court.
