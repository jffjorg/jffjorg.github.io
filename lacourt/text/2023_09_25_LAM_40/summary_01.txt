Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Javier Alarcon Munoz v. Juvenal Alejandro Farias, Bargain Rent-A-Car, Schoolsfirst Insurance Services, LLC, Ivonne Maria Guerrero, Mercury Insurance Company, Mercury General Corporation, and Does 5-50. The plaintiff, Munoz, is seeking to deem as admitted the genuineness of documents and the truth of matters specified in requests for admission (Set 1) against the defendants Schoolsfirst Insurance Services, LLC and Ivonne Maria Guerrero. The judge has granted the motion, deeming the genuineness of any documents and the truth of any matters specified in Request for Admission (Set 1) against the defendants admitted. The judge has also awarded sanctions to the plaintiff in the amount of $1,867.34, which includes costs and attorney's fees. The defendants are ordered to remit these sanctions to the plaintiff within 14 days of the ruling.
