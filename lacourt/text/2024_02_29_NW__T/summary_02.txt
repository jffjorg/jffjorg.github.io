The judge has issued a tentative ruling denying Defendant Francis L. Perera's claim of exemption to prevent Plaintiff Creditors from enforcing a judgment worth $38,281.21, which has grown to $41,151.49 due to interest. The defendant claimed that some of the funds in his bank account are exempt from enforcement under California and federal laws, including the paid earnings exemption and the financial hardship exemption. However, the judge found that the defendant did not provide sufficient evidence to support his claims, and that the funds in his bank account are not wages or earnings. The judge also found that the defendant's financial hardship exemption was not persuasive when considering all of the defendant's and his spouse's assets, not just his liquid assets. As a result, the defendant's claim of exemption is denied.
