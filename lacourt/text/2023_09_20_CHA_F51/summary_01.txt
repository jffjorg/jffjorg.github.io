The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to compel deposition filed by Petitioner Interinsurance Exchange of the Automobile Club. The motion seeks to compel Respondent William H. Bennett, Jr. to appear in person for a deposition, as well as $1,246.45 in monetary sanctions. As a result, the judge has not granted the motion to compel deposition at this time.
