The judge has issued a tentative ruling on two motions filed by the plaintiff, Seran Ng, D.M.D., Inc. The first motion is to compel further responses to requests for production of documents, set 11, and for monetary sanctions. The second motion is to compel further responses to form interrogatories, set 10, and for monetary sanctions.

The judge has denied the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents, set 11, number 154 because the defendant's representation of her inability to comply with that demand is not incomplete, inadequate, or evasive. The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents, set 11, numbers 155-159 because the objections in the responses are without merit.

The judge has denied the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents, set 11, number 160 because the request is overbroad. The judge has denied the plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions against the defendant.

The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to form interrogatories, set 10, number 17.1 (as to Requests for Admission, numbers 106-113) because the subject interrogatory responds to Requests for Admission, set Nine. The judge has denied the plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions.

The judge has ordered the defendant to serve further written responses that comply with Code of Civil Procedure sections 2031.210-2031.250 to the plaintiff's requests for production of documents, set 11, numbers 155-159, and to produce all documents and things in her possession, custody, or control which are responsive to those requests, within 20 days of the date of service of the order.

The judge has ordered the defendant to serve further, full and complete answers to the plaintiff's form interrogatories, set 10, number 17.1 as to Requests for Admission, set Nine, numbers 106-113, which comply with Code of Civil Procedure sections 2030.210-2030.250, within 20 days of the date of service of the order.
