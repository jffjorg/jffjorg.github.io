 Case Number:  23PSCV02982    Hearing Date:   February 29, 2024    Dept:  K Plaintiff
La Verne Business Center’s Application for Default Judgment is GRANTED;
however, attorney’s fees are reduced from $1,889.00 to $1,781.00 [see
below]. Plaintiff is to provide the court with an amended proposed judgment
at or before the time of the hearing. 
Background   
This is an
unlawful detainer action involving the commercial property located at 2986 1st
Street, Suites #C and #D, La Verne, California 91750.
 
On September
25, 2023, Plaintiff filed a complaint for Unlawful Detainer against Steven
Allan Young, Steven Allan Young III (together, “Defendants”) and Does I-III.
 
On November 16, 2023, Defendants’ defaults were entered. On
November 27, 2023, a clerk’s default judgment for possession only was entered.
A Case Management Conference is set for February 29, 2024.
Discussion
Plaintiff’s Application for Default Judgment is granted; however,
attorney’s fees are reduced to $1,781.00 (i.e., $690 (+) 3% of $36,394.40).
(Local Rule 3.214, subd. (a).) Plaintiff is to provide the court with an
amended proposed judgment at or before the time of the hearing.
