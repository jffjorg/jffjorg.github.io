Ruling:                               MOTION TO CONTINUE TRIAL IS DENIED
 
 
CCP § 1005(b)
provides “all moving and supporting papers shall be served and filed at least
16 court days before the hearing.  
 
CCP §
1010.6(a)(3)(B) provides “Any period of notice, or any right or duty to do any
act or make any response within any period or on a date certain after the
service of the document, which time period or date is prescribed by statute or
rule of court, shall be extended after service by electronic means by two court
days.”    
 
The present
motion was filed on March 27, 2024, with a hearing date of April 17, 2024.
However, pursuant to the California Code of Civil Procedure, the earliest
permissible date to hear this motion is April 23, 2024. Therefore, the motion
is denied without prejudice.
