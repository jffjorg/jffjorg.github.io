Ruling:                               MOTION FOR TERMINATING, EVIDENTIARY SANCTONS AND ISSUE PRECLUSION
IS DENIED.
 
CCP § 1005(b) provides “all moving and supporting
papers shall be served and filed at least 16 court days before the hearing. 
 
CCP § 1010.6(a)(3)(B) Provides “Any period of
notice, or any right or duty to do any act or make any response within any
period or on a date certain after the service of the document, which time
period or date is prescribed by statute or rule of court, shall be extended
after service by electronic means by two court days.”¿  
 
The present motion was filed on April 2, 2024,
with a hearing date of April 17, 2024. However, pursuant to the California Code
of Civil Procedure, that hearing date provides insufficient notice. Therefore,
the motion is denied without prejudice.
