The tentative ruling denies the plaintiff's motion to stay the proceedings in a subrogation action brought against Super Center Concepts, Inc. The plaintiff, The Complete Logistics Company, seeks to stay the proceedings until the conclusion of the workers' compensation claim of its employee, Lydia Gomez. Gomez was allegedly injured while making a delivery at a Super Center Concepts, Inc. grocery store. The trial was set for July 29, 2024, but was continued to February 13, 2025. The court finds that there is little prejudice to the plaintiff if the stay is denied, as the parties will still be able to continue discovery and proceed with the case. The court exercises its discretion to deny the motion to stay proceedings.
