 Case Number:  21STCV10864    Hearing Date:   January 16, 2024    Dept:  30 SEUNGSHIK HAN, et al. vs ARCADIA OPCO LLC, A DELAWARE LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY, et al.TENTATIVEDefendant Arcadia Opco LLC dba Embassy Suites Hotel’s motions to compel Plaintiff Seung Shik Han to provide responses to form interrogatories, and requests for production for documents are GRANTED.Plaintiff Seung Shik Han is ordered to provide responses without objection to Defendant’s request for form interrogatories, set one, and request for production, set one, within 20 days of this order.Moving party is ordered to give notice.DiscussionOn July 3, 2023, Defendant served Form Interrogatories, Set One, and Request for Production of Documents, Set One, on Plaintiff Seung. (Eldridge Decl., Exhs. A.) Responses were not provided despite multiple extensions being granted to respond. (Id., ¶¶ 4-8.)As Defendant properly served discovery requests and Plaintiff has failed to serve responses, the Court finds Defendant is entitled to a court order directing Plaintiff to provide verified responses without objections to the discovery requests served on Plaintiff. Therefore, the motions are granted.
