The court has granted the motion to approve the Stipulated Consent Judgment between plaintiff Keep America Safe and Beautiful, Inc. and defendants Janico, Inc. and Aramsco, Inc. The judgment was reached after a settlement was reached between the parties, which includes a warning label warning consumers of the presence of DEHP, a chemical known to cause cancer and reproductive harm. The court found that the warning label complies with Proposition 65 and that the amount allocated for attorneys' fees and costs in the Consent Judgment is reasonable. The court also found that the civil penalty amount provided for in the Consent Judgment is reasonable.
