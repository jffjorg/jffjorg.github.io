This is the Judge's tentative ruling on the unopposed motions to compel filed by the Defendant, Whole Foods Market California, Inc., ET AL. The Defendant is seeking to compel the Plaintiff, Nico Quiros, to respond to discovery requests that were propounded on August 17, 2022, and to pay sanctions for failing to respond. The Plaintiff failed to respond to the discovery requests despite being properly served and despite a meet and confer letter being sent by the Defendant. The Court has granted the Defendant's motions to compel and ordered the Plaintiff to serve verified responses to the discovery requests within 30 days. The Court has also imposed sanctions on the Plaintiff in the amount of $810.
