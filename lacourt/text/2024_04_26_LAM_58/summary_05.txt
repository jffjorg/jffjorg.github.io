The motion to compel arbitration is granted. The court finds that the defendant has carried its burden of demonstrating the existence of a valid, binding arbitration agreement. The plaintiff's claims fall within the scope of the agreement. The court concludes that the defendant has shown that the plaintiff accepted the terms and conditions, including the arbitration provision, multiple times. The plaintiff's claims are within the scope of the arbitration provision in the terms and conditions. The court grants the motion to compel arbitration and stays the matter pending the outcome of the arbitration.
