And here is the summary in English:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling denying the motions of Plaintiffs Kenneth Washington, Kimberly Hagen, and Marley Washington to compel further responses from Defendant City of Hawthorne to Requests for Admission of Genuineness of Documents, Set One, and Form Interrogatories, Set Two. The Plaintiffs also sought monetary sanctions. The Court found that the motions were procedurally defective because the Plaintiffs failed to schedule and complete an Informal Discovery Conference (IDC) before the hearing. Additionally, the Court found that the Plaintiffs failed to make a good faith attempt to resolve the issues through meet and confer. As a result, the Court denied the motions and the request for monetary sanctions.
