Here is the YAML summary of the Tentative Ruling:

**Case Information**
- Case Number: 24STCV11895
- Hearing Date: January 22, 2025
- Dept: 47

**Parties**
- Plaintiff: Roberto Rivas
- Defendant: Ultra Solution, Inc.

**Facts**
- The case involves a wage and hour and whistleblower retaliation action filed on May 10, 2024.
- Plaintiff alleges he was retaliated against and constructively terminated for complaining about unsafe and unlawful working practices.
- Defendant demurs to the first, second, third, eighth, and ninth causes of action in the Complaint.

**Legal Standard**
- A demurrer tests whether the complaint states a cause of action.
- Courts read the allegations liberally and in context.

**Tentative Ruling**
- Defendant's Demurrer to the Complaint is SUSTAINED without leave to amend as to the eighth and ninth causes of action and otherwise OVERRULED.

**First Cause of Action: Whistleblower Retaliation (Labor Code ยง 1102.5)**
- Defendant demurs to the first cause of action for failure to state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action.
- The Complaint alleges that Plaintiff complained of not receiving rest or meal breaks, not receiving full pay for all hours worked, and protesting against instructions to carry overweight cargo.
- The Court overrules the demurrer to this cause of action.

**Second Cause of Action: Violation of Labor Code ยง 6310**
- Defendant demurs to the second cause of action for failure to state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action.
- The Complaint alleges that Plaintiff complained of unsafe working conditions and was instructed to load and carry overweight cargo.
- The Court overrules the demurrer to this cause of action.

**Third Cause of Action: Wrongful Termination**
- Defendant demurs to the third cause of action for wrongful termination as derivative of the first two causes of action.
- The Court overrules the demurrer to this cause of action.

**Eighth Cause of Action: Failure to Provide Wage Statements (Labor Code ยง 226)**
- Defendant demurs to the eighth cause of action for failure to provide wage statements as barred by the one-year statute of limitations.
- The Complaint admits that Plaintiff requested wage statements on December 2, 2022 and April 7, 2023, but delayed filing this action until May 10, 2024.
- The Court sustains the demurrer to this cause of action without leave to amend.

**Ninth Cause of Action: Failure to Provide Personnel Records (Labor Code ยง 1198.5)**
- Defendant demurs to the ninth cause of action for failure to provide personnel records as barred by the one-year statute of limitations.
- The Court sustains the demurrer to this cause of action without leave to amend.

**Uncertainty**
- Defendant demurs to the first three causes of action as uncertain.
- The Court overrules the demurrer to these causes of action.

**Leave to Amend**
- Plaintiff's Complaint is incapable of amendment with respect to the eighth and ninth causes of action, and the Court does not grant leave to amend as to those causes of action.

**Conclusion**
- Defendant's Demurrer to the Complaint is SUSTAINED without leave to amend as to the eighth and ninth causes of action and otherwise OVERRULED.

**Significant Statements**
- "A demurrer tests whether the complaint states a cause of action."
- "Courts read the allegations liberally and in context."
- "The ultimate facts alleged in the complaint must be deemed true, as well as all facts that may be implied or inferred from those expressly alleged."
