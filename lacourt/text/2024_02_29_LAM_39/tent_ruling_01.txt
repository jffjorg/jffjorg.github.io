  Case Number:  21STCV42732    Hearing Date:   February 29, 2024    Dept:  39 Jane Roe v.
Sterling Jewelers, Inc., et al.
Case No.
21STCV42732
Ex Parte
Application
 
            Plaintiff
Jane Roe filed an ex parte application to advance the hearing on her motion to
compel and to continue the hearing on Defendant’s motion for summary
judgment.  The Court grants the ex parte
application and orders as follows:
 
            1.         The Court advances the hearing on
Plaintiff’s motion to compel from May 22, 2024, to April 16, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.
 
            2.         The Court advances and continues the
hearing on Defendant’s motion for summary judgment from May 22, 2024, to June
17, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.
 
            3.         The Court advances and vacates the
final status conference and trial dates. 
The Court shall hold a trial setting conference on June 17, 2024, at
8:30 a.m.  The Court provides notice that
it does not intend to continue the discovery deadlines, which shall remain set
based on a trial date of July 9, 2024.  
 
            4.         Plaintiff’s counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
