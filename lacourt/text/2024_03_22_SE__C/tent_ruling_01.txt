  Case Number:  22NWCV00071    Hearing Date:   March 22, 2024    Dept:  C State Farm General Insurance Company vs Lori
Gonzales, et al., Case No. 22NWCV00071
This is a landlord-tenant dispute. 
Plaintiff State Farm General Insurance applies Ex Parte
for an Order Specially Setting Hearing on Motion to Deem Admitted Plaintiff’s
Requests for Admission, Set One, to Defendant Mark Hassan.
A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative
factual showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other
statutory basis for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).) 
The Hearing for the Motion to Deem Request for Admissions
Admitted is currently set for January 2, 2025. Trial in this action is
scheduled for July 1, 2024. The Court finds that Plaintiff would be irreparably
harmed by not hearing this motion prior to trial. 
Accordingly, Plaintiff State Farm General Insurance’s Ex
Parte Application is GRANTED. The hearing is ADVANCED from Thursday, January 2,
2025 to today’s date and CONTINUED to Thursday, May 23, 2024 in Department
SE-C at 10:30 a.m. 
Plaintiff to give notice.
