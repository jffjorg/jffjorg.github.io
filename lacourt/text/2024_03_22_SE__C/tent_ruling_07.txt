 Case Number:  24NWCV00208    Hearing Date:   March 22, 2024    Dept:  C David Garfinkle vs Universal Fitness Club
Norwalk, LLC, Case No. 24NWCV00208
This is an unlawful detainer action. 
Plaintiff David Garfinkle applies ex parte to advance the
hearing on Defendant’s Motion to Strike the Complaint for Unlawful Detainer. 
A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative
factual showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other
statutory basis for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).) 
Unlawful detainer actions are entitled to an expedited
scheduling. Defendants’ Motion to Strike is currently set for April 18, 2024.
The Court finds Plaintiff would be harmed as they would be deprived of a right
to an expedited summary judgment procedure for regaining possession of his real
property. Accordingly, Plaintiff’s Ex Parte Application to Advance
Hearing on Defendant’s Motion to Strike the Complaint is ADVANCED from
Thursday, April 18, 2024 to this date and CONTINUED to Thursday, April 4,
2024 in Department SE-C at 10:30 a.m. 
Plaintiff to give notice.
