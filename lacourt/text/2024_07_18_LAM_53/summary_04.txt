This is a tentative ruling by Judge Robert B. Broadbelt III dated July 18, 2024, regarding a hearing for case number 23STCV09246. The case involves a motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories and requests for production of documents by plaintiff kenneth t. leonhardt against defendant FCA US LLC. The court grants the motion in part, ordering the defendant to serve further, full, and complete answers to special interrogatories numbers 14, 20, 39, and 43, as well as to produce documents responsive to certain requests for production of documents. The court also grants the motion in part, ordering the defendant to provide further responses to certain requests for production of documents, but denies the motion as to other requests. The ruling is based on the court's evaluation of the responses provided by the defendant and the lack of proof of service for supplemental responses.
