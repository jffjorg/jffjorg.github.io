The Court is continuing the hearings on the petitions for approval of the terms of the compromises. The issues with the petitions include incomplete items and unclear Medi-Cal liens. The Proposed Orders provide for the creation and/or funding of special needs trusts for each minor plaintiff. The Court is also requiring the trustee to file a Notice of Commencement of Proceedings for a Court-Supervised Trust and to provide ongoing review hearings.
