And here is the English-text summary:

This tentative ruling grants the defendants' motion for leave to file a cross-complaint against the plaintiff, Kaizou Group, Inc. The defendants, Sang "Tony" Pan and Julie Pan, are seeking to file a cross-complaint against the plaintiff and Shang Yang Zhao for various claims, including common counts, conversion, accounting, unjust enrichment, and declaratory relief. The defendants allege that they overpaid for goods received from the plaintiff and are seeking to recover the alleged overpayment. The court finds that the defendants acted in good faith in bringing the cross-complaint, as they discovered the evidence for their claims while responding to discovery propounded by the plaintiff. As a result, the court grants the defendants' motion for leave to file the cross-complaint.
