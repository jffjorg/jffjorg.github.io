Soto Capital v. SPA4U, LLC, Case
no. 24SMCV00859
Hearing date November 19, 2024
Defendant’s
Counsel’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel
Plaintiff
Soto Capital, LP sues defendants SPA4U, LLC, Adi Keizman and Michael Mann for
breach of lease and guaranty arising from defendants’ alleged failure to pay
rent. Attorney Cristina M. Saca, counsel for defendant Keizman, moves to be
relieved as counsel citing breach of retainer, lack of cooperation and
unreasonable difficulty. Decl. Saca para. 2.
An
application to be relieved must be made on judicial counsel form MC-051 (Notice
of Motion and Motion) per Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(a), MC-052
(Declaration) per CRC rule 3.1362(c), and MC-053 (Proposed Order) per CRC rule
3.1362(e).¿The proposed order must specify all hearing dates scheduled in the
action or proceeding, including the date of trial, if known. CRC rule
3.1362(e). Defendant Keizmann’s counsel has met all requirements under Cal.
Rules of Court 3.1362.
As
no trial date has been set, there is no prejudice to defendant Keizman.
GRANTED. Counsel Saca to give notice.
