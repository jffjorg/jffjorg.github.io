 On July 26, 2024, Plaintiffs Ava Stone, Camile Cabale, David Benetiz, Rhonda St. John, and Steven Bicknel filed a Second Amended Complaint (“SAC”) in this matter for (1) violation of Gov. Code § 815.6, (2) nuisance, (3) negligence, and (4) violations of the UCL. Plaintiffs complain that the property located at 11966 Wagner Street, Culver City, CA 90203, is illegally functioning as a 10-unit apartment building or “boarding house” when it is subject to R-1 zoning, which requires low density single-family dwellings.  Defendants Arlene Katzman, Trustee of the Katzman Family Trust, Rishelle Maman, Trustee of the Eric Rishelle Maman Revocable Trust, Eric Maman, Trustee of the Eric Rishelle Maman Revocable Trust, Arlene Katzman, Rishelle Maman, Linda Maman, and Eric Maman  now demur to the SAC for uncertainty and failure to state sufficient facts.Previously, the Court sustained the above Defendants’ demurrer to the FAC, with 20 days leave to amend.No opposition was filed to the instant demurrer.  Therefore, the SAC is deemed abandoned.  (Herzberg v. County of Plumas (2005) 133 Cal.App.4th 1, 20.) The Demurrer is sustained, without leave to amend. Moving party to give notice.
