The court has granted Defendant Tesla Motors, Inc.'s motion to compel binding arbitration in the case brought by Plaintiff Juwan Lamar Lewis. The dispute arises from the sale of a Tesla Model Y, which was defective and not properly repaired by Defendant. The court found that Plaintiff had agreed to the arbitration provision in the Motor Vehicle Order Agreement (MVOA) and that the agreement was enforceable.
