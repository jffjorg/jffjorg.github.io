The Court has reviewed the petition
by Petitioner Karina Jimenez (Petitioner) on behalf of Claimant Maxximiliana Luna, age 5. The Court GRANTS
the petition.
 
Petitioner seeks approval of a $350,000 gross settlement
to Claimant.
 
Attorney for Claimant seeks $87,500 in attorney fees
which represents 25% of the gross settlement. The Court finds this to be a
reasonable amount. 
 
Petitioner also provides documentation of the $10,660 in
costs, sought to be paid from the proceeds. 
 
Therefore, Claimant’s net settlement is $251,840, to be
invested in a single-premium deferred annuity, subject to withdrawal only on
authorization of the court. 
Petitioner includes attachment 18b(3) describing the annuity
terms and conditions. 
 
Moving party shall give notice of
the Court’s order and file a proof of service of such.
