MOTIONS
TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO DISCOVERY 



 
MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
James Green
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant Sunflower
Corporation
 
 
This is a wrongful termination action arises from a breach
of a written contract.  On January 10, 2024,
Plaintiff, James Green, filed these motions to compel Defendant, Sunpower
Corporation, to provide further responses to Plaintiff’s First Set of Form
Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, Set One, and Request for Production
of Documents.  The motions are set to be
heard on February 28, 2024 and February 29, 2024.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the hearings of these motions.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearings, currently scheduled for February 28, 2024 and February 29, 2024, to April
29, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the parties to participate in an IDC.  The parties are to review the information for
conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located on the LA Superior Court website for Dept.
31.  Thereafter the parties are to contact the judicial assistant to
schedule an IDC date and time.
