This is a tentative ruling by Judge Kerry Bensinger regarding a motion to deem a request for admissions admitted against Defendant A1 MC Engineering and Shotcrete. The motion is unopposed, and the court finds that the plaintiff properly served the request for admissions on June 25, 2024, but the defendant failed to respond. As a result, the court will deem the request for admissions admitted and impose monetary sanctions on the defendant in the amount of $375. The court also grants the plaintiff's request for sanctions and orders the defendant to pay the sanctions to the plaintiff within 30 days.
