This tentative ruling by Judge Kevin Joseph Breznai addresses an application by Mark G. Simons to appear as counsel pro hac vice to represent the plaintiff, Kevin Joseph Breznai. The application does not comply with the requirements of California Rules of Court, rule 9.40, as there is no proof of service by mail of a copy of the application and notice of hearing on the State Bar of California at its San Francisco office. Therefore, the application is continued to allow counsel to file proof of service on the State Bar in San Francisco of the application and notice of the continued hearing.
