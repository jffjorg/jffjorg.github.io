  Case Number:  22STCV05411    Hearing Date:   January 30, 2024    Dept:  30 ROSA GARCIA-GUERRA, et al. vs JACQUELYN M WOLFETENTATIVEThe Court excuses the personal appearance of the claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.The Court has reviewed the Petition to Approve Compromise of Disputed Claim of a Minor Arturo Malik Garcia (Age 4). The Court finds the settlement is reasonable, however the following issues preclude the granting of the petition at this time:(1) The box for Item 9 of the petition is not checked.(2) Item 12a(5) of the petition states there are no liens, but item 12b5(a)(ii) states there is a lien for $388.10. Item 12a(5) needs to be corrected.(3) Retainer agreement is in Spanish and no translation has been attached. A certified interpreter is necessary.(4) Item 9a of the proposed order is not filled out.Accordingly, the petition is CONTINUED for 60 days to an appearance hearing. Counsel is ordered to correct the issues outlined above, and file a new petition and proposed order granting the petition. All documents must be filed at least five days before the next hearing.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
