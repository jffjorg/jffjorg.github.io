Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling:

The judge has tentatively ruled on a demurrer filed by General Motors, LLC (Defendant) to the Plaintiff's Fifth Cause of Action for fraud. The Plaintiff, Gabriel Santiago, Jr., alleges that General Motors concealed defects in the battery system of a 2019 Chevrolet Volt, which he purchased in 2019. The judge has sustained the demurrer, finding that the Plaintiff's fraud claim is time-barred and insufficiently pled. The judge has also granted the Defendant's motion to strike the Plaintiff's prayer for punitive damages. The Plaintiff has 20 days to amend his complaint.
