Attorney Seth Tangman (“Counsel”) moves to be
admitted pro hac vice as counsel for Defendants
Dallas Martin, Atlantic Records Group, LLC, and Warner Music Group Corporation (“Defendants”).
Pursuant to California Rules of Court, rule
9.40, attorneys who are licensed to practice and in good standing in other
states may, upon court approval, appear as counsel pro hac vice in a
pending case, if an active member of the State Bar of California also appears
as counsel of record.  (Cal. Rules of
Court, rule 9.40, subd. (a).)  Counsel is
licensed to practice and in good standing in Washington, Oregon, and Delaware, and
in federal courts.  Counsel resides in Oregon.  Counsel is associated with Defendants’
counsel, who is licensed to practice in California.  Counsel has applied to appear pro hac vice
in California four times in the past two years.  Further, Counsel has demonstrated that she
served this application on the State Bar of California at its San Francisco office
and paid the $50 fee as required.  (Cal.
Rules of Court, rule 9.40, subds. (c), (e).) 
The motion is granted.  
Defendants are ordered to provide notice of
this order and to file proof of service of same.
