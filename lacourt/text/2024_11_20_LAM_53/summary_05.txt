The Judge has reviewed the proposed settlement agreement between plaintiff Anthony Gonzalez and defendant Almansor Court, Inc. under the Labor Code Private Attorneys General Act of 2004 (PAGA). The settlement agreement provides for a payment of $250,000, with 75% going to the Labor and Workforce Development Agency and 25% to the aggrieved employees. The Judge has considered the terms of the settlement, including the amounts to be paid and the procedures for administration, as well as the declarations of plaintiff's counsel and plaintiff. Based on these factors, the Judge finds that the settlement is fair, reasonable, and adequate. Therefore, the Judge grants plaintiff's motion for approval of the PAGA settlement.
