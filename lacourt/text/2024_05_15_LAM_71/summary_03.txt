The Court grants Plaintiff Thorsten D. Meier's unopposed motion to vacate the arbitration stay and reopen the case in Superior Court. The Court finds that Triller, Inc., Mahi de Silva, and Paul Kahn (collectively, "Defendants") breached the arbitration agreement by failing to timely pay a JAMS arbitration invoice on August 28, 2023, which was due upon receipt. The Court grants Plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $111,186.80 against Defendants, jointly and severally. The Court awards this amount to compensate Plaintiff's counsel for reasonable attorneys' fees and costs incurred as a result of the breach.
