STATEMENT
OF MATERIAL FACTS AND/OR PROCEEDINGS:
            
            This is an action for breach of
contract and fraud that was filed on April 2, 2024. Plaintiff alleges that
Defendants misrepresented their services, incurred excessive costs, and
delivered substandard work on a contract to produce a music video for
Plaintiff. 
 
Plaintiff moves for attorney
Raeesabbas Mohaed to appear as Plaintiff’s counsel pro hac vice.
