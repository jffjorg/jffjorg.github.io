The Judge has granted the Defendant's Motion to Compel Responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One, and Request for Sanctions. The Plaintiff, Bin Yang, is ordered to serve verified responses to the discovery requests without objections within 20 days of service of this order. Additionally, the Plaintiff is ordered to pay sanctions of $285.00 to defense counsel within 20 days of service of this order. The Judge found that the Plaintiff's failure to serve initial responses to the discovery requests constitutes a misuse of the discovery process and that sanctions are appropriate under Code of Civil Procedure sections 2023.010 and 2023.030. The Judge based their decision on the Plaintiff's failure to timely respond to the discovery requests and the Defendant's proper notice of the motion.
