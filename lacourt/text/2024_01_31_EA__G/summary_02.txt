The Judge's tentative ruling grants the petition filed by Zahir Dadabhoy, the guardian ad litem for minor plaintiff A.D., seeking approval of a $50,000 settlement reached between A.D. and the defendants, Waste Management, Inc. and Waste Management of California, Inc. The settlement arose from a motor vehicle collision that occurred on Silver Hawk Drive in Diamond Bar, California, in April 2022. The Judge also approves the request to deposit the funds into an account with Chase Bank, subject to court approval for withdrawal.
