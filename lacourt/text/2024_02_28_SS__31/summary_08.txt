The judge grants the unopposed motion to compel discovery responses from the plaintiff, Elio Vallecillo Rivera, to the defendant, Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority (LACMTA). The plaintiff was properly served with discovery requests, but failed to respond. The judge orders the plaintiff to serve verified responses to the discovery requests, without objections, within 15 days. The judge also imposes sanctions of $500 on the plaintiff and/or plaintiff's counsel, jointly and severally, for their failure to respond to the discovery requests. The judge awards the defendant $500 in attorney's fees for the motion.
