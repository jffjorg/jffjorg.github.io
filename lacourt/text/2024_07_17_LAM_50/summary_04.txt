```
- Tesla moved to compel arbitration and stay the action pending arbitration.
```

```
- The parties agreed to arbitrate disputes through the American Arbitration Association (AAA) Consumer Arbitration Rules.
- Cohen did not opt out of the arbitration provision within 30 days of signing the agreement.
```

**Legal Issues**
```
- The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act does not preempt the Federal Arbitration Act (FAA).
- The FAA requires courts to enforce arbitration agreements according to their terms.
- The court finds that the arbitration agreement covers all of Cohen's claims, including those alleging misrepresentation.
```

**Ruling**
```
- The court grants Tesla's motion to compel arbitration and stay the action pending arbitration.
- The entire action is stayed pending completion of arbitration of Cohen's arbitrable claims.
```
