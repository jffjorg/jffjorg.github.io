TENTATIVE
RULING
 
Defendant
Sara Gutierrez’s Motion to Set Aside Default is DENIED. 
 
Moving
Party to give NOTICE.
 
 
This
is a disability access case. Plaintiff James Rutherford sues Gutierrez Family
Investment Partnership, L.P.  for
violation of the Unruh Civil Rights Act. 
 
On
January 9, 2024, Plaintiff requested default against Gutierrez Family
Investment Partnership, L.P. and the Court entered it the same day. 
 
On
May 9, 2024, Sara Gutierrez moved to set aside the default. But she is not a
named defendant. 
 
Thus,
the Court DENIES the motion.
