On April 3, 2023, Plaintiff, Armanda Guerrero, filed this
motion to compel Defendant, Kia America, Inc., to provide further responses to
Plaintiff’s Request for Production of Documents, Set One, Nos. 16-21.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of this motion.  Accordingly, the Court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motion to March 22, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The court
has a standard Song-Beverely Discovery Order.
 
Clerk of the Court to give notice and to provide a copy of
the Song-Beverly Discovery Order to counsel.
