This tentative ruling addresses the Defendant's motion to compel a mental examination of the Plaintiff, Christopher Matthew Stumpf, who alleges mental, emotional, and cognitive injuries resulting from a subject accident. The parties have disagreed on the scope of the examination, including the release of raw test data and testing materials to Plaintiff's counsel.
