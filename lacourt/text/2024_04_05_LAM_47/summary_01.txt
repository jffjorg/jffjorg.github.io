The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to requests for production and special interrogatories, as well as a request for sanctions. The motion was filed by the Defendant, Charles Schwab & Co., Inc., against the Plaintiff, Lauren Redmond, in an employment discrimination case. The Judge granted the motion, ordering the Plaintiff to produce verified, code-compliant responses without objections, excluding those revealing medical information or records, within 30 days of the order. The Judge also denied the Defendant's request for sanctions. The order is conditioned on the Defendant paying $60 in filing fees within 5 days of the ruling.
