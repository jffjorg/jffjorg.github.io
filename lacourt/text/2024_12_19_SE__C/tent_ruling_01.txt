  Case Number:  22NWCV00691    Hearing Date:   December 19, 2024    Dept:  C Melendez-Rodriguez v. Citistaff Solutions, Inc. 22NWCV00691This wrongful termination action was filed on August 10, 2022. On January 10, 2023, this Court granted Defendants Motion to Compel Arbitration and stayed proceedings in this case pending conclusion of arbitration. On December 18, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Motion for Reconsideration of this Court's January 10, 2023 Order, citing a change in law with the recent decision from the Second District Court of Appeal of Kader v. Southern California Medical Center, Inc. (2024) 99 Cal.App.5th 214. Plaintiff's Motion for Reconsideration of this Court's January 10, 2023 Order is scheduled to be heard on Tuesday, September 30, 2025. Arbitration is set for January 27-31, 2025. Plaintiff now moves ex parte to advance the hearing date on Plaintiff's Motion for Reconsideration and/or a Stay of Arbitration Proceedings. The Court notes that this Court retains jurisdiction over this case for reconsideration of it's order compelling arbitration, and can reconsider it's Order upon the emergence of a new law. (See Malek v. Blue Cross (2004) 121 Cal.App.4th 44, 60.) Plaintiff's Ex Parte Application to Advance the Hearing on her Motion for Reconsideration of this Court's January 10, 2023 Order Granting Defendants' Motion to Compel Arbitration is GRANTED. Plaintiff's Motion for Reconsideration is ADVANCED from Tuesday, September 30, 2025 to today's date and CONTINUED to Tuesday, February 4, 2025 at 10:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C. Opposition and Reply to be filed and served per Code in accordance with the new hearing date. Arbitration is STAYED pending a ruling on Plaintiff's Motion for Reconsideration. Moving Party to give notice.
