  Case Number:  22STCV10091    Hearing Date:   June 6, 2024    Dept:  19 After  consideration of the briefing filed and oral argument at the hearing,  Plaintiff's  1) Motion to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, By Defendant Lucy Guevara is GRANTED, and 2) Motion to  Compel Responses to Special Interrogatories, Set One to Defendant Lucy Guevara  is GRANTED.     Defendant  is ordered to provide verified responses to the above discovery requests without objections by July 8, 2024.  The request  for sanctions is also granted in part against each Defendant Lucy Guevara and  counsel of record, Arash Mepour, jointly and severally, in the reduced amount  of $1,060.00 for each motion, for a total of $2,120.00 payable to Plaintiff  through his attorney of record within 30 days.  The Court finds that  Defendant Lucy Guevara has engaged in the abuse of the discovery process in  failing to timely respond to a valid method of discovery, warranting sanctions for  reasonable expenses incurred by Plaintiff, including motion filing fees. (Code  of Civ. Proc. section 2023.010(d); 2023.030(a)). The Court takes into account  that no reply briefs were filed in reducing the amount of  sanctions awarded to a reasonable amount. Moving  party to submit proposed orders within 5 days and give notice.
