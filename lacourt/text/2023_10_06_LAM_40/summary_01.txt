The Judge's tentative ruling denies the motion by Defendant Marjan Ravanbakhsh to compel further responses to requests for admissions by Plaintiff Farzan Alamirad. The Judge finds that Plaintiff's responses to the requests for admissions are sufficient and do not require further supplementation. The Judge also denies the request for monetary sanctions.

The Judge grants the motion by Defendant Marjan Ravanbakhsh to compel further responses to form interrogatories by Plaintiff Farzan Alamirad. The Judge finds that Plaintiff's responses to the form interrogatories are incomplete and require further supplementation. The Judge orders Plaintiff to provide further responses to the form interrogatories within 10 days of this ruling.

The Judge grants monetary sanctions against Plaintiff Farzan Alamirad and his counsel, Law Offices of Morteza Aghavali, in the amount of $1,060, comprised of two hours expended on drafting the motion at a reasonable rate of $500 per hour and $60 expended in filing the motion.
