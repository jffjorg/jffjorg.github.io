English-text summary:

This tentative ruling is regarding a motion to strike filed by Southern California Edison Company (Edison) to strike portions of the First Amended Cross-Complaint filed by cross-complainants Tim Fejtek and Denise Fejtek. Edison seeks to strike the allegations supporting punitive damages and those it characterizes as immaterial and inflammatory. The court holds that claims for punitive damages cannot be alleged by conclusory allegations that defendant acted intentionally, willfully, or fraudulently. The court finds that the allegations in the cross-complaint support the theory that Edison prioritized profits over safety, as a matter of corporate policy. The court denies Edison's motion to strike in its entirety.
