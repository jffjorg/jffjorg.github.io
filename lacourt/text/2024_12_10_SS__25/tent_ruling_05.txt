ANALYSIS:
 
I.                   
Background

 
On September 11, 2024, Plaintiff Joanne
Doram (“Plaintiff”) filed a Complaint against Defendants Experian Information
Solutions, Inc. (“Experian”), Trans Union, LLC (“Trans Union”), and Equifax
Information Services, LLC (“Equifax”) (“Defendants”) alleging a single cause of
action for Violations of California Consumer Credit Reporting Agencies Act.
(Comp., p. 1.) 
 
On October 25, 2024, Trans Union filed
an Answer. 
 
On November 14, 2024, Plaintiff filed
the instant Demurrer to Trans Union’s Answer (“Demurrer”). No Opposition has
been filed. 
 
On November 19, 2024, Plaintiff filed
a Stipulation and Proposed Order for Electronic Service and Discovery
Reservation between Plaintiff and Trans Union (the “Stipulation”).  
 
The Demurrer and Stipulation are both
before the Court. 
 
On December 6, 2024, Defendant Trans
Union LLC’s filed its First Amended Answer and Defenses to Plaintiff’s
Complaint. 
 
On December 9, 2024, Plaintiff filed a
Dismissal as to Experian Information Solutions, Inc. 
 
II.                
Conclusion
& Order
 
Since Defendant Trans
Union LLC has filed its First Amended Answer on December 6, 2024, Plaintiff
Joanne Doram’s Demurrer to the Answer is OVERRULED as moot. 
 
Moving party is ordered to
give notice.
