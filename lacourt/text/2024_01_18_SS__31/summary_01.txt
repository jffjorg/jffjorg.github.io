The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Sandra Salvador (Plaintiff) against Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority, ET AL. (Defendant). The Plaintiff is seeking to compel the Defendant to produce documents related to a bus incident that occurred on January 27, 2023. The Defendant has produced its entire DVR bus videos, which consisted of 7 cameras, and has claimed that one external camera was not operational. The Plaintiff argues that the Defendant is concealing existing footage and is seeking a declaration from the Defendant's custodian of records affirming that no exterior video footage of the incident exists. The Court has denied the Plaintiff's motion, finding that the Defendant has produced all available video footage and that the Plaintiff's contention that the Defendant is concealing existing footage is without support.
