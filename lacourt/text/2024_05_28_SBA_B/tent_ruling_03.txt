 Case Number:  23TRCV00352    Hearing Date:   May 28, 2024    Dept:  B  JON-MARC WESTON vs SHAWN RANDOLPH
Case No. 24TRCV00352
Plaintiff Jon-Marc Weston moves to compel arbitration
pursuant to the purchase agreement entered into between the parties and to stay
proceedings. Defendant does not oppose Plaintiff’s request.
Defendant filed a “response” to the motion stating :
“Defendant does not oppose Plaintiff’s request to have those
claims resolved in arbitration pursuant to the arbitration provision in the
purchase agreement entered into between the parties. To date, Defendant’s
counsel has proposed using either ADR Services, Inc. or JAMS as the arbitration
services provider and Plaintiff’s counsel has proposed Judicate West. Defendant
is amenable to utilizing Judicate West provided that a retired judge serve as
the arbitrator.”
Plaintiff’s motion to compel arbitration is GRANTED.
Plaintiff’s motion to stay proceedings is GRANTED.
