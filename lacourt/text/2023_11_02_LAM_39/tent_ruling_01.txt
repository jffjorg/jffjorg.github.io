  Case Number:  21STCV16534    Hearing Date:   November 2, 2023    Dept:  39 American Express National
Bank v. Kevin Rathbun
Case No. 21STCV16534
Motion to Enforce
Settlement Agreement and Enter Judgment
 
The Court posts this order on
Monday, October 30, 2023, in advance of the hearing on Defendants’ motion to
strike, which is scheduled for Thursday, November 2, 2023, at 8:30 a.m.  The Court provides notice that if Defendant
does not appear, either remotely or in-person, absent good cause, Defendant
shall waive the right to be heard on this motion and shall submit to entry of
this order.  If Defendant wishes to
appear remotely but is experiencing difficulty, Defendant  may contact the Court’s clerk, Roberto
Mendoza, at (213) 633-0159 for assistance. 

 
            The parties
settled this case and stipulated that the Court may retain jurisdiction to
enter a judgment under Code of Civil Procedure section 664.6.  Defendant has defaulted on the payment plan.  Therefore, the Court grants Plaintiff’s
motion and signs the proposed judgment for $16,563.  Plaintiff’s counsel shall provide notice and
file proof of such with the Court.
