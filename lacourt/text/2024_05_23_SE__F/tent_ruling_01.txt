  Case Number:  21NWCV00421    Hearing Date:   May 23, 2024    Dept:  F Eric
Cleveland vs Fan Shin Investment, LLC, Case No. 21NWCV00421
This lawsuit
alleges violations of the American with Disabilities Act and the Unruh Civil
Rights Act.  Plaintiff Eric Cleveland (“Plaintiff”) applies ex parte for
an order shortening time for notice and hearing on his Motion to Continue
Trial.
Plaintiff
contends there are numerous discovery issues pending, including scheduling the
depositions of Defendant PB’s PMK, Gonzalo Ponce, and Alfredo Ponce, all of
which were compelled by Judge Lee Tsao on May 15, 2024.  Plaintiff’s
Motion to Continue Trial is currently scheduled for June 12, 2024,.  Trial
is currently set for June 26, 2024. 
Plaintiff’s Ex
Parte application is DENIED.  In the alternative, Plaintiff’s Motion to
Continue Trial is ADVANCED to this date and GRANTED.  Trial is continued
to September 12, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-F.  Final Status Conference
is scheduled for August 29, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-F.  All pretrial
discovery and motion deadlines are extended in compliance with the new trial
date.  The previously scheduled dates for Trial, Final Status Conference,
and Motion to Continue Trial are VACATED.  
Moving party to give notice.
