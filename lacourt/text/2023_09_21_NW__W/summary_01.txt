This tentative ruling addresses the motions to compel discovery responses filed by Plaintiff Leon Barnett Hall against Defendant Paulina Cruz. The motions were filed after Defendant failed to serve timely responses to Plaintiff's discovery requests. Defendant claims that they were delayed in responding due to issues with communication and assigning tasks to associates. The court finds that the motions to compel are moot since Defendant has now served responses. The court also grants Plaintiff's request for sanctions in the reduced amount of $1,111.65 per motion.
