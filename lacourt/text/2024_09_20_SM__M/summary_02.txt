The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case Can Capital Inc. v. Breaking Barriers Developmentally Disabled Services et al. regarding a motion to compel initial discovery responses. The Plaintiff, Can Capital Inc., served discovery requests on the Defendants, Benjamin Sarcadi and Breaking Barriers Developmentally Disabled Services, on March 26, 2024. The Defendants failed to respond to the Requests for Admission, and the Plaintiff seeks to deem matters admitted as to those requests. The Court granted the motion to deem the matters admitted, but denied the motion to compel initial responses to the Form Interrogatories, citing that the Defendants provided objections-only responses. Mandatory sanctions of $840.00 are imposed on the Defendants and their counsel, payable within 30 days.
