The judge has granted Defendant Anthony Castellanos' motions to compel responses to Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, and Demand for Production of Documents served on Plaintiff Nelly Alvarez. The Plaintiff has failed to respond to these discovery requests despite being granted numerous extensions. The judge has ordered the Plaintiff to provide responses to these discovery requests within 30 days of the order.

The judge has also imposed monetary sanctions on the Plaintiff and Plaintiff's counsel in the amount of $2430.00, which includes 3 hours of work at $250.00 per hour for each motion plus three $60.00 filing fees. The sanctions are to be paid within 30 days of the date of this Order.

The judge has given notice that parties who intend to submit on this tentative must send an email to the Court at SSCDEPT27@lacourt.org indicating their intention to submit on the tentative. The judge has also advised that if a party submits on the tentative and elects not to appear at the hearing, the opposing party may nevertheless appear at the hearing and argue the matter.
