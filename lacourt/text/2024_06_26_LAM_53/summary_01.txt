The judge has issued a tentative ruling denying the plaintiff's motion to deem admissions admitted and for sanctions. The plaintiff, Immigrant Rights Defense Council, LLC, sought to deem certain requests for admission admitted and to award monetary sanctions against the defendant, Laura E. Vaca, et al. The court found that the plaintiff did not comply with statutory requirements for serving the discovery requests on the defendant, including serving the requests within the time permitted by statute and at the defendant's address of record. As a result, the court denied the plaintiff's motion. The defendant had not made an appearance in the action at the time of service, and the court found that the service by mail was not valid.
