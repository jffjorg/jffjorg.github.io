 Case Number:  23STCV21397    Hearing Date:   February 9, 2024    Dept:  52 The Cabrera Firm,  APC’s Motions to Be Relieved as Counsel for Defendants  Guillermo Cabrera of The  Cabrera Firm, APC moves to be relieved as counsel for defendants Speedy Weedy  Vista, LLC, Speedy Weedy Santa Ana, LLC, and Welcome The Healing Touch, LLC.  The motions meet all requirements under  California Rules of Court, rule 3.1362. The Cabrera Firm, APC’s motions  to be relieved as counsel for defendants are granted.  The court will sign the proposed orders on Judicial  Council form MC-053.  The orders will be effective upon filing proof  of service of (1) the signed orders granting leave to withdraw, (2) notice of  the trial and final status conference dates, and (3) the trial preparation  order on defendants Speedy Weedy Vista, LLC, Speedy Weedy Santa Ana,  LLC, and Welcome The Healing Touch, LLC.
