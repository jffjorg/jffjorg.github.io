This wrongful death action was filed on July 24, 2023.
Trial has not yet been scheduled. Plaintiff now moves ex parte for an order to
continue the December 10, 2024, Demurrer Hearing. Plaintiff argues good cause
exists because due to a calendaring error, Plaintiff missed the opposition
deadline. The parties met and conferred and agreed to continue the hearing to a
date on or after January 3, 2025. 
 
Plaintiff’s ex parte application to Continue the December
10, 2024, Demurrer Hearing is GRANTED. The hearing on Defendant’s
Demurrer is CONTINUED to January 7, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.
Opposition and Reply to be filed and served per Code in accordance with the new
hearing date. Moving party to give notice.
