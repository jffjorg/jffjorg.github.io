 Case Number:  24NWCV00880    Hearing Date:   December 5, 2024    Dept:  SEC Ruvalcaba v. Los Angeles County Sheriff’s
Department, et al. 24NWCV00880
 
This personal injury action was filed on March 25, 2024.
Trial is currently scheduled for January 5, 2026. Plaintiff moves ex parte for
an order Shortening Time to Hear Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Deposition from
June 26, 2025, to a date within the next 15-20 days. Plaintiff argues she will
suffer irreparable harm because of a delay in discovery. In opposition,
Defendants argue they have timely served objections to previous notices of
depositions and have provided dates for a deposition in December of 2024 and
January of 2025. 
 
Plaintiff’s ex parte application for an order Shortening
Time to Hear Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Deposition is DENIED.
Plaintiff has not shown good cause warranting ex parte relief under California
Rules of Court rule 3.1202(c). Moving party to give notice.
If necessary, counsel
should contact Dept. C to inquire into the availability of an informal
discovery conference.
