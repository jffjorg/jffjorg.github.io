Plaintiff Creditors Adjustment Bureau, Inc.

1346 LA LLC in the total amount of $38,855.42, comprising
$32,416.61
in damages, $4,165.31 in interest, $911.00

Plaintiff has sufficiently supported its request with the submitted
declarations and evidence. Therefore, Plaintiff’s request is granted, and the
Court will sign the judgment.
