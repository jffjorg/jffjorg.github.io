[2] Plaintiff’s Complaint
consists of allegations on pleading paper in unnumbered paragraphs followed by
Judicial Council Forms. This is improper. If Plaintiff files an Amended Complaint, he is ordered comply with Cal.
Rule of Court, rule 1.45(c), which states: “Each paragraph within a cause of
action must be numbered consecutively beginning with one. Each paragraph number
must be preceded with one or more identifying letters derived from the title of
the cause of action.” Failure to comply with that Rule in any future complaint may
result in the Court striking it from the docket altogether.
