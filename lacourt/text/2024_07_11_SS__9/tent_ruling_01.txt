  Case Number:  21STCV36615    Hearing Date:   July 11, 2024    Dept:  9  On July 11, 2024, Counsel for the parties voluntarily participated
in an informal discovery conference with the Court, at the conclusion of which
the parties agreed as follows: No later than July 22, 2024, Defendant will voluntarily serve
further supplemental verified responses to Special Interrogatories 14, 15, and
16 by also identifying Plaintiff. Plaintiff’s Request for Production, Set Two No. 70 shall be
rephrased as two separate requests for production as follows: RPD 70a (“all
documents that Defendant presented to Plaintiff for signature at the same time
that Defendant presented the arbitration agreement to Plaintiff for signature
in or around February 2018”), and RPD 70b (“all documents that Defendant
presented to a representative sample of 20% of the COVERED EMPLOYEES for
signature at the same time that Defendant presented the arbitration agreement
to those COVERED EMPLOYEES for signature in or around February 2018”).  No later than July 22, 2024, Defendant will serve
verified responses agreeing to allow RPDs 70a and 70b in full and agreeing to produce
all responsive documents (which may have already been produced in its prior
production of complete personnel files for 69 representative employees). Plaintiff and Defendant stipulate to stay all litigation concerning
Special Interrogatories, Set Three, Numbers 22, 23, 26, 27, 30, 31, 34, 35, 38,
39, 42, 43, 46, 47, 50, 51, 54, 55, 58, and 59, meaning that until the Court
rules on Defendant’s pending motion to compel arbitration, Defendant need not
serve supplemental responses to these Special Interrogatories, and Plaintiff’s
deadline to move to compel further responses to these Special Interrogatories
is tolled and shall be extended until 45 days after the Court rules on Defendant’s
pending motion to compel arbitration.  Defendant requested a hearing date and briefing schedule for
an anticipated motion to disqualify Plaintiff’s handwriting expert.  The Court provides the following requested
briefing schedule:  Moving papers shall be
filed and served by August 19, 2024.
