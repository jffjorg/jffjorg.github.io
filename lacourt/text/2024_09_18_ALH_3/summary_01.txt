And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has granted the defendant's motion to compel the plaintiff to serve responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, which were propounded on March 12, 2024. The plaintiff failed to serve timely responses to the discovery requests, waiving any objections to the requests. The Judge has also granted the defendant's request for monetary sanctions in the reduced amount of $285 against the plaintiff, to be paid within 20 days of the date of the order. The plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses without objections to the defendant's Form Interrogatories within 20 days of the date of the order.
