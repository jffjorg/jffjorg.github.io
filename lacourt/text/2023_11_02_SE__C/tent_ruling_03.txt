 Case Number:  21STCV42547    Hearing Date:   November 2, 2023    Dept:  C Tenaud v. State of California, 21STCV42547Defendant
People of the State of California’s (Defendant) Ex Parte Application for Leave
to File First Amended Answer is GRANTED given that the parties stipulate to
allowing Defendant to amend its Answer to correctly name the Defendants.
Defendant states that its Answer to the Second Amended Complaint mistakenly
omitted that Defendant was answering as the “erroneously served and sued as State of California; California Department of
Transportation (Caltrans).” Thus, Defendant’s Ex Parte Application is GRANTED and Defendant is ordered to file its proposed
Answer, attached as Exhibit B to Mr. Goldstein’s Declaration, within ten days
of this Order.
