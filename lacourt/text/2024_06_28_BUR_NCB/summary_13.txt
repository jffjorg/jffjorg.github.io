Summary in English:

This Tentative Ruling grants Plaintiff Rene De La Nuez's motion for trial setting preference. The Plaintiff, who is 91 years old, alleges elder abuse and neglect by the defendants, including Burbank Healthcare, Inc. dba Burbank Healthcare and Rehabilitation Center, Longwood Management LLC, and others. The Plaintiff's counsel, Louanne Masry, submitted a declaration stating that the Plaintiff's health and mental decline make it necessary for the trial to be set preferentially to prevent prejudice to the Plaintiff's interests. The Court finds that the Plaintiff has met the required burden for a trial preference under CCP § 36(a) and grants the motion. The trial will be set for October 21, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in this department.
