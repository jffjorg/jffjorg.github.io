The judge has issued a tentative ruling denying Arizona Pipeline Co.'s (APC) motion to strike allegations for emotional distress damages from the complaint. The plaintiff, Mishel Dir Ghazarian, alleges that APC and Southern California Gas Company (SoCal Gas) delayed the completion of work to relocate a gas meter at his property, causing emotional distress. The judge found that the allegations for emotional distress do not solely result from negligent injury to the property, and that the plaintiff may pursue emotional distress and general damages in connection with the negligence cause of action. The judge also denied APC's motion to strike, allowing the plaintiff to proceed with his claims.
