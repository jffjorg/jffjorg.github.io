




            This
is a debt collection action on a defaulted credit card account. The Complaint alleges
causes of action for (1) account stated, and (2.) open book. 
            
            The
Complaint was filed on December 9, 2023. Default was entered as to Defendant Miriam
Yani (Defendant) on February 2, 2024. 
 
            Plaintiff
Cavalry SPV I, LLC now moves
for default judgment against Defendant. Does 1-100 have been dismissed. The
request for entry of default judgment is granted. 
 
Discussion
 
            Plaintiff
seeks default judgment in the amount of $27,442.60, which includes $503.50 in costs.

 
            Plaintiff
submits sufficient evidence to support the amount incurred and owed by Defendant’s
breach of the credit account. (Ryder Decl., ¶¶ 1-12.) Further, Plaintiff substantiates
the request for costs in the amount of $503.50. (CIV-105, ¶ 10.)
 
            Plaintiff
is not seeking attorney fees or interest. Plaintiff’s request for entry of default judgment in the
amount of $27,442.60
is granted.
