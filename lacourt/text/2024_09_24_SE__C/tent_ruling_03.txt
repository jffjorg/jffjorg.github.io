 Case Number:  22NWCV00320    Hearing Date:   September 24, 2024    Dept:  C THE PACIFIC SOUTHWEST DISTRICT OF THE LUTHERAN
CHURCH-MISSOURI SYNOD vs IRENE JENSEN, et al., Case No. 22NWCV00320 
This action concerning real property was filed by Plaintiff
THE PACIFIC SOUTHWEST DISTRICT OF THE LUTHERAN CHURCH – MISSOURI SYNOD on April
26, 2022. Defendant and Cross-Complainant Peace Lutheran Church (“PLC”) applies
ex parte for an order continuing the hearing of the Motion for an order
awarding attorney’s fees and costs.  PLC
contends that trial has been continued to March 19, 2025 and a new date to hear
the motion for sanctions should be scheduled for March 25, 2025.  The ex parte application is DENIED.  PLC has not demonstrated irreparable harm,
immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex parte.
(Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)  Moving party to
give notice.
