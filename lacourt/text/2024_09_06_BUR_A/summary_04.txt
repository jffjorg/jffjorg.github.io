This is a tentative ruling by Judge F.M. Tavelman regarding a motion for evidentiary and issue sanctions filed by Complete Gym Solutions, LLC (the Plaintiff) against Lance Zavela and Ryde Enterprises, LLC (the Defendants). The Plaintiff alleges that Zavela, the former National Sales Manager, misappropriated trade secrets and used them to convince Plaintiff's customers to rent equipment from Ryde. The Plaintiff seeks an order establishing the facts regarding the Defendant's use of trade secrets and to bar the Defendants from presenting evidence that they did not use trade secrets. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's motion is procedurally defective and orders the hearing continued to October 4, 2024 at 9:00 a.m. The Plaintiff is required to file a separate statement with the Court by September 19, 2024, and the Defendants may file a separate statement in opposition by September 26, 2024.
