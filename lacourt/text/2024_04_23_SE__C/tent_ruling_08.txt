Tentative Ruling 
The Demurrer and Motion to Strike are MOOT. 
Plaintiff to give notice. 
 
This is a lemon law action. Plaintiff Claudia Patricia
Amaya Solano filed this action on September 11, 2023. Plaintiff filed causes of
action for: (1) Breach of Express Warranty; (2) Breach of Implied Warranty; (3)
Violation of the Song-Beverly Act Section 1793.2; and (4) Fraudulent
Inducement-Concealment. 
Defendant generally demurs to fourth cause of action. 
On April 19, 2024, instead of filing an opposition,
Plaintiff filed a request for dismissal without prejudice as to the fourth
cause of action of action, fraudulent inducement-concealment, and punitive
damages.  The instant motions were directed
to the dismissed fourth cause of action. 
 
Accordingly, the Demurrer and Motion to Strike are deemed MOOT.
