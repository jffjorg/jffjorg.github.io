Plaintiff Fernando Arana filed this wage and hour action
against Defendant Rudy’s Wholesale Corp. on December 1, 2021.  Plaintiff filed a First Amended Complaint to
include Defendants Rodolfo Casasola and Teresa Casasola on June 8, 2023.  
The FAC lists six causes of action for: (1) Failure to Pay
Wages; (2) Failure to Provide Meal Periods; (3) Failure to Provide Rest
Periods; (4) Failure to Issue Accurate Itemized Wage Statements; (5) Failure to
Pay Overtime Wages; (6) Unlawful/Unfair Business Practices. 
Plaintiff moves for Summary Judgment or in the alternative
Summary Adjudication for each cause of action. 
On April 19, 2024, without leave of court, Defendants filed
a Sur-Reply.  Later on the same day,
Plaintiff filed an objection to the unauthorized Sur-Reply.  The Court will exercise its discretion to
consider the Sur-Reply.  Plaintiff’s
Motion for Summary Judgment is CONTINUED to May 21, 2024 at 10:30 a.m. in Dept.
SE-C.  Plaintiff may file a response to
Defendants’ Sur-Reply on or before April 30, 2024.  No additional briefing will be considered.
