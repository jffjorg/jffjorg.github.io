The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion for a preliminary injunction. The plaintiff, Ronald M. Beilke, is seeking to prevent a foreclosure on his home and to seek damages for alleged misappropriation of funds. The judge finds that the plaintiff has demonstrated a reasonable probability of success on the merits of his claims.

The judge also finds that the plaintiff will suffer irreparable harm if he is foreclosed upon and evicted from his home. The judge orders the plaintiff to post a bond of $192,201.34 to protect the HOA against potential damages.
