The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion for summary judgment or summary adjudication filed by Defendant GetAround, Inc. The motion seeks to dismiss the claims of Plaintiff Xochitl Alvarez Espindola, who was injured in a motor vehicle accident caused by Defendant Luke Mitchell Crawford, who was driving a vehicle procured through a rideshare program operated by GetAround, Inc.
