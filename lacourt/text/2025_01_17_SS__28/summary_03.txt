And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has granted Plaintiff State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company's motion to deem admitted the truth of matters specified in requests for admission, set one, served on Defendant Akill Raejon Otis. The Court has also granted Plaintiff's request for sanctions against Defendant and ordered Defendant to pay Plaintiff $560.00 by February 17, 2025. The Court's ruling is based on California Code of Civil Procedure section 2033.280, which provides that if a party fails to serve a timely response to requests for admission, the requesting party may move for an order deeming the truth of the matters specified in the requests admitted, as well as for a monetary sanction. The Court found that Defendant did not serve timely responses to the requests for admission and that Plaintiff's motion was unopposed.
