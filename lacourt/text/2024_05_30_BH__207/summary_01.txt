And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge grants Defendant's request to clarify that the "Wing Piece" is not part of the Wall Fence encompassed by the Court's Preliminary Injunction Order. The Court finds that the Wing Piece is not included in the Wall Fence as depicted in the Survey Map supporting Plaintiffs' request. Defendant may proceed with removing the Wing Piece as part of its planned renovation project, subject to the condition that it does not damage or undermine the structural integrity of the Wall Fence or any other portion of the Disputed Area.
