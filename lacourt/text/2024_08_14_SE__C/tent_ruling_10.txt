Atilio Navarrete, et al. vs Sproul Street One, L.P., et al., Case No.

This is a
personal injury action. Plaintiffs HAROLD ARTILO NAVARRETTE and BRENDA
NAVARRETTE apply ex parte for an order advancing the hearing on Defendants’
Demurrer and Motion to Strike, currently scheduled for April 17, 2025.
 Trial has not been set in this matter.  The ex parte application is
GRANTED.  Although Plaintiffs have not demonstrated irreparable harm or
immediate danger, the Court exercises its discretion to advance the
motions.  Accordingly, Defendant’s demurrer and motion to strike are
ADVANCED from April 17, 2025 to today’s date and CONTINUED to December 11, 2024
at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.  Plaintiffs to give notice.
