 Case Number:  23STCV11291    Hearing Date:   October 23, 2024    Dept:  61 LA MAISON ARDMORE HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION vs DIAMOND SOLAR, INC.,, et al.TENTATIVEThe present motion pertains to attorney E. Jay Gotfredson of the firm Gotfredson & Associates, counsel for Defendant Diamond Solar, Inc. Gotfredson presents a declaration stating that Diamond Solar is out of business and has been terminated by the California Secretary of State, and that he is unable to communicate with Diamond Solar. The motion complies with the above requirements, including a proposed order with all upcoming hearing dates. Trial is set for September 2, 2025, almost a year hence, and granting the motion is unlikely to prejudice Diamond Solar.E. Jay Gotfredson’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel for Diamond Solar, Inc. is GRANTED, effective upon moving counsel filing with the court proof of service of copies of the signed order on the client, as set forth in Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1362(e).Counsel to give notice.
