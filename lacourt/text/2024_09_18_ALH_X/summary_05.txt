The judge has issued a tentative ruling in a dog bite case where the plaintiff is seeking to compel the defendant to respond to discovery requests and deem admitted the defendant's responses to requests for admissions. The plaintiff claims injuries and damages from the dog bite incident and has served discovery requests on the defendant. Despite extensions, the defendant failed to respond to the discovery requests. The judge grants the plaintiff's motions to compel responses to the discovery requests and deems admitted the defendant's responses to the requests for admissions. The judge also awards sanctions to the plaintiff's counsel in the amount of $2,456.73 for the reasonable costs and fees associated with bringing the motions. The defendant is ordered to provide responses to the discovery requests without objections and is required to pay the sanctions within 30 days of mailing of the notice of ruling.
