 Case Number:  23STCV28599    Hearing Date:   February 8, 2024    Dept:  68 Dept. 68 Date:  2-8-24 Case  #22CHCV28599 Trial  Date: 2-8-24   ADMISSIONS   MOVING  PARTY: Plaintiff, United Broadway Real Estate Company, LLC RESPONDING  PARTY: Unopposed/Defendant, Enrique Martinez   RELIEF  REQUESTED Motion  to Deem Request for Admissions Admitted    SUMMARY  OF ACTION Plaintiff  United Broadway Real Estate Company, LLC filed a complaint for unlawful  detainer against defendant Enrique Martinez arising from the breach of a five  year lease at a rent of $9,800/month. Plaintiff alleges delinquency of $39,200  as of October 31, 2023.    The  court overruled the demurrer of Defendant on December 15, 2023. Defendant, in  pro per, answered the complaint on December 18, 2023.   RULING: Granted. Plaintiff  United Broadway Real Estate Company, LLC moves to deem Request for Admissions (set  one) served on defendant Enrique Martinez admitted.   Defendant represents service of requests for admissions on December  29, 2023. [Declaration of Scott Spinrad, ¶ 4, Ex. 1-2.] According to Defendant,  at the time of the filing of the motion, no responses were received. [Id., ¶ 5.]     The motion is unopposed. The court electronic filing system  shows no reply filed at the time of the tentative ruling publication cutoff. The  unopposed motion is granted. Request for Admissions (set one) is deemed  admitted. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280, subd. (b).) Sanctions in the amount of  $250 imposed against defendant Martinez, and payable within 30 days. (Code Civ.  Proc., § 2033.280, subd. (c).)   Trial  set for February 9, 2024. Motion to Compel Production of Documents scheduled  for February 13, 2024.   Defendant to provide notice.
