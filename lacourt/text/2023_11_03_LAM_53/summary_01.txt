Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court has considered the motions to compel further responses to special interrogatories and form interrogatories filed by plaintiff Dan Sackheim. The court grants in part the motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories from Skydance Media, LLC, ordering the defendant to serve further, full and complete answers to interrogatories numbers 21-22 and 39-42. The court denies the motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories numbers 1-10, 13, 18, 27-29, 31, and 33-38, citing that the answers to those interrogatories are not evasive or incomplete.

The court also grants in part the motion to compel further responses to form interrogatories from Amazon Studios, Inc., ordering the defendant to serve further, full and complete answers to interrogatories numbers 3.7, 15.1, 16.1, and 16.9. The court denies the motion to compel further response to form interrogatory number 16.10, citing that the answer to that interrogatory is not evasive or incomplete.

The court denies the plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions against the defendants, finding that the circumstances presented would make the imposition of sanctions unjust. The court orders the defendants to serve further, full and complete answers to interrogatories within 20 days of the date of this order.
