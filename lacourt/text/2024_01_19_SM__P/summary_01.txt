The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case Jadidi v. Azzurra Homeowners Association (Case No. 22SMCV00693). The Defendant, Azzurra Homeowners Association, has moved to compel the Plaintiff, Jadidi, to answer their form interrogatories and to appear for deposition. The Judge finds that the Plaintiff has failed to respond to the interrogatories, which were served on August 31, 2023, and that the Plaintiff failed to appear for the deposition, which was scheduled for October 11, 2023. The Judge grants the Defendant's motion to compel the Plaintiff's answers to the interrogatories and to appear for deposition.

Regarding sanctions, the Judge grants the Defendant six hours at $175 per hour for drafting the two motions and $120 for filing fees, and an additional hour for appearing at the hearing. The total sanctions are $1,620.

The hearing is set for January 19, 2024.
