This is a tentative ruling by Judge Theresa M. Traber on a motion to enforce a settlement agreement in a wage and hour action. The agreement was executed on September 21, 2022, and provides for settlement of the claims brought by the plaintiffs. Defendant J.D. Meints agreed to pay the plaintiffs $9,500 in exchange for dismissal of the action. However, Meints has only made one payment of $1,000, and has failed to comply with the agreement. The court finds that Meints has breached the agreement and grants the motion to enforce the settlement. Judgment is entered for the plaintiffs against Meints in the amount of $8,500, plus $1,250 in attorney's fees and $60 in costs.
