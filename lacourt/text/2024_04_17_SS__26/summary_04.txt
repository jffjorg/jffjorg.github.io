The Judge grants the Plaintiff's motion to compel defendants' responses to special interrogatories and requests for sanctions. The defendants, RAR2 Villa Marina Center CA SPE, Inc. and ISP2 Del Mar, LLC, failed to respond to the propounded discovery requests despite a meet and confer effort and an extended deadline. The Judge finds that the defendants' failure to respond constitutes a misuse of the discovery process, warranting sanctions. The defendants are ordered to serve verified responses to the special interrogatories within 20 days of the order and pay sanctions of $500.00 to the Plaintiff's counsel within 20 days of the order.
