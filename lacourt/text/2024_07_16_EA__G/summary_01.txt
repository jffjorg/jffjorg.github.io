The Court grants the Defendant Claremont Unified School District's motion to compel non-party Meta Platforms, Inc. to comply with a deposition subpoena seeking electronically stored information related to the Plaintiff's social media posts. The Court also grants Meta's unopposed motion to seal supporting exhibits containing identifying and personal information for a minor victim of sexual assault. The Court orders Meta to serve the requested records on the Defendant within twenty days of the issuance of this order.
