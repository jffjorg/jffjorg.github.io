The Judge has issued a tentative ruling granting the Defendant's motion to transfer venue from Los Angeles County to Ventura County. The Plaintiff, Joseph Viens, filed a complaint against the Defendant, C & C Boats - Newport Landing, alleging violation of the Jones Act. The Defendant filed a motion to transfer venue, which was continued to allow for discovery and supplemental briefing on venue issues. The Plaintiff alleges that he worked as a seaman on vessels owned and operated by the Defendant in the Santa Barbara Channel and was injured, and also alleges exposure to crude oil from the Refugio Beach oil spill. The Defendant contends that Ventura County is the proper venue because that is where the injury is alleged to have occurred and where the Defendant's principal place of business is currently located. The Judge concludes that Ventura County is the proper venue and grants the Defendant's motion to transfer venue.
