The judge has ruled on the Demurrer and Motion to Strike filed by Dr. Bhargav Mudda, M.D. and Neurospine Institute. The Demurrer challenges the plaintiffs' claims for medical negligence, battery, and fraud. The judge sustains the Demurrer for the claims of battery and fraud, finding that the plaintiffs' allegations are insufficient and do not meet the legal requirements for these claims. The judge also grants the Motion to Strike punitive damages from the Complaint, citing California Code of Civil Procedure § 425.13. The judge orders the plaintiffs to file an amended pleading by December 12, 2024.
