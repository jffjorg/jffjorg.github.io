The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to quash deposition subpoenas filed by defendants Ruben Jesus Jaramilla, Jr., Andiamo Logistics, Inc., Element Fleet Corporation, Gelco Fleet Trust, LSR, and Amazon.com Services LLC. The motion was filed in response to deposition subpoenas served on United Parcel Service, Inc. (UPS) for records related to Jaramilla's employment. The judge has denied the motion as moot since the deposition subpoenas have been withdrawn. The judge has also awarded monetary sanctions against the plaintiffs in the amount of $1,635.00, which represents the reasonable cost of defendants' counsel's time spent preparing and arguing the motion.
