The Judge's tentative ruling is on Defendant Cooperative of American Physicians, Inc.'s Motion to Quash Plaintiffs' deposition subpoena. The Plaintiffs, Nancy Brusegaard Johnson, Paul Rogus, and Jorge Lira, brought causes of action against Defendant for violation of Labor Code section 1102.5, wrongful termination in violation of public policy, and defamation. Defendant moved to quash the deposition subpoena, arguing that the documents sought are attorney work product and not relevant to the case. The Court granted Defendant's motion in part and denied it in part. The Court ordered Defendant to turn over all records consistent with the subpoena, except those containing attorney impressions, conclusions, or legal opinions, which may be redacted. The Court also denied Defendant's motion to quash the subpoena in part, finding that the documents sought are relevant to the case.
