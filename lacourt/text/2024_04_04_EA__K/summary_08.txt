And here is the English text summary:

The tentative ruling is that the defendants' demurrer to the complaint is sustained. The plaintiff, Ruben Olague, alleges that he was a resident at Inland Valley Care and Rehabilitation Center from May 2023 to July 2023 and developed severe pressure sores as a result of deficiencies in care. The defendants, Inland Valley Partners, LLC, and Renew Health Consulting Services, LLC, demurred to the complaint, alleging that it fails to state a cause of action for dependent adult abuse. The court found that the plaintiff failed to plead with specificity the facts regarding the alleged neglect and failed to plead a causal link between the alleged neglect and the injury. The court also found that the plaintiff failed to plead with specificity the facts demonstrating corporate authorization or ratification of any wrongful conduct. As a result, the defendants' demurrer is sustained. The motion to strike is denied as moot.
