Summary:

This is a tentative ruling on a motion for a protective order filed by Long Beach Memorial Medical Center, Desiree Thomas, and Memorial Health Services (Defendants) regarding the deposition of Ikenna Mmeje, a former COO of Long Beach Memorial Medical Center and current CEO of USC Hospital Arcadia. The motion was filed after the subpoena for Mmeje's deposition was served on September 13, 2023, and the deposition was scheduled to take place on September 28, 2023. The court grants the motion and limits the scope of Mmeje's deposition to specific matters related to the hernia pilot program, the proctoring issue, the plaintiff's termination, discrimination, harassment, or retaliation, and conversations with or about the plaintiff. The court also directs the plaintiff to meet and confer with the defendants to select an alternative date for the deposition.
