[TENTATIVE] ORDER DENYING MOTION TO
  COMPEL DEPOSITIONS OF PLAINTIFFS 
 
Dept. 48

On March 26, 2024,
Defendants Greg Zandate and Laura Zandate filed a motion to compel the
depositions of Plaintiffs Noemi Ceja, Carlos Figueroa, and Dominique Santana.
On
April 11, 2024, while this Department was dark, the Honorable Gail Killefer
held an informal discovery conference, at which counsel agreed that Defendants
would take Plaintiffs’ depositions on April 29, 2024 and May 1, 2024.
Accordingly,
the Motion to Compel Depositions of Plaintiffs is DENIED AS MOOT.
