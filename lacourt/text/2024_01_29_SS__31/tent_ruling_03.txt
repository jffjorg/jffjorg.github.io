Alvarado (“Eduardo”), and $300,000 being apportioned to Dalary Alvarado (“Dalary”).   Jose Alvarado is the petitioner and guardian ad litem for minor claimants Carlos and Eduardo. Maria Vazquez is the petitioner and guardian ad litem for Dalary.  Claimants Carlos Alvarado and Eduardo A. Alvarado  Pertaining to Carlos, if the settlement is approved, $120,000 will be used for attorney fees. The net balance of $180,000 will be apportioned, with $10,000 to be delivered to the guardian of the minor in order to help purchase a home in El Salvador, and the remaining $170,000 to be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity.  Pertaining to Eduardo, the settlement is the same. If the settlement is approved, $120,000 will be used for attorney fees. The net balance of $180,000 will be apportioned, with $10,000 to be delivered to the appointed guardian of the minor in order to help purchase a home in El Salvador, and the remaining $170,000 to be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity.  Petitioner requests 40% of both Carlos’ and Eduardo’s settlement proceeds be used for attorney fees. Based upon the declarations submitted, the Court is satisfied with the efforts in this case to justify such a high fee.   Further, Petitioner Jose Alvarado requests the Court to grant the request for $10,000, from each of Carlos’ and Eduardo’s settlements, to go towards purchasing a home abroad, and that Petitioner will provide proof of purchase of the property. As to the $10,000 from each of claimants’ settlement, Petitioner marks Item 18(b)(1) requesting a guardian of claimants’ estate be appointed and money to be paid to the person so appointed.   However, the petitions for Carlos and Eduardo are denied for the following reason:  Petitioner’s request regarding the purchase of property, and any conditions which may apply, is more appropriately indicated on Item 20 as opposed to on Item 18(b)(1). The Court will require additional details from Petitioner to understand the proposed real estate transaction, including details of how the property will be held and any proposed mechanisms to ensure the property will be or eventually will become the property of minor claimants Carlos and Eduardo moving forward.  Based on the foregoing, the Court DENIES the petition for Carlos and Eduardo.¿Petitioner must submit a new petition curing the above-noted issue at his earliest convenience. ¿ Claimant Dalary Alvarado  If the settlement is approved, $120,000 will be used for attorneys’ fees. The net balance of $180,000 will be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity.  Petitioner Maria Vazquez requests 40% of both Carlos’ and Eduardo’s settlement proceeds be used for attorney fees. Based upon the declarations submitted, the Court is satisfied with the efforts in this case to justify such a high fee.   Based on the foregoing, the Court approves and GRANTS the petition for Dalary.¿¿  The Court sets an Order to Show Cause Re: Compliance: Proof of Purchase of Annuity(ies) on____________________ IN DEPARTMENT SS-31 AT 8:30 AM.¿¿¿  Moving party is ordered to give notice.
