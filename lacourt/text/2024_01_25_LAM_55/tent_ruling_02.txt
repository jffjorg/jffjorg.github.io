 Case Number:  22STCV37027    Hearing Date:   January 25, 2024    Dept:  55 NATURE OF PROCEEDINGS:  Motion of Counsel of Record to be Relieved as
Counsel of Record as to Defendant Junior's Apparel Inc.In this wage and hour employment case, counsel for
Defendant JUNIOR'S APPAREL INC. has filed a motion to be relieved as counsel.
No opposition has been filed.  The form
notice, declaration, proposed order, and proof of service comply with the
requirements for a motion to be relieved as counsel. See
CRC Rule 3.1362. Counsel’s declaration provides adequate reasons for withdrawal
as counsel for Defendant. The Court therefore grants the motion and will sign
the form proposed order.
