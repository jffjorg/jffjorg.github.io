The court has reviewed the evidence and arguments presented by the parties in connection with the plaintiff's motion to tax costs incurred by the defendant. The court has granted the motion in part, denying it in part. The plaintiff's motion is granted as to $146.45 in "Other" costs, but is denied in all other respects.
