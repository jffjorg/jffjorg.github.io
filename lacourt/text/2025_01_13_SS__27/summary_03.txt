Here is the text summary:

The Honorable Lee S. Arian, Judge of the Superior Court, has issued a tentative ruling granting Plaintiff Michael McCarthy's motion to compel the deposition of Defendant Environmental Service Concepts, LLC's PMK (Person Most Knowledgeable). The Plaintiff slipped and fell in Topanga Mall in July 2021 and served the first notice of deposition for the PMK on August 16, 2024. However, the Defendant refused to produce the PMK, citing difficulty in identifying it, and did not provide an available date for the deposition until December 4, 2024. The Court finds good cause to grant the motion and orders the PMK to sit for deposition within 20 days of today. Additionally, the Court issues a monetary sanction of $1,000 against the Defendant and its attorney, jointly and severally, payable to the Plaintiff within 20 days of today.
