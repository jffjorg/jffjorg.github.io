Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Petitioner, McCulloch Builders, Inc., has filed a petition to vacate or alter an arbitration award issued by Arbitrator Leslie Steven Marks of the American Arbitration Association (AAA) on November 15, 2023. The Petitioner claims that the award was procured through corruption, fraud, or other undue means and that the Arbitrator exceeded their powers. The Respondents, Johanna Schwarzenberger and Rob De Vrij, filed an opposition, arguing that there is no evidence that the award was procured through undue means or caused substantial prejudice to the Petitioner. The Court has reviewed the petition and finds that the Petitioner's arguments are without merit. The Court denies the Petition to Vacate or Alter Arbitration Award filed by Petitioner McCulloch Builders, Inc.
