RELIEF REQUESTED: An order
relieving Dennis Russel as counsel for Plaintiffs Nicole A. Parke; E.O.A.

RULING: The motion is placed off calendar.  
 
On 4/17/24, attorney Dennis Russell filed the instant
motion seeking an order relieving him as counsel for Plaintiffs Nicole A.
Parke; E.O.A. Global Holdings, LLC and E.O.A. Management Group, LLC (collectively,
Plaintiffs) in this action.  The motion
was originally scheduled for hearing on 12/4/24, which is the hearing date
listed on the moving papers, but was rescheduled by attorney Russel to 8/9/24
on 7/2/24.  
 
A proof of service for the motion has not been
filed.  Additionally, a notice of the new
hearing date and proof of service for same has not been filed.  No oppositions/responses to the motion by
Plaintiffs or the other parties in the action have been filed to cure the
defect in notice.  Therefore, the motion
is placed off calendar.
