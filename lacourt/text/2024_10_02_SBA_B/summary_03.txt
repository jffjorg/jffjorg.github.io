And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has granted the plaintiff's application for a writ of possession for six pieces of equipment: a John Deere 35G Compact Excavator, a Furukawa FX45 Breaker, a John Deere 317G Compact Track Loader, a Kent FX55 SS Hydraulic Breaker, a Trail King TKT12U Tilt Utility Trailer, and another Furukawa FX45 Breaker. The plaintiff, John Deere Construction & Forestry Company, has a purchase money security interest in the equipment and the defendants, First On Xite Equipment Services LLC, Vanessa Angela De La Torre, and Arturo De La Torre, have defaulted on their payment obligations. The plaintiff has filed a complaint and obtained a UCC-1 Financing Statement, and has made a demand on the defendants to return the equipment, but they have refused. The Judge has found that the plaintiff has met its burden to establish the probable validity of its claims and has granted the application for a writ of possession.
