The Judge is ruling on a motion by HK Realty Inc. and Legacy Partners, Inc. to strike punitive damages from the Complaint filed by Sara Gomez. Gomez alleges that HK Realty and Legacy Partners failed to remediate defective conditions, including water leakage, mold, and dust mites, at a residential property in Rowland Heights. Gomez claims that HK Realty and Legacy Partners engaged in despicable conduct and are liable for punitive damages. However, the Judge finds that the allegations do not support a claim for punitive damages. The Judge grants the motion to strike punitive damages without leave to amend and orders that the defendants file an answer to the Complaint with punitive damages stricken.
