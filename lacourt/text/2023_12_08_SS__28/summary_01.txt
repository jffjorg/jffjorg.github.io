The Court has considered the moving, opposition, and reply papers and rules as follows. The City of Los Angeles requests that the Court grant its motion for judgment on the pleadings without leave to amend. Cross-Complainants, Charter Communications, Inc., Gerald Rivera, and Jason Lizarraga, request that the Court deny the motion.

The Court grants the City's request for judicial notice. The City argues that Cross-Complainants did not comply with the claims presentation requirement of the California Tort Claims Act. Cross-Complainants argue that they did comply and that the City waived its objections under Government Code section 946.4.

The Court finds that Cross-Complainants did not comply with the claims presentation requirement of the California Tort Claims Act. The Court grants the motion for judgment on the pleadings because Cross-Complainants did not comply with the claims presentation requirement of the California Tort Claims Act.

The Court enters judgment for Cross-Defendant City of Los Angeles on the cross-complaint filed by Cross-Complainants Charter Communications, Inc. et al. The moving party is ordered to give notice of this ruling. The moving party is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within five days.
