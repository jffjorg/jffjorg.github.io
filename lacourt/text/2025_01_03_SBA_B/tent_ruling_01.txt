Case No. 24TRCP00352
Demurrer of Defendant City of Rancho Palos Verdes
The Court discloses that Judge Stern served on the Rancho
Palos Verdes City Council from December 1999 until December 2011.  One of the council members serving a portion of
that time was present councilmember Barbara Ferraro.  Judge Stern does not believe that he is
required to recuse himself pursuant to Code
of Civil Procedure § 170.1 et seq. 
If any party believes that Judge Stern should be recused in this action
the party should notify the Court as soon as possible, but in any case no later
than at the time that the matter is called for hearing.
