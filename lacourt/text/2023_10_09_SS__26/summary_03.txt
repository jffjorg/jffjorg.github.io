The judge's tentative ruling grants the defendant's motions to compel responses to discovery requests and to award sanctions. The plaintiff, Yohanson Walkir Castellanos Orozco and Ana Lucia Elizabeth Lantan Garcia, failed to respond to the defendant's discovery requests despite an extended deadline. The defendant, Manuel Ocampo, sought to meet and confer with the plaintiff, but there was no response. The judge finds that the plaintiff's failure to respond constitutes a misuse of the discovery process and awards sanctions in the amount of $520.00 to the defense counsel. The plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses to the discovery requests within 20 days of the order and to pay the sanctions within the same time frame.
