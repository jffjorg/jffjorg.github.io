The court issues a tentative ruling granting the petition for approval of compromise of claim or action for minor or person with a disability. The petition was filed by John Roe, a minor student who was subjected to sexual abuse in school restrooms by another minor student. The settlement agreement between Roe and Hacienda La Puente Unified School District (HLPUSD) provides for a payment of $200,000 to Roe. The court finds the amount awarded to Roe is reasonable and approves the allocation of settlement funds into an insured bank account and a single-premium deferred annuity. The court also approves counsel's recovery of fees in the amount of $69,861.70, or 34.93% of the total recovery. A future hearing date is set for an OSC re: Proof of Deposit of Settlement Funds and Dismissal.
