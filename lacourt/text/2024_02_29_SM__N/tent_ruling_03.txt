 Case Number:  23SMCV02518    Hearing Date:   February 29, 2024    Dept:  N TENTATIVE RULINGAndy Pirnia and The Pirnia Law Group’s Motion to Withdraw as Counsel for Defendant Javad Billoo is GRANTED.Counsel has asserted a valid reason for withdrawal, i.e., the attorney-client relationship has broken down. (Rules Prof. Conduct, rule 1.16(b)(4), (b)(5).) Counsel has provided the required forms under rule 3.1362 of the California Rules of Court and served the opposing parties and client with the same. There being good cause to grant counsel’s request to withdraw, Andy Pirnia and The Pirnia Law Group’s Motion to Withdraw as Counsel for Defendant Javad Billoo is GRANTED. This ruling granting counsel’s motion to withdraw shall become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon Defendant Javad Billoo.
