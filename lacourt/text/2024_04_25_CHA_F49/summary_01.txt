**Case Summary**

The Judge's tentative ruling is on a Motion to Compel Deposition Attendance and Production of Documents by Defendant Santa Monica BMW's Person Most Knowledgeable. The case involves a Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act lawsuit over alleged defects in a 2021 BMW 430i Coupe.

**Key Findings**

* The Court concludes that Plaintiff has made good faith attempts to reschedule the deposition.
* The Court finds that Defendant BMW of North America, LLC refused to produce its Person Most Knowledgeable.
* The Court grants the Motion to Compel Deposition Attendance and Production of Documents.
* The Court awards monetary sanctions in the amount of $816.75 against Defendant BMW of North America, LLC and its attorney(s) of record.

**Orders**

* Defendant Santa Monica BMW is ordered to produce its Person Most Knowledgeable at a deposition to be scheduled by Plaintiff.
* Plaintiff must provide at least ten days' notice of the deposition, with extended notice as applicable under Code of Civil Procedure.
* Defendant BMW of North America, LLC and its attorney(s) of record are ordered to jointly and severally pay $816.75 to Plaintiff.
