MOVING PARTY:                 Russell H. Falconer    
 
RESPONDING PARTY:       Unopposed
Application of Russell H. Falconer to be Admitted Pro Hac Vice
The court
considered the moving papers filed in connection with this motion.  No opposition papers were filed. 
DISCUSSION
            Applicant Russell H. Falconer
requests an order permitting him to appear as counsel pro hac vice for
defendants Lockton Companies, LLC – Pacific Series and Lockton Investment
Advisors, LLC, in this action. 
            The court finds that the application
complies with California Rules of Court, rule 9.40  The court therefore grants the application
and orders that Russell H. Falconer is permitted to appear as counsel pro hac
vice for defendants Lockton Companies, LLC – Pacific Series and Lockton
Investment Advisors, LLC in this action. 
(Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40, subd. (a).) 
            The court orders defendants Lockton
Companies, LLC – Pacific Series and Lockton Investment Advisors, LLC to give
notice of this ruling.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
