The Judge's tentative ruling grants the motion to compel arbitration brought by the Defendants, Wayne Uei Wang and Jessie Wang. The motion was unopposed by the Plaintiff, Xiao Yong Chen. The Judge finds that the Residential Purchase Agreement (RPA) contains an arbitration provision that covers any dispute or claim arising out of the RPA or resulting transaction.
