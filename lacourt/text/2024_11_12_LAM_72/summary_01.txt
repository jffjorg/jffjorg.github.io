Summary:

The Judge's tentative ruling grants summary adjudication on the first, third, and fourth claims against Hooper, a manager of Crown Estate Holding, LLC, for public nuisance, unpermitted construction and operation, and narcotics abatement. The Court finds that Hooper knew or should have known about the illegal activity at the property and failed to take adequate measures to prevent it. The Court asks Plaintiff to provide evidence on Hooper's sophistication and net worth to determine the appropriate penalty. The total civil penalty requested is $1,327,000.
