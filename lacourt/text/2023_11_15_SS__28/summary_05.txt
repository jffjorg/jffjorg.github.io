The judge has considered the moving papers and rules as follows. The case involves a plaintiff who was injured on a bench at defendants' premises, and the defendants knew or should have known about the dangerous condition. The defendant Jameswood Holdings, LLC has filed a motion for leave to file a cross-complaint against Carson, alleging that Carson is obligated under a lease agreement to indemnify and defend Jameswood in the plaintiff's action. The court finds that Jameswood acted in good faith in failing to file the cross-complaint initially and grants the motion. Jameswood is ordered to file and serve the cross-complaint within 30 days of the hearing.
