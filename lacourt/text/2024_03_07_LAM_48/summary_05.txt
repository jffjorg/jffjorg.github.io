And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling on Plaintiff Frank Rojas's motion to compel further responses to Special Interrogatories and Request for Production of Documents against Defendant Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. The Court grants in part and denies in part both motions.

Regarding the motion to compel further responses to Special Interrogatories, the Court grants Plaintiff's request for further responses to Special Rog Nos. 2, 7, 17-27, and 32. The Court denies Plaintiff's request for further responses to Special Rog Nos. 17-27, finding that Defendant's responses were adequate.

Regarding the motion to compel further responses to Request for Production of Documents, the Court grants in part and denies in part Plaintiff's request. The Court grants Plaintiff's request for production of documents related to the same make and model as Plaintiff's vehicle, but denies Plaintiff's request for production of documents related to other vehicles.

The Court orders Defendant to provide verified, supplemental responses within 30 days. The Court also advises the parties that they must send an email to the Court indicating their intention to submit on the tentative ruling. If all parties submit, no appearances before the Court are required unless a companion hearing is also on calendar.
