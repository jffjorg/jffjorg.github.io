MOTION
TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO DISCOVERY 



 
MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
William Robert Fonseca 
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant General
Motors, LLC
 
 
This is a Song-Beverly action.  On January 18, 2024, Plaintiff, William
Robert Fonseca, filed this motion to compel Defendant, General Motors, LLC, to
provide further responses to Plaintiff’s Request for Production of Documents,
Set One, Nos. 16-21.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the hearing of this motion.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motion to April 24, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31.  The parties are to contact
the judicial assistant to obtain a copy of the court’s tentative Song- Beverly
discovery order.  Thereafter the parties
are to contact the judicial assistant to schedule an IDC date and time. 
 
Clerk of the Court to give notice.
