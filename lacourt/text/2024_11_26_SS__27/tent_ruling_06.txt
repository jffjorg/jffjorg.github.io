Ruling: GRANTED 

 
On January 30, 2023,
Plaintiff filed the present case against Defendants Southern California Gas
Company and LA Harvard Property LLC. On November 20, 2024, the Court granted
Defendant Southern California Gas Company’s motion to dismiss. imposing
terminating sanctions against Plaintiff based, in part, on its finding that
Plaintiff had abandoned the case.
On November 25, 2024,
the Court granted Defendant LA Harvard Property LLC’s multiple motions to
compel, ordering Plaintiff to respond to Defendant’s discovery requests within
20 days. Trial is currently scheduled for January 7, 2025. 
The Court will continue
the case for 60 days. This extension provides Plaintiff with ample time to
comply with the Court’s November 25, 2024, discovery order. Should Plaintiff
fail to comply and continue to neglect his case and discovery obligations, the
extension will allow sufficient time for Defendant to file and have any further
motions heard before the new trial date.
The new Trial Date is set for March 7, 2025, at 8:30 a.m.
The Final Status
Conference is continued to February 21, 2025, at 10:00 a.m. All case-related deadlines will
follow the new trial date.
