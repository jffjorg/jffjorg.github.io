 Case Number:  23STCV20823    Hearing Date:   December 30, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On August
29, 2023, Plaintiff Mason Szuch (“Plaintiff”), a minor, by and through his guardian
ad litem Alexander Szuch, filed this action against Defendants Todd Felderstein
(“Defendant”) and Does 1-50 for motor vehicle tort and general negligence. 
On
September 14, 2023, the Court appointed Alexander Szuch to serve as Plaintiff’s
guardian ad litem. 
On
October 24, 2024, Petitioner Alexander Szuch (“Petitioner”)
filed a petition for expedited approval of the compromise of Plaintiff’s
claims. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
asks the Court to grant the petition for expedited approval of the compromise
of minor Plaintiff's claims. 
DISCUSSION 
Section 14b of the petition lists $3,125.00 in costs
for “Med Pay.”  Medical expenses should
not be listed in Section 14b.  Petitioner
should explain the basis for this cost item. 
Section 11a of the petition states that the gross
settlement amount is $43,125.00.  Section
17a states that the gross settlement amount is $40,000.00.  The figures should be consistent. 
The Court denies the petition.         
CONCLUSION 
The
Court DENIES without prejudice the petition to approve the compromise of minor
Plaintiff Mason Szuch’s claims filed by Petitioner Alexander Szuch on October
24, 2024. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
