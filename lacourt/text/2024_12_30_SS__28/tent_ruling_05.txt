 Case Number:  22STCV14003    Hearing Date:   December 30, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On April
27, 2022, Plaintiffs Ana R. Rodriguez,
Cesar U. Hernandez, and Hazel Rodriguez (“Hazel Rodriguez”), a minor, by and
thru her guardian ad litem, Ana R. Rodriguez, filed this action against
Defendants Eva Marie Sandoval (“Defendant”) and Does 1-20 for motor vehicle
tort and general negligence. 
On April
29, 2022, the Court appointed Ana R. Rodriguez to serve as Plaintiff Hazel
Rodriguez’s guardian ad litem. 
On
June 29, 2022, Defendant filed an answer. 
On
October 17, 2024, Petitioner Ana R. Rodriguez (“Petitioner”)
filed a petition for expedited approval of the compromise of Plaintiff Hazel
Rodriguez’s claims. 
Trial
is currently scheduled for April 16, 2025. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
asks the Court to grant the petition for expedited approval of the compromise
of minor Plaintiff Hazel Rodriguez’s claims. 
DISCUSSION 
          Petitioner
has submitted a complete petition with all required information.  The Court grants the petition.  The Court orders that
the net settlement proceeds of $1,811.00 be paid or delivered to Petitioner at 326
N. Virmargo Street, Visalia, California, 93292, on the terms and under the
conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without bond. 

CONCLUSION 
The Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval
of the compromise of minor Plaintiff Hazel Rodriguez’s claims filed by
Petitioner Ana R. Rodriguez on October 17, 2024.
  The net settlement proceeds of $1,811.00 are be paid or
delivered to Petitioner Ana R. Rodriguez at 326 N. Virmargo Street, Visalia,
California, 93292, on the terms and under the conditions specified in Probate
Code sections 3401-3402, without bond.   
The Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net
Settlement on March 3, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring
Street Courthouse. 
          The
Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on March 3, 2025, at 8:30
a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner is ordered to file the proof of service of
this ruling with the Court within five days.
