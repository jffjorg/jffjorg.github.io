SUMMARY
OF ACTION
On
January 23, 2023, a vehicle driven by Nabil Khalil, with passengers Bassam and
Mram Khalil were struck by a vehicle driven by defendant Riley Runyon. On
August 23, 2023, Plaintiffs filed their complaint for Negligence.
 
RULING: Granted.
Plaintiff Nabil Khalil submitted a petition for approval for
minors compromises on behalf of Bassam Khalil (16) for a settlement with
defendant Runyon for $9,000. Petitioner represents total deductions of
$4,122.89 as follows: $2,400 in medical expenses, $2250 in attorney fees (25%),
less $227.11 in filing fees, thereby leaving a net balance of $4,122.89.
 
The balance is below $5,000, and therefore payable to the
parent. (Probate Code § 3401, 3611, subd. (a).)
 
Moving party to give notice.
