  Case Number:  23BBCV02572    Hearing Date:   November 18, 2024    Dept:  X PLAINTIFF’S MOTION TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO DISCOVERYThis is a lemon law case arising from Plaintiffs’ purchase of a 2022 GMC Yukon.  Plaintiffs move to compel further responses to their RFP (Set One) and request sanctions.Tentative RulingPlaintiff’s Motion is taken off-calendar.On October 16, 2024, the Court ordered the parties to meet and confer over the discovery at issue in the subject motion.  (10/16/24 Minute Order.)  For issues which remain in dispute, the parties could address those at the IDC scheduled for November 7, 2024.On November 5, 2024, the parties filed a joint informal discovery conference statement which informs the Court that the parties met and conferred telephonically on October 31, 2024 regarding the following RFP Nos. 1, 2, 3, 7, 8, 15, 16, 19, 20, 27, 28, 37, 38, 49, 50, 67, 68, 69, 70, 78, 79, 84, 86, 88, 97, 111, 112, 113, 114, 115, and 116.  (11/5/24 Joint IDC Statement.)The parties confirm they “met and conferred in good faith and have fully resolved the discovery dispute.  No further issues remain outstanding regarding Plaintiffs’ pending motion to compel further responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One.”  (Id., ¶ 4.)  Defendant agrees to provide further verified code-compliant responses and responsive documents in the next 60 days, not later than January 3, 2025.  (Id., ¶ 3.)The Court presumes all issues remain resolved and there is nothing going forward at this time. Thus, the motion to compel is taken off-calendar.In the absence of any appearing party, Plaintiff is to give notice.
