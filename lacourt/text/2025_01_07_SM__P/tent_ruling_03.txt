Weinstein v. Rise Luxury Rehab, LLC
Case no. 23SMCV03723
Hearing date January 7, 2024
Plaintiff’s
Counsel Saunders’ Motion to be Relieved as Counsel
Plaintiff
Eric Weinstein sues defendant Rise Luxury Rehab, LLC for negligence arising
from alleged violations of plaintiff’s privacy rights and safety during
plaintiff’s inpatient stay at defendant’s facility. Counsel
for plaintiff, Gary S. Saunders, moves to be relieved as counsel for
plaintiff, citing a breakdown in communications, plaintiff’s noncompliance with
discovery and general difficulties. Decl. Saunders para. 2. 
An
application to be relieved must be made on judicial counsel form MC-051 (Notice
of Motion and Motion) per Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(a), MC-052
(Declaration) per CRC rule 3.1362(c), and MC-053 (Proposed Order) per CRC rule
3.1362(e).¿The proposed order must specify all hearing dates scheduled in the
action or proceeding, including the date of trial, if known. CRC rule
3.1362(e). Plaintiff’s counsel has met all requirements under Cal. Rules of
Court 3.1362. 
Trial
is set for 3/24/25. Plaintiff has sufficient time to retain new counsel, so
will not be prejudiced via the granting of this motion. GRANTED.
