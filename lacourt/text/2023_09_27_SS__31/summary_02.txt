The tentative ruling grants unopposed motions to compel discovery responses from the plaintiff, Nicolet Barbarasa, and awards sanctions against the plaintiff. The defendant, FJS, INC. d/b/a The Anabella Hotel, served discovery requests on the plaintiff on April 25, 2023, but the plaintiff failed to respond. The defendant filed motions to compel responses, which were not opposed by the plaintiff. The court finds that the plaintiff was properly served with discovery requests and failed to respond, and therefore grants the motions to compel. The court awards sanctions in the amount of $1,180, including $1,000 in attorney fees and $180 in costs, against the plaintiff and their attorney. The plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses to the discovery requests within 20 days.
