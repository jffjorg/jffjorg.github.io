This is a tentative ruling by a judge regarding a case between Canyon Country Mobile Home Estates, LLC (the plaintiff) and Admiral Insurance Company (the defendant). The case revolves around an insurance policy issued by Admiral to Canyon Country, which covers Cleanup Costs resulting from a Pollution Condition at the Property. The judge grants summary judgment in favor of Admiral, determining that the connection of the septic system to the municipal sewer system is not a Corrective Action or Restorative Action, and that it falls under the "Compliance Actions and Improvements" exclusion. The judge concludes that Admiral's denial of coverage was proper.
