 ORDER RE: 
  
 
PLAINTIFF’S REQUEST FOR DEFAULT JUDGMENT
 




            
Plaintiff
Creditors Adjustment Bureau, Inc. (“Plaintiff”) requests entry of default
judgment against Defendant Mystery Baking Company, LLC aka Mystery Baking
Company. Plaintiff requests judgment in the total amount of $54,434.06, comprising
$46,804.98 demanded in the Complaint, $5,231.90 in
interest, $603.04 in costs, and $1,794.14 in attorney fees. 
Plaintiff
has filed the appropriate request for court judgment (Form CIV-100), a revised proposed
judgment (Form JUD-100), and a supplemental declaration to address the defects pertaining
to the requested interest as noted in the Court’s June 18, 2024 Order.
Consequently, the Court grants Plaintiff’s request for default judgment. The Court
has signed the revised judgment. No hearing is required.
