The judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Shareholder Representative Services, LLC v. SSCA, Inc. The judge has denied Respondent's request for judicial notice and overruled its objections to various portions of the Declaration of Kourtney Kinsel submitted in support of Shareholder Services' petition. The judge has also noted that Respondent must support its privilege objections with a privilege log.

The judge has concluded that Shareholder Services' petition to enforce the subpoena is granted and its motion to compel production of documents is granted as set forth above.
