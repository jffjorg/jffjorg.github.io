And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has ruled that the demurrer to the petition for writ of mandamus under CCP § 1094.5 is sustained. The respondent, Los Angeles County Child Support Services, is not a proper party in the case as it did not participate in the administrative proceedings. The petitioner, Earnest A. Davis, has failed to state how he is entitled to relief from the respondent under CCP § 1094.5. Additionally, the writ petition was filed untimely, and the petitioner has not alleged the filing of a claim with a government agency for money damages. The Judge has also denied the petitioner's request to amend the petition and has ordered the respondent to submit a proposed judgment of dismissal within five court days.
