**Significant Statements**

* "A party to an action who asserts a real property claim may record a notice of pendency of action in which that real property claim is alleged." (CCP § 405.20)
* "A lis pendens may be expunged on any of the following grounds: defects in the statutory service and filing requirements; the complaint does not contain a real property claim; the claimant cannot prove the probable validity of the real property claim by a preponderance of the evidence; or 'adequate relief' can be 'secured to the claimant by the giving of an undertaking.'" (CCP §§ 405.23, 405.31, 405.32, 405.33)
* "Probable validity, with respect to a real property claim, means that it is more likely than not that the claimant will obtain a judgment against the defendant on the claim." (CCP § 405.32)
* "A quiet title action is a type of COA that affects title to or the right to possession of specific real property." (CCP 760.020)
