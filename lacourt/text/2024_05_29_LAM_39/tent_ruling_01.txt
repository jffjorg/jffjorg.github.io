MOTIONS
            Judgment
Creditor Jacksonville Jaguars, LLC (“Judgment Creditor”) moves in two separate motions
to compel responses from Judgment Debtor Fuelster Technologies, Inc. (“Judgment
Debtor”) to Special Interrogatories, set one (“SROG”) and Requests for
Production, set one (“RPD”).  
ANALYSIS
Judgment Creditor
served the SROG and RPD on Judgment Debtor on July 7, 2023 by mail.  Judgment Debtor’s responses were thus due by no
later than August 11, 2023.  As of the
filing date of these motions, Judgment Debtor has not responded to the
discovery.  Accordingly, the motions to
compel responses to the SROG and RPD are granted pursuant to Code of Civil
Procedure sections 2030.290 and 2031.300, respectively.  Judgment Debtor is ordered to serve verified responses
to Judgment Creditor’s SROG and RPD without objections, within 30 days of service
of this order.
CONCLUSION AND ORDER
Judgment Creditor’s motions to compel responses
to the SROG and RPD are granted.  Judgment
Debtor is to serve verified responses, without objections, within 30 days of
notice of this order.  
Judgment Creditor is ordered to provide
notice of this order and file proof of service of same.
