And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has granted the Hollywood Foreign Press Association's (HFPA) motion for attorneys' fees in the reduced total amount of $322,354.90. The fees were incurred from May 18, 2022, to the present in connection with the anti-SLAPP motion and preparing the instant motion. The Court reduced the requested hours by 30% of the requested total, or $138,152.10. The Court found that HFPA's counsel's hourly rates were not reasonable in their community of practice and that the requested fees were excessive. The Court also reduced the hours claimed by HFPA's counsel for opposing and drafting evidentiary objections to Plaintiff's Discovery Motion.
