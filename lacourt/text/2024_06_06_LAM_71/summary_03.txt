**Case Information**

* Case Number: 23STCV05272
* Hearing Date: June 6, 2024
* Department: 71 Superior Court of California
* County: Los Angeles

**Parties**

* Plaintiff: SAMANTHA ANOBILE, CO-TRUSTEE OF THE SELMA L. ADELMAN TRUST OF 2009
* Cross-Defendant: JH PLUMBING CORPORATION, INC.
* Cross-Complainants: THE I. GRACE COMPANY, COMMISSIONED PRIVATE RESIDENCES, INC., SIERRA TOWERS 17 PROPERTY, LLC

**Summary**

* Cross-Defendant demurred to Cross-Complaint on the following bases:
	+ 1st cause of action for implied indemnity: fails to state facts because no tort can be alleged by Cross-Complainants against JH Plumbing and Cross-Complainants do not plead additional acts not covered by the express indemnity allegation.
	+ 2nd cause of action for equitable indemnity: fails to state facts because no tort can be alleged by Cross-Complainants against JH Plumbing and Cross-Complainants do not plead additional acts not covered by the express indemnity allegation.
	+ 5th cause of action for premises liability-negligence: fails to state facts because Cross-Complainants only plead economic damages, precluded in a negligence action.
	+ 7th and 8th causes of action for declaratory relief: sustained with 20 days leave to amend as to the 2nd and 5th causes of action and overruled as to the 1st, 7th, and 8th causes of action.

**Ruling**

* Cross-Defendant's demurrer to Cross-Complainant's 1st cause of action is overruled.
* Cross-Defendant's demurrer to Cross-Complainant's 2nd cause of action is sustained with 20 days leave to amend.
* Cross-Defendant's demurrer to Cross-Complainant's 5th cause of action is sustained with 20 days leave to amend.
* Cross-Defendant's demurrer to Cross-Complainant's 7th and 8th causes of action is overruled.
