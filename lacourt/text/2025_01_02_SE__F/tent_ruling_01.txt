  Case Number:  22NWCV01410    Hearing Date:   January 2, 2025    Dept:  F White v. Davita Inc.
(22NWCV01410)
This premises liability action was
filed on November 23, 2022. Trial is set for January 23, 2025. Plaintiff
Benjamin White now moves ex parte to continue trial to June 23, 2025, or a date
thereafter available to the Court. Plaintiff argues good cause exists to
continue trial because Plaintiff’s critical health condition has delayed his
ability to sit for deposition. Plaintiff has confirmed to be deposed on January
9, 2025. Further, additional discovery must be completed before trial. Lastly,
the parties have filed a Stipulation for an Order to Continue Trial, indicating
mutual agreement and good cause.
Plaintiff’s Ex Parte Application
for an Order to Continue Trial is GRANTED pursuant to the parties’ Stipulation
and CRC Rule 3.1332(c)(6)—A party's excused inability to obtain essential
testimony, documents, or other material evidence despite diligent efforts. The
Court will confer with the parties to select a new trial and final status
conference date. Discovery and all trial deadlines will track the
new trial date. Moving Party to give notice.
