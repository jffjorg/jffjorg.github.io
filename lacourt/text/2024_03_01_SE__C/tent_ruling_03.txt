 Case Number:  24NWCV00081    Hearing Date:   March 1, 2024    Dept:  C Del Rio
7444, LLC vs Juan Gonzales, et al., Case No. 24NWCV00081
This is an
unlawful detainer action. 
Plaintiff files
an Ex Parte Application for an order shortening Time to hear Defendant’s
Demurrer. 
The court, on
its own motion or on application for an order shortening time supported by a
declaration showing good cause, may prescribe shorter times for the filing and
service of papers than the times specified in Code of Civil Procedure §1005.
(Rule of Court Rule 3.1300.) 
All demurrers
and motions to strike filed in an unlawful detainer action shall be set for
hearing within ten calendar days consistent with California Rule of Court
3.1320(d). The Demurrer in this action was filed on February 9, 2024. However,
Plaintiff did not file ex parte relief until February 27, 2024. 
Accordingly, to
give Defendant an appropriate opportunity to prepare and be heard on the merits
the Court ADVANCES the Demurrer to today and CONTINUES the hearing to Thursday,
March 7, 2024 at 10:30 a.m. in Department SE-C. 
Plaintiff to
give notice.
