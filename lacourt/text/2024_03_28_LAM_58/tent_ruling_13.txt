




            This
is an unpaid invoices action. The Complaint alleges causes of action for (1) breach
of written contract, (2.) goods and services rendered, (3.) open book account,
and (4.) account stated. 
            
            The
Complaint was filed on September 11, 2023. Default was entered as to Defendant Made
by the Water, LLC (Defendant) on December 15, 2024. 
 
            Plaintiff
Packform USA LLC now moves
for default judgment against Defendant. Does 1-10 have been dismissed. The
request for entry of default judgment is granted. 
 
Discussion
 
            Plaintiff
seeks default judgment in the amount of $152,466.86, which includes $698.25 in costs
and $3,373.94 in attorney fees. 
 
            Plaintiff
submits sufficient evidence to support the amount incurred and owed by Defendant
based on the parties’ purchase orders. (Jackson Decl., ¶¶ 5-8, Exs. A-D.) Further,
Plaintiff substantiates the request for costs in the amount of $698.25. (CIV-100,
¶ 7; Wirtschafter Decl., ¶ 13.)
 
            Plaintiff
also substantiates the request for attorney’s fees in the amount of $3,373.94,
which is consistent with Los Angeles Superior Court Local Rule 3.214. (Wirtschafter
Decl., ¶¶ 10-12.) Plaintiff is not seeking prejudgment interest. 
 
Plaintiff’s request for entry of default judgment in the
amount of $152,466.86
is granted.
