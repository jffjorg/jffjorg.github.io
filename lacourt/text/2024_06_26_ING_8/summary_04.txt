**Case Information**

* Case Number: BC700634
* Hearing Date: June 26, 2024
* Department: 8

**Parties**

* Plaintiff: Jesse Franklin Esphorst
* Defendant: Tung Ming
* Intervenor: State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company

**Facts**

* On March 1, 2021, Plaintiff filed a lawsuit against Defendant and others
* On January 26, 2024, the Court entered judgment in favor of Plaintiff and against Defendant Tung Ming in the amount of $58,762,500
* On April 26, 2024, the Court reduced the non-economic damages by 20% and entered an Amended Judgment against Defendant Tung Ming in the amount of $47,410,000
* On May 3, 2024, Defendant Tung Ming filed a Notice of Appeal
* On May 14, 2024, Defendant Tung Ming and insurer served an undertaking on appeal in the amount of $60,000

**Legal Issues**

* The Court must determine whether the undertaking amount of $60,000 is sufficient to stay enforcement of the judgment
* The Court must apply Code of Civil Procedure section 917.1(a)(1) and (b) to determine the sufficiency of the undertaking

**Tentative Ruling**

* The Court DENIES Plaintiff's motion to object to the undertaking amount and motion for order that sufficient bond be given
* The Court finds that State Farm's undertaking in the amount of $60,000 is sufficient to stay enforcement of the part of the judgment within the policy limits
* The Court orders that only $60,000 of Plaintiff's judgment amount will be stayed pending appeal, not the rest of the $47,350,000
