The Judge's tentative ruling is a response to the Plaintiff's motion to set aside the dismissal of the case and enter judgment against the Defendant. The case was filed on June 11, 2020, and a Settlement Agreement and Stipulation for Dismissal was entered into on December 7, 2021. The Stipulation provided for Defendant to pay Plaintiff $4,800.00, with a portion of the payment to be made by Defendant's insurance carrier. However, Defendant has defaulted on payments, and Plaintiff has mailed a letter regarding the missed payments.

The Judge finds the Stipulation to be valid and enforceable under California Code of Civil Procedure section 664.6. However, the Judge is CONTINUING the Plaintiff's request pending additional clarification regarding its calculation of the principal and costs. The Judge is also ordering Plaintiff to provide additional clarification regarding its calculation of the principal and costs.
