BACKGROUND
            On June 25, 2024, Plaintiff Alonzo
Cornelius Avera filed this action for negligence and premises liability against
Defendant 1645 NW, LLC and Does 1 through 100.

request for entry of default against Defendant Houston Hospitality LLC (Doe 4).

Hospitality LLC filed the instant motion to set aside default. 
CONCLUSION

default has been entered against this Defendant. Defendant shall file a
responsive pleading within 5 days of this order.
