The court has issued a tentative ruling on three motions filed by the petitioner, Interinsurance Exchange of the Automobile Club. The motions ask the court to compel the respondent, Deshawn Smith, to respond to form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production of documents.

The court has granted the motions to compel responses to form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production of documents, with some limitations. The court has ordered respondent Smith to respond to form interrogatories, set one, without objections, within 20 days. The court has also ordered respondent Smith to respond further to special interrogatories, Nos. 14, 39, 40, 41, and 42, and to serve a verification, within 20 days. Additionally, the court has ordered respondent Smith to respond further to request for production of documents, set one, No. 12, and to serve a verification, within 20 days, but has denied the motion as to Nos. 21, 30, 31, 33, 36, 38, 40, 41, 42, and 44.

The court has also imposed sanctions against respondent Smith and his attorney of record in the amount of $2280.00, payable to the petitioner's counsel within 30 days. The court has ordered the moving party to give notice of the ruling.
