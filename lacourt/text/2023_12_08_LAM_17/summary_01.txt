The court has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to compel further responses to Requests for Production (RFPs) filed by Plaintiff Nicholas A. Thompson against Defendant General Motors, LLC. The court grants the motion in part, ordering Defendant to produce certain documents and materials, including the "Warranty Policy and Procedure Manual", internal analysis and investigation regarding transmission defects, customer complaints, and documents evidencing policies and procedures used to evaluate customer requests for repurchase. However, the court denies the motion in part, finding some of the RFPs to be overly broad in scope. The court also declines to award sanctions at this time. The parties who intend to submit on this tentative must send an email to the court by 4 p.m. the day prior to the hearing date.
