  Case Number:  19SMCV01536    Hearing Date:   June 21, 2024    Dept:  N TENTATIVE RULINGNick M. Campbell and Builders Law Group, Inc.’s Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Defendant J Baron Construction, Inc. is GRANTED.Counsel has asserted a valid reason for withdrawal, i.e., the attorney-client relationship has broken down. (Rules Prof. Conduct, rule 1.16(b)(4).) Counsel has provided the required forms under rule 3.1362 of the California Rules of Court and served the opposing parties and client with the same. There being good cause to grant counsel’s request to withdraw, Nick M. Campbell and Builders Law Group, Inc.’s Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Defendant J Baron Construction, Inc. is GRANTED. This ruling granting counsel’s motion to withdraw shall become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon Defendant J Baron Construction, Inc. Defendant J Baron Construction, Inc. is reminded that a corporation cannot represent itself in litigation and must be represented by licensed counsel. (CLD Construction, Inc. v. City of San Ramon (2004) 120 Cal.App.4th 1141, 1145.)
