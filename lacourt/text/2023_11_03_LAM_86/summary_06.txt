* Case Number: 23STCV07315

* Parties:
  - Plaintiff: Westlake Flooring Company, LLC (dba Westlake Flooring Services)
  - Defendant: LJT Holdings, LLC (dba Infiniti of Mission Viejo) and John Anthony Thompson

**Summary of the Ruling**

* The judge denies the application for a writ of possession against LJT Holdings, LLC (dba Infiniti of Mission Viejo) for approximately 34 vehicles.
* The judge denies the application for a writ of attachment against LJT Holdings, LLC and John Anthony Thompson in the amount of $2,678,264.

**Relevant Procedural History**

* The case was filed on April 3, 2023, and the answer was filed on September 6, 2023.
* The applications for a writ of possession and a writ of attachment were filed on June 30, 2023, and October 27, 2023, respectively.

**Applicable Law**

* Code of Civil Procedure sections 512.010, 512.040, 512.070, 484.010, 484.040, 484.090, and 484.050
* Epstein v. Abrams (1997) 57 Cal.App.4th 1159, 1168
* Balboa Ins. Co. v. Aguirre (1983) 149 Cal.App.3d 1002, 1010
* Edwards v. Superior Court (1991) 230 Cal.App.3d 173, 178

**Analysis**

* The plaintiff failed to demonstrate wrongful detention of the vehicles by LJT Holdings, LLC (dba Infiniti of Mission Viejo).
* The plaintiff failed to provide a statement of the value of each of the vehicles for which it seeks a writ of possession.
* The plaintiff failed to show probable cause to believe the vehicles are located at the address specified in the application.

**Conclusion**

* The application for writ of possession is denied.
* The application for writ of attachment is denied.
* The request for a preliminary injunction is denied.
