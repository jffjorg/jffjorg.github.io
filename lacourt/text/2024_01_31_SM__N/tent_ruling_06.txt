 Case Number:  23SMCV05004    Hearing Date:   January 31, 2024    Dept:  N TENTATIVE RULINGDennis P. Block and Dennis P. Block & Associates’ Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff Shawky Saad is GRANTED.Counsel has asserted a valid reason for withdrawal, i.e., the attorney-client relationship has broken down. (Rules Prof. Conduct, rule 1.16(b)(4), (b)(5).) Counsel has provided the required forms under rule 3.1362 of the California Rules of Court and served the opposing parties and client with the same. There being good cause to grant counsel’s request to withdraw, Dennis P. Block and Dennis P. Block & Associates’ Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff Shawky Saad is GRANTED. This ruling granting counsel’s motion to withdraw shall become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon Plaintiff Shawky Saad.
