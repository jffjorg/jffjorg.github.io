And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to compel responses to discovery requests. The motion was made by Plaintiff Orlando Garcia, who seeks to compel defendants A & D Properties, Inc. and Ani Grocery & Deli, Inc. to respond to discovery requests that were served on June 7, 2023. The defendants failed to serve timely responses, and the Plaintiff moved for sanctions.

The Judge granted the motion to compel responses to discovery and ordered the defendants to serve verified responses to the discovery requests within 10 days. The Judge also granted the motion to deem the truth of matters specified in the Requests for Admissions admitted as true.

The Judge ordered the defendants to pay additional filing fees of $420 and awarded attorney's fees of $1,600 to the Plaintiff. The Judge also noted that the defendants had waived their objections to the discovery requests and ordered them to respond to the requests.

The Judge reminded counsel that the Court will continue to conduct and encourage audio or video appearances, and encouraged counsel to make arrangements in advance if they wish to appear remotely.
