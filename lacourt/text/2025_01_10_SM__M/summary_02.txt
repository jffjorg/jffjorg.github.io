Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling:

The judge grants the motion to compel further responses and production of documents by Plaintiff Jose Fuentes. The motion is based on Plaintiff's Request for Production of Documents (RPD) Nos. 16, 19-21, 32, and 42, which seek documents related to General Motors LLC's internal investigation and analysis of the infotainment system defect in vehicles of the same year, make, and model as Plaintiff's vehicle. The judge finds that the requested documents are relevant to the issues related to General Motors LLC's good faith compliance with the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act and that the requests are reasonably tailored. The judge disagrees with General Motors LLC's argument that the requests are largely irrelevant and overbroad and orders further responses within 20 days.
