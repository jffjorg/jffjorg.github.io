 Case Number:  21STCV20677    Hearing Date:   October 12, 2023    Dept:  28 Having reviewed Defendant Jasmin Floyd’s unopposed Motion to Compel Responses to Requests for Production, the Court rules as follows.On July 10, 2023, Defendant Jasmin Floyd served Supplemental Requests for Production on Plaintiff Ernesto Gonzalez.Responses were due August 9, 2023. Plaintiff Ernesto Gonzalez has failed to provide timely responses. Defendant Jasmin Floyd now moves to compel code-compliant responses and the requested production.The Court grants the motion pursuant to Code of Civ. Proc. § 2031.300 and orders Plaintiff Ernesto Gonzalez to provide verified responses pursuant to Code of Civ. Proc. § 2031.210 and to produce the documents, electronically stored information, and/or other things requested without objections by October 22, 2023.Defendant Jasmin Floyd requests monetary sanctions totaling $960.00.The Court awards sanctions pursuant to Code of Civ. Proc. § 2023.030 (a) and Code of Civ. Proc. § 2031.300 (c) in the amount of $460.00, calculated as follows:Defendant requests $960.00 in sanctions, based on 4.5 hours of attorney’s work at a rate of $200.00 per hour and 1 $60.00 filing fee. 1 hour was spent drafting the motion, 2 hours are anticipated in replying to any opposition and 1.5 hours are anticipated in attending the hearing on the motion. The Court notes the motion is unopposed. The Court award sanctions totaling $460.00, based on 2 hours of attorney’s work.The sanctions total $460.00 and are payable by Plaintiff Ernesto Gonzalez and his counsel by November 11, 2023.Moving party is to give notice of this ruling.
