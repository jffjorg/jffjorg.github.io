The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a Motion to Compel Further Responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One, filed by Defendant Wendy M. Taylor and Jag Tax Management & Financial Services (Defendants). The Motion was filed on February 13, 2024, and seeks to compel Plaintiff W.H.S. Humble & Associates, LLC (Plaintiff) to provide further verified responses to the Request for Production of Documents, Set One, which was served on November 29, 2023.

The Judge has granted the Motion in part, compelling Plaintiff to provide supplemental responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One, Nos. 3, 7-39, 47-48, 50, 68, and 80-84. The Judge has also denied the Motion to Compel as to Nos. 1-2, 4-6, 40-46, 54, 56-59, 67, 69, 71-72, 75-76, 79, and 85-86, finding that Plaintiff's responses to these requests are compliant with the relevant code sections.

The Judge has also denied the Defendant's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $5,311.65, finding that the mandatory sanctions under Code of Civil Procedure section 2031.310 do not apply in this instance, and that there is no evidence of any misuses of the discovery process by the parties.

The Judge's ruling is based on the parties' conduct and the applicable code sections, including Code of Civil Procedure sections 2031.280, 2031.310, 2031.220, 2031.230, and 2031.240, as well as Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1345(a)(3), and Hill v. National Collegiate Athletic Assn. (1994) 7 Cal. 4th 1.
