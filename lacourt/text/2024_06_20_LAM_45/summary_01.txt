The Judge, Mel Red Recana, has issued a tentative ruling in a medical negligence and elder abuse action brought by the estate of Mary Lloyd against various defendants, including Kindred Healthcare Operating, LLC (KHO) and Sarthi Shah, M.D.

The court has denied KHO's motion for summary judgment with respect to the Fourth Amended Complaint, finding that Plaintiffs have created a triable issue of material fact as to the agency and/or joint venture relationship between KHO and Kindred Hospital. The court has also denied KHO's motion for summary adjudication as to the first, second, third, and fourth causes of action of the Fourth Amended Complaint.

In contrast, the court has granted Defendant Shah's motion for summary judgment, finding that Plaintiffs have conceded the merits of the motion and have not disputed any of Defendant's material facts.

The case involves allegations of medical negligence and elder abuse against Kindred Hospital, which was owned and operated by KHO during the period of decedent's hospitalization. The court has found that KHO and Kindred Hospital had an agency relationship or joint venture, and that KHO exercised control over Kindred Hospital's operations, including setting policies and procedures.
