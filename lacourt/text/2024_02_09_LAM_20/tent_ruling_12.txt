This is an employment action.  Plaintiff Alberto Moncada seeks to compel Chubb Group of Insurance Companies to comply with a subpoena issued to it.  Plaintiff seeks “workers compensation claims and diary notes as well as investigative records for Plaintiff’s workers compensation bearing Chubb Group claim number 076916026971 and Los Angeles Workers' Compensation Appeals Board Case Number ADJ10605607.”Because there is no opposition, the Motion is granted.  (Cal. Rules of Court, Rule 8.54(c); Sexton v. Superior Court (1997) 58 Cal.App.4th 1403, 1410.)  The production is to be completed within 30 days.  Plaintiff requests $3,200 in sanctions, but the Court awards reduced sanctions in the amount of $1,000.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
