And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge grants the Petitioner's request for default judgment against the Respondent, Yuval Ziv. The Petitioner, Dry Care Restoration, Inc., performed emergency mitigation services at the Respondent's property but was refused payment. The parties had agreed to mandatory and binding arbitration, which resulted in an award in favor of the Petitioner in the amount of $63,131.61, plus interest, fees, and costs. The Respondent was served by publication and did not respond, and as a result, the Petitioner successfully requested entry of default. The Judge finds that the Petitioner has complied with all substantive and procedural requirements for a default judgment and awards default judgment in the amount of $75,520.22 in favor of the Petitioner.
