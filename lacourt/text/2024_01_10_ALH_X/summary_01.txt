This tentative ruling addresses a motion filed by the defendants, TLD Transport, Inc. and Zheng Zhao, seeking leave to conduct neurological examinations of the plaintiffs, Veronica Prado and Anthony Narango. The plaintiffs were injured in a motor vehicle accident and have sought medical attention and been examined by neurologists. The defendants argue that they need to conduct the examinations to assess and evaluate the plaintiffs' claims, and claim that good cause exists for the examinations. The court finds that good cause has been shown and denies the motion without prejudice, stating that the defendants have failed to provide sufficient information for the court to grant the motion. The court orders the defendants to provide additional information regarding the manner, diagnostic tests and procedures, conditions, and scope of the examination.
