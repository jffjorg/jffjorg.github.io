And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted Plaintiff Keenan McNair's motion to compel responses to special interrogatories and request for sanctions. The defendant, LA Live Theater, LLC, is ordered to serve verified responses to the special interrogatories without objections within 20 days of the order. The defendant and their counsel are also jointly and severally ordered to pay sanctions of $500.00 to Plaintiff's counsel within 20 days of the order. The judge found that the defendant's failure to timely respond to the propounded discovery was a misuse of the discovery process and that sanctions were appropriate under California Code of Civil Procedure sections 2023.010 and 2023.030.
