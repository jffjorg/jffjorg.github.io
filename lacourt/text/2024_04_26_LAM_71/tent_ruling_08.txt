Plaintiff 1200
Management LLC’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On August 18, 2023,
1200 Management LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed this action against Defendants Jackie
Bunao and Alan Bunao (collectively, “Defendants”) for recovery of COVID-19
Rental Debt. 
On September 29,
2023, the clerk entered default against Defendant Alan Bunao.  On October 5, 2023, the clerk entered default
against Defendant Jackie Bunao.  On December
26, 2023, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be heard on January 8,
2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendants Jackie Bunao and Alan Bunao and award Plaintiff $62,346.98
consisting of $46,908.80 as the demand of the complaint, $14,736.98 in
prejudgment interest, and $701.20 in costs. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff
submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments.
The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants
Plaintiff’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application
for default judgment filed on December 26, 2023, is granted.  Judgment of $62,346.98 is entered against Defendants.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
