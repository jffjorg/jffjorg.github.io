This is a matter relating to alleged misrepresentations as to the need to obtain an “add-on” package when purchasing a vehicle, as well as the failure to provide the “add-on” benefits and an undamaged vehicle.Defendant HODLA Motors, LLC seeks to compel arbitration based on Plaintiff’s vehicle purchase agreement.Because there is no opposition, the Motion is granted.  (Cal. Rules of Court, Rule 8.54(c); Sexton v. Superior Court (1997) 58 Cal.App.4th 1403, 1410.) This action is stayed pending the resolution of arbitration.  (Code Civ. Proc. § 1281.4.)  Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
