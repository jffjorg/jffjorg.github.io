The Judge's tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel further responses to form interrogatories filed by Plaintiff Uriel Calzada Martinez and Aracely De Los Santos against Defendant Jerome Winfield and Does 1 through 50. The case arises from a motor vehicle accident on January 16, 2021, near the intersection of South Western Avenue and West 89th Street in Los Angeles. The Plaintiff served discovery requests on August 22, 2023, and Defendant provided untimely responses on October 25, 2023. The responses include both objections and substantive responses, but are not verified. The Plaintiff attempted to meet and confer with Defendant to obtain the verifications and substantive responses, but Defendant did not provide the verifications and suggested an exchange instead. The Plaintiff seeks to compel further responses and seeks sanctions against Defendant. The Judge will hear from counsel regarding the current status of the discovery issues and will rule on the motion.
