Plaintiff Vail-Date Properties, LLC’s Request
for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On December 19,
2023, Vail-Date Properties, LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed its Complaint against Defendant
LJ & J Distributors, Inc. (“Defendant”) for unlawful detainer.
On March 13, 2024,
the clerk entered default against Defendant. 
On July 24, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be
heard on September 17, 2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendant and award Plaintiff $107,410.37 consisting
of $57,754.41 in past-due rent, $46,121.40 in holdover damages, $2,928.76 in
attorneys’ fees, and $605.80 in costs. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted
a completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments. The Court
finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants Plaintiff Vail-Date
Properties, LLC’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application
for default judgment filed on July 24, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $107,410.37 is entered against Defendant LJ & J Distributors, Inc.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
