The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to quash service of summons and complaint brought by the defendants, Choice Hotels International Inc., Paladin Equity Capital LLC, Philip Powers, and Hotel Management and Consulting HMC. However, the judge finds that this is not an unlawful detainer proceeding and therefore section 1167.1 is not the proper statutory basis for the defendants' motion. The judge denies the motion to quash and orders the defendants to provide notice of this order and to file proof of service of same.
