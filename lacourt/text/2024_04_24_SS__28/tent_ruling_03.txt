 Case Number:  20STCV36707    Hearing Date:   April 24, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
September 24, 2020, Plaintiff Caleb Chevy Bardling-Velasco (“Plaintiff”), by
and through his guardian ad litem Allena Velasco, filed a complaint against
Defendants Bandini Street Elementary School, Los Angeles Unified School
District, LA’s Best, and Does 1-100 for (1) vicarious liability under
Government Code sections 815, 815.2, 815.4, 815.6 and 820, (2) breach of
mandatory duty under Government Code section 815.6, and (3) general negligence. 
On
March 24, 2021, Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint against Los Angeles
Unified School District (“Defendant”) and Does 1-50 for liability under
Government Code sections 815, 815.2, 815.4, 815.6 and 820. 
On
April 26, 2021, Defendant filed an answer. 
On
May 26, 2023, Plaintiff filed a notice of settlement. 
On
March 29, 2024, 2024, Petitioner Allena Velasco (“Petitioner”) filed a petition
to approve minor’s compromise to be heard on April 24, 2024. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Allena Velasco asks the Court to approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff
Caleb Chevy Bardling-Velasco’s pending action. 
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed petition with all applicable attachments. The Court
grants the petition. 
Petitioner
requests that the net balance be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity
as explained in Attachment 18a(3)(b). The Court orders the net balance so
distributed. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff Caleb
Chevy Bardling-Velasco’s pending action filed by Petitioner Allena Velasco on
March 29, 2024.  The net balance of the
settlement proceeds ($43,263.48) is to be invested in a single-premium deferred
annuity as explained in Attachment 18a(3)(b) to the petition. 
The
Court sets an Order to Show Cause Re: Distribution of Net Settlement on June
27, 2024, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
The
Court sets an Order to Show Cause Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on June
27, 2024, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
