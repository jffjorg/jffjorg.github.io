The court has granted a tentative decision to grant a writ of possession to Sierra Credit Corporation (Sierra) against Guadelupe Ramos (Ramos) to recover a 2008 Toyota Tundra. Ramos breached the contract by failing to make timely payments and the vehicle was assigned to Sierra. The court found that Sierra has established the probable validity of its claim to possession of the vehicle and that the undertaking requirements of CCP section 515.010 are satisfied. The undertaking for the writ of possession is $5,153.86. The court also granted a turnover order directing Ramos to transfer possession of the vehicle to Sierra.
