The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion for summary judgment filed by Apple, Inc. in a case brought by Diego Mejia Duarte, alleging violations of the Private Attorneys General Act (PAGA). Apple argues that Duarte does not have standing because he did not suffer any Labor Code violations. Duarte argues that his COVID-19 bonus should have been included in his regular rate of pay. The court finds that there are triable issues for the matter of standing and denies Apple's motion for summary judgment.
