


[1] The court is cognizant Plaintiff
is a self-represented litigant.  However,
the court must still apply the law as it would with any other litigant.  (See, e.g., Nwosu v. Uba (2004)
122 Cal.App.4th 1229, 1247 [self-represented litigants are  “ ‘treated like any other party and [are]
entitled to the same, but no greater consideration than other litigants
and attorneys.’ [Citation.]”].)
