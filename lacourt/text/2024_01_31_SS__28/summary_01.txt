The Court rules as follows on the parties' requests. The City of West Hollywood seeks to tax its costs in an amount no greater than $6,734.21 and award $8,283.77 in costs. Plaintiff Gloria Edel requests that the Court tax $14,938.76 of the City's costs. After considering the moving, opposition, and reply papers, the Court grants in part Plaintiff's motion to tax costs and taxes the City's costs in the amount of $7,853.79. The Court awards the City $7,164.19 in costs. The City's request for $650.00 in costs for the late cancellation of a deposition is denied, and the Court taxes this amount. The Court also grants Plaintiff's motion to tax $407.10 in costs for service of process and denies the motion to tax the remaining $1,495.54 in costs for subpoenaed records/production. The Court grants Plaintiff's motion to tax $6,327.11 in costs for investigation and expert fees.
