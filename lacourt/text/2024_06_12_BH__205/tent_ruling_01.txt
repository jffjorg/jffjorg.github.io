  Case Number:  24SMCV00244    Hearing Date:   June 12, 2024    Dept:  205    Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles – West District  Beverly Hills Courthouse / Department 205   OSLY PENA,   Plaintiff, v.  WESTWOOD PLACE INVESTORS, LLC, et al.,   Defendants.    Case No.:  24SMCV00244     Hearing Date:  June 12, 2024   [TENTATIVE] ORDER RE:    DEFENDANTS WESTWOOD PLACE     INVESTORS, LLC AND DOUGLAS     EMMETT INC.’S DEMURRER AND     MOTION TO STRIKE      This action arises from a slip and fall accident involving Plaintiff Osly Pena.  The slip and fall allegedly occurred on March 10, 2022 at 10866 Wilshire Boulevard, Los Angeles, which Plaintiff claims is owned by both Defendants Westwood Place Investors, LLC and Douglas Emmett, Inc. The operative complaint alleges claims for (1) general negligence, and (2) premises liability.  Plaintiff personally served the complaint on Defendants on January 23, 2024.  Defendants were required to respond by February 22, 2024.  Defendants failed to do so, instead filing a declaration for automatic extension on March 7, 2024, well after their deadline to respond had already passed.   But even assuming they had a right to the automatic extension, Defendants were required to file their responsive pleading within 30 days, or by April 6, 2024.  Defendants did not file their demurrer and motion to strike until May 1, 2024.   Accordingly, the Court OVERRULES Defendants’ demurrer and DENIES their motion to strike as untimely.      IT IS SO ORDERED.  DATED: June 12, 2024    ___________________________ Edward B. Moreton, Jr. Judge of the Superior Court
