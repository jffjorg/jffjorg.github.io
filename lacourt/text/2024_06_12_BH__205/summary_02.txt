This is a tentative ruling regarding a motion to quash service of summons filed by Defendant Faisal AlHusseini (Moving Defendant). The Plaintiff, Clover Private Credit Opportunities Origination (Levered) II, LP, filed a complaint alleging two claims for fraudulent transfer related to the Saudi Loan and the Venice Transfer. The Court finds that Moving Defendant's motion is untimely and denies the motion to quash service of summons. The Court concludes that the Plaintiff properly served the summons and complaint on Moving Defendant by substituted service on April 3, 2024, and that the motion to quash was due by May 13, 2024.
