And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The tentative ruling grants summary judgment as to Defendant Gore Bros, finding that they conducted a reasonable inspection in 2016 and found no defects. The court denies summary judgment and summary adjudication as to Defendant 4301 Beverly, finding that they failed to detect the defective condition during the final walk-through inspection.

The court finds that the defendants conducted thorough inspections in 2016 and 2017-2018, and found no dangerous conditions. The court also finds that the plaintiff's expert, Hughes, opines that the condition depicted in the photograph would not have developed over a period of only four months, and that the defendants failed to detect the defective condition during the final walk-through inspection.

The court concludes that the defendants breached their duty to the plaintiff by failing to detect the defective condition, and that they contributed to the subject incident. The court grants summary judgment as to Defendant Gore Bros, and denies summary judgment and summary adjudication as to Defendant 4301 Beverly.
