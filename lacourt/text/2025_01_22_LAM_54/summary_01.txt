The judge has tentatively ruled that the plaintiff's motion to enforce a settlement agreement is granted. The settlement was executed on March 5, 2024, between the plaintiff, Maria Luna, and the defendant, Jose Angel Rodriguez, dba JR Construction and Concrete. The settlement provides for the defendant to pay the plaintiff $50,000 in eight monthly installments of $6,250 each, beginning on March 15, 2024, and ending on October 15, 2024.
