This tentative ruling grants defendant's motions to compel responses from plaintiff Gal Moshe Faigel to its Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, Requests for Production (RFPs), and Requests for Admissions (RFAs). The defendant is also sanctioned jointly and severally with counsel $700 per motion. The plaintiff failed to respond to the discovery requests despite being granted extensions. The defendant's motions were not opposed by the plaintiff, and therefore, the plaintiff did not provide a substantial justification for the failure to respond.
