The tentative ruling denies the motion to dismiss the plaintiff's (J&C Global Holdings, Inc.) first amended complaint, filed by defendants NMS Fin Holdings, LLC, NMS Capital Advisors, LLC dba HB Securities, LLC, NMS Capital Group, LLC, BDMGMT Partners, LLC, and Trevor Saliba. The defendants had moved to dismiss the complaint due to the plaintiff's lack of capacity and failure to provide proof of its corporate status. The plaintiff had previously filed a notice of revival and provided certified copies of documents showing it is an active corporation in good standing in Nevada. The court found that the plaintiff's revival paperwork is genuine and that the plaintiff may continue in the action.
