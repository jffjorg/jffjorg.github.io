This is a tentative ruling by the Judge regarding a motion filed by Plaintiff Lorenzo Cerullo for attorney fees and costs. The Plaintiff is seeking $35,055 in attorney fees and $2,657.70 in other expenses under Civil Code section 1794(d). The Defendant, Rolls-Royce Motor Cars NA, LLC, objects to the declaration of Stephen Parnell and argues that the Plaintiff's counsel's hourly rates should be based on the 2021 Real Rate Report. The Judge overrules the objection and finds that the Plaintiff's counsel's rates reflect the fair market value of their services. The Judge also reduces the Plaintiff's lodestar by $2,035 due to excessive billing for the motion. However, the Judge rejects the Plaintiff's request to adjust the lodestar for inflation or by a 1.25 multiplier. The Judge grants the Plaintiff's motion for attorney fees and costs in part, awarding the Plaintiff $33,020 in attorney fees and $2,657.70 in other expenses.
