 Case Number:  21STCV35437    Hearing Date:   July 19, 2024    Dept:  61 CHEVALIER HARRIS, et al. vs FRANK ELIAS, et al.TENTATIVEThe present motions pertain to attorney Michael A. Lanphere of the Lanphere Law Group, P.C., counsel for Plaintiffs Chevalier Harris, individually and as successor in interest for Decedent Freddie Harris. Lanphere submits a declaration stating he has previously put his client on notice of his intention to retire on June 30, 2024, but that despite providing substitution of attorney forms to his client, no response has been offered. (Lanphere Decl. at p. 1.) The motion complies with the above requirements, and trial is not set until August 26, 2025.Michael A. Lanphere’s Motions to be Relieved as counsel for Plaintiffs Chevalier Harris and Chevalier Harris as a successor in interest for Decedent Freddie Harris are GRANTED, effective upon moving counsel filing with the court proof of service of copies of the signed orders on the client, as set forth in Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1362(e).Moving Counsel to give notice.
