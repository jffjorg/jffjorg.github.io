 Case Number:  23STCV11652    Hearing Date:   October 4, 2023    Dept:  14 Case Background
 
Plaintiffs allege that Defendants
are trying to foreclose on an expired lien against Plaintiffs’ property.
 
            On May 23,
2023, Plaintiffs filed their verified Complaint for (1) Quiet Title, (2) Declaratory
Relief, (3) Cancellation of Instruments, and (4) Injunctive Relief against
Defendants Clear Recon Corp. (“Clear”), PNC Bank, N.A. (“PNC”), and DOES 1-20. 
 
            Defendants
Clear and PNC were served on May 26, 2023. No responsive pleading has been
filed, and no attempt to enter default has been made.
 
            No trial
date is currently set.
            
Instant Motion
 
            Plaintiffs
now move this court for an order enjoining Defendants from proceeding with
foreclosure while this action is pending.
 
Decision
 
            Plaintiffs’
Request for Judicial Notice is GRANTED.
 
            The
motion is GRANTED.
 
The motion is unopposed. This
operates as a concession by the other parties that the motion has merit. See California Rules of Court Rule 3.1113(a); see also
Rule 3.1320(f); Herzberg v. County of Plumas (2005) 133 Cal.App.4th
1, 20.
