And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has reviewed the legal sufficiency of the Complaint and has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by TransUnion and the motion to strike filed by TransUnion. The Court sustains the demurrer as to the second and third causes of action, overrules the demurrer as to the first cause of action, and grants the motion to strike in part.

The Court finds that the alleged violations of the ICRAA fail to support a tort cause of action for invasion of privacy. The Court agrees with TransUnion that Plaintiffs have failed to allege any actual, present controversy.

The Court denies TransUnion's motion to strike the remainder of Plaintiff's Prayer for Relief on the basis that the ICRAA claim fails. The Court finds that Plaintiffs have pled sufficient facts to support recovery of damages and punitive damages pursuant to the first cause of action.

The Court grants Plaintiffs 10 days to amend their Complaint and overrules TransUnion's demurrer as to the first cause of action. The Court sustains the demurrer as to the second and third causes of action and grants the motion to strike in part.
