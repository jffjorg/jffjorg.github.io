This tentative ruling grants in part the Plaintiff's Motion for Discovery Sanctions against Defendant Jo Bog Yoo. The Plaintiff, Immigrant Rights Defense Council, LLC, has been seeking sanctions due to Defendant's failure to comply with the court's orders and discovery requests. The Defendant failed to provide responses as ordered by the court, and this failure required two court orders and one order of monetary sanctions to obtain responses. The Court awards monetary sanctions against the Defendant in the amount of $3,310.00, payable to the Plaintiff within 30 days. Furthermore, the Court warns the Defendant that further failure to comply with the court's discovery orders may result in default being entered against him.
