On
November 3, 2023, Defendant Daniel E. Park filed motions to compel Plaintiff Central
Metal, Inc.’s responses to Special Interrogatories and to Deem Requests for
Admission Admitted.
Concurrently
with filing its Oppositions, Plaintiff produced responses.  (Eanet Decl. ¶ 7.)  Plaintiff provides a copy of the responses
and proof of this electronic service.  (Eanet
Decl., Exs. A-D.)  Defendant confirms that
late responses have been provided.  (See generally
Replies.)
Accordingly,
the Motion to Compel Responses to Special Interrogatories is DENIED.  The Motion to Deem Requests for Admission
Admitted is also DENIED.  (See Code Civ.
Proc., § 2033.280, subd. (c).)
The
requests for sanctions are also denied.
