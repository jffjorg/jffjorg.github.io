This is a matter relating to an underinsured motorist claim.  Petitioner James River Insurance Company seeks dismissal of this suit because Respondent Imienfan Odigie has failed to retain counsel or to take any action with regard to discovery or the arbitration proceedings between the parties.Code Civ. Proc. § 583.410 allows the Court to dismiss an action in its discretion in appropriate circumstances due to delay in prosecution.Because there is no opposition and it appears that Respondent has failed to prosecute its claim, the Motion to Dismiss is granted.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
