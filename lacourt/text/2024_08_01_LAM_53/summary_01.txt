And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court grants plaintiff Seran Ng, D.M.D., Inc.'s motion to compel defendant Yang Lu to serve responses to requests for production of documents, Set 12, and for monetary sanctions. The court finds that defendant did not serve timely responses to the requests and that defendant's objections to the requests were not timely. The court orders defendant to serve full and complete verified responses, without objections, to the requests and to produce all documents and things in defendant's possession, custody, or control that are responsive to the requests. The court also orders defendant to pay monetary sanctions to plaintiff in the amount of $2,186.65.
