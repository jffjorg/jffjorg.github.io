The judge has granted Jaime Adolfo De La Vega's application for default judgment against Mynor Rolando Mejia Hernandez in the reduced amount of $34,838.46. The court found that De La Vega had submitted sufficient evidence to support the judgment. The damages awarded to De La Vega are limited to the costs incurred in hiring a new contractor to complete the work and correct the deficient work done by Mejia Hernandez, which totals $28,544.60. The court reduced the award of interest to $2,518.18 and the award of attorney fees to $1,246.34. The total judgment awarded is $34,838.46. The court also set an OSC Re: Default Judgment for August 8, 2024.
