The Judge's tentative ruling sets an Order to Show Cause (OSC) for proof of service for the plaintiff, Pawnee Leasing Corporation, to show cause why it has failed to file proof of service against the defendants, One Mode Nutrition and James Lee Woods, for over 6 months. The Judge notes that the complaint must be served on all named defendants and proof of service filed within 60 days after filing of the complaint, and that violation of this rule may lead to monetary sanctions against the plaintiff or the plaintiff's attorney.
