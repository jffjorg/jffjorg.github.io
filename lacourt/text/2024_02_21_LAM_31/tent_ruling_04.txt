MOTIONS
TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO DISCOVERY 



 
MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Sig Ep Housing of California Beta, LLC
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant Savas
Sirikci
 
 
This is action arises from a breach of a written contract.  On January 12, 2024, Plaintiff, Sig Ep
Housing of California Beta, LLC, filed these motions to compel Defendant, Savas
Sirikci, to provide further responses to Plaintiff’s Special Interrogatories,
Set One, and Request for Production of Documents, Set One.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of these motions.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motions to April 25, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31.  Thereafter the parties are
to contact the judicial assistant to schedule an IDC date and time.  
 
Clerk of the Court to give notice.
