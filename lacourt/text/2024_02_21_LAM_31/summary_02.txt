This is a tentative ruling by Judge Kerry Bensinger in the case of The Redeemed Christian Church of God-Jesus Embassy, Los Angeles v. Mutiu Olatunji Durosinmi-Etti, et al. (Case No. 21STCV35730). The case concerns a breach of contract regarding real property. Plaintiff filed a motion for sanctions against Defendants and their counsel on December 4, 2023. However, the motion is procedurally defective because it was not served on the Defendants and does not state the grounds for issuance of the order. As a result, Judge Bensinger denies the motion for sanctions without prejudice.
