And here is the English-text summary of the Tentative Ruling:

The Judge has ruled on the demurrer filed by the defendants, WorldMark, The Club, and Wyndham Resort Development Corporation, to the 1st and 2nd causes of action of the complaint filed by the plaintiff, James Cisneros. The Judge has OVERULED the demurrer, meaning that the complaint will proceed to the next stage.

The plaintiff alleges that the defendants breached a written contract to utilize certain timeshare units allotted under a contract from January 20, 2004, for paid premiums. The plaintiff seeks damages for breach of contract and common counts, including money had and received.

The Judge has granted the request for judicial notice as to Exhibits 3-4 and denied it as to Exhibits 1-2. The Judge has also noted that the fact that the defendants suspended the plaintiff's account due to his conviction is not judicially noticeable.

The Judge has ordered the defendants to file and serve their Answers within 10 days.
