Plaintiff is to contact Department 17 to schedule an IDC.
 
            On
9/25/2023, Plaintiff James Reynolds (Plaintiff) filed suit against Stewart
Title of California, and Steve M. Lessack (collectively, Defendants), alleging:
(1) sexual harassment (quid pro quo; hostile work environment; and perceived
sexual orientation); (2) retaliation; (3) failure to prevent; and (4) wrongful
termination.  
 
            On
7/31/2024, Plaintiff moved to compel further responses to his Requests for
Production (RFPs) and Form Interrogatories. 
 
This Court requires that parties participate in an
informal discovery conference (IDC) before motions to compel further discovery
can be heard on the merits.
 
Plaintiff is to contact Department 17 to schedule an IDC.
                    
 
It is so ordered.
