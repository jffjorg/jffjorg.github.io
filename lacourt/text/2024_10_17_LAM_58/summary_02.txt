The tentative ruling grants the Petition to Confirm Minor's Compromise filed by Plaintiff Alba Ceren Hernandez on behalf of her minor children, Junior Torres and Khloe Ceren. The petition relates to a real property action filed on September 21, 2022, alleging that the defendants subjected the plaintiffs to substandard and dangerous living conditions and caused them to pay rent for illegal dwelling units. The settlement amount is $5,000.00, which will be paid or delivered to Junior Torres, the claimant, via check, as the parent of the claimant, without bond, on the terms and under the conditions specified in Probate Code Sections 3401-3402. The proposed order approving the compromise is signed.
