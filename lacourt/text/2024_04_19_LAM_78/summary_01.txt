This is a tentative ruling by Judge Jill Feeney regarding a motion to compel responses from Defendant Jorge Porras Chavez to Plaintiff Westlake Services, LLC's Request for Production, Set One. The Defendant failed to serve timely code-compliant responses to the requests, despite being granted an extension. The Court finds that the Defendant's failure to respond is a misuse of the discovery process and imposes sanctions of $1,250. The Court orders the Defendant to serve verified code-compliant responses and produce any responsive documents within 20 days.
