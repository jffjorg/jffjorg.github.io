The court has granted the motion filed by Rock Creek Capital, LLC (plaintiff) seeking an order deeming the truth of matters specified in the Request for Admissions served on Norma S. The court has ruled that due to the defendant's failure to respond, the plaintiff is entitled to an order deeming the matters admitted, as per California Civil Procedure Code section 2033.280(b), (c).
