 Case Number:  24NWCV00981    Hearing Date:   May 1, 2024    Dept:  C  
Cerritos
Retail Centercal, LLC, vs Wokcano Cerritos, LLC, Case No. 24NWCV00981
This is an
unlawful detainer action.  Plaintiff Cerritos Retail Centercal, LLC moves
ex parte for an order shortening time on Defendant’s Motion to Quash Service of
Summons and Complaint. 
The hearing is
currently set for Wednesday, January 29, 2025. Unlawful detainers are summary
proceedings entitled to priority in trial setting. (CCP § 1170.5 and 1179(a).)
CCP § 1167.4(a) states that hearings on motions to quash in unlawful detainer
actions should be heard within three to seven days after the filing date of the
Motion to Quash. 
The ex parte
application is GRANTED.  Defendant’s Motion to Quash is ADVANCED from
January 29, 2025 to this date and CONTINUED to Thursday, May 9, 2024 at 9:30
a.m. in Department SE-C.  Plaintiff’s Opposition was filed on April 30,
2024.  Defendant’s Reply is due May 6, 2024.  
Moving party to
give notice.
