The Court GRANTS the Petition to Approve Compromise of
Pending Action of a Minor Isaiah Avila, age 8,.
 
The Court GRANTS the Petition to Approve Compromise of Pending Action of a Minor Kylee
Jimenez, age 3. 
 
Discussion
 
On August 11, 2023, the Court
issued a minute order raising several deficiencies with the petitions:
 
With regard to Isaiah
Avila, age 8, first Petitioner has not identified the total medical expenses
incurred in Section 12a (1). 
 
Second, Petitioner has not
provided information about Claimant’s medical service providers in Section
12b(5)(b). 
 
Third, there is a
discrepancy in the balance to be paid to the claimant after accounting for fees
and expenses. The total fees and expenses are $578.48, but Petitioner says the
total is $467.50 and that the net balance of proceeds is $421.52. Petitioner also
says that the balance of proceeds is $356.52 and that $356.52 is the amount to
be paid to the minor by way of the guardian
 
With regard to Kylee
Jimenez, age 3, Petitioner has not provided information about Claimant’s
medical service providers in Section 12b(5)(b).
 
 
On October
12, 2023, Petitioner filed amended petitions and declarations of counsel that
address and resolve the issues presented above.  As amended, the petition meets all substantive and procedural requirements.  Therefore, the Court GRANTS the petitions.
