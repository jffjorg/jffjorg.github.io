 Case Number:  22NWCV00320    Hearing Date:   December 12, 2023    Dept:  C The Pacific Southwest
District of The Lutheran Church-Missouri Synod Vs Irene Jensen, et al.,
22NWCV00320
This action was filed on
April 26, 2022. Trial is set for July 25, 2024. The hearing for the four motion
to compel further discovery responses are currently scheduled for July 24,
2024. Plaintiff argues it should have the opportunity to conduct discovery
prior to trial. Plaintiff makes a showing that it would be irreparably harmed
if not granted relief ex parte.  (See Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)
 
In opposition, Defendant
makes a request, due to unavailability, that this motion not be heard until
after March 20, 2024. 
Accordingly, Plaintiff
Pacific Southwest District of the Lutheran Church – Missouri Synod’s Ex Parte
Motion to ADVANCE the four Motions to Compel Further is GRANTED. 
The four Motions to Compel Further Discovery is ADVANCED from Wednesday,
July 24, 2024, to Wednesday, April 3, 2024. Opposition
and Reply papers to be filed and served per Code in accordance with the new
hearing date. 
 
Moving Party(s)
to give notice.
