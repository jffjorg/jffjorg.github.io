And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Charles Olaniyi v. AJIM Baksh dba Baksh Construction, et al. The Plaintiff, Charles Olaniyi, is seeking judgment against Defendant Mary Castellon for alleged damages totaling $259,822. The Plaintiff has filed a fourth amended complaint alleging two causes of action for fraud and violation of Civil Code § 1102 against Defendant Castellon.

The Court has reviewed the evidence and finds that the Plaintiff has not provided sufficient evidence to support the full amount of damages requested. The Court orders the Plaintiff to file a supplemental declaration substantiating the full amount requested and additional evidence if necessary.

The Court also orders the Plaintiff to resubmit the amended Request for Entry of Default as described above and a supplemental declaration substantiating the principal amount of damages requested. The hearing has been continued to November 28, 2023, at 8:30 a.m. in Department A of the Compton Courthouse.
