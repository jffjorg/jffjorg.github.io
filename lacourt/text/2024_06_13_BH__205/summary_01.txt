The court has issued a tentative ruling on a plaintiff's motion to compel responses to special interrogatories and for sanctions. The court has granted the motion to compel responses to special interrogatories without objections and has awarded sanctions in the amount of $452.82 against Castillo and his counsel, jointly and severally.
