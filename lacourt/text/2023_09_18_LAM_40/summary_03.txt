Here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The court has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by the State of California to the plaintiff's Third Amended Complaint. The plaintiff, Matthew Stibbe, alleges that the Franchise Tax Board recklessly disregarded published procedures during an audit, resulting in debits to his bank account. The court finds that the claim is barred by the statute of limitations and cannot be cured based on the pleadings and the pleadings' attachments before the court. The court sustains the State of California's demurrer, Without Leave to Amend, and dismisses the case.
