 Case Number:  20STCV08041    Hearing Date:   October 4, 2023    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
February 26, 2020, Plaintiffs Horacio Casillas, Kourtine Alexia Rae Scott, Ava
Blankevoort, Camila Casillas, and Krystal Greer filed this action against
Defendants Ink Transportation LLC and Surjay Pandey for motor vehicle negligence
and general negligence. 
On
July 13, 2020, Defendants filed an answer. 
On
September 11, 2023, Petitioner Kandyce Lance (“Petitioner”) submitted a petition
for approval of minor’s compromise to be heard on October 4, 2023. 
PARTY’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Kandyce Lance asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of
Plaintiff Ava Blankevoort (“Claimant”). 
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed petition with all necessary attachments. The Court
grants the petition. 
Petitioner
requests the net balance be deposited in an insured account at Wells Fargo
Bank, 10540 Foothill Blvd., Suite 110, Rancho Cucamonga, CA 91730. The Court
orders the net balance so distributed. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of the pending action of minor
Ava Blankevoort filed by Petitioner Kandyce Lance.  The Court orders that the net balance be
placed in an insured account at Wells Fargo Bank, 10540 Foothill Blvd., Suite
110, Rancho Cucamonga, CA 91730. 
The
Court sets a OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement into Blocked Account on December
4, 2023, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
The Court
sets a OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on December 4, 2023, at 8:30
a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
