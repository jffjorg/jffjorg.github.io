 Case Number:  22STCV01287    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2023    Dept:  14 Instant Motion
 
Plaintiff now moves this court for
an order permitting her to file a Fourth Amended Complaint which adds some
clarifying allegations and an additional cause of action for FEHA Harassment.
 
Decision
 
            The motion
is GRANTED. Plaintiff is to file her Fourth Amended Complaint by 4 pm on
November 3, 2023. 
 
The motion is unopposed. This
amounts to a concession that it has merit. See California Rules of Court Rule
3.1113(a); see also Rule 3.1320(f); Herzberg v. County of Plumas (2005)
133 Cal.App.4th 1, 20.
