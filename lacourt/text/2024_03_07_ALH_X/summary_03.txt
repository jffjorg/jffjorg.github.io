This is a tentative ruling by Judge Joel L. Lofton regarding the case of Jose Iniestra and Silvia Garcia (Plaintiffs) v. Thanh Lam (Defendant). The case arises from a motor vehicle accident allegedly caused by the Defendant, and the Plaintiffs filed a complaint on March 17, 2023. The Defendant served discovery requests on the Plaintiffs on July 21, 2023, but the Plaintiffs failed to provide responses. The Defendant filed motions to compel discovery responses and deem admitted, and the Judge has granted these motions. The Judge has ordered the Plaintiffs to provide code-complaint responses without objections within 20 days, and has also granted the Defendant's requests for sanctions in the amount of $993.2.
