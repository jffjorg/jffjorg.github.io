And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court grants the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents in part. The defendant is ordered to produce supplemental verified, code-compliant responses to the requests for production of documents and produce all responsive documents that are not part of the public record within 30 days. The Court limits the scope of certain requests for production of documents, including RFP Nos. 68 and 73 to documents from 2019, and RFP Nos. 45 and 79 to documents concerning the subject vehicle. The Court also limits the scope of requests for production of documents regarding communications with governmental agencies and suppliers. The Court declines to impose sanctions and does not grant the defendant's request for cost shifting.
