The defendant, Los Angeles Country Club, moves for leave to file a cross-complaint against Living Colors Inc. in response to an alleged slip-and-fall incident that occurred while the plaintiff, Felipe Hernandez, was working for Living Colors Inc. The defendant presents a proposed cross-complaint for indemnity and argues that new information came to light during the exchange of discovery, which highlights the need to file a cross-complaint to protect their interests in this litigation. The judge grants the motion for leave to file the cross-complaint, finding no bad faith on the record. The defendant is ordered to file the proposed cross-complaint within five days. Additionally, the trial date is vacated and a trial setting conference is set for November 18, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.
