 Case Number:  23SMCV05099    Hearing Date:   September 13, 2024    Dept:  205    Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles – West District  Beverly Hills Courthouse / Department 205   CANDICE WILKINSON,    Plaintiff, v.  GUCCI AMERICA, INC., et al.,    Defendants.    Case No.:  23SMCV05099    Hearing Date:  September 13, 2024   [TENTATIVE] order RE:   defendants’ motion to    compel mink advanced imaging    AND BLUE SHIELD OF CALIFORNIA to    comply with deposition    subpoenas for production of    business records and for    sanctions       This hearing is on Defendants Gucci America, Inc. and Krystian Gutierrez’s motion to compel Mink Advanced Imaging (“Mink”) and Blue Shield of California (“Blue Shield”) to comply with a deposition subpoena for production of medical records and for sanctions.  Mink and Blue Shield have refused to comply with the subpoena based on objections raised by Plaintiff Candice Wilkinson.  This Department’s rules require that on a discovery dispute, when the parties cannot informally resolve the dispute, the parties are to schedule an informal discovery conference (“IDC”).  No IDC was scheduled.   Accordingly, the Court DENIES Defendants’ motions to compel compliance with deposition subpoena and request for sanctions.   IT IS SO ORDERED.  DATED: September 13, 2024    ___________________________ Edward B. Moreton, Jr. Judge of the Superior Court
