The English text summarizing the judge's tentative ruling is as follows:

The Court grants the unopposed motion of Defendant Electrolux Home Products, Inc. to deem the truth of matters specified in Defendant's Request for Admissions Set One. Plaintiff Sashile Wood failed to serve timely responses substantially complying with the provisions of CCP § 2033.220 prior to the hearing on this motion. The Court orders that all matters specified in Defendant's Requests for Admission, Set One, to Plaintiff Sashile Wood are deemed admitted as true, pursuant to CCP § 2033.280(c). The Court also awards mandatory monetary sanctions of $1,475.00 in favor of defendant Electrolux Home Products, Inc. and against plaintiff Sashile Wood, payable within 30 days.
