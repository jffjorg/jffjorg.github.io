And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court is considering a motion by Wesco Insurance Company (Plaintiff) for an order assigning to itself the interest, if any, of Defendant Native Creations Inc. and dba Creative Innovations in its rights to payment of money due or to become due. The Plaintiff seeks to assign these rights to the extent necessary to satisfy the judgment entered in this action. The motion is unopposed.

The Court notes that the Plaintiff has provided adequate evidence of purported payments due or to become due to Defendant, but requires further explanation regarding the calculation of the total sum due. The Court continues the hearing on the motion to a later date and orders the Plaintiff to file and serve a declaration addressing the request for additional information.
