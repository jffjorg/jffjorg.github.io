The court has granted the motion for summary adjudication filed by the plaintiffs, Sabbagh and the cross-defendants, against the defendant, Culver 12 LLC. The court finds that Culver 12 LLC violated California Civil Code section 1950.5 by failing to return the security deposit of $15,000 and providing an itemized statement. The court rejects the defendant's affirmative defenses of estoppel, waiver, unclean hands, and mitigation of damages, finding that they are without merit.
