The plaintiff has filed four motions to compel further responses to discovery requests, including form interrogatories, requests for admissions, special interrogatories, and requests to produce documents. The defendant has not opposed these motions. The court grants the motions, compelling the defendant to provide verified and complete responses to the discovery requests. The responses are due within 5 days, by October 1, 2024, and the sanctions are due within 30 days of mailing of the notice of ruling.
