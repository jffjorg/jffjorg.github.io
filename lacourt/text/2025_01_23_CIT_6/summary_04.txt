Foremost Insurance Company Grand Rapids, Michigan filed discovery motions against Earline Wilbourn and sought to compel her to respond to their requests. The judge has denied Foremost's motion to compel response to Request for Production of Documents without prejudice, as proper service on Wilbourn was not shown. The judge has also deemed the truth of the matters specified in Request for Admission (Set One) admitted and conclusively established as to Earline Wilbourn. Furthermore, the judge has ordered Wilbourn to provide verified, code-compliant responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Number One) within 20 days of the order.
