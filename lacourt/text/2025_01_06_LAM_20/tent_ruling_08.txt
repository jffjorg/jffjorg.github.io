This is an employment matter.  Plaintiff Selim Hoxha seeks to compel further responses to his form interrogatories, set one, nos. 216.1 and 217.1 propounded on Defendant The Regents of the University of California.  These form interrogatories seek information about Defendant’s affirmative defenses and denials of requests for admission. Plaintiff seems to take issue with the lack of contact information provided for witnesses and the references to voluminous documents.   The Court finds that the responses are sufficient.  Further information can be obtained via other discovery and correspondence with defense counsel. The Motion is denied.  The Court declines to award sanctions.Moving party to give notice.
