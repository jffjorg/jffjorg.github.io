 Case Number:  24NWCV00798    Hearing Date:   June 6, 2024    Dept:  C MJ Global Enterprise, Inc. v. Everytable, PBC 24NWCV00798
 
This
unlawful detainer action was filed on March 14, 2024. Trial is scheduled for
June 21, 2024. There is a CMC and 2 Motions to Compel Further Discovery
Responses scheduled to be heard after trial. Plaintiff moves to advance the
hearing dates on Plaintiff's Motions to Compel Further Responses, which are
presently set for February 25, 2025 and March 5, 2025. 
 
In
an action for unlawful detainer, "a discovery motion may be made at any
time upon giving five days' notice." (CCP 1170.8.)  Moreover,
"[i]n all proceedings brought to recover the possession of real
property...all courts... shall give such actions precedence over all other
civil actions therein... in the matter of the setting the same for hearing or
trial, and in hearing the same, to the end that all such actions shall be
quickly heard and determined." (CCP 1179a.) Plaintiff's Ex Parte
Application to Advance the Hearing Dates on Plaintiff's Motions to Compel
Further Responses is GRANTED. Plaintiff's Motion to Compel Further
Responses to Request for Admissions (set one) is ADVANCED from February
25, 2025 to today's date and CONTINUED to Thursday, June 20, 2024 at
10:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C. Plaintiff's Motion to Compel Further Responses to
Form Interrogatories - General (set one) is ADVANCED from March 5, 2025
to today's date and CONTINUED to Thursday, June 20, 2024 at 10:30
a.m. in Dept. SE-C. Oppositions to be filed and served by no later than
June 13, 2024. Replies to be filed and served by no later than June 17, 2024.
Moving Party to give notice.
