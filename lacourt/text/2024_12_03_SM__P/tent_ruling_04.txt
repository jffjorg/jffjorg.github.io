 Case Number:  24SMCP00399    Hearing Date:   December 3, 2024    Dept:  P Tentative Ruling
Rivera v. Ascencia, Case no. 24SMCP00399
Hearing date December 3, 2024
Plaintiff
Rivera’s Motion to Consolidate
Plaintiff
Alis Margareta Rivera, in pro per, sues defendants Ascencia, Healthcare
in Action, BlockbyBlock and Stepup/Bhot for civil harassment and personal
injury. Plaintiff moves to consolidate this action with previously dismissed small
claims actions 24BHSC01414 and 24BHSC01619 under Cal. Code Civ. Proc. §1048(a).
No opposition was filed.
When
actions involving a common question of law or fact are pending before the
court, it may order a joint hearing or trial of any or all the matters in issue
in the actions; it may order all the actions consolidated and it may make such
orders concerning proceedings therein as may tend to avoid unnecessary costs or
delay. Cal. Code Civ. Proc. §1048(a).
Case
no. 24BHSC01619 Rivera v. Santangelo, was dismissed without prejudice by Commr.
Pamela A. Davis on 10/4/24. 
Case
no. 24BHSC01414 Rivera v. Luz Security Ambassador was deemed not related to the
instant matter within the meaning of California Rules of Court 3.300(a) on
10/8/24. See Min. Order 10/8/24. 24BHSC01414 was subsequently dismissed without
prejudice by Commr. Davis on 11/15/24. 
The
court cannot relate dismissed cases to an open matter. DENIED.
