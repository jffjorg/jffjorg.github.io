EAST
  WEST BANK 
  
         vs.
 
VALENTINA
  PAPAZIAN, et al. 
  


 Case No.: 
  24STCV09146 
 
 
 
 Hearing
  Date:  September 12, 2024




 
 
Plaintiff’s
motion to compel Defendant Takuhi Papazian to provide responses to Plaintiff’s
Form Interrogatories (Set One) and Special Interrogatories (Set One) is
GRANTED. Responses are due within 20 days. 
Defendant is sanctioned, jointly and severally with counsel, $700
payable within 30 days.
 
On 4/11/2024,
Plaintiff East West Bank (Plaintiff) filed suit against Valentina Papazian,
Takuhi Papazian, Arshavir Khachikian, Gevork Papazian, and Hagop Papazian
(collectively, Defendants) filed a complaint for unlawful detainer.  
 
            On
7/15/2024, Plaintiff moved to compel Defendant Takuhi Papazian to provide
responses to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories (Set One) and Special
Interrogatories (Set One). 
 
Discussion 
 
On 5/24/2024,
Plaintiff propounded Form Interrogatories (Set One) and Special Interrogatories
(Set One) upon Defendant via overnight mail in an effort to determine whether
Defendant is a tenant and the terms of the tenancy, if any. Responses to
Plaintiff’s interrogatories are defective, contain meritless objections that
have been waived, and evasive or otherwise incomplete responses. (Sgroi Decl.
¶¶3-10 and Exs. A and B.)
 
Plaintiff’s
motion to compel Defendant Takuhi Papazian to provide responses to Plaintiff’s
Form Interrogatories (Set One) and Special Interrogatories (Set One) is
granted. Defendant is sanctioned, jointly and severally with counsel, $700.
 
It is so ordered.

, 2024

Plaintiff’s
motion to have its Requests for Admission (RFAs) deemed admitted as to
Defendant Takuhi Papazian is GRANTED. Defendant is sanctioned, jointly and
severally with counsel, $700 payable within 30 days. 
 
On 4/11/2024,
Plaintiff East West Bank (Plaintiff) filed suit against Valentina Papazian,
Takuhi Papazian, Arshavir Khachikian, Gevork Papazian, and Hagop Papazian
(collectively, Defendants) filed a complaint for unlawful detainer.  
 
On 7/15/2024,
Plaintiff moved to have its Requests for Admission (RFAs) deemed admitted as to
Defendant Takuhi Papazian. 
 
Discussion 
 
On 5/24/2024,
Plaintiff propounded a Request for
Admissions (Set One) upon Defendant via overnight mail in an effort to
determine whether Defendant is a tenant and the terms of the tenancy, if any.
Despite Defendant’s claim to have provided responses to the RFAs, to date, no
responses have been received. (Sgroi Decl. ¶ 6 and Ex. B.)
 
Plaintiff’s
motion to have its Requests for Admission (RFAs) deemed admitted as to
Defendant Takuhi Papazian is granted. Defendant is sanctioned, jointly and
severally with counsel, $700 ($350/hr x 2 hr.)
 
It is so ordered.
