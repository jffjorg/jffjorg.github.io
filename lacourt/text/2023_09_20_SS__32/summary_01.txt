Summary in English text:
This is a tentative ruling by a judge in a personal injury lawsuit filed by Plaintiff Joseph William Davison against Defendants Universal Waste Systems, Inc. and Juan Manuel Zarate. The Defendants move to compel a mental examination of the Plaintiff, citing that the Plaintiff's claims of a traumatic brain injury put his mental condition at issue. The Court finds that the Defendants have demonstrated good cause for the examination and grants the motion. The Court also orders the Plaintiff to pay sanctions to the Defendant's counsel for failing to confer in a reasonable and good faith manner.
