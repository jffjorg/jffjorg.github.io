The court grants the defendant's motion to transfer the action to the San Bernardino Superior Court. The defendant establishes that they are the only California defendant in the action and are domiciled in San Bernardino County. The defendant also shows that the witnesses in the action will largely consist of their service technicians and other personnel who are all employed in San Bernardino County. The court finds that the defendant has established statutory grounds for change of venue pursuant to CCP § 395(a). The plaintiff failed to respond to the defendant's request to stipulate to the change of venue. The court orders the plaintiff to pay sanctions in the amount of $1,735.00.
