And here is a brief English-text summary:

The tentative ruling is a decision by Judge Michael E. Whitaker on a demurrer filed by Defendant Carrington Mortgage Services, LLC to the First Amended Complaint (FAC) filed by Plaintiff Erin Hughes. The FAC alleges six causes of action: breach of insurance policy contract, breach of implied covenant of good faith and fair dealing, negligence, fraud, negligent misrepresentation, and unfair business practices. The Court sustains the demurrer to the third, fourth, and fifth causes of action, finding that the economic loss doctrine bars Plaintiff's claim for negligence and that Plaintiff failed to plead the fraud-based causes of action with requisite specificity. The Court overrules the demurrer to the sixth cause of action, finding that Plaintiff's allegations are sufficient to state a claim under the Unfair Competition Law. The Court orders Defendant to file an Answer to the FAC on or before January 12, 2024.
