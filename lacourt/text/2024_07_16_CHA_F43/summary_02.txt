Summary:

The Judge's Tentative Ruling grants the Plaintiff's motion for summary judgment on both the account stated and open book account claims. The Plaintiff, American Express National Bank, alleged that the Defendants, Anahit Khrimian and St. Jacob Hospice, Inc., had an outstanding balance of $150,397.63 on their credit card account. The Plaintiff presented evidence that it had sent monthly billing statements to the Defendants, which accurately reflected the balance due on the account, and that the Defendants did not dispute the balance. The Judge ruled that the Plaintiff had established an account stated and an open book account, and that there were no triable issues of material fact. The Plaintiff is awarded damages in the amount of $150,397.63 and costs are to be requested.
