 Case Number:  23STCV07280    Hearing Date:   October 26, 2023    Dept:  30 PATRICIA PETERS vs PAINTCRAFT SUPPLY & HARDWARE CO. INC., et al.Motion to Compel Responses to Request for Production of DocumentsTENTATIVE Defendant’s Motion to Compel Plaintiff Patricia Peters’ Responses to Requests for Production of Documents, (Set One) is GRANTED. Plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses, without objection, within 20 days.  The request for sanctions is denied.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONDefendant provides the Declaration of Pierro H. Babaian (Babaian Dec.) which states that the discovery request was served on May 29, 2023. (Babaian Dec., ¶ 2.) Responses were due on June 30, 2023. After corresponding with Plaintiff’s counsel, responses were never received. A party waives objections to a discovery request when it des not serve a timely response to the request. CCP section 2030.290(a). As this motion is unopposed, the Court declines to award sanctions.
