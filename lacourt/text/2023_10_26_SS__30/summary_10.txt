And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The defendant, Paintcraft Supply & Hardware Co. Inc., et al., has filed a motion to compel the plaintiff, Patricia Peters, to respond to their request for production of documents. The defendant claims that they served the request on May 29, 2023, and that the responses were due on June 30, 2023, but they never received responses. The court has granted the motion and ordered the plaintiff to serve verified responses, without objection, within 20 days. The request for sanctions is denied, as the motion was unopposed.
