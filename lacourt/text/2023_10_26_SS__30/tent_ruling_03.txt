 Case Number:  19STCV46013    Hearing Date:   October 26, 2023    Dept:  30 KRISTY ALLEN, AN INDIVIDUAL vs JORGE HERNANDEZ, AN INDIVIDUAL, et al.Motion to Continue Trial DateTENTATIVEThe Motion to Continue Trial Date is GRANTED.  Judicial Assistant is to calendar a trial date in early May 2024, as well as the Final Status Conference date.  All trial-related deadlines are continued in accordance with the new trial date.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONOn September 29, 2023, Defendant MV Transportation filed the current Motion to Continue Trial. As of October 23, 2023, no opposition has been filed. Defendant moves to have the trial continued to April 2024. Previously, the parties stipulated to a trial continuance until November 29, 2023. However, Defendant asserts that Plaintiff has failed to appear for two noticed physical examinations and the designated expert is not available to examine Plaintiff until January 2024. (Motion 4: 11-19.) Additionally, Plaintiff has not replied to multiple discovery requests which are the subject of upcoming motions to compel. Defendant asserts that they will be prejudiced if Plaintiff is not examined and does not provide the requested discovery. Given the motions to compel are set for February, this Court continues the trial date to May 2024 to accommodate any discovery compliance and attendant motions.  All trial related deadlines are continued in correspondence with the new trial dates.
