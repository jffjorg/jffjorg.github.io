 Case Number:  23STLC02679    Hearing Date:   June 18, 2024    Dept:  26  Taing v. Worldwide ATM, Inc., et al.MOTION
TO COMPEL RESPONSES TO REQUEST TO INSPECTWRITINGS;
REQUEST FOR SANCTIONS(CCP
§§ 2030.290, 2023.010)
TENTATIVE RULING:
 
            
Plaintiff Richard Heng Taing dba
USA Donuts’ Motion to Compel Responses to Request to Inspect Writings and
Request for Sanctions is PLACED OFF CALENDAR AS MOOT.
 
 
ANALYSIS:
 
On April 24, 2023, Plaintiff Richard Heng Taing DBA USA
Donuts (“Plaintiff”) filed the instant action against Defendant Worldwide ATM,
Inc. (“Defendant”). Defendant filed an answer and cross-complaint on June 28,
2024 and July 5, 2023, respectively. . On March 5, 2024, the Court granted
defense counsel’s motion to be relieved as counsel. (Minute Order, 03/05/24.)
On May 22, 2024, Plaintiff filed the instant Motion to Compel Responses to
Request to Inspect Writings to Enforce Order Dated 11-20-23, and Request for
Sanctions. On May 7, 2024, at the order to show cause regarding legal
representation for Defendant, there was no appearance by Defendant and the
Court struck its answer. (Minute Order, 05/07/24.) The Court set an Order to
Show Cause Re: Default/Default Judgment for November 5, 2024. Accordingly, the
instant Motion is placed off calendar as moot.
 
Conclusion
 
Plaintiff Richard Heng Taing dba USA Donuts’ Motion to
Compel Responses to Request to Inspect Writings and Request for Sanctions is PLACED
OFF CALENDAR AS MOOT.
