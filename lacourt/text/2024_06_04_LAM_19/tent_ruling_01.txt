  Case Number:  22STCV10091    Hearing Date:   June 4, 2024    Dept:  19 Plaintiff's unopposed Motions to  Compel 1) Responses to Special Interrogatories , Set One, By Defendant Lucy Guevara; and 2) Responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One, By Defendant Lucy Guevara are GRANTED.    Defendant is ordered to provide verified responses without  objections by July 5, 2024.    The request for sanctions is also granted in part against  each Defendant Lucy Guevara and counsel of record, Arash Mepour, jointly and severally, in  the reduced amount of $1,185.00 for each motion, for a total of $2,370 payable to Plaintiff through his attorney of record within 30 days.  The  Court finds that Defendant has engaged in the abuse of the discovery process  in failing to respond to a valid method of discovery, warranting sanctions for reasonable expenses incurred by Plaintiff,  including motion filing fees. (Code of Civ.  Proc. section 2023.010(d); 2023.030(a)). The Court takes into  account that no formal oppositions or reply briefs were filed in reducing the  amount of sanctions awarded.    Moving party to submit proposed orders within 5 days and give notice.
