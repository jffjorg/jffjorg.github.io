And here is the English-text summary:

This tentative ruling grants Defendant Boswell Construction Inc.'s motion for leave to file a Cross-Complaint against Plaintiff K.G. Mullen, Inc. The Defendant seeks to file the Cross-Complaint after discovering that leaks in a house were caused by defects in the work performed by the Plaintiff. The Defendant was not aware of the leaks until after its answer was filed in August 2022. The Court finds that the Defendant has met its burden to show the discovery of new evidence not in existence at the time the answer was filed and good faith in seeking this relief. The Court also finds that the Plaintiff's objections to the motion are unpersuasive. The Defendant's Proposed Cross-Complaint is ordered to be filed forthwith.
