 Case Number:  23VECV01491    Hearing Date:   October 25, 2023    Dept:  T BOUYER V DIAR 23VECV01491
[TENTATIVE] ORDER: 
Defendant Diar, Inc.’s Motion to Dismiss is DENIED and the alternative
Motion to Transfer is GRANTED.  No
opposition was filed.  Defendant's Diar,
Inc.’s request for attorneys’ fees and costs is GRANTED in the amount of
1,923.00 against Plaintiff’s Counsel only. 
Plaintiff is ORDERED TO PAY for the LA Superior Court transfer fees and
the Ventura Superior Court transfer fees.
Introduction 
Defendant Diar, Inc. (Defendant) moved to dismiss Plaintiff
Anthony Bouyer’s (Plaintiff) action or alternatively to transfer the action to
Ventura County.  Defendant also requested
attorneys’ fees and costs in the amount of $1,923.00 against Plaintiff’s
counsel.  Plaintiff did not file an
opposition. 
Discussion  
Defendant argued that the instant action arose out of
violations occurring at 2671 East Thousand Oaks Blvd., Thousand Oaks 91362,
which is within the jurisdiction of Ventura County.  (Compl. pars. 2, 13-14.)  Per Code of Civil Procedure, secs. 395,
395.5, and 396b, Defendant argued that the action should be dismissed or
transferred to Ventura County and sanctions should be awarded against
Plaintiff’s Counsel.  The Court finds
good cause to have the action transferred to Ventura County and to award
attorneys’ fees and costs.  The Court
does not find good cause to dismiss the action. 
The Motion to Dismiss is DENIED.  The alternative Motion to Transfer and award
Defendant attorneys’ fees and costs is GRANTED. 
The address is within the County of Ventura.
IT IS SO ORDERED, CLERK TO GIVE NOTICE.
