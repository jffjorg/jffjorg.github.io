The court has issued a tentative ruling in favor of the defendants, The Matthew L. Tuck & Associates, et al., in a case filed by Arno Patrick Kuigoua. The plaintiff's complaint alleges claims that are barred by a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) that was signed by the parties in February 2024. The MOU included a release of all claims, known and unknown, against the defendants. The court has concluded that the plaintiff's claims are barred by the MOU and has sustained the defendants' demurrer, without leave to amend. The court has also noted that the plaintiff has not alleged any facts that could show that material terms were missing from the MOU or that could show he did not intend to agree to be bound by the MOU terms.
