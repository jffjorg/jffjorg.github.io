The tentative ruling grants the motion by Plaintiff David Jackson to approve the proposed PAGA settlement with Defendant Costar Realty Information, Inc. The settlement is reasonable and adequate in light of the strength and complexity of Plaintiff's case and the risks to be posed by litigation. The court finds that the settlement furthers the purposes of PAGA, which are to remediate present labor law violations, deter future ones, and to maximize enforcement of state labor laws. The settlement is also fair and reasonable in that it only disposes of Plaintiff's individual claims without prejudice to any other aggrieved employees.
