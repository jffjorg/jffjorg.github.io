And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Defendant's Motion to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Two) is granted. The Plaintiff, Roddy Castroll, failed to respond to the Defendant's form interrogatories served on November 30, 2022, and has not provided any responses since. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's failure to respond waives any objection to the demand. As a result, the Plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses, without objection, within 30 days of this order. The Request for Sanctions is denied, as the motion is unopposed.
