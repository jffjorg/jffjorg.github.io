 Case Number:  21STCV13286    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2023    Dept:  30 NARDA JANINNA TERCERO vs BAILEY RAE BRIETZKEMotion to Compel Responses to Request for Production of Documents (Set One)TENTATIVEDefendant’s Motion for an Order Compelling Plaintiff Narda Janinna Tercero’s Responses to Demand for Production, Set One is GRANTED. Plaintiff is ORDERED to serve verified responses, without objection, within 20 days from notice of ruling. Request for sanctions is DENIED.  Moving party is ordered to give notice.DISCUSSIONDefendant Bailey Rae Brietzke filed this motion to compel responses to Request for Production of Documents, Set One from Plaintiff Narda Janinna Tercero.  Defendant served Demand for Production, Set One, on Plaintiff on January 6, 2023. (Declaration of Carla Guidice-Hu Decl. ¶ 3, Ex. “A.”) To date, Defendant has not received any responses. (Id. at ¶ 5.) A party waives its objections to a discovery request when it does not serve a timely response to the request. (Code Civ. Proc. § 2031.300, subd. (a).) The Court declines to award sanctions as this motion is unopposed.
