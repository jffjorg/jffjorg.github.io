 Case Number:  21STCV40950    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2023    Dept:  30 LAVEL FLOWERS vs DOES 1 THROUGH 10, et al.Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for PlaintiffTENTATIVEMotion to be Relieved as Counsel is GRANTED. Entry of Order is delayed pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1362(e). Counsel for Plaintiff is ordered to serve signed Order within five (5) days and file proof of service with the Court.  Counsel to give notice to all parties and Client.DISCUSSIONCounsel has provided all the required documents under California Rules of Court Rule 3.1362. The Proof of Service attached to form MC-053 indicates that the motion was served on both Plaintiff and Defendant via mail, return receipt requested. The Court finds that Plaintiff’s counsel has provided sufficient reasoning as to why withdrawal is appropriate and that Client will not be unduly prejudiced by Counsel's withdrawal at this time.
