 Case Number:  20STCV10477    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2023    Dept:  30 MAGUMI IKEDA vs DAVID LOPEZ, et al.Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for PlaintiffTENTATIVEMotion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff is DENIED without prejudice.  Counsel is to give notice.DISCUSSIONCounsel has complied with CRC Rule 3.1362 as it relates to its motion. The Court is convinced that Counsel has a compelling reason to withdraw. However, given that the Final Status Conference is less than one month away, the Court finds that the Client will suffer undue prejudice by the late withdrawal.
