  Case Number:  19STCV28264    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2023    Dept:  30 RODY CASTROLL, AN INDIVIDUAL vs CALIFORNIA POLICE ATHLETIC FEDERATION, et al.Motion to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Two)TENTATIVEDefendant’s Motion to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories, Set Two is GRANTED. Plaintiff shall serve verified responses, without objection, within 30 days of this order. The Request for Sanctions is DENIED.  Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONPlaintiff did not respond to Defendant’s Form Interrogatories (Set Two) served on November 30, 2022.  As of the date of filing this Motion, no responses have been provided. If a party fails to serve timely responses, "the party making the demand may move for an order compelling response to the demand.” (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.300, subd. (b).) By failing to respond, the offending party waives any objection to the demand. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.290, subd. (a).)¿Accordingly, the Court finds that Plaintiff failed to timely respond to Defendant’s Form Interrogatories, Set Two. As this motion is unopposed, the Court declines to award sanctions.
