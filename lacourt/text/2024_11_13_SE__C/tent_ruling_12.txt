 Case Number:  23NWCV01797    Hearing Date:   November 13, 2024    Dept:  C Gabriela Cabrera vs Super 8 Gas
Corporation, Case No.: 23NWCV01797
This is a civil rights action. Counsel
for Defendant Super 8 Gas Corp. applies ex parte for an order granting the
motion to be relieved as counsel.  The hearing is currently scheduled for
August 21, 2025. Trial is scheduled for April 24, 2025. Given that a motion to
be relieved as counsel is a noticed motion, the Court may not grant the motion
on an ex parte basis. Accordingly, the ex parte application is DENIED.  In
the alternative, the motion is ADVANCED from April 24, 2025 to this date and CONTINUED
to December 3, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Department SE-C.  Any Opposition to be
filed and served no later than November 22, 2024.  Moving party to give
notice.
