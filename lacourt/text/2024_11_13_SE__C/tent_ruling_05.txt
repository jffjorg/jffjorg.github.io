 Case Number:  23NWCV00614    Hearing Date:   November 13, 2024    Dept:  C Charlie Lara vs Parsec, Inc., Case
No.: 23NWCV00614
This is a personal injury action.
Plaintiff Charlie Lara applies ex parte to (1) allow Slyvia Lara, Successor in
Interest to Charlie Lara, to continue the action pursuant to Code Civil
Procedure Section § 377.31; (2) Amend the Compliant to Substitute Slyvia Lara,
Successor in Interest to Charlie Lara; and (3) Continue the Summary
Judgment/Adjudication motion. The hearing for motion for summary judgment is
scheduled for January 15, 2025. Trial is currently set for February 10, 2025,
but the Court notes a motion to continue trial is currently set for November
21, 2025. A Motion to amend a complaint must be a noticed motion. Therefore,
the Court application is DENIED.  In the alternative, the Court specially
sets the motion to amend the complaint on April 29, 2025 at 10:30 a.m. in
Department SE-C.  The Court finds good cause to continue the motion for
summary judgment to Tuesday, May 27, 2025 at 10:30 a.m. in Department SE-C.
 Opposition and Reply briefs to be filed and served in compliance with the
new hearing dates.  Moving party to give notice.
