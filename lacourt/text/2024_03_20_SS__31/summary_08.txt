Summary:
This is a tentative ruling on a motion to compel discovery responses and sanctions. The Plaintiff, Jose Morales, propounded special interrogatories on August 16, 2023, and the Defendant, Jose Noyola, failed to respond. The Plaintiff's counsel made numerous calls and sent a meet and confer letter, but the Defendant still failed to respond. The Plaintiff seeks an order compelling the Defendant to respond and pay sanctions. The Court grants the motion and orders the Defendant to serve verified responses to the special interrogatories, set one, without objections, within 15 days. The Court also awards sanctions to the Plaintiff in the amount of $250 for the motion.
