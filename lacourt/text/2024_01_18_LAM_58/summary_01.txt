Summary in English:

The Judge's Tentative Ruling grants the Motion to Approve Settlement of Claims brought under the Private Attorneys General Act (PAGA) in the case of Etemad v. Raymond James & Associates, Inc. (Case No. 21STCV35039). The settlement was the result of a full-day, private mediation and arm's length bargaining between the parties. The Judge finds that the proposed settlement is fair, reasonable, and adequate to all concerned parties and approves the settlement. The settlement amount is $340,000, with 25% going to the Labor and Workforce Development Agency (LWDA) and 75% going to the aggrieved employees. The Judge also approves the payment of attorney's fees and costs, as well as the issuance of IRS Form 1099s. The release under the Settlement Agreement is also deemed fair and reasonable.
