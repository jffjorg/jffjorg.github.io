The Court has considered the motion by Casey E. Kilday, Esq. to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff Erwin Quintanilla. The Court has determined that the motion was not properly served on Plaintiffs Gerber Izaguirre and Eddy Lopez, and therefore continues the hearing to June 24, 2024. Counsel is ordered to file proof of service of the moving papers on the Plaintiffs by June 18, 2024. Counsel is also ordered to give notice of this ruling and to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within five days.
