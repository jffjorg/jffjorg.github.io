 Case Number:  24STCV05834    Hearing Date:   November 19, 2024    Dept:  76             Defendants failed to pay for
goods, wares and merchandise sold by Plaintiff.
            Plaintiff
moves for leave to file a Second Amended Complaint.
TENTATIVE RULING
Plaintiff Hundred Flowers Inc.
did not sufficiently comply with Rule 3.1324. As such, the motion
for leave to amend is CONTINUED to December 9, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. Plaintiff is
ordered to file a revised declaration which sufficiently addresses the
requirements of Rule 3.1324, by November 29, 2024.
ANALYSIS
Motion For Leave To Second Amended Complaint
Discussion
            Plaintiff
moves for leave to file a second amended complaint to correct the amounts due
and owing.
Cal. Rules of Court, Rule 3.1324(a)(1) & (2)
provide:
(a) Contents of
motion A motion to amend a pleading before trial must:
. . .
(2) State what
allegations in the previous pleading are proposed to be deleted, if any, and
where, by page, paragraph, and line number, the deleted allegations are
located; and
(3) State what
allegations are proposed to be added to the previous pleading, if any, and
where, by page, paragraph, and line number, the additional allegations are
located.

Cal Rules of Court, Rule 3.1324 
Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1324(b)
states: 
A separate declaration must accompany
the motion and must specify:
 
(1)  The effect of the
amendment;
 
(2)  Why the amendment is
necessary and proper;
 
(3)  When the facts giving
rise to the amended allegations were discovered; and
 
(4)  The reasons why the
request for amendment was not made earlier. 

   (Cal. Rules of Court, 3.1324(b).)
            Plaintiff did
not sufficiently comply with Rule 3.1324.  As such, the motion for
leave to amend is CONTINUED to December 9, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. Plaintiff is ordered
to file a revised declaration which sufficiently addresses the requirements of
Rule 3.1324, by November 29, 2024.
