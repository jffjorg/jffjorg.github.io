And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge grants Defendant's motion for relief from waiver of discovery objections due to its failure to timely serve responses to Plaintiff's Form Interrogatories. The Defendant claims that its failure was due to mistake, inadvertence, or excusable neglect, and that it served code-compliant responses to Plaintiff's Form Interrogatories on July 3, 2024. The Judge finds that the Defendant's failure to provide timely responses was due to mistake, inadvertence, or excusable neglect, and that its responses were substantially compliant, such that relief is appropriate. The Judge notes that a response is in substantial compliance if it is in the form expected for an answer to the given request and conveys some information.
