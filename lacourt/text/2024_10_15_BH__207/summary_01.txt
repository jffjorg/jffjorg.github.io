The Court has issued a tentative ruling in this unlawful detainer dispute. The Plaintiff, SM 10000 Property LLC, has moved to compel the Defendant, Paul Roberts, to respond to Requests for Production and Special Interrogatories regarding post-judgment discovery. The Defendant has failed to serve a timely response, and the Court finds this failure to be an abuse of the discovery process. As a result, the Court grants the Plaintiff's motion to compel and orders the Defendant to serve verified responses to the Requests for Production and Special Interrogatories within 30 days. The Court also imposes monetary sanctions against the Defendant in the amount of $1,100, representing three hours of attorney time to prepare the moving papers and one hour to attend the hearing.
