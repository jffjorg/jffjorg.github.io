Plaintiff Jila Babazadeh-Shraga seeks to compel responses to her requests for production, set three, from Defendant Gohar Babazadeh.  Jila contends that Gohar never provided responses.Gohar indicates that responses have now been served and that her waiver of objections should be set aside.Because responses have now been served, the Motion to Compel is denied as moot.  The Court awards reduced sanctions in the amount of $1000 to Jila.  The request to set aside Gohar’s waiver of objections cannot be made via opposition briefing.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
