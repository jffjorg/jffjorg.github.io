The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the discovery motions filed by Defendant Subaru of America, Inc. The motions seek to compel Plaintiff Rudy I. Arias Chavez and non-party Hector Arias to appear for deposition and to inspect the allegedly defective vehicle purchased by Plaintiff. The judge has granted the motions, ordering the depositions and vehicle inspection to occur within 30 days. The judge has also awarded Defendant monetary sanctions in the amount of $3,941.50, representing 10 hours of drafting, filing fees, and deposition costs. The judge's decision is based on the failure of Plaintiff's counsel to comply with discovery requests and the lack of timely opposition to the motions.
