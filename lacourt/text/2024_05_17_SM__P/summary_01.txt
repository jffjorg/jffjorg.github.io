The court issued a tentative ruling on May 17, 2024, regarding a motion by Elysienne, LLC for issue, evidentiary, and monetary sanctions against Zadeh and Pineapple Ventures for failure to comply with discovery orders. The motion was unopposed. The court found that Zadeh and Pineapple Ventures failed to comply with the court's December 19, 2023 minute order regarding discovery, which is a misuse of the discovery process. As a result, the court granted the motion and imposed sanctions on Zadeh and Pineapple Ventures. The court also awarded Elysienne, LLC $2,320.00 in monetary sanctions for 8 hours of attorney time spent preparing the motion and attending the hearing.
