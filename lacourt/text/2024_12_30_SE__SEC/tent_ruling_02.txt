 Case Number:  23NWCV00651    Hearing Date:   December 30, 2024    Dept:  SEC Rodriguez et al. v. General
Motors LLC (23NWCV00651)
 
This lemon law action was filed on
March 2, 2023. Trial is set for January 22, 2025. 
 
Defendant General Motors LLC’s
Motion for Summary Judgment is set for hearing on March 13, 2025.
 
 Plaintiffs Estella Rodriguez and Mario Luis
Rodriguez’s Motion for Leave to Amend Complaint is set for March 18, 2025.
Plaintiffs now move ex parte to continue the hearing on the Motion for Summary
Judgment on a date after the hearing on the Motion for Leave to Amend Complaint
and to continue trial to a date convenient to the Court.
 
Plaintiffs argue that the
Complaint relies on caselaw that was recently overturned. 
 
Plaintiffs argue they will be
irreparably harmed because their current causes of action may be dismissed via
the Motion for Summary Judgment or at trial.
 
Plaintiffs also have yet to
complete all necessary discovery.
Plaintiff’s Ex Parte Application
for an Order Continuing the Hearing on Defendant’s Motion for Summary Judgment
and for an Order Continuing Trial and All Pre-Trial Related Dates is GRANTED
pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1332(c)(6)—A party's excused inability to obtain
essential testimony, documents, or other material evidence despite diligent
efforts; and (7)—A significant, unanticipated change in the status of the case
as a result of which the case is not ready for trial. Defendant’s Motion for
Summary Judgment is ADVANCED to today’s date and CONTINUED to October 14, 2025.
 
 Plaintiffs are referred to Department F to
schedule a new trial date. The trial date should be at least 30 days after the
hearing on the Motion for Summary Judgment in accordance with CCP § 437c, subd.
(a)(3).
 
The Court will confer with the
parties to select a new final status conference and trial date. 
 
Discovery and
all trial deadlines will track the new trial date.
 
 Moving Party to give
notice.
