  Case Number:  23NWCV00035    Hearing Date:   December 30, 2024    Dept:  SEC Johnsrud v. Guzman, M.D.
(23NWCV00035)
 
This medical malpractice action
was filed on January 5, 2023. Trial is set for July 9, 2025. 
 
Defendant AHMC Whittier Hospital
Medical Center LP’s Demurrer is set to be heard on September 17, 2025.
 
Defendant now moves ex parte to
advance the hearing date for the Demurrer to the Second Amended Complaint from
September 17, 2025 to June 24, 2025, or in the alternative, continue the trial
date from July 9, 2025, to on or after October 6, 2025. 
 
Defendant argues the Demurrer
should be advanced or trial should be continued to a date after the Demurrer
hearing to facilitate potential resolution of the case before trial.
 
Defendants’ Ex Parte Application
to Specially Set Hearing Date on Demurrer is GRANTED. 
 
Exigency has been established. The
hearing on Defendant’s Demurrer to the Second Amended Complaint is ADVANCED to
this date and CONTINUED to June 24, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. in Department SE-C. 
 
Opposition and Reply to be filed
and served per Code in accordance with the new hearing date.
 
Moving party to give notice.
