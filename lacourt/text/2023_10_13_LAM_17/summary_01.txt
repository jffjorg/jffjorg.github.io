The Judge's tentative ruling grants in part and denies in part the Plaintiff's motion to compel deposition. The Plaintiff, Rachel Tolliver and Carmen Sims, seek to compel the deposition of General Motors' Person Most Knowledgeable (PMK) regarding complaints by other customers in similar vehicles. The Judge finds that many of the topics sought are relevant, but some are overbroad. The Court orders General Motors to produce a PMK to testify on categories of information related to the subject vehicle, warranty policy, customer complaints, and technical service bulletins. The Judge declines to award sanctions at this time.
