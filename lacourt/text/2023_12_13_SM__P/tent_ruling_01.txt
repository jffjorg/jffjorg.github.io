Palm Finance
Corporation v. Hoffman, Case No. 20SMCV00366
Hearing Date December
13, 2023
Plaintiff Palm
Finance Corporation’s Motion for Award of Attorney’s Fees (UNOPPOSED)
 
On January 11, 2022
the court granted Palm Finance’s motion for summary judgment against defendant
Hoffman. Hoffman appealed on January 21, 2022. On September 26, 2023 the Court
of Appeal affirmed the trial court’s ruling and awarded Palm Finance costs and
reasonable attorney’s fees on appeal. Palm Finance moves for an award of
attorney’s fees and costs. 
 
Palm Finance’s
attorney John F. Kurtz states his work on appeal generated $17,043.00 in
attorney’s fees. Kurtz billed at $380 per hour in 2022 and $390 per hour in
2023, while his paralegal billed at $100 per hour. Kurtz declaration ¶9. Palm Finance
seeks $469.28 in appellate costs consisting of filing fees, efiling fees and
recording fees. Kurtz declaration exhibit B. The itemization indicates the time
spent on appeal-related tasks was reasonable, as are costs. Id. There is no
opposition. 
 
GRANTED. Palm Finance
awarded $17,043 in fees and $469.28 in costs, totaling $17,502.28.
