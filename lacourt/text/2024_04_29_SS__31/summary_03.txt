This tentative ruling grants the unopposed motions to deem admissions admitted against the defendants, Joseph M. Radgowski, Mark A. Radgowski, and Ariana Arambula. The defendants failed to respond to the requests for admissions, and therefore, the admissions are deemed admitted. The court also imposes monetary sanctions against the defendants in the amount of $260 each, to be paid within 20 days.
