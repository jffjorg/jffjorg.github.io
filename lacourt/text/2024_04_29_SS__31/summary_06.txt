And here is a brief summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The tentative ruling finds that the motions to compel responses and deem demands for admissions admitted are moot since responses were indeed served. The Court awards $225 in attorney's fees and $120 in costs to Trader Joe's Company, and orders Plaintiff and Plaintiff's counsel to pay sanctions of $620. The Court grants sanctions to compensate for the delay and inconvenience caused by Plaintiff's failure to timely serve responses.
