This is the tentative ruling on the motion to compel compliance with the deposition subpoena served on Pacific MRI. The plaintiff, Martha Maldonado Luna, filed this action against defendants Baraat Moller and Curtis Dailey for damages arising from an automobile accident. The defendants moved to compel the non-party Pacific MRI to produce its custodian of records for deposition and to produce documents pursuant to a deposition subpoena. The motion was unopposed and the court granted the motion.

The court ordered the custodian of records for Pacific MRI to appear for deposition at a date and time to be noticed by the defendants. The court also awarded sanctions to the defendants in the amount of $600, which must be paid within 20 days. The court also ordered the defendants to give notice of the deposition.
