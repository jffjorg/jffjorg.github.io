  Case Number:  23TRCV00195    Hearing Date:   August 16, 2024    Dept:  B THOMAS A. BECHLER, et al. vs TORRANCE MEMORIAL MEDICAL
CENTER, et al.
Case No. 23TRCV00195
Ex Parte Application of Defendant Torrance Memorial Medical
Center to Specially Set Hearing on Defendant’s Motion for Summary Judgment or in
The Alternative to Continue the Trial Date.
Tentative Ruling on Ex Parte Application
This wrongful death action was commenced by Plaintiffs
Thomas A. Belcher, Thomas (“Scott”) Bechler and Kimberly Bechler on January 23,
2023. Answers were filed in April, 2023. On May 23, 2023 this Court established
the trial date of November 20, 2024.
Defendant Torrance Memorial Med. Center has filed this ex
parte application
“… for an order to specially set
(shortening time) hearing on Defendant’s Motion for Summary Judgment (the
“Motion”), or in the alternative, to continue the trial date of November 20,
2024, and all trial-related dates, to a date thirty (30) days or beyond October
24, 2024, the current reservation date for hearing on Moving Defendant’s
Motion.”
The Court tentatively intends to grant the ex parte application by establishing new dates for the Trial and Final Status
Conference.  The Court suggests a new Trial
Date of June 9, 2025 at 10:00 a.m.  The Final
Status Conference would be on May 30, 2024 at 9:00 a.m. All deadlines would be
based on the new trial date. The parties should meet and confer in advance of
the hearing to let the Court know if these new dates work for the parties, and
if any other dates require adjustment.
