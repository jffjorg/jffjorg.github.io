 Case Number:  22STCV37585    Hearing Date:   May 23, 2024    Dept:  52 Plaintiff Lorenzo
Cerullo’s Motion to Enforce Settlement
Plaintiff Lorenzo
Cerullo moves to enforce the settlement between himself and defendant Rolls
Royce Motor Cars North America, LLC.  Plaintiff
seeks monetary sanctions against defendant and an order to comply with the
terms of the judgment.  
The court will
exercise its discretion to deny the motion. 
The court entered judgment for plaintiff in this action on November 30,
2023.  Plaintiff may pursue formal
efforts to collect on that judgment under the Enforcement of Judgments
Law.  
Plaintiff Lorenzo
Cerullo’s motion to enforce settlement is denied.
