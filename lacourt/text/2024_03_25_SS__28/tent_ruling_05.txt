BACKGROUND 
On
March 7, 2022, Plaintiffs Hoon Sung and Kanaan Sung, a minor by and through his
guardian ad litem Hoon Sung, filed this action against Defendants RHGP
Trucking, Inc. (“RHGP”), Freedom Truck, LLC (“Freedom”), Bhangoo Truck Line,
Inc. (“Bhangoo”), Abraham Meza (“Meza”), and Does 1-25 for motor vehicle tort. 
On
July 14, 2022, Plaintiffs amended the complaint to include Defendant Estate of
Abraham Meza, Deceased, as Doe 1 (“Estate”). 
On
December 6, 2022, RHGP, Freedom, and Estate filed answers. 
On
July 6, 2023, the Court dismissed Freedom, Estate, and Meza without prejudice
at Plaintiffs’ request. On August 3, 2023, the Court dismissed Bhangoo without
prejudice at Plaintiffs’ request. 
On
February 13, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On March
7, 2024, Petitioner Hoon Sung (“Petitioner”) filed a petition for expedited
approval of a minor’s compromise. 
No
trial date is currently scheduled. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Hoon Sung asks the Court to approve the compromise of Claimant Kanaan Sung’s
action. 
LEGAL STANDARD 
“‘[W]ithout
trial court approval of the proposed compromise of the ward’s claim, the
settlement cannot be valid. [Citation.] [¶] Nor is the settlement binding [on
the minor] until it is endorsed by the trial court.’” (Pearson v. Superior
Court (2012) 202 Cal.App.4th 1333, 1338; see Prob. Code, §§ 3500, 3600, et
seq.; Code Civ. Proc., § 372.) 
To
obtain court approval of the settlement of a minor’s claims, the petitioner
must file a complete and “verified petition for approval of the settlement and
must disclose ‘all information that has any bearing upon the reasonableness of
the compromise.’ [Citations.]” (Barnes v. Western Heritage Ins. Co.
(2013) 217 Cal.App.4th 249, 256; see Cal. Rules of Court, rule 7.950.) 
DISCUSSION      
          The petition contains the following information about the
proposed settlement: 
Gross
settlement amount: $2,500.00 Medical
expenses: $1,940.00 Reductions: $1,106.67 Amount to be paid or reimbursed from settlement proceeds: $833.33 Balance
of proceeds: $1,666.67 
The Court grants the petition, which contains all necessary information.
At Petitioner's request, the net balance will be paid or delivered to Petitioner Hoon Sung at 249
S. Lafayette Park Pl., #122, Los Angeles, CA 90057.
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor Kanaan
Sung’s action filed by Petitioner Hoon Sung on March 7, 2024.
