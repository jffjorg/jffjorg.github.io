This tentative ruling grants in part the motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents filed by Plaintiffs Josefina Diaz and Jose Aguilera against Defendant General Motors LLC. The Plaintiffs sought further responses to three categories of documents, including documents related to Defendant's general policies and procedures, documents explaining the various codes used in repair orders, and documents showing Defendant's handling of similar complaints from other consumers. The Defendant objected to some of the requests, but the Court has granted the motion in part, requiring Defendant to produce supplemental responses related to the same make and model as Plaintiffs' vehicle. The Court has denied the requests for sanctions.
