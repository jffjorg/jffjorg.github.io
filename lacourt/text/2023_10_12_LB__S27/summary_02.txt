Judge's Tentative Ruling: Motion for Determination of Good Faith Settlement

In Case Number 21STCV01257, Plaintiff Matthew Hernandez filed an action against Silverstar Express, Inc., Direct Chassislink, Inc., and Apollo Global Management, LLC for personal injury damages. Hernandez alleges that he was injured when a Utility Tractor Rig and chassis he was driving overturned while moving a reel of steel cable. The case has undergone settlement discussions, and Silverstar Express, Inc. has agreed to pay Hernandez $1,000,000 in exchange for a release of all claims against it. This amount is the limit of Silverstar's insurance policy covering Hernandez's claims.

Silverstar Express, Inc. has filed a motion for determination of good faith settlement, seeking to confirm that its settlement with Hernandez is valid. The motion was properly served on all parties, and no opposition was filed. As a result, the motion is granted due to lack of opposition.

The parties who intend to submit on this tentative must send an email to the court indicating their intention to submit on the tentative. If a party does not submit on the tentative, they should make arrangements to appear remotely at the hearing on October 12, 2023.
