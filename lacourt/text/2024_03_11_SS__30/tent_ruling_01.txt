  Case Number:  20STCV17991    Hearing Date:   March 11, 2024    Dept:  30 NANETTE PRICE vs CAL- QUAKE CONSTRUCTION, INC., A CORPORATION, et al.TENTATIVEDefendants Cal-Quake Construction, Inc., John Taferner, and Joseph Goldberger’s motion to compel responses to requests for production for documents is GRANTED.Plaintiff Nanette Price is ordered to provide responses without objection to Defendant’s request for production, set one, within 20 days of this order.Defendant’s request for sanctions is DENIED.Moving party is ordered to give notice.DiscussionOn June 20, 2023, Defendant served Request for Production of Documents, Set One, on Plaintiff. (Davis Decl., Exh. A.) To date, Plaintiff has failed to provide responses. (Id., ¶ 7.)As Defendant properly served discovery requests and Plaintiff has failed to serve responses, the Court finds Defendant is entitled to a court order directing Plaintiff to provide verified responses without objections to the discovery requests served on Plaintiff. Therefore, the motion is granted.Sanctions are only mandatory in connection with motions to compel responses to request for production when the motion is unsuccessfully opposed. (Code Civ. Proc. § 2031.300(c).) The motion was not opposed. As such, the request for sanctions is denied.
