**Case Information**

* Case Number: 22STCV12986
* Hearing Date: February 21, 2024
* Department: 56
* Court: Superior Court of California, County of Los Angeles - Central District

**Parties**

* Plaintiff: Ryan Hall
* Defendant: General Motors LLC, M.K. Smith Chevrolet, and Does 1-40

**Background**

* This is a lemon law action arising from the purchase of a 2020 Chevrolet Silverado from M.K. Smith Chevrolet.

**Motion**

* Defendant General Motors LLC moves to tax costs requested by Plaintiff Hall's counsel, Writz Law APC.

**Issues**

* Defendant GM argues that Counsel has not provided sufficient evidence for the claimed costs, and that some of the costs are unreasonable and unnecessary.

**Plaintiff's Opposition**

* Plaintiff Hall argues that the claimed costs are reasonable and necessary for the prosecution of the action, and that Counsel has provided sufficient documentation to support the claims.

**Judge's Ruling**

* The Court finds that the filing and motion fees, jury fees, deposition costs, court reporter fees, and fees for electronic filing are allowable costs under Code of Civil Procedure, Section 1033.5, subdivision (a).
* The Court also finds that the motion for attorney fees was filed and granted on January 29, 2024.
* The Court notes that Plaintiff Hall is the prevailing party in this case and is entitled to recover the requested costs and fees under Code of Civil Procedure, Section 1033.5, subdivision (a) and California Civil Code, Section 1794, subdivision (d).
* The Court DENIES Defendant General Motors LLC's Motion to Strike or Tax Costs.
