The Court is considering a motion for an order of discharge filed by Cypress Escrow and Settlement Services, Inc. (Plaintiff) from this action. The Plaintiff held $50,000 in escrow in connection with a real property sale, but a dispute arose between the parties regarding the disposition of the funds. The Plaintiff filed an interpleader complaint and deposited the funds with the Court. The Court finds that, under these circumstances, the discharge of the Plaintiff is warranted. The Plaintiff is also entitled to reimbursement of attorney fees and costs incurred in litigating this action, which the Court grants in the amount of $8,562. The Plaintiff is ordered to provide notice of this order and to file proof of service of same.
