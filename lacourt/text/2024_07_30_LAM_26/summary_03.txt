Summary:
The court has granted in part a motion to compel further production of documents from the defendant, Santa Monica-Malibu Unified School District, to the plaintiffs, S.T. and C.R., who are alleging that they were bullied as students at Santa Monica High School. The plaintiffs are seeking documents related to student discipline and employee discipline at the school. The court has denied the plaintiffs' request to compel further responses to some categories of requests, but has granted their request to compel further responses to other categories. The court has also ordered the defendant to provide verified, complete, and code-compliant responses to certain requests for production of documents.
