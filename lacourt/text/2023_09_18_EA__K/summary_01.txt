Summary in English text:

The Tentative Ruling concerns a motion for a protective order and a motion for sanctions filed by BJ's Restaurants, Inc. ("BJ's") in a personal injury lawsuit filed by Ethan Bergeron and Victoria Bergeron against several defendants, including BJ's. The lawsuit alleges causes of action for premises liability, negligence, product liability, harassment, assault and battery, and failure to prevent harassment. BJ's seeks to strike Category No. 4 from Plaintiffs' deposition notice for BJ's Person Most Knowledgeable (PMK) and to require the parties to meet and confer in good faith on a date and time for the PMK deposition. The court grants BJ's request to order the parties to meet and confer, but denies its request to strike Category No. 4. The court also declines the parties' respective requests for sanctions.
