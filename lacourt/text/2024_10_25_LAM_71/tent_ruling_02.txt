 Case Number:  20STCV48250    Hearing Date:   October 25, 2024    Dept:  71 Barboza v. Howard Management
20STCV48250

Hearing on Order to Show Cause re
Entry of Judgment

 
            On August
19, 2024, the Court granted Defendant’s unopposed motion to enforce the
settlement entered into between it and Plaintiff on Augst 4, 2024.  Pursuant to the settlement, Defendant agreed
that Plaintiff would be paid $195,000. 
The Court ordered Defendant to submit a judgment within 10 days and set
a hearing on an Order to Show Cause regarding the entry of the judgment on September
25, 2024. 
            At the September
25, 2025,  hearing on the Order to Show
Cause, Plaintiff did not appear, only Defendant’s counsel did.  The Court continued the hearing on the Order
to Show Cause to October 25, 2024.
            Defendant filed
a proposed judgment on October 8, 2024, along with a Declaration from Christina
Newell, Esq. re her attempts to communicate with Plaintiff re same.   On October
10, 2024, Plaintiff filed a” Request for Purposed Order” in which she outlines
her objections to the settlement.  
            The Court
finds that the Judgment in this matter should simply be entered in favor of
Plaintiff and against the settling Defendants in the amount of $195,000.  The settling Defendants can then issue checks
paying the judgment in accord with the identified liens.
