And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion filed by Plaintiff angela brown to compel Defendant smart resources nursing registry to provide further responses to special interrogatories. The judge finds that Defendant's counsel has not filed a valid proof of service of the signed "Order Granting Attorney's Motion to be Relieved as Counsel – Civil", and therefore has not been relieved as counsel for Defendant. The judge grants the motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories for numbers 2 and 5-6, as well as numbers 11 and 13, due to evasive and incomplete answers. The judge also grants the request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,810. The order requires Defendant to serve further, full and complete answers to the special interrogatories within 20 days and to pay the sanctions within 30 days.
