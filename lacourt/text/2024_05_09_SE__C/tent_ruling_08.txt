 Case Number:  23NWCV03585    Hearing Date:   May 9, 2024    Dept:  C Rutherford v. Gutierrez
Family Investment 
 
Defendant GUTIERREZ FAMILY
INVESTMENT PARTNERSHIP, LP's Ex Parte Application to Shorten Time for Hearing
on Motion to Set Aside Default and Judgment is DENIED without prejudice for
failure to comply with the Notice requirements set forth by CRC Rule
3.1204(b)(1), which states that a declaration regarding notice must accompany
an ex parte application. The declaration regarding notice must state:
"[t]he notice given, including the date, time, manner, and name of the
party informed...." Moreover, if notice was provided later than 10:00 a.m.
the court day before the ex parte appearance, the declaration regarding notice
must explain why. (CRC Ruel 3.1204(c).) Here, no such declaration is attached
to/lodged with the ex parte application. The ex parte is procedurally defective
and must be denied without prejudice. Moving Party to give notice.
