**Case Summary**

**Case Number:** 22STCV38566
**Hearing Date:** April 22, 2024
**Case Title:** SERVICE MANAGEMENT GROUP HOLDINGS, LLC, Plaintiff, v. SMG MOUNTAIN INTERMEDIATE HOLDINGS, LLC, Defendant.

**Tentative Ruling**

The court denies the defendant's motion for summary adjudication of the first, third, sixth, seventh, and tenth causes of action in the First Amended Cross-Complaint (FACC) regarding two customers, Sainsbury's and DSG.

**Summary of the Case**

The plaintiff, Service Management Group Holdings, LLC, filed an action against the defendant, SMG Mountain Intermediate Holdings, LLC, alleging a single cause of action for breach of contract. The defendant filed a cross-complaint alleging fraud by misrepresentation and fraud by omission against the plaintiff's officers, including Andrew Fromm.

**Facts**

* The parties entered into an Equity Purchase Agreement (EPA) for the sale of the plaintiff's subsidiary.
* The EPA included representations regarding the financial performance of the subsidiary.
* The defendant allegedly knew that these representations were false.

**Legal Analysis**

* The court applied Delaware law, which prohibits "bootstrapping" a contract claim into a fraud claim.
* The court found that the defendant's representations in the EPA were false and that the defendant knew they were false at the time of signing the EPA.

**Ruling**

* The court denies the defendant's motion for summary adjudication of the first, third, sixth, seventh, and tenth causes of action in the FACC regarding two customers, Sainsbury's and DSG.

**Conclusions**

* The court finds that the defendant's representations in the EPA were false and that the defendant knew they were false at the time of signing the EPA.
* The court denies the defendant's motion for summary adjudication, allowing the case to proceed to trial.
