Plaintiff Wells Fargo Bank, N.A.’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On November 17, 2023, Wells
Fargo Bank, N.A. (“Plaintiff”) filed its Complaint against Defendant Katrina M.
Mattox (“Defendant”) for breach of contract and common counts.
On March 6, 2024, the
clerk entered default against Defendant. 
On June 14, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be
heard on September 24, 2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendant and award Plaintiff $30,513.15 consisting of
$29,187.50 in damages, $800.00 in attorneys’ fees, and $525.65 in costs. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted a
completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments. The Court
finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants Plaintiff Wells
Fargo Bank, N.A.’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application for
default judgment filed on June 14, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $30,513.15 is entered against Defendant Katrina M. Mattox.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of this
ruling.
