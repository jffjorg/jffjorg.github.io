 Case Number:  23SMCV05097    Hearing Date:   May 16, 2024    Dept:  I This is a motion to tax costs.  Plaintiff sued defendants for fraud and later
dismissed the action without prejudice. 
Defendants filed a cost memo seeking $914.35 in filing fees.  Plaintiff moves to tax them.  Plaintiff contends the memorandum was
untimely.  It is not.  The defense had additional days to respond
due to the manner of service.  Turning to
the merits, defendants are the prevailing party and the costs asserted are
substantiated.  The motion is
DENIED.
