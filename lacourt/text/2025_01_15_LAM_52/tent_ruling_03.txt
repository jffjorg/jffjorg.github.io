 Case Number:  24STCV01445    Hearing Date:   January 15, 2025    Dept:  52 Tredway, Lumsdaine
& Doyle, LLP’s Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel
Attorneys Roy J. Jimenez
and Sonal D. Imbulamure of Tredway, Lumsdaine & Doyle, LLP move to be relieved
as counsel for defendant Pico-Union Housing Corporation.  The motion meets all requirements under
California Rules of Court, rule 3.1362.  

The motion to be relieved
as counsel for defendant Pico-Union Housing Corporation is granted.  The court will sign the proposed order on
Judicial Council form MC-053.  The order
will be effective upon filing proof of service of the signed order on defendant
Pico-Union Housing Corporation.
