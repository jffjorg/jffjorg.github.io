**Tentative Ruling**

The Court has reviewed the Expedited Petition for approval of compromise of a disputed claim of minor claimant, Khilyn Williams. The Petition is defective as it exceeds the $50,000 limit for expedited approval. The settlement of $250,000 offered by Defendants to Petitioner exceeds the limit. The Petition is denied without prejudice. The moving party is ordered to give notice.

**Conclusion**

Based on the foregoing, the Petition is DENIED WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
