Here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by the defendant, American Honda Motor Co., Inc. The plaintiff, Andrew Lee, has filed a complaint alleging that the defendant failed to repair defects in a 2015 Honda Odyssey that he purchased. The defendant has demurred to the complaint, arguing that the allegations are insufficient and that the statute of limitations has expired.

The Judge has sustained the demurrer with leave to amend as to the first, second, third, and fourth causes of action in the complaint. The Judge has found that the allegations are insufficient and have not been plead with the required specificity. The Judge has also found that the claims must have accrued on or before December 13, 2015, and that the four-year statute of limitations under Commercial Code §2725 expired no later than December 13, 2018.

The Judge has given the plaintiff 20 days to amend the complaint to address the defects in the allegations and to plead the claims with the required specificity.
