The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion for attorneys' fees filed by Plaintiffs Yesenia Barragan and Jose G. Garcia against Defendant FCA US LLC. The judge grants the motion in part and denies it in part. The judge awards Plaintiffs $33,981.00 in fees, which is the total amount of fees incurred by Plaintiffs' counsel and paralegals. However, the judge denies the additional request for $2,500.00 in fees for reviewing the opposition, drafting the reply brief, and attending the hearing, as no such briefs have been filed. The judge notes that the Defendant failed to oppose the motion, which may be deemed a consent to the granting of the motion. The judge also finds that the amount of time charged and the hourly rates charged are reasonable.
