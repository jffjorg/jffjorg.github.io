

 
Tentative Ruling:                    Defendant Cassandra Davis’s
Motion to Deem Plaintiff’s Responses to Requests for Admission, Set One, as
Admitted and Request for Monetary Sanctions is GRANTED IN PART.
                                                
                                                Defendant
Cassandra Davis’s Motion to Compel Plaintiff’s Responses to Defendant Cassandra
Davis’s Request for Production of Documents, Set One, and Request for Monetary
Sanctions is GRANTED IN PART. 
 
Plaintiff is ORDERED to
provide verified, complete, and code-compliant answers, without objections, to
Defendant Davis’s Request for Production of Documents, Set One, within 30 days
of the date of notice of this order. 
 
The RFA Motion is GRANTED IN
PART and the Court deems the matters set forth in Set One of Defendant Davis’s
Requests for Admission as admitted. 
 
Defendant
Davis’s request for monetary sanctions as to each of the Motions is GRANTED IN
PART. 
 
The Court
awards Plaintiff total monetary sanctions in the sum of $825 as to both
Motions. Monetary sanctions are to be paid by Plaintiff to Defendant Davis in
the total sum of $825 within 30 days of the date of notice of this order.
Sanctions are not ordered against Plaintiff’s attorneys.
