(CCP § 515.010; Miller Decl. ¶¶ 16, 20 & Ex. 3.)At the hearing, the Court will hear from counsel as to what undertaking shall be required by the defendant for redelivery or to stay delivery in accordance with CCP § 515.020(a). and its reference to CCP § 515.010.
