SUMMARY
OF ACTION
On
February 8, 2024, Plaintiff Kassidy Salcido and her father, Joshua Salcido,
were walking towards her school when Defendant Christopher Kelly, who was
driving a car, struck Kassidy and her father. Kassidy suffered physical
injuries after the accident. On March 11, 2024, Plaintiffs filed their
complaint.
 
Plaintiff Joshua Salcido, Kassidy’s father, submits a petition
for approval for minors compromise on behalf of Kassidy (12) for a settlement with
Defendant Christopher Kelly for $100,000. Petitioner represents total deductions
of $31,134.32 as follows: $6,134.32 in medical expenses, and $25,000.00 in
attorney fees (25%), thereby leaving a net balance of $68,865.68.
 
The payment will be deposited into a blocked account.
(Probate Code §§ 3610, 3611(b).)
 
Proof of service by mail was filed
with the petition on May 10, 2024. The petition and proposed order were filed
on the MC-350 and MC-351 forms. The petition complies with CCP § 372; Probate
Code §§ 3500, 3600-3613; and Cal. Rules of Court Rules 3.1384, 7.101,
7.950-7.952. The proposed order for the blocked account was filed on the MC-355
form.
 
The Court approves the Petition to
Confirm Minor’s Compromise filed on behalf of Kassidy Salcido.
 
The Court will set a hearing to verify the deposit of funds
into the blocked account. 
 
Moving party to give notice.
