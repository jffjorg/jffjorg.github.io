The Judge issued a tentative ruling on a demurrer to the First Amended Cross-Complaint filed by Rowlee and Golden Hammer against JETNET, LLC and ADSBExchange.com, LLC. The demurrer was filed by ADSBExchange.com, LLC and challenges the sufficiency of the First Amended Cross-Complaint. The Judge ruled that the demurrer to the First Cause of Action for Fraud and Conspiracy to Commit Fraud is overruled, but the demurrer to the Second Cause of Action for Breach of the Implied Covenant of Good Faith and Fair Dealing is sustained with leave to amend. The Judge found that the First Cause of Action alleged facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action, but the Second Cause of Action did not plead facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action. The Judge gave Rowlee and Golden Hammer 30 days leave to amend the Second Cause of Action.
