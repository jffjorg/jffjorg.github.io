LLC’s unopposed motion to compel Defendant Redpoint Investment Inc., dba

LLC (“LACAA”) (“Plaintiff”) moves unopposed to compel Defendant Arthur

Interrogatories (General) (Set Two) (“FROG 2”). 
(Notice of Motion FROG 2 Yoon, pg. 2; C.C.P. §§2017.010, et seq.,
2023.010 et seq., 2030.290 et seq.; CRC, Rule 3.1345.)  Plaintiff also requests an award of sanctions

2030.290(c).)
Plaintiff moves unopposed
to compel Defendant Redpoint Investment Inc., dba Redpoint Realty (“Redpoint”)
(“Defendant”) to provide responses to its FROG 2.  (Notice of Motion FROG 2 Redpoint, pg. 2;
C.C.P. §§2017.010, et seq., 2023.010 et seq., 2030.290 et seq.; CRC, Rule
3.1345.)  Plaintiff also requests an

1.    
Motion to Compel Yoon- FROG 2
Having reviewed Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Responses from Yoon to
Plaintiff’s FROG 2, the Court rules as follows.
          On or about February 27,
2024, Plaintiff served FROG 2 on Yoon. 
(Decl. of Operini Yoon ¶2, Exh. B.) 
On April 3, 2024, after Yoon did not respond, Plaintiff’s counsel sent
Yoon’s counsel a meet and confer letter indicating that Yoon did not timely
respond and that he waived his objections to said discovery.  (Decl. of Operini Yoon ¶4, Exh. 1.)  Yoon’s counsel requested an extension, and
Plaintiff’s counsel granted Yoon an extension until April 25, 2024, to
respond.  (Decl. of Operini Yoon ¶5, Exh.
2.)  On July 1, 2024, Yoon’s counsel
requested an extension until July 12, 2024, which Plaintiff’s counsel
granted.  (Decl. of Operini Yoon ¶10.)  
On or about September 25, 2024, Yoon served his responses to FROG 2.  (Reply, pg. 2.)
          Plaintiff’s motion to
compel responses is denied as moot.
Plaintiff requests monetary sanctions totaling $2,590.69 against Yoon and his counsel, jointly and severally. 
          The
Court awards sanctions pursuant to C.C.P. §2030.290(c) in the amount of $818.91,
calculated as follows:
[(3 hours to prepare instant
motion x $500.00)/2 Defendants] + $60.00 filing fee + $7.26 efiling fee for
motion + $7.26 efiling fee for reply + $8.91 reservation and processing fee = $833.43

The sanctions are payable by
Yoon and his attorney, jointly and severally, within 20 days of this order.
Moving Party is to give
notice of this ruling.
