And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has considered the motion to strike punitive damages allegations filed by Defendant Isaac Abelsky and has issued a tentative ruling. The Court finds that the allegations in the Complaint are insufficient to support a claim for punitive damages. The Complaint alleges that the Defendant's conduct was malicious and oppressive due to his failure to repair his property despite requests and knowledge of the damage being caused to the Plaintiff's property. However, the Court finds that these facts are insufficient to allege conduct that may form the basis for a punitive damages award. The Court grants the motion to strike with thirty days leave to amend the Complaint.
