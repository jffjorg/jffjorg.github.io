 Case Number:  21STCV40043    Hearing Date:   December 6, 2024    Dept:  28           Having considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules  as follows.  BACKGROUND  On  November 1, 2021, Plaintiffs Maria Martinez (“Martinez”) and Jocelyn Luna  (“Luna”) filed this action against Defendants Anna Ashley Le (“Le”), Dao Hong  Tran (“Tran”), and Does 1-50 for motor vehicle tort and general negligence.  On  December 12, 2022, the Court appointed Martinez to serve as Luna’s guardian ad  litem.  On  March 10, 2023, Le and Tran (“Defendants”) filed an answer.  On  October 23, 2023, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement.  On October  15, 2024, Petitioner Maria Martinez (“Petitioner”)  filed an amended petition to approve the compromise of Luna’s  claim. The petition was set for hearing on December 6, 2024.  PETITIONER’S REQUEST  Petitioner  asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of minor  Plaintiff Jocelyn Luna.  DISCUSSION  Petitioner  has submitted a complete petition with all required information.  The Court grants the petition. The net settlement amount of $17,019.14 is to be  deposited in an insured, blocked account at Bank of America, 17445 Bellflower  Blvd., Bellflower, CA 90706, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the  Court.CONCLUSION The Court GRANTS the petition for approval of the  compromise of minor Plaintiff Jocelyn Luna’s claims filed by Petitioner Maria  Martinez on October 15, 2024.  The net  settlement amount of $17,019.14 is to be deposited in an insured, blocked  account at Bank of America, 17445 Bellflower Blvd., Bellflower, CA 90706,  subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court.  The Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net  Settlement on February 25, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring  Street Courthouse.  The Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case  (Settlement) on February 25, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring  Street Courthouse.  Petitioner is ordered to give notice of this ruling.  Petitioner is ordered to file the proof of service of  this ruling with the Court within five days.
