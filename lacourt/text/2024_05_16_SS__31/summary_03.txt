The plaintiff, Wawanesa General Insurance Company, is seeking a default judgment against defendants Ana Pacifuente and Elton Rivera for damages resulting from an automobile collision. The plaintiff claims a total of $36,681.03 in damages, consisting of $32,078.03 in property damage, $4,000 in uninsured motorist payments, and $603 in costs. The plaintiff's custodian of records, Cathryn Davis, has provided a declaration in support of the damages. Pursuant to California Vehicle Code Section 17151(a), the liability of the vehicle owner, Pacifuente, is limited to $5,000 for property damage and injury to one person. As a result, Pacifuente may be held responsible for up to $5,000 of the $32,078.03 in property damage, and the remaining amount of $27,681.03 is sought against Rivera. The court grants the request for default judgment in the amounts of $9,000 against Pacifuente and $27,681.03 against Rivera.
