The tentative ruling is regarding a request for default judgment against Cameron Hughes (Defendant) by Robert Bookman (Plaintiff) for an amount of $511,622.25. The Plaintiff is seeking damages for breach of contract, account stated, and fraud/misrepresentation related to the sale of wine. The Defendant failed to pay for the sold wine under four separate agreements. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's request for default judgment is denied because the Plaintiff seeks damages in excess of those demanded in the Complaint. Additionally, the Plaintiff failed to submit the Statement of Damages with the request for entry of default judgment. The Court orders the Plaintiff to amend the complaint and serve it on the Defendant before entering default judgment.
