 Case Number:  22STCV11083    Hearing Date:   September 21, 2023    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
April 1, 2022, Plaintiff Liam Vicente, by and through his guardian ad litem
Sandra Gomez (“Plaintiff”), filed this action against Defendants Dmitry
Romanenko, M.D. (“Romanenko”), Mother Teresa Medical Center of Huntington Park,
Gage Medical Center, Inc., Hollywood Presbyterian
Medical Center (“Hollywood Presbyterian”), and Does 1-100 for general
negligence and emotional distress. 
On
June 23, 2022, Hollywood Presbyterian filed an answer.  On July 14, 2022,
Romanenko filed an answer. On October 28, 2022, Gage Medical Clinic, Inc., dba
Mother Theresa Medical Center of Huntington Park (erroneously sued as Gage
Medical Center, Inc. and Mother Teresa Medical Center of Huntington Park) filed
an answer. 
On
July 12, 2023, the Court dismissed Hollywood Presbyterian with prejudice at
Plaintiff’s request. 
On
August 3, 2023, Petitioner Sandra Gomez (“Petitioner”) filed a petition for approval
of minor’s compromise to be heard on September 21, 2023. 
PARTY’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Sandra Gomez asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of
Plaintiff Liam Vicente. 
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed application with all applicable attachments. The
Court grants the application. 
Petitioner
requests that the entire net balance be placed into a single-premium
deferred annuity. The Court orders the net balance so distributed. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of the pending action of minor
Liam Vicente filed by Petitioner Sandra Gomez. The Court orders that the net
balance be distributed into the single-premium deferred annuity referenced in
attachment 18b(3) of the petition. 
The
Court sets a OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement into Single-Premium
Deferred Annuity on November 30, 2023, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the
Spring Street Courthouse. 
The
Court sets a OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on November 30, 2023, at
8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
