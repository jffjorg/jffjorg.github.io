The judge has made a tentative ruling on the plaintiff's motions to compel production of documents from the defendants. The plaintiffs had filed a complaint alleging breach of implied warranty of habitability, negligence, nuisance, and violation of Civil Code section 1942.4. The defendants had failed to produce documents as stated in their responses, and when they did produce documents on November 4, 2024, they were incomplete and redacted without a privilege log explaining the redactions. The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel production and ordered the defendants to produce responsive documents within 15 days of the order. Sanctions were denied due to substantial justification for the failure to produce.
