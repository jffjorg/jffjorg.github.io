The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to strike portions of the complaint. The complaint alleges that the defendants, Dorylus, LP, Lavi & Associates, Inc., and South Bay Management Services, were negligent and failed to maintain the property in a habitable condition. The complaint includes allegations of mold and mildew, leaks, and other defects. The defendants argue that the allegations are insufficient to support a claim for punitive damages. The court finds that the allegations are sufficient to support a claim for punitive damages under the fifth cause of action for breach of habitability. The court grants the motion to strike as to paras. 24, 32, 36, and 53 and prayer at 2. as to first, second, third, and sixth causes of action. The court also denies the motion as to para. 49 and prayer at 2. as to the fifth cause of action.
