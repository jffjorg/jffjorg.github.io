Jane Doe v. Edward
Bass Films, et al., Case No. 23SMCV02581
Hearing Date December
11, 2023 
Defendant Bass’s
Motion to Quash Complaint for Lack of Service
 
Defendant Bass
moves to quash the complaint on the grounds that it was not personally served.
Plaintiff Doe filed a proof of substituted service, which states process server
Jorge Rivera made two attempts to personally serve Bass at his residence before
serving Bass’s brother at the same location. 6/22/2023 proof of service pg. 3
(Rivera decl.). Per the proof of service, Rivera mailed a copy of the summons
and complaint to the residence. Id. pg. 1. 
 
The proof of
service indicates Doe complied with all the requirements for substituted
service under Cal. Code of Civ. Proc. §415.20(b). Bass’s motion does not
dispute that the documents were substitute served. DENIED. Defendant to answer
within 20 days.
