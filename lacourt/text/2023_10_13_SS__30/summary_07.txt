The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel defendant Lawrence Richard Rodriguez to respond to plaintiff's Form Interrogatories, General, Set One, Number 17.1. The judge has ordered defendant Rodriguez to serve verified responses, without objection, within 20 days. The request for sanctions is denied as the motion is unopposed. The judge notes that a party who fails to timely respond to interrogatories waives any objection to the interrogatories, including one based on privilege or work product protection. The judge declines to award sanctions in this case.
