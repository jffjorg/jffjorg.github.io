Here is the English-text summary:

This is a tentative ruling by Judge Michael E. Whitaker in a case where Petitioner Vertical Entertainment, LLC is seeking to confirm an arbitration award in the amount of $683,978.25 against Respondents Redbox Entertainment, LLC, Redbox Holdings, LLC, and Redbox Automated Retail, LLC. The arbitrator issued the award on May 21, 2024, and Petitioner filed the petition on June 5, 2024. The hearing is scheduled for July 16, 2024. However, Respondents did not respond to the petition. The Court finds that Petitioner did not adequately serve the Notice of Hearing on Respondents, as the notice was served electronically but not in accordance with the arbitration agreement. Therefore, the Court denies the petition without prejudice on procedural grounds.
