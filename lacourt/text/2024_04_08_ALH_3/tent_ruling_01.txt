On February 9, 2024, defendants
Harley-Davidson Motor Company Group, LLC (“Harley-Davidson”) (erroneously sued
as Harley-Davidson Motor Company, Inc.) and TMCHB, Inc. dba Huntington Beach
Harley-Davidson (“TMCHB”) (erroneously sued as The Motorcycle Company, LLC)
were ordered to file an amended notice of motion and proposed order at least 5
days before the hearing in support of their motion to seal. The hearing was
scheduled for April 2, 2024. On March 20, 2024, the Court continued the hearing
to April 8, 2024. Notwithstanding the continuance, the required documents were
not filed until 1:36 p.m. on April 5, 2024. Accordingly, the hearing on the motion
is continued to April 25, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.
