MOTION
Plaintiff Rodrigo
Orozco (“Plaintiff”) moves to compel Defendant Top USA Properties, LLC
(“Defendant”) to produce its person most knowledgeable for deposition.  Plaintiff served this motion on Defendant by
electronic service on October 18, 2024. 
That is not sufficient notice under Code Civil  Procedure section 1005, which requires moving
and supporting papers to be served and filed at least 16 court days before the
hearing and Code Civil  Procedure section
1010.6(a)(3)(B), which provides “[a]ny period of notice . . . shall be extended
after service by electronic means by two court days.”  Therefore, with the intervening court holiday
on November 11, 2024,  Plaintiff’s motion
was required to be served electronically on Defendant no later than October 17,
2024 for the hearing set on November 13, 2024. 

Plaintiff is ordered
to provide notice of this order and file proof of service of same.
