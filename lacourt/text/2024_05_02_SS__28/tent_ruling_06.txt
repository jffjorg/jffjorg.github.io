Company, J Orozco Enterprises Inc dba Orozco Landscape and Tree Company, Carlos

guardian ad litem. 
On

Cross-Defendants Roes 1-50 for equitable/implied indemnity, declaratory relief,

On
August 22, 2022, Kochanpillai filed an answer and a cross-complaint against
Cross-Defendants Carlos Orozco, J. Orozco Enterprises Inc., dba Orozco

On

Enterprises Inc., dba Orozco Landscape and Tree Company filed an answer to
Kochanpillai’s cross-complaint. 
On

No
trial date is currently scheduled. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Paulina Oropeza Martin asks the Court to approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff Yasmin
Gutierrez’s claims. 
LEGAL STANDARD 
“‘[W]ithout
trial court approval of the proposed compromise of the ward’s claim, the
settlement cannot be valid. [Citation.] [¶] Nor is the settlement binding [on
the minor] until it is endorsed by the trial court.’” (Pearson v. Superior
Court (2012) 202 Cal.App.4th 1333, 1338; see Prob. Code, §§ 3500, 3600, et
seq.; Code Civ. Proc., § 372.) 
To
obtain court approval of the settlement of a minor’s claims, the petitioner
must file a complete and “verified petition for approval of the settlement and
must disclose ‘all information that has any bearing upon the reasonableness of
the compromise.’ [Citations.]” (Barnes v. Western Heritage Ins. Co.
(2013) 217 Cal.App.4th 249, 256; see Cal. Rules of Court, rule 7.950.) 
DISCUSSION      
The petition contains the following information about
the proposed settlement: 
Gross settlement amount: $4,500.00
Medical expenses: $519.95
Negotiated, contractual, and statutory reductions:
$129.98
Amount to be paid or reimbursed from proceeds: $389.97

Balance of proceeds: $2,871.67 
Petitioner has submitted a completed petition with all
necessary attachments. The Court grants the petition.
