 Case Number:  22STCV19172    Hearing Date:   September 26, 2023    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
June 10, 2022, Plaintiff Cristina Castellanos Hernandez (“Plaintiff”) filed
this action against Defendant City of Los Angeles for general negligence and
premises liability. 
On
October 18, 2022, Plaintiff filed a First Amended Complaint adding Defendant
Los Angeles Unified School District. 
On
August 21, 2023, Petitioner Cristina Castellanos Hernandez (“Petitioner”)
submitted a petition for approval of minor’s compromise to be heard on
September 15, 2023. 
PARTY’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Cristina Castellanos Hernandez asks the Court to approve the compromise of the
pending action of Kevin Hernandez (“Claimant”). 
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed petition with all necessary attachments.  The Court grants the petition. 
Petitioner
makes three requests for distribution of the net balance: (1) deposit in an
insured account, (2) any conditions determined to be in the best interest of
the minor, and (3) place in a blocked account.  The Court orders that the net balance be
deposited into a blocked, insured account at the requested address: Citibank
Santa Ana, 2677 N. Main Street, Santa Ana, CA 92706. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of the pending action of
minor Kevin Hernandez filed by Petitioner Cristina Castellanos Hernandez. The
Court orders that the net balance be deposited into a blocked, insured account
at Citibank Santa Ana, 2677 N. Main Street, Santa Ana,
CA 92706. 
The
Court sets a OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement into Blocked Account on
November 30, 2023, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street
Courthouse. 
The Court
sets a OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on November 30, 2023, at 8:30
a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
