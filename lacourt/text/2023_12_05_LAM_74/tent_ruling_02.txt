 Case Number:  21STCV46789    Hearing Date:   December 5, 2023    Dept:  74 Charlene Shee v. Diane Sherwood, et
al.
 
Motion to Consolidate Cases.
 
The
court considered the moving papers. No opposition was timely filed. (Code Civ.
Proc., § 1005, subd. (b) [“All papers opposing a motion . . . shall be filed
with the court and a copy served on each party at least nine court days . . . before
the hearing.”].)
BACKGROUND 
            This
action arises from an alleged fraudulent conveyance.
            On
December 22, 2021, Plaintiff Charlene Shee (Plaintiff) filed a complaint
against Diane Sherwood, individually and as trustee of the Diane M. Sherwood
Family Trust (Defendant). The complaint alleges two causes of action: “to set
aside and annul fraudulent conveyance,” and “injunctive relief.”
            On
June 13, 2023, Plaintiff filed another complaint against Defendant and Tarek
Buys Houses, LLC (Charlene Shee v. Diane Sherwood,
et al., Los Angeles Superior Court, case number 23STCV13506). The
complaint alleges two causes of action: “to set aside and annul fraudulent
conveyance,” and “injunctive relief.” 
            On
July 12, 2023, Plaintiff filed this motion to consolidate this action with case
number 23STCV13506.           
LEGAL STANDARD 
            The
court denies the motion to consolidate without prejudice because the motion does
not comply with California Rule of Court, rule 3.350.
            Plaintiff
shall give notice.
