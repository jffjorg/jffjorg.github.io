  Case Number:  20STCV03325    Hearing Date:   November 25, 2024    Dept:  28 On
October 21, 2024, the Court continued the hearing on the petition to approve
the compromise of minor Plaintiff N.A.’s action to November 25, 2024 and
ordered Petitioner Noushin Anvarzadeh to submit (1) a proof of service of the
September 25, 2024 petition and proposed order on the three State agencies and
(2) a revised proposed order with a specific 14-month calendar due date and a
specific date and time for the OSC to be set in this Department in
approximately 60 days to ensure the funding of the settlement, submission of
bond, and the filing of LASC Form PRO 044 to open a trust supervision action in
the Probate Court. 
On
November 8, 2024, Petitioner filed a copy of the September 25, 2024 petition to
approve the compromise of N.A.’s action along with a proof of service of the petition
on the three State agencies.  Petitioner
also filed a revised proposed order with the requested information and a proof
of service showing service on the three State agencies. Therefore, the Court grants the petition to
approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff N.A.’s action filed by Petitioner
Noushin Anvarzadeh on September 25, 2024, as
supplemented by the proposed order filed on November 8, 2024. 
Petitioner
is to give notice. 
Petitioner
is to file a proof of service of this ruling with the Court within five days.
