




            This
is a default on a credit card account. The Complaint alleges causes of action
for (1) breach of contract, and (2.) common counts. 
            
            The
Complaint was filed on May 15, 2023. Default was entered as to Defendant Joanna
Bernheimer (Defendant) on October 10, 2023. 
 
            Plaintiff
Wells Fargo now moves for default judgment against Defendant. There are no Doe
defendants. The request for entry of default judgment is granted. 
 
Discussion
 
            Plaintiff
seeks default judgment in the amount of $27,403.82, which includes $510 in costs
and $800 in attorney fees. 
 
            Plaintiff
submits sufficient evidence to support the amount incurred and owed by Defendant’s
breach of the credit card agreement. (Salvo Decl., ¶¶ 1-9.) Further, Plaintiff substantiates
the request for costs in the amount of $510 and attorney fees in the amount of
$800. (CIV-100, ¶ 7; Blanda Decl., ¶¶ 4-6.)
 
            Plaintiff
is not seeking prejudgment interest. Plaintiff’s
request for entry of default judgment in the amount of $27,403.82 is granted.
