The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the motions for attorney fees and costs filed by defendants Geronimo Perez, Larry Moore, and Daniels, Fine, Israel, Schonbuch & Lebovits, LLP. The Court granted the motions, but reduced the amounts requested. The Court found that the defendants' requests for fees and costs were unreasonable due to the high hourly rates charged by their attorneys and the excessive time spent on certain tasks. The Court exercised its discretion to reduce the hourly rates and the amounts of fees and costs awarded. The Court granted Defendant Larry Moore's Motion for Attorney's Fees and Costs Pursuant to Code of Civil Procedure section 425.16 in the reduced amount of $27,200. The Court also granted Defendants Geronimo Perez and Daniels, Fine, Israel, Schonbuch & Lebovits, LLP's Motion for Attorneys' Fees and Costs Pursuant to Cal. Code Civil Proc. Section 425.16(c) in the reduced amount of $21,860. Costs shall be awarded per code.
