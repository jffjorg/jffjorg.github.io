Attorney Joseph M. Delich (“Counsel”) moves to
be admitted pro hac vice as counsel
for Plaintiffs Filipe Espinha and FE&A Sociedade de Advogados, SP, RL (“Plaintiffs”).  Counsel has not fully complied with California
Rules of Court, rule 9.40 in submitting the application.  For example, there is no indication the
application was served on all parties who have now appeared in the case and on
the State Bar of California and that the applicant or his sponsor paid the $50
fee. (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 9.40(c),(e).)        Accordingly, the application is denied without prejudice.  
Plaintiffs are ordered to provide notice of
this order and to file proof of service of same.
