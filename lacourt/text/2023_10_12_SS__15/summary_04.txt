And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to quash the deposition notice of Plaintiff Lawrence Cossid. The motion was filed by Defendant American International Industries, which argued that the deposition notice was served too soon. The Judge agreed, finding that the plaintiff did not serve any defendant with the summons 20 days before serving the deposition notice, as required by Code of Civil Procedure section 2025.210. The Judge granted the motion in part, ordering the parties to meet and confer on a deposition date for Lawrence Cossid. If they cannot agree, the plaintiff is to file a motion to compel the deposition. The Judge also warned that if the plaintiff does not serve the complaint and summons on Glo-Fire, Inc. and Conagra Grocery, Inc. sufficiently before the deposition, the plaintiff will be subject to a second deposition taken by those defendants.
