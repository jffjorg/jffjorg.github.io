This is a tentative ruling in a case between Plaintiffs Omar Haro Avila and Monica Yescas, and Defendant Nissan North America, Inc. The case concerns a vehicle purchase and alleged nonconformity to warranty. The Plaintiffs seek to compel the deposition of Defendant's Person Most Qualified (PMQ) to gather information relevant to their claims. The Court grants the motion, ordering Defendant to produce its PMQ for deposition regarding all noticed categories, and to produce specified documents by May 12, 2024. The Court also imposes $750.00 in monetary sanctions against Defendant for its unreasonable delay in providing a deposition date.
