**Tentative Ruling**

The Court has considered Defendant Charles R. Drew University of Medicine and Science's motion to set aside the default and default judgment entered against it. The motion was filed on October 13, 2023, and is timely. The Court finds that Defendant's attorney's mistake constitutes excusable neglect and that the default judgment was entered as a result of Defendant's attorney's mistake.

The Court grants Defendant's motion and sets aside the default and default judgment. Defendant is ordered to pay Plaintiff $6,270.00 in attorney's fees within 20 days of the date of this Order. Defendant is also ordered to file its answer to the Complaint within 10 days of the date of this Order.
