The judge has issued a tentative ruling granting the petition to confirm an arbitration award brought by Teamsters Local Union 572 against Ralphs Grocery Co., et al. The petition was filed on June 6, 2024, and the arbitration award was issued on October 11, 2023. The award requires Ralphs Grocery Co. to make whole those employees who were laid off or lost wages and benefits when the company transferred the work of producing paychecks, printing identification tags, and processing Vestcom price tags and materials. The judge found that the petition complies with the requirements of California Code of Civil Procedure sections 1285, 1285.4, 1286, 1287.6, 1288, 1288.2, 1288.4, and 1290.4, and that the arbitration award was properly served on the respondent. Therefore, the judge has granted the petition and ordered Ralphs Grocery Co. to make whole the affected employees.
