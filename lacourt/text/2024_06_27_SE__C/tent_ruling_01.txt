  Case Number:  21NWCV00580    Hearing Date:   June 27, 2024    Dept:  C Lyons,
et al. v. White, et al., Case No. 21NWCV00580
 
This is a
partnership dispute. Plaintiffs allege that Defendants diverted clients,
assets, and business opportunities. Defendants move ex parte for an order to
stay the proceedings, or alternately, to shorten the time to hear the motion.
Trial is set for August 26, 2024, and the motion hearing date is October 29,
2024.
 
A party may
seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable
harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based on the
above, the Court grants the motion. The motion is ADVANCED from Tuesday,
October 29, 2024 to today and CONTINUED to Tuesday, August 13, 2024 at 10:30
A.M. in Dept. SE-C.
