 Case Number:  22NWCV01601    Hearing Date:   June 27, 2024    Dept:  C Argueta
vs. American Honda Motor Company, Inc., Case No. 22NWCV01601
 
This is a
Song-Beverly action. Plaintiff moves ex parte for an order advancing the
hearing of Plaintiff’s motion to compel deposition of Defendant’s PMK or in the
alternative, continue the trial date. The hearing is currently set for November
21, 2024 and the trial date is August 15, 2024.
 
A party may
seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable
harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based on the
above, the court grants the motion. The hearing is ADVANCED from Thursday,
November 21, 2024 to today and CONTINUED to Tuesday, July 9, 2024 at 10:30 A.M.
in Dept. SE-C.
