 Case Number:  24NWCV01143    Hearing Date:   June 27, 2024    Dept:  C Garcia
vs. American Honda Motor Company, Inc., Case No. 22NWCV01143
 
This is a
Song-Beverly action. Plaintiff moves ex parte to advance the hearing of
Plaintiff’s motion to compel Defendant’s PMK deposition, or continue the trial
date. The hearing is currently set for October 23, 2024. Trial is set for
October 3, 2024.
 
A party may
seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable
harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based on the
above, the court grants the motion. The hearing is ADVANCED from Wednesday,
October 23, 2024 to today and CONTINUED to Wednesday, September 11, 2024 at
10:30 A.M. in Dept. SE-C.
