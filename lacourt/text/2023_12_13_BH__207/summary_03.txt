The court has issued a tentative ruling granting the motion of Federal Insurance Company to consolidate two cases, Teresija Sigmund v. Darlington Villas Homeowners Association, Inc., et al. (Super. Ct. L.A. County, 2023, No. 23SMCV01467) and Federal Insurance Company v. Loya Roofing Co., Inc. (Super. Ct. L.A. County, 2023, No. 23SMCV02768). The court finds that consolidation is warranted because the cases involve a common question of law or fact and arise from the same flood of a residential building, which resulted in a cause of action for negligence. The court orders that the cases are consolidated for all purposes, with Teresija Sigmund v. Darlington Villas Homeowners Association, Inc., et al. being designated as the lead case. The court also orders that all proceedings and hearing dates in Federal Insurance Company v. Loya Roofing Co., Inc. are vacated.
