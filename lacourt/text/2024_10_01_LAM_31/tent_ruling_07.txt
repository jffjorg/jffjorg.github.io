PROC. § 425.16(g) MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiffs Hong Tao Su and Xiao Ling Tao RESPONDING PARTIES: Defendant First General Bank  I.         INTRODUCTION This case stems from the non-judicial foreclosure of the property located at 314 E. Mission Road, San Gabirel, California 91766.  Plaintiffs Hong Tao Su and Xiao Ling Tao (collectively, Plaintiffs) filed a Complaint against Defendant First General Bank, among others, for (1) Failure to Give Notice of Non-Judicial Foreclosure; (2) Violation of Plaintiffs’ Rights as Eligible Tenant Buyers; 93) Unlawful Chilling Restraint of Bids; (4) Negligence; (5) Cancellation of Instrument (Trustee’s Deed); (6) Violation of Cal. Bus. & Prof. Code § 17200, et seq; and (7) Declaratory Relief. On July 29, 2024, Defendant First General Bank filed a Special Motion to Strike Portions of the Complaint pursuant to Code of Civil Procedure (CCP) section 425.16 (the Anti-SLAPP Motion).  The Anti-SLAPP Motion is scheduled for November 14, 2024. On August 29, 2024, Plaintiffs filed this motion seeking leave to conduct discovery pursuant to CCP section 426.16(g) to oppose the Anti-SLAPP Motion.  The court will continue the hearing for Plaintiffs’ motion to be heard concurrently with Defendant’s Anti-SLAPP Motion.  II.        CONCLUSION             Plaintiffs’ Motion for Leave to Conduct Discovery is CONTINUED to November 14, 2024. The clerk of the court to give notice.  Dated:   October 1, 2024                                     Kerry Bensinger  Judge of the Superior Court
