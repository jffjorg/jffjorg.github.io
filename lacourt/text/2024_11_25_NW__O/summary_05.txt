The judge grants in part the defendants' motion for attorney's fees and costs. The defendants are the prevailing party in the current action and are entitled to reasonable attorney's fees and costs under Code of Civil Procedure § 1717(a). The judge finds that the defendants' requested fees for the prior action are untimely and excessive, and awards them only for their work in the current action. The judge reduces the requested fees by $6,850.00 for Mr. Hermansen and $2,300.00 for Mr. Gharagozli, and awards them a total of $16,000.00 in fees.
