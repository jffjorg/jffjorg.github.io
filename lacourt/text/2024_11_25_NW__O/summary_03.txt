And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has issued a tentative ruling sustaining Defendant Marye Delany Silvers's demurrer with leave to amend as to Plaintiff Ehsan Safavi's first through third causes of action. The court finds that Plaintiff has failed to state a cause of action for breach of contract, conversion, or failure to reimburse against Silvers, as the Complaint does not contain any factual allegations against Silvers or any information about her alleged role in Plaintiff's former employment with Lux Garage and Closet, Inc., dba Inspired Closets. The court orders Plaintiff to file an amended complaint on or before December 20, 2024.
