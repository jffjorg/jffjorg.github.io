The defendants, CP IV Glendale, LLC and Greystar Real Estate Partners, LLC, have moved to compel responses to the plaintiff's, Sashile Wood, requests for production of documents. The plaintiff has failed to provide timely responses to these requests, and has waived all objections. The court orders the plaintiff to serve verified responses to the defendants' requests for production, and awards monetary sanctions in the amount of $687.50 to the defendant CP IV Glendale, LLC, and $437.50 to the defendant Greystar Real Estate Partners, LLC.
