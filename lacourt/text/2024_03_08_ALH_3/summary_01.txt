This tentative ruling addresses two motions filed by Defendant Richard Dale Gentile, D.D.S. on October 2, 2023. The motions seek to compel Plaintiff Rosevianney C. Ogumsi's responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Requests for Admission (Set One), as well as request monetary sanctions. The court finds that the Defendant's motions to compel responses and deem admitted the truth of matters specified in requests for admission are denied, as Plaintiff served responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Requests for Admission (Set One) on December 4, 2023. However, the court grants the Defendant's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $240, consisting of 2 hours at defense counsel's hourly rate of $180 and $60 in filing fees, to be paid within 20 days of the date of this order.
