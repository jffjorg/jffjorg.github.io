The Court has considered the motion for leave to file a cross-complaint against Todd Osman, filed by Defendant Brandon Weise, and has ruled in favor of the motion. Defendant seeks to file a cross-complaint to assert claims for implied equitable indemnity, comparative contribution, equitable indemnity, and declaratory relief against Osman, alleging that Osman's negligent conduct caused the accident and the plaintiffs' alleged injuries.

The Court finds that Defendant has acted in good faith and that the motion is timely. The Court grants Defendant leave to file a cross-complaint against Osman in the interest of justice.

The Court orders Defendant to file and serve the cross-complaint within 30 days of the hearing on this motion. The moving party is ordered to give notice of this ruling and to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within five days.
