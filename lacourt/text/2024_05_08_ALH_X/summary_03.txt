This is a tentative ruling in a lawsuit between Plaintiff Citlali Pacheco-Pliego and Defendant American Honda Motor Company, Inc. regarding a defective vehicle. Plaintiff is seeking to compel the deposition of Defendant's Person Most Qualified (PMQ) and has made a good faith effort to meet and confer with Defendant's counsel about the timing of the deposition. Defendant objected to the deposition, citing the inconvenience of the scheduled date, but the court finds that this is not a valid objection under California Code of Civil Procedure § 2025.410. Therefore, Plaintiff's motion to compel the deposition of Defendant's PMQ is granted.
