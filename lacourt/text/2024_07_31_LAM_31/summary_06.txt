Summary:

This is a tentative ruling by Judge Kerry Bensinger in the case of Directors Guild of America, Inc. v. Y-Stone Production, LLC, et al. (Y Film Yapimcilik Produksiyon Reklam Ve Organizasyon Hizmetleri Anonim Sirketi). The case concerns the confirmation of an arbitration award issued by Arbitrator Mark Burstein on March 27, 2024, in favor of Directors Guild of America, Inc. and against Y-Stone Production, LLC and Y Film Yapimcilik Produksiyon Reklam Ve Organizasyon Hizmetleri Anonim Sirketi. The award directs the respondents to pay residuals and late charges totaling $90,645.24, plus interest. The petition is unopposed, and the court grants the petition and confirms the arbitration award.
