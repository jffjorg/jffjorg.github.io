SUMMARY
OF ACTION
On
June 2, 2023, Ana Villatoro filed a complaint for Partition of Real Property
and Ejectment involving 4063-4065 So. Normandie Ave., Los Angeles. On October
12, 2023, Joe and Elias Bolaños answered and filed cross-complaint against
Villatoro for Quiet Title, Quiet Title – Resulting Trust, Breach of Fiduciary
Duty, Quiet Title – Constructive Trust, and Declaratory Relief. Villatoro
answerd the cross-complaint on November 7, 2023.
 
RULING: Granted.
Plaintiff/Cross-Defendant
Ana Villatoro moves to deem Request for Admissions (set one) served on defendant
Jose Bolaños admitted.
 
Defendant represents service of requests for admissions on October
16, 2023. [Declaration of Gregory Salvato, ¶ 5, Ex. A.] According to Defendant,
at the time of the filing of the motion, no responses were received, even after
an extension to respond. [Id., ¶ 10.] 
 
The motion is unopposed. The court electronic filing system
shows no reply filed at the time of the tentative ruling publication cutoff. The
unopposed motion is granted. Request for Admissions (set one) is deemed
admitted. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280, subd. (b).) Sanctions in the amount of
$250 imposed against defendant Jose Bolaños joint and several with counsel, and
payable within 30 days. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280, subd. (c).)
 
Trial
set for October 21, 2024. 
 
Defendant to provide notice.
