Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court has granted the plaintiff's motions to compel defendants to provide responses to discovery requests and imposed monetary sanctions. The plaintiff, Creditors Adjustment Bureau, Inc., sued defendants Addition Building & Design, Inc. and Michael Ross aka Michael Edward Ross dba Green Build Design LA for breach of contract, open book account, account stated, and reasonable value. The defendants failed to respond to discovery requests, and the plaintiff filed motions to compel responses. The court found that the failure to respond to discovery requests was unjustified and granted the motions. The court also imposed monetary sanctions in the amount of $5,308.25. The defendants are ordered to serve responses to discovery requests within 30 days and to remit payment of the sanctions to the plaintiff.
