 Case Number:  24NWCV00762    Hearing Date:   September 11, 2024    Dept:  C Sturgeon Electric
California, LLC vs. City of Whittier, Case No. 24NWCV00762
 
This is a breach of contract case
about a public works project. Defendant and Cross-Complainant moves ex parte to
advance City of Whittier’s motion to consolidate. It is currently scheduled for
April 17, 2025.
 
The ex parte application is DENIED
without prejudice.  A notice of related cases must be filed, served, and
approved by the Court before a motion to consolidate may be heard. (Cal. Rules
of Court, rule 3.200; Super. Ct. L.A. County, Local Rules, rule 3.3, subds. (f)
and (g).)
 
Moving party to give notice.
