  Case Number:  21NWCV00062    Hearing Date:   September 11, 2024    Dept:  C Global Med Group, LLC vs.
Kaiser Permanente, Case No. 21NWCV00062
 
This is a breach of contract
action. Defendant and Cross-Complaint moves ex parte to advance the hearing on
the motion for judgment on the pleadings. It is currently scheduled for June
24, 2025. Trial is scheduled for April 28, 2025.
 
A party may seek ex parte relief
upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger,
or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of
Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based on the above, the Court
grants the motion. The motion is ADVANCED from Tuesday, June 24, 2025 to TODAY
and CONTINUES it to Tuesday, November 19, 2024 at 10:30 A.M. in Dept. SE-C.
Opposition and Reply briefs to be filed and served in compliance with the new
hearing date.  The Court cannot grant Plaintiff’s request of October 29,
2024 because its calendar is full that day.
 
Moving party to give notice.
