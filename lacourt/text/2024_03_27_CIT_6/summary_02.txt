And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court grants the plaintiff's motion to compel the deposition of Seidner Enterprises, LLC. The person most qualified for Seidner Enterprises, LLC must appear for deposition within 30 days of the court's order and produce all responsive documents requested in connection therewith.

Additionally, the court grants the plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the reduced amount of $1,060.00. Seidner Enterprises, LLC and its counsel must pay said monetary sanctions to counsel for the plaintiff within 30 days of the court's order.

The court also orders the plaintiff to give notice of the court's ruling within five calendar days of this order.
