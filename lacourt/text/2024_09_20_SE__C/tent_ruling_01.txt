  Case Number:  23NWCV00851    Hearing Date:   September 20, 2024    Dept:  C Sandoval, III v. Advanced Building Systems, Inc. 23NWCV00851
 
Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Application to advance the hearing date on the Motion to Compel
responses, or continue trial, is procedurally defective. The Declaration
attached to the Ex Parte Application does not satisfy the notice requirements
set forth by CRC Rule 3.1204(b). Plaintiff's Counsel does not state that
any attempt to notify Defendants of this ex parte hearing was made. 
 
However,
in the interests of judicial efficiency, on the Court's own motion, the Motion
to Compel Responses is ADVANCED from Thursday, May 15, 2025 to today's
date and CONTINUED to Thursday, December 5, 2024 at 9:30 a.m .in
Dept. SE-C. Opposition and Reply papers to be filed and served per Code in
accordance with the new hearing date. Moving Party to give notice.
