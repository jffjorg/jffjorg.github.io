MOVING PARTY:                 Applicant Perry S. Heidecker 
 
RESPONDING PARTY:       Unopposed
Application of Perry S. Heidecker to Appear as Counsel Pro Hac Vice 
The court
considered the moving papers filed in connection with this application.  No opposition papers were filed. 
DISCUSSION
Applicant Perry S. Heidecker requests an order permitting him to
appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendant A&A Maintenance Enterprise,
Inc. in this action. 
The court finds that the application complies with California Rules of
Court, rule 9.40.  The court therefore
grants the application and orders that Perry S. Heidecker may appear as counsel
pro hac vice for defendant A&A Maintenance Enterprise, Inc. in this
action.  (Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40,
subd. (a).) 
 
 
            The court orders defendant A&A
Maintenance Enterprise, Inc. to give notice of this ruling.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
