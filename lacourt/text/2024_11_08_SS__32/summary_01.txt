**Summary**: This tentative ruling is related to a personal injury action arising from a motor vehicle incident on September 2, 2017. Defendant Cawley served Defendant Almache with Form Interrogatories, Set Two and Requests for Admission, Set One, but Defendant Almache failed to provide responses. The Court grants Defendant Cawley's motion to compel responses to Form Interrogatories, Set Two and deems Admitted Requests for Admissions, Set One Nos. 1-11. The Court also awards sanctions in the reduced amount of $620.00 against only Defendant Almache.
