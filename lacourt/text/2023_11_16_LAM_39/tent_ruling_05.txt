 Case Number:  22STCV10372    Hearing Date:   November 16, 2023    Dept:  39 Florian Schmitz v.
Beach City Capital, LLC, et al.
Case No. 22STCV10372
Motions To Be
Relieved
 
            Counsel for
Jason Muller and Beach City Capital Management, LLC move to be relieved.  Good cause having been shown, the Court
orders as follows:
 
            1.         The Court grants Counsel’s motion
conditional on Counsel complying with this order.
 
            2.         The Court discharges the Order to Show
Cause because this is a post-judgment matter. 

 
            3.         The Court orders Counsel to serve a
copy of this minute order, as well as the signed order, upon all parties,
including his former client. 
 
            4.         The Court orders Counsel to file a
proof of service indicating that BOTH this minute order and the signed order
were served on his former client.
 
            5.         Once Counsel complies with Item #3 and
Item #4 in this order, he shall be automatically relieved.
 
            6.         Counsel shall provide notice and file
proof of such with the Court.
