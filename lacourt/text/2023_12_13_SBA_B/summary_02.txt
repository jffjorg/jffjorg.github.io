The Court has granted the Motion to Approve Settlement filed by Keep America Safe and Beautiful, Inc. (Plaintiff) against Puka Creations, LLC, et al. (Defendant). The Plaintiff alleges that the Defendant manufactures, packages, distributes, markets, and/or sells consumer products containing DEHP (a toxic chemical) without warning consumers in California, in violation of Proposition 65. The parties have finalized a settlement, which includes a consent judgment, and the Court has approved the settlement after finding that the warning required by the settlement complies with applicable laws and that the award of attorneys' fees and penalty amount are reasonable. The Court has ordered the Plaintiff to give notice of this ruling.
