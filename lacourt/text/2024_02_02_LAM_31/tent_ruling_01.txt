MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Jorge Medrano
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant BYD
Coach & Bus LLC
 
 
This is a disability discrimination, failure to accommodate,
and wrongful termination action. On September 14, 2023, Plaintiff, Jorge
Medrano, filed this motion to compel Defendant, BYD Coach & Bus LLC, to
provide further responses to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories, Set One, Nos. 4.1,
12.2, 15.1, 16.3 and 16.6.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of this motion.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motion to March 14, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31. 
Thereafter, the parties are to contact the judicial assistant to schedule the date and time for the IDC.
