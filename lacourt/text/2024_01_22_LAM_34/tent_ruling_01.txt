  Case Number:  23STCV18321    Hearing Date:   January 22, 2024    Dept:  34 PLAINTIFF'S
REQUEST FOR ENTRY OF DEFAULT JUDGMENT 
 
 
I.         
BACKGROUND
 
On August 2, 2023, Plaintiff Eastern Funding LLC filed its Complaint
against Defendants Roundtree Towing and Recovery Services, LLC and Alfonzie
Green on causes of action arising from allegedly missed loan payments. 
 
On October 26, 2023, the Court entered default on Defendant Roundtree
Towing and Recovery Services, LLC. 
 
On November 2, 2023, the Court entered default on Alfonzie Green. 
 
No other documents have been filed. 

 
II.      
ANALYSIS
 
The request is DENIED without prejudice. Plaintiff did not submit
multiple items that are required, including: (1) Form CIV-100, Request for
Court Judgment; (2) Declaration of Mailing; (3) Summary of the Case; (4)
Evidentiary Declaration(s); (5) Interest Computation (if any); (6) Attorney Fee
Declaration (if any); (7) Memorandum of Costs (if any); (8) Declaration of
Non-Military Status (one for each defendant); (9) Form CIV-110, Request for
Dismissal of Does; (10) Original Documents, or Declaration Explaining Their
Loss/Destruction/Unavailability, or Proposed Order for Court to Accept
Authenticated Copy in Lieu of Original; and (11) Form JUD-100, Proposed
Judgment.
 
        The Court
continues the OSC re Entry of Default Judgement until February 27, 2024.  If default judgment is not entered by that
date, the court will dismiss the action.
