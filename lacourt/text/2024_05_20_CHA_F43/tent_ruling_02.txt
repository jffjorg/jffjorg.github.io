SUMMARY
OF ACTION
On
July 10, 2021, Plaintiff Andrea Morfin (Plaintiff), her mother Isela Morfin,
and her brother Manuel Morfin were injured in a car accident. On February 17,
2023, Plaintiffs filed their complaint.
 
Plaintiff submits a petition for approval of minor’s
compromise on behalf of Andrea Morfin (17) for a settlement with Defendant
Michael Christopher Quinones for $8,000.00. Petitioner represents total deductions
of $5,316.66 as follows: $2,650.00 in medical expenses, and $2,666.66 in
attorney fees; thereby leaving a net balance of $2,683.34.
 
It appears that everything is in
order to approve the Minor’s Compromise. The petition and proposed order were
filed on the MC-350 and MC-351 forms. The petitions comply with CCP § 372;
Probate Code §§ 3500, 3600-3613; and Cal. Rules of Court Rules 3.1384, 7.101,
7.950-7.952.
 
The Court approves the Petition to
Confirm Minor’s Compromise filed on behalf of Andrea Morfin.
