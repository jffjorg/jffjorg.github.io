 Case Number:  22NWCV00987    Hearing Date:   March 21, 2024    Dept:  C David Woolsey, et al. vs Security Paving Company,
Inc., et al.
This is a Wrongful Death/Products Liability action. 
 
Defendant & Cross-Complainant Spaulding Equipment
Company, Inc. applies ex parte for an Order Advancing and Specially Setting the
Hearing Date for its Motion for Good Faith Settlement and for an Order
Shortening Time for Hearing on Motion for Good Faith Settlement.
 
A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual
showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis
for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)
 
Defendant & Cross-Complainant Spaulding Equipment
Company, Inc. has filed a Motion for Good Faith settlement, seeking an order of
this court pursuant to Code Civ. Proc. § 877.6(a)(2) that the settlement
constitutes a good faith settlement of any potential liability on its part. The
hearing is currently set for January 2, 2025. Trial is set for April 24, 2024. 
 
The Court finds that Defendant & Cross-Complainant would
be irreparably harmed by not allowing the motion to be heard prior to trial. 
 
Accordingly, Spaulding Equipment Company, Inc.’s Ex Parte
Application is GRANTED. The hearing is ADVANCED from Thursday, January 2, 2025
to today’s date and CONTINUED to Thursday, April 11, 2024 at 10:30 a.m. in
Department SE-C.  Any Opposition is due by April 4, 2024.  
 
Moving party to give notice.
