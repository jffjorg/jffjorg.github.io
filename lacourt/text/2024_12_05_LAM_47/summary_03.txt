This is a tentative ruling by Judge Theresa M. Traber regarding a motion to admit Attorney Tessa Mansfield Hirte pro hac vice as counsel for the defendants in a toxic tort action. The plaintiff, Martin Melendez-Murillo et al., alleges that they developed silicosis and consequential injuries by breathing in dust from the defendants' stone products. The defendants, AKG Trading (USA), Inc., et al., move to have Attorney Hirte admitted to appear as their counsel pro hac vice. The Court finds that all of the requirements of Rule 9.40 have been satisfied, and grants the motion.
