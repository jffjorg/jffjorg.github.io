  Case Number:  23PSCV01830    Hearing Date:   June 13, 2024    Dept:  O Tentative Ruling
 
DEFENDANT Honda’s Motion to Compel Binding Arbitration is
CONTINUED pending the Ochoa decision. 
 
Background
 
This is a lemon law case. 
 
On June 20, 2023, Plaintiff filed suit.
 
On August 4, 2023, Defendant filed the instant motion. 
 
On January 30, 2024, Plaintiff filed her opposition to the
motion. 
 
On February 6, 2024, Defendant filed its reply. 
 
On February 14, 2024, the court continued the hearing
pending the Ochoa decision.
 
On February 29, 2024, Defendant filed its answer. 
 
Discussion
 
The
court again continues the hearing as Plaintiffs rely upon Ford Motor Warranty
Cases (2023) 89 Cal.App.5th 1324 (“Ochoa”), which has created a split of
authority, and the case is on appeal to the California Supreme Court.[1]
 
Conclusion
 
Based
on the foregoing, the motion to compel arbitration is continued.
