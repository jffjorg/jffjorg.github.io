The court has issued a tentative ruling on September 23, 2024, in the case of Kelvin Garcia and Mark Pascua (Plaintiffs) against Valerio RCFE Inc., Kittridge RCFE LLC, and Francis Martir (Defendants). The Plaintiffs moved to deem requests for admissions admitted, which the court granted. The court found that the Defendants failed to serve a timely response to the requests for admissions, and therefore, the truth of the matters in the requests is deemed admitted. The court also imposed sanctions in the amount of $718.91 against the Defendants and their counsel, William R. Bailey, jointly and severally, to be paid within 30 days of the order. The court noted that electronic service is a permitted method of serving discovery requests and that the Los Angeles Superior Court's local rules require electronic filing and service.
