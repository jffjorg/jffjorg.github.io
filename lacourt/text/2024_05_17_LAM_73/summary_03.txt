The Judge, Hon. Rolf Treu, issued a tentative ruling in the case LETESHIA DRISDOM, et al. v. JOSE MORENO III, et al. (22STCV28248) on May 17, 2024. The case concerns a dispute over the condition of a rental property and the amount of damages owed to the minor plaintiff, Marshawn Hemmans. The defendants offered to settle the matter in the total amount of $75,000. The Judge granted the Petition to Confirm Minor's Compromise, approving the settlement agreement and the allocation of the settlement payment between the claimants. The Judge found that the settlement was reasonable and approved the fee of 25% of the gross settlement for the minor's attorney.
