The Judge's Tentative Ruling Regarding Plaintiff's Motion to Vacate Notice of Settlement

In this case, MID CENTURY INSURANCE COMPANY (Plaintiff) filed an action against BIG Q AUTO REPAIR, LLC (Defendant) for unpaid premiums on a workers compensation insurance policy. On May 16, 2023, Plaintiff filed a Notice of Settlement with the Court, which was conditioned on Defendant completing specified matters within 45 days. However, Defendant failed to comply with the terms of the settlement agreement. As a result, Plaintiff moved to vacate the Notice of Settlement. The Judge has granted this motion, finding that grounds exist to vacate the Notice of Settlement due to Defendant's non-compliance. The case is reinstated, and a case management conference is set for January 20, 2025.
