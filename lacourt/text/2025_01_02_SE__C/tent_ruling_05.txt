This
action concerning real property was filed by Plaintiff ANGEL ORTIZ
(“Plaintiff”) (in pro per) against Defendants TEAM DELTA, INC.; ASSURED LENDER
SERVICES, INC.; and ALL PERSONS UNKNOWN on March 14, 2024. 
 
Plaintiff
alleges that he is “entitled to a judicial declaration that Plaintiff is the
!00% owner of the Subject Property[]” and “seeks to Foreclose to the Property
in order to recover the amounts owed in liens.” (Complaint ¶¶16 and 18.) 
 
On
October 3, 2024, Plaintiff failed to appear for the Case Management Conference.  The court set an OSC Re: Dismissal of the
entire case on December 31, 2024.  On
that date, Plaintiff failed to appear again, and the Complaint was dismissed
without prejudice.  
 
Accordingly,
Plaintiff’s Motion for Temporary Injunction Order is MOOT.
