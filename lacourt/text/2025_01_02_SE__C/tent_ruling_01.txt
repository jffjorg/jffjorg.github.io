Referee MATTHEW L. TAYLOR’s motion for confirmation of the sale of real property

Moving
Party to give notice.
 
No
Oppositions filed as of December 30, 2024. 
 
This
action for partition of real property located at 13024 Lefloss Avenue, Norwalk,
California (“Subject Property”) was filed on March 27, 2023. 
 
On
February 6, 2024, this Court entered an Interlocutory Judgment of Partition by
Sale of Real Property and Appointment of Referee, ordering the private sale of
the Subject Property and appointing Matthew L. Taylor as partition referee to
sell the Subject Property. 
 
Matthe
L. Taylor now moves for confirmation of the sale of the Subject Property for
$712,000 to buyers Gilberto Contreras, Leticia Contreras, Jennifer Contreras,
and Adrian Fernandez as the highest bidders. 
 
The
Court finds that the proposed sale is reasonable and thus GRANTS the Petitioner
Referee’s motion. 
 
The
Court will sign the Proposed Order filed in conjunction with the Subject
Motion.
