This is a tentative ruling on a motion to strike a claim for punitive damages brought by the plaintiff, Matlynn Rozell Giles, against the defendants, 7-Eleven, Inc., R. Berg Enterprises, Inc., and Ralph Berg. The plaintiff alleges that she sustained severe burns from hot coffee purchased at a 7-Eleven store. The defendants argue that the plaintiff has not sufficiently alleged malice, oppression, or fraud to support a claim for punitive damages. The court agrees with the defendants and strikes the plaintiff's claim for punitive damages. The court also grants the defendants' motion to strike certain paragraphs of the complaint.
