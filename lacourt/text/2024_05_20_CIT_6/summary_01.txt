This is a tentative ruling by a judge regarding a motion by La Verne Commons LLC to set aside a default entered against it on October 25, 2023. The judge grants the motion, finding that La Verne Commons' failure to notify its carrier of the action immediately upon receipt and its termination as a business entity on January 23, 2023, constitute excusable neglect. The judge also finds that La Verne Commons' counsel acted promptly to seek relief from the default and that Plaintiff did not suffer prejudice if the default is set aside. The judge declines to impose monetary sanctions against La Verne Commons. The judge's ruling allows La Verne Commons to file and serve its proposed answer to the complaint within five days of the order.
