DEPARTMENT 17
 
TENTATIVE RULING
 



EAST WEST BANK 
 
  
         vs.
 
VALENTINA PAPAZIAN, et al. 
 
  


 Case No.: 
  24STCV09146 
 
 
 
 Hearing Date:  September 4, 2024



 
Plaintiff’s motion to compel
Defendant Valentina Papazian to provide responses to Plaintiff’s Form
Interrogatories (Set One) and Special Interrogatories (Set One) is GRANTED. Defendant
Valentina Papazian is sanctioned, jointly and severally with counsel, $700.00. 
 
Plaintiff’s motion to compel
Defendant Takuhi Papazian to provide responses to Plaintiff’s Demand for
Inspection of Documents is GRANTED. Defendant Takuhi Papazian is sanctioned,
jointly and severally with counsel, $700.00. 
 
On 4/11/2024,
Plaintiff East West Bank (Plaintiff) filed suit against Valentina Papazian,
Takuhi Papazian, Arshavir Khachikian, Gevork Papazian, and Hagop Papazian
(collectively, Defendants) filed a complaint for unlawful detainer.  
 
            On
7/12/2024, Plaintiff moved to compel Defendant Valentina Papazian to provide
responses to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories (Set One) and Special
Interrogatories (Set One). 
 
            On
7/15/2024, Plaintiff moved to compel Defendant Takuhi Papazian to provide
responses to Plaintiff’s Demand for Inspection of Documents. 
 
            The motions
are unopposed.
 
            For ease,
the Court has consolidated its analysis into a single ruling. 
 
Discussion
 
            On
5/24/2024, Plaintiff propounded a Demand for Inspection of Documents (Set One)
upon Defendant Takuhi Papazian via overnight mail to determine whether
Defendants is a tenant and the terms of the tenancy, if any. To date, no
responses have been received and Defendant did not oppose this motion. 
 
            On
6/14/2024, Plaintiff propounded Form Interrogatories (Set One) and Special
Interrogatories (Set One) upon Defendant Valentina Papazian via personal
service to Defendant’s counsel. To date, no responses have been received and
Defendant did not oppose this motion.
 
Plaintiff’s motion to compel
responses are granted. Defendant Valentina Papazian is sanctioned, jointly and
severally with counsel, $700.00. ($350/hr x 2 hr.) Defendant Takuhi Papazian is
sanctioned, jointly and severally with counsel, $700.00. ($350/hr x 2 hr.)
 
 
It is so ordered.
