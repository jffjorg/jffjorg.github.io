And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted the motions filed by Petitioner Kevork Antablian against Respondent State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company. The motions sought an order deeming Requests for Admissions, Set 1, served on Respondent admitted, as well as an award of sanctions against Respondent and/or its counsel. The judge found that Respondent failed to serve responses to Petitioner's discovery requests, including Requests for Admissions, Form Interrogatories, and Demands for Production of Documents. As a result, the judge deemed the matters admitted and ordered Respondent to serve responses to the discovery requests. The judge also ordered sanctions against Respondent and/or its counsel in the amount of $3,246.60. The judge concluded that the motions are granted, and the requests for admission are deemed admitted.
