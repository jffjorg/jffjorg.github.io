This is a tentative ruling on a hearing for summary judgment motions filed by three defendants in a medical malpractice case. The plaintiff, Norma Berry, brought the case against the defendants, including Sandeep Khanna, M.D., Miguel Unzueta, M.D., and Mercy Eye Care Medical, Inc., alleging medical malpractice and lack of informed consent. The defendants argued that they met the standard of care and did not cause the plaintiff's injury. The court granted the summary judgment motion filed by Defendant Khanna and continued the hearings on the motions filed by Defendants Unzueta and Mercy.
