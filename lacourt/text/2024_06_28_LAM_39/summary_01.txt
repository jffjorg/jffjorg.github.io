The tentative ruling is in response to Defendant Steven C. Kim's motion to compel further discovery responses from Plaintiff 3800 West Sixth Street, LLC. Plaintiff opposes the motion. The court finds that Plaintiff's responses to Special Interrogatories are unsatisfactory and grants the motion to compel further responses to SROG Nos. 2, 5, 11, 14, 17, 20, 26, 41, 44, 47, 50 and 53. The court orders Plaintiff to serve the further responses within 30 days. The court declines to award sanctions to Defendant, finding that under the circumstances, an award of sanctions would be unjust.
