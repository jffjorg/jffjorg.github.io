The Court advances the hearing date on Plaintiffs’ Motions for
an Order Deeming RFAs Admitted to 10/14/24. 
On 6/26/24, Plaintiff Tony Lee served Defendants Safae and
Herrera with Requests for Admission (Set Two), Special Interrogatories (Set
Two), and Requests for Production of Documents (Set Two). To date, Defendants
have failed to serve responses. Nor have they filed oppositions to the motions
to compel. The Court grants the motions. Defendants shall serve verified written
responses, with no objections, to the RFPs and Special Interrogatories and
produce documents by 5 p.m. on 10/28/24. The RFAs are deemed admitted. The
Court awards sanctions of $710 per motion, for a total of $4,260 for the six
motions. The sanctions shall be jointly and severally paid by Defendants and
their attorney to Plaintiff’s counsel by 10/28/24.
