And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has granted Wow Media Inc.'s motion for leave to serve third-party discovery in this action and approving the Los Angeles Subpoena. The Plaintiff sought permission to serve discovery to identify potential defendants, despite the 20-day hold on deposition notices after service of the summons. The Court found that the Plaintiff has demonstrated good faith in its delay in suing particular named defendants until it had knowledge of sufficient facts to cause a reasonable person to believe liability is probable.

The Plaintiff has demonstrated that it does not have knowledge of the person who circulated the Outdoor Advertising Review (OAR), but that Davidson Kempner Capital Management, LP has information that would be useful in identifying potential defendants in the instant action. The Court granted the motion, finding that the Plaintiff has sufficiently demonstrated its need to proceed with discovery to identify potential defendants.
