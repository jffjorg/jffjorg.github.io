This tentative ruling is regarding a motion for an order approving the transfer of structured settlement payment rights. The payee, Robert Bravo, Jr., entered into a Structured Settlement Payments Purchase Agreement with Petitioner J.G. Wentworth Originations, LLC, under which the payee will sell future payments totaling $509,146.92 in exchange for a purchase price of $27,583.41. The court found that the transfer is fair, reasonable, and in the best interest of the payee. The payee was advised in writing to seek independent professional advice, but chose not to. The court granted the petition for approval of the structured settlement payments, conditionally on the petitioner providing the payee's full address to the court at the hearing on this motion.
