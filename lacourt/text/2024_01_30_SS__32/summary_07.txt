The tentative ruling grants the motion for trial preference filed by Plaintiffs Vanessa Jung Tirman, Estate of Jonathan Dongin Jung, Jung Sik Jung, and Insoon Jung. The motion was filed in support of Insoon Jung, who is 76 years old and suffers from dementia, Alzheimer's Disease, and overall failing health. The Court finds that Insoon has a substantial interest in the action as a whole and that her health is such that a preference is necessary to prevent prejudicing her interest in the litigation. The ruling also continues the hearing on the motion to exclude to the trial date of May 9, 2024, and advances the hearings on the discovery motions to February 28, 2024.
