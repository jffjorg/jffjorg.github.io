And here is the English text summary:

The tentative ruling is regarding the motion to deem request for admissions (RFAs) admitted against Plaintiff Samuel Yun. The Defendant 21ST CENTURY INSURANCE COMPANY propounded RFAs on December 15, 2022, but received no response from Plaintiff. Despite sending meet and confer emails, Plaintiff failed to provide verified responses. The Defendant seeks an order deeming the RFAs admitted against Plaintiff and imposing sanctions.

The Court finds that the Defendant properly served RFAs on Plaintiff and that Plaintiff's unverified responses are not in compliance with CCP ยง 2033.220. Therefore, the Defendant's motion is granted, and the RFAs, set one, are deemed admitted against Plaintiff.

The Court also imposes sanctions on Plaintiff and Plaintiff's counsel in the amount of $300, which includes attorney fees and costs. The Defendant is awarded one hour to prepare each motion to compel and one hour to appear at the hearing, all at the reasonable rate of $120 per hour.
