 Case Number:  24STCV15076    Hearing Date:   January 13, 2025    Dept:  14 #10Case Background This is an action for unlawful detainer. Plaintiff  alleges he entered into an oral month-to-month tenancy with Defendant. The  tenancy was terminated for at-fault just cause. On June 17, 2024, Plaintiff Farhad Yaghoubi filed his  Complaint against Defendant Clarence Tate. On August 14, 2024, Defendant filed a notice of stay of  proceedings. On December 4, 2024, Defendant filed this demurrer. On December 5, 2024, Plaintiff filed an opposition. On December 10, 2024, the Court advanced the hearing  date on the demurrer to January 13, 2025, at Defendant’s request. Instant Pleading Defendant demurs to Plaintiff’s Complaint. Decision Defendant filed for bankruptcy and this matter is  subject to the automatic stay. On the Court’s own motion, the demurrer  scheduled for January 13, 2025, is continued to February 10, 2025.  Discussion Defendant filed a notice of stay of proceedings due to  an automatic stay caused by his bankruptcy filing. The filing of a bankruptcy  proceeding automatically stays all state and federal proceedings outside of the  bankruptcy court against the debtor and the debtor’s property. (11 U.S.C.,  section 362(a).) Here, this matter is stayed until the automatic stay is  lifted. The demurrer is continued to February 10, 2025. Conclusion Defendant filed for bankruptcy and this matter is  subject to the automatic stay. On the Court’s own motion, the demurrer  scheduled for January 13, 2025, is continued to February 10, 2025.
