 Case Number:  23NWCV03490    Hearing Date:   August 1, 2024    Dept:  C Lee v. Hernandez, Case No. 23NWCV03490
 
This is a personal injury lawsuit involving a motor vehicle
collision.  Defendant applies ex parte to advance the hearing on
Defendant’s motion to vacate and set aside default and default judgment. It is
currently scheduled for April 29, 2025. Defendant requests a date on or before
August 30, 2024.  Defendant argues that otherwise, evidence may be lost,
and witnesses may forget what happened.  The ex parte application is
GRANTED in part.  The Court is unable to accommodate the motion on its
calendar on or before August 30, 2024.  The hearing is ADVANCED from April
29, 2025 to today’s date and CONTINUED to October 15, 2024 at 10:30 A.M. in
Dept. SE-C.  Moving party to give notice.
