 Case Number:  23NWCV02000    Hearing Date:   August 1, 2024    Dept:  C CHAMBERS v. MOLINA
CASE NO.: 23NWCV02000
HEARING:  08/01/24
 
ADD ON
 
Plaintiff Ahkeel Chambers’s Motion to Set Aside Dismissal is
DENIED.
 
Court Clerk to give notice. 
 
This personal injury
action was filed by Plaintiff AKHEEL CHAMBERS (“Plaintiff”) on June 27, 2023. 
 
A dismissal was
entered on January 22, 2024 based on a lack of prosecution. 
 
On July 15, 2024,
Plaintiff moved ex parte before this Court to set aside the dismissal.
 
On July 15, 2024,
this Court issued the following Order: “Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Application to Set Aside the Dismissal is DENIED. The
Alternative Request for an Order Shortening Time is GRANTED.
Plaintiff's Motion to Set Aside Dismissal will be heard on Thursday,
July 25, 2024 at 10:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C. The ex parte application is
deemed to be the moving papers.  Plaintiff is ordered to lodge a First
Amended Complaint with the Court by July 18, 2024.  Opposition, if any,
may be filed by July 22, 2024.” (See 07/15/24 M.O.)
 
On July 24, 2024, this Court issued the
following Order: “As the parties may be aware, the Superior Court of Los
Angeles County experienced a ransomware cyberattack which was discovered in the
early morning hours of July 19, 2024 that caused the Court to disable its
network systems. As a result, the Court recognizes that Plaintiff, through no
fault of their own, may have [been] unable to comply with this Court’s 07/15/24
Order. This matter is CONTINUED as indicated above. [¶] The
Ex Parte Application is deemed the Moving Papers. Plaintiff is ORDERED to file
and serve a Declaration in conjunction with a Proposed First Amended Complaint
by no later than July 29, 2024. Opposition, if any, may be filed and served by
no later than July 31, 2024.” 
 
As of July 30, 2024, no Proposed First Amended Complaint has
been filed/lodged with this Court. The Motion is DENIED.
