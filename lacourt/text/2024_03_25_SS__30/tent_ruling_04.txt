 Case Number:  22STCV05294    Hearing Date:   March 25, 2024    Dept:  30 YODIT GIRMA, AN INDIVIDUAL vs GREGORIO CORTES DE LA CRUZ, AN INDIVIDUAL, et al.TENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff, Yodit Girma, is GRANTED, effective upon filing proof of service of the signed order on the client. Plaintiff’s counsel has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). Further, trial is not yet set and thus, Plaintiff has sufficient time to retain new counsel, and will not be prejudiced by the granting this motion.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
