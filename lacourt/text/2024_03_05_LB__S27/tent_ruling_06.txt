S27 8:30 a.m. March 5, 2024      Plaintiff filed this action on 8/08/23.   Plaintiff filed proof of service of the summons, complaint, and related documents  on Defendant on 10/18/23.  On 12/08/23,  at Plaintiff’s request, the Clerk entered Defendant’s default.  On 1/08/24, the Court held a CMC.  Plaintiff and Defendant both appeared at the  CMC.  Defendant indicated an intention to  file a motion to vacate the default.  The  Court specially set the motion to vacate the default for hearing on 3/05/24 at  8:30 a.m., and also set a default prove-up hearing for the same date and time.   Pursuant to CCP §1005, any moving papers in support of the motion to  vacate default needed to be filed on or before 2/08/24.  To date, Defendant has not filed moving  papers.  The Court therefore takes the motion  to vacate default off calendar.     The Court will proceed with the default judgment prove-up hearing as  scheduled.     DATED:  March 5, 2024                         _____________________________________                                                             MARK  C.
