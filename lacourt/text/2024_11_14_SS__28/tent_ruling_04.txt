 Case Number:  22STCV27856    Hearing Date:   November 14, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
August 26, 2022, Plaintiffs Omar Eduardo Coreas and Christopher Eduardo Coreas,
a minor by and through his guardian ad litem Omar Eduardo Corea [sic], filed
this action against Defendants Anthony Wright (“Wright”) and Does 1-25 for
negligence. 
On
September 6, 2022, the Court appointed Omar Eduardo Coreas to serve as
Plaintiff Christopher Eduardo Coreas’s guardian ad
litem. 
On
November 14, 2022, Plaintiffs amended the complaint to add Defendant Patricia
Tillman (“Tillman”) as Doe 1. 
On
January 3, 2023, Wright and Tillman filed an answer. 
On
February 5, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On
August 12, 2024, Petitioner Omar Eduardo Coreas (“Petitioner”) filed a petition
for expedited approval of the compromise of minor Plaintiff Christopher Eduardo
Coreas’s claims. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
asks the Court for expedited approval of the compromise of the pending action
of minor Plaintiff Christopher Eduardo Coreas. 
DISCUSSION 
The
petition contains the following information: 
Gross
settlement amount:                    $9,500.00
Total
medical expenses:                       $3,715.30
Medical
expenses paid:                        $11.64
Reductions:                                          $3,006.57
To
be paid etc. from proceeds:             $708.73
Liens:                                                   $708.73
Attorney’s
fees:                                   $2,375.00
Costs:                                $435.00
Net
balance:                      $5,981.27 
Petitioner
has submitted a complete petition with all required information.  The Court grants the petition and orders Petitioner
to deposit the net settlement amount of $5,981.27 in insured accounts in Bank
of America, 5931 Pacific Blvd., Huntington Park, CA 90255, subject to
withdrawal only on authorization of the Court. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor
Plaintiff Christopher Eduardo Coreas’s action filed by Petitioner Omar Eduardo Coreas on August 12, 2024.  Petitioner is to deposit the net proceeds of $5,981.27
in insured accounts in Bank of America, 5931 Pacific Blvd., Huntington Park, CA
90255, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court.

Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement on January 3, 2025, at

The
Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on January 3, 2025, at
8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.
