The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Halley, et al. v. California Fair Plan Ass'n., et al. (Case No. 23SMCV01291). The case involves a dispute over insurance policies for a house and guest house that were damaged in the 2018 Woolsey fire. The Judge has overruled the defendants' demurrer to the plaintiffs' First Amended Complaint (FAC) on both the breach of contract and breach of the covenant of good faith and fair dealing causes of action. The Judge has also struck the punitive damages allegations in the FAC, finding that there are insufficient factual allegations to sustain a claim for punitive damages. The case is set for a hearing on July 25, 2024.
