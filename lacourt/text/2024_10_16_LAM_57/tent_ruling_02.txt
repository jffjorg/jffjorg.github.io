 Case Number:  20STCV03322    Hearing Date:   October 16, 2024    Dept:  57 The Court adheres to its tentative ruling issued on October 4, 2024 to deny Plaintiffs' motion to compel the deposition of Defendant Los Angeles Community College District's Person Most Qualified.  The Court agrees with the position of the Defendants set forth in their opposition to the motion that the temporal scope of the deposition topics and the topics themselves are too broad.The Court is denying the Defendants' motion to quash the subpoena Plaintiffs issued for the production of business records related to Defendant Edmond Guzman's prior employment at United Parcel Service, Inc.
