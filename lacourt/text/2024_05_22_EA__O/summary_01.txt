The judge sustains General Motors' demurrer to the plaintiff's first amended complaint, allowing the plaintiff to amend the complaint. The demurrer was filed in response to the plaintiff's claims of fraudulent inducement—concealment, alleging that General Motors knew about the defects in the 2022 Chevrolet Silverado Ltd but concealed this information from the plaintiff. The judge finds that the plaintiff's complaint fails to plead specific facts as to General Motors' knowledge of the defect and lacks sufficient allegations to support a fraud claim. The judge also finds that the plaintiff's allegations of intent to conceal information are conclusory and lack specific details. The judge's tentative ruling sustains the demurrer with leave to amend, and the motion to strike punitive damages is moot.
