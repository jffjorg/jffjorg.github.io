 Case Number:  23STCP04405    Hearing Date:   March 11, 2024    Dept:  26 Petitioner has failed to provide any
form of identification tied to a fingerprint on file, which is necessary for
Probation to run a criminal history assessment. 
On January 29, 2024, the Court continued the petition noting this issue
and ordered Petitioner to provide Probation with some form of identification
verified by a fingerprint to enable Probation to run a criminal history
assessment and identified how this may be done. 
However, Petitioner failed to do so. 
Accordingly, the instant petition is DENIED WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
Petitioner may submit a new petition addressing the lack of identification tied
to a fingerprint for Probation to run a criminal history assessment.
