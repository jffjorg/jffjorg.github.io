The judge is considering a motion by Tomatera Gomez USA, Inc. (Tomatera) for an order granting leave to file a cross-complaint against Nutman Enterprises, LLC (Nutman Enterprises) in an unlawful detainer action. Tomatera seeks to file a cross-complaint alleging causes of action for breach of contract, common counts, and declaratory relief. The motion is unopposed.

The judge notes that Tomatera filed an answer to the Complaint on September 28, 2023, and that during settlement discussions, Tomatera became aware of a dispute over the return of its security deposit and whether it owes for any damage to the property occurring during its tenancy.

The judge finds that Tomatera has demonstrated good cause for leave to file the proposed cross-complaint under California Code of Civil Procedure section 426.50. The judge grants the motion and orders Tomatera to file and serve the proposed cross-complaint within 3 days of the date of this order.
