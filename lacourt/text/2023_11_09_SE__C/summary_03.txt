This tentative ruling is issued by Judge C Simmons regarding the breach of lease action filed by Plaintiffs Elaine M. Simmons and Janet K. Cunningham against Defendants Colima Professional Building, LLC and Victor Fahmy Khalil. The ruling sustains the demurrer to the First Amended Complaint with 20 days leave to amend. The Plaintiffs alleged that they own a 50% interest in real property and that the lease was transferred to the Simmons Family Trust. The Defendant Khalil acquired lessee's interest in the lease and is currently paying monthly rent of $4,679.16. However, the Plaintiffs believe that the property value is $6,000,000.00 and that the monthly rental should be about $30,000.00. The Judge rules that the Plaintiffs lack standing to bring claims as individuals for harm to the Trust and that the demurrer is sustained.
