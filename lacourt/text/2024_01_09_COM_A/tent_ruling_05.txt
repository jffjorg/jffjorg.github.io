 Beatrice et al. v. State of California, et al.

[TENTATIVE] ORDER MOTION TO
 BE RELIEVED AS COUNSEL
 
      The complaint alleges that Defendant
struck and killed Plaintiffs’ decedent with her motor vehicle. Defendant, State
of California, negligently designed the roadway where the incident happened.
Plaintiffs allege claims for negligence and a survivor claim for Plaintiffs’
decedent.               Counsel for Plaintiffs
 filed this motion on December 7, 2023, for an order to be relieved
as counsel of record because of a breakdown in communication with the
 client.  Counsel timely served
the motion on the clients by mail at an address confirmed by an asset and
location search within the last 30 days. Counsel has also lodged a proposed
order. 
      Counsel has complied with the requirements
of California
Rules of Court, Rule 3.1362. Accordingly, the motion is GRANTED. The order
is effective upon the filing of the proof of service of the signed order upon
the client.
