The Court has issued a tentative ruling on a demurrer to the first cause of action for breach of written contract filed by the defendants, DRM Marketing International, LLC and Derek White. The plaintiff, Scott Reiter, alleges that the defendants breached a lease agreement by failing to pay rent and maintain the property. The defendants argued that the complaint is uncertain and fails to state a cause of action. The Court has overruled the demurrer, finding that the plaintiff has adequately pleaded a cause of action for breach of written contract. The Court also notes that the expiration of the lease agreement is a factual issue that will be determined at a later stage of the litigation. The defendants are ordered to file an answer to the complaint on or before February 13, 2024.
