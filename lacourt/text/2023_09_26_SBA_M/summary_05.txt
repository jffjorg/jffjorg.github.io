Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge ruled on a demurrer filed by the defendants, Oasis NOI Holding, LP, and 13605 Vermont Apartments, LLC, to the 7th cause of action for intentional infliction of emotional distress (IIED) in the complaint filed by Guillermo Flores, et al. The court found that the allegations did not show that the defendants engaged in extreme and outrageous conduct, as required by California law. The allegations were deemed insufficient and conclusory, and the demurrer was sustained with 20 days leave to amend.

The judge also ruled on a motion to strike punitive damages, which was granted with 20 days leave to amend. The court found that the allegations supporting punitive damages were conclusory and did not show oppression, fraud, or malice.

The judge ordered the defendants to give notice of the ruling.
