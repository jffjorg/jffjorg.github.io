 Case Number:  23STCV28285    Hearing Date:   September 19, 2024    Dept:  76             Plaintiffs
allege wage and hour violations and disability discrimination, including
wrongful termination.
             Defendant Rochelle Sterling, dba Beverly Hills
Plaza Hotel & Spa filed a Cross-Complaint against Contract Services Group,
Inc. for indemnity in providing payroll services which violated wage and hour
laws.
Defendant Rochelle Sterling, dba
Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel & Spa (Doe 1) moves to compel responses to form
interrogatories and requests for production of documents, and requests
sanctions. 
TENTATIVE RULING 
In light of Plaintiff’s service
of responses after this motion was filed, Defendant Rochelle Sterling, dba
Beverly Hills Plaza Hotel & Spa (Doe 1)’s motion to compel responses to
form interrogatories and requests for production of documents is MOOT. The
Court notes that Defendant was required to reserve and file separate motions,
but combined two motions into one,. If Defendant requires further responses,
Defendant may file separate motions as appropriate. 
Defendant’s request for
sanctions against Plaintiff and her counsel Lipeles Law Group APC is GRANTED[1] in
the reduced amount of $690 (2 hours at $315/hour, plus a $60 filing fee).
Sanctions are to be paid to Defendant’s counsel within 20 days.
