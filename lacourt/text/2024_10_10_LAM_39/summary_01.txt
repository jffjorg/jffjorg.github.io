The judge is considering a motion for leave to file a cross-complaint by the defendants, Julian Ledesma, Sweatheory LLC, Sweatheory Wellness, LLC, and the Sweatheory Group, LLC. The defendants seek to assert a cross-complaint against Paragon Resources, Inc. and Royfield, Inc. (Cross-Defendants) for breach of contract, indemnity, and contribution. The defendants leased a unit from the Cross-Defendants without knowledge of the plaintiffs' presence on the premises. The judge grants the motion, finding it in the interest of justice to resolve all claims regarding the dispute over the plaintiffs' right to the premises in this action. The defendants are ordered to provide notice of this order and file proof of service of same.
