 Case Number:  23VECV05363    Hearing Date:   May 14, 2024    Dept:  T 
Tentative ruling:  Motion to consolidate 23VECV02331 Laines v Romo and 23VECV05363 Matias v Romo is Granted.  The cases are consolidated for all purposes.  Given that Laines v Romo is already set for trial, the court will discuss with counsel the status of Matias v Romo and whether the trial date should be modified.  All future documents shall be filed under the Laines v Romo case name and case number showing the consolidated case number.  Documents filed in 23VECV05363 Matias v Romo will not be considered.
