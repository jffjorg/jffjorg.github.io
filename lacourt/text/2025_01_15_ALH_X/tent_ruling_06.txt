 Case Number:  24NNCV03569    Hearing Date:   January 15, 2025    Dept:  X #18 – Edit Garibyan v. Christopher LopezMOTION TO COMPEL RESPONSES TO SPECIAL INTERROGATORIES AND REQUEST  FOR SANCTIONS IN THE AMOUNT OF $12,560The Court has reviewed the moving,  opposing, and reply papers.  The Motion  to Compel Responses to Special Interrogatories is moot where responses have  been provided.  This is without prejudice  to Plaintiff seeking further relief from the Court concerning the adequacy of  the responses.The Court awards sanctions in the amount  of $400.00 in favor of Plaintiff and against Defendant Christopher L. Lopez and  his counsel of record, Bretoi, Lutz & Stele, jointly and severally.  Sanctions are payable to Plaintiff’s counsel  and due within 30 days of mailing of a notice of ruling.Moving Party is to give notice.
