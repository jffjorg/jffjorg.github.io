And here is a summary in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a demurrer filed by Defendant The Regents of the University of California to the 1st cause of action for premises liability in a lawsuit filed by Plaintiff Deborah Harris. The Plaintiff alleges she sustained bodily injuries on March 23, 2022, at a medical facility in Burbank, owned and operated by Defendants UCLA Health Systems, UCLA Burbank Neurology & Cardiology Care, Burbank Specialty Care, and Golden State Medical Plaza. The Plaintiff claims that the facility was negligent in allowing an unsecured bench to tip over, causing her injuries. The Defendant Regents argues that it was not named in the original complaint and that the Plaintiff has failed to provide sufficient facts to show how the Defendant is liable for the incident. The Judge has sustained the demurrer with leave to amend, giving the Plaintiff 20 days to correct and clarify the pleadings.
