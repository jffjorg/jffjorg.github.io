The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion brought by Plaintiff Veronica Alejandra Segura against Defendant Walmart, Inc. The motion is to compel Walmart to produce a complete and timely medical report from their expert, Dr. Russell W. Nelson, and to impose sanctions for Walmart's alleged abuse of the discovery process. The Judge finds that Walmart's initial report was incomplete and vague, and that Walmart refused to provide a reciprocal agreement to extend Plaintiff's time to file the motion. The Judge grants the motion and orders Walmart to produce a complete and timely report. The Judge also imposes sanctions of $2,627.50 on Walmart and their counsel, Lisa Mallinson, jointly and severally, payable to Plaintiff within 10 days.
