MOVING PARTY:                 Applicant Shahin Eghbal        
 
RESPONDING PARTY:       Unopposed
Application of Shahin Eghbal to Appear as Counsel Pro Hac Vice
The court
considered the moving papers filed in connection with this application.  No opposition papers were filed. 
DISCUSSION
Applicant Shahin Eghbal requests an order permitting him to appear as
counsel pro hac vice for defendants Sommer Ray Beaty and Sommer Ray, LLC in
this action. 
The court finds that the application substantially complies with
California Rules of Court, rule 9.40. 
The court therefore grants the application and orders that Shahin Eghbal
is permitted to appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendants Sommer Ray Beaty
and Sommer Ray, LLC in this action. 
(Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40, subd. (a).) 
 
            The court orders defendants Sommer
Ray Beaty and Sommer Ray, LLC to give notice of this ruling.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
