The tentative ruling grants plaintiff's motion to compel compliance with Department D's standing order regarding discovery in Song-Beverly litigation. The court finds that defendant FCA US, LLC failed to timely respond to authorized method of discovery, failed to respond to repeated attempts by plaintiff to gain access to a purported document production, and failed to respond to meet and confer efforts pointing out deficiencies in the March 8, 2024 unverified responses. The court awards monetary sanctions in the amount of $3,150.00 to the plaintiff and against the defendant and defendant's attorney, jointly and severally, payable within 30 days.
