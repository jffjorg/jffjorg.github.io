 Case Number:  23VECV00077    Hearing Date:   January 31, 2024    Dept:  T   23VECV00077 MINAS MICHAEL AVSHARIAN, AN... vs ARPY  GOULOOMIAN  [TENTATIVE] ORDER:   Plaintiff Minas Michael Avsharian’s Unopposed Motion for Leave to Amend  the Second Amended Complaint is GRANTED.   Plaintiff Minas Michael Avsharian is ordered to file the Proposed Third  Amended Complaint within 20 days. Introduction  Plaintiff Minas Michael Avsharian (Plaintiff) moved to amend  the Second Amended Complaint (SAC) as to the remaining breach of contract and  negligence claims against remaining Defendants James Wellbaum, Triwell  Properties Inc., and 14936 Ventura LLC (collectively, Defendants).  Defendants stipulated to the requested relief  and do not intend to oppose the motion.   Plaintiff attached a copy of the Proposed Third Amended Complaint (PTAC).  The motion is a result of the parties’ meet  and confer efforts. Discussion   Plaintiff argued that there is good cause to amend the SAC  due to the dismissal of Co-Defendants Arpy Gouloomian and RP&A LLC.  Plaintiff provided that the claims have been  reduced to two causes of action for breach of contract and negligence against  the remaining Defendants.  Plaintiff  provided that the motion will not prejudice Defendants and the motion was a  result of the dismissal of Arpy Gouloomian and RP&A LLC.  Plaintiff sufficiently provided good cause in  support of the motion based upon the above facts. The motion for leave to amend is GRANTED.   IT IS SO ORDERED, CLERK TO GIVE NOTICE.
