  Case Number:  23LBCV02312    Hearing Date:   December 4, 2024    Dept:  S25 Tentative RulingDefendant’s Motion to Compel Plaintiff Joseph Del Crognale’s Deposition is GRANTED.Plaintiff’s counsel indicates counsel is working to provide deposition dates between December 2, 2024 thru January 31, 2025 to Defendant, however, Defendant is entitled take Plaintiff’s deposition within a reasonable period from the Deposition Notice.While the Court recognizes the efforts made by Plaintiff’s counsel to comply with discovery obligations and counsel’s attempts to communicate with Plaintiff, Plaintiff is ordered to provide deposition dates no later than 10 days from today’s hearing and appear for deposition no later than January 17, 2025. Plaintiff is also to provide code-complainant responses to the request for production in the deposition notice. As Defendant does not seek monetary sanctions, the Court will not award monetary sanctions.
