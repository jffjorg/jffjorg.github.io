The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motions to compel responses and sanctions brought by Plaintiffs Miguel Angel Torres Alvarez and Nelly Torres against Defendants Bennettis Italia, Inc., Sara Ahmadinia, and Mohammad Ahmadinia.
