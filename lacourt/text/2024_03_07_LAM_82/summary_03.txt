The judge has granted Bank of America, N.A.'s (plaintiff) unopposed applications for right to attach orders with respect to defendants Dinesh V. Savalia and Babulal A. Savalia. The Court finds that the claim is one upon which attachment may be issued, and that the plaintiff has established the probable validity of the claim. The defendants are in default under a loan agreement and an equipment agreement, and have failed to pay amounts owed by Performance Engineered Products, Inc. (the plaintiff's borrower). The Court has determined that the amount to be attached is $777,136.79, which includes $771,471.55 for the loan agreement and $5,665.24 for the equipment agreement. The writ will issue upon the posting of two separate bonds in the amount of $10,000 each for the two defendants.
