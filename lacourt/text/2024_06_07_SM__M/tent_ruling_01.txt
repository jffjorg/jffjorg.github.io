  Case Number:  19SMCV01976    Hearing Date:   June 7, 2024    Dept:  M Plaintiff requests that the attorney's fee award reflect additional $900.00 to Dr. Siegemund for the fees her
counsel incurred in connection with attending the April 23, 2024, hearing. The Court agrees that its order should have included this additional amount.  
Plaintiff’s motion to amend the April 23, 2024, minute order
nunc pro tunc is GRANTED. (CCP 473(d).) The Court strikes the following
language from the order: “the motion for fees is GRANTED in the reduced total amount
of $21,902.50.” and replaces it with the following: “the motion for fees is
GRANTED in the reduced total amount of $22,802.50.”
