Summary:

Judge Holly J. Fujie has issued a tentative ruling regarding a demurrer filed by the defendant, California Department of Housing and Community Development, Department of the State of California, to the entire complaint. The plaintiff, Scottie J. Thompson Jr., has alleged breach of contract, violation of Unruh Civil Rights Act, etc. The demurrer argues that the plaintiff failed to comply with the Government Tort Claims Act by not filing a timely government claim with the Department of General Services (DGS). The court has taken judicial notice of the DGS's rejection of the plaintiff's government claim. The court finds that the plaintiff has not alleged compliance with the Government Tort Claims Act and has not shown that he filed a timely government claim. Therefore, the demurrer is sustained, and the complaint is dismissed.
