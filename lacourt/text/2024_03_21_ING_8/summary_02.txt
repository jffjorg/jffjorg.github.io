Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge's tentative ruling is in response to a motion to compel further responses to plaintiff's request for production of documents, set one, and a request for sanctions. The plaintiffs, Gina Thomas and Ronald Williams, brought the motion against the defendant, General Motors, LLC, alleging that the defendant failed to provide code-compliant responses and documents responsive to their requests.

The judge grants the motion in part and denies it in part. The judge orders the defendant to provide a verified further written response and production of documents by April 12, 2024. The judge also orders the defendant to identify in its verified further written responses the RFP category corresponding to the documents that are responsive to the request.

The judge is inclined to order the defendant to produce documents responsive to certain requests, including requests for OBDII codes, vehicle symptom codes, and labor operation codes. The judge is also inclined to order the defendant to identify in its verified further written responses the RFP category corresponding to the documents that are responsive to the request.

The judge will entertain a discussion from the defendant's counsel at the hearing as to why no responsive documents have been produced or if they have been. The judge is inclined to order the defendant to produce the documents and to provide a verified further written response by April 12, 2024.
