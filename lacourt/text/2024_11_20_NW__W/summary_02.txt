And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has granted Plaintiff Kelsi Smith's motion to compel Defendant AMFP V Eton Warner LLC and Abacus Capital Group, LLC to respond to a request for admission that was served on June 11, 2024. The Defendant failed to respond to the request despite agreements to do so, and the Judge has deemed the truth of the facts specified in the request for admission to be admitted. The Judge has also ordered the Defendant to produce verified discovery responses without objection within 10 days.

Additionally, the Judge has granted Plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the reduced amount of $510.00. The sanction is based on the time and effort spent by Plaintiff's counsel in preparing the motion, reviewing the opposition, and appearing at the hearing. The Judge has reduced the amount of the sanction from the original request of $1,635.00.
