 Case Number:  23VECV02755    Hearing Date:   January 24, 2024    Dept:  T SIMHAI V TESLA 23VECV02755 [TENTATIVE] ORDER:   Defendant Tesla Inc.’s Unopposed Motion to Compel Arbitration and Stay  the Action is GRANTED.   Defendant Tesla Inc.’s Request for Judicial Notice is  GRANTED but not as to any hearsay or facts in dispute. Arbitration Status Conference is set for 1/30/2025 at 8:30  a.m.  Mandatory appearance on status of  arbitration unless the case is dismissed or judgment entered. Introduction  Defendant Tesla Inc. (Defendant) moved to compel arbitration  and to stay Plaintiffs Sassan Simhai and Eden Water Conservation Inc.’s  (collectively, Plaintiffs) action.   Plaintiffs filed a notice of non-opposition on January 10, 2024. Discussion   Defendant provided that the Purchase Order Agreement  (Agreement) entered on June 21, 2022 included an arbitration provision  requiring arbitration of any and all disputes arising out of or relating to any  aspect of the relationship between Plaintiff and Defendant.  (Raymond Kim (Kim) Decl. pars. 3-4, Exh. 1,  pg. 3.)  With submission of the  arbitration agreement, the burden transfers to Plaintiffs.  Because Plaintiffs filed a notice of  non-opposition, Plaintiffs submitted on the motion.  The Court does not find any dispute as to the  relief requested and finds the motion persuasive. The motion to compel arbitration and stay the action is  GRANTED.   IT IS SO ORDERED, CLERK TO GIVE NOTICE.
