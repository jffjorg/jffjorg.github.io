And here is the English text summary:

This tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents filed by the plaintiff, Justine Amanda Cruz Echavez, against the defendant, California Institute of the Arts. The plaintiff requested verified responses to his requests for production, but the defendant failed to provide verification. The court granted the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests for production. The defendant is ordered to provide verified responses within 30 days. Additionally, the court imposed sanctions of $2,100 against the defendant and their counsel of record for misusing the discovery process.
