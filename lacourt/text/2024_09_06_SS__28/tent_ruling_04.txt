 Case Number:  22STCV24349    Hearing Date:   September 6, 2024    Dept:  28  Having considered the
petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
July 28, 2022, Plaintiff Miguel Angel Perdomo and Melanie Perdomo,
a minor by and through her guardian ad litem Miguel Angel Perdomo, filed this
action against Defendants Duro Roofing, Inc., Young Soon Park, and Does 1-20
for motor vehicle tort and general negligence. 
On
January 11, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On
August 6, 2024, Petitioner Miguel Angel Perdomo (“Petitioner”) filed a petition
to approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff Melanie Perdomo’s action.  The petition was set for hearing on heard on
September 6, 2024. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Miguel Angel Perdomo asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending
action of minor Plaintiff Melanie Perdomo. 
DISCUSSION 
The
petition includes the following information: 
Gross
settlement amount:                                        $2,330.00
Total
medical expenses:                                            $830.00
Reductions:                                                              $630.00
To
be paid/reimbursed from proceeds:                     $200.00
Liens:                                                                       $200.00
Attorney’s
fees:                                                        $582.50
Costs:                                                                      
 $200.00
Net
settlement amount:                                             $1,347.50
 Petitioner
has submitted a completed petition with all required information.  The Court grants the petition.  The sum of $1,347.50 is to be paid or
delivered to Miguel Angel Perdomo, 5119 Tenaya Avenue, Apartment A, South Gate,
California,  90280, without bond, on the
terms and under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402. 
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff Melanie
Perdomo’s action filed by Petitioner Miguel Angel Perdomo on August 6, 2024.
 The net
balance of $1,347.50 is to be paid or delivered to Miguel Angel Perdomo, 5119
Tenaya Avenue, Apartment A, South Gate, California,  90280, without bond, on the terms and under
the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
