 Case Number:  22STCV18203    Hearing Date:   December 5, 2024    Dept:  28           Having considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules
as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
A.   Case number 22STCV18203 
On
June 3, 2022, Plaintiffs Danilo Diaz and Karina
Siguenza filed an action against Defendants German Joel Iraheta (“Iraheta”)
and Does 1-25 for negligence.  (Case
number 22STCV18203.) 
On
August 11, 2022, Iraheta filed an answer. On October 27, 2023, the Court
dismissed Iraheta. 
On
October 30, 2023, the Court dismissed the complaint without prejudice at the
request of Plaintiffs Danilo Diaz and
Karina Siguenza. 
B.   Case number 22STCV20252 
On
June 21, 2022, Plaintiffs Domingo Castro, Daniela Castro, a minor, Arturo
Castro, a minor, and Andres Castro, a minor, by and through their guardian ad litem
Domingo Castro filed an action against Defendants Iraheta, Danilo Esteban Diaz
Munoz, Hector Alcides Rojas Vasquez (“Rojas Vasquez”), and Does 1-30 for motor
vehicle tort and general negligence.  (Case number 22STCV20252.) 
On
June 23, 2022, the Court appointed Domingo Castro to serve as guardian ad litem
for Plaintiffs Andres Castro and Arturo Castro. 
On June 27, 2022, the Court appointed Domingo Castro to serve as
guardian ad litem for Plaintiff Daniela Castro. 
On
September 16, 2022, Iraheta filed an answer. 
On
October 17, 2022, Plaintiffs amended the complaint to include Defendant German
Custom Glass and Mirror LLC (“GCGM”) as Doe 1. 
On
May 2, 2023, Rojas Vasquez filed an answer and a cross-complaint against
Cross-Defendants Danielo Esteban Diaz Munoz, Iraheta, GCGM, and Roes 1-20 for
apportionment of fault and indemnification. 
On August 8, 2023, Iraheta and GCGM filed an answer to the
cross-complaint. 
On
May 6, 2024, the Court dismissed Rojas Vasquez from Plaintiffs’ complaint without
prejudice at Plaintiffs’ request. 
On
July 11, 2024, the Court dismissed Iraheta, GCGM, and Roes 1-20 from Rojas
Vasquez’s cross-complaint with prejudice at Rojas Vasquez’s request. 
C.   The Court relates and consolidates the cases 
On
July 3, 2023, the Court found that case numbers 22STCV18203 and 22STCV20252 are
related within the meaning of California Rules of Court, rule 3.300(a).  Case number 22STCV18203 became the lead case.  The cases were assigned to Department 28 at
the Spring Street Courthouse for all purposes. 
On
October 16, 2023, the Court consolidated the actions for all purposes,
including trial, based on the parties’ stipulation. Case number 22STCV18203 remained the lead case. 
D.   These petitions 
On
October 7, 2024, Petitioner Domingo Castro
(“Petitioner”) filed (1) a petition for approval of the compromise of Plaintiff
Andres Castro’s claims and (2) a petition for approval of the compromise of
Plaintiff Arturo Castro’s claims.  The
petitions were set for hearing on December 5, 2024. 
PETITIONER’S REQUESTS 
Petitioner
Domingo Castro asks the Court to approve the
compromise of the claims of minor Plaintiffs Andres Castro and Arturo Castro. 
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted complete petitions with all required information.  The Court grants both petitions.
The net settlement amounts are to be deposited in insured,
blocked accounts at Wells Fargo Bank, 6800 Van Nuys Boulevard, Van Nuys, CA
91405, Tel: (818) 787-1802, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the
Court. 
CONCLUSION
 The Court GRANTS the petition for approval of the
compromise of minor Plaintiff Andres Castro’s claims filed by Petitioner Domingo
Castro on October 7, 2024.  The net settlement amount of $825.00 for Plaintiff
Andres Castro is to be deposited in an insured, blocked account at Wells Fargo
Bank, 6800 Van Nuys Boulevard, Van Nuys, CA 91405, Tel: (818) 787-1802, subject
to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court. 
The Court GRANTS the petition for approval of the
compromise of minor Plaintiff Arturo Castro’s claims filed by Petitioner
Domingo Castro on October 7, 2024.  The
net settlement amount of $825.00 for Plaintiff Arturo Castro is to be deposited
in an insured, blocked account at Wells Fargo Bank, 6800 Van Nuys Boulevard,
Van Nuys, CA 91405, Tel: (818) 787-1802, subject to withdrawal only on
authorization of the Court. 
The Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net
Settlements on February 5, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring
Street Courthouse. 
The Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case
(Settlement) on February 5, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring
Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner is ordered to file the proof of service of
this ruling with the Court within five days.
