And here is the English-text summary:

This Tentative Ruling is issued in a quiet title action, where the plaintiff, Heidi A. Christiansen, is seeking to enforce a settlement agreement against the defendant, Carmiel Cohen, et al. The settlement agreement was entered on January 19, 2017, and provides for child support payments and a cash payment of $600,000 to the plaintiff. The defendant has breached three terms of the agreement, including failure to make the cash payment, failure to make child support payments, and failure to make the additional payment of $862,000 for the benefit of the parties' children. The court finds that the defendant is in breach of the agreement and will hear from the parties regarding the issues noted herein.
