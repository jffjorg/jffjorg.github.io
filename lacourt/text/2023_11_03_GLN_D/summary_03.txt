And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The tentative ruling grants the motion to compel further responses to requests for admissions filed by defendant and cross-complainant Mix & Match Apparel, Inc. against plaintiff Korea Trade Insurance Corporation. The court orders plaintiff Korea Trade Insurance Corporation to serve further complete verified responses to requests for admissions, set no. one, requests nos. 1 through 8, inclusive, which fully comply with CCP section 2033.220, based on information or knowledge of responding party plaintiff Korea Trade Insurance Corporation, not its assignor, as well as responding party's reasonable inquiry and information readily known or readily obtainable by plaintiff.

The court also awards monetary sanctions of $2,068.91 to defendant and cross-complainant Mix & Match Apparel, Inc. payable within 30 days. The sanctions are awarded against plaintiff Korea Trade Insurance Corporation for serving evasive responses to requests for admissions and for opposing the motion to compel further responses.

The court finds that the total and reasonable amount of attorney's fees and costs incurred for the work performed in connection with the pending motion is $2,000.00 (5.0 hours @ $400/hour) plus $61.65 in filing fees and $7.26 in efiling fees.
