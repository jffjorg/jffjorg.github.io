Salazar et al.,TENTATIVE RULING:Plaintiff Midway Rent a Car, Inc. dba Midway Car Rental’s Motion to Deem Requests for Admission Admitted and Request for Sanctions is GRANTED. DEFENDANT CARLOS A. SALAZAR IS ORDERED TO PAY SANCTIONS OF $250.00 TO PLAINTIFF’S COUNSEL WITHIN 20 DAYS’ SERVICE OF THIS ORDERANALYSIS:Plaintiff Midway Rent a Car, Inc. dba Midway Car Rental (“Plaintiff”) filed this action against Defendant Carlos A. Salazar (“Defendant”) on October 19, 2023. Defendant answered in propria persona on December 1, 2023. On December 7, 2023, Plaintiff served Requests for Admissions on Defendant. (Motion, Moss Decl., Exh. 1.) To date, despite a meet and confer effort, Defendant has not served verified responses to the Requests for Admission. (Id. at ¶¶6-7 and Exh. 2.) Plaintiff filed the instant Motion to Deem Requests for Admission Admitted (“the Motion”) on July 11, 2024.The Motion initially came for hearing on September 10, 2024, at which time Defendant appeared through an attorney, Evan Selik (“Selik”). (Minute Order, 09/10/24.) The Court, on its own motion, continued the hearing to September 24, 2024. (Ibid.) On September 24, 2024, the matter was again continued to allow the parties to engage in settlement discussions. (Minute Order, 09/24/24, p. 2.) No substitution of attorney, nor additional papers,  regarding the Motion, have been filed to date.The Motion initially came for hearing on September 10, 2024, at which time Defendant appeared through an attorney, Evan Selik (“Selik”). (Minute Order, 09/10/24.) The Court, on its own motion, continued the hearing to September 24, 2024. (Ibid.) On September 24, 2024, the matter was again continued to allow the parties to engage in settlement discussions. (Minute Order, 09/24/24, p. 2.) No substitution of attorney, nor additional papers regarding the Motion, have been filed to date. DiscussionThere is no requirement for a prior meet and confer effort before a motion to deem requests for admission can be filed. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.) Further, the motion can be brought at any time after the responding party fails to provide the responses. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.) Due to Defendant’s failure to timely serve verified responses and resultant waiver of objections, Plaintiff is entitled to an order deeming the Requests for Admission admitted. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.)The Court is required to award sanctions under the moving statute. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280, subd. (c).) The sanctions sought are properly noticed and are granted against Defendant in the amount of $250.00 to include the $60.00 filing fee. (Motion, Moss Decl., ¶8.)ConclusionPlaintiff Midway Rent a Car, Inc. dba Midway Car Rental’s Motion to Deem Requests for Admission Admitted and Request for Sanctions is GRANTED. DEFENDANT CARLOS A. SALAZAR IS ORDERED TO PAY SANCTIONS OF $250.00 TO PLAINTIFF’S COUNSEL WITHIN 20 DAYS’ SERVICE OF THIS ORDER.Moving party to give notice.
