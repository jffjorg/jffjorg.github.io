 Case Number:  23STLC04036    Hearing Date:   December 9, 2024    Dept:  26 TENTATIVE RULING:Expedited Petition to Approve Minor’s Compromise of Disputed Claim on behalf of Plaintiff Bohdan Bahlaievskyi is DENIED.ANALYSIS:On June 26, 2023, Plaintiff Bohdan Bahlaievskyi (“Plaintiff”) filed the instant action against Defendant Janet Petrosian (“Defendant”) for motor vehicle negligence. When Defendant failed to file a responsive pleading, Plaintiff obtained their default on October 11, 2023. On April 30, 2024, the Court denied Defendant’s Motion to Vacate Default. (Minute Order, 04/30/24.)On May 28, 2024, non-party Petitioner Natalya Kocheshevia (“Petitioner”) filed an Expedited Petition to Approve Compromise of Pending Action on behalf of Claimant Bohdan Bahlaievskyi / Plaintiff. On August 19, 2024, the Court issued an order regarding the Expedited Petition that documented numerous defects. (Minute Order, 08/19/24.) Petitioner was ordered to file papers correcting the defects by October 24, 2024 and was warned that failure to do so might result in the Expedited Petition being denied. (Ibid.) The Court set the Expedited Petition for hearing on November 21, 2024. (Ibid.)At the hearing on November 21, 2024, the Court issued a tentative ruling finding that no additional papers had been filed as ordered and denying the Expedited Petition. (Minute Order, 11/21/24.) Petitioner appeared and sought additional time to comply with the Court’s order. (Ibid.) The Court continued the hearing again to December 9, 2024. To date, it remains that no additional papers have been filed.ConclusionTherefore, the Expedited Petition to Approve Minor’s Compromise of Disputed Claim on behalf of Plaintiff Bohdan Bahlaievskyi is DENIED.Court clerk to give notice.
