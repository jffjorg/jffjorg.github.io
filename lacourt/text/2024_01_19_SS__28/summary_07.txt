And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court has reviewed the unopposed motions to compel responses to discovery requests filed by Unlimited Security Specialists, Inc. (Defendant). The Defendant served discovery requests on April 25, 2023, and Plaintiff Hermenejildo Banuelos failed to provide timely, verified responses. As a result, the court grants the motions to compel responses to the form and special interrogatories and orders Plaintiff to provide verified responses by February 20, 2024. Additionally, the court grants the motion to compel responses to the request for production of documents and orders Plaintiff to produce the requested documents, electronically stored information, and/or other things by February 20, 2024. The Defendant requested monetary sanctions of $1,060.00, which the court awards, reduced to $980.00. The court orders Plaintiff to pay Defendant $980.00 in sanctions by February 20, 2024.
