 Case Number:  23STCV04437    Hearing Date:   May 1, 2024    Dept:  57 The Court is granting Plaintiff's unopposed motion for terminating sanctions.   In the Court's view, Plaintiff has shown that Defendants have willfully disregarded prior orders of the Court in connection with discovery motions that Plaintiff filed and that the monetary sanctions that the Court previously imposed on Defendants have not deterred Defendants from disregarding those orders.
