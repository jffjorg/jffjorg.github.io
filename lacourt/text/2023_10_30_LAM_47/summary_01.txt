The tentative ruling is in the case of David J. Randall, et al. v. Karine Gasparian, et al. (Case No. 19STCV16926). The moving parties, Plaintiffs David J. Randall and Mary M. Zakrasek Randall, seek to compel the deposition of Defendant Armen Gasparian and obtain production of documents responsive to the deposition notice. The Plaintiffs also request sanctions against Defendant Gasparian in the amount of $4,485. The Court finds that the Plaintiffs have satisfied their meet and confer obligations and that Defendant Gasparian failed to appear for deposition and produce documents responsive to the deposition notice. Therefore, the Court grants the Plaintiffs’ motion to compel deposition and orders Defendant Gasparian to appear for deposition and produce documents responsive to the deposition notice within 30 days. The Court also grants the Plaintiffs’ request for sanctions in the amount of $2,692.50. The hearing date is October 30, 2023.
