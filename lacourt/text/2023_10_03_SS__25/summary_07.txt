And here is the English-text summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling:

The Judge has ruled in favor of Western Surety Company's motion to deposit funds, exoneration of surety bond, and for attorney's fees and costs. The motion was filed to resolve a dispute over a $25,000 bond issued to Blue Line Construction Pros Inc. as principal and the Contractors State License Board as obligee. The bond was the subject of a complaint filed by Western Surety Company in an interpleader action against multiple defendants. The Judge finds that Western Surety Company is a stakeholder with no interest in the bond and has satisfied all requirements for the motion. As a result, the Judge grants the motion and orders Western Surety Company to deposit the bond funds with the court in the amount of $20,760.33. The Judge also awards Western Surety Company attorney's fees and costs of $4,239.67, which will be deducted from the interpleader funds prior to their deposit with the court.
