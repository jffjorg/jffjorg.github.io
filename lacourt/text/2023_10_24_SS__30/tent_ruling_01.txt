  Case Number:  21STCV38633    Hearing Date:   October 24, 2023    Dept:  30 JACK MELKONIAN vs EDWARD PASCO, et al.Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for DefendantTENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel is GRANTED.  The Order is delayed pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1362(e).  Counsel is ordered to give notice of this ruling within five (5) days.  Counsel remains attorney of record until full compliance with the Court's order.  Counsel to give notice to Client and all parties.DISCUSSIONCounsel has filed the requisite forms pursuant to CRC Rule 3.1362. Counsel declares that she must withdraw due to a conflict of interest.The Final Status Conference is set for February 1, 2024, and the Non-Jury Trial is set for February 15, 2024. The updated MC-053 form includes the correct dates for the scheduled hearings. Because trial is over three months away, the Court determines that Plaintiff will not suffer prejudice if Counsel is permitted to withdraw.Counsel has served the forms on Plaintiff’s counsel and Defendant’s current address that has been confirmed in the past 30 days by a private investigator who confirmed the address with Defendant’s son and brother.
