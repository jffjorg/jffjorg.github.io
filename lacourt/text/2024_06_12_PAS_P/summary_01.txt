Here is the English text summary of the judge's tentative ruling:

The court has granted the plaintiffs' motion to compel further responses to requests for admissions and has imposed sanctions against defendant PG Senior Living in the amount of $1,710.00. The plaintiffs are Chinese nationals who invested in a real estate development project as a means of obtaining permanent U.S. residency. They provided a construction loan to defendants for use in building an assisted living facility in Florida, but defendants allegedly absconded with the money. The court has ordered defendant PG Senior Living to provide complete and compliant responses to the requests for admissions. The court has also awarded sanctions to the plaintiffs in the amount of $1,710.00, which includes four hours of counsel's time at a rate of $275.00 per hour, plus $60.00 in filing fees.
