 Case Number:  22STCV02520    Hearing Date:   December 13, 2023    Dept:  30 ABIGAIL ZWAANS vs ARNOLD MONTEAGUDO PRESTONMotion to Continue the Trial DateTENTATIVEDefendant's motion to continue the trial date is denied.  The Court, instead, advances the hearing on the motion for summary judgment to February 1, 2024, at 1:30 pm, in Dept. 30.  Moving party to give notice. DiscussionEach party has a right to have a timely filed motion for summary judgment heard before trial. A summary judgment motion must be filed at least 75 days before the hearing date, and 30 days before trial. The Court advances the summary judgment hearing date accordingly.
