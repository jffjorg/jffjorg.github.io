 Case Number:  20STCV21891    Hearing Date:   December 13, 2023    Dept:  30 PHILLIP RICHARDSON, AN INDIVIDUAL vs JULISSIA BROWN, AN INDIVIDUAL, et al.Motion to Be Relieved as CounselTENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff, Phillip Richardson, is GRANTED, effective upon filing proof of service of the signed order on the client.  Counsel for Plaintiff has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). The moving papers comply with CRC Rule 3.1362. Moreover, Plaintiff will not be prejudiced by the granting of the motion as trial is not until April 2024 and Plaintiff will have sufficient time to seek new representation if he so chooses.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
