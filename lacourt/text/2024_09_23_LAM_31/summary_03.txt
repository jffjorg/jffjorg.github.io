The tentative ruling grants the unopposed motions of Leslie Ann Boyce to be relieved as counsel for the defendants and cross-complainants, Azealia Banks and Lasagna Girl, LLC. The motions were filed on September 3, 2024, and were advanced to a hearing on September 23, 2024 due to an ex parte application. The court concludes that the motions are granted and effective upon the filing of the proof of service of this signed order upon the defendants/cross-complainants.
