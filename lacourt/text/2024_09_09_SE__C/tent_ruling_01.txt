  Case Number:  23NWCV01391    Hearing Date:   September 9, 2024    Dept:  C Sutton
v. LIF Consulting, et al., Case No. 23NWCV01391
 
This
is about a motor vehicle collision. Cross-Defendant Wilbert Ernesto Flores
Vizcarra moves ex parte to advance the motion for determination of good faith
settlement. It is currently set for May 21, 2025. Trial is set for May 8, 2025.

 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based
on the above, the Court grants the motion. It ADVANCES the motion from
Wednesday, May 21, 2025 to today and CONTINUES it to November 13, 2024 at 10:30
A.M. in Dept. SE-C. 
 
Moving
party to give notice.
