The judge grants the Petition for Permanent Injunction filed by Pomona Islander, LP dba Pomona Islander Mobile Home Park against Michael Whitacre and Eria Guaydacan. The Petitioner seeks to enjoin Respondents from continuing violations of the mobile home park rules, including failure to maintain property, unauthorized guests, and threats to park employees and residents. The judge finds that the Petitioner has provided clear and convincing evidence of Respondents' violations and that Respondents have failed to respond to the Petition. The judge orders the Petitioner to file a proposed judgment within 10 days of the order.
