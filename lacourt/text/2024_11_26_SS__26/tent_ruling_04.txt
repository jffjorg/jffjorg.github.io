 Case Number:  24STLC00518    Hearing Date:   November 26, 2024    Dept:  26 Loan Asset Issuer LLC Series M 2 v. Islam, et al. Case. No. 24STLC00518TENTATIVE RULING:Plaintiff Loan Asset Issuer LLC Series M 2’s Motion to Deem Requests for Admission Admitted is GRANTED.ANALYSIS:On August 30, 2024, Plaintiff Loan Asset Issuer LLC Series M 2 (“Plaintiff”) served Requests for Admissions on Defendant Asif Islam (“Defendant”). (Motion, D’Anna Decl., Exh. A.) To date, Defendant has not served verified responses to the Requests for Admission. (Id. at ¶3.) Plaintiff filed the instant Motion to Deem Requests for Admission Admitted (“the Motion”) on October 17, 2024. No opposition has been filed to date.Plaintiff moves for an order deeming the requests for admission served on Defendant admitted pursuant to Code of Civil Procedure section 2033.280, subdivision (b). There is no requirement for a prior meet and confer effort before a motion to deem requests for admission can be filed.(Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.) Further, the motion can be brought at any time after the responding party fails to provide the responses. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.) Due to Defendant’s failure to timely serve verified responses and resultant waiver of objections, Plaintiff is entitled to an order deeming the Requests for Admission admitted. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.)ConclusionPlaintiff Loan Asset Issuer LLC Series M 2’s Motion to Deem Requests for Admission Admitted is GRANTED.Moving party to give notice.
