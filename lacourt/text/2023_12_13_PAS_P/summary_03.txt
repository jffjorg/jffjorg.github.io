The Judge has tentatively ruled that the demurrer filed by the Defendants is sustained with leave to amend. The Plaintiff, Espoir & Amour LLC, has alleged that the Defendants, Misc Group LLC and Ye Wu, breached a contract to manage the Plaintiff's Amazon storefront and committed fraud and unfair business practices. The Judge has found that the complaint fails to state a cause of action for breach of contract, breach of the implied covenant of good faith and fair dealing, unfair business practices, and fraud. The Judge has given the Plaintiff 20 days' leave to file and serve a First Amended Complaint.
