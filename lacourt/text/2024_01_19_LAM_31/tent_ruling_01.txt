MOTION
TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO DISCOVERY 



 
MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Elizabeth Heard
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant Kia
America, Inc.
 
 
On December 11, 2023, Plaintiff, Elizabeth Heard, filed this
motion to compel Defendant, Kia America, Inc., to provide further responses to
Plaintiff’s Request for Production of Documents, Set One, Nos. 16-21 and 49-51.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of this motion.  Accordingly, the Court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motion to March 15, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  
 
The Court has a standing Song-Beverly Discovery Order.  Prior to the IDC, counsel are to review the Court’s
Standing Discovery Order.  Thereafter,
counsel shall meet and confer to resolve any issues prior to scheduling the IDC.  If the matters are not resolved, counsel are
to schedule the IDC.   
 
Clerk of the Court to give notice.
