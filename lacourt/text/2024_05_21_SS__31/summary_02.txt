This tentative ruling addresses two motions: one to compel responses to interrogatories and request for production of documents, and another to deem requests for admissions admitted. The defendant, Los Angeles Community College District, propounded discovery requests on January 30, 2023, but the plaintiff, Graham Kaye, failed to respond. The defendant's motions to compel are granted, and the plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses to the discovery requests. The defendant's motion to deem requests for admissions admitted is also granted. The defendant is awarded sanctions in the amount of $800, which includes attorney fees and court costs. The plaintiff and/or plaintiff's attorney are ordered to pay the sanctions to the defendant.
