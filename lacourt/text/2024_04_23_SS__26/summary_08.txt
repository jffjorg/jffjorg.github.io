This tentative ruling grants the Plaintiff, iFab, Inc.'s motion to enforce the settlement agreement against the Defendant, The WCCD, Inc. The settlement agreement was signed by both parties and their attorneys. The agreement required the Defendant to pay the Plaintiff $24,000.00 in three monthly payments, but the Defendant defaulted on the payments. As a result, the Plaintiff is entitled to enter judgment against the Defendant in the amount of $16,000.00 principal. The Plaintiff is required to file a request to dismiss the Defendant Business Alliance Insurance Co. and a proposed judgment within 20 days of this order.
