Defendants,
Beecan Health CT, LLC; Beecan Health, LLC; and Serrano Post Acute, LLC dba

RULING


Defendants’, Beecan Health CT, LLC; Beecan
Health, LLC; and Serrano Post Acute, LLC dba Hollywood Premier Healthcare

On October
6, 2023, Defendants filed a notice of withdrawal of the instant motion stating,
“Defendants Beecan Health CT, LLC, Beecan Health, LLC, and Serrano Post Acute,
LLC dba Hollywood Premier Healthcare Center hereby withdraw, without prejudice,
their Motion to Set Aside Default and Default Judgment that was previously
noticed to be heard on October 27, 2023, at 8:30 a.m., in Department E of the
above-entitled Court, located at 600 East Broadway, Glendale, CA 91206.” (Def.
Notice of Withdrawal.)
