  Case Number:  19NWCV00820    Hearing Date:   May 16, 2024    Dept:  C Blaine Holding & Development, LLC v. Vivera Pharmaceuticals,
Inc. 19NWCV00820
 
Plaintiff/Judgment
Creditor BLAINE HOLDING & DEVELOPMENT, LLC's Ex Parte Application for an
Order Advancing the Hearing Date for the Motion for Attorney's Fees Pursuant to
CCP 128.5 is DENIED without prejudice for failure to comply with
CRC Rule 3.1203(a), which states that "[a] party seeking an ex parte order
must notify all parties no later than 10:00 a.m. the court day before the ex
parte appearance, absent a showing of exceptional circumstances that justify a
shorter time for notice." Here, the email attached as Exhibit 7 to the
instant Ex Parte Application indicates that Attorney Yost sent notice of this
ex parte hearing on Wednesday, May 15, 2024 at 12:01 p.m.-- which is after
10:00 a.m. the day before the ex parte hearing. Moving Party to give notice.
