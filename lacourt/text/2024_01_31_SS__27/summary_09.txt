This Tentative Ruling is issued by Judge Lee S. Arian regarding Plaintiff David Brian Rios' Motion for Leave to File First Amended Complaint. The motion is granted, and Plaintiff may file an amended complaint within 20 days. The ruling is based on the fact that the amendment is in the furtherance of justice, and Plaintiff has shown good cause to amend the operative complaint. The Plaintiff is seeking to add the identity of Doe Defendant Xlenno, LLC to the complaint, which was uncovered during the discovery process. The Court finds that the parties have sufficient time to complete discovery, and Plaintiff has provided adequate notice for an ex parte application.
