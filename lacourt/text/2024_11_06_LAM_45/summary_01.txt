And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has considered the motion for attorneys' fees filed by Cha Hollywood Medical Center, L.P. (Petitioner) and has tentatively ruled that Petitioner is the prevailing party. The Court finds that Petitioner achieved its objectives of releasing the mechanic's lien on the property and is entitled to its attorneys' fees. However, the Court is concerned about the reasonableness of the requested attorneys' fees and has ordered counsel for Petitioner to file and serve a supplemental declaration or declarations setting forth the reasonableness of the tasks performed and time spent, and to provide the Court with billing records for the work done in this case.

The Court has continued the hearing on the motion to January 15, 2025, and has ordered that the supplemental declaration(s) be filed and served 9 court days before the hearing. Any responsive declaration or declarations shall be filed and served 5 court days before the hearing. No reply declaration or declaration(s) will be allowed.
