Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge grants the unopposed motion to deem the requests for admission (RFAs) admitted as to the defendant, Allen Joseph Charbonnet, Sr. The defendant failed to serve timely responses to the RFAs, which were propounded on or about August 9, 2023. As a result, the judge awards sanctions against the defendant and his counsel, jointly and severally, in the amount of $1,856.25, to be paid within 30 days of the issuance of the order. The judge also notes that unverified responses are equivalent to no responses at all, and that sanctions are mandatory under California Code of Civil Procedure section 2033.280(c).
