 Case Number:  22STCV25040    Hearing Date:   November 16, 2023    Dept:  30 NJDEH KARABEDIAN vs LILYBETH GOMEZ-GARCIAMotion to Compel Defendant's Responses to Request for Production of DocumentsTENTATIVEPlaintiff’s Motion to Compel Responses to Request for Production is DENIED as MOOT. Plaintiff’s request for sanctions is DENIED.Discussion:  On September 28, 2022, Plaintiff served upon Defendant Request for Production and Inspection, Set One. (Mirzaie Decl., ¶ 3.) To date, no responses to the discovery were provided.Defendant’s counsel argues in reply that the firm has been attempting to contact Defendant since the commencement of this action and on October 31, 2023, Defendant served responses without objection to the request for discovery. Defense counsel argues Defendant was not aware a lawsuit was filed against her.As Defendant has responded to the discovery at issue, the motions are moot.As to sanctions, defense counsel has provided substantial justification for the failure to serve timely responses. Moreover, the motion was not unsuccessfully made. As such, the request for sanctions is denied.
