English-text summary:

The tentative ruling grants the defendant's motion to compel the production of documents in response to requests for production, set one. The plaintiff failed to respond to the requests and the defendant's counsel spent 3 hours preparing the motion. The court awards sanctions in the amount of $1,425.00 to the defendant. The plaintiff is ordered to provide responsive documents within 15 days and pay the sanctions within 30 days. The court also declines to find the hourly rate of $285 reasonable for the defendant's counsel's preparation of the motion and awards only 3 hours of preparation time.
