And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted the unopposed motion for entry of a stipulated consent judgment between the plaintiff, Tamar Kaloustian, and defendant, Keto Buns, Ltd. The consent judgment is based on a stipulation signed by both parties, which requires Keto to be permanently enjoined from manufacturing or selling a product in California that exposes individuals to more than 4.1 micrograms per day of cadmium, unless it meets certain warning requirements. Keto also agrees to pay a total settlement amount of $90,000. The court has found the stipulation to be valid and enforceable under California Code of Civil Procedure section 664.6. The court will sign the proposed judgment and consent judgment, and will retain jurisdiction of the matter to implement or modify the consent judgment.
