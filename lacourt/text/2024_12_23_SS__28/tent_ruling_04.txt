 Case Number:  23STCV05877    Hearing Date:   December 23, 2024    Dept:  28 Having  considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows.  BACKGROUND  On  March 16, 2023, Plaintiff a Minor, by and through Or Amsalam, guardian ad  litem, filed this action against Defendants Haim Mazar and Does 1-50 for strict  liability (Civ. Code, § 3342), negligence, and premises liability.  On  July 25, 2023, Plaintiff L.A. (“Plaintiff”), by and through her guardian ad  litem, Or Amsalam, filed a first amended complaint against Defendants Haim  Mazar (“Haim Mazar”), Katya Mazar (“Katya Mazar”), and Does 1-10 for strict  liability (Civ. Code, § 3342), negligence, and premises liability.  On  August 8, 2023, the Court granted Or Amsalam’s application to serve as  Plaintiff’s guardian ad litem.  On  September 12, 2023, Haim Mazar filed a general denial. On November 21, 2023,  Katya Mazar filed a general denial.  On  April 12, 2024, Haim Mazar and Katya Mazar filed a cross-complaint against  Cross-Defendants Orly Amsalam, Or Amsalam, and Roes 1-20 for equitable  indemnity and apportionment of fault. On May 14, 2024, Orly Amsalam and Or  Amsalam filed an answer to the cross-complaint. On August 6, 2024, the Court  dismissed the cross-complaint without prejudice at the request of Haim Mazar  and Katya Mazar.  On August  12, 2024, the Court granted Plaintiff’s motion for trial preference.  On  November 5, 2024, Petitioner Or Amsalam (“Petitioner”) filed a petition to  approve the compromise of minor Plaintiff Lia Amsalam’s claims.  No  trial date is currently scheduled.  PETITIONER’S REQUEST  Petitioner  asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of minor Plaintiff  Lia Amsalam (“Plaintiff”).  DISCUSSION            The petition states the following:  Gross settlement:                                            $375,000.00 Total medical expenses:                                 $1,809.00 Medical expenses paid:                                  $271.24 Reductions:                                                    $1,537.38 To be paid/reimbursed from proceeds:           $271.24 Attorney’s fees:                                             $131,250.00 Costs:                                                             $26,916.26 Net balance:                                                   $216,562.50    Petitioner has submitted a complete petition with all  necessary information.  The Court grants the petition. CONCLUSION  The Court GRANTS the petition to approve the  compromise of minor Plaintiff Lia Amsalam’s claims filed by Petitioner Or  Amsalam on November 5, 2024.  The Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net  Settlement on March 11, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring  Street Courthouse.  The Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case  (Settlement) on March 11, 2025, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring  Street Courthouse.  Petitioner is ordered to give notice of this ruling.  Petitioner is ordered to file the proof of service of  this ruling with the Court within five days.
