The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a demurrer to the third amended complaint (TAC) filed by the plaintiff (Clayton, et al.) against the defendant (Malibu West Swimming Club, et al.). The defendant had demurred to the TAC, arguing that it failed to state a cause of action for breach of contract. The judge has concluded that the TAC does not state a duty or breach under certain sections of the CC&Rs (Conditions, Covenants and Restrictions), except for Section 7.2, which requires the Board to consider the environmental effects of removing dead trees. The judge finds that the defendant breached Section 7.2 by not considering these effects, and that the plaintiff was precluded from rebuilding its home, resulting in delays and damages. The demurrer is therefore overruled, and the defendant is ordered to file an answer within 20 days.
