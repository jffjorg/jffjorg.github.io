 Case Number:  21STCV24492    Hearing Date:   January 30, 2024    Dept:  29 This action arises from a motor vehicle accident which occurred on July 6, 2019. On July 1, 2021, Plaintiff Lloyd Smith (“Plaintiff”) filed a complaint Defendants Angelica Bianca Diaz (“Diaz”), Luis Rodriguez (“Rodriguez”), and Does 1 to 50, alleging a single cause of action for negligence. On February 1, 2023, Diaz and Rodriguez filed answers to the complaint.   On February 1, 2023, Rodriguez filed a cross-complaint against Diaz and Roes 1 to 10 alleging causes of action for: (1) total indemnity; and (2) declaratory relief and apportionment of fault.   On February 6, 2023, Diaz filed a cross-complaint against Rodriguez alleging causes of action for: (1) indemnification; (2) apportionment of fault; and (3) declaratory relief.   On March 29, 2023, Rodriguez filed these motions to compel responses to Form Interrogatories (Set One), Special Interrogatories (Set One), and Requests for Production (Set One) from Plaintiff.  No opposition has been filed. Subsequent to the filing of this motion, Plaintiff and Rodriguez settled, and Plaintiff dismissed all claims against Rodriguez. The Court would like to hear from the parties regarding whether the settlement resolves the pending motions to compel.   If so, the Court will take the motions off calendar.  If not, then the Court will continue the hearing to 2/23/24, when other motions are on calendar for this matter.
