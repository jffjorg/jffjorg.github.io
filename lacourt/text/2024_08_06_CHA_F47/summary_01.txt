Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The plaintiff, GGP Northridge Fashion Center, LP, has filed a motion for attorney fees in the amount of $82,103.00 against the defendants, Alibaba Express Mediterranean Grill, Inc., Shamdan Restaurant, Inc., Ali Mirghaffari, and Yana Mirghaffari. The defendants did not oppose or respond to the motion.

The court has reviewed the case and has found that the plaintiff is the prevailing party in this action, as judgment was entered in its favor on February 6, 2024. The lease and guaranty on which this action was based provide for the recovery of attorney's fees by the prevailing party.

Using the lodestar method of calculation, the court has determined that the plaintiff reasonably incurred $82,103.00 in attorney fees in obtaining the judgment against the defendants.

Therefore, the court grants the motion and awards the plaintiff $82,103.00 in attorney's fees against the defendants.
