The tentative ruling is a summary of the judge's decision on a demurrer and motion to strike filed by General Motors LLC (GM) in a case against Francisco Reyes. The case involves allegations of fraud and concealment by GM related to the sale of a vehicle with a defective transmission.

The judge finds that the allegations in the complaint are sufficient to state a cause of action for fraud and concealment, and therefore overrules the demurrer. The judge also denies GM's motion to strike the prayer for punitive damages, finding that the allegations are sufficiently detailed to support the claim.

The judge notes that the allegations are exceptionally detailed and that the court is required to accept them as true for purposes of the demurrer. The judge also finds that the issuance of publicly available technical service bulletins (TSBs) does not constitute affirmative concealment by GM.

The judge orders that the demurrer be overruled and the motion to strike be denied, and that the parties be notified of the decision.
