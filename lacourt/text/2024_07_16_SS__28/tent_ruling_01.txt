  Case Number:  21STCV41887    Hearing Date:   July 16, 2024    Dept:  28 Having  considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows.  BACKGROUND  On  November 15, 2021, Plaintiffs Noe Moreno, Amalia Moreno, Yulisa Rojo (“Rojo”),  a minor, by and through her guardian ad litem Natalia Moreno, and Monique De  Haro (“De Haro”), a minor, by and through her guardian ad litem Natalia Moreno,  filed this action against Defendants Lei Zhuo and Does 1-50 for motor vehicle  tort and general negligence.  On  November 16, 2021, the Court appointed Natalia Moreno to serve as guardian ad  litem for De Haro. On November 19, 2021, the Court appointed Natalia Moreno to  serve as guardian ad litem for Rojo.  On  April 28, 2023, Plaintiff “Noe Romero” filed a notice of settlement.  On  May 1, 2023, Plaintiffs’ counsel informed  the Court that the parties had settled.  On  June 10, 2024, Plaintiff filed a notice of errata stating that Rojo’s correct  name is Yulissa Rojo.  On  June 17, 2024, Petitioner Natalia Moreno (“Petitioner”) filed petitions to  approve the compromise of the actions of De Haro and Rojo.  The petitions were set for hearing on July  16, 2024.  No  trial date is currently scheduled.  PETITIONER’S REQUESTS  Petitioner  asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending actions of minor  Plaintiffs De Haro and Rojo.  DISCUSSION  A.   Monique De Haro  Petitioner  has provided a completed petition with all required information.  The Court grants the petition.  The net settlement amount of $399.92 will be  deposited in an insured account at Chase Bank, 12193 Central Avenue, Chino, CA  91710, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court.  B.   Yulissa Rojo  Petitioner  has provided a completed petition with all required information.  The Court grants the petition.  The net settlement amount of $408.42 will be  deposited in an insured account at Chase Bank, 12193 Central Avenue, Chino, CA  91710, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court.  CONCLUSION  The Court GRANTS the petition to approve the  compromise of minor Plaintiff Monique De Haro’s action filed by Petitioner Natalia  Moreno on June 17, 2024. The Court orders that the net settlement amount of  $399.92 be deposited in an insured, blocked account at Chase Bank, 12193 Central Avenue,  Chino, CA 91710, for minor Plaintiff Monique De Haro's benefit, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court. The Court GRANTS the petition to approve the  compromise of minor Plaintiff Yulissa Rojo’s action filed by Petitioner Natalia  Moreno on June 17, 2024. The Court orders that the net settlement amount of $408.42  be deposited in an insured, blocked account at Chase Bank, 12193 Central Avenue, Chino,  CA 91710, for minor Plaintiff Yulissa Rojo's benefit, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlements on September 17, 2024, at  8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on September 17, 2024, at  8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  Petitioner  is ordered to give notice of this ruling.  Petitioner  is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within  five days.
