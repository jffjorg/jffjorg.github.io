The Court has issued a tentative ruling granting Uber's unopposed motion to compel arbitration in this case. The dispute arises from a motor vehicle accident involving Veronica Ortez, who registered for an Uber account on November 7, 2016, and accepted Uber's Terms of Use, which included an arbitration agreement. Uber argues that Ortez agreed to arbitrate any claims against Uber by clicking a checkbox on multiple occasions to agree to the updated Terms of Use. The Court finds that Uber has established the existence of an enforceable arbitration agreement and grants the motion to compel arbitration. The case is stayed pending binding arbitration as to the entire action.
