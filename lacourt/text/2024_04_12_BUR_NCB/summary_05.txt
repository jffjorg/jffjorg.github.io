And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The defendant American Contractors Indemnity Company (ACIC) moves to be discharged from liability regarding a $15,000 Contractor's License Bond issued to On Point Builders, Inc. (On Point) as principal and ACIC as surety. ACIC seeks to be discharged from liability and seeks recovery of $3,000 in attorney's fees and costs. The Court grants ACIC's motion and orders that ACIC be discharged from liability regarding the bond. The Court also orders that ACIC be awarded $3,000 in attorney's fees and costs, which shall be taken from the $15,000 bond amount. The remaining bond amount of $12,000 shall be deposited with the Clerk of the Court. The Court also dismisses ACIC from the present action without prejudice.
