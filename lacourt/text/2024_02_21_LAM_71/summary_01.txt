And here is the English text summary:

The tentative ruling grants Marina Pacifica, LLC's petition to take the oral depositions of Craig Edgecumbe and Robert Rocchi for the purpose of preserving their testimony for use in the event an action is subsequently filed. The petition was filed on December 18, 2023, and the opposition was filed on February 6, 2024. The petition seeks to perpetuate the testimony of the two witnesses, who are the only two known living witnesses to the negotiation and execution of the original Leases in 1972 and 1976. The petition also seeks to depose the two witnesses to elicit facts related to the negotiation and meaning of lease terms, including the intent of Paragraph 25 of the Leases. The court finds that Marina Pacifica has made the requisite showing to grant the relief requested, and the petition is granted.
