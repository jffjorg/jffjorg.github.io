The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion filed by Plaintiffs Ana Martinez and Walter Melendez to deem the truth of the matters asserted in the Request for Admission (Set Two). As a result, the Judge deemed the truth of the matters specified in the Requests for Admission as true. The Judge also imposed monetary sanctions on the Defendant in the amount of $436.65, calculated based on 1.5 hours of attorney work, multiplied by counsel's billing rate of $250 per hour, plus a $61.65 filing fee.
