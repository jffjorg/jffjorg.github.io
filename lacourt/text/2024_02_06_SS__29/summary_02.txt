The Court has issued a tentative ruling granting the motion to compel responses to special interrogatories (set two) and supplemental interrogatories filed by Defendant Tower Urology, Inc. The motion was filed on January 5, 2024, after Defendant served Plaintiff with the interrogatories on September 20, 2023, and September 29, 2023, but Plaintiff failed to respond. The Court finds that Plaintiff has waived all objections by failing to respond in a timely manner and grants the motion to compel responses. Additionally, the Court orders Plaintiff to pay monetary sanctions of $85.00 to Defendant for the costs of bringing the motion. The Court also orders Plaintiff to provide written, verified, code-complaint responses to the special interrogatories (set two) and supplemental interrogatories within 21 days of the notice.
