ALW’s motion to compel further is MOOT. The Court declines to award
sanctions at this time.
 
On 9/12/2022, Plaintiff Thomas D. Perez and Delia Lopez De Perez
initiated tis suit against 31 Defendants, alleging: (1) negligence; (2) strict
liability—failure to warn; (3) strict liability—design defect; (4) fraudulent
concealment; (5) breach of implied warranties; and (6) loss of consortium. 
 
            Now, Defendant A.L. Wilson Chemical
Company (ALW) moves to compel Plaintiff Delia Lopez de Perez’s (Plaintiff)
production of documents. 
 
Discussion
 
            ALW served the subject discovery on
Plaintiff on 6/30/2023. 
 
            In opposition, Plaintiff indicates
that on 9/22/2023, she provided responsive discovery. This production included
7,250 pages via Dropbox link (Bates stamped ALW-00001-00085, STN 00001-06646, and
PER 00242-00760) consisting of documents from Stoneview Corp., Carriage Regal
Cleaners, A.L. Wilson Chemical Co. and medical records from medical providers.
 
            Accordingly, ALW’s motion to compel
further is moot. The Court declines to award sanctions at this time. 
 
 
It is so
ordered.
