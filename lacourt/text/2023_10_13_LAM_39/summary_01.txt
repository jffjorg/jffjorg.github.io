And here is the English-text summary:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling on a demurrer to answer filed by Plaintiff Kenneth Oleesky against Defendants Lithia Motors, Inc. and Van Nuys-L, Inc. The Plaintiff has filed a lawsuit asserting seven causes of action under the Fair Employment and Housing Act (FEHA) and one cause of action for failure to pay timely wages upon discharge. The Defendants have raised 39 affirmative defenses to the second amended complaint, which the Plaintiff has demurred to. The Court has reviewed each affirmative defense and ruled on each one, sustaining or overruling the demurrer as appropriate. The Court has sustained the demurrer to the 31st and 39th affirmative defenses without leave to amend, and has overruled the demurrer in all other respects. This order is without prejudice to Plaintiff seeking to exclude inadmissible evidence by way of a motion in limine.
