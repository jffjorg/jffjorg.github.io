The tentative ruling grants the motion of Farbood Majd, counsel for Tadi Productions, Inc. dba 25Band and Askar & Ahadi, to be relieved as counsel. The court finds that there has been an irreparable breakdown in communication between counsel and client, and that the client has ample time to procure new counsel before the trial set for December 2, 2024. However, the court notes that corporations cannot represent themselves, and that Tadi Productions, Inc. must find new counsel. The motion is granted pending the completion of the required form MC-053 and service on the client and all parties.
