Hearing on Plaintiffs’ Motion for Sanctions – Continue.The court
is currently engaged in a 5-week jury trial which will be followed by another 5-week
jury trial.  There is insufficient time
for the court to complete its review of the various motions in the
interim.  Also, the court would prefer to

discretion to control its calendar and will move all dates due to court
congestion to allow it sufficient time to prepare for the seven MSJs, the Motion
for Sanctions and the Motion for Leave to File a Cross-complaint.The court’s
proposed schedule is as follows:Trial is
continued to June 25, 2024 at 9:30 a.m.FSC is
continued to June 18, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. 
The FSC order of May 5, 2025 will apply to the new FSC date, including due
date for Motions in Limine, trial documents, etc.Motion for Sanctions
continued to May 14, 2024 at 8:30 a.m.All MSJs
continued to April 16, 2024 at 8:30 a.m.Motion to
file Cross-complaint continued to March 13, 2024 at 8:30 a.m.No cutoff
dates tied to the current trial date of March 19th are continued.  The
discovery cutoff, expert designation, etc. will be as of the statutory period
prior to the current trial date of March 19, 2024.Opposition
and replies to the MSJs are due according to the currently scheduled date of Feb. 15, 2024.
