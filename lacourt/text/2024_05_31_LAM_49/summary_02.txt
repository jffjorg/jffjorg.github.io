The tentative ruling is a summary of a court hearing held on May 31, 2024. The case involves a dispute between Plaintiff Emir Jr Angulo and Defendant Ford of Montebello over the purchase of a 2023 Ford F-150. Plaintiff alleges that Defendant defrauded him by selling the vehicle above the advertised price and by selling an optional service contract without his consent. Defendant demurred to the first, second, third, fourth, and fifth causes of action, arguing that they are duplicative. The court sustained the demurrer and granted leave to amend. Defendant also moved to strike portions of the complaint, including references to punitive damages. The court denied the motion, ruling that Plaintiff has alleged facts sufficient to support a claim for punitive damages under the California Consumer Legal Remedies Act.
