This tentative ruling addresses Defendant Sokaina Quabadi's motions to compel responses to form interrogatories and requests for production for documents. The motions are GRANTED, and Plaintiff Frank E. Grodski is ordered to provide responses without objection to Defendant's form interrogatories, set one, and request for production, set one, within 20 days. Defendant's request for sanctions is DENIED, as sanctions are only mandatory when the motion is unsuccessfully opposed. The court finds that Defendant properly served discovery requests and Plaintiff failed to provide responses, entitling Defendant to a court order directing Plaintiff to provide verified responses.
