 Case Number:  23PSCV01580    Hearing Date:   October 26, 2023    Dept:  K Plaintiff Allstate Insurance Company’s Application for Default Judgment is DENIED without prejudice.
Background   
On May 25, 2023, Plaintiff Allstate Insurance Company (“Plaintiff”)
filed a complaint, asserting a cause of action against Jennica Ramos
(“Defendant”) and Does 1-10 for:
1.                 
Motor Vehicle 
On September 15, 2023, Defendant’s default was entered.
A Case Management Conference is set for October 26, 2023.
Discussion
Plaintiff’s Application for Default Judgment is denied without
prejudice. The following defects are noted:
1.                 
Plaintiff has failed to provide the court with a brief
summary of the case, as per California Rules
of Court rule 3.1800, subdivision (a)(1). 
2.                 
It appears to the court that Plaintiff is seeking
subrogation; however, Plaintiff’s representative Jane Snyder
(“Snyder”) fails to attach a copy of the policy between Plaintiff and its
insured, let alone provide the court with any information regarding same or
with any information regarding the damages allegedly incurred to the insured
premises. Snyder also fails to authenticate the payment ledger with report date
of January 16, 2023.
