 Case Number:  23STCV11642    Hearing Date:   September 6, 2024    Dept:  57 DEMURRER TO THE THIRD AMENDED COMPLAINT The rulings on the Defendants' demurrer to the Plaintiff's Third Amended Complaint ("TAC") are as follows:As to Defendant PopID, the demurrer is sustained without leave to amend with respect to the First through Fourth Causes of Action in the TAC and overruled as to the Fifth through Twelfth Causes of Action.  PopID is directed to file an answer to the remaining claims against it in the TAC by September 20, 2024.As to every one of the individual Defendants, the demurrer is sustained without to leave to amend with respect to all of the causes of action asserted against them in the TAC.MOTION TO STRIKE PopID moved to strike the prayer for an award of punitive damages against PopID in connection with the First and Second Causes of Action in the TAC.  Because the Court is sustaining PopID's demurrer to the First and Second Causes of Action without leave to amend, PopID's motion to strike is denied as moot.
