The Court has considered the moving and opposition papers and has denied Plaintiff Shahab Khangholi's motions to compel further responses to his requests for production of documents, set two, and his special interrogatories, set one. The Court found that Plaintiff had not met his burden in showing good cause to compel further responses to his second set of requests for production of documents.
