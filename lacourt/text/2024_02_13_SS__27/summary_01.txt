This is a tentative ruling by Judge Hon. Lee S. Arian in the case of Sean Casey vs. CVS Pharmacy, Inc. et al. (Case No. 20STCV11847). The motion is to continue the trial and all discovery dates. The defendants, CVS Pharmacy, Inc., Garfield Beach CVS, LLC, and GRB Service Systems, Inc., argue that good cause exists for the continuance due to their inability to secure deposition of the plaintiff's personnel and their desire to forestall incurring expert costs and fees until after mediation. The parties have stipulated to the continuance. The judge has granted the motion, continuing the trial to April 22, 2024, and all pre-trial dates to follow the new trial date. The judge has also disfavored any further efforts to continue the trial.
