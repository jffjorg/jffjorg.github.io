The Honorable Judge Lee Arian issued a tentative ruling on February 13, 2024, in the case of Zhoie Perez v. MMR Center, Inc. et al. (Case No. 22STCV18378). The case involves a motion by counsel Jeff Katofsky to be relieved as counsel for the defendants, MMR Center, Inc., Green Angels, and Tony Santillan.

The motion was filed on January 8, 2024, and the opposition was filed by the plaintiff, Zhoie Perez, on January 30, 2024. Counsel Katofsky's reply was filed on February 5, 2024.

The Judge granted the motion, citing the legal standards set forth in Code of Civil Procedure § 284(2) and California Rules of Court Rule 3.1362. The Judge noted that counsel Katofsky and his clients have irreconcilable differences and that MMR Center, Inc. failed to pay its legal bills.

The Judge also considered the case law cited by counsel Katofsky, including Ferruzzo v. Superior Ct. (1980) 104 Cal.App.3d 501, 504 and Gamet v. Blanchard (2001) 91 Cal.App.4th 1276, 1284, which support the granting of the motion.
