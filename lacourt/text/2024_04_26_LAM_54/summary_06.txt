And here is a summary in English text:

The tentative ruling denies the petitioners' request for a preliminary injunction to stop the removal of 240 mature trees at the Harvard-Westlake River Park Project. The court finds that the petitioners have not shown a likelihood of success on the merits of their claims, including their claims that the City failed to comply with the California Environmental Quality Act (CEQA) in approving the project. The court also finds that the petitioners have not shown that the removal of the trees will cause irreparable harm. The court grants the respondent's request for judicial notice and denies the petitioners' motion for preliminary injunction.
