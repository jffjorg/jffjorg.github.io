Background FactsPlaintiff, Interpool, Inc. dba Trac Intermodal filed this action against Defendants, HHM Intl., Inc., Mohan A. Sharma, Shriak M. Sharma, and Vishu M. Sharma for breach of contract, common counts, conversion, and possession arising out of Defendants’ use of Plaintiff’s chassis without invoice payment.Plaintiff filed the complaint on 10/01/21. Plaintiff dismissed the individual defendants. HHM failed to respond to the complaint, and on 4/04/22, the Court entered judgment by default against HHM in the amount of just over $150,000.2. Motion to Amend Judgmenta. Relief SoughtPlaintiff seeks an order amending the judgment to add HHM International LLC as a judgment debtor. Plaintiff contends the LLC entity is the alter ego of the Inc. entity, such that amendment of the judgment is appropriate.b. Prior HearingThe Court was previously scheduled to hear this motion on 9/10/24. The Court continued the hearing because Plaintiff had not served the proposed judgment debtor with the moving papers, and also because Plaintiff sought injunctive relief but did not provide authority for the request.c. AnalysisPlaintiff filed amended papers on 9/16/24. Plaintiff no longer seeks injunctive relief, and has filed proof of service of the moving papers on the proposed judgment debtor. The motion remains unopposed. The motion is granted. Plaintiff established HHM International LLC is the sister company of HHM Intl Inc., the judgment debtor in this action. Plaintiff established both entities share the same address, phone number, principals, and business activities, and have such similar names to be intentionally confusing.Pursuant to McClellan v. Northridge Park Townhome Owners Ass’n, Inc. (2001) 89 Cal.App.4th 746, in light of the above, an order amending the judgment to include HHM International LLC as a judgment debtor is appropriate. In the absence of opposition, the motion is granted.Plaintiff must submit a proposed amended judgment for processing.Plaintiff is ordered to give notice.Parties who intend to submit on this tentative must send an email to the court at gdcdepts27@lacourt.org indicating intention to submit on the tentative as directed by the instructions provided on the court website at www.lacourt.org.
