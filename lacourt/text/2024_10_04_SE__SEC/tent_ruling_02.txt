Joslevin Realty Corporation of LA, et al., Case No. 23NWCV01813This is a complaint in interpleader. Defendant moves ex parte to do the following: (1) vacate the hearing dates on the MSC and trial; (2) stay this action pending determination of the Clapkins’ motion to compel arbitration; and (3) advance the hearing date on the Clapkins’ motion to compel arbitration.A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)Defendant asserts that the MSC is scheduled for October 10, 2024 and trial for January 9, 2025. The motion to compel arbitration is scheduled for July 10, 2025.Based on the above, the Court GRANTS the motion in part. The Court ADVANCES the motion to compel arbitration to today and CONTINUES it to January 4, 2025 at 10:30 A.M. in Dept. C.Moving party to give notice.
