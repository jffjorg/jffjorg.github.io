Here is the English-text summary:

This tentative ruling grants the petition for approval of the transfer of structured settlement payment rights from Pacific Life Insurance Company to J.G. Wentworth Originations, LLC. The payee, Kyle McLaughlin, seeks to transfer three payments of $40,000, $50,000, and $60,000 in exchange for a lump sum purchase amount of $77,000. The court finds that the transfer is in the best interest of the payee, taking into account the welfare and support of the payee's dependents, and that the payee has been advised in writing by the transferee to seek independent professional advice regarding the transfer. The transferee has complied with the notification requirements and provided the payee with a disclosure form that complies with Section 10136 of the Insurance Code. The court also finds that the payee understands the terms of the transfer agreement, including the terms set forth in the disclosure statement required by Section 10136.
