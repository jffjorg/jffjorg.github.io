Case No.
22STCV25084

Plaintiff's ex parte application for an order compelling
further responses to Plaintiff’s 1st set of Requests for Production


The ex parte application is denied for the
following reasons:

1) The Court previously denied the same requested
relief on November 8, 2024;
2) The relief sought must be requested by a noticed
motion.  (St. Paul Fire & Marine Ins. Co. v.
Superior Court (1984) 156 Cal.App.3d 82, 86; CCP § 2031.310.);
3) The application relies on statutes governing
interrogatories to compel further responses to requests for production.  (CCP §§ 2030.210 and 230.300); and
4) The application exceeds 15 pages. (California Rules of
Court, Rule 3.1113(a).
