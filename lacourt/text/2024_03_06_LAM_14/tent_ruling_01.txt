  Case Number:  21STCV17497    Hearing Date:   March 6, 2024    Dept:  14 Case Background
 
Lemon law involving Plaintiffs’ 2020
Audi Q8 Quattro.
 
On May 10, 2021, Plaintiffs filed their
Complaint for (1) Breach of Express Warranty; (2) Breach of Implied Warranty;
and (3) Violation of Civil Code § 1793.2(b) against Defendants Volkswagen
Group of America, Inc. (“Volkswagen”) and DOES 1-10. 
 
On June 9, 2021, Defendant Volkswagen
filed its Answer.
 
On November 8, 2022, Plaintiffs
filed a Notice of Settlement.
 
Instant Motion
 
            Plaintiffs
now move this court for an order awarding them $151,082.60 in attorneys fees
and costs.
 
Decision
 
The motion is GRANTED. 
 
The motions is unopposed. The lack
of an opposition operates as a
concession that the motion has merit. See California Rules of Court Rule
3.1113(a); see also Rule 3.1320(f); Herzberg v. County of Plumas (2005)
133 Cal.App.4th 1, 20.
