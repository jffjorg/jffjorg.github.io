 On May 2, 2023, a Second Amended Complaint (“SAC”) was filed relating to the failure to pay attorneys’ fees after losing an unlawful detainer action and to repair property pursuant to commercial leases.   On June 23, 2023, a Cross-Complaint was filed relating to the failure to pay rent.Defendant PR2020, LLC now seeks to compel Plaintiff Heang Krouch to comply with his responses to Defendant’s requests for production. Defendant also seeks to compel initial responses to its form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests of production from Plaintiff Robert Gooch.   As responses have not been provided by Gooch, the three Motions to Compel directed at this Plaintiff are granted.  Responses, without objections, are to be served within thirty days.  The Court awards reduced sanctions in the amount of $1,000. The Motion directed at Plaintiff Krouch is denied as moot because the Court already entered a terminating sanction against Krouch by striking his SAC and striking his answer to the Cross-Complaint.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
