English-text summary:

The Court issues a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses and production of documents filed by the Plaintiff, Maria Rivero, in a wrongful termination case against the County of Los Angeles and others. The motion seeks to compel the production of documents related to complaints by other employees and to limit the scope of the discovery requests. The Court grants the motion in part, limiting the timeframe for the discovery requests to January 1, 2020 to the present, and ordering the County to provide a privilege log for any documents it contends are covered by the attorney/client, work product, settlement or mediation privileges. The Court also holds that the "me too" evidence of complaints by other employees is relevant and admissible in discrimination and harassment cases. Finally, the Court denies the request for sanctions, finding that the County did not act frivolously in opposing the motion.
