 Case Number:  23STCV11361    Hearing Date:   May 31, 2024    Dept:  17 Superior court of california  County of Los Angeles   DEPARTMENT  17   DEFAULT JUDGMENT        OMNINET KIPLING, L.P.                   vs.   NKRUMAH DAVIDSON          Case    No.:  23STCV11361        Hearing Date:  May 31, 2024                      After  review, the Court has identified the following deficiencies with Plaintiff’s  default judgment packet:   -          Plaintiff’s default judgment must be  accompanied by a summary of the case.   -          The declaration submitted by Vileta  Vitkova was not signed.   -          CCP section 580, subdivision (a) provides  that the “[t]he relief granted to the plaintiff, if  there is no answer, cannot exceed that demanded in the complaint.” Here,  Plaintiff sought a maximum of $25,000 in damages, plus attorney fees and costs.  As such, while Plaintiffs estimates the alleged breaches to total $26,534.73,  the Court cannot award a sum greater than that alleged in the Complaint.
