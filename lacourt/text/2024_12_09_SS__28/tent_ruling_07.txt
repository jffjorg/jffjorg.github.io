 Case Number:  22STCV27029    Hearing Date:   December 9, 2024    Dept:  28 Having  considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows.  BACKGROUND  On  August 18, 2022, Plaintiffs Stephanie Roxeanne Castanon, Valerie Roxeanne  Montes, a minor by and through her guardian ad litem Jaime Montes De La Rosa,  Alina Valentina Montes, a minor by and through her guardian ad litem Jaime  Montes De La Rosa, Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon, a minor by and through her  guardian ad litem Jaime Montes De La Rosa, and Jaime Balthazar Montes Castanon,  a minor by and through his guardian ad litem Jaime Montes De La Rosa filed this  action against Defendants Juan Carlos Garibay, Teresa Ornelas, and Does 1-50  for negligence, negligent entrustment, and negligence per se—Vehicle Code  sections 21453 and 22350.  On  August 22, 2022, the Court appointed Jaime Montes De La Rosa to serve as  guardian ad litem for Plaintiffs Valerie Roxeanne Montes, Alina Valentina  Montes, Jaime Balthazar Montes Castanon, and Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon.  On  November 15, 2022, Defendants Juan Carlos Garibay and Teresa Ornelas filed a  general denial.  On  January 17, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement.  On  August 15, 2024, the Court (1) granted the petition to approve the compromise  of minor Plaintiff Alina Valentina Montes’s action filed by Petitioner Jaime  Montes De La Rosa on May 30, 2024 and (2) granted the petition to approve the  compromise of minor Plaintiff Valerie Roxeanne Montes’s action filed by  Petitioner Jaime Montes De La Rosa on May 30, 2024.  On  October 10, 2024, Petitioner Jaime Montes de la Rosa (“Petitioner”) submitted  (1) a petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor  Plaintiff Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon’s claims  and (2) a petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor Plaintiff Jaime Balthazar Montes’s claims.  PETITIONER’S REQUESTS  Petitioner  asks the Court for expedited approval of the compromises of the claims of minor  Plaintiffs Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon and Jaime Balthazar Montes.  DISCUSSION  A.   Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon          The petition provides the following information:  Gross settlement amount:                    $5,000.00 Total medical expenses:                       $1,340.55 Medical expenses paid:                        $55.55 Reductions:                                          $927.23 To be paid/reimbursed:                        $413.32 Liens:                                                   $1,340.55  (before reductions)           Attorney’s  fees:                                   $1,250.00           Costs:                                                   $633.40           Net settlement  amount:                        $2,703.28             According  to the petition, the gross settlement amount for Plaintiff Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon is $5,000.00. Out of  this amount, $413.32 will be used to pay or reimburse medical expenses,  including $41.67 to the Department of Health Care Services and $371.65 to Pain  Management Care. In addition, $1,250.00 will be used to pay attorney’s fees and  $633.40 will be used to pay for non-medical expenses. (Section 14b of the  petition lists a larger figure for non-medical expenses, but an attachment to  the petition clarifies that the larger figure applies to the combined expenses  for all the plaintiffs.) As a result, the net settlement amount for Plaintiff Jamie  Miranda Montes Castanon is $2,703.28.  The Court approves the petition as described above and  orders that the net settlement amount of $2,703.28 be delivered to Plaintiff Jamie  Miranda Montes Castanon’s parents, Stephanie Roxeanne Castanon and Jaime Montes  De La Rosa, at 11222 Thienes Avenue, South El Monte, CA 91733, on the terms and  under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without  bond.  B.   Jaime Balthazar Montes   The petition provides the  following information:  Gross  settlement amount:                    $5,000.00 Total  medical expenses:                       $1,260.27 Medical  expenses paid:                        $71.46 Reductions:                                          $851.86 To  be paid/reimbursed:                        $408.41 Liens:                                                   $1,260.27  (before reductions)           Attorney’s fees:                                   $1,250.00           Costs:                                                   $633.40           Net settlement amount:                        $2,708.19            According to the petition, the gross settlement amount for  Plaintiff Jaime Balthazar Montes is $5,000.00. Out  of this amount, $408.41 will be used to pay or reimburse medical expenses,  including $71.46 to the Department of Health Care Services and $336.95 to Pain  Management Care. In addition, $1,250.00 will be used to pay attorney’s fees and  $633.40 will be used to pay for non-medical expenses. (Section 14b of the  petition lists a larger figure for non-medical expenses, but an attachment to  the petition clarifies that the larger figure applies to the combined expenses  for all the plaintiffs.) As a result, the net settlement amount for Plaintiff Jaime Balthazar Montes is $2,708.19.  The  Court approves the petition as described above and orders that the net  settlement amount of $2,708.19 be delivered to Plaintiff Jaime Balthazar Montes’s  parents, Stephanie Roxeanne Castanon and Jaime Montes De La Rosa, at 11222  Thienes Avenue, South El Monte, CA 91733, on the terms and under the conditions  specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without bond.  CONCLUSION  The  Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor Plaintiff  Jamie Miranda Montes Castanon’s claims filed by Petitioner Jaime Montes de la  Rosa on October 10, 2024. The net  settlement amount of $2,703.28 is to be delivered to Plaintiff Jamie Miranda  Montes Castanon’s parents, Stephanie Roxeanne Castanon and Jaime Montes De La  Rosa, at 11222 Thienes Avenue, South El Monte, CA 91733, on the terms and under  the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without bond.  The  Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor  Plaintiff Jaime Balthazar Montes’s claims filed by Petitioner Jaime Montes de  la Rosa on October 10, 2024. The net settlement amount of $2,708.19 is to be  delivered to Plaintiff Jaime Balthazar Montes’s parents, Stephanie Roxeanne  Castanon and Jaime Montes De La Rosa, at 11222 Thienes Avenue, South El Monte,  CA 91733, on the terms and under the conditions specified in Probate Code  sections 3401-3402, without bond.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlements on February 07, 2025, at  8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on February 07, 2025, at  8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  Petitioner  is ordered to give notice of this ruling.  Petitioner  is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within  five days.
