 Case Number:  23STCV09552    Hearing Date:   February 21, 2024    Dept:  74 Juan Manuel
Correa v. SKT Investment Group, LLC, et al.
Defendant SKT Investment Group,
LLC’s Demurrer to Complaint
BACKGROUND 
            This
action arises out of the enforcement of a promissory note secured by deed of
trust.
            On
April 28, 2023, Plaintiff Juan Manuel Correa (Plaintiff) filed a complaint
against Capital Foreclosure Services, Inc. and SKT Investment Group, LLC
(Defendant).
            On
June 20, 2023, Defendant filed this demurrer.
            On
September 1, 2023, Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint (FAC).
 DISCUSSION  
            This
demurrer is moot because Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint after
defendant filed its demurrer. (Code Civ. Proc. § 472,
subd. (a); Sylmar Air Conditioning v. Pueblo Contracting Services, Inc. (2004)
122 Cal.App.4th 1049, 1054.) 
CONCLUSION 
The
court finds the demurrer moot.
Defendant
shall give notice.
