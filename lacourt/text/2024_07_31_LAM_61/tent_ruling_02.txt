 Case Number:  23STCV16408    Hearing Date:   July 31, 2024    Dept:  61 GLAUCO PEREZ OTERO vs INTERNATIONAL CERTIFIED SAFETY INC., A CALIFORNIA CORPORATIONTENTATIVEAttorney Tizoc Perez-Casillas, counsel for Plaintiff Glauco Perez Otero. Perez-Casillas presents a declaration stating he brings the present motion because of an “[i]rreparable breakdown of the attorney-client relationship.” The motion complies with statutory and code requirements, except that the order lists the upcoming case management conference set for August 22, 2024, and does not list the Order to Show Cause re: Failure to File Proof of Service also set for that date. No trial date has yet been set in this matter.Tizoc Perez-Casillas’ Motion to be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff Glauco Perez Otero is GRANTED. Counsel is ordered to include in its order, among the upcoming hearings, the OSC re: failure to file proof of service scheduled for August 22, 2024. This order granting the motion to be relieved shall be made effective upon moving counsel filing with the court proof of service of copies of the signed amended orders on the clients, as set forth in Cal. Rules of Court Rule 3.1362(e).
