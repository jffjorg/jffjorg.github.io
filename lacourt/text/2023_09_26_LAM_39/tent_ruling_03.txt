 Case Number:  22STCV26319    Hearing Date:   September 26, 2023    Dept:  39 Veronica Mendez v.
Guilford Glazer Properties, Inc., et al.
Case No.
22STCV26319
Ex Parte
Application to Continue Trial Date and all Related Dates
 
            Plaintiff
Veronica Mendez (“Plaintiff”) filed this employment action against Guilford
Glazer Properties, Inc. and the Diane and Guilford Glazer Foundation
(collectively, “Defendants”).  Trial is
set for February 20, 2024.  Now,
Defendants seek to continue the trial date, and all related dates, to June 17,
2024.  The ex parte application is not
opposed.
 
            The ex
parte application is granted in part and denied in part.  The Court grants the ex parte application to
the extent it seeks a trial continuance and sets trial on its first available
date.  The Court advances and vacates all
dates and sets the following dates:
 
            Hearing on
Motion for Summary Judgment:             April
22, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.     
 
Final Status Conference:                                            September
20, 2024, at 9:30 a.m. 
 
Trial:                                                                           October
1, 2024, at 9:30 a.m. 
 
The expert designations deadlines and discovery cutoff shall
be based on the new trial date (October 1, 2024).  The fact discovery cutoff shall be based on
the former trial date (February 20, 2024).
 
            Defendants’
counsel shall provide notice and file proof of such with the Court.
