The Judge has issued a tentative ruling regarding a motion filed by Plaintiff Latoya Redd. The motion seeks to strike all pleadings and motions filed by Defendants Marc S. Feldman, Boshra G. Khoder, Brett G. Hampton, William R. Johnson, and Thomas Kovich after their defaults were entered on May 15 and July 1, 2024. The Defendants have opposed the motion, arguing that their defaults should be set aside. The Judge has ruled that the defaults cannot be set aside based on a request in an opposition and that the Defendants have no right to participate in the action after defaulting. As a result, all of their filings after their defaults are void, except for their motions to set aside default. The Judge has granted the motion and ordered that the filings be struck.
