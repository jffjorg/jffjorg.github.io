 Case Number:  23STCV14511    Hearing Date:   April 19, 2024    Dept:  26 REQUEST FOR ENTRY OF DEFAULT JUDGMENT IS GRANTEDThe complaint alleges 2 causes of action for (1)
Breach of Contract and (2) Common Counts. In sum, the complaint alleges on
January 13, 2022, Plaintiff and Salina Valley Auto Sales Inc., entered into
Master Dealer Agreement for Plaintiff to extend credit to Salinas for various
loans; Defendants signed a Personal and Continuing Guarantee for the Master
Dealer Agreement. Defendants failed to make the loan payments leaving an unpaid
balance of $31,975.04.Plaintiff seeks entry of default judgment against Defendants who have submitted sufficient evidence to demonstrate its entitlement to judgment Plaintiff’s request for default judgment is granted
against Defendants for $39,245.30 consisting of  $31,975.04 in demand, $5,116.01
in interest (09/13/2022-04/19/2024), $1,349.25 in attorney’s fees, and  $805.00
in costs. In the Form CIV-100. it appears Plaintiff forgot to add the cent
amounts, i.e., $31,975.04  instead of $31,975. If Plaintiff is prepared to waive the four (4) cents, the Court will enter the Order as  presented.
