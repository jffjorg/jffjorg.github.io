On March 14, 2024, Judge Kerry Bensinger issued a tentative ruling in the case of Grandma Investments, LTD, et al. v. RT Features U.S., LLC, case number 24STCP00257. The case involves a motion to seal certain portions of a petition to confirm a contractual arbitration award. The petitioners, Grandma Investments, LTD, et al., filed a partially redacted petition and seek to file under seal certain exhibits in support of their petition.

The judge found that there exists an overriding interest in the confidentiality of the documents relating to the contractual arbitration award, and that the proposed sealing is narrowly tailored to the documents subject to a protective order entered in the matter. The judge also found that no less restrictive means exists to achieve the overriding interest.

The motion to seal is continued to April 26, 2024, at 9:00 AM, pending proof of service of the motion to seal. The petitioners are required to file proof of service within five court days of this order.
