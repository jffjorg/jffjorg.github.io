And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by the defendants (Habibi Café, Jan Fathi, and Saad Fathi) to the complaint filed by the City of Los Angeles. The City is seeking $66,022.59 in costs incurred in connection with nuisance abatement proceedings and its appeal. The defendants argue that the complaint lacks specific facts about the costs incurred and that some of the costs may predate the statute of limitations. The judge has overruled the demurrer, finding that the City has adequately alleged the requisite elements for a common count for money had and received. The judge has ordered the defendants to file and serve an answer to the complaint by August 28, 2024, and has continued the case management conference to October 30, 2024.
