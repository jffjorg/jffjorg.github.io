 Case Number:  22STCV23274    Hearing Date:   December 12, 2023    Dept:  78 Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles Department 78  SCOTT MURRAY,Plaintiff,           vs. SUBARU OF AMERICA, INC., et al., Defendants.  Case No.:  22STCV23274 Hearing Date:  December 12, 2023  [TENTATIVE] RULING RE:  PLAINTIFFS’ MOTION FOR ATTORNEY’S FEES The Court requires further briefing on the issue of the reasonableness of Plaintiff’s counsel’s billing. Here, Defendants propose to deduct 186.3 hours from Plaintiff’s counsels’ proposed billing. Defendants grouped together parts of Plaintiff’s counsel’s billing and proposed the number of hours to be taxed. However, Defendants do not point to any specific billing entry or explain why a particular entry is unreasonable. The Court cannot determine from the chart Defendants provided which entries are unreasonable and the specific reasons why. As the party challenging the hours billed, it is Defendants’ burden to point out the specific items challenged and make sufficient arguments that the items are unreasonable.The Court requires further briefing on this issue. Defendants must provide a detailed chart of each billing entry they challenge, the date of the billing, the description of the billing, the name of the attorney who billed the time, the number of hours originally billed, the amount of time Defendants propose to be deducted, and the reasons why the time billed is unreasonable.For example:Date Biller Description  Hrs billed Proposed reduction ReasonThe parties are to appear (remotely or in person) to set a further briefing schedule.Moving party to provide notice.DATED: December 12, 2023 ________________________Hon.
