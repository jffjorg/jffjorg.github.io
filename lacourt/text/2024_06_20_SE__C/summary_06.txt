This tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel compliance with a court order. The plaintiff, Hae Won Lee and Bora Violet Park, seek an order to compel the defendant, American Honda Motor Co., Inc., to comply with a court order. The defendant failed to comply with the court's order and refused to participate in the discovery process. The court finds that terminating sanctions are warranted, as the defendant's refusal to comply with discovery obligations is a misuse of the discovery process. The court grants the motion for terminating sanctions and strikes the defendant's answer.
