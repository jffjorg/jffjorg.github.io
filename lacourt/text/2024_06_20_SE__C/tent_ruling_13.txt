 Case Number:  24NWCV00151    Hearing Date:   June 20, 2024    Dept:  C The
Ted Herz, LLC vs. Sanchez, Case No. 24NWCV00151
 
This
is a breach of contract action for damages arising out of a commercial lease.
Plaintiff moves ex parte to advance the hearing on the motion to compel
discovery, which is currently scheduled for January 14, 2025. 
 
Plaintiff
argues that because trial is scheduled for February 28, 2025, it will need more
time to conduct discovery. 
 
The
Court grants the motion. It advances the motion from January 14, 2025, to June
20, 2024 and continues it to Tuesday, August 6 at 10:30 A.M. in Dept. SE-C.
