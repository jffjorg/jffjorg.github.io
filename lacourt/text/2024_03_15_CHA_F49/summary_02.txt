English-text summary:

The Judge has denied the Motion to Compel Joinder of Indispensable Parties filed by Defendant Vision Media Management & Fulfillment, LLC. The motion sought to join Horizon Personnel Services and Employers Outsourcing, LLC as indispensable parties in the lawsuit alleging wage and hour violations. The Judge found that Vision Media failed to demonstrate that these parties are necessary and indispensable under Code of Civil Procedure section 389. The Judge concluded that complete relief can be accorded among the parties already joined in the action, and that Vision Media is responsible for complying with Labor Code sections 2698 et seq. as a client-employer.
