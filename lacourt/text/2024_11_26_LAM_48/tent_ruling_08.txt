On March 29, 2024, Plaintiff
Giancarlo Carmona filed this action against Defendant 8490 Hospitality LLC.
On
May 31, 2024, Defendant filed a motion to compel arbitration and stay proceedings.
On
November 13, 2024, Plaintiff filed a notice of non-opposition.  Plaintiff does not oppose the Court entering an
order consistent with Defendant’s proposed order to compel Plaintiff’s individual
PAGA claims to arbitration and to stay Plaintiff’s representative PAGA claims pending
the outcome of arbitration.
Accordingly,
the motion to compel arbitration is GRANTED. 
The Court will sign Defendant’s proposed order.  This action is STAYED pending the completion of
arbitration.
A
Status Conference Re: Arbitration is scheduled for November 25, 2025 at 8:30 a.m.
in Department 48 at Stanley Mosk Courthouse.
