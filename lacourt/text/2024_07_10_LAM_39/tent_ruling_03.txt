MOTION
              Defendants
Solari Enterprises, Inc. and Gianna Solari Richards (“Defendants”) move to compel
arbitration of the claims Plaintiff Rocio Murillo (“Plaintiff”) asserts against
Defendants.  Plaintiff filed a notice of
non-opposition to the motion.  As such,
the Court grants the motion as stipulated.
 This case is stayed pending arbitration.  The court will schedule a date for an order
to show cause regarding dismissal upon completion of arbitration.
Defendants are ordered to provide notice of
this order and to file proof of service of same.
