The Judge's tentative ruling grants the applications of Bryan P. Thompson and Michael Kind to be admitted Pro Hac Vice as counsel for Petitioner Jennifer Haywood in the case against Respondent Snap, Inc. The applications were unopposed and satisfied the requirements of California Rules of Court Rule 9.40. The Judge noted that both attorneys are not employed or residing within California, have the necessary addresses and admission information for other courts, and are in good standing in those jurisdictions. The Judge also noted that both attorneys have not appeared in California in the previous two years. The Judge ordered that the applications be granted and that moving party give notice, unless waived. The hearing is set for November 29, 2023.
