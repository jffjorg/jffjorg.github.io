 Case Number:  22SMCV01107    Hearing Date:   November 26, 2024    Dept:  N TENTATIVE RULINGBrittany Shaw and Lawyers for Justice, PC’s Motion to Withdraw as Counsel for Plaintiff Margarita Fabiola Martinez Becerril is GRANTED.Counsel has asserted a valid reason for withdrawal, i.e., the client has terminated counsel as her attorneys, but there is no signed substitution of attorney form. (Rules Prof. Conduct, rule 1.16(b)(4).) Counsel has provided the required forms under rule 3.1362 of the California Rules of Court and served the client and opposing parties with the same. There being good cause to grant counsel’s request to withdraw, Brittany Shaw and Lawyers for Justice, PC’s Motion to Withdraw as Counsel for Plaintiff Margarita Fabiola Martinez Becerril is GRANTED. This ruling granting counsel’s motion to withdraw shall become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon Plaintiff Margarita Fabiola Martinez Becerril.
