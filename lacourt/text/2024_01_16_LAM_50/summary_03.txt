The tentative ruling by Judge Teresa A. Beaudet addresses a motion by defendants Michael Simms and Carmen Santillan to compel third-party Green Accounting and Consulting's (GAC) compliance with a deposition subpoena for production of business records and to request monetary sanctions in the amount of $4,093.00. The court orders the parties to participate in an Informal Discovery Conference (IDC) to resolve their discovery disputes and sets forth the procedures for the IDC. The court also orders the parties to meet and confer in person or via telephone to attempt to resolve their issues before the IDC. If the parties resolve their discovery disputes, the defendants are ordered to take both the IDC and the motion off calendar as soon as possible.
