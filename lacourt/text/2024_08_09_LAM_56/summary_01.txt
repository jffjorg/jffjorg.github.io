Judge Fujie has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel responses to requests for production of documents and special interrogatories. The plaintiff, Elizabeth Shuai Wong, filed the motion against the defendant, Flipfit, fka Humans, Inc., alleging that the defendant failed to serve timely responses to discovery requests. The defendant argued that the delayed responses were caused by unexpected medical issues experienced by the previous attorney handling the case and that there were multiple changes in counsel representing the defendant. The Court found that, under the circumstances, there was substantial justification for the delayed responses and that monetary sanctions were not warranted. The motions are denied as moot, and the requests for monetary sanctions are also denied. The parties must file a notice of this ruling.
