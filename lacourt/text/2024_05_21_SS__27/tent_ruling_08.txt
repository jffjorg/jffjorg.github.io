Ruling: MOTIONS TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO
PLAINITFF’S SPECIAL INTERROGATORIES, SET ONE, AND FORM INTERROGATORIES, SET ONE ARE DENIED
 
On
April 2, 2024, Plaintiff moved the Court to compel Defendant Craig Smith to
provide further responses to Plaintiff’s Special Interrogatories, Set One, and
Form Interrogatories, Set One. On April 11, 2024, the parties attended an IDC
for the discovery responses at issue. On May 6, 2024, a minute order for the
IDC was issued, stating that the "issues have been resolved" during
the IDC. Since the discovery issues have been resolved, the present motions are
moot and therefore denied.
