Circa 1200, LLC’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On March 18, 2024,
Circa 1200, LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed its Complaint against Defendant Jinglin
Yue aka Jing Lin Yue aka Michael Yue (“Defendant”) for breach of contract and common counts.
On July 11, 2024,
the clerk entered default against Defendant. 
On October 24, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be
heard on November 14, 2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendant and award Plaintiff $76,855.53 consisting of
$58,676.53 in damages, $16,979.00 in prejudgment interest at the annual rate of
10%, $697.00 in attorneys’ fees, and $503.00 in costs. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff
submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments.
The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants
Plaintiff Circa 1200, LLC’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application
for default judgment filed on October 24, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $76,855.53 is entered against Defendant Jinglin Yue aka Jing Lin Yue
aka Michael Yue.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
