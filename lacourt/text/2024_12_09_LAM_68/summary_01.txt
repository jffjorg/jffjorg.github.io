Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling:

The court has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by Catani, LLC to the first amended cross-complaint filed by Brand Boulevard Associates, L.P. The demurrer challenges the sufficiency of the pleading, specifically the failure to state sufficient facts in support of the causes of action for indemnity, contribution, express indemnity, and breach of contract.

The court has sustained the demurrer as to the first, second, and third causes of action, with leave to amend. The court has overruled the demurrer as to the fourth cause of action, finding that the pleading is sufficient to state a cause of action for breach of contract.

The court has given Brand Boulevard Associates, L.P. 30 days leave to amend the cross-complaint. If Brand Boulevard elects to forego filing an amended cross-complaint, Catani is ordered to answer the remaining cause of action within 10 days of the lapsed amendment deadline.
