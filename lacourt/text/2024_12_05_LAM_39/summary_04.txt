The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer and motion to strike filed by General Motors, Inc. (Defendant) against Chase Love and Steve Love (Plaintiffs). The Plaintiffs allege that General Motors, Inc. leased them a defective vehicle and concealed the battery defect. The judge has overruled the demurrer to the first cause of action (fraud by concealment) as the Plaintiffs' allegations are sufficient for pleading purposes. However, the judge has sustained the demurrer to the second cause of action (violation of Business and Professions Code section 17200) without leave to amend, requiring the Plaintiffs to allege with greater specificity the actions by General Motors, Inc. that they contend violated the statute. The judge has also granted the Plaintiffs an opportunity to amend their pleading concerning their request for punitive damages. The Plaintiffs are required to file a further amended complaint within 20 days. The judge's ruling is subject to change upon receipt of further information or argument by the parties.
