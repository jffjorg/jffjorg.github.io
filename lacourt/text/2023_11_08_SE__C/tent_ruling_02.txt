Plaintiff Martin Naranjo (Plaintiff) moves for
an order setting a hearing on  Compensatory Adjustment in connection with a
partition sale. Plaintiff’s counsel declares he has sent letters to Defendant
Daniel Sanchez (Defendant) to meet and confer regarding compensatory
adjustments (for improvements, taxes, insurance, repairs, mortgage payments,
credits, interest, and rent) but has received no response. In a partition
action, the court may order allowance, accounting, contribution, or other
compensatory adjustment among the parties, according to the principles of
equity. (CCP § 872.140.) Thus, Plaintiff’s Motion to Set Hearing on a
Compensatory Adjustment is GRANTED and a hearing is set for January 3, 2024 at
9:30 AM in Dept. SE-C. Briefing schedule is per code. 
Plaintiff to give NOTICE.
