 Case Number:  22STCV19481    Hearing Date:   January 26, 2024    Dept:  39 James
Wahl v. American Broadcasting Company, Inc., Case No. 22STCV19481
 
The
Court ruled on Plaintiff’s ex parte application in chambers:
 
The
Court grants Plaintiff’s unopposed ex parte application to continue the trial
date.  The Court advances and vacates the final status conference and
trial dates (March 1 and March 18, 2024, respectively).  The Court shall
hold a trial setting conference on February 5, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.  The
parties shall be prepared to discuss potential trial dates and whether the
expert and discovery deadlines shall be based on the new trial date.  The
Court will resolve any disputes on those issue at the trial setting
conference.  Plaintiff’s counsel shall provide notice and file proof of
such with the Court.
