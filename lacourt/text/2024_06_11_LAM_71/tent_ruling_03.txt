JPMorgan Chase
Bank, N.A.’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents
submitted in support of a default judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On October 20, 2023,
JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. (“Plaintiff”) filed this action against Defendant National
Automobile and Casualty Insurance Co. (“Defendant”) for cancellation of
instrument and declaratory relief. 
On December 27,
2023, the clerk entered default against Defendant National Automobile and
Casualty Insurance Co.  On May 2, 2024,
Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be heard on June 3, 2024.  This Court continued the hearing to June 11,
2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendant National
Automobile and Casualty Insurance Co. and award Plaintiff as follows: (1) the
deed of trust encumbering the property located at 5917 Miramonte Blvd, Los
Angeles CA 90001 (“Property”) that was recorded on June 29, 1995, as Document
No. 95 1045700 in the Official Records of the Los Angeles County Recorder’s
Office (“1995 Deed of Trust”) is cancelled, void, and of no further force or
effect; (2) the 1995 Deed of Trust is cancelled and expunged from the Official
Records of the Los Angeles Recorder’s Office; (3) Plaintiff is entitled to
record this Judgment in the Official Records of the Los Angeles County
Recorder’s Office as reconveyances of the 1995 Deed of Trust; and (4) upon
presentation of a certified copy of this Judgment and payment of any fees required
by law, the Recorder of Los Angeles County is to accept this Judgment for
recording and record it within the Official Records of the Los Angeles County
Recorder’s Office.
 
Discussion
Plaintiff
submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments.
The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants
Plaintiff’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application
for default judgment filed on April 10, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of (1) the deed of trust encumbering the
property located at 5917 Miramonte Blvd, Los Angeles CA 90001 (“Property”) that
was recorded on June 29, 1995, as Document No. 95 1045700 in the Official
Records of the Los Angeles County Recorder’s Office (“1995 Deed of Trust”) is
cancelled, void, and of no further force or effect; (2) the 1995 Deed of Trust
is cancelled and expunged from the Official Records of the Los Angeles
Recorder’s Office; (3) Plaintiff is entitled to record this Judgment in the
Official Records of the Los Angeles County Recorder’s Office as reconveyances
of the 1995 Deed of Trust; and (4) upon presentation of a certified copy of
this Judgment and payment of any fees required by law, the Recorder of Los
Angeles County is to accept this Judgment for recording and record it within
the Official Records of the Los Angeles County Recorder’s Office, is entered
against Defendant National Automobile and Casualty Insurance Co.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
