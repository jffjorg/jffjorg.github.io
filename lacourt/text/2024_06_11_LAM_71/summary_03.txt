This tentative ruling is in response to JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A.'s request for a default judgment against National Automobile and Casualty Insurance Co. The plaintiff is seeking to cancel the 1995 Deed of Trust that encumbers the property located at 5917 Miramonte Blvd, Los Angeles CA 90001. The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants the plaintiff's request in full. The Court orders the cancellation of the 1995 Deed of Trust, its expungement from the Official Records, and the recording of the judgment in the Official Records. The plaintiff is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
