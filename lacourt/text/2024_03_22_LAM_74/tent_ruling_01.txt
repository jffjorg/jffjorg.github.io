  Case Number:  19STCV09709    Hearing Date:   March 22, 2024    Dept:  74 Charlene Shee v. Janet Sherwood, et
al.
Plaintiff’s Motion for Leave to File
a Fourth Amended Complaint
The court considered the moving
papers, opposition, and reply.
BACKGROUND
            This action arises from a dispute
between neighbors. 
            On
March 20, 2019, Plaintiff Charlene Shee (Plaintiff) filed a complaint against
Defendant Janet Sherwood (Defendant) and others.
            On
April 25, 2019, Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint (FAC).
            On
October 1, 2020, the court granted Plaintiff’s motion for leave to amend the
operative complaint and Plaintiff thereafter filed a second amended complaint.
            On
July 13, 2023, the court granted Plaintiff’s motion for leave to amend the
operative complaint and Plaintiff thereafter field a third amended complaint.
            On
January 24, 2024, Plaintiff filed this motion for leave to file a fourth
amended complaint.
DISCUSSION/CONCLUSION
            This
motion is off calendar. The record indicates Plaintiff filed her motion for
leave to file a fourth amended complaint on January 24, 2024. On January 25,
2024, Plaintiff withdrew the motion. According to Plaintiff, on January 24,
2024, she filed the motion, withdrew it, and filed an amended motion. (Response
to Defendant’s Objection, ¶¶ 1, 2.) But Plaintiff’s account is inconsistent
with the record. Moreover, the motion on file with the court is not labeled as
an amended or corrected motion.
