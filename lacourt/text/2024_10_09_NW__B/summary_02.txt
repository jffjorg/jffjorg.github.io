And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court has granted Petitioner Gary Zaccaria's motions to compel Respondent Teachers Insurance and Annuity Association of America's responses to discovery requests. Respondent failed to provide any responses to the discovery requests, despite being given an extension of time to do so. As a result, the court has ordered Respondent to provide verified responses to the discovery requests, without objection, within 15 days. Additionally, the court has ordered Respondent to pay $1,580 in sanctions, representing 4 total hours of work at a rate of $350 per hour plus a $60 filing fee for each motion. The court's ruling is based on California Code of Civil Procedure (CCP) sections 2030.290(b), 2030.290(c), 2023.030(a), and 2031.300(b).
