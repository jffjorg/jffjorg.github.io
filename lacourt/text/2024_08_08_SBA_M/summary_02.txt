The plaintiff, Worku Assefa, and defendant, Krystelle Angie Espinoza, were involved in a motor vehicle accident. The defendant served discovery requests on December 27, 2023, but the plaintiff failed to respond. The defendant filed a motion to compel responses to the discovery requests, and since the plaintiff did not file opposition, the motion is granted. The plaintiff is ordered to serve responses without objections within 10 days of the date of this ruling. Additionally, the defendant's request for monetary sanctions is granted, and the plaintiff is sanctioned $667.00, which is the amount requested by the defendant. The sanctions are payable within 30 days of the date of this ruling, and the defendant is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
