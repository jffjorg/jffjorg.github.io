The court has issued a tentative ruling denying the motions of Zulu Ali, Esq. and Law Offices of Zulu Ali & Associates, LLP to be relieved as counsel for defendants Oscar Favela and CP Electric, LLC. The motions were not served with sufficient notice, and the moving party did not comply with California Rules of Court, rule 3.1362. The court finds that the moving party failed to provide the defendants with at least 16 court days' notice of the motions, and also failed to file the required declarations and proposed orders. The court orders the moving party to give notice of this ruling to the defendants and all other parties who have appeared in the action.
