 Case Number:  23STCV15148    Hearing Date:   April 24, 2024    Dept:  26 JESSE CANTU, Plaintiff, v. FINDIT, INC., a Nevada corporation d/b/a FINDIT.COM, Defendants. Case No. 23STCV15148 REQUEST FOR ENTRY OF DEFAULT JUDGMENT      Plaintiff's request for entry of default judgment against Findit, Inc. is GRANTED, in part.  Plaintiff has failed to cite any authority, statutory or otherwise, that would entitle Plaintiff to attorneys' fees.       Plaintiff is ordered to present a new form of judgment which reflects the correct amount of the judgment.
