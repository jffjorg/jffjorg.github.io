Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has considered the motions to compel plaintiff Dayvid McCrary's responses to discovery requests, and grants the motions. The plaintiff failed to serve timely responses to the discovery requests, and the Court finds no substantial justification for the delay. Therefore, the Court imposes monetary sanctions of $1,060 on the plaintiff.

The Court orders the plaintiff to serve complete, Code-compliant and verified responses to the discovery requests within 20 days. The discovery requests include requests for production of documents, specially prepared interrogatories, and form interrogatories. The plaintiff is also ordered to pay $470 in monetary sanctions for each of the four motions, representing two hours of attorney time at a rate of $205 per hour, plus a $60 filing fee.
