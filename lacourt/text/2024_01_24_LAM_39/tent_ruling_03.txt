 Case Number:  22STCV27786    Hearing Date:   January 24, 2024    Dept:  39 Joann
A. Verduzco v. Impact Reality, Inc., Case No. 22STCV27786
 
The
Court rules in chambers as follows: 
 
Defendant
filed ex parte applications to advance hearings on his motion to dismiss and
motion for sanctions to the same date/time as the Order to Show Cause re:
Dismissal (Arbitration).  The ex parte
application is denied.  Instead, the
Court advances and continues the Order to Show Cause re: Dismissal
(Arbitration) to from March 13, 2024, to April 17, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.  The Court advances and continues the hearing
on Defendant’s motion to dismiss from April 4, 2024, to April 17, 2024, at 8:30
a.m.  The Court orders counsel for all
parties to appear at the hearings, either remotely or in-person.  Defendant’s counsel shall provide notice and
file proof of such with the Court.
