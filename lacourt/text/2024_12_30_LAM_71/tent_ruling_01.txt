Plaintiff Itria Ventures LLC’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On November 22, 2023, Itria
Ventures LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed its operative Complaint against Defendants Forty
General Building, Inc., Jose Cuarenta Jimenez, and Jesus Edgar Cuarenta (collectively,
“Defendants”) for appointment of receiver, preliminary injunction, breach of
written contract, and breach of written guaranties.  On December 20, 2023, Defendants filed their
answer.  On August 21, 2024, this Court
struck Forty General Building, Inc.’s answer. 
On October 23, 2024, this Court struck Jose Cuarenta Jimenez’s and Jesus
Edgar Cuarenta’s answer.
On August 21, 2024, the clerk
entered default against Forty General Building, Inc.  On October 29, 2024, the clerk entered
default against Jose Cuarenta Jimenez and Jesus Edgar Cuarenta.  On November 14, 2024, Plaintiff filed a
Request for Court Judgment to be heard on December 30, 2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendants and award Plaintiff a judgment of $77,531.93
consisting of $67,237.50 in damages, $6,833.82 in prejudgment interest at a
total rate on the contract of 10%, $1,225.86 in costs, and $2,234.75 in
attorneys’ fees. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted a
completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments. The Court
finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants Plaintiff Itria
Ventures LLC’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application for default
judgment filed on November 14, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $77,531.93 is
entered against Defendants Forty General Building, Inc., Jose Cuarenta Jimenez,
and Jesus Edgar Cuarenta.
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
