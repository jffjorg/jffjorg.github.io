**Parties**

* Plaintiff: City of Burbank
* Defendant: State of California, Department of Transportation (Caltrans)
* Cross-Defendants: City of Burbank

**Facts**

* Relinquishment Agreement: A 1963 agreement between the State of California and the City of Burbank, relinquishing the city's rights to a portion of the highway right of way.
* Vacation Resolution: A 1998 resolution by the City of Burbank, vacating a portion of the highway right of way.
* 2035 General Plan: A plan adopted by the City of Burbank, which does not show the Subject Property as a street.

**Issues**

* Caltrans' Motion for Summary Judgment (MSJ) on Issue 1 and 2, alleging that the City has no defense against the State's first cause of action for Declaratory Relief.
* City's opposition to Caltrans' motion, arguing that the Relinquishment Agreement created a restriction on the use of the Subject Property.

**Rulings**

* The Court grants Caltrans' request for judicial notice in support of Caltrans' motion.
* The Court grants the City's request for judicial notice in support of the City's opposition to Caltrans' motion.
* The Court overrules the City's objections to the Declaration of Ngim Nancy Pe and the Declaration of Dan Murdoch.
* The Court sustains Caltrans' objections to the City's Amended Opposition to Separate Statement of Undisputed Material Facts in Support of Motion for Summary Judgment.

**Statute of Limitations**

* The Court notes that there is a four-year statute of limitations applicable to an action upon a contract and a five-year statute of limitations applicable to an action for violation of a restriction.
* The Court finds that Caltrans' claims are time-barred, as the statute of limitations has run.

**Tolling**

* The Court notes that the statute of limitations was tolled by the City's lawsuits and the parties' tolling agreement in 2019.

**Next Steps**

* The Court will continue the hearing on the motions to a later date.
* The parties are directed to file a joint status report updating the Court on the status of the motions.
