  Case Number:  21NWCV00211    Hearing Date:   June 17, 2024    Dept:  C Anaya,
et al. v. Moreno, Case No. 21NWCV00211
 
This
is a quiet title action concerning real property located at 13340 Ankerton
Street, Whittier, Califiornia 90610. Plaintiffs Javier Anaya and Bertha Alicia
Roman-Anaya move ex parte to shorten time for hearing on a motion to enforce
the settlement agreement. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Here,
Plaintiffs attach a proposed Memorandum of Points and Authorities which does
not contain a concise statement of the law and a discussion of the statutes and
cases cited in support of the motion. (Cal. Rules of Court, Rule 3.1113, subd.
(b).) 
 
Also,
the Court typically requests that a party seeking an earlier hearing date first
make a reservation on the Court reservation system, file and serve the motion,
and then apply ex parte to advance the hearing date.  
 
Moving
party to give notice.
