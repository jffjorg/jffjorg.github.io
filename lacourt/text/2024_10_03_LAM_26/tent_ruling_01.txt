  Case Number:  21STCV33023    Hearing Date:   October 3, 2024    Dept:  26 TENTATIVE RULING:Defendant's motion to compel the in-person deposition of Plaintiff is granted on the following conditions: (1) that the deposition be conducted in a conference room large enough to seat at least 12 persons and that the attendees distance themselves from Plaintiff as much as practicable; (2) that attendance at the deposition be limited to the court reporter, 2 lawyers for defendants, and Plaintiff; (3) that all attendees test negative for COVID-19 within 4 hours prior to the deposition (a representation on the record to that effect will suffice) ; and (4) that all attendees wear a KN-95, N-95, or comparable mask throughout the deposition (except when taking a drink).Absent further order of the Court or agreement of the parties, the deposition shall occur within 10 days of the date of this order.Defendant's request for monetary sanctions is denied.
