And here is the English summary:

This is a tentative ruling regarding the motions to compel defendant's responses to form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production, as well as to deem requests for admissions admitted. The court grants the motions and orders the defendant to serve verified responses without objections within 30 days after the date of this order. The court also awards sanctions in the amount of $1,711.65 against the defendant and its counsel of record for failing to respond to discovery requests. The ruling is based on the plaintiff's allegations that the defendant failed to repair or repurchase a 2020 Mercedes-Benz S560 vehicle with serious defects, despite the express written warranty.
