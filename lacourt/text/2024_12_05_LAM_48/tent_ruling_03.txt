On October 27, 2023, Plaintiff
Michael Lynn Waters filed this action against Defendant Dawn Harrision.  Plaintiff alleges that he has been wrongfully
imprisoned for 26 years, and he seeks monetary compensation from Los Angeles County
through Defendant.
On
October 24, 2024, Defendant filed a motion for judgment on the pleadings.
On
November 5, 2024, Plaintiff filed a request to dismiss this case.
Pursuant
to the request of Plaintiff, the Court orders this action DISMISSED WITHOUT PREJUDICE.
The
motion for judgment on the pleadings is taken off calendar.
Moving
party to give notice.

who intend to submit on this tentative must send an email to the Court at SMCDEPT48@lacourt.org
indicating intention to submit.  If all parties
in the case submit on the tentative ruling, no appearances before the Court are
required unless a companion hearing (for example, a Case Management Conference)
is also on calendar.
