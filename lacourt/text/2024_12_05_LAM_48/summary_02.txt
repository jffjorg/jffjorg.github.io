The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel the deposition of defendant's expert witness, Roland H. Hamilton. The defendant had objected to the deposition notice, claiming that the plaintiff unilaterally selected the date without seeking input from the defendant. The judge ruled that it is not improper to unilaterally pick a date for a deposition and that the plaintiff has the right to take Hamilton's deposition prior to trial.
