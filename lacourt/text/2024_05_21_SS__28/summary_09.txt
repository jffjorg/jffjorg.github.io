The Court has considered the applications for pro hac vice admission of Jordan T. Dollar and Timothy E. Dollar on behalf of the Plaintiffs, Joshua Nassir and Omid Nassir. The Court finds that the applications comply with the requirements of California Rules of Court, rule 9.40, with one exception. The proofs of service attached to the applications do not list Intervenor Technology Insurance Company, Inc., who was a party when the Plaintiffs filed the applications. The Court continues the hearing and orders the Plaintiffs to file proofs of service of the applications on Intervenor Technology Insurance Company before the next hearing date. The Court also continues the applications for pro hac vice admission to June 18, 2024, at 1:30 p.m. in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.
