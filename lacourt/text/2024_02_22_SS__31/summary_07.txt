Summary:

The tentative ruling addresses Defendant's motion for leave to file a cross-complaint against third party Amber Estrada. The Defendant alleges that the proposed cross-complaint arises out of the same transaction as Plaintiff's claim against Defendant for negligent supervision. The Plaintiff opposes the motion, arguing that Defendant was on notice since the date of the incident that Dominguez attacked Plaintiff and that there are multiple videos of Dominguez repeatedly punching Plaintiff. The Court finds that the proposed cross-complaint is a permissive claim and grants the motion, ordering Defendant to file a separate copy of its proposed cross-complaint within five days.
