And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The court has granted defendant Inderjit Kaur's motion to compel plaintiff Julie Muradyan's responses to outstanding discovery. The defendant propounded supplemental interrogatories on February 17, 2023, and the plaintiff failed to respond. The court notes that the plaintiff was properly served with the discovery request, but failed to respond despite a two-week extension. The court has ordered the plaintiff to serve verified responses to the defendant's supplemental interrogatories within 15 days.

The court has also awarded sanctions to the defendant in the amount of $350 in attorney fees and $61.65 in costs. Additionally, the court has ordered the plaintiff to pay sanctions to the defendant in the total amount of $411.65, jointly and severally with the plaintiff's attorney.

The court encourages the parties to meet and confer to reach an agreement before the hearing on February 22, 2024. If a party intends to submit to the tentative ruling, they must send an email to the court with the subject line "SUBMIT" followed by the case number.
