  Case Number:  22STCV21211    Hearing Date:   January 22, 2024    Dept:  1 TENTATIVE RULINGThe Court hereby GRANTS final approval and awards/approves the following: (1) $43,333.33 for attorney fees to Class Counsel, Sirmabekian Law Firm, P.C.; (2) $5,000 for attorney costs to Class Counsel; (3) an enhancement payment of $5,000 to Plaintiff Franz Chavez Hidalgo; (4) $7,500 (75% of $10,000 PAGA penalty) to the LWDA; and (5) $6,500 for settlement administration costs to Phoenix Settlement Administrators. The proposed Order will be entered on the date of the hearing. The Court orders class counsel to file a final report summarizing all distributions made pursuant to the approved settlement, supported by declaration. The Court will set a non-appearance date for submission of a final report for October 28, 2024.
