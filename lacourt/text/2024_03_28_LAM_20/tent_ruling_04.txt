This is a lemon law action.  Defendant American Honda Motor Co., Inc. seeks to compel an inspection of the vehicle of Plaintiffs Veloria James and Steven James.  Defendant contends that Plaintiffs have failed to provide an agreeable date.Plaintiffs indicate that they do not refuse the inspection, but that, in the normal course of discovery with opposing counsel, the inspection is done after Defendant’s PMK deposition, which has not in this case been offered.The Motion to Compel is granted.  There is nothing that requires that the inspection take place after a PMK deposition.  The inspection is to take place within 30 days on a date to be chosen by Defendant.  Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
