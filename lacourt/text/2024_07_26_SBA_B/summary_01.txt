This is a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories and requests for production of documents. The plaintiff, Martin Hutto, filed a complaint against the City of Torrance and Torrance Police Department alleging violations of the Public Safety Officers Procedural Bill of Rights Act (POBRA) and seeking declaratory relief and Labor Code §1102.5 relief. The defendant, City of Torrance, served discovery requests and plaintiff responded with objections. The defendant moved to compel further responses to the discovery requests, which the court granted. The court ordered plaintiff to serve further responses to the discovery requests within twenty days. The defendant is also ordered to give notice of the ruling.
