The Judge has issued a tentative ruling granting Plaintiff State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company's motion to set aside dismissal and enter judgment against Defendant Alfredo Campos Robledo. The dispute arose from a motor vehicle incident, and Plaintiff paid for damages to its insured. The parties had previously entered into a stipulation for entry of judgment, which stated that if Defendant breached the terms of the stipulation, Plaintiff could enter judgment for the full amount of $31,373.65. Defendant breached the stipulation, and Plaintiff filed a motion to set aside dismissal on June 27, 2023. The Judge finds that Defendant breached the terms of the stipulation and grants Plaintiff's motion, ordering the entry of judgment in the amount of $22,413.14.
