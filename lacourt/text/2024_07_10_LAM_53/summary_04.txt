This is a tentative ruling by Judge Robert B. Broadbelt III on a motion for attorney's fees and costs filed by Plaintiffs Norma Gomez and Juan Gomez against Defendant Kia America, Inc. The Judge finds that Plaintiffs are entitled to recover attorney's fees and costs from Defendant in the amount of $38,417.09, consisting of $36,738.50 in attorney's fees and $1,678.59 in costs. The Judge bases this finding on the parties' settlement agreement, which provided for payment of reasonable costs, expenses, and attorney's fees, and on the evidence presented by Plaintiffs, including their verified time statements and billing records. The Judge also rejects Defendant's arguments that some of the attorney's fees claimed by Plaintiffs were unreasonable or unnecessary.
