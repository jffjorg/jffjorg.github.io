 Case Number:  23NWCV02824    Hearing Date:   October 5, 2023    Dept:  C Passport Foods USA v. Passport Foods SVC
 
Plaintiff’s Ex Parte Application for a Temporary Restraining
Order (TRO) is GRANTED because Plaintiff has shown that Plaintiff would suffer
greater harm and it has a probability of prevailing on the merits. Plaintiff
provided notice to each Defendant on October 3, 2023. No opposition was filed.
Thus, Defendants are ordered to immediately transfer all funds obtained through
the Employee Retention Tax Credit for Q2, Q3, Q4 of 2020 and Q1, Q2 and Q3 of
2021 (“ERTC Funds”), to an escrow account provided by Plaintiff’s counsel. An
OSC re preliminary injunction is scheduled for November 7, 2023.
