Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court rules on the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to Requests for Production (RPDs) from the Defendant, Pomona Healthcare & Wellness Cener, LLC, et al. The Court grants the motion with respect to most of the RPDs, requiring the Defendant to provide further verified responses and sanctions for the Defendant's failure to comply with the motion.

The Court grants the motion with respect to RPD Nos. 51, 52, 57, 59, 61-64, 90, 93, 94, 101-103, 106, 107, 111, 113, 117, 153, 155, 158, 159, 165, 167, 168, 170-184, requiring the Defendant to provide further responses.

The Court grants the motion in part and denies it in part with respect to RPD Nos. 168, 170, 172, 181, requiring the Defendant to provide unredacted Resident ID numbers.

The Court grants the motion with respect to RPD Nos. 183-184, requiring the Defendant to provide audit data and medical record audits.

The Court denies the motion with respect to RPD Nos. 61, 62, 113, 97, finding that the requests are overbroad, irrelevant, or not readily accessible.

The Court orders sanctions in the amount of $2,840 for four hours of attorney time at a rate of $650 per hour plus filing fees against the Defendant for its failure to substantially justify its opposition to the motion.
