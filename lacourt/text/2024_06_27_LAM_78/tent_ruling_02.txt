 Case Number:  23STCV23671    Hearing Date:   June 27, 2024    Dept:  78 Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles Department 78   BLAIR COBB, et al.,Plaintiffs, vs. ELSIE WYNN, et al.,  Defendants.  Case No: 23STCV23671 Hearing Date: June 27, 2024    [TENTATIVE] RULING RE:  DEMURRER FILED BY DEFENDANTS ELSIE WYNN, THOMAS JAMES CAPITAL, INC., AND THOMAS BEADEL Based on the case management statement filed by Defendants, it appears that the First Amended Complaint which was filed late with the court was timely served on Defendants.
