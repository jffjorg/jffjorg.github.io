Here is the summary:

The case is about a wrongful death action filed by Larisa Stepantsova and Lana Kruglyakova against DOCS Surgical Hospital, Rajan Patel, M.D., Ernest Schmidt, M.D., and DOES 1 through 100. The plaintiffs claim that the defendants were negligent in the care and treatment of Vyacheslav Stepantsov, who died after a surgical procedure on January 16, 2020. The defendants move for summary judgment, arguing that they complied with the standard of care and that their conduct did not cause Vyacheslav Stepantsov's death. The court considers the evidence and finds that there are triable issues of fact regarding whether the defendants complied with the standard of care and whether their conduct caused Vyacheslav Stepantsov's death. The court grants the defendants' motion for summary judgment, ruling that the plaintiffs have failed to meet their burden in demonstrating triable issues of material fact as to ostensible agency.
