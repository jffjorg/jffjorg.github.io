 Case Number:  21STCV43025    Hearing Date:   December 4, 2023    Dept:  30 REBECCA CALE PERICO vs BONNIE BRAE INVESTMENT SERVICES, LLC, A CALIFORNIA CORPORATIONMotion to Be Relieved as CounselTENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff, Rebecca Cale Perico, is DENIED without prejudice. On November 7, 2023, this court continued this hearing because counsel for Plaintiff had not submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). Specifically, the declaration on MC-052 had not been filed. In addition, the proposed order had the incorrect FSC and trial dates, listed as March 1, 2023, and March 14, 2023. The FSC and trial dates are on March 1, 2024, and March 14, 2024.Counsel was ordered to file a corrected proposed order, listing the correct hearing dates in this matter, the mandatory declaration on MC-052, and serve the client and defendant, then file proof of service.Counsel has yet to do so.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
