  Case Number:  20STCV08041    Hearing Date:   November 8, 2023    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
February 26, 2020, Plaintiffs Horacio Casillas, Kourtine Alexia Rae Scott, Ava
Blankevoort, and Camila Casillas filed this action against Defendants Ink
Transportation LLC, Surjay Pandey, and Does 1-50 for motor vehicle negligence
and general negligence. 
On
July 13, 2020, Defendants Ink Transportation LLC and Surjay Pandey filed an
answer. 
On
May 18, 2022, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On October
5, 2023, Petitioner Kandyce Lance (“Petitioner”) submitted a petition for
approval of minor’s compromise to be heard on November 8, 2023. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Kandyce Lance asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of
Plaintiff Camila Casillas.
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed petition with all necessary attachments. The Court
grants the petition. 
Petitioner
requests that the net balance be deposited in an insured account at Wells Fargo
Bank, 10540 Foothill Blvd., Suite 110, Rancho Cucamonga, CA 91730. The Court
orders the net balance so distributed. 
CONCLUSION 
The Court
GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of the pending action of minor Camila
Casillas filed by Petitioner Kandyce Lance. 
The
Court orders that the net balance be placed in an insured account at Wells
Fargo Bank, 10540 Foothill Blvd., Suite 110, Rancho Cucamonga, CA 91730. 
The
Court sets a OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement into Blocked Account on
December 11, 2023, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street
Courthouse. 
The
Court sets a OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on December 11, 2023, at
8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
