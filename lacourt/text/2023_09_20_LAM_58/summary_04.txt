And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has made a tentative ruling on a motion to dismiss filed by Defendant Webcor Construction, L.P., Defendant Next Century Partners, LLC, and Defendant Next Century Partners Mezzanine Borrower, LLC. The Plaintiffs, Control Air Enterprises, LLC, filed a complaint alleging various causes of action, including breach of contract, fraud, and disgorgement.

The Court sustained Defendant Webcor's demurrer with leave to amend as to the fifth cause of action, and sustained without leave to amend as to the ninth and tenth causes of action. The Court overruled Defendant NCP's demurrer to the first cause of action. Defendant NCPMB's demurrer was sustained with leave to amend as to the first, second, sixth, seventh, and eighth causes of action.

The Court concluded that the Plaintiff misconceives the demurrer's argument regarding the NCP Agreement, and that there exist ambiguities in the contract interpretations that cannot be resolved on demurrer. The Court also found that the alter ego allegations are insufficient to attach liability onto NCPMB from other defendants' conduct.

The amended complaint must be served and filed on or before October 19, 2023.
