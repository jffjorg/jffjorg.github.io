[TENTATIVE] ORDER GRANTING AMENDED
 MOTION TO BE RELIEVED AS COUNSEL FOR PLAINTIFF 
 
Estefan Encarnacion requests an order to be
relieved as counsel for Plaintiff, Juan Gutierrez, because of the
client’s breach of the representation agreement. Counsel served the motion on
the client by mail at an address confirmed by telephone within the
last 30 days. Counsel has also lodged a proposed order. 
Counsel has complied with the requirements of
California Rules of
Court, Rule 3.1362.
Accordingly, the motion is GRANTED. The order is effective upon the filing of
the proof of service of the signed order upon the client.
