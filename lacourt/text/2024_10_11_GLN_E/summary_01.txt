Summary:

This tentative ruling concerns three motions to compel responses to discovery requests filed by Plaintiff Andrey Pilikyan. The motions were filed against three separate Defendants: Vache Agazaryan, Apex Home Loans, LLC, and Elvis Keshishyan. The Plaintiff alleges that each of the Defendants has failed to provide timely responses to discovery requests, despite attempts to confer and serve the requests. The Court has granted each of the motions, ordering the Defendants to provide verified responses within ten days of the hearing. The Plaintiff has also requested monetary sanctions against the Defendants and their counsel in the amount of $1,060.00. The Court will consider the requests for sanctions at the hearing.
