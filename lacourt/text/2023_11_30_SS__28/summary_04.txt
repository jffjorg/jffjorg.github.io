The Court has reviewed the parties' papers and ruled as follows. The case was filed on January 5, 2021, by Plaintiff Arlex Parra against Defendant ASM Global Parent, Inc. and others. The Defendant filed an answer on March 10, 2022, and later filed a motion to compel the Plaintiff's further deposition testimony on August 18, 2023. The Plaintiff filed an opposition on November 15, 2023, and the Defendant filed a reply on November 20, 2023.

The Court finds that the Plaintiff's deposition was taken remotely on June 7, 2023, and that the Plaintiff failed to answer certain questions about previous lawsuits and workers' compensation claims. The Court also finds that the Plaintiff failed to produce documents requested in the deposition notice.

The Court grants the Defendant's motion to compel further answers to questions about previous lawsuits and workers' compensation claims. The Court orders the Plaintiff to appear for a further deposition within 30 days of the hearing and to answer the questions he previously refused to answer and produce the responsive documents he previously did not produce.

The Court also orders the Plaintiff to produce all documents requested in the Defendant's deposition notice that the Plaintiff did not previously produce. Finally, the Court denies the Plaintiff's request for sanctions.
