And here is the summary in English text:

This tentative ruling is regarding the motion to compel responses to discovery filed by National Ready Mixed Concrete Company (Defendant) against Isidro Patino (Plaintiff). The Defendant served the discovery requests on June 16 and 21, 2023, but the Plaintiff failed to serve responses. As a result, the Defendant is entitled to an order directing the Plaintiff to provide responses to the discovery requests. The Court also grants the Defendant's request for sanctions, ordering the Plaintiff to pay $640 in sanctions. The Plaintiff is ordered to provide the responses and pay the sanctions within 30 days of the date of this order.
