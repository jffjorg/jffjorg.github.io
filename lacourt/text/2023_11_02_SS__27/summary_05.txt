Summary:

The Judge's tentative ruling addresses a Demurrer filed by Defendant California Commerce Club Inc. to the First Amended Complaint (FAC) filed by Plaintiff Angelina Yu. The Demurrer argues that the claims are barred by the statute of limitations. The Court finds that the allegations in the FAC show a different legal relationship between Commerce and Coach Max, and that the doe amendment is proper. The Court overrules the Demurrer and orders the defendant to serve and file its Answer to the FAC within 10 days.
