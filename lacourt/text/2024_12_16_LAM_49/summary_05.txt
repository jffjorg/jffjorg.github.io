The judge has granted the motions filed by Daniel L. Germain, counsel for defendants Vista West Properties, Inc. and BS Broadway Village II, LP, to be relieved as counsel. The motions were unopposed. The judge has discretion to allow an attorney to withdraw as counsel, and such a motion should be granted provided that there is no prejudice to the client and it does not disrupt the orderly process of justice.
