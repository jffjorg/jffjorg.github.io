This tentative ruling addresses a motion by Lawrence C. Ecoff, counsel for Computer Institute of Technology, Inc. and Rene Aguero, to be relieved as counsel. The motion is unopposed. The court grants the motion to relieve Ecoff as counsel for Aguero, but grants the motion to relieve Ecoff as counsel for Computer Institute of Technology, Inc. only on the condition that Ecoff submits a new order indicating that the next hearing will be an OSC re: Striking of Complaint and Answer on January 31, 2025. If Computer Institute of Technology, Inc. does not have a new attorney of record by that date, its complaint and answer may be struck. The OSC re: Settlement is continued to January 31, 2025.
