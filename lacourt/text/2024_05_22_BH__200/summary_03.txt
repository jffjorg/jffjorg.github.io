And here is the English-text summary of the tentative ruling:

The court has granted the motion to sever and in limine the causes of action against Westside Investment Group, 1647 Ocean Front, Michelle Hohman, Victor Westin, and Hugh Farzaneh, individually and dba Hugh Construction and Development. The court finds that holding a separate trial would assist in avoiding prejudice to the severed defendants from the effect of the collateral estoppel order. The court also tolls the 5-year statute from this date through the entry of a final order resulting from the trial scheduled to start on July 8, 2024. The court has also granted the motion in limine, ordering the defendants to make their experts available for deposition.
