 Case Number:  23NWCV02779    Hearing Date:   September 12, 2024    Dept:  C Jane
W.A. Doe vs. Norwalk La Mirada Unified School District, et al., Case No.
23NWCV02779
 
This
is an action for child sexual assault. Defendant moves ex parte to advance the
hearing on the motion to be relieved as counsel. It is currently set for June
5, 2025. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Defendant
asserts that the motion to be relieved as counsel will occur after various
other motions. 
 
Based
on the above, the motion is GRANTED. The Court ADVANCES the hearing from
Thursday, June 5, 2025 to TODAY and CONTINUES it to Tuesday, September 24, 2024
at 10:30 A.M. in Dept. SE-C. Opposition due September 19, 2024.  
 
Moving
party to give notice.
