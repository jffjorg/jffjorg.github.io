 Case Number:  24NWCV01795    Hearing Date:   September 12, 2024    Dept:  C National
Commercial Recovery, Inc. vs. C&B Croissants, Inc., Case No. 24NWCV01795
 
This
is an action for common counts between two businesses. Plaintiff moves ex parte
to enforce the settlement agreement against Defendants under Code of Civil
Procedure section 664.6.  Plaintiff
contends that on August 21, 2024, the parties entered into a conditional
settlement which required Defendants to make weekly payments beginning on
August 22, 2024, and ending on November 7, 2024 for a total sum of $35,
292.05.  Defendants have not made any
payments to date.  
 
The
ex parte application is DENIED.  In the
alternative, the Court specially sets a hearing on Plaintiff’s motion to enforce
settlement agreement on October 9, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.  The ex parte application is deemed to be the
moving papers.  Opposition and Reply briefs
to be filed and served in compliance with the new hearing date.
 
Moving
party to give notice.
