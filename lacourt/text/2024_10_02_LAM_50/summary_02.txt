The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the motion for entry of stipulated consent judgment in the case of Keep America Safe and Beautiful v. Voyage Foods, Inc. The case concerns alleged violations of Proposition 65 by Defendant Voyage Foods, Inc. regarding the presence of cadmium in its products.

The Court finds that the warning required by the settlement complies with Proposition 65 and its implementing regulations. The Court also finds that the amount allocated for attorney's fees and costs in the Proposed Consent Judgment is reasonable. Additionally, the Court finds that the civil penalty amount provided for in the Proposed Consent Judgment is reasonable.

The Court grants the motion for entry of stipulated consent judgment and orders the proposed Judgment on Proposition 65 Settlement submitted on August 13, 2024. Plaintiff is ordered to provide notice of this Order.
