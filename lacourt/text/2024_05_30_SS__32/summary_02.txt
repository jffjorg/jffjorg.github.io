This tentative ruling denies the Plaintiff's motion to compel the deposition of Defendant Justin Vonhecht and grants Defendant's motion to compel the deposition of Plaintiff Ingrid Finicle and Plaintiff Alfred Profeet. The Court finds that Plaintiffs failed to timely object and failed to appear at their November 17, 2023 depositions. The Court grants monetary sanctions against Ingrid Finicle and her counsel in the amount of $895.65 and against Alfred Profeet and his counsel in the amount of $747.60. The Court also orders Plaintiffs to appear within 15 days' notice of this order for their depositions.
