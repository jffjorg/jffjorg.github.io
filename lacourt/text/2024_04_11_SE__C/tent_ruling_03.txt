Defendant
Guadalupe Meave moves Ex Parte for a Motion to Shorten Time or Advance the
Hearing on Defendant’s Motion for Judgment on the Pleadings and/or Continue
Trial. 
A party may
seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing "irreparable
harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting relief ex
parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)
Defendant
contends that there would be irreparable harm if his Motion is not heard prior
than the November 5, 2024 trial date. The Motion for Judgment on the Pleadings
is currently scheduled for December 17, 2024. 
Accordingly,
Defendant Guadalupe Meave’s Ex Parte Application to Advance the Hearing on
Defendant’s Motion for Judgment on the Pleadings is ADVANCED from Tuesday,
December 17,2024 to this date and CONTINUED to Tuesday, June 11, 2024 at 10:30
a.m. in Department SE-C.
