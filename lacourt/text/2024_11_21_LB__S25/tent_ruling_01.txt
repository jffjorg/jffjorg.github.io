  Case Number:  23LBCV00912    Hearing Date:   November 21, 2024    Dept:  S25 Tentative RulingThe Court has reviewed the proposed settlement and finds that the settlement itself is fair and reasonable. The minor claimant here, Richard Phan, has agreed to dismiss his claims and will receive $4,000.00 from Defendants Miro Dragich Jr. and The Estate of Miro Dragich, Sr. / Safeco Insurance Company (“Defendants”). (Pet. ¶ 10.) Settlement payments are also being made to the other three Plaintiffs in this action by the Defendants, Sakura Luu, Vien Trieu and Ruby Phan, for a total of $59,995.00. (Pet., ¶ 11.b; see also Attachment 11b.3 & 11.b.6.)The amount of attorney’s fees requested, represents 33.325 percent of the gross settlement to minor claimant, is $1,333.00. (See Attachment 13a [Declaration of John D. Trieu, Esq. In Support of Petition to Approve Compromise of Disputed Claim of Minor, Richard Phan].) The Claimant, through his parent and Guardian ad litem, Sakura Luu, agreed to a 33 1/3 percent contingency fee arrangement. (See Attachment 13a at 2:22-24, Attachment 17a.2 [Retainer Agreement].) In relevant part, the retainer agreement provides: “Attorney's fee shall be calculated as follows:(I) If the matter is resolved before filing a lawsuit, arbitration, mediation or formal initiation of proceedings, then Attorney's fee will be 33 1/3 % (excluding all costs and expenses) of the gross recovery; and(II) If the matter is resolved after the date initially set for the trial, mediation, or arbitration of the matter, then Attorney’s fee will be 40% (excluding all costs and expenses) of the gross recovery.”(Attachment 17a.2 ¶ 3(A).) The petition also seeks to use $740.00 to pay for medical expenses. (Pet. ¶¶ 12a, b; Attachment 12b.5.) The petition further seeks $208.00 to pay costs other than medical expenses. (Pet. ¶ 13.b.) Costs and litigation expenses are provided for under Retainer Agreement. (See Attachment 17a.2 ¶ 7.) After payment of all fees and expenses, Claimant is left with a balance of $1,719.00.The Court finds that Plaintiffs’ counsel has set forth sufficient facts breaking down and explaining the legal fees and costs incurred in litigating this matter. (See Cal. Rules of Court, rule 7.955(b).)Plaintiffs’ Petition to Confirm Minor’s Compromise of Disputed Claim as to Richard Phan is GRANTED.
