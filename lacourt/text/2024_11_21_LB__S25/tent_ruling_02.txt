 Case Number:  23LBCV01444    Hearing Date:   November 21, 2024    Dept:  S25 Tentative RulingAlthough continuances of trials are disfavored, the Court may grant a continuance if there is an affirmative showing of good cause requiring the continuance. (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1332(c).) The parties have demonstrated good cause.Defendant City of Long Beach’s Motion to Continue Trial is granted. Current FSC and Jury Trial dates are vacated. The Court sets a Final Status Conference on May 22, 2025 at 8:30 a.m. and Jury Trial on June 2, 2025 at 9:30 a.m.. All related dates and deadlines, including expert discovery, to follow the new trial date.
