The Court has reviewed the demurrer to the Cross-Complaint filed by Pacific Coast Energy Company, LP (PCEC) against Bridgeland Resources, LLC (Bridgeland). The Court has sustained the demurrer to the sixth cause of action for foreclosure on liens without leave to amend, as the Cross-Complaint was filed 15 days after the 180-day deadline to commence the action. The Court has also sustained the demurrer to the eighth cause of action for unfair business practices with leave to amend, as the Cross-Complaint fails to plead any unlawful, unfair, or fraudulent business practice.
