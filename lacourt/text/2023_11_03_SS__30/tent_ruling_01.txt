  Case Number:  20STCV03496    Hearing Date:   November 3, 2023    Dept:  30 ARMANDO RUIZ CARLOS, AN INDIVIDUAL vs HD SUPPLY CONSTRUCTION & INDUSTRIAL WHITE CAP, et al.Motion to Compel Plaintiff's Responses to Supplemental InterrogatoriesTENTATIVEMotion to Compel Responses is GRANTED.  Plaintiff is ORDERED to serve responses, without objections, to Defendant’s Supplemental Requests, within 20 days of the issuance of this order. Request for Sanctions is denied. Moving party is to give notice of this ruling.DISCUSSIONOn May 15, 2023, Defendant propounded on Plaintiff supplemental responses to interrogatories, both Form and Special. On August 18, 2023, Plaintiff had not provided any responses. Defendant’s counsel contacted Plaintiff’s counsel, who acknowledged that no responses had been served. However, as of the date of the motion, no responses have been provided. (Motion 4: 1-3)  A party waives objections to a discovery request when it does not serve a timely response.  CCP 20230.290(a).  The Court declines to award sanctions as the motion is unopposed.
