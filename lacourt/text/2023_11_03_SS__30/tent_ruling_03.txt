 Case Number:  21STCV04353    Hearing Date:   November 3, 2023    Dept:  30 SIRANOUCHE MELKONIAN vs ELIT PROPERTY MANAGEMENT INC., et al.Motion to Compel Plaintiff to Respond to Defendant's Supplemental Request for Production of DocumentsTENTATIVEDefendants’ motion to compel Plaintiff to respond to Defendants’ supplemental Request for Production of Documents, Set
One is GRANTED. Plaintiff is ORDERED to serve verified responses, without objection, to
Defendants’ supplemental Request for Production of Documents, Set One within 20 days of the issuance of this order.
Request for Sanctions is DENIED. Moving party to give notice.DISCUSSIONDefendants are entitled to an order compelling Plaintiff to serve a complete verified response
without objection to Defendants’ supplemental Request for Production of Documents, Set One. (Code Civ. Proc., §
2030.290, subd. (a).) On May 10, 2023, defense counsel served Defendants’ supplemental Request for Production of Documents, Set One to Plaintiff’s counsel with a due date of June 13, 2023. (Declaration of
William G. Sorkin, ¶ 3; Ex. “A.”) On July 21, 2023, defense counsel sent a letter to Plaintiff’s
counsel demanding responses by no later than July 31, 2023. (Sorkin Decl., ¶ 4; Ex. “B.”) Responses were served after the motion was filed.In opposition, Plaintiff contends that Defendants’ motion is moot because Plaintiff intends
to provide supplemental responses to Defendants’ request. Given that responses have been served.  The Court declines to impose sanctions.
