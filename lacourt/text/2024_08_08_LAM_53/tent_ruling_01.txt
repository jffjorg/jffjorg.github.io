The court considered
the moving papers filed in connection with each application.  No opposition papers were filed as to either
application. 
APPLICATION OF CHRISTOPHER S. SUNDBY
Applicant Christopher S.
Sundby requests an order permitting him to appear as counsel pro hac vice for
defendant Moises Gilinski in this action.
The court finds that the
application substantially complies with the requirements of California Rules of
Court, rule 9.40.  The court therefore
grants the application and orders that Christopher S. Sundby is permitted to
appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendant Moises Gilinski in this
action.  (Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40,
subd. (a).)
APPLICATION OF GERALD E. GREENBERG
Applicant Gerald E. Greenberg requests an order permitting him to
appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendant Moises Gilinski in this action. 
The court finds that the application
substantially complies with the requirements of California Rules of Court, rule
9.40.  The court therefore grants the
application and orders that Gerald E. Greenberg is permitted to appear as
counsel pro hac vice for defendant Moises Gilinski in this action.  (Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40, subd. (a).)
            The court orders defendant Moises
Gilinski to give notice of this ruling.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
