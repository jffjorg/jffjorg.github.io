English-text summary:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to a request for production of documents. The plaintiff, Erika Gomez Sandoval, alleges that American Honda Motor Co., Inc. (Honda) failed to produce adequate documents in response to her request. Honda opposes the motion, arguing that the request is overbroad and seeks irrelevant information. The court finds that the plaintiff has not shown good cause for further production of documents Nos. 1-5, 9-12, 14-15 and Nos. 6-8, 13, 16-29, 30 and 32. However, the court grants the motion to compel further responses as to RFPD No. 31, which requests documents related to Honda's investigation and analysis of the defect in the 2018 Honda Odyssey. The court orders production of documents to be made within 30 days.
