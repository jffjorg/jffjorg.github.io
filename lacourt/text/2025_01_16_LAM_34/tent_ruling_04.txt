Discussion
  
Counsel Applicant, Samantha
W. Frankel, seeks admission to appear as counsel pro
hac vice to represent Plaintiffs/Cross-Defendants FN Logistics, LLC and
Fashion Nova, LLC and to be associated with Counsel Kevin E. Gaut. 
 
Counsel Applicant is a member in good
standing of the State of New York and various federal courts. 
 
Counsel Applicant has appeared once in
California in the last two years in the United States District Court, Central
District of California. Currently, Counsel Applicant is also seeking pro hac
vice admission in the Los Angeles Superior Court for Case No. 24STCV19478.
 
Counsel Applicant has provided proof that
the $50.00 fee required to be admitted pro hac vice has been paid to the
State Bar of California and that the State Bar has been served with her
Application. 
 
Conclusion
 
Samantha
W. Frankel’s Application to Appear Pro Hac Vice is GRANTED.
