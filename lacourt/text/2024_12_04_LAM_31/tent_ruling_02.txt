I.          INTRODUCTION
 
            On April 23, 2024, Plaintiffs Joseph and Evaristo and
Catherine Evaristo, as Trustees of the Evaristo Family Trust Dated May 4, 2016 (Plaintiffs)
filed this unlawful detainer action against Defendants Aina Dumlao (Dumlao) and
Bru Muller (Muller) (collectively, Defendants) for failure to pay rent for the
property 3450 N. Cahuenga Blvd. West, Unit #510, Los Angeles, CA 90068 (the
Property).  Defendants are
self-represented.
 
            On
September 30, 2024, Plaintiffs filed six discovery motions.  Plaintiffs seek to compel Defendants’
responses to Form Interrogatories—General (Set One), Form
Interrogatories—Unlawful Detainer (Set One), and Request for Production of
Documents (Set One).  Plaintiffs request
sanctions against Defendants.
 
On October 7, 2024, Miller filed a Notice of Stay of
Proceedings (Bankruptcy).  
 
On November 27, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a status report of
bankruptcy proceedings.  The status
report indicates the bankruptcy court terminated the automatic stay on November
21, 2024.
 
Accordingly, the court will hear from the parties regarding
a continued hearing date for Plaintiffs’ discovery motions as well as an FSC
and trial date.
