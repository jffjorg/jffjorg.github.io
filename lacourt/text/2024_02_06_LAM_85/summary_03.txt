**Tentative Ruling**

This is a tentative ruling on the motion to compel further responses to Requests for Production (RFPs) Nos. 4-7, 12-13, 16-17, and 19-20. The motion is granted in part.

**Summary of the Case**

The City of Beverly Hills (City) and its City Council are defendants in a petition filed by Public Land for Public Use (PLPU) alleging causes of action for traditional mandamus. The petition concerns the City's attempt to dispose of five acres of City-owned property on Foothill Road between W. 3rd Street and Alden Drive in Beverly Hills, CA. The City placed Item E-6 on its January 24, 2023 meeting agenda, which simply declared that the subject ordinance would exempt the property from the California Environmental Quality Act (CEQA).
