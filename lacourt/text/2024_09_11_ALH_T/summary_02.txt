The judge is issuing a tentative ruling on a motion to enforce a court order that requires the plaintiff, Raymond Villalobos, Jr., to undergo a mental examination by a neuropsychological expert retained by the defendant, United Pacific Services Inc. The defendant has been attempting to conduct the examination since July 2023, but the plaintiff has failed to respond to the defendant's attempts to meet and confer to agree on the scope of disclosure and use of the raw test data and audio recording of the examination. The judge is granting the defendant's motion and ordering the plaintiff to attend the mental examination within 45 days, limiting the scope of disclosure and use of the raw test data and audio recording to the plaintiff and the defendant's respective licensed experts. The judge is also granting the defendant's request for sanctions in the amount of $1,380 against the plaintiff and his counsel for failing to respond to the meet and confer attempts.
