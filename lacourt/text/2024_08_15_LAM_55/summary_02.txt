And here is a summary in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling denying the Motion for a Protective Order filed by Defendants Logan Wood and Vertex Funding, LLC. The Plaintiff, Bondit LLC, is seeking discovery of financial information of the Judgment Debtors, including Jovan Dawkins, HMG Studios, Inc., Heritage Music Group, Inc., Full Range Backline LLC, and Jil Pro Group, LLC. The defendants argue that the information is protected by confidentiality provisions and privacy rights, but the court finds that the balancing of interests tilts in favor of the Plaintiff. The court also finds that the confidentiality provision does not apply to all Judgment Debtors and that the Defendants have not shown that the provision applies to the shareholders, subsidiaries, and related entities.
