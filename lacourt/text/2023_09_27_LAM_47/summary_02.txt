The defendants, After Dark Films, LLC, CSS Entertainment, LLC, California Maple Holdings, LLC, and Courtney Solomon, argue that they have a reasonable possibility of prevailing on their claims, including an alter ego claim.

The Judge has based her decision on the evidence presented, including the declarations of the parties, and has concluded that the defendants have a reasonable possibility of prevailing on their claims.
