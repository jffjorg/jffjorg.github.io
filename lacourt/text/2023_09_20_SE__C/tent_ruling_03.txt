 Case Number:  21NWCV00133    Hearing Date:   September 20, 2023    Dept:  C Saadia
Square LLC v. All-Ways Pacific LLC 21NWCV00133
Plaintiff's Ex
Parte Application for Relief Under CCP 437c(b)(2) is GRANTED. The Court
finds good cause to excuse the untimely filing (by one day) of Plaintiff's
Summary Judgment Opposition papers on September 13, 2023. Plaintiff's Counsel's
Declaration establishes good cause for a one-day extension. (CCP 437(b)(2).)
 If Defendants are prejudiced by the untimely filing, they can request a
continuance of the September 26,2023 motion hearing.  Moving Party to give
notice.
