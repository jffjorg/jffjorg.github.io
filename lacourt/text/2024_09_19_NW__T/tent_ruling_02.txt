 Case Number:  23VECP00458    Hearing Date:   September 19, 2024    Dept:  T 23VECP00458  Harges Family Partnership v Landmark Motion to withdraw is granted. The motion to withdraw was served 6/21/2024.  Plaintiffs were served with an Order to show cause why case should not be dismissed for failure to have an attorney on 8/7/2024 by email.  Plaintiffs have known since at least June 2024 that new counsel should be retained.  Because the trustee and LP cannot be self represented, in the absence of  new counsel for the plaintiffs, the case is dismissed without prejudice.  Clerk to give notice.
