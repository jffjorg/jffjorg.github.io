MOTION
TO COMPEL FURTHER RESPONSES TO DISCOVERY 



 
MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Jose Centeno Nolasco
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant General
Motors, LLC
 
 
This is a Song-Beverly action.  On January 12, 2024, Plaintiff, Jose Centeno
Nolasco, filed this motion to compel Defendant, General Motors, LLC, to provide
further responses to Plaintiff’s Request for Production of Documents, Set One,
Nos. 16-21.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of this motion.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motion to March 28, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31.  Thereafter the parties are
to contact the judicial assistant to schedule an IDC date and time.   Counsel should also request from the Judicial
Assistant a copy of Dept. 31’s Song-Beverly discovery order. 
 
Clerk of the Court to give notice.
