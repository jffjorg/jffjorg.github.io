DISCUSSION
Defendant University of Southern California (“Defendant”) moves the
court for an order (1) compelling plaintiff Gloria Ibe (“Plaintiff”) to
arbitrate the claims alleged in her Complaint, and (2) dismissing, or,
alternatively, staying proceedings in this action pending completion of
arbitration.
Plaintiff filed her opposition to the motion late but explained in her
opposition the reason for filing it late. 
The court exercises its discretion to consider Plaintiff’s late-filed
opposition and to continue the hearing on the motion to give Defendant an
opportunity to file reply papers. 
The court orders that (1) the hearing on Defendant’s Motion to Compel
Arbitration and for Dismissal or, Alternatively, Stay of
Proceedings is continued from October 9, 2023 to October 24, 2023, at 10:00
a.m., and (2) Defendant may file reply papers no later than October 17,
2023.  
The court orders that the (1)
Case Management Conference, and (2) Order to Show Cause re failure to file
proof of service of summons and complaint are continued from October 16, 2023
to October 24, 2023, at 10:00 a.m.  
The court orders defendant University of Southern California to give
notice of this ruling. 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
