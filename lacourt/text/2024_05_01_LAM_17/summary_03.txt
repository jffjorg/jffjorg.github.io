The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Daniel Eliezer Corral Pulido vs. BMW of North America, LLC, et al. The Plaintiff, Daniel Eliezer Corral Pulido, has moved to compel further responses to special interrogatories and Requests for Production (RFPs). The Court has granted the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories Nos. 14, 20, and 23. The Court has also granted the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to RFPs in part, denied in part. The Court has imposed a sanction of $700.00 on the Defendant, jointly and severally with counsel, for failing to comply with the Court's discovery orders. The Court has ordered the Defendant to produce certain documents and information, including the "Warranty Policy and Procedure Manual", internal analysis or investigation regarding defects, customer complaints, and policies and procedures used to evaluate customer requests for repurchase. The Court has declined to award sanctions at this time.
