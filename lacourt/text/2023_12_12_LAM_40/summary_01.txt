The judge has granted the plaintiffs' motion to compel responses to their requests for production of documents. The motion was filed on August 28, 2023, and the defendants, BMW of North America, LLC and Does 1-10, opposed the motion on November 30, 2023. The judge has found that the defendants failed to provide timely and compliant responses to the requests for production of documents, despite claims that they had agreed to extend the deadline for response. The judge has ordered the defendants to provide Code-compliant responses without objections within 30 days.
