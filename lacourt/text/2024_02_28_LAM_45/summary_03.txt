English-text summary:

This is a tentative ruling on a petition to approve a minor's compromise of a disputed claim. The petition was filed by Amia Little, the guardian ad litem of Treasure Anderson, a minor, against Desert View Apartments LP. The petition is unopposed and requests approval of a settlement in the amount of $4,000.00, with $1,000.00 going towards attorney's fees and the remaining $3,000.00 to be deposited into a blocked account for the benefit of the minor. The Court finds that the proposed settlement is fair and reasonable and grants the petition. The Court also sets an OSC for proof of deposit and requires an acknowledgement of receipt by the financial institution before the hearing.
