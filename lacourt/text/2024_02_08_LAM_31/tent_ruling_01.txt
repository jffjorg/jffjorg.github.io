MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Ranae Fields
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant General
Motors, LLC
 
 
This is a Song-Beverly action.  On December 16, 2023, Plaintiff, Ranae Fields,
filed this motion to compel Defendant, General Motors, LLC, to provide further
responses to Plaintiff’s Request for Production of Documents, Set One, Nos. 16-21.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of this motion.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motion to March 27, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31.  The parties are to contact
the judicial assistant to obtain a copy of the court’s Song-Beverly Act tentative
discovery order.  Thereafter the parties
are to contact the judicial assistant to schedule an IDC date and time.
