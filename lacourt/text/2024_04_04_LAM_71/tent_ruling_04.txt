Plaintiff Hashem 26, LLC’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having
considered all documents submitted in support of a default judgment, the Court
rules as follows.
 
Background
On November 30, 2022, Plaintiff Hashem 26, LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed this
action against Defendants Victor Manuel Olazabal, Joselyn Olazabal, and Chemo’s
Upholstery (collectively, “Defendants”) for breach of lease, breach of personal
guaranty, unfair business practice pursuant to Business & Professions Code
§17200, and retaliation.
On November 14, 2023, the clerk entered default against Defendants. On
March 1, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be heard on
April 4, 2024.
 
Party’s
Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendants Victor Manuel Olazabal,
Joselyn Olazabal, and Chemo’s Upholstery and award Plaintiff $102,200.92 consisting of $89,450.00 as the
demand of the complaint, $8,945.00 in prejudgment interest, $2,679.00 in
attorney’s fees, and $1,126.92 in costs.
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all
applicable attachments. The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the
requested award and grants Plaintiff’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application for default judgment filed on March 1, 2024, is GRANTED.
Judgment of $102,200.92 is entered against Defendants
Victor Manuel Olazabal, Joselyn Olazabal, and Chemo’s
Upholstery.
