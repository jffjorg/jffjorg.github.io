**Case Number:** 21STCV17355

**Tentative Ruling:**

The Court has considered the moving, opposition, reply, and supplemental papers. The case involves an employment law action where the plaintiffs, Linda Suniga and Claudia Raya, allege that the City of Hawaiian Gardens retaliated against them for engaging in protected activities under Labor Code section 1102.5 and the California Fair Employment and Housing Act.

**Summary of the Case:**

The plaintiffs, employees of the City of Hawaiian Gardens, alleged that the City retaliated against them for engaging in protected activities, including reporting sexual harassment and union-related complaints. The defendants moved for summary judgment, arguing that the plaintiffs' claims lack merit.

**Tentative Ruling:**

The Court finds that the defendants' motion for summary judgment is partially granted and partially denied. The Court grants the defendants' motion as to the time-barred nature
