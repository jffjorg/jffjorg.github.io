 Case Number:  22VECV01831    Hearing Date:   February 9, 2024    Dept:  T 22VECV01831 GUS SLIKA vs KASSIR MAKHLOOF
Tentative ruling:  Motion is granted despite the absence of a separate statement.  The subpoena is quashed.  No opposition was filed. Income tax returns are protected by the constitutional right to privacy and by case law.  There has been no evidence of waiver in this matter based upon the moving papers.
Monetary sanctions are denied because they were not requested in the notice of motion, including against whom, in what amount, and the statutory basis for sanctions.
