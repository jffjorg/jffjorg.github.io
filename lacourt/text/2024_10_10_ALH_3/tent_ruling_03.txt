On July 21,
 2023, plaintiff Financial Credit Network, Inc. (“Plaintiff”)
filed this action against defendant Gildarda
 B. Martinez dba JL Screens & Doors (“Defendant”). Defendant, who
is self-represented, filed an answer on August 28, 2023. On May 24,
 2024, Plaintiff served Form Interrogatories (Set One) on Defendant.
No responses were received. 
On June 28,
 2024, Plaintiff sent Defendant
a letter attempting to meet and confer regarding the status of Defendant’s
responses. No response to the letter was received and no discovery responses
have been served. Plaintiff filed this motion on July 10, 2024. 
Where a party fails to serve timely
responses to discovery requests, the court may make an order compelling
responses. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.290.) A party that fails to serve timely
responses waives any objections to the request, including ones based on
privilege or the protection of attorney work product. (Code Civ. Proc., §
2030.290, subd. (a).) 
Defendant
did not oppose the Motion and it is undisputed responses were not served. Plaintiff’s
Motion is GRANTED and Defendant is ordered to serve verified responses
without objections to Defendant’s Form Interrogatories (Set One) within 20
days of the date of this Order. 
Plaintiff’s
request for monetary sanctions is GRANTED and imposed against Defendant
in the reduced amount of $290 for 1 hour
at Plaintiff’s
counsel’s hourly rate of $230 and $60 in filing fees, to be paid
within 20 days of the date of this Order. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.290, subd.
(c).) 
Moving party to give notice.
