The tentative ruling concerns a demurrer filed by defendants Rick Hawthorne, Larry Thornton, and Valley View Vaulters, Inc. to the first amended complaint filed by plaintiffs Irwin Jacobowitz, Pearl Jacobowitz, Dakota Dusty Zeigerman-Jacobowitz, Arizona Jacobowitz, and Montana Jacobowitz. The demurrer challenges the sufficiency of pleading for private nuisance, negligent misrepresentation, and failure to return security deposit causes of action. The court finds that the demurrer is sustained as to the 5th, 12th, and 17th causes of action and grants plaintiffs 30 days leave to amend to try to cure the defects. The court also notes that the 11th cause of action for breach of habitability is still "alive" after the granting of the special motion to strike, but it is not the subject of this demurrer.
