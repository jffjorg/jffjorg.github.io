On
November 3, 2023, Defendant Daniel E. Park filed a motion to compel Plaintiff Central
Metal, Inc.’s responses to Requests for Production of Documents (Set One), served
on March 2, 2023.
Concurrently
with filing its Opposition, Plaintiff produced responses and documents.  (Eanet Decl. ¶ 7.)  Plaintiff provides proof of this electronic service.  (Eanet Decl., Exs. A-B.)  Defendant confirms that late responses have been
provided.  (See generally Reply.)
Accordingly,
the motion to compel initial responses to Requests for Production of Documents (Set
One) is DENIED.  The request for sanctions
is also denied.
