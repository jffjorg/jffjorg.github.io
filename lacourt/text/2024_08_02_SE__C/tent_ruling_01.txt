  Case Number:  21NWCV00797    Hearing Date:   August 2, 2024    Dept:  C Arana
v. Rudy’s Wholesale Corporation, et al., Case No. 21NWCV00797
 
This
is a wage-and-hour case. Defendants move ex parte for a temporary stay of
enforcement because of the filing of an appeal of this action. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
The
parties have agreed to stay enforcement until August 9. (Supp. Decl. Biggins, ¶
2.) 
 
Based
on the above, the enforcement is stayed until August 9. 
 
Moving
party to give notice.
