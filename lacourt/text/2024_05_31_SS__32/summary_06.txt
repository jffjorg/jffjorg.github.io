This tentative ruling addresses the motions filed by Defendant Ralphs Grocery Company, Inc. (Defendant) to deem admitted Requests for Admission, Set One, and to compel verified responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, Special Interrogatories, Set One, and Request for Production, Set One. The Defendant served the requests on November 6, 2023, and granted extensions, but Plaintiff Maria Nunez has not responded. The Court grants the motions to compel and awards monetary sanctions of $1,039.95 against Plaintiff and her counsel. The Court reduces the requested monetary sanctions from $1,296.65 to $1,039.95.
