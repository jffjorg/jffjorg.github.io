This is an action filed by a pro per litigant, Plaintiff Raymond Van Der Hoek.  Defendants County of Los Angeles and Deputy Allen Mata seek Judgment on the Pleadings as to the entire operative Complaint for uncertainty and failure to state sufficient facts.  Because there is no opposition, the subject causes of action are deemed abandoned.  (See Herzberg v. County of Plumas (2005) 133 Cal.App.4th 1, 20.)  The Motion is granted, without leave to amend.
