[Tentative] Order
 
            The Court
posts this tentative order on Thursday, October 5, 2023, in advance of the
hearing on October 10, 2023, at 8:30 a.m. 
The Court provides notice that any party who does not appear shall waive
the right to be heard and shall submit to entry of this or any other order on
this motion.
 
            Petitioners
Midel Kosim and Oen Intan (collectively, “Petitioners”) filed a petition to
compel Allstate Insurance Company (“Respondent”) to arbitration their claims
for damages pursuant to the uninsured motorist portion of their insurance
policy.  Petitioners also ask the Court
to appoint the arbitrator.  The petition
seeks no other relief.
 
            Good cause
having been shown, the Court orders as follows:
 
            1.         The Court grants the petition and
orders the parties to arbitrate the uninsured motorist claims.
 
            2.         The Court appoints Gary Galton as the
arbitrator.  If he is not available, the
Court appoints Adam Gilbert as the arbitrator. 
If he is not available, the Court appoints Robert Fink as the
arbitrator.  If he is not available, the
Court appoints Cherif Elsheik as the arbitrator.
 
            3.         Having granted all relief requested in
the petition, the Court sets no further dates. 

 
            4.         Petitioners’ counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
