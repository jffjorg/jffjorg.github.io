 Case Number:  21STCV24483    Hearing Date:   October 10, 2023    Dept:  39 Aggreko LLC v. The
Little Cottage Caregivers, LLC
Case No.
21STCV24483
Motion to Deem
Admitted
 
            Plaintiff
Aggreko LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed this action for breach of contract and open
book account against Defendant The Little Cottage Caregivers, LLC
(“Defendant”).  Plaintiff served Requests
for Admission (“RFA”) asking Defendant to admit that it owes Plaintiff the
principal sum of $63,740.40 as of September 18, 2019.  Defendant did not serve a verified
response.  Plaintiff moves for an order
to deem Defendant to have admitted this fact. 
Defendant did not oppose the motion or file anything with the Court
establishing that it served a verified response, without objections, before the
hearing.
 
            Based upon
the foregoing, the Court orders as follows:
 
            1.         Plaintiff’s motion is granted.  Defendant is deemed to have admitted that it
owes Plaintiff the principal sum of $63,740.40 as of September 18. 2019.
 
            2.         The Court orders Defendant to pay
Plaintiff sanctions, as required by Code of Civil Procedure sections 2033.280, in
the amount of $1,660, by and through counsel, within thirty (30) days.
 
            3.         Plaintiff’s counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
