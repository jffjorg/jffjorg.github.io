The Court has issued a tentative ruling in response to Hollywood Glass Co., Inc.'s motion to compel plaintiff Ron York to respond to outstanding discovery. The Court finds that the motion to compel is moot in light of the responses served on Hollywood prior to the hearing. The Court will, however, award monetary sanctions against plaintiff and his counsel for their failure to comply with discovery rules. The Court awards 1 hour for each motion to compel, and 1 hour to appear at the hearing, at the requested rate of $200 per hour, for a total $600 as attorney's fees. The Court also orders plaintiff and/or his counsel to pay sanctions to Hollywood in the total amount of $600 within 20 days.
