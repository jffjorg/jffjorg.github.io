The tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel discovery responses brought by defendant VEJ Pomona 3. The defendant propounded discovery requests on October 5, 2023, but the plaintiff failed to respond. The plaintiff's counsel was relieved, and the plaintiff did not file an opposition to the motion to compel. The court granted the motion to compel, ordering the plaintiff to respond to the discovery requests without objections within 30 days. The court also awarded sanctions against the plaintiff, ordering the plaintiff to pay $370 in attorney's fees and $1,577.95 in filing fees.
