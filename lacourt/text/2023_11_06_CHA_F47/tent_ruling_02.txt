RELIEF REQUESTED: An order admitting Susan
Kaczmarcyzk to appear as counsel in this action pro hac vice for Defendant
Coexist Ventures LLC.
 
RULING: 
 
The application and supporting papers were served one day
short of the required 16 court days plus 2 court days notice required for
electronic service.  See CCP
1005(b); CCP 1010.6(a)(3)(B).  While the
application/motion indicate that they were served on the State Bar of
California at its San Francisco office, the proof of service for same does not
show service on the State Bar of California as required.  See CRC 9.40(c)(1). 
 
The declaration of Susan Kaczmarcyzk does not set forth
her home address as required.  See
(Kaczmarcyzk Decl. ¶1); CRC 9.40(d)(1).
 
If Plaintiff waives the timeliness defect, a proof of
service showing service on the State Bar of California is filed and the
residence address of Susan Kaczmarcyzk is provided, the application will be
granted.  If not, the hearing on the
petition will be continued to allow the foregoing defects to be cured.
