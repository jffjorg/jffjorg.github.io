[TENTATIVE] ORDER GRANTING
 MOTION TO BE RELIEVED AS COUNSEL FOR OF DEFENDANTS 
 
Patrick
 W. Kelly and Aliya L. Astaphan, request an order to be relieved as
counsel of record for Defendants, Ambiance
 Realty, Inc. and Jared Nunez, as it has become unreasonably difficult
for defense counsel to continue representing them. Counsel served the motion on
the clients by mail at an address confirmed by mail and electronic
mail within the last 30 days. Counsel has also lodged a proposed order. 
Counsel has complied with the requirements of
California Rules of
Court, Rule 3.1362.
Accordingly, the motion is GRANTED. The order is effective upon the filing of
the proof of service of the signed order upon the client. Moving party is
ordered to give notice.
