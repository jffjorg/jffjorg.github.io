Claimant
Scarlett Crismari Maldonado, a minor, by and through her parent and guardian ad
litem, Lucila Raquel Maravilla Aguillon, has agreed to settle her claims against
Defendants Parallax Properties LLC, Xenon Investment Corp., and Temeku Properties
LLC in exchange for $2,500.00.  Of this amount,
$625.00 is allocated for attorney fees, and $151.25 is allocated for costs, leaving
a balance of $1,723.75 for Claimant.  If approved,
the funds will be delivered to a parent of Claimant, without bond, on the terms
and under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401 and 3402.
Claimant
Jade Yaxelly Rodriguez, a minor, by and through her parent and guardian ad litem,
Lucila Raquel Maravilla Aguillon, has agreed to settle her claims against Defendants
Parallax Properties LLC, Xenon Investment Corp., and Temeku Properties LLC in exchange
for $2,500.00.  Of this amount, $625.00 is
allocated for attorney fees, and $151.25 is allocated for costs, leaving a balance
of $1,723.75 for Claimant.  If approved, the
funds will be delivered to a parent of Claimant, without bond, on the terms and
under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401 and 3402.
Court
approval is required for all settlements of a minor’s claim.  (Probate Code §§ 3500, 3600, et seq.; Code
Civ. Proc. § 372.)  The Court has reviewed
the proposed settlement and finds that it is fair and reasonable.  The Court also finds that the attorney fees, which
are 25% of the funds, are fair and reasonable.
Accordingly,
the Petitions to Approve Minor’s Compromise are GRANTED.
