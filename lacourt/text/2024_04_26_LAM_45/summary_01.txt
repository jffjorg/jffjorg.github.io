The Tentative Ruling grants the Petitions for Approval of Minor's Compromise filed by Walter Giron on behalf of his minor children, Emilson Giron, Abigail Giron, and Mayrin Giron. The Petitions were filed in response to the settlement agreement reached between the Claimants and Defendant Alina Sanchez, individually and as Trustee of the Dominguez Family Trust, and EAJS, LLC. The agreement provides for a $5000 settlement for each of the Claimants, with $1,250 in attorney's fees to be deducted from each Claimant's settlement fund. The net balance of $3,750 will be deposited in an insured account subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the court. The Court finds that the settlement is reasonable and in the best interests of the minor children.
