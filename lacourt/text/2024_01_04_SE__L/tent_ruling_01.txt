Plaintiff’s Motion
to Strike the Answer Entirely or Alternatively Portions of the Answer Filed by
Defendant CLPM, INC. is
DROPPED as MOOT. 
 
Moving Party to give notice.
 
A motion to strike lies either when (1) there is “irrelevant, false or
improper matter inserted in any pleading”; or (2) to strike any pleading or
part thereof “not drawn or filed in conformity with the laws of this state, a
court rule or order of court.” (CCP §436.)
 
Plaintiffs move to strike either the entire Answer of Defendant CLPM,
INC, or alternatively, portions of the Answer of Defendant CLPM, INC.
 
This action was filed on July 25, 2023. On October 5, 2023, Plaintiffs
filed the subject Motion to Strike the Answer of Defendant CLPM, INC. On
December 1, 2023, Plaintiffs filed the Notice of Lodging the Answer of the Prop
Per Defendant. 
 
The Motion is DROPPED as MOOT. As of January 2, 2024, no Answer has been
filed by Defendant CLPM, INC. with the Court.
