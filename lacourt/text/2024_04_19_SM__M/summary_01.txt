Summary:

The tentative ruling grants Plaintiff Colleen McCullough's motion to compel the deposition of Defendant General Motors LLC's person most qualified (PMQ) with production of documents. The Court finds good cause for the categories of testimony and document requests, which are all within the scope of permissible discovery in lemon law actions. The Court orders the Defendant to produce its PMQ for deposition at a mutually agreeable time and location within 30 days, and awards sanctions in the amount of $2,970.00 to be paid in 30 days. The Court also orders the parties to meet and confer on executing a standard protective order regarding confidential discovery.
