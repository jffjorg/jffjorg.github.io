The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Complete Merchant Services, Inc. (Plaintiff) vs. Signature Payments, LLC (Defendant) regarding the Defendant's Motion to Compel Further Discovery Responses. The Plaintiff alleges that the Defendant improperly transferred a merchant account, resulting in the Defendant's refusal to pay residuals owed under their agency contract. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's motion is actually three separate motions improperly presented as one and is inclined to continue the combined motion to January 24, 2025. The Plaintiff is ordered to pay an additional $120 in filing fees and file proof of payment within 7 days or all but one motion will be taken off calendar. The Court cautions that future violations of its policy may result in motions being taken off calendar, the imposition of sanctions, or both.
