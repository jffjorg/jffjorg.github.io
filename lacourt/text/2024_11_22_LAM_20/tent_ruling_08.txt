This is a lemon law matter.  Plaintiff Jose Martinez seeks to compel Defendant General Motors, LLC to provide initial responses to his first set of form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for production.  Plaintiff also seeks to deem his requests for admission admitted.Because responses have now been served, the Motions to Compel/Deem Admitted are denied as moot.  The Court declines to award sanctions at this time.Defendant also seeks relief from its waiver of objections.  Because there is no opposition, the Motion is granted.  (See Code Civ. Proc. §§ 2030.290(a), 2031.300(a), 2033.280(a).)Defendant to give notice.
