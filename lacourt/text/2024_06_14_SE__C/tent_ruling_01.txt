  Case Number:  23NWCV03502    Hearing Date:   June 14, 2024    Dept:  C Marisa Torres, et al. vs McDonald’s Restaurants of
California, et al., Case No. 23NWCV03502
This is a personal injury action. 
 
Plaintiffs Marisa Torres and Liam Torres (collectively
“Plaintiffs”) apply ex parte for an order advancing the hearing date set for
Plaintiffs’ Motion for an Order Compelling Defendant N.L.C. Enterprises to
Provide Further Responses to Plaintiff’s Requests for Production, Set One, and
Motion for an Order Compelling Further Responses to Plaintiff’s Special
Interrogatories, Set One. 
 
A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual
showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis
for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. R. Ct. 3.1202(c).)
 
The two motions to compel further responses are currently
set for Wednesday, March 12, 2025. The jury trial is currently scheduled for
August 12, 2024.  The Court cannot accommodate the hearings before the
trial date.  The Court notes there are various other motions scheduled for
October and December 2024.  
 
Accordingly, Plaintiffs’ ex parte application to advance the
hearing dates for the Motion for an Order Compelling Defendant N.L.C.
Enterprises to Provide Further Responses to Plaintiff’s Requests for
Production, Set One, and Motion for an Order Compelling Further Responses to
Plaintiff’s Special Interrogatories, Set One is GRANTED in part.  The
motions are advanced to this date and CONTINUED to Thursday, October 17, 2024
at 10:30 a.m in Department SE-C, to be heard together with Defendants’ Motion to
Strike.  The parties are not foreclosed from seeking to continue trial in
Dept. SE-F.  
 
Moving party to give notice.
