Discussion
 
Plaintiff
went on Defendant Cybercartel International, Inc.’s website, bodycandy.com,
seeking to purchase a gift card and used the chat feature offered to
communicate with Defendant’s website about their products. Plaintiff alleges
that without her knowledge or consent, Defendant used spyware technology, Klaviyo,
to access Plaintiff’s device, install spyware, extract personal data, and expose
Plaintiff’s identity and personal information to other companies.
