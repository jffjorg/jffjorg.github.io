 Case Number:  24VECV00042    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2024    Dept:  T 24VECV00042
Shaker v. Lucky 8 Motors
Tentative ruling:  The court
grants the Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Lucky 8 Motors LLC and as the
LLC cannot be self represented and no attorney has substituted into the case
for the defendant, the answer of Lucky 8 Motors of 4/2/2024 is stricken and
default entered against it.  The court
will set an OSC re default judgment as to both defendants.  The court will sign the order relieving
counsel which must be served by mail on Lucky 8.
