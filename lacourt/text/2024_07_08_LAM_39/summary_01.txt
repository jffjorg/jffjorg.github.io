This tentative ruling is related to a hearing on a demurrer to the third amended complaint filed by Plaintiff Mario Cazares against Defendant TGB Promotions, Inc. and Tom Brown. The Plaintiff alleges that the Defendant breached the Professional Boxing Safety Act and induced him to contract on unfavorable terms. The Defendant demurred to the second, fourth, and sixth causes of action in the complaint. The Court has reviewed the allegations and has concluded that the second and fourth causes of action are sufficient to state a claim against the Defendant, while the sixth cause of action for negligence is not. The Court has sustained the demurrer to the sixth cause of action without leave to amend. The Defendant is ordered to answer the third amended complaint within 10 days.
