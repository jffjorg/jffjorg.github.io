 Case Number:  22STCV13356    Hearing Date:   February 22, 2024    Dept:  39 Carlos Patlan v.
Group V San Bernardino L.P.
Case No.
22STCV13356
Ex Parte
Application
 
            Defendant
filed an ex parte application for an order continuing the hearing on
Plaintiff’s motion to compel the deposition of Mike Nijjar from February 29,
2024, to March 19, 2024.  Defendant also
seeks to advance its motion for a protective order from May 22, 2024, to March
19, 2024, or in the alternative, to hear the motion on shortened time on
February 29, 2024.
 
            The Court
will be engaged in a jury trial until March 8, 2024, and thus is not available
to handle the matters set for hearing on February 29, 2024.  Therefore, the Court orders as follows:
 
            1.         Defendant’s ex parte application is
granted.
 
            2.         The Court discharges the Order to Show
Cause re: Sanctions as all defendants have been served.
 
            3.         The Court advances and continues the
case management conference from February 29, 2024, to March 19, 2024, at 8:30
a.m.
 
            4.         The Court advances and continues
Plaintiff’s motion to compel from February 29, 2024, to March 19, 2024, at 8:30
a.m.
 
            5.         The Court advances the hearing on
Defendant’s motion for a protective order from May 22, 2024, to March 19, 2024,
at 8:30 a.m.
 
            6.         Defendant’s counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
