 Plaintiff Brian B. Wong, as Trustee of The James K. Wong Revocable Trust dated October 12, 2011, seeks to compel initial responses to his form interrogatories, set one, from Defendant Winnie Chan.  Plaintiff also seeks to deem admitted his requests for admission, set one, propounded on Chan. Because responses have apparently not been served, the Motions are granted.  The requests for admission are deemed admitted.  Responses, without objections, are to be served for form interrogatories within 30 days. The Court awards reduced sanctions in the total amount of $1,000.
