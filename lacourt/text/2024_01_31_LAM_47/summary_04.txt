The Judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Andreas Kratky, et al. v. Downtown Prime, LLC, et al. (Case No. BC682898). The case involves a breach of lease action, where the Plaintiffs sued the Defendants for habitability violations. The parties entered into a move-out settlement agreement, but Plaintiff Jenna King contested the settlement and was eventually ordered to pay judgment on July 20, 2020. The Defendants then appealed, and the Court of Appeal affirmed the judgment.

The Defendants are now seeking an order for the sale of the dwelling under Code of Civil Procedure section 704.750. The Judge has ruled that the application does not comply with the requirements of Code of Civil Procedure section 704.760 and has ordered the Defendants to submit supplemental documentation within 10 days. The Judge has also continued the hearing to March 1, 2024.
