T.H. Community,

9, 2023 
Defendants
Romero’s Demurrer/Motion to Strike Portions of Complaint (Unopposed)
 
Defendants demur
to this unlawful detainer action, arguing plaintiff T.H. Community, LLC is
suspended by the Franchise Tax Board for failure to pay corporate taxes so
cannot prosecute this lawsuit. Plaintiff does not oppose.
 
A corporation with
FTB suspended status cannot prosecute or defend a lawsuit until its status is
restored. Center for Self-Improvement & Community Development v. Lennar
Corp. (2009) 173 Cal.App.4th 1543, 1552. 
 
The court takes
judicial notice under Cal. Evid. Code §452 (h) that the California Secretary of
State online record for T.H. Community, LLC states “Suspended -FTB” for failure
to pay taxes. Plaintiff cannot prosecute this lawsuit until its status has been
restored. SUSTAINED without leave to amend. The motion to strike is MOOT.
