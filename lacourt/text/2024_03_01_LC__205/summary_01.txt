This is a tentative ruling on a motion for leave to file a cross-complaint against Turo, Inc. by Defendant Paul Toutonghi. The Plaintiff, Benjamin Friedman, had rented a McLaren 570S sportscar to Defendant Toutonghi through Turo's website, but the vehicle was involved in a collision. The Plaintiff is seeking $309,396 in damages for loss of use of the vehicle. The Defendant is seeking to file a cross-complaint against Turo, Inc. for the majority of the Plaintiff's loss of use damages. The Court concludes that leave to file the cross-complaint should be granted because it arises out of the same transaction or occurrence as the main action. The Court grants the Defendant's motion for leave to file a cross-complaint against Turo, Inc.
