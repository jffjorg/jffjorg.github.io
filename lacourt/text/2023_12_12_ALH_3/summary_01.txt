This is a tentative ruling by Judge Hon. William A. Crowfoot regarding a motion to compel the deposition of defendant Frunzik Sargsyan. The case involves a civil action filed by plaintiffs Krikor Kassabian and Christina Chobanian against the City of Glendale, Sargsyan, and others arising from a motor vehicle collision. The City filed a motion to compel Sargsyan's deposition, which Sargsyan's counsel opposed, citing legal troubles with ICE and other California agencies. The judge granted the City's motion, ordering Sargsyan to appear telephonically for his deposition within 20 days. The judge also ruled that Sargsyan may assert the privilege against self-incrimination on a question-by-question basis.
