TENTATIVE RULING
  RE:  
 
PLAINTIFF’S REQUEST FOR DEFAULT JUDGMENT 
 



 
Plaintiff Insurance Company of the West (“Plaintiff”) filed this action
on June 8, 2023 against Defendant Sudden Valley Holdings (“Defendant”). The
Complaint alleges one cause of action for breach of agreement. 
            On August 1, 2023, default was
entered against Defendant. On August 16, 2023, Plaintiff filed a request for
default judgment against Defendant. 
The Court’s October 11, 2023 minute order in this matter
provides, inter alia, that “Order to Show Cause Re: Review of Default
Judgment submitted 8/16/23 is scheduled for 12/12/23 at 10:00 AM in Department
50 at Stanley Mosk Courthouse.” 
            On November 15, 2023, Defendant
filed a motion for an order to vacate and set aside the default entered
against Defendant, and provide Defendant with leave to file its answer to
Plaintiff’s Complaint. Defendant’s motion is scheduled for hearing on December
18, 2023. 
///
///
///
In light of the foregoing, the
Court continues the hearing on Plaintiff’s request for default judgment to December 18, 2023 at
10:00 a.m. in Dept. 50.
