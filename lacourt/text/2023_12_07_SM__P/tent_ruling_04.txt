Shafner v. Pete Zakery et al., Case No. 23SMCV03220 
Hearing
Date December 7, 2023 
Application
for Admission of Kevin T. Sasse to the Bar Pro Hac Vice 
 
Oregon
attorney Kevin Sasse applies to represent defendant Pete Zakery pro hac vice
in this case. The court has reviewed the application and identified one
issue: 
 
-         
California
Rules of Court Rule 9.40 (b)(4) requires the applicant to state “that the
applicant is not currently suspended or disbarred in any court.” Sasse’s
application states “I have not been disbarred in any court,” but does not state
whether he is currently suspended. Application pg. 2 at 4. 
 
Sasse to
supplement the application to include this information.
