  Case Number:  20STCV34041    Hearing Date:   November 14, 2024    Dept:  57 Pending before the Court is the motion of Defendant Patricia Gucci for an order compelling (1) Plaintiff Alexandra Zarini to produce all documents Dr. James Ethan Rosenberg provided to Plaintiff’s counsel pursuant to Defendant’s deposition subpoena served on Dr. Rosenberg, including but not limited to, documents he used to refresh his recollection during his deposition, and (2) Dr. Rosenberg to sit for a second deposition at which he would be examined about the documents.The Court is granting the motion to the extent that it seeks to compel the production of the documents (notes) that Dr. Rosenberg used to refresh his recollection during his initial deposition.  The Court also is granting the motion to the extent that it seeks to compel Dr. Rosenberg to sit for a second deposition.  The second deposition will be limited to examining Dr. Rosenberg about the documents he used to refresh his recollection at the initial deposition.  Plaintiff is ordered to pay Dr. Rosenberg's hourly fees for his attendance at the second deposition.    Defendant's motion is denied in all other respects, including as to the request for an order imposing sanctions on Plaintiff and her counsel.
