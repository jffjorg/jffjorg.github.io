This
is a petition for approval of transfer of structured settlement. Petitioner One
10ths Finance LLC moves ex parte moves to advance the hearing on petition for
approval for transfer of payment rights, which is currently set for January 23,
2025. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Petitioner’s
counsel states that the current economic conditions present a unique
opportunity for investment that may not be present if the petition is heard on
the original scheduled date. (Decl. Sheida, ¶ 6.) Based on this, the Court
finds that Petitioner met its showing for ex parte relief. 
 
Petitioner’s
ex parte application is GRANTED.  The hearing on the Petition for Approval
of Transfer of Payment Rights is advanced from January 23, 2025 to this date
and continued to May 14, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.
 
Moving
party to give notice to payee.
