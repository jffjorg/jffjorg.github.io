erroneously filed as a Personal Injury ("PI") matter.The Court orders this action referred to the Master Calendar Department, Department 1 for 
review and determination as to whether this case should or should not be reassigned to an 
Independent Calendar ("IC") Court. On the Court's own motion, the Hearing on Demurrer - with Motion to Strike (CCP 430.10) to 
Plaintiff's Complaint scheduled for 09/28/2023, and Hearing on Motion to Strike (not anti SLAPP) - without Demurrer the 2nd Cause of Actions and any Punitive Damages and Other 
Improper Allegations in the 1st and 2nd Causes of Action in Plaintiff's Unverified Complaint 
scheduled for 09/28/2023 are vacated, subject to being reset and notified Re: new hearing date 
by the receiving court at the direction of Department 1. (NOTE: If Department 1 determines this matter to be non-PI, all hearings currently set in 
Department 30 of the Spring Street Courthouse are taken off calendar subject to being reset and 
notified by the receiving court.)Clerk hereby gives notice.
