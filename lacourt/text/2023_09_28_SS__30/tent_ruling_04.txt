 Case Number:  21STCV14715    Hearing Date:   September 28, 2023    Dept:  30 EVELYN PREZA, et al. vs WYNDHAM WORLDWIDE CORPORATION, et al.Motion to Compel Responses to Request for ProductionMotion to Compel Responses to Form InterrogatoriesMotion to Compel Responses to Special InterrogatoriesRuling:  The Court GRANTS Defendant Oh So Original, Inc. f.k.a J.P. Allen, Inc's motions to compel. Plaintiff Evelyn Preza is ORDERED to serve code-complaint written verified responses, without objections, to the: Request for Production, (Set  One); Form Interrogatories, Set One; and Special Interrogatories, Set One within 30 days of  notice of this order. The request for sanctions is denied. Discussion:In support of each of the respective motions, Defendant’s counsel, Frederick L. Stoker, III (“Stoker”) declares that: (1) on March 1, 2023, Defendant served Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, and Requests for Production on Plaintiff (Stoker Decl., ¶ 3); and (2) as of the date of filing of the motions, Defendant has not been served responses to the Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, or Requests for Production from Plaintiff (Id., ¶ 12.) Each of the declarations provided by Stoker attaches the requisite discovery served on Plaintiff as Exhibit A thereto.Defendant has shown a basis for an order compelling responses from Plaintiff without objection pursuant to California Code of Civil Procedure, Sections 2030.290(a) and 2031.300(a).
