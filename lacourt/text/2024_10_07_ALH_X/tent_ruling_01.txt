  Case Number:  21BBCV00436    Hearing Date:   October 7, 2024    Dept:  X DEFENDANTS' MOTION TO BIFURCATE ISSUE OF PUNITIVE DAMAGESDefendants/Cross-Complainants
Sedgwick Claims Management Services, Inc. and The Vons Companies move for an
order bifurcating the issue of punitive damages and to exclude evidence of Vons
and Sedgwick’s financial condition before the punitive damages phase of the
trial.  There is no opposition to the motion.Defendants’
motion to bifurcate the issues of liability and punitive damages and to exclude
evidence of Defendants' financial condition prior to the punitive damages phase
of trial is GRANTED.  (See Civ. Code, § 3295(d).)Counsel for moving parties is to give notice.
