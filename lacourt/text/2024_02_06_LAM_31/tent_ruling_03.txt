MOTIONS

MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Elizabeth Martinez
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant Morbro
Logistics
 
 
This is a disability discrimination, failure to accommodate,
and wrongful termination action. On July 20, 2023, Plaintiff, Elizabeth
Martinez, filed these motions to compel Defendant, Morbro Logistics, to provide
further responses to Plaintiff’s Form Interrogatories-Employment, Set One, and
Request for Production of Documents, Set One.
 
The parties have not participated in an Informal Discovery
Conference (IDC) prior to the filing of these motions.  Accordingly, the court will continue the
hearing for Plaintiff’s motions to March 25, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. to allow the
parties to participate in an IDC.  The
parties are to review the information for conducting IDC’s in Dept. 31 located
on the LA Superior Court website for Dept. 31. 
Thereafter the parties are to contact the judicial assistant to schedule
an IDC date and time.
