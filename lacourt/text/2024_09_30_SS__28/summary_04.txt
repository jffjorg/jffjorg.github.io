The Court has considered the motion of Victor Assouline, the plaintiff, for relief from jury trial waiver. The plaintiff inadvertently failed to post jury fees prior to the commencement of the initial case management conference or within one year of the date of filing the action. Therefore, the Court exercises its discretion to grant the motion. The Court grants the motion of Victor Assouline for relief from jury trial waiver.
