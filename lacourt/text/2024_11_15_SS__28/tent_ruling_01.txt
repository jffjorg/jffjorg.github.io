  Case Number:  21STCV03391    Hearing Date:   November 15, 2024    Dept:  28 On
September 18, 2024, Defendant Lyft Inc. (“Lyft”) filed (1) a motion for judgment on
the pleadings on Plaintiff Haran Jackson’s
second cause of action for products liability and (2) a motion to strike Plaintiff’s
punitive damages claim.  The motions were
set for hearing on November 15, 2024. 
On
November 4, 2024, the Court dismissed Plaintiff’s products liability claim with
prejudice and struck Plaintiff’s punitive damages claim with prejudice based on
the stipulation of Plaintiff and Lyft. 
The November
4, 2024 dismissal renders Lyft’s motion for judgment on the pleadings and
motion to strike moot.  The Court takes
the motions off calendar.
