As for the summary text, here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling:

The Court has considered the demurrer filed by Defendants Joanne Kim and Youngjun Go to the Second Amended Complaint (SAC) filed by Plaintiff Steven Yun dba Lohi General Construction. The Court sustains the demurrer to the first claim for breach of contract without leave to amend due to Plaintiff's lack of licensure at the time of the alleged breach. The Court overrules the demurrer to the second claim for slander per se, finding that the alleged statements by Defendant Go may be actionable. Finally, the Court denies the request for sanctions.
