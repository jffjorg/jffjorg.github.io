  Case Number:  23STCV31447    Hearing Date:   June 26, 2024    Dept:  14 #2Case Background
            Plaintiff
is the assignee of State Compensation Insurance Fund (“Assignor”).  Plaintiff alleges Defendant entered into an
agreement with Assignor for provision of worker’s compensation insurance to
Defendant.  Plaintiff alleges Defendant
failed to pay its insurance premiums.  
 
            On December
26, 2023, Plaintiff filed a complaint against Defendant for (1) breach of
contract; (2) open book account; (3) account stated, and (4) reasonable value. 
 
Defendant has not filed any answer
to the complaint.  Default was entered
against Defendant on May 16, 2024.  
 
Plaintiff’s Request for Entry of Default Judgment
 
Plaintiff submitted a Request for Entry of Default and Default Judgment
on May 16, 2024 asking that the Court enter default and default judgment
against Defendant One10 Solutions Inc. DBA One10 Solutions Inc in the amount of
$323,152.74 based on (1) $289,097.62 in principal damages; (2) $32,276.40 in
interest; (3) $1,200 in attorney’s fees and (4) $578.72 in costs.  
 
Decision
 
            Plaintiff’s Request for Default
Judgment is granted.
