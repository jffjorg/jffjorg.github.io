And here is the English-text summary:

This is a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to Requests for Production of Documents (RFPs) filed by Plaintiff Rakeia Pratt against Defendant Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC. The Plaintiff is seeking to compel further responses to RFPs 1-32, alleging that the Defendant failed to provide adequate responses and production. The Defendant objected to the motion, arguing that some of the RFPs were overly broad and that the Plaintiff did not provide sufficient good cause to support the request. The Court has granted the motion as to RFPs 1-15 and 32, finding that the Defendant's objections were evasive and that the Plaintiff showed good cause for the request. The Court has denied the motion as to RFPs 16-23, finding that the Plaintiff did not provide sufficient good cause for the request. The Court will hear argument on RFP 31.
