             Defendant Transdev Services, Inc. (“Transdev”)
moves pursuant to Civil Code section 3295(d) to bifurcate the claim for
punitive damages asserted by Plaintiff Shanna Charles (“Plaintiff”).  Plaintiff has not filed an opposition.
 
            The Court
GRANTS Transdev’s motion. The issue of whether Transdev is liable to Plaintiff
for punitive damages will be tried separate from the other issues concerning
liability and compensatory damages.
 
            Transdev
shall give notice.
