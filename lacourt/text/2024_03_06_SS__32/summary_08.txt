The tentative ruling is in regards to a motion filed by Plaintiff Rosa Yen to compel further responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, propounded on Defendant Ernesto Ramos Esparza. The motion was filed after Defendant served responses with objections on December 13, 2022. The Court finds that the objections lack merit and grants the motion to compel further responses.

The Court also awards sanctions in the amount of $1,260 against Defendant and his counsel of record, representing an hourly rate of $600 and the $60 filing fee. The sanctions are to be paid to counsel for Plaintiff within 30 days of the date of this order.

The Court orders that Defendant shall serve further responses, without objections, within 10 days.
