Defendant’s

            On 5/15/2024, Plaintiff Olivia Dizon (Plaintiff) filed
suit against Jacqueline Sauls, Frederick Sauls, and Karoline Sauls, alleging:
(1) failure to pay wages; (2) waiting time wage continuation; (3) failure to
provide accurate itemized wage statements; (4) failure to indemnify and
reimburse in violation of Labor Code section 2802; and (5) unfair business
practices. 
 
            On 8/6/2024, Defendants Jacqueline and Fredrick Sauls
(collectively, Defendants) demurred to Plaintiff’s Complaint. Defendants also
moved to strike portions of Plaintiff’s Complaint. 
 
            In lieu of an opposition, Plaintiff filed a first amended
complaint (FAC). 
 
            Based on the foregoing, Defendant’s demurrer is moot.
Accordingly, Defendant’s motion to strike is also moot. 
 
It is so ordered.
