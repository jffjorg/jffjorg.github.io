The judge has granted Progressive Express Insurance Company's motion for leave to intervene in the case GEICO General Insurance Company v. Link Engineering Incorporated. The case arises out of an automobile accident that occurred on September 8, 2020. Progressive is seeking to intervene because it is the insurance carrier for the vehicle driven by the defendant's employee and Chief Executive Officer, and is potentially liable for any judgment entered against the defendant. The judge found that Progressive has a sufficient basis to intervene under Code of Civil Procedure section 387, and has satisfied all applicable procedural requirements. The motion is granted, and Progressive is granted leave to file an Answer in Intervention within 7 days of the hearing.
