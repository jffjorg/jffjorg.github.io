 Case Number:  21STCV43901    Hearing Date:   November 9, 2023    Dept:  28  Having reviewed Plaintiff
Cedric Taite’s unopposed motions to compel responses to form interrogatories, special Interrogatories, request
for production of documents, and requests for admission, the Court rules as
follows.
            On June 6, 2023, Plaintiff Cedric Taite served form interrogatories, set one, special interrogatories, set one, request for production of documents, set one, and requests for admission, set one, on Defendant J.J. Kane and
Associates Inc..
            Responses were due on July 10, 2023. Defendant J.J.
Kane and Associates Inc. failed to provide timely responses. Plaintiff Cedric
Taite now moves to compel code-compliant responses.
            The Court grants the motions to compel responses
to the form and special interrogatories.  (Code Civ. Proc., §
2030.290, subd. (b).)  The Court orders Defendant J.J. Kane and Associates Inc. to provide
verified responses to the interrogatories compliant with Code of Civil Procedure sections 2030.210, subdivision (a), and 2030.220 without objections by December 9, 2023.  The Court grants the motion to compel responses to requests for admission.  (Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280.)  The Court orders Defendant J.J. Kane and Associates
Inc. to provide verified responses to the requests for admission compliant with Code of
Civil Procedure sections 2033.210, subdivision (a), and 2033.220 without objections by December 9, 2023.  The Court grants the motion to compel responses to the
request for production of documents.  (Code Civ. Proc., §
2031.300.)  The Court orders Defendant J.J. Kane and Associates Inc. to provide verified
responses compliant with Code of Civil Procedure section 2031.210 and to produce the
documents, electronically stored information, and/or other things requested
without objections by December 9, 2023. Moving party is to give notice of this
ruling.
