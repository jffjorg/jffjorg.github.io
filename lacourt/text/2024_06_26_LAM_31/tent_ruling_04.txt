CASE:                         Libra Securities

DEMURRER
WITH MOTION TO STRIKE

 
MOVING PARTIES:           Plaintiffs
Libra Securities Holdings, LLC and Jess Ravich
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     Defendant
Jennifer Robles
 
 
            On October
2, 2023, Plaintiffs Libra Securities Holdings, LLC (“Libra”) and Jess Ravich
(“Ravich”) sued Defendant, Jennifer Robles, for damages and injunctive relief.  
 
            On October
31, 2023, Plaintiffs filed a motion to compel arbitration.  The motion was heard on March 22, 2024.  After oral argument, the court took the
matter under submission.
 
            On March
25, 2024—while the motion to compel arbitration was under submission—Defendant
filed a Cross-Complaint against Plaintiffs and TCW Group, Inc., for sexual
harassment, wrongful termination, and breach of contract.  
 
            On March
26, 2024, the court issued its ruling denying Plaintiffs’ motion to compel
arbitration.
 
            On April
22, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a Demurrer and Motion to Strike Defendant’s
Cross-Complaint.
 
            On May 6,
2024, Plaintiffs filed a Notice of Appeal of the court’s March 26, 2024 ruling,
and concurrently filed a Motion for Stay of Proceedings.  The court granted the Motion for Stay of
Proceedings on May 31, 2024.
 
            On June 12,
2024, Defendant filed an opposition to the Demurrer and Motion to Strike and
concurrently filed an Amended Cross-Complaint.     
 
            Given that
the court granted Plaintiffs’ motion to stay proceedings in this matter, the  Demurrer and Motion to Strike are taken off
calendar and may be renoticed after the stay is lifted.           The Court Clerk to give notice.
 
Dated:   June 26, 2024
