 Case Number:  23VECV05528    Hearing Date:   April 4, 2024    Dept:  T 23VECV05528 McCall v. GM
Tentative ruling:  The
motion to quash is granted.  Defendant
provided evidence that General Motors Company is not a resident of California.
General Motors Company does not conduct business in California, maintain any
offices or employees in California, or market in California.  Plaintiff provided no evidence to the
contrary.  The court was not provided
with statutory authority to dismiss this case. 
Accordingly, that request is denied.
