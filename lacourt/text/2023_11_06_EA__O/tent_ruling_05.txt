 Case Number:  23PSCV02515    Hearing Date:   November 6, 2023    Dept:  O Tentative Ruling
 
Petitioner
Christina Montano’s PETITION FOR APPROVAL OF COMPROMISE OF CLAIIN OR
ACTION OR DISPOSITION OF PROCEEDS OF JUDGMENT FOR MINOR/CLAIMANT Aliyah
Richards is GRANTED. 
 
Background
 
This case arises from a slip and fall. 
 
On August 17, 2023, Plaintiff ALIYAH RICHARDS, a minor by
and through her guardian ad Litem, CHRISTINA MONTANO filed suit against
Defendant CHEDRAUI USA, INC for negligence. 
 
On October 20, 2023, the instant petition was filed. 
 
Legal
Standard
 
CCP
§372(a)(1) provides: 
 
When a minor, a person who lacks legal
capacity to make decisions, or a person for whom a conservator has been
appointed is a party, that person shall appear either by a guardian or
conservator of the estate or by guardian ad litem appointed by the court in which the
action or proceeding is pending, or by a judge thereof, in each case.” 
 
Discussion
 
Petitioner brings forth the petition on behalf of Aliyah, who
is a minor. As a result of the 2/16/22 accident wherein the minor slipped on a
clear liquid substance and fell. She suffered from a right forearm displaced
radius shaft re-fracture. Claimant was treated with a closed reduction and long
arm case immobilization. The total settlement is $17,500.00. After attorney
fees and other expenses, claimant will be entitled to $11,331.91. With no
defects noted, the court grants the petition. 
 
Conclusion
 
Based on the foregoing, the petition is granted.
