Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge sustains the demurrer filed by the Defendants, Arrowhead Financial Group, Todd J. Schellback, Cal Trustee Group, Trustee of the Arrowhead Financial Group Trust, and Michael Henschel, to the second amended complaint brought by the Plaintiffs, Michael Yoakum and Highland Associates, a Limited Partnership. The judge finds that the Plaintiffs have not pleaded sufficient facts to show that the statute of limitations does not bar their claim. However, the judge finds that the Plaintiffs have sufficiently pleaded fraud in the inducement, financial elder abuse, cancellation of an irrevocable trust, and quiet title. The judge also finds that paragraphs of the second amended complaint are not vague or ambiguous and that the complaint is not contradictory. The judge allows the Plaintiffs to amend their complaint to cure any defects.
