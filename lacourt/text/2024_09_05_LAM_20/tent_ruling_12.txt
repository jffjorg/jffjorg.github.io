 The First Amended Complaint in this action was filed on December 19, 2022.Defendant Aetna Life Insurance Company filed its Answer on January 18, 2023.Defendant Aetna Life Insurance Company now seeks for the Court to allow it to file a late demurrer because a demurrer could resolve this action.CCP 473(a)(1) states, “The court . . . may, upon like terms, enlarge the time for answer or demurrer. . . .” To the extent an answer was already filed, the Court believes that a demurrer has been forfeited and that Defendant has the option to file a motion for judgment on the pleadings instead.  (See CCP § 438(f)(2).)  Thus, the Motion is denied.
