 Case Number:  23NWCV01574    Hearing Date:   October 23, 2024    Dept:  C Jones v. Kaiser Foundation Health Plan, Inc. 24NWCV01574
 
This
premises liability action was filed on May 20, 2024. There is a Motion to
Compel Arbitration set for hearing on March 6, 2025. 
 
Defendants
apply ex parte for an order staying this action until the Court has determined
Defendants' Motion to Compel Arbitration and Stay the Action, or alternatively,
for an order shortening time to hear a Motion to Stay. 
 
Defendants'
Ex Parte Application for an Order Staying this Action until the Court has
Determined Defendants' Motion to Compel Arbitration and Stay the Action is GRANTED
under CCP 1281.4. "If an application has been made to a court of
competent jurisdiction for an order to arbitrate a controversy... and such
application is undetermined, the court in which such action or proceeding is
pending shall, upon motion of a party to such action or proceeding, stay the
action or proceeding until the application for an order to arbitrate is
determined." (CCP 1281.4.) This action is STAYED until a
determination is made on Defendants' Motion to Compel Arbitration and Stay the
Action. Moving Party to give notice.
