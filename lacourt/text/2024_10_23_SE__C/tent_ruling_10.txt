 Case Number:  24NWCV02787    Hearing Date:   October 23, 2024    Dept:  C Simin LP v. Wells International Marketing Group, Inc. 24NWCV02787
 
This
commercial unlawful detainer action was filed on August 26, 2024. There is a
Demurrer scheduled for hearing on August 6, 2025. 
 
Plaintiff
moves ex parte to advance the hearing date on Defendant's Demurrer and to set a
trial date under CCP 1170.5. CCP 1179a states: "In all proceedings brought
to recover the possession of real property pursuant to the provisions of this
chapter all courts, wherein such actions are or may hereafter be pending, shall
give such actions precedence over all other civil actions therein....to the end
that all such actions shall be quickly heard and determined." (Id.) 
 
Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Application to Advance the Hearing Date on the Demurrer is GRANTED.
Defendant's Demurrer is ADVANCED from Wednesday, August 6, 2025 to
today's date and CONTINUED to Wednesday, November 13, 2024 at 9:30
a.m. in Dept. SE-C. Opposition and Reply to be filed and served per Code in
accordance with the new hearing date. 
 
Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Request to Set a Trial Date under CCP 1170.5 is DENIED without
prejudice. This request is properly made in Dept. SE-F. The Court notes
that a Notice of Rejection of Electronic Filing was issued on October 17, 2024,
rejecting Plaintiff's Request to Set this Case for Trial. 
 
Moving
Party to give notice.
