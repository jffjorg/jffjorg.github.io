The Judge has granted the motion filed by Parker Group, Inc. adba Parker Group Technologies, Inc. to enforce the Settlement Agreement and enter judgment against Anthony Alcazar. The parties had entered into a Settlement Agreement on August 4, 2023, which required Mr. Tortilla, Inc. and Anthony Alcazar to make certain payments. However, Mr. Tortilla, Inc. defaulted under the Settlement Agreement and Anthony Alcazar did not oppose or respond to the motion.

The Judge relied on California Code of Civil Procedure (CCP) section 664.6(a), which allows the court to enter judgment pursuant to the terms of a settlement agreement if the parties have stipulated to the settlement in writing or orally. The Judge found that the parties had entered a valid and binding settlement agreement and that Anthony Alcazar had defaulted under the agreement.

As a result, the Judge ordered that judgment be entered in favor of Parker Group, Inc. adba Parker Group Technologies, Inc. against Anthony Alcazar in the amount of $172,500.00, which represents the remaining balance due under the Settlement Agreement.
