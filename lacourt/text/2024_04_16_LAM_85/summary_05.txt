The Court has considered the application for a writ of possession and turnover order against Yoelin Machado. The application was filed by Ally Bank, the holder of a first priority security interest in a 2016 Maserati Ghibli motor vehicle. Machado breached the contract on March 11, 2023, by failing to make the $478.49 monthly installment due. The balance due and owing is $21,607.94. The Court grants the application and orders the issuance of a writ of possession and turnover order. The plaintiff must file a proposed order for writ of possession and turnover order within two court days of this hearing. The redelivery bond will be $46,186. The levying officer may enter a private residence at 157 E 93rd St, Los Angeles, CA 90003, as well as any commercial or public address.
