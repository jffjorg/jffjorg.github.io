 Case Number:  20STCV39597    Hearing Date:   September 29, 2023    Dept:  30 WILLIE PERRYMON vs KYLE SHORES, et al.Tentative Ruling:  he Motion to Compel Willie Perrymon’s Responses to Supplemental Interrogatories (Set One), the Motion to Compel Willie Perrymon’s Responses to Special Interrogatories (Set One), and the Motion to Compel Willie Perrymon’s Responses to Supplemental Request for Production (Set One) are all GRANTED.  Plaintiff is ordered to serve responses, without objection, to each of the discovery requests within 20 days.  The request for sanctions is denied. Moving party to give notice.Discussion:  All three Motions contain the Declaration of Mark P. Lascola (“Lascola Dec.”) which provides the same information. On December 22, 2022, Defendant served Plaintiff with multiple sets of discovery which included Supplemental Interrogatories (Set One), Special Interrogatories (Set One), and Supplemental Request for Production of Documents (Set One). (Lascola Dec., ¶ 5.) After no responses were received by the deadline, Defendant followed up on January 25, 2023, but again received no response from Plaintiff. (Id. at ¶ 6.) Therefore, the Court will grant all three motions. The Court declines to award sanctions as the motions are unopposed.
