The court has considered the motions for summary judgment and summary adjudication filed by the San Marino Unified School District (SMUSD) in response to the complaint filed by John Doe, alleging negligence and breach of duty by SMUSD in connection with the sexual abuse of John Doe by Howard Cheung, a teacher at the school.

The court has granted the motions for summary adjudication on the fourth and sixth causes of action, finding that there is no triable issue of material fact regarding the negligent hiring, supervision, and retention of an unfit employee, and negligent supervision of a minor. The court has denied the motions for summary adjudication on the fifth and seventh causes of action, finding that there are triable issues of material fact regarding breach of mandatory duty, failure to report suspected child abuse, and negligent failure to warn, train or educate.

The court has set trial for June 24, 2024.
