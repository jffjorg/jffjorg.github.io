  Case Number:  21SMCV00608    Hearing Date:   October 24, 2024    Dept:  N TENTATIVE RULINGMoses S. Bardavid’s Motions to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff/Cross-Defendant Papillones, LLC and Cross-Defendant Michael Hakim are GRANTED.Counsel has asserted a valid reason for withdrawal, i.e., the attorney-client relationship has broken down. (Rules Prof. Conduct, rule 1.16(b)(4).) Counsel has provided the required forms under rule 3.1362 of the California Rules of Court and served the opposing parties and client with the same. There being good cause to grant counsel’s request to withdraw, Moses S. Bardavid’s Motions to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff/Cross-Defendant Papillones, LLC and Cross-Defendant Michael Hakim are GRANTED. This ruling granting counsel’s motion to withdraw shall become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon Plaintiff/Cross-Defendant Papillones, LLC and Cross-Defendant Michael Hakim. Plaintiff/Cross-Defendant Papillones, LLC is reminded that a corporation cannot represent itself in litigation and must be represented by licensed counsel. (CLD Construction, Inc. v. City of San Ramon (2004) 120 Cal.App.4th 1141, 1145.)
