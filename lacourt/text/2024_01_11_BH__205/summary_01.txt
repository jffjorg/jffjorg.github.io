And here is the English text summary:

This tentative ruling is related to a petition to enforce a subpoena for production of documents in connection with an action outside of California. The action, known as the Missouri Action, arises from the sale of a property management business in February 2018. The respondent, St. Cloud Cap. Partners SBIC, L.P., a Los Angeles-based private equity firm, had a subordinated promissory note with the petitioner, Worth Clark, Inc. Worth Clark alleges that St. Cloud failed to disclose the default of the senior note at the time of the sale and seeks sanctions for St. Cloud's failure to meet and confer and produce the responsive documents. The court concludes that there is good cause to grant the petition and orders St. Cloud to pay $4,000 in sanctions to Worth Clark.
