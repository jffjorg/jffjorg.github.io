The Judge's Tentative Ruling approves the settlement and stipulated consent judgment between CALSAFE RESEARCH CENTER, INC. (Plaintiff) and OVERSEAS FOOD DISTRIBUTION, LLC (Defendant) regarding Proposition 65 violations.
