This is a tentative ruling by the court in a case between Plaintiff Fred Stalley and Defendant Todd C. Morse dba T. Cabot Construction. The Defendant filed a motion to compel responses to discovery requests, including Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, and Requests for Production, which were served on February 7, 2024. The Plaintiff failed to respond to the requests, and the Defendant filed a motion for sanctions in the amount of $1,400.00 for each motion. The court granted the motion to compel responses and reduced the amount of sanctions to $1,200.00. The court ordered the Plaintiff to serve responses to the discovery requests within 20 days and the Plaintiff and Plaintiff's counsel to pay sanctions in the amount of $1,200.00 to the Defendant within 30 days.
