And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has denied the defendant's ex parte application to quash a deposition subpoena and production of banking records for Kumi Kawamura. The defendant failed to meet the requirement of making an affirmative factual showing in a declaration, as required by California Rules of Court, rule 3.1202, subdivision (c). The judge has given the defendant an opportunity to file a motion to quash or modify the subpoena on regular notice. In the meantime, Bank of America is not required to produce records in response to the subpoena concerning Kumi Kawamura before September 12, 2024. The judge has ordered the defendant to notify all parties and Bank of America of this order.
