And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has ruled in favor of the Plaintiff, Welding Evolution, Inc., and against the Defendant, Eric Cortes. The Plaintiff had served the Defendant with Requests for Production of Documents, but the Defendant failed to respond timely. The Defendant eventually served responses, but they were not code-compliant and contained improper objections. The Judge has ordered the Defendant to provide code-compliant, objection-free responses to each individual request for production. Additionally, the Judge has granted the Plaintiff's request for sanctions in the amount of $2,175.00 against the Defendant and his counsel for the motion.
