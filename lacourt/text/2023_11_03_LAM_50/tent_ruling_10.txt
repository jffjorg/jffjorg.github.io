TENTATIVE RULING
  RE:
 
APPLICATION FOR
  ADMISSION OF PAUL MORDARSKI TO THE BAR OF THIS COURT PRO HAC VICE



 
Defendants Michael J. Forde III, ERC Consulting Experts, LLC,
In Media LLC, and Positive Insights Consulting, LLC (collectively, “Defendants”) request that the Court grant the
application of Paul Mordarski to appear as their counsel pro hac vice in the
instant action. 
The application
is submitted with an “Application for Admission of Paul Mordarski to the Bar of
this Court Pro Hac Vice” made under penalty of perjury that is in
compliance with the requirements set forth in California Rules of Court,
rule 9.40, subdivision (d). Accordingly, the unopposed application is granted.
Defendants are ordered to provide notice of
this ruling.
