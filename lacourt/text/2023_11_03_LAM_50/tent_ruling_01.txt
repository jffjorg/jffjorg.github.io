TENTATIVE RULING
  RE:  
 
PLAINTIFF’S REQUEST FOR DEFAULT JUDGMENT 
 



 
            Plaintiff Tina Marie Barrales (“Plaintiff”) requests that the Court
enter default judgment against Defendant Mundo Terra CA LLC. Plaintiff seeks judgment
in the total amount of $35,362.63, comprising $12,183.12 in damages, $1,929.51 in interest, $250.00 in costs,
and $21,000.00 in attorney’s fees. 
The
Court finds that Plaintiff has sufficiently supported her request with the
submitted declarations and evidence. Therefore, Plaintiff’s request is granted,
and the Court will sign the judgment. No appearance at the hearing is
necessary.¿¿
