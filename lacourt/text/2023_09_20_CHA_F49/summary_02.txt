The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel responses to form interrogatories served on the defendant, Anthony Chiang. The plaintiff, Milad Nowrozani, alleges that an agreement was made to purchase an Audemars Piguet watch for $28,000, but a counterfeit watch was delivered instead. The defendant refused to provide a refund. The plaintiff filed a complaint and served form interrogatories on the defendant, but no responses were received. The motion is unopposed, and the judge has ordered the defendant to serve verified responses to the interrogatories within ten days. Additionally, the judge has imposed sanctions of $250 against the defendant, payable within 30 days. The motions to compel will be heard on September 21, 2023.
