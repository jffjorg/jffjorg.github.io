And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has denied the motion filed by the plaintiffs (Farid Zahreddine and Caroline Zahreddine) to compel the deposition of General Motors, LLC's Person(s) Most Knowledgeable and to produce documents requested in the deposition notice. The judge found that the plaintiffs failed to establish good cause for the production of the documents and failed to comply with the requirements of CCP 2025.450 and CRC 3.1345. Specifically, the judge noted that the plaintiffs did not provide a separate statement as required by CRC 3.1345, failed to meet and confer after the scheduled deposition date, and failed to provide authority to support their argument that the meet and confer efforts prior to noticing the deposition satisfy the requirements of CCP 2025.450(b). The judge also denied the plaintiffs' request for sanctions against General Motors, LLC in the amount of $6,463.26.
