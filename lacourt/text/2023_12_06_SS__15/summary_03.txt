This is a tentative ruling on a demurrer and motion to strike filed by Mary Kay Inc. in a case filed by Silvia Roasas, Elsa Herrera, Carlos Rosas, Santos Rosas, and Marta Ruvulcaba alleging that the company's products caused Otilia Rosas' exposure to asbestos. The Judge has ruled that the third and fourth causes of action are defective and vague, and that the request for punitive damages is conclusory and lacks specific facts about the company's conduct. The Judge has sustained the demurrer to these causes of action and granted the motion to strike the request for punitive damages. The Plaintiffs will be given the opportunity to amend their complaint at the hearing, but must specify how they intend to remedy the defects in their pleading.
