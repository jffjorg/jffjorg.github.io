The Judge's tentative ruling grants the Motion to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories filed by the Defendant, Emil J. Simanian, and grants in part the request for monetary sanctions. The Plaintiff, Liliana Castillo Onofre, failed to serve timely responses to the Form Interrogatories, and the Defendant's counsel attested that the responses were due on April 1, 2024. The Judge finds that the Plaintiff waived any objection to the interrogatories and grants the motion to compel responses.
