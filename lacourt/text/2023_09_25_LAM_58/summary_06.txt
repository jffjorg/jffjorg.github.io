This is a tentative ruling by Judge Bruce G. Iwasaki regarding two motions filed by Defendant Farmers Group, Inc. to award costs against Plaintiffs M.L. Enterprise Textiles, LLC and Rotax, Inc. in the amounts of $7,522.68 and $4,753.86, respectively. The motions were unopposed, and the Judge granted them based on the fact that the Plaintiffs failed to file timely motions to tax costs. The Judge noted that the Plaintiffs had filed requests for dismissal of the Defendant, which were granted, and that the Defendant had filed memoranda of costs, which were not opposed. The Judge cited various California laws and court rules to support the ruling, including Code of Civil Procedure sections 1032 and 1033.5, and California Rules of Court, Rule 3.1700(b).
