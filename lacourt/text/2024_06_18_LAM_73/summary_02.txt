This tentative ruling by Judge Rolf Treu concerns the case of Puig v. Goodpeople Roasting LLC, et al. (23STCV27674). The Plaintiff, Eric Puig, brought this action individually and on behalf of all other aggrieved employees against the Defendants, Goodpeople Roasting LLC and GP Group LLC. The Plaintiff alleges that the Defendants own and operate coffee bars and restaurants, which also operate as marijuana dispensaries, in Southern California. The Plaintiff and other employees worked at these locations, and the Defendants allegedly violated California wage and hour laws.

The Plaintiff filed motions to compel the Defendants' response to special interrogatories, Set One, and request for production of documents, Set One. The Plaintiff also requested monetary sanctions against the Defendants and their counsel. The Court granted the motions to compel and awarded monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,750. The Court also ordered the Defendants to provide verified responses to the special interrogatories and request for production of documents within 30 days.
