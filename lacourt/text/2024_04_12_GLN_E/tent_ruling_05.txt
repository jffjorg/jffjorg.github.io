RULING
There
is a motion on calendar for 4/12/2024 at 9:00am that is titled on eCourt as
“Hearing on Motion for Order Receiver’s Motion for Further Instructions and
Approval of Interim Fees and Costs.” 
On 3/27/2024, Plaintiff City of Duarte, Defendants Levon H.
Bardakjian and Sylvia R. Bardakjian, and Former Receiver Mark Adams and
California Receivership Group Inc. (Former Receiver) filed a stipulation to
continue this hearing to May 3, 2024. 
The Stipulation states:
1. WHEREAS Former Receiver’s Ex Parte Application for
Instructions Re November 29, 2023 Attorney Fee Order (“Former Receiver
Application”) is scheduled for hearing in this department on April 12, 2024, at
9:00 a.m.; 
2. WHEREAS Court-Appointed Receiver Eric P. Beatty’s (“the
Receiver”) Motion for Further Instructions and Approval of Interim Fees and
Costs (“Receiver Motion”) has not yet been filed, but is also scheduled for
hearing in this department on April 12, 2024, at 9:00 a.m.; 
3. WHEREAS the Receiver has requested that the hearings
regarding the Former Receiver Application and Receiver Motion, currently
scheduled for April 12, 2024 be continued to May 3, 2024, at 9:00 a.m., or such
other date as is convenient for the Court as the Receiver is currently on
medical leave until approximately April 1, 2024.
 
The Court will discuss scheduling with the parties.
