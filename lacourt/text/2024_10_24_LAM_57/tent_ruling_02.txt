 Case Number:  22STCV01760    Hearing Date:   October 24, 2024    Dept:  57 The demurrer of the Bells to the Donovans' Second Amended Cross-Complaint is sustained without leave to amend as to the first cause of action for reformation of deeds, the fifth cause of action for adverse possession, and the sixth cause of action for prescriptive easement.  The demurrer is overruled as to the seventh cause of action for equitable easement, the eighth cause of action for loss of use, and the ninth cause of action for declaratory relief.  The Court will discuss these rulings with the parties at the hearing on the demurrer.
