 Case Number:  23SMCV02259    Hearing Date:   February 6, 2024    Dept:  205 Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles – West District Beverly Hills Courthouse / Department 205   CHRISTINA HWA,   Plaintiff, v.  MCDONALD’S RESTAURANT OF CALIFORNIA, INC., et al.,   Defendants.    Case No.:  23SMCV02259     Hearing Date:  February 6, 2024      [TENTATIVE] ORDER RE:   PLAINTIFF’S MOTION TO COMPEL    DEFENDANTS’ FURTHER RESPONSES    TO REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION OF    DOCUMENTS (SET TWO)     This hearing is on Plaintiff Christina Hwa’s motion to compel Defendant McDonald’s Restaurant of California Inc.’s further responses to Requests for Production of Documents (Set Two).   On a motion to compel further responses, this Department’s rules require the moving party to file a joint statement consisting of four columns: the first column will identify the number of the discovery request; the second, the text of the discovery request; the third, the text of the response, and the fourth, brief bullet-point statements, one from each party as to why a further response should or should not be compelled.  No joint statement was filed.   Based on the foregoing, the Court DENIES without prejudice Plaintiff’s motion to compel further responses to her requests for production of documents (set two).      IT IS SO ORDERED.  DATED: February 6, 2024    ___________________________ Edward B. Moreton, Jr. Judge of the Superior Court
