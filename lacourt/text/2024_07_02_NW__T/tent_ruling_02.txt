Fernandez v Mulholland
Tentative
ruling:  Motion to withdraw – Grant as to
Mr. Ben David and continued as to Mulholland. 
An LLC cannot be self represented. The motion as to Mulholland will be
continued for to 9/19/2024 at 8:30 a.m. with notice to Mulholland that if it
does not have an attorney by the continued date, the court will strike the
answer and enter default for failure of the LLC to have an attorney.  Counsel must file proof of service of the conformed
order on Mr. Ben David.  She will remain
counsel of record until that proof of service is filed.
