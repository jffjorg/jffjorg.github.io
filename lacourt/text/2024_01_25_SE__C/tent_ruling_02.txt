 Case Number:  21NWCV00633    Hearing Date:   January 25, 2024    Dept:  C PRISCILLA OLAES vs RD RESTAURANT GROUP, INC., et al.,
Case no. 21NWCV00633
The estate of deceased Plaintiff Priscilla Olaes files an ex
parte application for an order authorizing Emmanuel Olaes, Roslyn Granger, the
Olaes Family Trust Dated June 25, 1991 to continue the instant action on behalf
of the Plaintiff due to the death of the Plaintiff. On December 21, 2023, Mrs.
Olaes passed away. Four of the seven sets of Loan Documents provide that upon
Mrs. Olaes’ death, they are to be made payable to Emmanuel Olaes and Roslyn
Granger. The other three sets of Loan Documents provide that upon Mrs. Olaes’
death, they are to be made payable to the Olaes Family Trust Dated June 25,
1991 (the “Trust”). Emmanuel Olaes is the Trustee of the Trust (the “Trust”). A
pending action does not abate by reason of the death of a party if the cause of
action survives. Code Civ. Proc. § 377.21.
 
“The court in
which an action is commenced or continued under this article may make any order
concerning parties that is appropriate to ensure proper administration of
justice in the case, including appointment of the decedent’s successor in
interest as a special administrator or guardian ad litem.” (Cal. Code
Civ. Proc. § 377.33.)
 
The Court finds that such an order may be Granted, given the
passing of Plaintiff. The loan documents presented show that the loans are to
be made payable to the respective parties. 
 
Accordingly, the ex parte application is GRANTED.
