 Case Number:  22NWCV01586    Hearing Date:   January 25, 2024    Dept:  C JESSICA MARIE PERRIN vs JOE'S SWEEPING, INC., et al.,
Case No. 22NWCV01586
Defendant Joe’s Sweeping Inc. applies ex parte to advance
the hearing date for its motion to compel discovery, currently scheduled for
August 14, 2024; or in the alternative to continue trial, currently scheduled
for November 15, 2024.  Defendant seeks to compel the production of
medical records so Defendant’s expert can review the records and complete the
discovery process.  A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative
factual showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other
statutory basis for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. R. Ct.
3.1202(c).)  Defendant’s ex parte application is GRANTED.  The Motion
to Compel Discovery is advanced to this date and continued to March 27, 2024
at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.  Moving party to give notice.
