  Case Number:  22VECV01213    Hearing Date:   January 30, 2024    Dept:  T TENTATIVE]  ORDER:  Plaintiff Forwardline Financial,  LLC’s Motion to Vacate the Dismissal and Enter Judgment per Stipulation is  GRANTED.   IntroductionPlaintiff Forwardline  Financial, LLC (Plaintiff) moved to vacate the dismissal and enter judgment  against Defendants Neil Anthony Scotti and Neil A. Scotti Law Offices  (Defendants).  (Code Civ. Proc. sec.,  664.6.)   Plaintiff requested entry of  judgment in the amount totaling $30,230.44, which included $495 in costs (i.e.,  $435.00 filing fee and $60 motion fee).   Opposition was due January 17, 2024 and none was filed.                  DiscussionIf  parties to pending litigation stipulate, in a signed writing for settlement of  the case, the court, upon motion, may enter judgment pursuant to the terms of  the settlement. (Code Civ. Proc. sec. 664.6.)   Plaintiff submitted facts showing entry into the settlement agreement  with Defendants, Defendants’ obligation to make monthly payments, Defendants  failure to pay as of July 2023; Plaintiff’s notice to Defendants of the  default; Plaintiff’s damages; and the signed stipulation for the Court to  retain jurisdiction prior to dismissal.   (Jon Blanda Decl. pars. 2-10, Exhs. 1 and 2).  Based upon these facts, Plaintiff presented  good cause in support of the requested relief. The  motion to vacate the dismissal and enter judgment in favor of Plaintiff is  GRANTED IT IS SO ORDERED, THE COURT WILL SIGN THE JUDGMENT, CLERK TO ENTER AND GIVE NOTICE OF ENTRY.
