This is a habitability matter.  Defendants 3J1M LLC, Ironwood Velvet LLC, and Aperto Property Management Inc. seek to compel further responses to their requests for production, set one, propounded on Plaintiffs Dawnielle Desensi, Shyann Redmond, and Alexis Redmond.The discovery at issue is basic and pertains to documents showing defective conditions and damages arising therefrom.  Further, the objections are all unsubstantiated.  The Court notes, however, that for request no. 6 Plaintiffs need not provide conversations with their attorneys.  In sum, the Motion to Compel is granted.  Further responses and production are to be provided within 30 days.  The request for $1,005 in sanctions is granted.  Moving parties to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
