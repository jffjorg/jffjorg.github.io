Summary:
The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the motion to quash subpoenas issued by the Plaintiff, Walter Pearce, against the Defendant, Gardena Retirement Center, Inc. dba Gardena Retirement Center. The subpoenas seek records from the Community Care Licensing Division (CCLD) regarding the Defendant's alleged negligence in caring for the Plaintiff, who sustained at least second-degree burns in a bathtub. The Defendant argued that the subpoenas are overbroad, irrelevant, and contain privileged information, but the Judge finds that the records are discoverable under the limited scope of July 1, 2021 to March 1, 2022. The Judge also finds that the Defendant's privacy concerns can be mitigated through redaction and deletion of personal identifying information. The motion to quash is denied, and the scope of the subpoenas is narrowed to the specified time period.
