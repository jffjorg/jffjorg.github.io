 Case Number:  22STCV19604    Hearing Date:   January 16, 2024    Dept:  29 Motion for Summary Judgment filed by Defendant Fire Insurance Exchange,
erroneously sued as Farmers Insurance Company.

 
Discussion 
 
On June
15, 2022, Plaintiffs Goerge Bellgardt and Sylvia Bellgardt (collectively
“Plaintiffs”) filed this action against Defendants A C Roofing, Farmers
Insurance Company, and DOES 1 to 40 for (1) Negligence, (2) Private Nuisance,
(3) Breach of Contract, (4) Breach of the Implied Covenant of Good Faith and
Fair Dealing, (5) Elder Abuse, Welfare Code section 15600, et seq.
 
Defendant Fire Insurance Exchange, erroneously sued as Farmers Insurance
Company, (“Defendant”) filed its motion for summary judgment on October 27,
2023. 
 
On November 6, 2023, Plaintiffs dismissed Defendant.
 
As such, Defendant’s motion for summary judgment is DENIED as MOOT.
 
Conclusion
 
The Court DENIES as MOOT Defendant’s motion
for summary judgment.
 
The Court will give notice.
