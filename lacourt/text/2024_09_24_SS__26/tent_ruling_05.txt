 Case Number:  23STLC07821    Hearing Date:   September 24, 2024    Dept:  26  
Schroeder
v. Rodriguez, et al.MOTION
TO QUASH SERVICE(CCP § 418.10)
TENTATIVE RULING:
 
Defendant Carlos Rodriguez’s
Motion to Quash Service of the Summons and Complaint is PLACED OFF CALENDAR AS
MOOT.
 
 
ANALYSIS: 
 

On December 11, 2023, Plaintiff Aaron
Michael Schroeder (“Plaintiff”) filed the Complaint in this action against
Defendant Carlos Rodriguez (“Defendant”). Plaintiff filed proof of substitute
service on July 17, 2024. Defendant filed the instant Motion to Quash Service
of the Summons and Complaint on August 9, 2024. 
 
On September 6, 2024, Plaintiff
filed a proof of personal service and an opposition to the instant Motion. In
light of Plaintiff filing a subsequent proof of service of the Summons and
Complaint, Defendant Carlos Rodriguez’s Motion to Quash Service of the Summons
and Complaint is PLACED OFF CALENDAR AS MOOT.
