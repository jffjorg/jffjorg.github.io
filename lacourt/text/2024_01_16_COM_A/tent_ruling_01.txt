ORDER CONTINUING
 THE HEARING ON PETITION FOR CHANGE OF NAME
 
      Both
Petitioners, who are 14 years old, request an order changing their last name
from Gallegos Nuñez to Luna. Both birth parents must consent to and sign the
petitions. If only one parent consents to the name change, Petitioners are required
to serve the other parent with the Order to Show Cause at least 30 days before
the hearing. (Code Civ. Proc., § 1277(a)(4).) The OSC shall be personally
served on the other parent if that parent resides in California. (Id.)
Service outside California may be accomplished by mail, return receipt
requested. (Code Civ. Proc., § 415.40.) Petitioner shall file the proof
of service with the court prior to the hearing date.
      Petitioners
are required to make diligent efforts to locate the non-consenting parent. If that
person cannot be located, Petitioners shall file a declaration stating what
attempts were made to locate the other parent and why service cannot be
reasonably accomplished. The declaration shall be filed no less than 10 court
days before the continued hearing date. Additionally, Petitioners must comply with
the requirements for publication of the Order to Show Cause. Code Civ. Proc., §
1277.
      Accordingly,
the court continues the hearing to March 5, 2024, at 8:30 a.m. in Department A of the
Compton Courthouse. Petitioner is ordered to comply with publication
requirements prior to the date of the hearing.
