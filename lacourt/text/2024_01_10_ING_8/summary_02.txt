And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to a request for production of documents. The motion was brought by the plaintiff, Lambe B. Papoulias, against the defendant, Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. and County-Wide Rambler, Inc. dba Volkswagen of Garden Grove.

The judge has denied parts of the motion and granted others. The denied portions include requests for production of documents related to warranty repairs performed on the 2022 VW Atlas and documents evidencing, describing, referring, or relating to Volkswagen's rules, policies, or procedures for escalating customer complaints.

The granted portions include requests for production of documents related to Volkswagen's Call Center Policies and Procedures for escalating customer complaints, documents evidencing, describing, referring, or relating to Volkswagen's rules, policies, or procedures for evaluating buy-back demands, and documents relating to Volkswagen's policy and procedure for issuing refunds to buyers or providing replacement vehicles to buyers in California.

The judge has also ordered the parties to give notice of the ruling. The parties are required to comply with the ruling and to provide any additional information or documents that are requested.
