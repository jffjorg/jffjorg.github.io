The judge has granted the motion to compel responses to special interrogatories, set one, filed by Defendant Emil J. Simanian D.D.S. Inc. The motion was filed on May 23, 2024, and seeks to compel Plaintiff Liliana Castillo Onofre to serve verified responses, without objection, to the special interrogatories. The judge has also granted in part the request for monetary sanctions against the plaintiff and her attorney.

The judge's ruling is based on the fact that the plaintiff failed to serve timely responses to the special interrogatories, despite being served with the interrogatories and being given an opportunity to respond. The judge has found that the plaintiff has waived any objection to the interrogatories, including those based on privilege or the protection for work product, as a result of their failure to serve timely responses.

The judge has ordered the plaintiff to serve verified responses, without objection, to the special interrogatories, set one, within 20 days. The judge has also ordered the plaintiff and her attorney to jointly and severally pay $485.00 to Defendant Emil J. Simanian D.D.S. Inc. in connection with the motion.
