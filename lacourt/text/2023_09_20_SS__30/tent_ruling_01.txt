  Case Number:  20STCV33142    Hearing Date:   September 20, 2023    Dept:  30 LORNA HOYLE vs MAX FOLLMER, et al.Motion to Compel Independent Medical ExaminationsRuling:  Defendant’s IME motion is DENIED. Moving party to give notice.Discussion:  The Court finds good cause exists to conduct the examinations.  The Court finds, however, that the notices lack the specificity required.  Namely, the Court finds that "deemed necessary" by the respective physician as a limitation on the scope of examination is too uncertain. For this reason, the motion is denied.
