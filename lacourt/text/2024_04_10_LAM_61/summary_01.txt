Here is the English text summarizing the judge's tentative ruling:

The court grants Plaintiff Chelsea Flowers' motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories and requests for production from Defendants LA Organic Pharmacy, Inc. and Mecca Mid City. The court finds that the Defendants' responses were incomplete, inadequate, and evasive, and that they failed to provide sufficient explanations for their lack of responsiveness. The court orders the Defendants to provide further responses to the interrogatories and requests for production. The court also awards sanctions against the Defendants and their counsel in the amount of $3,396.60 plus fees of $246.60.
