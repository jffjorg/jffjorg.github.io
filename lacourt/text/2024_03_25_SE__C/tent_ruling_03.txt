 Case Number:  24NWCV00081    Hearing Date:   March 25, 2024    Dept:  C Del
Rio 7444, LLC vs. Gonzales, et al., Case No. 24NWCV00081
 
This
is an unlawful detainer action. Plaintiff Del Rio 7444, LLC moves ex parte for
an order shortening time to hear its motion for summary judgment, which is
currently set for May 21, 2024. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Plaintiff
asserts that its motion for summary judgment should be heard before trial.
(Decl. Andrews, ¶ 4.) However, the court has not set a trial date. Therefore,
Plaintiff’s ex parte application is DENIED.
