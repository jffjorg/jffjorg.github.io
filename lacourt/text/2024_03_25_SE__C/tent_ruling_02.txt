This
is an unlawful detainer action. Plaintiff Abner Hinojos moves ex parte for an
order shortening time to hear and overrule the defendant’s demurrer to
Plaintiff’s complaint. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Plaintiff
asserts that unlawful detainer actions are summary proceedings, but that the
demurrer is set for December 13, 2024. (Decl. Heckmann, ¶ 3.) Plaintiff also
makes arguments to overrule the demurrer. (Decl. Heckmann, ¶ 4.) 
 
The
hearing on Defendant’s demurrer is ADVANCED from December 13, 2024, to this
