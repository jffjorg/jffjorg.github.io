  Case Number:  22NWCV00320    Hearing Date:   March 25, 2024    Dept:  C Pacific
Southwest District of the Lutheran Church-Missouri Synod, Case No. 22NWCV00320
 
This
is a quiet title action over land occupied by a church’s former congregation.
Plaintiff Pacific Southwest District of the Lutheran Church-Missour Synod seeks
ex parte relief to advance the hearing for its motion to compel further
responses to form interrogatories to April 23, 2024. It is currently set for
September 12, 2024. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Trial
is currently set for July 25, 2024, and plaintiff asserts that it will be
prejudiced if the motion is not heard before then. (Decl. Leverone, ¶ 6.) 
 
Based
on the above, the motion is ADVANCED from Thursday, September 12, 2024 to this
day and CONTINUED to Thursday, May 2, 2024 at 10:30 A.M. in Dept. SE-C.

party to give notice.
