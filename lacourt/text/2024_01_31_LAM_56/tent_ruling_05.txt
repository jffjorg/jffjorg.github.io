BACKGROUND
Plaintiff’s
complaint (the “Complaint”) alleges: (1) breach of written contract; and (2)
common count – services rendered.  
 
On September 19,
2023, Plaintiff filed a motion to compel further responses to the Form
Interrogatories that were propounded on Defendant (the “Motion”).  
 
 
On October 4,
2023, Plaintiff filed a Notice of Settlement indicating that Plaintiff and
Defendant had agreed to settle this matter, and on November 28, 2023, a
judgment was entered on the Complaint. 
 
Given that this
matter has settled and a judgment has been entered, the Motion is MOOT. 
 
Moving party is ordered to give notice
of this ruling.
