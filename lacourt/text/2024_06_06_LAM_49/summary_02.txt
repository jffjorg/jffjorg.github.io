And here is the English-text summary of the tentative ruling:

The court grants the plaintiff's motion for leave to file a supplemental complaint to add treble damages to the prayer for relief due to the defendant's alleged failure to allow a physical audit. The court finds that the amendment would not be futile and that the defendant has not demonstrated that the plaintiff lacks authority to amend the complaint. The court also notes that the defendant has not been prejudiced by the amendment. The court orders that a stand-alone supplemental complaint must be filed and served to all current parties within 10 days.
