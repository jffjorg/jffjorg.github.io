[TENTATIVE] ORDER TAKING
 OFF CALENDAR MOTION TO BE RELIEVED AS COUNSEL FOR PLAINTIFF
 
The complaint, filed on September 8,
2022, arises from alleged dental malpractice. Gilbert Quiñones, counsel for Plaintiff,
filed this motion on August 29, 2023, for an order to be relieved as
counsel because of a breakdown in the attorney-client relationship. 
Counsel served the notice of hearing only
on Plaintiff. The proof of service does not indicate that Counsel served the
declaration and proposed order. (California
Rules of Court, rule 3.1362 (d). Accordingly, the motion is taken
off calendar.
