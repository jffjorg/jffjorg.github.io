 Case Number:  24LBCV00331    Hearing Date:   May 28, 2024    Dept:  S25 Tentative RulingOn April 23, 2024, Defendant Dana Robertson-Tanner (“Robertson-Tanner”), specially appearing, filed the instant motion to quash service of summons and complaint.Code Civ. Proc., § 1005, subd. (a), provides that: “[w]ritten notice shall be given . . . [for a] Motion to Quash Summons.” Code Civ. Proc., § 1005, subd. (b), states that: “[u]nless otherwise ordered or specifically provided by law, all moving and supporting papers shall be served and filed at least 16 court days before the hearing.”Defendant Robertson-Tanner failed to provide proof of service that she properly and timely served this Motion to Quash on Plaintiff City of Long Beach. Accordingly, the motion to quash is denied without prejudice.
