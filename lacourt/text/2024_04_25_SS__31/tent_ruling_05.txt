(“Samooha”) of B&D Law Group, APLC moves to be relieved as counsel for plaintiff Jayden Reyes, a minor, by and through his Guardian Ad Litem, Priscilla Serrano (“Plaintiff”) contending relief is necessary because of a breakdown in communication.  Counsel declares he has been unable to confirm Plaintiff’s address after calling the last known telephone number and contacting Plaintiff’s aunt. The Court previously instructed that if Counsel was unable to serve Plaintiff at a confirmed address, then in addition to serving Plaintiff, Counsel must also serve the moving papers on the Clerk of the Court pursuant to CCP §1011 and California Rules Court, rule 3.1362(d).  The Court notes that Counsel attempted to serve the Clerk of the Court. The service was rejected on the grounds that the sealed envelope did not conform to the proper format pursuant to CRC 3.252. (Notice of Rejection, March 27, 2024.) Counsel was served with the Notice of Rejection on March 28, 2024. (Ibid.).
