And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling regarding the motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents. The Court finds that the Plaintiff has fulfilled the meet and confer requirement and that the Defendant has agreed to serve further responses to some of the requests. The Court grants the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests 1 and 7, which seek documents regarding the Subject Vehicle. The Court grants the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests 18-33, 42, 43, 64, and 65, which seek information regarding Defendant's internal analysis or investigation, customer complaints, failure rates, and fixes regarding electrical and transmission defects in Kia Vehicles. The Court grants the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests 66, 67, 70, and 71, which seek documents relating to communication with governmental agencies and suppliers regarding the defects in Kia Vehicles. The Court grants the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests 111-118, which seek documents relating to communication with governmental agencies and suppliers regarding the defects in Kia Vehicles. Finally, the Court denies the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests 129 and 130, which seek documents concerning performance standards relating to the electrical system in Kia Vehicles and the 6-speed Transmission in Kia Vehicles.
