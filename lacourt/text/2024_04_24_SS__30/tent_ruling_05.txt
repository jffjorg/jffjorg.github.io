 Case Number:  23STCV13167    Hearing Date:   April 24, 2024    Dept:  30 EDWIN ZEPEDA vs CITY OF LOS ANGELES, et al.TENTATIVEDefendant's Unopposed Motion to Compel Discovery Responses is GRANTED, effective upon moving party paying for three additional filing fees (3 x $60 = $180) to cover the Motion to Compel Further Responses to Special Interrogatories, Motion to Compel Further Responses to Request for Production of Documents, and Motion to Compel Further Responses to Request for Admissions.Defendant is ordered to provide further responses without objections to the Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, Request for Production of Documents, and Request for Admissions within 20 days of Notice of Ruling filed and served by Moving Party.Request for Sanctions is GRANTED.
