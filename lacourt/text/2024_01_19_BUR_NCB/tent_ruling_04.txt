[TENTATIVE] order RE:
motions to compel responses



 
There are three
discovery motions on calendar.  On December
18, 2023, Plaintiff Ariella Lousky (“Plaintiff”) filed 2 motions to compel
initial responses from Defendant Douglas Peter Richter (“Defendant”) for: (1)
Form Interrogatories (“FROG”), set one; and (2) Request for Production of
Documents (“RPD”), set one.  Plaintiff also
filed a motion for order deeming Requests for Admissions (“RFA”), set one,
admitted.  
On November 1,
2023, Plaintiff served on Defendant the discovery requests, such that responses
were due by December 6, 2023.  As of the
filing of the motions, Plaintiff states that she has not received responses
from Defendant.  
Plaintiff’s unopposed motions
to compel responses to the FROG and RPD are granted pursuant to CCP §§ 2030.290
and 2031.300.  Defendant is ordered to
provide verified responses to Plaintiff’s discovery requests, without
objections, within 20 days of notice of this order.  
Plaintiff’s unopposed motion
for an order deeming the RFAs admitted is granted, pursuant to CCP §
2033.280.  
Plaintiff requests
sanctions against Defendant and his attorney in the amount of $2,760 for each
motion.  The requests are granted in the
total sum of $1,500 in attorney’s fees and $180 in filing fees for the 3
motions.  Defendant and his counsel of record, jointly and severally, are ordered to
pay monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,680 to Plaintiff, by and through
counsel, within 20 days of notice of this order. 
Plaintiff shall provide
notice of this order.
