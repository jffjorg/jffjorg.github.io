 Case Number:  22STCV21838    Hearing Date:   April 19, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
July 6, 2022, Plaintiffs Santos Ricardo Regalado, Brittany Louren Moreno, Brianna
Isabel Moreno, and Daymon Alvarado filed this action against Defendants Daniel
Jacob Nahmias (“Defendant”) and Does 1-20 for negligence. 
On
May 23, 2023, Defendant filed an answer. 
On
November 15, 2023, the Court appointed Brittany
Louren Moreno to serve as Plaintiff Daymon Alvarado’s guardian ad litem. 
On
December 6, 2023, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On
March 22, 2024, Petitioner Britanny Louren Moreno (“Petitioner”) filed a petition
to approve the compromise of Plaintiff Daymon Alvarado’s claims. 
No trial
date is currently scheduled. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
Brittany Louren Moreno asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending
action of Plaintiff Daymon Alvarado. 
DISCUSSION      
Proposed
settlement terms:
          Gross settlement amount:                    $15,000.00
          Attorney’s fees:                                   $3,750.00
          Net balance:                                         $8,754.30 
Petitioner
checked the box in Section 18b(2) of the petition, affirming that $8,754.30 is
to be deposited in insured accounts in one or more financial institutions in
this State, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the court.  Section 18b(2) also states that the name,
branch, and address of each depository are specified in Attachment 18b(2).  However, Petitioner did not provide an
Attachment 18b(2). 
The
Court denies the petition without prejudice. 
CONCLUSION 
The Court DENIES without prejudice the petition for
approval of the compromise of the pending action of Plaintiff Daymon Alvarado filed
by Petitioner Brittany Louren Moreno on March 22, 2024. 
          Petitioner is ordered to give notice of the ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of the ruling with the Court within
five days.
