 Case Number:  22STCV15123    Hearing Date:   April 19, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
May 6, 2022, Plaintiffs Juan Carlos Meza, Rosa Sandoval Villasenor, Maria
Contreras, and Carlie Sandoval, a minor, by and through her guardian ad litem
Juan Carlos Meza, filed this action against Defendants Carlos Delgado
(“Defendant”) and Does 1-10 for negligence and negligence per se. 
On
July 6, 2022, Defendant filed an answer. 
On
September 8, 2022, the Court found that this case (case number 22STCV15123) and
case number 22STCV09631 are related within the meaning of California Rules of
Court, rule 3.300(a). Case number 22STCV09631 became the lead case. The cases
were assigned to Department 28 at Spring Street Courthouse for all purposes. 
On
January 16, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On
March 19, 2024, Petitioner Juan Carlos Meza (“Petitioner”) filed a petition to
approve minor’s compromise to be heard on April 15, 2024. On April 8, 2024,
Petitioner filed an amended petition.  On
April 15, 2024, the Court heard argument and continued the matter to April 19,
2024. 
On
April 15, 2024, Petitioner filed a second amended petition and submitted an amended
proposed order. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of Plaintiff
Carlie Sandoval (“Plaintiff”). 
DISCUSSION      
Petitioner
has corrected the defects in the previous version of the petition and proposed
order.  The Court grants the second
amended petition filed on April 15, 2024 and orders the net balance of the
settlement proceeds ($2,813.00) to be distributed as specified in section
8b(2) of the amended proposed order submitted on April 15, 2024. 
CONCLUSION 
The Court GRANTS the second amended petition for
approval of the compromise of the pending action of Plaintiff Carlie Sandoval filed
by Petitioner Juan Carlos Meza on April 15, 2024. 
          Petitioner is ordered to give notice of the ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of the ruling with the Court within
five days.
