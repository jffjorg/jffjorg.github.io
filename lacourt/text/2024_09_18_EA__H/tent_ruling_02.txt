


[FN1] Health and
Safety Code § 11570 provides that “[e]very building or place used for the purpose of unlawfully
selling, serving, storing, keeping, manufacturing, or giving away any
controlled substance, precursor, or analog specified in this division, and
every building or place wherein or upon which those acts take place, is a
nuisance which shall be enjoined, abated, and prevented, and for which damages
may be recovered, whether it is a public or private nuisance.”[FN2] In connection
with the first cause of action, Plaintiff seeks, inter alia, a finding that
“the property. . . be deemed a public nuisance.” (Complaint, 12:25-27).
