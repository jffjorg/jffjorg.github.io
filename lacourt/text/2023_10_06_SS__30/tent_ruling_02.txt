 Case Number:  21STCV10542	    Hearing Date:   October 6, 2023    Dept:  30  RAFAEL QUIJANO vs POSTMATES, INC.Re: Motion to Continue TrialRuling: Defendant’s motion to continue trial is granted. Trial is set for August 26, 2024, and Final Status Conference is set for August 12, 2024.  All trial-related deadlines are continued in accordance with the new trial date. Moving party to give notice.Rationale: The Court finds good cause exists for a continuance in this matter. No prejudice has been shown. This noticed motion is unopposed.California Rules of Court, rule 3.1332, subdivision (c) states that “[a]lthough continuances of trials are disfavored, each request for a continuance must be considered on its own merits. The court may grant a continuance only on an affirmative showing of good cause requiring the continuance.” Good cause appearing, the motion is granted.
