 Case Number:  23STCV07280	    Hearing Date:   October 6, 2023    Dept:  30 PATRICIA PETERS vs PAINTCRAFT SUPPLY & HARDWARE CO. INC., et al.Re: Motion to CompelRuling: Defendant’s Motion to Compel Plaintiff Patricia Peters’ Responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, is granted.  Plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses, without objections, within 20 days. The Request for Sanctions is denied. Moving party is ordered to give notice.Rationale: Defendant served Form Interrogatories, Set One, on Plaintiff on May 29, 2023, and to date Defendant has not received  responses.  If a party to whom interrogatories are directed fails to serve a timely response, the propounding party may move for an order compelling responses and for a monetary sanction. (Code Civ. Proc section 2030.290(b).) A party waives its objections to a discovery request when it does not serve a timely response to the request. (Code Civ. Proc section 2030.290(a))  The Court declines to award sanctions because the motion is unopposed.
