  Case Number:  21STCV05141	    Hearing Date:   October 6, 2023    Dept:  30 ESONIE CAIN vs LUZVIMINDA MONTECILLO, M.D., et al.Re: Motion to CompelRuling: Defendant’s Motion to Compel Plaintiff Responses to Special Interrogatories, Set Two, is granted.  Plaintiff is ordered to serve verified responses, without objections, within 20 days. The Request for Sanctions is denied. Moving party is ordered to give notice.Rationale: Defendant served Special Interrogatories, Set Two, on Plaintiff on June 23, 2023, and to date Defendant has not received  responses.  If a party to whom interrogatories are directed fails to serve a timely response, the propounding party may move for an order compelling responses and for a monetary sanction. (Code Civ. Proc section 2030.290(b).) A party waives its objections to a discovery request when it does not serve a timely response to the request. (Code Civ. Proc section 2030.290(a))  The Court declines to award sanctions because the motion is unopposed.
