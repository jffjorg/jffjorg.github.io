Benson v. Kanizo, Case no. 23SMCV05880
Hearing date October 10, 2024
Plaintiff’s
Counsel’s Motion to be Relieved as Counsel
Now-deceased
plaintiff Benson sued defendants Kanizo, Lavish Shadow and H&R Block for injuries
sustained in a slip and fall. Defendant Kanizo cross-complained against Lavish
Shadow and H&R Block. Moving parties Bahari and Graves, counsel for Benson,
now seek to be relieved. 
After
learning of plaintiff’s passing, counsel communicated with plaintiff’s heirs. The
heirs informed counsel they do not wish to proceed with the action. See Bahari
decl. As such, counsel seeks to be relieved.
An
application to be relieved must be made on judicial counsel form MC-051 (Notice
of Motion and Motion) per Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(a), MC-052
(Declaration) per CRC rule 3.1362(c), and MC-053 (Proposed Order) per CRC rule
3.1362(e).¿ The proposed order must specify all hearing dates scheduled in the
action or proceeding, including the date of trial, if known. CRC rule
3.1362(e).
Plaintiff’s
counsel met their obligations under Cal. Rules of Court 3.1362. GRANTED. The
court will set an OSC re dismissal of the matter.
