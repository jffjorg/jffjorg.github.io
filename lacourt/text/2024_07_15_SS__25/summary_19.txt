The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion filed by Defendant 3D Engineering & Manufacturing Inc. to set aside and vacate a Judgment entered on February 20, 2024, against the Defendant. The motion was timely filed and the Defendant has demonstrated good cause to set aside the Judgment, as it was entered through the mistake of the Defendant's prior counsel. Specifically, the prior counsel paid $15,231.14 to the Plaintiff instead of the Labor Commissioner, as agreed upon in a settlement agreement. The Judge has granted the motion and sets an Order to Show Cause Re: Dismissal (Settlement) for August 19, 2024, at 9:30 a.m. The Defendant is ordered to give notice of this ruling and a responsive pleading or a Request for Dismissal is to be filed within the next 20 days.
