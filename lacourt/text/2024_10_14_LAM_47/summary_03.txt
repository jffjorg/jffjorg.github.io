And here is a brief summary in English text:

This is a tentative ruling on a motion to compel further responses to requests for production and requests for admissions, as well as a request for sanctions. The plaintiff, MONARCH LLC, alleges that the defendant, Total Resources International, Inc., failed to disclose information about first-aid kits sold. The defendant objects to the discovery requests, claiming that the plaintiff is a non-existent entity. The court grants the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to requests for production, except for one request, and grants the motion to compel further responses to requests for admissions. The court denies the request for sanctions. The defendant is ordered to provide verified, code-compliant responses without objections within 30 days.
