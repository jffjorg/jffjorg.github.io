Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling in favor of plaintiff Glovis America, Inc. in its motion to compel defendant Haravinder Singh to provide further responses to interrogatories and requests for production. The judge finds that defendant's responses were evasive and incomplete, and that defendant waived any objections to the discovery requests by failing to serve timely responses. The judge orders defendant to provide further verified responses to the interrogatories and requests for production without objections within 30 days, and imposes sanctions on defendant and defendant's counsel of record, Thomas P. Bleau, in the amount of $2,682, $3,714, and $5,808 respectively.
