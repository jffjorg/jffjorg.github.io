And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has considered Defendant Kia America, Inc.'s motion to strike or tax costs incurred by Plaintiffs Gabriel Barahona and Bertha Barahona. The Plaintiffs filed a breach of contract and warranty claim under the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act and prevailed at trial. The Defendant seeks to tax or strike various costs incurred by the Plaintiffs, including filing fees, jury fees, deposition costs, service of process costs, expert witness fees, court reporter fees, and other expenses.

The court has reviewed the evidence and arguments presented by both parties and has granted in part and denied in part the Defendant's motion. The court has taxed Plaintiffs' costs in the amount of $2,138.35.
