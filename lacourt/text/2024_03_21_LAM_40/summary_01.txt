And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on Defendant Garrett Electric's demurrer, which challenges the sufficiency of the Complaint's fourth, fifth, and seventh causes of action. The judge has overruled the demurrer as to the fourth and seventh causes of action, finding that the Complaint adequately alleges intentional interference with contractual relations and intentional infliction of emotional distress against Garrett Electric. The judge has sustained the demurrer as to the fifth cause of action, finding that there is no cause of action for negligent interference with a contract in California.

The judge has found that the Auto Club and Garrett Electric acted in bad faith to deny Plaintiff's insurance claim, and that Garrett Electric submitted false reports to aid the Auto Club's claim denial. The judge has also found that the Plaintiff suffered damages as a result of the Auto Club's and Garrett Electric's actions.
