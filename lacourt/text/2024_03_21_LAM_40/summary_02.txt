And here is the English-text summary:

The judge's tentative ruling grants the motion to compel further responses to the request for production of documents, set one, filed by the plaintiffs, Elisa Morales Sanchez and Luis Aguilar Galvan, against General Motors LLC and Does 1 through 10. The motion was filed in connection with a complaint alleging claims for violation of the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act and breach of express and implied warranties. The court finds that General Motors LLC's objections to the request for production of documents are without merit and grants the motion as to requests 16-21, subject to limitations. The court also orders General Motors LLC to provide a privilege log and to produce responsive documents without attorney-client or work product privilege.
