This tentative ruling is regarding the case of Ernest Hill vs. Litco Entertainment Inc. Ernest Hill, the plaintiff, is seeking a default judgment against Litco Entertainment Inc. for damages arising from a glass front door shattering and falling on him when he attempted to enter Litco's building on November 6, 2020. The plaintiff is seeking damages for general negligence and premises liability. The court has granted the application for default judgment in the principal amount of $23,722.20 and $1,357.15 in costs against Litco Entertainment Inc. The court has limited the plaintiff's recovery for past medical expenses to $8,722.20 and reduced the amount of general damages from $350,000 to $15,000 due to a lack of sufficient evidence and justification for the requested amounts.
