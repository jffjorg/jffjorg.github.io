The Judge's tentative ruling grants in part the motion filed by Defendant Geoffrey Thomas Craighead, requiring Plaintiffs Rolando Enrique Restrepo and Nina Restrepo to post an undertaking pursuant to California Code of Civil Procedure section 1030. The Court finds that Defendant has shown a "reasonable possibility" of prevailing, which is sufficient under the statute. The undertaking amount is set at $23,050.70, covering costs and expert fees already incurred and reasonably expected to be incurred by the conclusion of the matter. If Plaintiffs fail to file the undertaking within the time allowed, their action is subject to dismissal.
