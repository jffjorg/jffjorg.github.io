And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling granting default judgment to Centra Freight Services, Inc. (Plaintiff) against defendants Anthony Velazquez and Centra Global Logistics LLC. The court finds that the defaulting party has confessed to the material allegations of the complaint. The Plaintiff is seeking recovery of principal damages, interest, and attorney fees.

The court grants the following amounts:

* Against Anthony Velazquez for the First Cause of Action: $250,000 in principal damages, $25,560.90 in interest, and $4,390 in attorney fees.
* Against Anthony Velazquez and Centra Global Logistics LLC, jointly and severally, for the Second Cause of Action: $260,000 in principal damages, $93,906.30 in interest, $4,490 in attorney fees, and $547.50 in costs.
* Against Centra Global Logistics LLC for the Third Cause of Action: principal damages of $61,359.41.

The court notes that both Anthony Velazquez and Centra Global Logistics LLC are jointly and severally liable for the $260,000 (Note No. 2). The court also clarifies that it will grant principal damages in the amounts of $250,000 against Anthony Velazquez for Note No. 1, $260,000 against both Anthony Velazquez and Centra Global Logistics LLC for Note No. 2 on a joint and several liability basis, and $61,359.41 against Centra Global Logistics LLC for the outstanding balance due in the account for services rendered.
