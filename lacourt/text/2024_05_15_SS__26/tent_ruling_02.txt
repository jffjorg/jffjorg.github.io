 Case Number:  22STLC04716    Hearing Date:   May 15, 2024    Dept:  26  
State Farm v. Salazar, et al.MOTION TO AMEND
JUDGMENT(CCP § 473(d))
TENTATIVE RULING:
 
Judgment State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company’s
Motion to Amend Judgment is GRANTED.
 
 
ANALYSIS:  
 
Judgment Creditor State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance
Company (“Judgment Creditor”) filed the instant action against Judgment Debtor
Jaime Salazar aka Jaime Andres Salazar on July 15, 2022. Judgment pursuant to
stipulation was entered on April 6, 2023.
 
Judgment Creditor filed the instant Motion to Amend Judgment
on March 15, 2024. To date, no opposition has been filed.  
 
Discussion
 
The Motion is brought pursuant to
Code of Civil Procedure section 473, subdivision (d), which provides that the
court may “correct clerical mistakes in its judgments or orders as entered, so
as to conform to the judgment or order directed . . . .” (Code Civ. Proc., §
473, subd. (d).) The judgment was to be entered solely against Judgment Debtor
but inadvertently included “and City of Long Beach.” (Motion, Espinosa Decl.,
¶¶2-3.) The request to correct the clerical error is hereby granted pursuant to
Code of Civil Procedure section 473, subdivision (d). Judgment Creditor
proposed amended judgment submitted on March 15, 2024 is signed and entered
this date. 
 
Conclusion
 
Judgment State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company’s
Motion to Amend Judgment is GRANTED.
