The court has issued a tentative ruling in a case where Defendant Muranaka Farm is seeking to compel responses from Plaintiff Miguel Magdaleno to Form Interrogatories (FROG). The FROG was due on or before April 25, 2024, but Plaintiff did not respond until August 14, 2024. The court finds that Plaintiff's failure to timely respond was an abuse of the discovery process and awards sanctions against Plaintiff and his counsel in the amount of $1,845. The court also orders Plaintiff to provide code-compliant further responses to FROG Nos. 8.1 and 8.7 within 10 days of notice of this order. Additionally, the court orders Plaintiff and his counsel to pay the sanctions to Defendant within 30 days of notice of this order.
