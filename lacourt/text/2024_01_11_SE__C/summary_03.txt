**English Summary**

The Judge's tentative ruling continues the hearing to February 8, 2024, to allow time to review the parties' untimely filed joint statement. The case involves a breach of contract and alleged violations of California's Consumer Legal Remedies Act (CLRA) and the California Unfair Competition Law (UCL). The Defendant, CERTIFIED RECORDS MANAGEMENT, LLC, is seeking to compel further responses to requests for production of documents. The Plaintiff, SENIOR OPERATIONS LLC, has refused to produce certain documents, citing objections and the inability to conduct a reasonable search for responsive documents. The Judge is considering the parties' arguments and will issue a final ruling at the continued hearing.
