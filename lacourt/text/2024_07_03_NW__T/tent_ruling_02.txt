 Case Number:  23VECV03775    Hearing Date:   July 3, 2024    Dept:  T 23VECV03775 Caltagirone v Sky World
Tentative ruling on Defendant Sky World's Motion to Compel Deposition of
plaintiff Matthew Wade Buser: Granted.  Deposition of the plaintiff Buser
shall take place by Zoom on 7/23/2024 at 9 a.m.  Plaintiff is to produce
at deposition a driver’s license, state ID card, or passport.  Monetary
sanctions are assessed in the sum of $500.46 against plaintiff Matthew Wade
Buser to defendant payable by 7/30/2024 to the Client Trust Account of Courtney
D. Flannery & Associates, 655 North Central Ave., 12th Floor, Glendale,
CA 91203 pursuant to CCP section 2023.010, discovery abuse without substantial
justification. Defendants have been trying to depose the plaintiff since
October 2023 and this deposition should have been completed by now, 8 months
later.  There is no excuse for not producing the plaintiff for deposition.
