This is a tentative ruling on a motion to deem requests for admission as admitted. The Defendant, Danielle Zammit, served requests for admission on the Plaintiff, Julianne Lattner, but the Plaintiff failed to respond. As a result, the Defendant is seeking to deem the requests for admission as admitted and is asking for monetary sanctions. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's failure to respond warrants deeming the requests for admission as admitted and grants the motion. The Court also awards monetary sanctions in the amount of $880.00, which is reduced from the requested amount of $1,290.00. The Plaintiff is required to pay the monetary sanctions to the Defendant's counsel within 30 days.
