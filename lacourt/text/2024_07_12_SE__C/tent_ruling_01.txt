  Case Number:  23NWCV03897    Hearing Date:   July 12, 2024    Dept:  C Tebache
v. Ramirez, Case No. 23NWCV03987
 
This
is about an automobile collision. Plaintiff moves ex parte to advance the
motion for leave to file the first amended complaint, which is currently
scheduled for February 4, 2025. 
 
Plaintiff
argues the motion should be advanced because it will enable all parties to
prepare for trial by completing essential discovery related to the newly
discovered evidence—the identity of defendant driver.
 
Based
on the above, the Court grants the motion. The motion is ADVANCED from Tuesday,
February 4, 2025 to today and CONTINUED to Tuesday, October 1, 2024 at 10:30
A.M. in Dept. SE-C.
