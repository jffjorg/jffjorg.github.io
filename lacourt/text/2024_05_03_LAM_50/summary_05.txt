**Case Summary**

Case Number: 23STCP03468
Hearing Date: May 3, 2024

The court has denied the Petitioner's petition to correct a contractual arbitration award. The dispute arose from an agreement to purchase a mixed-use building, where the Respondent, Chestnut Holdings, Inc., alleged that the Petitioner, Ken-Lar, LLC, kept a $1 million security deposit after the deal fell through.

The arbitration award was made on June 14, 2023, requiring the Petitioner to pay $1,438,218.64. The Petitioner sought to correct the award, arguing that the arbitrator exceeded his powers by including certain "Disputed Fees" in the award.

The court found that the arbitrator did not exceed his powers and denied the Petition. The court also denied the Respondent's request for additional fees.
