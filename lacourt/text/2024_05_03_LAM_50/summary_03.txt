Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has denied the plaintiff's motion for an order to enter judgment pursuant to the terms of the stipulated settlement of this case executed by all parties on or about January 24, 2024. The court finds that the warning required by the settlement does not comply with Health and Safety Code section 25249.6. The court also finds that the warning does not meet the requirements of California Code of Regulations title 27, section 25603. The court denies the motion without prejudice. The court orders that the defendant, Baker's Breakfast Cookies, Inc., must discontinue distributing products in California that do not comply with the settlement. The court also orders that Baker's must provide a warning on the product label that complies with California Code of Regulations title 27, section 25603. Further, the court orders that Baker's must pay a civil penalty of $7,000.00.
