The judge has issued a tentative ruling in favor of the defendants, SRHT Property Management Co. and Hart Limited Partnership, in their motion to deem requests for admission (RFAs) admitted against plaintiff Juan Aispuro. The defendants served the RFAs on November 21, 2023, and responses were due on December 26, 2023. Since no responses were received, the defendants are granted their motion to deem the RFAs admitted. The plaintiff does not oppose the motion. The judge notes that if a party fails to respond to a request for admission, they waive any objection to the requests. The defendants are ordered to notice the plaintiff. The hearing date is scheduled for January 25, 2024, in Department 54.
