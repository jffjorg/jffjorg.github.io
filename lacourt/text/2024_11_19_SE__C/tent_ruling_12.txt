24NWCV04082
 
This
breach of contract action was filed on October 28, 2024. No trial date has been
set. There is a hearing on Plaintiff's Application for Right to Attach Order
and Writ of Attachment scheduled for Tuesday, September 2, 2025. Plaintiff
moves ex parte for an order shortening time on the hearing of Plaintiff's
Application for Right to Attach Order and Writ of Attachment. 
 
Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Application for an Order Shortening Time on Plaintiff's Application
for Right to Attach Order and Writ of Attachment is DENIED. The Court
declines to set the matter to be heard on shortened time. Alternatively, the
Court will advance Plaintiff's Application to be heard on a date sooner than
currently scheduled, in a manner affording statutory notice to the opposing
party(s). Plaintiff's Applicaiton for Right to Attach Order and Writ of
Attachment is ADVANCED from Tuesday, September 2, 2025 to today's date
and CONTINUED to Tuesday, January 14, 2025 at 10:30 a.m. in Dept.
SE-C. Opposition and Reply to be filed and served per Code in accordance
with the new hearing date. Moving party to give notice.
