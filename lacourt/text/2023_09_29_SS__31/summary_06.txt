Judge's Tentative Ruling:

On September 29, 2023, the court issued a tentative ruling in the case of CHRISTINE AL TAMIMI, et al. vs C1 TRANSPORTATION, INC., et al., regarding the petition to approve the compromise of the pending action of a minor, Khalid Al Tamimi. The court excuses the personal appearance of Claimant and the guardian ad litem, allowing counsel to appear by telephone or video conference call.

The court has reviewed the settlement and finds it fair and reasonable, with the total amount of $1,000,000 being apportioned to Khalid Al Tamimi ($312,500) and to attorneys' fees ($62,500). The net balance of $250,000 will be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity, subject to withdrawal only with the court's authorization.

The court has conditionally granted the petition, subject to resolution of the issue raised regarding how to handle settlement recovery payable to Claimant, which was inadvertently sent to Plaintiff's counsel and deposited into their IOLTA prior to obtaining an Order for Disposition of Funds. The court wishes to discuss this request with Plaintiff's counsel and sets a hearing date for a resolution.
