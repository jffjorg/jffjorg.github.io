This case is about a motor vehicle accident and the subsequent discovery process. The plaintiff, Jose Chico, seeks to compel further responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, and Requests for Production, Set One. The defendant, Swift Transportation Co. of Arizona, LLC, opposes the motion.

The parties have met and conferred on the discovery issues. The plaintiff seeks to compel further responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, numbers 13.1 and 13.2, which relate to surveillance footage. The defendant has admitted to surveilling the plaintiff.

The court grants the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to Request for Production, Set One, numbers 21 and 22. The defendant is ordered to serve further responses within 15 days. The court awards monetary sanctions in the amount of $2,160 as to the defendant and counsel for the defendant, jointly and severally.

The court's tentative ruling notes that the parties are encouraged to meet and confer concerning this tentative ruling to determine if a resolution may be reached. If the parties are unable to reach a resolution and a party intends to submit on this tentative ruling, they must send an email to the court indicating their intention to submit.
