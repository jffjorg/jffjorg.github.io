The Judge's tentative ruling grants default judgment in favor of the Plaintiffs, Amy Lloyd and Todd Benton, against the Defendants, Pablo Ortiz and One Day Builders Inc. The Plaintiffs had hired Ortiz for the remodeling of their property, but Ortiz was not a licensed contractor. The Plaintiffs claim that Ortiz was fraudulently allowed to use One Day Builders' license number, and that they are entitled to damages of $180,000, plus interest, attorneys' fees, and costs. The Judge finds that the Plaintiffs have met the procedural and substantive requirements for entry of a default judgment, and grants default judgment in the amount of $257,438.02.
