The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to vacate and set aside a dismissal entered against the plaintiff, Leonardo Pinto Rojas, in a personal injury action against Davinci Motors, LLC, et al. The court entered an order of dismissal without prejudice on May 15, 2024. The plaintiff's counsel filed a declaration of attorney fault and attested to the mistake and inadvertence, and the judge has found that the dismissal was a result of the mistake and inadvertence by the plaintiff's counsel, not by the plaintiff. The court has granted the plaintiff's motion to vacate and set aside the dismissal and has reinstated the underlying action.
