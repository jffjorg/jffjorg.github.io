The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer to the complaint filed by Ashchyan Transport, Inc. against Cummins, Inc. and In & Out Truck Repair, Inc. The complaint alleges that Cummins, Inc. breached the implied warranty of merchantability by manufacturing engine parts that did not conform to the quality established by the usage of trade, resulting in catastrophic failure. The Judge found that the complaint alleges sufficient facts to establish a cause of action for breach of implied warranty of merchantability and that the complaint is not necessarily barred by the statute of limitations. Therefore, the Judge overruled the demurrer and ordered Cummins, Inc. to file an answer to the complaint within 10 days.
