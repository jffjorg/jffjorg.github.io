And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The defendant, Los Angeles Unified School District, has moved for leave to conduct a mental examination of the plaintiff, Nala Mena, a minor, who is suing the district for negligence and negligent hiring, training, and supervision. The plaintiff alleges that she was physically and mentally bullied while attending kindergarten at Fullbright Avenue Elementary school. The defendant has demonstrated good cause for the mental examination, and the court has granted the motion under certain conditions.

The conditions include that the defendant may conduct the interview with the plaintiff on November 16, 2023, at 10850 Wilshire Blvd., Ste. 850, Los Angeles, CA 90024, and that the entire examination may be audio recorded. The examiner, Dr. Christopher R. Thompson, M.D., will use standardized, appropriate psychological testing and screening measures, and the examination will not involve any physical examination, x-rays or imaging studies, or blood tests or other intrusive medical studies or procedures. The court has also specified that the examination shall not exceed a total of four (4) hours, and that breaks shall be taken as needed.
