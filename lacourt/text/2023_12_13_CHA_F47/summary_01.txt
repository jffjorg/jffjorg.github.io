Summary:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling denying the application for a right to attach order and writ of attachment filed by Southern California Real Estate Management, Inc. (SCREM) against Team House Real Estate, Inc. (Team House). SCREM sought to attach any property of Team House with a value of $2,160,000.00, which includes $5,000.00 in estimated costs and $50,000.00 in estimated allowable attorney fees. The Court finds that SCREM has failed to establish that it more likely than not that it will obtain a judgment against Team House on the breach of contract claim. Additionally, the Court finds that SCREM has failed to establish that it will suffer great or irreparable harm if the alternative request for a protective order is not granted. The Judge concludes that the application is denied in its entirety.
