  Case Number:  20STCV14705    Hearing Date:   November 13, 2024    Dept:  28 On September
26, 2024, the Court granted the second amended petition to approve the
compromise of Plaintiff Xiaoling Mai's action filed by Petitioner Yu Mai on
September 20, 2024, subject to the Court receiving a revised proposed order
listing a bond amount of $75,340.26 and setting a first accounting due date in
October 2025.  On September 30, 2024,
Petitioner Yu Mai filed a revised proposed order with the necessary
modifications.
Therefore,
the Court orders the net settlement amount of $67,268.09 to be paid or
transferred to the trustee of the Special Needs Trust for Plaintiff Xiaoling
Mai established under Probate Code section 3604 for the benefit of Plaintiff Xiaoling
Mai.
