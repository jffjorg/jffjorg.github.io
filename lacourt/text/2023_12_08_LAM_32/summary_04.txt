The judge is considering a Pitchess motion filed by plaintiff Rinaldo Alberico, who is seeking to obtain personnel records related to himself and Deputy Errol Payne. The records are requested to prove allegations of discrimination, harassment, and retaliation by the County of Los Angeles.
