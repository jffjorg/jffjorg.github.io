Tentative ruling: Motions to withdraw
as counsel
The court considers these motions only
as to the civil action.  The
court does not have the ability to rule on the motions to withdraw as to the
arbitration. Any such motion must be brought before the arbitrator.

     order.  The order is effective only after the conformed order is
