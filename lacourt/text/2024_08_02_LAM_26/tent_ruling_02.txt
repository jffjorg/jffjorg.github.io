I. BACKGROUND
 
On December
28, 2023, Plaintiff LVNV Funding LLC filed the instant action against Defendant
Carlos Andrade alleging causes of action for breach of contract and open book
account. 
 
The Complaint
alleges the following. Plaintiff is the successor in interest to original
creditor, SoFi Lending Corp. Plaintiff is a debt buyer, and is the sole owner
of the debt at issue. Plaintiff alleges that within the last four years,
Defendant has failed to make payments owed under contract. Defendant owes
Plaintiff in the amount of $40,886.01. 
 
On
March 21, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Entry of Default Judgment. Plaintiff seeks judgment against Defendant
in the total amount of $45,880.89. 
 
II.ANALYSIS
 
A.      Legal
Standard
 
CCP § 585 permits entry of a judgment after a Defendant has
failed to timely answer after being properly served.  A party seeking
judgment on the default by the Court must file a Request for Court Judgment,
and: (1) a brief summary of the case; (2) declarations or other admissible
evidence in support of the judgment requested; (3) interest computations as
necessary; (4) a memorandum of costs and disbursements; (5) a proposed form of
judgment; (6) a dismissal of all parties against whom judgment is not sought;
(7) a dismissal of all parties against whom judgment is not sought or an
application for separate judgment under CCP § 579, supported by a showing of
grounds for each judgment; (8) exhibits as necessary; and (9) a request for
attorneys’ fees if allowed by statute or by the agreement of the parties. 
(CRC Rule 3.1800.)

Here,
Plaintiff has complied with all requirements for a default judgment.  
 
Plaintiff
submitted the declaration of Dianna Yocom, an authorized
representative for Plaintiff LVNV Funding LLC and Resurgent Capital Services,
LP. (Yocom Decl., ¶ 1.) Yocom provided a summary of
the case, attached the Chain of Title and Seller Data Document, the Loan
Agreement between the parties, and Defendant’s Account Statement showing the
payment due in the amount of $40,886.01. (Yocom Decl., Exs. A-C.) Plaintiff also submitted the declaration of
Plaintiff’s counsel, Christopher Mandarich, stating that Plaintiff calculated
the prejudgment interest at 10% per annum from June 26, 2023 to March 6, 2024.
(Mandarich Decl. Decl., ¶ 3.) Thus, Plaintiff claims $2,844.80 in total accrued
interest. Mandarich also states the attorneys fees were calculated according to
the local rule and totaling $1,616.58. (Id.,
¶ 4.)  
 
Since
Plaintiff has complied with the requirements, Plaintiff’s request for default
judgment is GRANTED.
 
III. CONCLUSION 
 
Plaintiff’s
request for default judgment is GRANTED.
