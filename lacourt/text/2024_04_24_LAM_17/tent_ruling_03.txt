Plaintiff’s motion to have its RFAs deemed admitted as to Defendant
Income Tax Solutions is GRANTED. Defendant is sanctioned, jointly and severally
with counsel, $700.00.
 
            On 10/16/2023, Plaintiff Immigrant
Rights Defense (Plaintiff) filed suit against Guadalupe Avitia-Dominguez and
Income Tax Solutions, seeking injunctive relief for violation of the
Immigration Consultant Act. 
 
            On 1/16/2024, Plaintiff moved to
have its Requests for Admission (RFAs) deemed admitted as to Defendant Income
Tax Solutions (Defendant). 
 
            The motion is unopposed. 
 
Discussion  
 
            On 11/7/2023, Plaintiff served
Defendant with RFAs. As such, responses were due by 12/12/2023. To date, no
response has been served. 
 
            Defendant did not oppose this motion,
and thus has not set forth any substantial justification for the failure to
respond.
 
            Based on the foregoing, Plaintiff’s
motions have its RFAs deemed admitted is granted. Defendant is sanctioned,
jointly and severally with counsel, $700 per motion. ($350/hr x 2 hr.) 
 
 It is so ordered.
