The judge has ruled that the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories, Set One, is granted. The defendant, Oscar Health Plan of California, is ordered to provide further responses to special interrogatories, Set One, Nos. 1-20 to the plaintiff within 20 days' notice of this order. The judge found that the defendant's responses to the special interrogatories were incomplete and evasive, and that the defendant failed to provide sufficient information regarding its payment methodology. The judge also found that the defendant's objections to the special interrogatories were without merit. As a result, the plaintiff's motion to compel further responses to special interrogatories, Set One, is granted.
