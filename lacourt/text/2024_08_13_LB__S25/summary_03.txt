And here is a summary in English text:

This tentative ruling addresses a demurrer filed by Defendant Peter S. Birnbaum, M.D. to the third cause of action for medical battery in a complaint filed by the plaintiff. The plaintiff alleges that Defendant Birnbaum removed a small bone from his thumb without his consent during a cyst removal procedure on his right hand. The plaintiff also claims that he has no recollection of discussing the procedure with Defendant Birnbaum or giving consent to remove the thumb bone. The court finds that the plaintiff has sufficiently alleged facts to state a claim for medical battery and overrules Defendant Birnbaum's demurrer. The defendant is ordered to answer the complaint within 20 days.
