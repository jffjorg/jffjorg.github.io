 Case Number:  21STCV40593    Hearing Date:   April 25, 2024    Dept:  28 Defendants
Mashcole Property Management, Inc. and Mellick Trust/Mellick Mark C. Tr. move for
an order bifurcating the issues of
liability and damages at trial, directing that the issue of liability be tried
first, and confirming that both phases of trial be tried before the same jury.  Plaintiff Yadira Rojas opposes the motion. 
The

June 3, 2024.  The Court that conducts
the trial will consider the motion. 
Moving
parties are ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Moving
parties are ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court
within five days.
