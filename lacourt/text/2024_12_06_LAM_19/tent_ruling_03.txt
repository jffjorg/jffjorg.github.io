 Case Number:  23STCV24337    Hearing Date:   December 6, 2024    Dept:  19  Sharobeem v Craftscape   OSC Re: Default Judgment TENTATIVE RULING After a review of the record, the Court notes that, on October 15, 2024, Plaintiffs filed a “Motion Seeking Determination That Automatic Stay Of 11 U.S.C. §362(A) Applies To All Co-Defendants” with a noticed hearing date of February 4, 2025. As such, on the Court’s own motion, the Order to Show Cause Re: Entry of Default Judgment and Status Conference Re: Bankruptcy, scheduled for 12/06/2024, are advanced to this date and CONTINUED to February 4, 2025 at 8:30 A.M. in Department 19 of the Stanley Mosk Courthouse.  The Court also notes that, in its August 14, 2024 Minute Order, the Court ordered counsel for Defendant Allen Abraham to file and serve a Status Report Re: Bankruptcy by November 21, 2024.However, to date, nothing has been filed. The Court orders counsel for Defendant Allen Abraham to file and serve a Status Report Re: Bankruptcy by January 28, 2024. The Court also orders counsel for Defendant Allen Abraham to appear at the February 4, 2025 hearing either in person or by way of LA Court Connect. Clerk to give notice to counsel for Plaintiffs and counsel for Defendant Allen Abraham. Notice waived as to Defendant Craftscape Creations, LLC, who is in default.
