 Case Number:  23NWCV01074    Hearing Date:   July 18, 2024    Dept:  C Edith
Rocio Sandoval Sanchez vs. Quy Trac, Case No. 23NWCV01074
 
This is a
personal injury action.  Defendant Quy Trac applies ex parte for an order
compelling response to Form Interrogatories (Set One), Special Interrogatories
(Set One) and Requests for Production of Documents (Set One) propounded upon
Plaintiff on March 11, 2024.  No responses have been received to
date.  Counsel attempted to reserve a hearing date for the motions, but
was informed the earliest hearing date would be in January 2025.  No
hearing date has been reserved yet.  Trial is currently scheduled for
March 24, 2025.  A party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative
factual showing "irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other
statutory basis for granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule
3.1202(c).) The Court determines that Defendant has not demonstrated
irreparable harm.  The ex parte application is DENIED.  Defendant is
not foreclosed from seeking a trial continuance in Dept. F.  Counsel is
advised that the Court typically requires parties to reserve a hearing date
through the Court’s reservation system and then move for ex parte relief if
necessary.  Moving party to give notice.
