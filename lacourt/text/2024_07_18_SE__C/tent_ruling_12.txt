 Case Number:  23NWCV03970    Hearing Date:   July 18, 2024    Dept:  C Maria
Aguirre vs. Amazon.com Services, LLC et al., Case No. 23NWCV03970
 
This is a
products liability case.  Defendant Sunbeam Products, Inc. applies ex
parte application to advance the hearing for its Motion to Compel Further
Responses to Request for Production of Documents, Request for Admissions, and
Special Interrogatories.  The motions are currently scheduled for April 2,
2025, which is past Defendant’s one-year time frame to remove the matter to
federal court.  Defendant argues that in order to seek removal, it
requires the information sought in the discovery requests.  Defendant’s ex
parte application is GRANTED.  The motions are ADVANCED from April 2, 2025
to today’s date and CONTINUED to September 19, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept.
SE-C.  Moving party to give notice.
