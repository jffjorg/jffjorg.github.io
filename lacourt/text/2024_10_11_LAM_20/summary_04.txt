The judge grants the motion to be relieved as counsel, filed by Mark V. Asdourian, Esq., on behalf of Defendant 1530 Date Street, LLC. The motion is unopposed. The judge concludes that the motion to be relieved as counsel is granted, and Mark V. Asdourian, Esq. is instructed to give notice.
