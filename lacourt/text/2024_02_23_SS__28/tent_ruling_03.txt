 Case Number:  21STCV39269    Hearing Date:   February 23, 2024    Dept:  28           On October 25, 2021, Plaintiffs David
Rosales Escobar and Matthew Escobar, a minor (“Matthew Escobar”), by and
through his guardian ad litem Aida Grisell Escobar, filed this action against
Defendants Elaheh Rezazadeh (“Rezazadeh”), Sixt Rent A Car, LLC (“Sixt”), Rafael
Hernandez Cuellar (“Hernandez Cuellar”), Jose A. Leonor, Henrey Chhang, and
Does 1-30 for motor vehicle tort and general negligence. 
          On January 12, 2022, the Court appointed Aida Grisell
Escobar to act as Matthew Escobar’s guardian ad litem. 
          On June 1, 2022, Defendant Henry Chhang (“Chhang”) filed an
answer.  On June 2, 2022, Chhang filed a
cross-complaint against Cross-Defendants Rezazadeh
and Roes 1-10 for implied indemnity, total indemnity, declaratory relief,
apportionment of fault, and property damage. 
          On December 8, 2022, the Court dismissed Chhang with
prejudice at Plaintiffs’ request.  On
December 29, 2022, the Court dismissed Chhang’s cross-complaint with prejudice at
his request. 
          On December 9, 2022, Sixt and Rezadadeh filed an answer to
the complaint.  On December 13, 2022,
Sixt and Rezazadeh filed a cross-complaint against Cross-Defendants Hernandez
Cuellar, Jose A. Leonor, Chhang, and Moes 1-50 for apportionment of fault,
indemnification, and declaratory relief. 
          On April 17, 2023, Defendants Hernandez Cuellar and Jose
Aristides Leonor Trejo, erroneously sued as Jose A. Leonor (“Leonor Trejo”), filed an answer to the complaint. 
          On June 14, 2023, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
          On January 8, 2024, Petitioner Aida Grisell Escobar (“Petitioner”)
filed a petition for expedited approval of a minor’s compromise.  The petition stated that Empire Fire and
Marine Insurance Company/Sixt Rent A Car had agreed to pay Matthew Escobar
$2,500.00 to settle his claim. 
On
January 26, 2024, the Court issued an order requesting additional information and revisions to the petition.  The Court continued the hearing to February 23, 2024. 
Petitioner
has not yet filed a revised petition. 
The Court denies the petition without prejudice. 
          Petitioner is ordered to give notice.
