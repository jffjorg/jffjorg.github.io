This is an unlawful detainer action.  Plaintiff Tetra Nevada, Inc. seeks to compel Defendant Ramin Fenchel to allow an inspection of the subject premises.  Because there is no opposition, the Motion to Compel is granted.  The subject premises are to be available for an inspection within 14 days.  The Court awards sanctions in the reduced amount of $1,000.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
