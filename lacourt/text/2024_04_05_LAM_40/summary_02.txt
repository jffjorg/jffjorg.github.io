**Parties**

* Plaintiff: Karen Flores
* Defendants: Everbrands, Inc. (CA and DE), Michael Florman, Yon Lai, and Does 1-25

**Case Facts**

* Karen Flores sued Everbrands, Inc., Michael Florman, Yon Lai, and others for failure to pay minimum wage, overtime, and provide rest and meal breaks.
* The claims arise from allegations that Flores was underpaid and not provided with required breaks during her employment.

**Motion**

* Plaintiff Flores filed a motion to compel further responses to Form Interrogatories, Set 1, and request for terminating sanctions, issue sanctions, evidence sanctions, and further monetary sanctions in the amount of $5,947.01 against Michael Florman and his attorney, Allyson Thompson.

**Tentative Ruling**

* The Court granted the motion in part and denied it in part.
* The Court denied the motion to compel further responses to Form Interrogatories, Set 1, and request for terminating sanctions, issue sanctions, and evidence sanctions.
* The Court denied the motion for further monetary sanctions in the amount of $5,947.01 against Michael Florman and his attorney, Allyson Thompson.

**Significant Statements**

* The Court found that the moving papers were defectively filed and failed to include a section explaining the reasons why Michael Florman's responses to Form Interrogatory No. 15.1 were deficient.
* The Court found that terminating sanctions would have been too drastic a remedy and denied the motion for terminating sanctions.
