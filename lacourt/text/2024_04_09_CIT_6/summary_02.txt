And here is the English-text summary:

This tentative ruling grants the petition to confirm a contractual arbitration award. The petitioner, Bill Me Later, Inc., filed a petition to confirm an arbitration award made on December 21, 2023, in the amount of $150,954.43, plus $8,313.75 in costs and arbitration fees. The respondents, Vision Trading Corporation d/b/a Xenon-Vision, HID-Warehouse, and Phouc Van To, did not respond to or oppose the petition. The court finds that the petitioner has met its burden in proving the award and establishing the existence of a valid arbitration agreement. The respondents' lack of response or opposition is construed as a tacit admission that the petition is meritorious. Therefore, the court grants the petition to confirm the contractual arbitration award and orders the petitioner to submit a proposed judgment.
