"This tentative ruling is in response to the demurrer to the third cause of action for promissory fraud filed by defendants B.A.C.C.O. Mechanical Inc., Jeffrey S. McGowan, and Alex Barrera. The court finds that this cause of action is not barred by the economic loss rule and that the plaintiff has alleged facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action. The court finds that the defendants made affirmative, false promises of performance without intending to perform, and that the plaintiff reasonably relied on those representations. The court overrules the demurrer and orders the defendants to file an answer to the complaint within 10 days of the date of this ruling."
