The Honorable Judge Lee S. Arian has issued a tentative ruling in the case of 22STCV26523, JESSICA MARIE MOORE et al. vs Pomona Unified School District. The case involves allegations of child abuse by Elvira Martinez, an employee of the Pomona Unified School District. The Defendant, Pomona Unified School District, has moved for leave to file a cross-complaint against Martinez for indemnity. The Court has granted the motion, finding good cause for the delay in filing the cross-complaint. The Defendant was unaware of Martinez's conviction under Penal Code 273a until it received discovery responses from the Plaintiff. The Court has also noted that the Plaintiff has not opposed the motion and that the Defendant has attached the proposed cross-complaint as Exhibit A.
