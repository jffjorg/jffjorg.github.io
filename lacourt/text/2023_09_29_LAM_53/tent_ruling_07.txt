MOVING PARTY:                 Russell H. Falconer    
 
RESPONDING PARTY:       Unopposed
Application of Russell H. Falconer to
Appear as Counsel Pro Hac Vice
The court considered the moving papers filed in connection with this application.  No opposition papers were filed. 
DISCUSSION
Applicant Russell H. Falconer requests an order permitting him to
appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendants Lockton Companies LLC – Pacific
Series, Lockton Investment Advisors, LLC, Lockton Investment Securities, LLC
f/k/a Lockton Financial Advisors, LLC, and Lockton Partners, LLC in this
action. 
The court finds that the application complies with California Rules of
Court, rule 9.40.  The court therefore
grants the application and orders that Russell H. Falconer is permitted to
appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendants Lockton Companies LLC – Pacific
Series, Lockton Investment Advisors, LLC, Lockton Investment Securities, LLC
f/k/a Lockton Financial Advisors, LLC, and Lockton Partners, LLC in this
action.  (Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40,
subd. (a).) 
The court orders defendants Lockton Companies LLC – Pacific Series,
Lockton Investment Advisors, LLC, Lockton Investment Securities, LLC f/k/a
Lockton Financial Advisors, LLC, and Lockton Partners, LLC to give notice of
this ruling.
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
