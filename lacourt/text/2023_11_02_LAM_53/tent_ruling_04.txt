MOVING PARTY:                 Applicant Jenna Johnson        
 
RESPONDING PARTY:       Unopposed
Application of Jenna Johnson to Appear as Counsel Pro Hac Vice
The court
considered the moving papers filed in connection with this application.  No opposition papers were filed.
DISCUSSION
Applicant Jenna Johnson requests an order permitting her to appear as
counsel pro hac vice for defendants Ethicon Endo-Surgery, Inc., and Ethicon
Endo-Surgery, LLC in this action. 
The court finds that the application complies with the requirements of California
Rules of Court, rule 9.40.  The court therefore grants the application and
orders that Jenna Johnson is permitted to appear as counsel pro hac vice for defendants
Ethicon Endo-Surgery, Inc., and Ethicon Endo-Surgery, LLC in this action.  (Cal. Rules of Ct., rule 9.40, subd. (a).) 
 
The court orders defendants Ethicon Endo-Surgery, Inc., and Ethicon
Endo-Surgery, LLC to give notice of this ruling.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
