Here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling denying the Petition to Confirm Arbitration Award filed by All Premium Contractors, Inc. (Petitioner) against Kenneth Caldera (Respondent). The Petitioner seeks to confirm an arbitration award issued by Stephen L. Backus, Esq. on November 7, 2022, in the amount of $22,249.70. The Respondent filed a General Denial and Demand for Arbitration.

The Judge found that the Petitioner failed to provide proof that the arbitrator served a copy of the award on each party in compliance with CCP, Section 1283.6. The Petitioner was ordered to file supplemental papers, including proof of service, but failed to do so. Therefore, the Petition is denied.
