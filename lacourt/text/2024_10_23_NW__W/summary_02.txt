Judge's Tentative Ruling:

The court has granted the Plaintiff's motion to compel the deposition attendance of a person most qualified and custodian of records of Defendant American Honda Motor Co., Inc. The motion was filed by Plaintiff Bryan Guino to compel Defendant to produce a witness for deposition. The court has ordered the parties to meet and confer regarding availability and has set a deadline for the deposition date, which is to be no earlier than 70 days from the date of this motion.

The Plaintiff alleged that the 2020 Honda Accord he leased had a sensing defect, which Defendant knew about and concealed. The Plaintiff filed a complaint against Defendant, asserting causes of action for Violation of Song-Beverly Act and Fraudulent Inducement – Concealment. The court has ordered the Defendant to produce a witness for deposition and has denied the Defendant's request for sanctions.
