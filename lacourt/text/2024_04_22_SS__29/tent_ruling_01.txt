  Case Number:  22STCV06273    Hearing Date:   April 22, 2024    Dept:  29 Motion to Compel Plaintiff’s deposition filed by
Defendant El Monte Union High School District.
 
Tentative
On February 22, 2022, Brandon Lara,
by and through his Guardian Ad Litem, Estela Balderrama, (“Plaintiff”) filed a
complaint against City of Los Angeles, County of Los Angeles, Los Angeles Unified
School District, El Monte City School District, City of El Monte, El Monte
Union High School District, City of Rosemead and Does 1 through 100 for Dangerous
Condition of Public Property and Vicarious Liability by a Public Entity
Employees causes of action.
 
On May 5, 2023, Defendant El Monte
Union High School District this motion for an order to compel the deposition of
Plaintiff. No opposition has been filed. 
 
On August 14, 2023, notice of
settlement of the entire case was filed in this matter. 
 
Accordingly, the Court DENIES AS MOOT
Defendant’s motion to compel the deposition of Plaintiff.
