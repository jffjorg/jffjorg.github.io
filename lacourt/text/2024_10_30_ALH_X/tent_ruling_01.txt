  Case Number:  23AHCV01487    Hearing Date:   October 30, 2024    Dept:  X DEFENDANT’S MOTION TO COMPEL RESPONSES TO DISCOVERY:Form Interrogatories (Set Two) (Motion No. 9447) Request for Production of Documents (Set Two) (Motion No. 2234)Defendant Patatanyan seeks an order compelling Plaintiff Leus to provide answers to Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Request for Production of Documents (Set Two).This case arises from a motor vehicle accident which occurred on October 15, 2022, at the intersection of Baldwin Avenue and Stanford Drive in Arcadia, California.  Plaintiff has claimed personal injuries because of the accident.  (Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 3.)  The parties conducted initial written discovery.  (Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 4.)  On or about June 7, 2024, this Court relieved Plaintiff’s counsel, Logan Quirk, as attorney of record.  (Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 5.)Tentative RulingDefendant’s motions to compel responses to Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Requests for Production (Set Two) are GRANTED.  Plaintiff is to provide verified responses within 30 days of Defendant’s mailing of a notice of ruling.AnalysisOn July 9, 2024, Defendant served by first-class mail Form Interrogatories (Set Two) and Requests for Production (Set Two) upon Plaintiff.  (Form Interrogatories—Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 6, Exh. A; Requests for Production—Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 6, Exh. A.)  Within 30 days of service of interrogatories, the party to whom the interrogatories are propounded shall serve the original of the response to them on the propounding party.  (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.260.)  Within 30 days after service of a demand for inspection, copying, testing, or sampling, the party to whom the demand is directed shall serve the original of the response to it on the party making the demand.  (Code Civ. Proc., § 2031.260.)  The answers were due on August 13, 2024.  (Form Interrogatories—Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 7; Requests for Production—Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 7.)  To date, Defendant has not received the discovery responses nor has Plaintiff requested an extension or sent any communication.  (Form Interrogatories—Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 8; Requests for Production—Petrosyan Decl., ¶ 8.)  No oppositions by Plaintiff have been filed. Accordingly, an order compelling Plaintiff to provide initial responses to the interrogatories and request for production is warranted.  (Code Civ. Proc., § 2030.290, subd. (b); Id., at § 2031.300, subd. (b).)  All objections are waived.  (Id., at § 2030.290, subd. (a); Id., at § 2031.300, subd. (a).)  No sanctions are requested.Because Plaintiff has provided no responses to Defendant’s discovery requests, Defendant’s motions to compel responses to form interrogatories and requests for production are granted. Moving Party is to give notice.
