Plaintiff’s unopposed Motion for Approval of PAGA Settlement
is GRANTED. 
 
Moving Party to give notice. 
 
No Opposition filed as of October 1, 2024. Due by September 19,
2024. (CCP §1005(b).)  
 
Labor Code §2699(l)(2) requires that the superior court
review and approve any settlement of any civil action filed pursuant to the
PAGA. Plaintiff’s unopposed Motion for Approval of PAGA Settlement is GRANTED.
The Court approves the PAGA settlement for the gross settlement amount of $450,000.
 
The Court retains jurisdiction to enforce the settlement
until performance in full of the terms of the settlement.  
 
Absent Opposition at the hearing, the Court is inclined to
sign the Proposed Order submitted in conjunction with the Moving Papers.
