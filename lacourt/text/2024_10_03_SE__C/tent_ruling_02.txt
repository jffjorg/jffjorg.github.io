 Case Number:  21STCV15339    Hearing Date:   October 3, 2024    Dept:  C Lee
vs. Payne, et al., Case No. 21STCV15339
 
This
is a medical malpractice action. Defendant Brian C. Payne, M.D. moves ex parte
to shorten time on the hearing on Defendant’s motion for summary judgment,
currently scheduled for March 13, 2025. Trial is scheduled for February 6,
2025. Defendant requests a date between December 11, 2024 and January 7, 2025
or in the alternative, within 30 days of trial. 
 
A
party may seek ex parte relief upon an affirmative factual showing
"irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for
granting relief ex parte." (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1202(c).)
 
Based
on the above, the motion is GRANTED.  The Court ADVANCES the hearing from
Thursday, March 13, 2025 to TODAY and CONTINUES it to Thursday, February 13,
2025 in Dept. SE-C at 10:30 A.M. The parties can move to continue trial with
Department F of this court. 
 
Moving
party to give notice.
