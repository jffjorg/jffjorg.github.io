The plaintiff, Alejandro Diaz, has filed a motion to compel defendants 631 Wilshire Boulevard, LLC and BL 631 Wilshire CA, LLC to respond to special interrogatories, set one, and a request for sanctions. The court has granted the motion. The defendants are ordered to serve verified responses to the special interrogatories, set one, without objections, within 20 days of the service of this order. The court has also awarded sanctions of $500.00 to the plaintiff's counsel against each defendant and their counsel of record, payable within 20 days of the service of this order. The court's ruling is based on the defendants' failure to respond to the discovery requests and their misuse of the discovery process.
