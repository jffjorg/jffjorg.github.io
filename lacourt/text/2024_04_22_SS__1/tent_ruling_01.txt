  Case Number:  22STCV00544    Hearing Date:   April 22, 2024    Dept:  1 The Court hereby GRANTS final
approval and awards/approves the following: 
(1) $203,333.33 for attorney fees to Class Counsel, Moon Law
Group, PC; (2) $15,786.27 for attorney costs to Class Counsel; (3)
enhancement payment of $7,500 to
the class representative, Adrian Velazquez (reduced form requested
$8,500); (4) $45,000
(75% of $60,000 PAGA penalty) to the LWDA; and (5) $7,500 for
settlement administration costs to ILYM
Group, Inc. 
 
Plaintiff’s counsel shall file an
amended proposed Order and Judgment, consistent with this ruling containing all
requisite terms, including the class definition, the enhancement award
determined by the Court and a separate Paragraph articulating the Gross
Settlement Award ( GSA) less all deductions therefrom and the Net Settlement
Amount (NSA) available to be paid to the class members. The Court orders class
counsel to file a final report summarizing all distributions made pursuant to
the approved settlement, supported by declaration. The Court will set a
non-appearance date for submission of a final report for February 13, 2025 with
the  report itself due five court days in
advance attesting among other things that all settlement funds have been
distributed.
