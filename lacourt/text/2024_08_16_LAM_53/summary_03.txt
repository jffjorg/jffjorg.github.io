The court has reviewed a petition for approval of a compromise of claim on behalf of minor claimant Armando Lopez De La O. The petition was filed by Petitioner Ana Maria De La O and is unopposed. The court finds that the settlement of $2,000,000 is fair and reasonable, and in the best interest of the minor claimant. The settlement includes a payment of $50,670.61 to the minor claimant, with $12,667.65 allocated to counsel for attorney's fees and $974.34 allocated to counsel for litigation costs. The remaining $37,028.62 will be invested in a single-premium deferred annuity, subject to withdrawal only with court authorization. The court grants the petition and sets an order to show cause re: proof of purchase of annuity for hearing on October 24, 2024.
