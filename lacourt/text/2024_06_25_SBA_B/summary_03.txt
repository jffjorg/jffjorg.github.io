**Tentative Ruling**

The Court has considered the motion to compel arbitration and the opposition, and has granted the motion. The action is stayed pending the outcome of the arbitration proceeding.

The case involves a dispute between Plaintiff Maksim Rusanov and Defendant Tesla Motors, Inc. over a 2023 Tesla Model Y that contained defects. The dispute arises from the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act.

The Court found that there is an arbitration agreement between the parties, and that Plaintiff did not opt-out. The arbitration agreement is contained in the Motor Vehicle Order Agreement (MVOA) and the Retail Installment Sale Contract (RISC).

The Court determined that the arbitration agreement is enforceable, and that the claims are within the scope of the agreement. The Court also found that the arbitration provisions are not unconscionable, despite being adhesion contracts.

The motion to compel arbitration is granted, and the action is stayed pending the outcome of the arbitration proceeding.
