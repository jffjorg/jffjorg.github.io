 Case Number:  23NWCV00567    Hearing Date:   October 9, 2024    Dept:  C Stewart Title Guaranty Company v. Jarrah 23NWCV00567
 
This
insurance coverage action was filed by Plaintiff Stewart Title Guaranty Company
on February 17, 2023. Trial is scheduled to begin on January 17, 2025.
Plaintiff's Motion for Summary Judgment is scheduled to be heard after trial on
March 25, 2025. 
 
Plaintiff
moves ex parte to advance the hearing date on its Motion for Summary Judgment
to be heard prior to the date for trial, but in no event earlier than December
18, 2024. 
 
Plaintiff's
Ex Parte Application for an Order to Advance the Hearing on Plaintiff's Motion
for Summary Judgment to a Date Prior to Trial is DENIED. This Court's
congested calendar cannot accommodate Plaintiff's request. Absent
agreement/stipulation of the parties, this Court is unable to continue the
trial date. The parties are not foreclosed from seeking a trial continuance in
Dept. SE-F. Moving Party to give notice.
