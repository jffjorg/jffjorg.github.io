The tentative ruling grants the stipulation for approval of the Private Attorney General Act (PAGA) settlement between Plaintiff Mayeli Becerra and Defendant BluSky Restoration Contractors, LLC. The settlement amount is $260,000, with $86,658 allocated to attorney fees, $15,000 to litigation costs, and $4,000 to settlement administration costs. The settlement also provides for a service award of $7,500 and allocates civil penalties to the Labor Workforce Development Agency (LWDA) and aggrieved employees. The court finds that the settlement is presumptively fair, the release is appropriately narrow, and the amount of attorney fees and costs is reasonable. The court will approve the settlement agreement, subject to certain conditions. The settlement agreement will be funded within 15 days of the administrator's transmission of the wiring instructions, and payments will be made to aggrieved employees and for other disbursements within 7 days after that.
