Court considers the moving papers. No opposition was filed.
 
“If a deponent fails to answer any question or to produce
any document, electronically stored information, or tangible thing under the
deponent's control that is specified in the deposition notice or a deposition
subpoena, the party seeking discovery may move the court for an order
compelling that answer or production.” (Code Civ. Proc., § 2025.480 (a).)
 
On December 3, 2024, Pedro Mota was served personally with a
deposition subpoena and notice of deposition for his deposition, and another for
his business. (Decl. Acosta, ¶¶ 4, 5, Ex. 1 and 2.) The depositions were set
for December 12, 2024 and December 11, 2024, respectively. (Decl. Acosta, ¶¶ 4,
5, Ex. 1 and 2.) The parties to be deposed did not appear on December 11 or 12.
(Decl. Acosta, ¶ 7.) 
 
The notice of deposition must be served at least 10
days before the date set for the deposition. (Code Civ. Proc., § 2025.270,
subd. (a).) Here, it was served eight and nine days before. 
 
            The motion to compel deposition is DENIED.
