  Case Number:  22NWCV01813    Hearing Date:   November 1, 2024    Dept:  C Travelers Property Casualty Company of America vs
Joslevin Realty Corp. Of L.A., et al., Case No.: 23NWCV01813
This is an interpleader case. Defendants Shanan Levin and
Tamara Levin (“Levins”) apply ex parte for an order advancing the hearing date
for their Motion to Compel Nonparty Saul Ewing LLP’s Compliance with Deposition
Subpoena for Production of Business Records, currently set for July 31, 2025 to
December 16, 2024.  In the alternative, Defendants request to continue
Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Arbitration from January 30, 2025 to a date 30 or
more days after July 31, 2025.  The Levins assert that they require
certain discovery to oppose a motion to compel arbitration.  The ex parte
application is GRANTED in part.  The Levins’ Motion to Compel Deposition
Subpoena for Production of Business Records is ADVANCED from July 31, 2025 to
today’s date and CONTINUED to April 3, 2025 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.
 Opposition and Reply briefs to be filed and served in compliance with the
new hearing date.
