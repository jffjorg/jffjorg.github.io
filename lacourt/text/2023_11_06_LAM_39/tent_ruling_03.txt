            This action came on regularly for trial on August 7,
2023, in Department #39 of the Stanley Mosk Courthouse, Los Angeles County
Superior Court, the Honorable Stephen I. Goorvitch Presiding.  The plaintiff, A. JAMAL SHAKIR, Jr.,
appearing by attorneys Carl E. Douglas and Jamon R. Hicks of Douglas / Hicks
Law, APC.  Defendants CITY OF LOS
ANGELES, ERIC ANDERSON, and ROBERT BECHTOL, appearing by attorneys Thomas C.
Hurrell and Kevin Rashidi of HURRELL CANTRALL LLP.  
A jury of twelve (12) persons and six (6) alternates
was regularly impaneled and sworn. 
Witnesses were sworn and testified. 
After hearing the evidence and arguments of counsel, the jury was duly
instructed by the Court and the cause was submitted to the jury and the answers
given by the jury, which verdict was in the words and figures, in Exhibit
A.  
As to the first cause of action—Violation of the Bane
Act—the jury found in favor of Defendants ERIC ANDERSON and ROBERT BECHTOL, and
against Plaintiff A. JAMAL SHAKIR, JR.
As to the second cause of action—Battery—the jury
found in favor of Defendant ROBERT BECHTOL, and against Plaintiff A. JAMAL
SHAKIR, JR.  
As to the third cause of action—Negligence—the jury
found in favor of Plaintiff A. JAMAL SHAKIR, JR., and against Defendants CITY
OF LOS ANGELES and ROBERT BECHTOL.  As to
damages, the jury awarded Plaintiff A. JAMAL SHAKIR, JR. a total of $3.75
million, based upon $1.25 in past non-economic losses and $2.5 million in
future non-economic losses.  The jury
found that Plaintiff A. JAMAL SHAKIR, JR. was not contributorily negligent.  The jury found that Defendant CITY OF LOS
ANGELES was 70% liable for the plaintiff’s injuries, and that Defendant ROBERT
BECHTOL was 30% liable for the plaintiff’s injuries. 
NOW THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED, ADJUDGED, and DECREED
THAT Plaintiff A. JAMAL SHAKIR, JR. has recovered $2,625,000 against Defendant
CITY OF LOS ANGELES and $1,125,000 against Defendant ROBERT BECHTOL, and shall
recover costs and disbursements from both defendants, jointly and severally, in
the amount of $_______________ pursuant to the filing of a memorandum of
costs.  
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
