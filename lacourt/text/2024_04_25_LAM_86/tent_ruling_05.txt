Case:                           Hermozo Textile,
LLC v. Reunion Apparel, Inc. et al. (23STCV24569)
 
TENTATIVE RULING:
 
Plaintiff Hermozo Textile, LLC’s UNOPPOSED
application for right to attach order with respect to defendant Reunion
Apparel, Inc. is GRANTED. 
 
Pursuant to CCP §484.090, the Court finds:

the claim is one upon
     which attachment may be issued;
plaintiff has established
     the probable validity of the claim;
attachment is not sought
     for any purpose other than recovery on the claim; and
the amount to be attached
     is greater than zero.

 
Plaintiff presents invoices for fabrics that were billed to defendant
Reunion Apparel, Inc. between July 2022 and March 2023. (Malekan Decl. ¶ 6
& Ex. 1.) The invoices provided that claims must be provided within 5 days
of receipt of goods. (Ibid.) The invoices also provided that defendant
agrees to pay attorney fees incurred in collection. (Ibid.) Defendant
accepted the shipments without dispute. (Id. ¶ 10.)  Defendant has not paid the invoices in any
amount. (Id. ¶ 8.) A balance of $25,833.43 remains outstanding. (Id.
¶ 8 & Ex. 2.) 
 
Based on the foregoing, the application will be granted in
the amount of $30,833.43, which includes the outstanding balance of $25,833.43,
plus estimated costs of $2,500.00 and estimated allowable attorney fees of $2,500.00.
(Malekan Decl. ¶ 6 & Ex. 1.) 
 
The Writ will issue upon the posting of bond in the amount
of $10,000. (CCP § 489.220.)
 
The Court will sign the proposed Right to Attache Order and
Order for Issuance of Writ of Attachment After Hearing (Form AT-120),
electronically received March 12, 2024.
