Summary in English text:

This is a tentative ruling on a motion to compel defendant Zhong Fang LLC to provide responses to plaintiff Xin He's discovery requests. The court grants the motion and orders the defendant to provide verified responses, without objections, to plaintiff's Form and Special Interrogatories and Requests for Production, Sets No. One, within 20 days from the date of the notice of ruling. Additionally, the court orders the truth of all matters specified in plaintiff's Requests for Admissions, Set No. One, deemed admitted. The court also imposes sanctions on the defendant and its counsel in the amount of $1,115.00, payable within 30 days from the date of the notice of ruling.
