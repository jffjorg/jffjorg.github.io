ALLEGATIONS: 
 
Victoria Farrington (“Farrington”), by and through her successor in interest,
Angel Shelburne, Angel Shelburne, John Shelburne, and Kathryn Avila bring this
action against NBCUniversal, LLC & NBCUniversal Media, LLC
(“Defendants”). Plaintiffs allege Farrington was injured while visiting
Universal Studios, a theme park owned by Defendants, and that these injuries
led to her eventual death. 
 
On June 1, 2023,
Plaintiffs filed their Complaint containing four causes of action for (1)
Negligence, (2) Violation of the Unruh Act, (3) Wrongful Death, and (4)
Survivor’s Action.  
 
On July 7, 2023.
Defendants filed their demurrer. Defendants demurred to the first, third, and
fourth causes of action. On October 13, 2023, Plaintiffs filed their First
Amended Complaint. As such, the Defendants’ demurrer is moot.
