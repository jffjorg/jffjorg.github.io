Bigfoot Ventures,
LTD. v. Knighton et al., Case No. 23SMCV01513
Hearing Date
December 5, 2023 
Bigfoot Ventures’
Motion for Leave to File First Amended Complaint 
 
Plaintiff Bigfoot
seeks leave to amend its complaint to add WayTools, LLC as a defendant and to
correct clerical errors and typos. Granting leave is proper, since the motion
is unopposed, and there is a strong public policy in favor of allowing
amendment at any phase of litigation. Atkinson v. Elk Corp. (2003) 109
Cal.App.4th 739, 761. GRANTED. 
 
Plaintiff to file
the first amended complaint within 5 court days.
