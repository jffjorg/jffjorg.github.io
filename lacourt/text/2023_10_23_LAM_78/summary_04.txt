The judge grants Plaintiff BLVD FOODS LLC's motion for summary judgment in this unlawful detainer action. The Plaintiff alleges that Defendant THE CHICKEN KOOP LLC defaulted on rent payments and failed to pay rent after a 10-Day Notice to Pay Rent or Quit was served. The Plaintiff provides evidence showing that Defendant is in possession of the premises, defaulted on rent, and failed to pay rent after the notice period expired. The judge finds that Plaintiff meets its burden of showing no triable issues of material fact remain as to each element of its unlawful detainer cause of action. The court notes that Plaintiff's recovery is limited to the $50,301.69 demanded in the 10-day notice and rental losses beginning after the expiration of the notice period until entry of judgment at a rate of $102.55 per day. The Defendant did not file a written opposition to this motion and therefore fails to meet its burden of proving that triable issues of material fact remain as to Plaintiff's unlawful detainer cause of action.
