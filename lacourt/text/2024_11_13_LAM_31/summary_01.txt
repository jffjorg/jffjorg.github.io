And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted the plaintiffs' unopposed motion for entry of judgment against defendant NBA Automotive, Inc. The plaintiffs had filed a "lemon law" action against NBA Automotive and others, and the parties had entered into a settlement agreement on January 29, 2024. However, NBA Automotive failed to pay the settlement amount of $15,000 within 30 days, as required by the agreement. The court has found that NBA Automotive breached the settlement agreement and has entered judgment against it in the amount of $16,728, which includes the settlement amount and attorneys' fees and costs. The court has also awarded the plaintiffs reasonable attorneys' fees and costs in the amount of $1,728.
