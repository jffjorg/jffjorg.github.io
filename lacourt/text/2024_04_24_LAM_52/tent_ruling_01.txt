  Case Number:  23STCV05932    Hearing Date:   April 24, 2024    Dept:  52 Hackler Flynn &
Associates, APC’s Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Defendant 
Elizabeth Ernster of
Hacker Flynn & Associates, APC moves to be relieved as counsel for
defendant C&M Trucking Services, LLC. 
The motion meets all requirements under California Rules of Court, rule
3.1362.  
The court notes, however,
that counsel’s proposed order on this motion does not include all scheduled
hearing dates.  After defendant’s counsel
filed this motion, plaintiff filed three motions to compel discovery responses.  Section 8 of the order must notify defendant
of the hearing dates.  
Hacker Flynn &
Associates, APC’s motion to be relieved as counsel for defendant is granted.  
The court hereby continues
the hearing on plaintiff Jose Pineda’s motion to compel discovery from May 20,
2024, to May 24, 2024, at 9:00 a.m.
Hacker Flynn &
Associates, APC shall forthwith submit an amended proposed order on Judicial
Council form MC-053 that lists the May 24 hearings on plaintiff’s three motions
to compel discovery.  The amended order will be effective upon
filing proof of service of the signed order on defendant C&M Trucking Services,
LLC.
