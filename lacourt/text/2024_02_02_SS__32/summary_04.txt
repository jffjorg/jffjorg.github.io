Summary:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to compel Plaintiff's neurological and orthopedic examinations. The motion was brought by Defendants Juan Carlos Olvera and American Way Management, Inc. The Court has denied the motion to compel the neurological examination, finding that the demand did not specify the time, place, manner, conditions, scope, and nature of the examination, and that the Defendant did not show good cause for Plaintiff to travel over 75 miles for the examination. The Court has granted the motion to compel the physical examination, but has ordered the Defendant to provide a more specific list of the areas to be tested in the amended demand. The Court has also declined to award monetary sanctions to either party, finding that the parties acted with substantial justification in making and opposing the motion.
