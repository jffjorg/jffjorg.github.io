The Court has issued a tentative ruling in favor of Defendant Cynthya Orozco's motion to compel responses to discovery requests and seek sanctions. The Plaintiff, Maria Ortez, has failed to timely respond to discovery requests, which constitutes a misuse of the discovery process. As a result, the Defendant is entitled to an order compelling service of verified responses to the discovery requests without objections. The Plaintiff is also ordered to pay sanctions of $370.00 to the Defense Counsel. The parties are directed to serve verified responses to the discovery requests within 20 days of service of this order.
