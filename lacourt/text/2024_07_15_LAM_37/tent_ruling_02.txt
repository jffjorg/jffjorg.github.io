


[1] CCP § 685.030
states: “(a) If a money judgment is satisfied in full pursuant to a writ under
this title, interest ceases to accrue on the judgment: (1) If the proceeds of
collection are paid in a lump sum, on the date of levy. (2) If the money
judgment is satisfied pursuant to an earnings withholding order, on the date
and in the manner provided in Section 706.024 or Section
706.028. (3) In any other case, on the date the proceeds of sale or collection
are actually received by the levying officer. (b) If a money judgment is
satisfied in full other than pursuant to a writ under this title, interest
ceases to accrue on the date the judgment is satisfied in full.”
