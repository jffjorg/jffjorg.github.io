The judge grants the motion to compel Defendant Archibuildz, LLC to provide responses to Plaintiffs' Requests for Production of Documents, Set One. The Plaintiffs, Richard Sweet and Lori Sweet, had served the production requests on October 17, 2023, but Defendant has not provided verified responses to date. The court finds that Defendant has waived all objections to the production requests. In addition, the court grants Plaintiffs' request for sanctions against Defendant and orders Defendant to pay sanctions in the sum of $675. The court orders Defendant to provide objection-free, verified responses to the discovery requests and to pay the sanctions within 30 days of the order.
