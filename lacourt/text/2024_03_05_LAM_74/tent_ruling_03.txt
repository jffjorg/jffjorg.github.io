 Case Number:  20STCV49545    Hearing Date:   March 5, 2024    Dept:  74 Brown
Strauss, Inc. v. Acme Metals & Steel Supply, Inc., et al.
Plaintiff’s Motion to Set Aside
Entry of Default
BACKGROUND 
            This
action arises from a contractual dispute.
            On
December 29, 2020, Plaintiff Brown Strauss, Inc. (Plaintiff) filed a complaint
against Acme Metals & Steel Supply, Acme Metals, LLC, Equitable
Transitions, Inc., and others.
            On
May 12, 2021, the court entered Equitable Transition Inc.’s default.
            On
May 19, 2021, the court entered Acme Metals & Steel Supply’s default. The
court also entered Acme Metals LLC’s default.
            On
December 26, 2023, Plaintiff filed a request for dismissal of the complaint
without prejudice. The request was rejected because of the parties in default.
The court instructed Plaintiff to set aside the defaults first.
            On
February 6, 2024, Plaintiff filed this motion to set aside the defaults.
DISCUSSION/CONCLUSION
            The
court grants Plaintiff’s motion to set aside the defaults so that Plaintiff can
dismiss the complaint in this action.
