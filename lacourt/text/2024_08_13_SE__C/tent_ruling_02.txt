 Case Number:  21STCV24604    Hearing Date:   August 13, 2024    Dept:  C Carina
Solares vs City of Norwalk, et al, Case No.: 21STCV24604
This lawsuit
involves a motor vehicle accident. Cross-Defendant Southern California Edison
moves ex parte for an order shortening time to hear its Motion to Strike, or in
the alternative to continue trial.  The
motion is currently scheduled for March 11, 2025. Trial is currently scheduled
for September 24, 2024.  The Court is unable
to hear the motion before the currently scheduled trial date.  Accordingly, Cross-Defendant Southern
California Edison’s Ex Parte Application to Shorten Time is DENIED.  Alternatively, the Motion to Strike is
ADVANCED from March 11, 2025 to this date and CONTINUED to November 14, 2024 at
10:30 a.m. in Department SE-C.  Opposition
and Reply briefs to be filed and served in compliance with the new hearing
date.  The parties are not foreclosed
from moving to continue trial in Dept. SE-F. 
Cross-Defendant to give notice.
