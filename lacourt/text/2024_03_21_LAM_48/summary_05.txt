The judge has granted the motion for approval of the PAGA settlement in the case of Eduardo Zelaya vs. Akash General Partnership, et al. The parties have agreed on the terms of the settlement, which includes a payment of $39,000.00 to be distributed as follows: 75% to the Labor and Workforce Development Agency (LWDA) and 25% to the aggrieved employees on a pro rata basis. The settlement was reached through arm's-length bargaining and is entitled to a presumption of fairness. The judge also finds that the attorney fees and costs of up to $13,000.00 and $1,491.00, respectively, are reasonable. The court will review and approve the settlement at the hearing on March 21, 2024, unless all parties submit on the tentative ruling.
