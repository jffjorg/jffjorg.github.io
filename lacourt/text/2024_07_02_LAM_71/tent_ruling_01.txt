JPMorgan Chase Bank N.A.’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having
considered all documents submitted in support of a default judgment, the Court
rules as follows:
 
Background
On November 11, 2023, JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A. (“Plaintiff”)
filed this action against Defendant Ewanda Jackson (“Defendant”) for common
counts. 
On February 5, 2024, the clerk entered default against Defendant.  On June 20, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request
for Court Judgment to be heard on July 2, 2024. 

 
Party’s
Request
Plaintiff
requests the Court to enter a default judgment against Defendant and award
Plaintiff damages in the amount of $28,775.15, for a total judgment of $28,775.15.
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all
applicable attachments. The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the
requested award and grants Plaintiff’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application for default judgment filed on June 20, 2024, is granted.
 Judgment in the amount of $28,775.15 is
entered against Defendant.
Plaintiff
is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
