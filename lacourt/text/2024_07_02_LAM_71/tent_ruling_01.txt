Muse Looks, Inc.’s Request for Default Judgment
          Having
considered all documents submitted in support of a default judgment, the Court
rules as follows:
 
Background
On November 20, 2023, Muse Looks, Inc. (“Plaintiff”) filed this
action against Defendants Emma Apparel, Inc., Seoul Yoo, and Sang Yoo (collectively,
“Defendants”) for fraud, common count, and breach of contract. 
On March 14, 2024, the clerk entered defaults against Defendants.  On May 29, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request
for Court Judgment to be heard on July 2, 2024. 

 
Party’s
Request
Plaintiff
requests the Court to enter a default judgment against Defendants and award
Plaintiff damages in the amount of $69,438.80, prejudgment interest at a rate
of 7% in the amount of $2,938.80, and costs in the amount of $435.00, for a
total judgment of $72,812.30.
 
Discussion
Plaintiff submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all
applicable attachments. The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the
requested award and grants Plaintiff’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application for default judgment filed on My 29, 2024, is granted.
 Judgment in the amount of $72,821.30 is
entered against Defendants.
Plaintiff
is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
