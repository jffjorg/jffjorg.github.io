The Plaintiff, Muds Production LLC and Xanakin Skywok, has filed a motion to serve the Defendant, Boom Records, LLC, through the California Secretary of State. The Plaintiffs have attempted to serve the Defendant personally, by mail, and through substitute service, but have been unsuccessful. The Plaintiffs have demonstrated that the Defendant cannot be served with reasonable diligence and that the Defendant's designated agent cannot be served with reasonable diligence. Therefore, the motion is GRANTED, and the Plaintiffs will be authorized to serve the Defendant through the California Secretary of State.
