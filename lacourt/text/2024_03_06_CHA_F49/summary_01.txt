This is a tentative ruling in the case of Mark A. Liker v. Roshmore Development, Inc., et al., a dispute over real property in Los Angeles. The plaintiff, Mark A. Liker, filed a complaint in October 2020 and served a Lis Pendens on the defendants in January 2021. The defendants filed a cross-complaint in April 2021, which was later amended in August 2023. The plaintiff filed a motion to compel compliance with the Court's November 1, 2023 order, which ordered the defendants to provide supplemental responses to the plaintiff's request for production of documents. The defendants have failed to comply with this order and have also failed to pay monetary sanctions ordered in the amount of $2,760.00. The plaintiff seeks additional monetary sanctions in the amount of $3,850.00. The Court grants the motion to compel compliance in part, ordering the defendants to comply with the Court's November 1, 2023 order and to pay monetary sanctions.
