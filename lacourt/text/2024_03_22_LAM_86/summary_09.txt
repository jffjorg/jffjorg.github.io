And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has denied the motion for the appointment of a receiver to manage the rehabilitation of a vacant commercial building in Huntington Park. The City of Huntington Park (Plaintiff) sought to declare the property a public nuisance and appoint a receiver to manage its rehabilitation due to hazardous and substandard conditions. The Defendants, Emelina Alvarez and Marie Fine, opposed the motion. The Judge found that the appointment of a receiver is not necessary under these facts, as there is no issue related to preservation of the property and the Plaintiff has other avenues available to address the problem. The Judge also noted that the property does not present a health and safety threat that requires the appointment of a receiver.
