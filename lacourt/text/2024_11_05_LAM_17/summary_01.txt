The judge has granted Defendant Dal-Tile Distribution, LLC's motion to strike certain allegations from the Second Amended Complaint (SAC). The judge found that Plaintiffs failed to seek leave of court to amend the complaint and add additional stone products, and that they went outside the scope of the court's order granting leave to amend. The judge also found that Plaintiffs failed to meet and confer with Defendant Dal-Tile Distribution, LLC before filing the SAC. The judge struck two additional stone products, "One Quartz" and "One Quartz Surfaces", from the SAC, as well as allegations regarding Defendants' corporate structure.
