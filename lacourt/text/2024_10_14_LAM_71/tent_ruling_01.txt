1.    
Motion to Compel Mi Young- FROG
Having reviewed Defendant’s
Motion to Compel Mi Young’s Answers to FROG, the Court rules as follows.
          On May 13, 2024, Defendant served FROG (Set One) on Mi
Young.  (Decl. of Kirschen ¶3, Exh. A.)  On June 20, 2024, after Mi Young did not
respond, Defendant’s counsel sent Mi Young’s counsel an email requesting
verified answers to discovery be provided. 
(Decl. of Kirschen ¶4, Exh. B.) 
As of the date of this motion no verified responses were served on
Defendant.  (Decl. of Kirschen ¶5.)  Defendant now moves to compel responses.
          Defendant’s motion to compel answers to FROG is granted pursuant
to C.C.P. §2030.290.  Mi Young is ordered
to provide verified responses pursuant to C.C.P. §2030.210 without objections within
20 days of this ruling.
Defendant requests monetary
sanctions totaling $510.00 against Mi Young. 
          The Court awards sanctions pursuant to C.C.P. §2030.290(c) in
the amount of $210.00, calculated as follows:
$60.00 filing fee +
($150.00/hour x 1 hour) = $210.00
The sanctions are payable by
Mi Young within 20 days of this order.
Moving Party is to give
notice of this ruling.
