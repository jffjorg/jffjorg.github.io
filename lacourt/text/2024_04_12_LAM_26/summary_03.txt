This is a tentative ruling by Judge Yolanda Orozco in the case of 1333 Las Palmas Avenue, LLC (Plaintiff) vs. Ryan Torigoe (Defendant). The case is an unlawful detainer action for failure to pay rent. The Plaintiff filed a motion to compel the Defendant to provide responses to requests for production and also requested monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,035.00. The Defendant failed to respond to the requests for production and did not file an opposition to the motion. The Court granted the motion to compel and imposed monetary sanctions in the amount of $385.00. The Court ordered the Defendant to provide notice of the order and file proof of service.
