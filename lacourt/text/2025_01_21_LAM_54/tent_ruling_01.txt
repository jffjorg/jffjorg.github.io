The Court considers the moving papers. No opposition has been received.
 
Plaintiff moves for an order granting approval of the proposed
settlement of claims brought pursuant to the Private Attorneys general Act of
2004 (“PAGA”), Cal. Lab Code § 2698, et seq.  Pursuant to the proposed settlement,
Defendants have agreed to pay $125,000.00 total, all of which is allocated for
the representative PAGA claims after cost deductions. The settlement will be
distributed as follows: (1) $41,666.67 in attorney’s fees and $10,942.84 in
costs; (2) $44,542.87 to be paid to LWDA and $14,847.62 to be paid to the
aggrieved employees; (3) $10,000.00 to the named Plaintiff; and (4) $3,000.00
settlement administration fee.
 
Based on the moving papers, the Court finds that the settlement is
reasonable and meets the requirements of PAGA. Defendants have not opposed. The
motion is GRANTED.
