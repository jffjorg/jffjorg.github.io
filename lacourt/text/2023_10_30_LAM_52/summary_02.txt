Summary in English text:

This is a tentative ruling by a Judge on a motion to compel further responses to requests for production. Plaintiff David Monge is seeking to compel defendant Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. to provide further responses to requests for production, while defendant is resisting the motion, arguing that it is untimely and that the requests are too broad. The Judge holds that the motion is timely and that defendant's objections to some requests are sustained, while overruling defendant's objections to other requests. The Judge orders defendant to provide further verified responses to requests Nos. 7, 9, 40, and 48-59, and to produce any additional responsive documents concurrently with its written responses.
