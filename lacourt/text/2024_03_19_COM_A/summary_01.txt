The Court has reviewed the parties' dispute regarding the purchase of examination gloves, which did not conform to specifications. The parties arbitrated their dispute and the arbitrator issued a final award in favor of the plaintiff for $9,155,642. The plaintiff has timely filed a motion to confirm the arbitration award, and no opposition has been filed. The Court finds that the arbitration award should be confirmed, as it meets the requirements of California Code of Civil Procedure sections 1286 and 1287.4. The Court grants the plaintiff's motion and orders the plaintiff to submit a proposed judgment.
