The Superior Court of California, County of Los Angeles, Department 72, has issued a tentative ruling granting the motions for approval of compromise in the case of Debraha Williams, Josiah Williams, and Jazaha Williams (collectively, "Plaintiffs") against Eva E Moreno Trust and Eva E Moreno (collectively, "Defendants"). The case involves a landlord-tenant dispute over habitability issues at a property allegedly owned by the Defendants. The Plaintiffs, who are minors, alleged various causes of action, including breach of covenant of quiet enjoyment, breach of implied warranty of habitability, and intentional infliction of emotional distress. The parties entered into a settlement agreement on November 15, 2023, which was filed on April 19, 2024. The Court granted the settlement and approved the compromises of Debraha and Josiah's claims. The settlement value is $22,500.00, which will be distributed evenly among the Plaintiffs. The Court also approved the reimbursement of Plaintiffs' counsel for costs advanced in the litigation.
