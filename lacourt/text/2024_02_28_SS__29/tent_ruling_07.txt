Personal Injury Hub Courts (at page 7), “PI Hub Courts will not hear Motions to
Compel Further Discovery Responses to Discovery until the parties have engaged
in an Informal Discovery Conference (IDC).
Accordingly, the hearing on these motions is
continued for approximately 60 days so that an IDC may be scheduled and
completed.
