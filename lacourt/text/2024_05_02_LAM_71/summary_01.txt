This tentative ruling concerns a lemon law action under the Song-Beverly Consumer Warranty Act. The parties entered into a Settlement Agreement for restitution, and the plaintiff, Thomas Jones, and his wife, Ilana Jones, are seeking attorneys' fees, costs, and expenses. The defendant, Jaguar Land Rover North America, LLC, challenged the plaintiff's counsel's hourly rates as excessive and objected to the billed hours. The court finds the hourly rates to be reasonable and reduces the billed hours by 8.3 hours. The court grants the plaintiff's request for attorneys' fees in the reduced amount of $85,435.00 and for costs in the amount of $5,145.24. The court also grants the motion for attorneys' fees, costs, and expenses in the total amount of $90,580.24.
