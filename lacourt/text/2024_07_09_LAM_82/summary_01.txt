The judge has tentatively ruled on the demurrers filed by the City and Metro. The court has found that the pleadings are sufficient to state a cause of action for writ of mandate and declaratory relief.

Regarding the City's demurrer, the court has found that the City's requirements for the Project, including the need for Metro's clearance, are not supported by the pleadings. The court has also found that the City's refusal to issue the permit without Metro's approval is not justified.

Regarding Metro's demurrer, the court has found that Metro has a ministerial duty to comply with the MRDC in its design of the Station. The court has also found that Metro's failure to account for the Project in its design of the Station is not justified.
