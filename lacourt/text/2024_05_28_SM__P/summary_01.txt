This tentative ruling addresses the motion to strike the prayer for punitive damages and requests for attorneys' fees in the case of Michel Nedjathaeim v. HOA Premier Management LLC, et al. The plaintiff alleges that the defendants failed to repair his condominium, resulting in lost profits. The defendants move to strike the prayer for punitive damages and request for attorneys' fees. The court grants the motion to strike the prayer for punitive damages, but allows the plaintiff to amend to allege facts establishing malice, fraud, and/or oppression. The court also denies the motion to strike the request for attorneys' fees, as it is contractually allowable.
