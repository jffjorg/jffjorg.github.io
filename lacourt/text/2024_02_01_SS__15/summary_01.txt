This is a tentative ruling on a motion for trial preference filed by David Rosedale and Kimberly Rosedale. The court has discretion to grant the motion if the party seeking preference provides clear and convincing medical documentation showing that they have an illness or condition that raises substantial medical doubt of their survival beyond six months. The court grants the motion and sets the trial date for May 13, 2024. The parties are ordered to meet and confer on a trial setting order.
