As for the English-text summary, here it is:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling in this personal injury action, sustaining the demurrer filed by defendant Regional Acceptance Corporation (RAC) with 20 days leave to amend. The Complaint alleges that RAC negligently entrusted Andrew Jacob Zwirez with a vehicle despite knowing of his propensities to drink and drive, and that Zwirez drove the vehicle under the influence and collided with the plaintiff's vehicle. The Court finds that the Complaint does not sufficiently plead RAC's connection to Zwirez, RAC's ownership interest in the vehicle, or any duty RAC owes to the plaintiff. Therefore, the demurrer is sustained, and the plaintiff has the opportunity to amend the Complaint within the specified timeframe.
