And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a Tentative Ruling granting the motion of specially appearing defendant Joselyn Ancheta to quash service of summons. The Judge finds that Plaintiffs Amanda Swulius, Bunny's Buddies, and The Organic Bunny Inc. have not met their burden to show that Defendant Joselyn Ancheta committed an intentional act that was aimed at or targeted California, with the knowledge that her act would cause harm in the state. The Judge also finds that Plaintiffs have not shown that Defendant purposefully availed herself of forum benefits. As a result, the Judge grants Defendant's motion to quash service of summons and orders that service of summons on Defendant is quashed, and Defendant is dismissed from the action.
