 Case Number:  22STCV14252    Hearing Date:   November 15, 2023    Dept:  55 Motion to be Relieved as Counsel of Record for
Defendant 6600 Selma Ave., LLCThe unopposed motion is granted.
The Court will sign and file the proposed, form order lodged with the motion.Joyce Law, P.C. moves to be relieved as counsel of
record, based upon form declarations evidencing that there has been a breakdown
in the attorney-client relationship and more information could be provided in
camera. No opposing papers have been filed in order to evidence any prejudice
to the client.  See  Rules Prof. Conduct,
Rule 1.16(d).  Moving counsel’s
declaration shows a cognizable ground for withdrawal:  Breakdown in the attorney-client relationship
(or personality clash).  Estate of
Falco v. Decker (1987) 188 Cal.App.3d 1004, 1014.Procedurally, the form notice, declaration, proposed
order, and proof of service are sufficiently in compliance.  See  CRC Rule 3.1362.
