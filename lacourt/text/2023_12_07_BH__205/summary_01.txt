The tentative ruling is about a theft of trade secrets case between TRAVEL, ENTERTAINMENT AND MARKETING, LLC (TEAM) and BRIAN DELINO, et al. TEAM provides event planning services for college fraternities and sororities. Brian Delino, a former sales representative at TEAM, allegedly stole TEAM's business and used confidential information. TEAM sued Delino and his company, BigDaddyFormals, and Delino countersued TEAM and its owner, Michael Olivas.

The hearing is about Plaintiff's motion to compel Defendants to provide further discovery responses to document requests and special interrogatories. The Court requires a joint statement with four columns, but the one filed did not include Defendants' position. The Court continues the hearing to January 8, 2024, and orders Plaintiff to re-file the joint statement with Defendants' position by December 15, 2023. Defendants must cooperate with Plaintiff to complete the joint statement.
