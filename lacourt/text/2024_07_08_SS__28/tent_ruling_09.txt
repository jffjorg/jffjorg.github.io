 Case Number:  22STCV32584    Hearing Date:   July 8, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On October
4, 2022, Plaintiffs Victor Choi, Cody Chen Hai Choi, and Steven
Chen Wei Choi filed this action against Defendants Stephen Groff (“Defendant”)
and Does 1-10 for motor vehicle tort and general negligence. 
On
November 1, 2022, the Court appointed Victor Choi to serve as guardian ad litem
for Plaintiffs Cody Chen Hai Choi and
Steven Chen Wei Choi. 
On
January 25, 2024, Defendant filed an answer. 
On
May 7, 2024, Petitioner Victor Choi filed petitions for expedited approval of
the compromises of the actions of minor Plaintiffs Cody Chen Hai Choi and Steven Chen Wei Choi. 
PETITIONER’S REQUESTS 
Petitioner
asks the Court for expedited approval of the compromises of the actions of
minor Plaintiffs Cody Chen Hai Choi and Steven Chen
Wei Choi. 
DISCUSSION 
The
petitions are complete and include all required attachments.  The Court grants the petitions. 
At
Petitioner’s request, the net balance of $1,923.52
for each minor Plaintiff will be paid or
delivered to Petitioner Victor Choi, the parent, at 17208 Brighton Way,
Gardena, CA 90247 on the terms and under the conditions specified in
Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without bond.   
CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor
Plaintiff Cody Chen Hai Choi’s action filed by Petitioner Victor Choi on May 7,
2024. The Court orders the net settlement amount of $1,923.52 to be paid or
delivered to Victor Choi at 17208
Brighton Way, Gardena, CA 90247 on the terms and under the conditions specified
in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without bond. 
The
Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the compromise of minor
Plaintiff Steven Chen Wei Choi’s action filed by Petitioner Victor Choi on May
7, 2024. The Court orders the net settlement amount of $1,923.52 to be paid or
delivered to Victor Choi at 17208 Brighton Way, Gardena, CA 90247 on the terms
and under the conditions specified in Probate Code sections 3401-3402, without
bond. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
