  Case Number:  20STCV35487    Hearing Date:   June 17, 2024    Dept:  57 Defendants' motion for an award of their attorney's fees and costs following entry of judgment in their favor on all of Plaintiff's claims is denied.   Plaintiff's claims under the Fair Employment and Housing Act were not frivolous, unreasonable, or groundless when brought, or at any time during the litigation, and her claims under the Labor Code for violations of wage and hour laws were not brought in bad faith.  Accordingly, Defendants are not entitled to an award of fees and costs.In light of this ruling on Defendants ' motion, Plaintiff's motion to tax the Defendants' costs is denied as moot.
