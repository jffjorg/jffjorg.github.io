Plaintiff L.A. Arena Company, LLC’s Request for
Default Judgment
          Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default
judgment, the Court rules as follows:
 
Background
On July 17, 2024,
L.A. Arena Company, LLC (“Plaintiff”) filed its Complaint against Defendant Icyhot
Heating & Air Conditioning Inc, aka “Icyhot Heating and Air Conditioning” (“Defendant”)
for breach of contract.
On September 5,
2024, the clerk entered default against Defendant.  On October 18, 2024, Plaintiff filed a
Request for Court Judgment to be heard on November 27, 2024.
 
Party’s Request
Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a
default judgment against Defendant and award Plaintiff $309,347.04 consisting
of $293,658.88 in damages, $15,030.56 in prejudgment interest at a rate of 10%,
and $657.60 in costs. 
 
Discussion
Plaintiff
submitted a completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments.
The Court finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants
Plaintiff L.A. Arena Company, LLC’s request in full.
 
Conclusion
The application
for default judgment filed on October 18, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $309,347.04 is entered against Defendant Icyhot Heating & Air Conditioning
Inc, aka “Icyhot Heating and Air Conditioning.”
Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of
this ruling.
