And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has tentatively ruled in favor of Manufactured Networks, Inc.'s petition to confirm an arbitration award against Vana Life Foods, L.P. The arbitration was held on July 10, 2024, and Vana Life Foods, L.P. did not appear. The arbitrator awarded Manufactured Networks, Inc. damages of $216,651.82 plus interest, attorney's fees, and costs. Manufactured Networks, Inc. served the petition to confirm the arbitration award on Vana Life Foods, L.P. via certified mail, and received confirmation of receipt via signature. The judge has deemed the service of the petition sufficient and has granted the petition. The judge orders Manufactured Networks, Inc. to file and serve a proposed judgment within 15 days incorporating the arbitrator's award, as consistent with this ruling.
