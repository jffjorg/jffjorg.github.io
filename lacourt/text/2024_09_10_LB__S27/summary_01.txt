Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on a motion to amend a judgment and request an injunction. The plaintiff, Interpool, Inc. dba Trac Intermodal, seeks to add HHM International LLC as a judgment debtor and to prevent HHM Intl., Inc. and its affiliates from engaging in certain activities that harm the plaintiff. The judge denies the motion for two reasons. First, the plaintiff has not served the proposed LLC alter ego judgment debtor with the moving papers, which is a necessary precondition to entry of judgment against it. Second, the Court cannot simply impose an injunction on noticed motion absent a pending action for fraudulent transfer. The judge orders the plaintiff to give notice to the Court and sets a hearing date of September 10, 2024.
