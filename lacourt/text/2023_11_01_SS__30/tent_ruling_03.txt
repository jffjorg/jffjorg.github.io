 Case Number:  21STCV46117    Hearing Date:   November 1, 2023    Dept:  30 LIANDRA DANENBERG-HELM vs MARK MARKWELL, et al.Re: Motion to TransferRuling:Motion to Transfer is granted. Moving party to give notice.Discussion:Venue is not proper pursuant to Code of Civil Procedure section 395. Proper venue lies in San   Diego County.   Matter is ordered transferred to said county upon payment of the transfer fee in the sum of   $50.00 payable to the Los Angeles Superior Court and the submission of a separate check for the   filing fee in the sum of $435.00 payable to San Diego Superior Court, within 30 days by MARK   MARKWELL (Defendant) and SHANNON MARKWELL (Defendant) in the Clerk's Office at   the Los Angeles Superior Court.
