The tentative ruling addresses the demurrer filed by the defendants, PQM, Inc. and Erin Hoy and Zale Harris, to the complaint filed by plaintiff Jesse Vargas. The complaint alleges various claims, including breach of contract, discrimination, harassment, retaliation, and unfair competition. The court sustains the demurrer to some of the claims, including breach of implied contract, breach of the covenant of good faith and fair dealing, and violation of Labor Code sections 230, et seq., but overrules the demurrer to others, including wrongful termination, discrimination, and retaliation claims. The court also grants leave to amend the complaint to some of the claims.
