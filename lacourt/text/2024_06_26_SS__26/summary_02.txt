And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel responses to the request for production of documents and request for sanctions. The defendant, Raymond Sharifpour dba White Diamond Auto Sales, is ordered to serve verified responses to the discovery requests without objections within 20 days of service of this order. Additionally, the defendant and their counsel are ordered to pay sanctions of $485.00 to the plaintiff's counsel within 20 days of service of this order. The judge found that the defendant failed to serve initial responses to the discovery requests, despite a meet and confer effort, and that this failure constitutes a misuse of the discovery process. The sanctions are awarded based on half-an-hour of attorney time billed at $650.00 per hour and costs of $160.00.
