 Case Number:  24STCV15151    Hearing Date:   December 11, 2024    Dept:  74 Silver
Strand Care, LLC v. Duenas et al. 
Defendant East Valley Adult Day
Health Center, Inc.’s Motion to Compel Initial Responses to Requests for
Admission.
 
On
October 9, 2024, Defendant filed its motion to compel Plaintiff to respond to requests
for admission. On October 18, 2024, Plaintiff served responses, making the
motion moot.
Defendant
requests discovery sanctions. The motion’s caption and the body of the motion identify
Plaintiff Silver Strand Care, LLC by name. (Mattco Forge, Inc. v. Arthur
Young & Co. (1990) 223 Cal.App.3d 1429, 1435.) Defendant requests
$1,200.00 in sanctions at $300 per hour for four hours of work.  (Taheripour Decl., ¶ 15-18.)  The Court finds this reasonable. 
CONCLUSION
            The
Motion is Moot. The Court orders Silver Strand Care, LLC to pay $1,200 to
Defendant’s counsel within 30 days.
