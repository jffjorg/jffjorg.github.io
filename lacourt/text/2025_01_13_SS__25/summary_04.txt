The petitioners, Iehab A. Ibrahim and Christina M. Ibrahim, have filed a petition to release property from a mechanic's lien recorded by Universal Floor Source, Inc. The petitioners have complied with the service requirements and provided a verified petition that meets the requirements of California Civil Code section 8484. The court has found that the time period to enforce the lien has expired and that the petitioners have given notice to the respondent, Universal Floor Source, Inc. Therefore, the petition is granted, and the petitioners may move for attorney's fees. The moving parties are ordered to give notice.
