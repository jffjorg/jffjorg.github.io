  Case Number:  21STCV12948    Hearing Date:   December 14, 2023    Dept:  30 TINNA JONSDOTTIR vs HONGSHI SUN, et al.TENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Defendant, Hongshi Sun, is CONTINUED. Counsel for Defendant has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). However, the papers have not been served on Defendant. (See CRC Rule 3.1362(d).) In addition, the proposed order and declaration have outdated hearing dates listed. (See CRC Rule 3.1362(e).) As a result, counsel is ordered to file a new proposed order, and declaration, list updated hearing dates, and serve Defendant with the notice of motion, motion and declaration.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
