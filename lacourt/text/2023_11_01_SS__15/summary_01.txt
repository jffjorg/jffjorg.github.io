Summary in English text:

This is a tentative ruling by a Judge regarding three motions to compel depositions filed by the defendant, Zenith Electronics LLC. The motions were filed after the plaintiffs, John D. Fernandez, Chirstina M. Fernandez, and Katherine Panaqua, failed to appear for depositions scheduled for September 13-15, 2023. The Judge granted the motions, ordering the plaintiffs to appear for depositions within 15 days of the date of the order. The Judge also imposed monetary sanctions against the plaintiffs in the amount of $480 each, payable within 20 days of the date of the order. The Judge based the ruling on California Code of Civil Procedure sections 2025.010, 2025.280, and 2025.450, which govern the taking of depositions. The Judge found that the defendant had served valid deposition notices and that the plaintiffs had failed to file objections or oppositions to the notices.
