Walter N. Marks,
Inc. et al. v. J. Freund, PC, Case No. 23SMCV04297
Hearing Date
February 28, 2024
Plaintiff Walter
N. Marks’ Motion for Stipulated Judgment (UNOPPOSED)
 
On December 5,
2023 the parties stipulated to settle this matter with defendant J. Freund, PC
agreeing to vacate plaintiff’s property by December 31, 2024 and to pay the
outstanding rent due within thirty days. The stipulation included language
allowing the court to retain jurisdiction under Cal. Code of Civ. Proc. §664.6.
 
Plaintiff Marks
presents a declaration stating defendant Freund has not made the required
payment. Wang decl. ¶¶1-3. The court retains jurisdiction under §664.6, and
there is no opposition. Entry of judgment is proper. GRANTED.
