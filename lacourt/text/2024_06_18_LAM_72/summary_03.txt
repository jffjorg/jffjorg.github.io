The judge's tentative ruling grants the petitions of Reina De Leon and the minor plaintiffs, Estrella, Lucas, and Juan, to approve the compromise of their claims against Queen 1, LLC and others. The compromise provides for a total of $90,000, with each of the adult plaintiffs, Reina De Leon and Rudy Lucas Tzic, receiving $37,500, and each of the minor plaintiffs receiving $5,000.
