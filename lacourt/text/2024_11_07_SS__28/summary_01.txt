The judge has considered the motions, oppositions, and replies in this case and has ruled as follows. The plaintiff, Maria Socorro Ortega, filed a complaint against McDonald's Restaurants of California, Inc., McDonald's USA, LLC, and Does 1-50 for general negligence and premises liability. The defendants filed answers, and the plaintiff filed a motion to compel the defendant to produce a person most qualified to testify about subjects listed in the deposition notice. The defendant opposed the motion, arguing that the witness, Maria Pedraza, was a person most qualified and that the defendant had agreed to produce another witness, Jose Miguel, for a deposition. The plaintiff's reply did not mention the defendant's agreement to produce Jose Miguel. The court denied the motion, finding that the plaintiff had not shown that the defendant failed to produce a person most qualified. The court ordered the moving party to give notice of this ruling and to file the proof of service of this ruling with the court within five days.
