MOTION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT (filed 10/3/204)
 
TENTATIVE RULING
 
            The Court grants the motion for summary
judgment. In the non-opposition motion, Plaintiff states that he does not
oppose the motion for summary judgment. Plaintiff further states that despite
Defendant appearing on Plaintiff’s paychecks and IRS Form W-2, Plaintiff does
not oppose Defendant’s position that Defendant rebutted the presumption that
Defendant was Plaintiff’s employer under California Government Code section
12928.
