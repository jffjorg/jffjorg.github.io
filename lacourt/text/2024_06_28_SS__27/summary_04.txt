English-text summary:

This is a tentative ruling by Judge Lee S. Arian on June 28, 2024, regarding a motion to compel initial discovery responses, a motion to deem RFAs admitted, and requests for sanctions. The plaintiff, Edwin Castanon, failed to respond to discovery requests despite being served with them on March 14, 2024, and the deadline was April 18, 2024. The defendants, Jose Francisco Cisneros, Bluagent Technologies, Inc., and ACV Logistics, LLC, moved the court to compel the plaintiff's initial discovery responses and deem the RFAs admitted. The court granted the motions, ordering the plaintiff to produce verified and complete responses to the discovery requests within 30 days. The court also granted sanctions in the amount of $1,500 ($500 per defendant) to the defendants.
