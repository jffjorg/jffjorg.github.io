* Defendant: City of Los Angeles et al., including Terraphase Engineering Inc.

**Background**

* Plaintiff was injured on August 11, 2021, when she tripped and fell on a broken manhole cover near 3191 Casitas Avenue.
* Plaintiff alleges two causes of action: general negligence and premises liability.

**Facts**

* Plaintiff was walking near 3191 Casitas Avenue when she stepped on a broken, deteriorating, or unsecured manhole cover, causing her to trip and fall.
* Plaintiff alleges that the defendants, including Terraphase, had actual or constructive notice of the dangerous condition and failed to correct it.

**Disputes**

* Defendant Terraphase moves to strike portions of Plaintiff's allegations that improperly classify it as a public entity.
* Plaintiff argues that the motion is procedurally improper and that Defendant lacks standing to bring the motion.

**Acts**

* Defendant requests judicial notice of its Secretary of State filings, which establish that it is a private corporation.
* Plaintiff objects to judicial notice, but the Court finds that the request is proper.

**Agreements**

* The Court grants Defendant's motion to strike portions of Plaintiff's allegations that improperly classify Defendant as a public entity.
* The Court grants Plaintiff leave to amend the complaint within 20 days to clarify its allegations against Defendant Terraphase.
