The Court has reviewed the supplemental filings of the parties and has found that Petitioner CHA Hollywood Medical Center, L.P.'s Motion for Attorneys' Fees is GRANTED in the amount of $10,454.50. The Court has determined that the time spent by Petitioner's counsels of record on this matter is reasonable, but has reduced the requested fee amount of Mr. Rayburn from $1,932.00 to $1,529.50 due to discrepancies in the billing entries.

The Court has considered the facts of the case, including the filing of a Petition to Release Property from Mechanic's Lien, the recordation of a mechanic's lien, and the conduct of hearings and supplemental briefings. The Court has applied the applicable laws, including California Civil Code Section 8488 and California Code of Civil Procedure Section 1032, and has determined that Petitioner is the prevailing party entitled to attorneys' fees.

The Court has also considered the arguments made by Respondent's counsel, Lauren B. Stec, regarding the reasonableness of the requested attorneys' fees, but has found that the time spent by Petitioner's counsels of record on this matter is reasonable. The Court has granted the motion in the amount of $10,454.50, which is the amount of fees requested by Petitioner minus the amount of fees already approved by the Court.
