Here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Yuehua Guo (Plaintiff) v. AWA Forged Composites, LLC (Defendant). The Plaintiff has moved to compel further responses from the Defendant to requests for production of documents, form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and requests for admission. The Defendant has not opposed the motions.

The Court has determined that the Defendant's responses to the discovery requests are insufficient and have failed to properly respond to the requests. The Court has ordered the Defendant to produce responsive documents and has granted the Plaintiff's motion to compel further responses.

The Court has also imposed sanctions on the Defendant in the amount of $2,940 for the Defendant's failure to properly respond to the discovery requests. The Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of this order and file proof of service of same.

The Court has set a hearing date of July 12, 2024, to allow the Defendant to retain counsel and oppose the motions. If the Defendant does not retain counsel and oppose the motions by that date, the Court intends to enter an order granting the Plaintiff's motions to compel and awarding the Plaintiff sanctions.
