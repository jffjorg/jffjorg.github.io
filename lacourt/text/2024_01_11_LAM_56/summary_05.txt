The motion sought an extension of time to submit responses to plaintiff YOGI SECURITIES HOLDINGS, LLC's requests for production and monetary sanctions against the plaintiff and its counsel. The court has considered the moving, opposition, and reply papers and has sustained plaintiff's objections to the evidence submitted in support of the reply papers.

The court finds that the motion is moot because the date of the requested extended deadline has passed. The court also denies the moving party's request for monetary sanctions. The court orders that the moving party give notice of this ruling.
