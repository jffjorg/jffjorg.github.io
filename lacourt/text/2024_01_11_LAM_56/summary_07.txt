Here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has considered the motions filed by the defendant, Comfort at Home, to compel further responses to discovery requests. The defendant moved to compel further responses to Requests for Production, Form Interrogatories, and Special Interrogatories. The Judge finds that the plaintiff, Alina Vayntrub, has not justified her objections to the discovery requests and grants the motions. The Judge orders the plaintiff to provide supplemental, code-compliant responses within 20 days of the date of the order.

The Judge also finds that the defendant's requests for monetary sanctions are reasonable and grants sanctions in the amount of $2,520. The Judge declines to issue sanctions in connection with the Requests for Production motion.

The Judge's ruling is subject to change pending any further submissions or appearances at the hearing.
