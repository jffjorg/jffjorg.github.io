The judge has issued a tentative ruling on Plaintiff Christina T. Perez's motion to serve Defendants California Gamma Chapter House and F&S Services, Inc. via the California Secretary of State. The Plaintiff is seeking an order directing service of the summons and complaint on the defendants by delivering by hand to the Secretary of State or to any person employed in their office.

The judge has also found that the defendants have been a suspended corporation since October 2021, and that attempts to serve them in person have been unsuccessful.

The judge has granted the Plaintiff's motion to serve Defendant California Gamma Chapter House via the California Secretary of State, as well as the Plaintiff's motion to serve Defendant F&S Services, Inc. via the California Secretary of State.
