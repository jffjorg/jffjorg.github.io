Defendants’
demurrer to the original Complaint is MOOT.
 
            On
10/24/2023, Plaintiffs James Davis, Paranotek, LLC, and Astra Veda Corporation
(collectively, Plaintiffs) filed suit against Scott Eppinga, Bradley M.
Listermann, Benjamin Eppinga, and Think Humble, Inc., alleging: (1) breach of
fiduciary duties; (2) rescission; (3) breach of the duty of good faith and fair
dealing; and (4) fraud. 
 
            On
2/23/2024, Defendants Scott Eppinga, Benjamin Eppinga, and Think Humble, Inc.
demurred to Plaintiffs’ Complaint. 
 
            On
3/18/2024, Plaintiffs filed a first amended complaint (FAC) in lieu of an
opposition.
 
            Accordingly,
Defendants’ demurrer to the original Complaint is moot. 
 
 
It is so ordered.
