ANALYSIS:
 
On August 4, 2023, Plaintiff Moldovan, Inc. dba
Servicemaster Restoration By Disaster Recovery Experts (“Plaintiff”) filed the
instant action against Defendant Alfreda V. Adger (“Defendant”). Defendant
filed an answer on September 12, 2023. 
 
Plaintiff filed the instant Motion to Compel Responses to
Form Interrogatories and Request for Sanctions on March 29, 2024. The Motion
contends that Plaintiff served Form Interrogatories, Set One, on Defendant on
January 23, 2024. (Motion, Tovar Decl., ¶4.)
No copy of the discovery requests are attached to the supporting declaration as
claimed, however. (Ibid.) Accordingly, the hearing is continued to allow
Plaintiff to file a supplemental declaration with the missing exhibits
attached.
