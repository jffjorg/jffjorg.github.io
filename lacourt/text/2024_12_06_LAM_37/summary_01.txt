The court has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer to the Cross-Complaint filed by Apex Development Inc. against the State of California, Department of Transportation (Caltrans). The court has sustained the demurrer without leave to amend as to the first cause of action (quantum meruit) because such causes of action are not allowed against government entities like CalTrans. However, the court has overruled the demurrer as to the second cause of action, finding that Caltrans can be held vicariously liable for the actions of its employee, Daryl Myatt, under Government Code § 815.2(a). The court has ordered the Plaintiff/Cross-Defendant to file an Answer by December 16, 2024, and has set an OSC Re: Answer and Case Management Conference for January 15, 2025.
