23SMCV04492 Van Vu MTC RFP Responses (POS:ok)
 
Plaintiff
Hoang Van Vu's Motion to Compel Responses to Requests for Production and
sanctions in the amount of $860.00 as to Defendant Murtaza Iqbal Dar is
GRANTED. The motion was filed on 5-7-24, and no opposition has been filed as of
8-3-24. Plaintiff served Defendant with RFP's, FROGS, SROGS, and RFAs on
11-28-23, with a 1-2-24 response deadline. (Cunningham Decl., ¶ 2; Ex. A.) No
responses were received by the deadline, or of the time of the motion filing,
and Plaintiff repeatedly made efforts to meet and confer to resolve the
outstanding discovery responses. (Id., ¶¶3, 4, 5; Ex. B.) Defendant is
ordered to provide code compliant responses to RFPs without objections and pay
sanctions in the amount of $860.00 (2 hrs. @ $400/hr. + $60 filing fee)
pursuant to CCP § 2031.300.

23SMCV04492 Van Vu Motion to Deem RFAs Admitted (POS: Ok)
 
Plaintiff
Hoang Van Vu's Motion to Deem RFAs admitted and sanctions in the amount of
$860.00 as to Defendant Murtaza Iqbal Dar is GRANTED. The motion was filed on
5-7-24, and no opposition has been filed as of 8-3-24. Plaintiff served
Defendant with RFP's, FROGS, SROGS, and RFAs on 11-28-23, with a 1-2-24
response deadline. (Cunningham Decl., ¶ 2; Ex. A.) No responses were received
by the deadline, or of the time of the motion filing, and Plaintiff repeatedly
made efforts to meet and confer to resolve the outstanding discovery responses.
(Id., ¶¶ 3, 4, 5; Ex. B.)  The Court orders RFA's No. 1–58 deemed
and  Defendant is ordered to pay sanctions in the amount of $860.00 (2
hrs. @ $400/hr. + $60 filing fee).
 
If
Defendant provides RFA responses in substantial compliance with CCP § 2033.220
prior to the 8-6-24 hearing, the RFA's will not be deemed admitted pursuant to
CCP § 2033.280, however, Plaintiff will still owe sanctions in the amount of
$860.00. (See Code Civ. Proc., § 2033.280c [The court shall order RFA's
admitted "unless it finds that the party to whom the requests for
admission have been directed has served, before the hearing on the motion, a
proposed response to the requests for admission that is in substantial
compliance with Section 2033.220. It is mandatory that the court impose a
monetary sanction under Chapter 7 (commencing with Section 2023.010) on the
party or attorney, or both, whose failure to serve a timely response to
requests for admission necessitated this motion."].)
