This tentative ruling is regarding a motion for leave to file a first amended complaint by Plaintiffs Travis Leonard, Donnie Fazard Hosein, Nhu Quynh Nguyen, Keisha Sharon Alfred, Peter Smith, Phenisha Gary, Lashaunda Bell, and Darlene Taylor against Defendant 6834 De Celis, LLC. The Plaintiffs seek to add claims and parties to the action, including a doe defendant, and to add causes of action for Negligence related to allegations against Defendants for negligently furnishing, constructing, and installing the components of the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. The Court may, in the furtherance of justice, allow a party to amend any pleading, and in this case, the motion is granted due to the lack of opposition and the absence of apparent prejudice to the Defendant.
