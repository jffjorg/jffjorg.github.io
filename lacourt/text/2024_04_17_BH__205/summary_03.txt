This tentative ruling is about a case between Plaintiff GINA RODRIGUEZ, a celebrity talent manager, and Defendant VICTORIA SOPHIA SANJURO, a cosmetic surgeon. The case arises from a botched cosmetic surgery procedure. The Plaintiff alleges professional negligence and violation of California's constitutional right of privacy. The Defendant seeks discretionary relief under Code Civ. Proc. 473(b) to set aside the default and default judgment. The Defendant claims mistake in believing she did not need to respond to the Complaint and that she was surprised by the entry of default and default judgment due to receiving the requests at the wrong address. The Judge concludes that discretionary relief is warranted and grants the Defendant's motion to vacate default.
