 Case Number:  23STCV31463    Hearing Date:   December 27, 2024    Dept:  71 Superior  Court of California County  of Los Angeles   DEPARTMENT 71   REVISED TENTATIVE  RULING      BANK OF HOPE,                  vs.   GREEN MASTERS BUILDING SERVICE, INC., et al.    Case    No.:  23STCV31463          Hearing Date:  December 27, 2024       Plaintiff Bank of Hope’s Request for Default Judgment           Having considered all documents submitted in support of a default  judgment, the Court rules as follows:   Background On December 26, 2023, Bank of  Hope (“Plaintiff”) filed its operative Complaint against Defendants Green  Masters Building Service, Inc., and Christopher M. Choi (collectively, “Defendants”)  for breach of note, breach of guaranty, claim and delivery, conversion, money  lent, account stated, and fair valuation. On April 15, 2024, the clerk  entered default against Green Masters Building Service, Inc.  On April 17, 2024, the clerk entered default  against Christopher M. Choi.  On December  17, 2024, Plaintiff filed a Request for Court Judgment to be heard on December 27,  2024.   Party’s Request Plaintiff requests the Court to enter a  default judgment against Defendants and award Plaintiff a judgment of $53,822.93  consisting of $45,155.00 in damages, $246.61 in general damages, $8,896.97 in  prejudgment interest at a total rate on the contract of 15%, $869.70 in costs, and  $1,654.65 in attorneys’ fees.    Discussion Plaintiff submitted a  completed default judgment packet, with all applicable attachments. The Court  finds sufficient evidence to justify the requested award and grants Plaintiff Bank  of Hope’s request in full.   Conclusion The application for default  judgment filed on December 17, 2024, is granted.  Judgment of $53,822.93 is  entered against Defendants Green Masters Building Service, Inc., and  Christopher M. Choi. Plaintiff is ordered to give notice of this  ruling.   Dated:  December _____, 2024
