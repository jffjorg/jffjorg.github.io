This is a tentative ruling by Judge Hon. Lee S. Arian regarding Defendants' motions to compel Plaintiff's responses to discovery requests and request for monetary sanctions. The Plaintiff, Sandra Gonzalez, filed this action against Defendants Larry Parker and Wanda Parker for injuries arising from a motor vehicle accident. The Defendants served Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, and Request for Production of Documents on July 27, 2023, but the Plaintiff failed to respond. The Court granted the motions to compel responses and imposed monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,680 against the Plaintiff and her counsel. The Court also ordered the Plaintiff and her counsel to pay the filing fees for each motion. The parties are advised to submit their intentions to submit on the tentative ruling to the Court and to assume that the opposing party may appear at the hearing to argue the matter.
