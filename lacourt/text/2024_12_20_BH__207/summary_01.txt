The judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Plaintiff Umit Bozer against Defendant Hakob Navasardyan. The case involves an incident that occurred on October 22, 2022, at Avra Restaurant in Beverly Hills, California, where the Plaintiff was assaulted and battered by the Defendant. The Plaintiff is seeking a default judgment for damages and costs.

The judge has granted the Plaintiff's request for default judgment in the amount of $250,769.36, which includes $200,000 in general damages, $50,000 in special damages, and $769.36 in costs. The judge has also denied the Plaintiff's request for $89.50 in "Criminal Record Retrieval" costs, finding that it is not an allowable cost under California Code of Civil Procedure Section 1033.5.

The judge has also noted that the Plaintiff has substantiated the requested $50,000 in lost profits and $200,000 in general damages. The judge has found that the Plaintiff is entitled to recover damages and costs and has granted the Plaintiff's request for default judgment.
