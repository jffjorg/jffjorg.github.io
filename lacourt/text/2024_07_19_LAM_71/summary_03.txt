Here is a summary in English text:

This tentative ruling grants several motions to compel defendants to provide responses to plaintiff's Form Interrogatories (FROG) and Request for Production (RFP). The plaintiff, LA Melrose Place No. 49, LLC, has been seeking responses from defendants Michael Della Femina and The Prosciutto Project, LLC, for several months. The defendants have failed to comply with the requests, despite multiple extensions granted by the court. The court awards monetary sanctions to the plaintiff in the amounts of $560.00, $560.00, $1,160.00, and $1,160.00, respectively, against the defendants. The court also orders the defendants to provide verified responses to the FROG and RFP within 20 days.
