  Case Number:  23SMCV00538    Hearing Date:   June 17, 2024    Dept:  O JOSE DECASTRO VS KATHERINE PETER CASE NO. 23SMCV00538 
6-18-24 Defendant’s Motion to Compel Plaintiff’s appearance
at his deposition. 
 Defendant Michael
Pierattini’s Motion to Compel Deposition of Plaintiff Jose DeCastro and request
for monetary sanction in the amount of $3,660 is GRANTED under CCP §§ 2025.450,
2025.260. 
Defendant served the notice of deposition on 12-11-23.
(Katrinak Decl., ¶ 2, Ex. A.) Plaintiff objected to the deposition notice stating
he does not reside 150 miles from the location where he is to appear for that deposition.
Plaintiff's address on all pleadings in this action is 1258 Franklin Street,
Santa Monica, CA within Los Angeles County and Plaintiff does not provide an
address or explanation for his objection. (Id., ¶¶ 3, 4, Ex. B; see Compl., ¶
8, and caption.) Defendant sent a meet and confer email explaining the need for
the Plaintiff's deposition on 1-12-24. (Id., ¶¶ 5, 6 Ex C.)  Plaintiff did not respond to the meet and
confer email, and Plaintiff failed to appear for deposition on 1-25-24. (Id., ¶
6.)  
On April 8, 2024, Plaintiff filed a “NOTICE OF CHANGE OF
ADDRESS OR OTHER CONTACT INFORMATION” 
stating his new street address is 330 S. Casino Center Blvd. Las Vegas,
NV 898101 his mailing address is 3909 S. Maryland Pkwy Ste 314, Las Vegas, NV
89119. Yet on that same date, Plaintiff filed a “Motion for Clarification...”
with his same Santa Monica address on the caption of his motion.
On May 2, 2024 the Court held the hearing on all of the
defendant’s motions to compel discovery, including the motion to compel Plaintiff to
appear for his deposition.  Plaintiff did
not appear at the hearing.  On
Defendant’s motion to compel Plaintiff to appear for his deposition, the Court
ordered the Plaintiff to file a declaration regarding whether the Nevada
address is his permanent address, and why he is still using the California
address on his pleadings.  On the Court's
own motion, the Hearing on Motion to Compel Deposition of Plaintiff and Request
for Monetary Sanctions in the Sum of $4,560 was continued to 06/18/2024 at
08:30 AM in Department O at Santa Monica Courthouse.  The Court has not received any declaration from
Plaintiff confirming his permanent address or further opposition to the
motion.  
The motion to compel Plaintiff to appear for his deposition
is granted.  Plaintiff is ordered to
appear for his deposition on _________________________________, 2024 at 9:00 am
at  LAW OFFICES OF R. PAUL KATRINAK 9663
Santa Monica Blvd., 458  Beverly Hills,
California 90210.  Further Plaintiff is
ordered to pay defendant sanctions in the amount of $3,660 (8 hrs. @ $450/hr. +
$60 filing fee) within 60 days.
