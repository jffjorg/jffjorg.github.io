  Case Number:  23TRCV03791    Hearing Date:   May 24, 2024    Dept:  P Tentative Ruling: Plaintiff’s request for entry of default  judgment against Defendants is DENIED without prejudice.I. BACKGROUNDA. FactualOn November 14, 2023, Plaintiff JPMorgan Chase, N.A.  (“Plaintiff”) filed this action against Defendants Steve Yang and CYG LLC for  open book account and account stated.Defendants defaulted on February 16, 2024.Plaintiff now request default judgment against Defendants in  the sum of $40,261.67.B.  ProceduralOn  February 16, 2024, Plaintiff filed the instant request for entry of default  judgment against Defendants.II. MOVING PARTY’S GROUNDS FOR THE MOTIONDefendants  defaulted and according to Plaintiff’s records, Defendants have a balance due  and owing on their account with Plaintiff in the amount of $40,261.67.III.  ANALYSISWhile Plaintiff presents evidence of the balance of  $40,261.67 due and owing on Defendant Yang’s account with Plaintiff, there is  no argument or evidence as to Defendant CYG LLC.Accordingly, the request is DENIED.IV. CONCLUSIONFor the foregoing reasons, Plaintiff’s request for entry of  default judgment against Defendants is DENIED. Moving party is ordered to give notice.
