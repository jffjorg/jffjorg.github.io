This is an employment matter.  Plaintiff Marta Coreas seeks to strike the Answer of Defendant Vista Care Home, Inc. because this entity is not represented by counsel.Defendant has not had counsel since March 2024.Because a corporate entity cannot represent itself, the Motion to Strike is granted.  The Court enters a default for Defendant.Moving party to give notice. If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
