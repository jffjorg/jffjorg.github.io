  Case Number:  22VECV01711    Hearing Date:   May 29, 2024    Dept:  T 22VECV01711
ODK Capital v Ultimate

Tentative ruling: Motion to withdraw as counsel for Ultimate Home Mortgage
Corp: Continue.  Corporations cannot be self-represented.  The court will continue the hearing for 60 days
and issues an order to Ultimate Home Mortgage that its attorney is seeking to
withdraw from the case, the court is continuing the hearing on the motion to
withdraw to _____ at 8:30 a.m. in Dept. T and that if Ultimate Home Mortgage does
not have a new attorney by that date, then the court will allow counsel to
withdraw and will strike its answer and enter default against Ultimate Home
Mortgage for being a corporation with an attorney.
