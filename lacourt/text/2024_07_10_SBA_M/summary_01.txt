This is a tentative ruling by Judge Gary Y. Tanaka in the case of Maria Luz Nieves, et al. v. Kevin George Aziz Riad, et al. The case involves a fatal automobile accident and the defendants' motion for summary judgment. The Court finds that the defendant K&K Limousine has met its initial burden to show that the plaintiffs' causes of action cannot be established and/or that there is a complete defense to the causes of action. The plaintiffs failed to dispute any of the separate statement of facts submitted by the defendants, including the fact that K&K Limousine did not give permission for the defendant Riad to operate the vehicle. Therefore, the Court grants the defendant K&K Limousine's Motion for Summary Judgment.
