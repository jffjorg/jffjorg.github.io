The judge has granted Plaintiff Vivian Kim's application for an order permitting service of the summons and complaint upon Defendant Joshua Seunghun Park by publication. The judge finds that Plaintiff has demonstrated that Defendant cannot be served with reasonable diligence despite 11 attempts at his address, and that a cause of action for fraudulent transfer exists against Defendant. The judge notes that Defendant owns an interest in real property that is subject to the jurisdiction of the court, and that Plaintiff has met the requirements for service by publication under California Code of Civil Procedure section 415.50(a) & (b). The judge orders that the summons and complaint be published in the Hermit & San Jacinto Chronicle, and that a copy of the summons, complaint, and order for publication be mailed to Defendant if his address is ascertained before the expiration of the time prescribed for publication.
