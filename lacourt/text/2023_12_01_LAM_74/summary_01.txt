The tentative ruling is in response to Defendant Chris Bagley's demurrer to the First Amended Complaint filed by Plaintiff Andrew Leavenworth on behalf of Joan Wight (Decedent). The case concerns a dispute over personal property and allegations of financial elder abuse, conversion, and violation of Penal Code section 496.

The court overrules the demurrer and orders Defendant Bagley to file and serve his answer within 20 days.
