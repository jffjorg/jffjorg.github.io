NOTICE: ok   Demurrer is to entire complaint:             1.  Breach of Contract             2.  Fraudulent Deceit              3.  Breach of the Covenant of Good Faith and Fair  Dealing             4.  Promissory Fraud             5.  Negligence   RULING: The demurrer is placed off calendar.     On 8/11/23, Plaintiffs Talal Altamini and Manking  Essential, Inc. (Plaintiffs) filed this action against Defendant Lief Organics,  LLC (Defendant) for: (1) Breach of Contract, (2) Fraudulent Deceit, (3) Breach  of the Covenant of Good Faith and Fair Dealing, (4) Promissory Fraud and (5)  Negligence.  After attempts to meet and  confer regarding the issues Defendant had with the complaint were unsuccessful,  on 11/20/23, Defendant filed and served the instant demurrer to the entire  complaint.    On 12/28/23, 9 court days before the 1/11/24 hearing  date, Plaintiffs filed and served a First Amended Complaint which renders the  demurrer moot.  See CCP 472(a);  CCP 1005(b).
