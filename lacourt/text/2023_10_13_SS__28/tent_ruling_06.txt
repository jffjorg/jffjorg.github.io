 Case Number:  21STCV10183    Hearing Date:   October 13, 2023    Dept:  28 I.  Liam Bachar Claimant  Liam Bachar (“Claimant”) was travelling in a rented motor home when the motor  home caught on fire. Claimant was evacuated from the motor home prior to  sustaining any physical injuries. Claimant experienced emotional distress from  the incident.  This  is an expedited petition without a hearing, which is permitted under California  Rules of Court, rule 7.950.5, if Petitioner Meytal Bachar (“Petitioner”) uses  the required Judicial Council forms and meets certain conditions. The  conditions are:  1. Petitioner is represented  by an attorney. 2. The claim is not for  wrongful death. 3. Settlement proceeds will  not be placed in a trust. 4. There are no unresolved  liens. 5. Petitioner’s attorney did  not become involved at the request of Defendant or the insurance carrier. 6. Petitioner’s attorney is  not employed by or associated with a Defendant or insurance carrier in connection  with the petition. 7. If an action is filed, all  Defendants that have appeared are participating in the compromise OR the court  has determined the settlement to be in good faith. 8. The settlement, exclusive  of interests and costs, is $50,000 or less OR if greater than $50,000, the  amount payable is the insurance policy limits AND all proposed contributing parties  would be substantially unable to use assets other than the insurance policy  limits AND the court does not otherwise order. SETTLEMENT:  $20,000.00  INJURIES:  Emotional distress  MEDICAL  EXPENSES: $0.00  COSTS:  $0.00  ATTORNEY’S  FEES: $5,000.00, which is 25% of the gross settlement  BALANCE  OF PROCEEDS: $15,000.00  DISCUSSION:  Petitioner  has submitted a completed application for Claimant with all applicable  attachments. The Court grants the petition.  Petitioner  requests that the balance of the proceeds be invested in a single-premium  deferred annuity, subject to withdrawal only on authorization of the Court. The  Court orders that the amount is to be invested according to the information  provided in Attachment 19a(2).  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net Balance on December 13, 2023 at 8:30  a.m. in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.  The  Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal (Settlement) on December 13, 2023 at 8:30 a.m.  in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse.II.
