And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge denies defendant Alexandra R. McIntosh, APC's motion for issue, evidence, or terminating sanctions against plaintiff Emilio Reyes. The motion was based on plaintiff's failure to respond to discovery requests and his refusal to submit to examination by counsel for defendant. The judge finds that plaintiff's refusal to submit to examination was willful and prejudiced defendant, but denies defendant's request for terminating sanctions because defendant did not demonstrate that plaintiff's actions were willful.

The judge orders plaintiff to attend and testify at a further session of deposition to allow counsel for defendant to examine plaintiff. The judge also orders defendant to give notice of this ruling.

The judge's ruling is based on Code of Civil Procedure sections 2023.010, 2023.030, 2025.610, 2025.290, and 2025.330, as well as Evidence Code sections 711, 773, and 776. The judge cites cases such as Los Defensores, Inc. v. Gomez and Lopez v. Watchtower Bible & Tract Society of New York, Inc.
