The Court has reviewed the
Petition to Approve Compromise of Pending Action of a Minor, filed by Petitioner Sasha Santana (Petitioner) on
behalf of Claimant Jayde Santana, age 11. The Court denies the petition without
prejudice for the following reasons. 
 
Petitioner checks item 14a, indicating she is not seeking
reimbursement. If this is correct, then 14b should not be filled out. 
 
It appears counsel is representing other parties in this
action. Item 17e must be changed accordingly and the attachment must be
provided. Counsel must include attorney fees he will receive through the other
minors’ compromises in this case, in item 17f. 
 
Accordingly, the Court denies the petition without
prejudice.
 
Petitioner shall give notice and file a
proof of service of such.
