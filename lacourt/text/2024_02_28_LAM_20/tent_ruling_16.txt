Defendants Access Control Related Enterprises, LLC, LLR Equity Partners IV, L.P., LLR Equity Partners Parallel IV., L.P., Seth Lehr, David Stienes, and Joseph Grillo seek to compel Plaintiff William West to “comply with the requirements of expert witness disclosure per California Code of Civil Procedure § 2034.260(b), in particular, the failure to submit an expert declaration with ‘a brief narrative statement of the general substance of the testimony that the expert is expected to give,’ and production of discoverable reports and writings.”Plaintiff does not oppose the Motion and states: “Plaintiff’s new undersigned counsel—who was only recently retained and substituted into this matter as of the same date as this Notice—will work with Plaintiff and Plaintiff’s designated expert to prepare and serve an amended expert declaration setting forth ‘a brief narrative statement of the general substance of the testimony that the expert is expected to give,’ as well as to produce discoverable reports and writings.”Because Plaintiff is willing to comply, the Motion is denied as moot.  The expert information should be provided within 20 days.Moving parties to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
