 Case Number:  20STCV47422    Hearing Date:   November 3, 2023    Dept:  29 Tentative
 
The
motion to compel further responses is denied as untimely under Code of Civil
Procedure section 2031.310, subdivision (c).
 
The
responses at issue were served on March 17, 2021. (Kashani Decl., ¶ 4.)
Although the parties discussed amended or supplemental responses, no such
responses were ever served. (Id., ¶¶ 5-8.) Moving party has presented no
evidence of an agreement to extend the 45-day period to file a motion to compel
further responses.
 
Moving
party filed this motion on April 19, 2023, beyond the 45-day period to file a
motion to compel further responses.  
 
Accordingly, the motion is DENIED.
 
Moving party to give notice.
