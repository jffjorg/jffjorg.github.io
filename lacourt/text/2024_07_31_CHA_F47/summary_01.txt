English-text summary:

This is a tentative ruling by a judge in a lawsuit filed by Crossroads Equipment Lease and Finance, LLC (Plaintiff) against Gold Standard Express LLC and Hermine Karapetyan (Defendants). The lawsuit arises out of a Master Equipment Finance Agreement between the Plaintiff and Gold Standard, and a Guaranty of that lease by Karapetyan. The Plaintiff alleges that Gold Standard and Karapetyan breached the terms of the Agreement by failing to make payments, and seeks summary adjudication of the 1st and 2nd causes of action in its favor. The judge grants the motion, finding that the Plaintiff has satisfied the elements of a breach of contract cause of action and that the Defendants have failed to refute the evidence. The judgment will be entered in favor of the Plaintiff and against the Defendants, jointly and severally, for a total amount of $84,616.72.
