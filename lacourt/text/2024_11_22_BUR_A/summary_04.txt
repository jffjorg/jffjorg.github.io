The judge has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Britecap Financial, LLC (plaintiff) against AGCO Construction, LLC & Donald J. Aquillard (defendants). The plaintiff alleges that the defendants are indebted to them in the amount of $52,325.97 due to the defendants' failure to make payments pursuant to a Loan Agreement. The plaintiff served a Request for Admissions (RFA) on the defendants, but the defendants failed to respond. The plaintiff's motion to deem the RFA matters admitted was unopposed, and the court granted the motion. The court notes that the defendants' failure to respond waived any objection to the requests. The court did not assess sanctions against the defendants. The case is set for a Case Management Conference on March 18, 2025.
