And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has reviewed the case and has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer brought by the City of Burbank to the Plaintiff's First Amended Complaint. The Plaintiff alleges that he slipped and fell in the alleyway behind 3614 Magnolia Blvd, Burbank, CA, and that the City of Burbank is responsible for the dangerous condition of the alleyway. The Court finds that the Plaintiff's allegations are insufficient to establish a cause of action against the City of Burbank. The Court notes that the Plaintiff's Claim Form alleges that a liquid slippery substance was present on the walkway, which does not equate to an allegation that the property was physically damaged, deteriorated, or defective. The Court grants the Plaintiff 20 days' leave to amend and intends to sustain the demurrer to the Plaintiff's sole cause of action.
