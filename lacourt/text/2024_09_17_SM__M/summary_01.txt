English-text summary:

The defendant, Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, has moved for summary judgment in its favor against the plaintiff, Scout Smith, who is seeking recovery for professional negligence and loss of consortium. The plaintiff alleges that the defendants failed to properly diagnose, monitor, and treat his mother, Sarah Smith, during a cesarean section procedure, causing her injuries and damages. The plaintiff claims that he suffered physical and emotional harm as a result of his mother's injuries and inability to breastfeed. The defendant argues that Scout Smith is not a proper plaintiff for loss of consortium, as his claimed damages stem from secondary effects of his mother's injuries. The court has granted the defendant's motion for summary judgment, holding that California law delimits liability for Scout's claim for loss of consortium and that only Sarah Smith may recover for her direct injuries caused by the defendants' malpractice, and only Chris Smith may recover for loss of consortium for Sarah's injuries.
