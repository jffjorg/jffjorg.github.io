Judge Michael E. Whitaker has issued a tentative ruling in a lawsuit brought by Main Street Corridor, LLC against Claude Zdanow, alleging two causes of action for fraudulent transfer and constructive fraudulent transfer. The lawsuit claims that Zdanow diverted funds and assets from Mediakix, a company he acquired, and used them for personal gain, rather than paying the company's debts, including back-owed rent to Main Street Corridor. The Court has ruled that the Complaint adequately alleges these facts and has overruled Zdanow's demurrer to the Complaint. The Court has ordered Zdanow to file an Answer to the Complaint on or before August 8, 2024.
