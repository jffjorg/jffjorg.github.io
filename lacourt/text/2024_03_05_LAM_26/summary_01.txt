Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has granted the plaintiff's motion to compel the defendant to comply with its agreement to produce responsive documents. The defendant, Imperial Irrigation District, failed to produce all responsive documents in a timely manner and failed to identify the documents responsive to each request. The judge has ordered the defendant to produce executed copies of board resolutions Nos. 28-2016, 29-2016, and 30-2016, and the 2018-2021 General Ledgers in native format. Additionally, the judge has awarded sanctions of $1,500 to the plaintiff against the defendant and its counsel for failure to comply with the court's orders.
