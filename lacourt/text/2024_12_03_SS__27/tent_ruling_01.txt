MOTION TO VACATE DEFAULT JUDGMENT
Hearing Date: 12/3/24 
CASE NO./NAME: 21STCV26591 INTERINSURANCE
EXCHANGE OF THE AUTOMOBILE CLUB VS ADAM VICTOR SCHWARTZ
Moving Party: Defendant ADAM VICTOR SCHWARTZ
Responding Party: Unopposed
Notice: Insufficient 
Ruling: DENIED WITHOUT PREJUDICE
 
On July 20, 2021,
Plaintiff Interinsurance Exchange of the Automobile Club filed the present
subrogation action against Defendant Adam Victor Schwartz. On October 7, 2022,
the Court entered default judgment against Defendant. Defendant, in pro per,
now moves the Court to set aside the default and default judgment.
Defendant’s motion does
not include a proof of service. Plaintiff is represented by James Aguirre of
the Law Offices of Richardson, Fair & Cohen and Steven Horn of the Law
Offices of Steven J. Horn. There is no proof that either of these counsel were
served with the motion or had an opportunity to respond. Accordingly, the
motion is denied without prejudice.
