English-text summary:

This is a tentative ruling on Defendant County of Los Angeles' motion to deem Plaintiff Ricky Lee Pinzon's failure to respond to requests for admissions as an admission. The motion seeks to deem the genuineness of documents and the truth of matters specified in the requests as admitted, as well as to impose a monetary sanction. The Plaintiff has failed to respond to the requests for admissions, despite service of the requests and multiple courtesy notifications. The Court grants the motion and deems Plaintiff's failure to respond as an admission. This ruling nullifies Plaintiff's entire action.
