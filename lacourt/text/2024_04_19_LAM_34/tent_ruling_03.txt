 Case Number:  23STCV03001    Hearing Date:   April 19, 2024    Dept:  34 The
complaint in this action was filed on February 10, 2023.  Default was entered against Defendant on January
17, 2024.
 
        On March 6, 2024, the Court held an OSC
re Entry of Default Judgement.  Plaintiff
failed to file any of the required documents for Entry of Default Judgement.  The Court Denied the Entry of Default
Judgement, and stated, “The Court sets and OSC re Entry of Default Judgement
for April 19, 2024. If default judgment is not entered by that time, the Court
will dismiss the case.”  (Minute Order, 3/6/2024.)
 
        Plalintiff has failed to file any documents
for Entry of Default Judgement and hence the Court cannot enter default
judgment.  
 
        The Court DISMISSES the case without prejudice.
