 Case Number:  23STCV26665    Hearing Date:   April 19, 2024    Dept:  34 PLAINTIFF'S
REQUEST FOR ENTRY OF DEFAULT JUDGMENT 
 
 
 
I.         
BACKGROUND
 
On October 31, 2023,
Plaintiff World Wrestling Entertainment, Inc. filed its Complaint against
Defendant Times 10 Entertainment, LLC on a cause of action for common counts. 
 
On January 16, 2024,
by request of Plaintiff, the Clerk’s Office entered default on Defendant. 
 
On February 20, 2024,
Plaintiff filed:
 
(1)       Judicial Council Form
CIV-100, Request for Court Judgment;
 
(2)       Judicial Council Form
CIV-110, Request for Dismissal;
 
(3)       Judicial Council Form
JUD-100, Proposed Judgment;
 
(4)       Declaration of
Alexander V. Hettena; and 
 
(5)       Declaration Re:
Underlying Action. 
 
On February 21, 2024,
by request of Plaintiff, the Clerk’s Office dismissed without prejudice the doe
defendants. 
 
II.       ANALYSIS
 
The Request for Default Judgment is GRANTED. 
 
Default
Judgment is AWARDED in favor of Plaintiff and against Defendant in the total
amount of $90,624.25 consisting of $81,000.00 in damages; $9,054.25 in
pre-judgment interest; and $570.00 in costs.
