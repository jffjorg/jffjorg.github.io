Plaintiff’s
motion to have his RFAs deemed admitted as to Defendant Filip Jan Rymsza is
GRANTED.
 
Defendant is
sanctioned, jointly and severally with counsel, $700 payable within 30 days.
 
On 8/18/2022,
Plaintiff Maksymilian Naporowski (Plaintiff) filed suit against Defendant Royal
Road Entertainment, LLC and Filip Jan Rymsza (collectively, Defendants),
alleging breach of a verbal agreement. 
 
            On
5/20/2024, Plaintiff moved to deem his Requests for Admission (RFAs) deemed
admitted as to Defendant Filip Jan Rymsza (Defendant.) 
 
            The
motion is unopposed.
 
Discussion 
 
            Plaintiff
served Defendant with RFAs (Set One) on 3/11/2024. To date, no responses have
been received.
 
            Defendant
did not oppose this motion, and thus has offered no justification for the
failure to respond. 
 
            Based
on the foregoing, Plaintiff’s motion to have his RFAs deemed admitted as to
Defendant Filip Jan Rymsza is granted. Defendant is sanctioned, jointly and
severally with counsel, $700. ($350/hr x 2 hr.) 
 
It is so ordered.
