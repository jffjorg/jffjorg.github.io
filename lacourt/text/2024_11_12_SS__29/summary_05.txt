The Court has ruled on the motion filed by 3010 S. Figueroa, LLC (Figueroa) for leave to file a cross-complaint against Ono Hawaiian BBQ, Inc. (Ono) for equitable and express indemnity. Figueroa seeks to assert a related cause of action against Ono arising out of the same set of facts as the Plaintiff's complaint. The Court finds that Figueroa has acted in good faith and grants the motion. The Court grants Figueroa leave to file its cross-complaint within 7 days of the hearing.
