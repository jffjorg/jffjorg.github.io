And here is the English-text summary:

The Judge's tentative ruling addresses a motion to compel further responses to requests for production (Set Three) filed by Defendant Rasier, LLC. The case arises from a vehicle accident on March 7, 2019, near Los Angeles International Airport Terminal 6. Plaintiff Uyanga Tserenchimed filed a complaint on March 2, 2021, alleging motor vehicle negligence and general negligence against Defendants Christophe Harbonville, Rasier LLC, Rasier-CA, LLC, and Does 1 through 50. The Defendant seeks further written responses to certain requests for production, including Requests for Production Nos. 50-59 and 61.

The Judge finds that the Defendant has shown good cause to compel further responses to certain requests, including Requests for Production Nos. 53, 54, 58, 59, and 61. The Judge also finds that Plaintiff has acted with substantial justification and denies the Defendant's request for sanctions.

The Judge grants the motion to compel in part, ordering Plaintiff to provide verified, written, code-compliant responses to the compelled requests within 21 days of notice of this ruling. The Judge denies the motion in part, finding that certain requests are vague, ambiguous, or unintelligible.
