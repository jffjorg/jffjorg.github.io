CASE:                                Christine Paulsen v. Pixel Labs, LLC, et al.
 
CASE NO.:                 23STCV22371
 





















 
MOTION
FOR LEAVE TO FILE FIRST AMENDED COMPLAINT



 
MOVING PARTY:               Plaintiff
Christine Paulsen 
 
RESPONDING PARTY:     No opposition
 
 
 
This is a discrimination and retaliation action.  On September 15, 2022, Plaintiff, Christine Paulsen,
filed a Complaint against Defendants, Pixel Labs, LLC, and Assembly Parent
Corp., for various Labor Code violations and violations of the Fair Employment
and Housing Act, including a cause of action for sexual harassment.
 
On January 17, 2024, Plaintiff filed this motion for leave
to file the First Amended Complaint.  Plaintiff
seeks to add allegations in support of Plaintiff’s cause of action for sexual
harassment.
 
On February 1, 2024, Defendants filed a non-opposition to
Plaintiff’s motion for leave to file the First Amended Complaint.
 
Given the lack of opposition. Plaintiff’s motion to file the
First Amended Complaint is GRANTED.  The
First Amended Complaint is deemed filed on the date of this order.  Plaintiff is further directed to separately
file the amended pleading.
