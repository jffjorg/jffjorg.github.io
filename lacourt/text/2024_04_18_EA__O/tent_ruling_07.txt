Discussion
 
LORI A. METCALF seeks to appear as counsel pro hac vice in
this action on behalf of Defendant Thomas “Kip” McKean. Attorney Metcalf is a
partner with the law firm of Quintairos, Prieto, Wood & Boyer, PA, which is
Defendant’s attorney of record in this case. 
 
Considering
that the application comports with California Rules of Court, Rule 9.40, the
court grants King’s application.

Based on the foregoing, the application is granted.
