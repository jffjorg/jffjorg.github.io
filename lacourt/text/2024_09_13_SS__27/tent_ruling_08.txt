PASHCHIN, vs NADLAN 1205 NORTH FORMOSA LLC et al.
Moving Party: Defendant 
Responding Party: Defendant Nadlan 1205 North Formosa
Notice: Sufficient 
Ruling: MOTION TO COMPEL DEPOSITION IS GRANTED
 
On August 26, 2024, the Court heard Defendant Nadlan 205 North
Formosa’s motion to compel Plaintiff’s deposition. The Court ordered Plaintiff
to file the indicated medical records demonstrating his inability to sit for
the deposition by September 5, 2024, and further ordered that if Plaintiff failed
to provide the records, Plaintiff’s deposition was to take place on September
20, 2024. Plaintiff did not file any medical records. Thus, Plaintiff is
ordered to sit for his deposition on September 20, 2024.
