Ruling: Demurrer and Motion to Strike Is Moot
 
On
April 18, 2022, Plaintiff filed the initial complaint. Defendant Universal
Protection Service LLC demurred and moved to strike portions of Plaintiff’s
original complaint. On May 24, 2024, and May 28, 2024, Defendant filed its
demurrer and motion to strike, respectively. On July 15, 2024, Plaintiff filed
his first amended complaint.
When a plaintiff files an amended complaint after a demurrer is filed but
before it is decided, the demurrer must be overruled as moot. (JKC3H8 v.
Colton (2013) 221 CA4th 468, 477; People ex rel. Strathmann v. Acacia Research Corp. (2012) 210 Cal.App.4th 487, 505-06; Sylmar
Air Conditioning v. Pueblo Contracting Services, Inc. (2004) 122
Cal.App.4th 1049, 1054.)
 
Should Defendant wish to demur or strike
portions of the first amended complaint, Defendant can do so through a new
motion.
