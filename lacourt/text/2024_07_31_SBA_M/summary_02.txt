This is the tentative ruling of Judge Gary Y. Tanaka in the case of Reza Shahifar et al. v. New Tech Aircraft Services, Inc. et al. (Case No. 23TRCV02644). The case concerns a dispute between the parties over the value of shares in New Tech Aircraft Services, Inc. and the dissolution of the company. The defendant, New Tech Aircraft Services, Inc., has moved to fix the value of its shares and stay the dissolution proceeding. The Court has granted the motion, ordering a stay of the dissolution proceeding and the entire action to determine and fix the fair value of the shares. The Court has also ordered the defendant to post a bond in the amount of $25,000 within ten days of the date of this ruling. The appraisal process will be conducted in accordance with Corporations Code § 2000, and the parties are encouraged to meet and confer on the specific procedural details of the process.
