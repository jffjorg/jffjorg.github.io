Case #21CHCV00576
 
MOTION TO BE
RELIEVED AS COUNSEL
 
Motion filed on 9/11/24.
 
MOVING ATTORNEY: Arthur Corona 
CLIENT: Defendant K12 School Services
 
RELIEF REQUESTED: An order relieving attorney
Arthur Corona as counsel for Defendant K12 School Services in this action.  
 
RULING: The motion is placed off calendar.
 
On 9/11/24, attorney Arthur Corona filed the instant
motion seeking an order relieving him as counsel for Defendant K12 School
Services in this action.  The hearing on
the motion was originally set for 3/5/25. 
No proof of service was filed for the motion.  
 
On 10/3/24, the Court advanced the hearing date on the
motion to 10/3/24 and continued same to 11/6/24.  (See 10/3/24 Minute Order, p.1).  The Court also ordered attorney Arthur Corona
to give notice to the client and file proof of service at least 10 days before
the 11/6/24 hearing date.  (Id,
p.2).  Attorney Corona has still not
filed a proof of service for the motion, a notice of the changed hearing date
or a proof of service regarding a notice for the changed hearing date.
 
The client has not responded to the motion to cure  the defect in notice.  
 
Additionally, the declaration filed in support of the
motion does not indicate who confirmed the address of the corporate client by
conversation.  (See Declaration,
No.3.b.(1)(c)).
 
Further, the proposed order does not include the client’s
address or telephone number in number 6 as required.
