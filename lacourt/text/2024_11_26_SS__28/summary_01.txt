The court has considered the motion filed by James River Insurance Company for leave to intervene in this case. James River seeks to intervene to protect the interests of its insured, Chris Solidum, who is a defendant in this action. The court has granted the motion, finding that James River's attempts to contact and communicate with Chris Solidum have been unsuccessful and that Chris Solidum has cooperated with James River throughout the litigation process. The court has ordered James River to file a complaint-in-intervention within 15 days of the hearing on this motion and to comply with the service requirements of Code of Civil Procedure section 387.
