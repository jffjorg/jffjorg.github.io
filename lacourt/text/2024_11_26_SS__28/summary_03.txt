And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Court has considered the motion filed by Defendants Birendra Dutt and Uplander Properties, LLC to limit and/or exclude excessive experts at trial. The Plaintiffs, Soraya Pastor and Firooz Nosratifar, have designated 37 non-retained expert witnesses, but did not specify which ones they intend to call at trial. The Court finds that this large number of experts imposes an undue burden and expense on the Defendants, and grants the motion to limit the number of expert witnesses who can testify at trial to five witnesses. The Court also orders the Plaintiffs to file and serve a list of the five non-retained expert witnesses who are expected to testify at trial, along with the subject area for each witness. The Plaintiffs are also required to make the non-retained experts in the list available for deposition.
