 Case Number:  23NWCV00008    Hearing Date:   September 25, 2024    Dept:  C CARLOTA SAENZ BY AND THROUGH HER
CO-SUCCESSOR IN INTEREST RUBY SAENZ, et al. vs RIVIERA NURSING &
CONVALESCENT HOME INC., et al., Case No. 23NWCV00008 
This is a case of professional
negligence. Plaintiffs, CARLOTA SAENZ, through her Co-Successor in Interest
RUBY SAENZ; RUBY SAENZ, RICHARD SAENZ; ROY SAENZ; and RUBEN SAENZ apply ex
parte for an order to enforce settlement, or alternatively, for an order
shortening time to hear plaintiff’s Motion to Enforce Settlement.  A
Notice of Settlement of Entire Case was filed on September 4, 2024, which
allowed the Court to retain jurisdiction to enforce terms of the settlement
agreement pursuant to  CCP § 664.6.  After the settlement was
effectuated, one of the six plaintiffs passed away.  Opposing plaintiffs
want to invalidate the settlement agreement and rework distributions. They do
not oppose a standard briefing schedule for the motion.  The ex parte
application is GRANTED in part.  The Motion to Enforce Settlement is
ADVANCED from Tuesday, May 13, 2025, to this date and CONTINUED to Tuesday,
January 21, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. in Department SE-C.  The ex parte
application is deemed to be the moving papers.  Opposition and Reply
briefs to be filed and served in compliance with the new hearing date.
 Moving parties to give notice.
