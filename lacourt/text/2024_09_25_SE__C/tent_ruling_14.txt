 Case Number:  24NWCV00762    Hearing Date:   September 25, 2024    Dept:  C STURGEON ELECTRIC CALIFORNIA, LLC vs
CITY OF WHITTIER, Case No. 24NWCV00762 
This action arises out of a
construction contract. Defendant City of Whitter applies ex parte for an order
advancing the hearing date for its Motion to Consolidate, currently scheduled
on Thursday, April 17, 2025.  The ex parte application is DENIED without
prejudice.  A notice of related cases must be filed, served, and approved
by the Court before a motion to consolidate may be heard. (Cal. Rules of Court,
rule 3.200; Super. Ct. L.A. County, Local Rules, rule 3.3, subds. (f) and (g).)
On September 24, 2024, the Notice of Related Case was denied without prejudice
because it must be filed in all pending cases listed in the notice and must be
served on all parties in those cases.  A Notice has not been filed in the
other action, Environmental Construction, Inc. v. City of Whittier, LASC
Case No. 24NWCV02394.  Moving party to give notice.
