 Case Number:  24NWCV00734    Hearing Date:   September 25, 2024    Dept:  C HECTOR ARRIETA, et al. vs KAISER
PERMANENTE, et al., Case No.: 24NWCV00734
This is a premise liability action.
 Defendants KAISER PERMANENTE, KAISER PERMANENTE, LLC, KAISER PERMANENTE
VENTURES, LLC, KAISER PERMANENTE HEALTH apply ex parte for an order to stay
this action pending determination of their petition to compel binding
arbitration, currently scheduled for January 21, 2025.  The ex parte
application is GRANTED.  A party moving to compel arbitration is entitled
to a stay upon request, (CCP § 1281.4.) Moving parties to give notice.
