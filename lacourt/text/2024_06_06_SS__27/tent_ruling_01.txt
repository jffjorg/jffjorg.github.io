Ruling: MOTION TO BE RELIEVED AS COUNSEL IS

On May 10, 2024,
Plaintiff’s counsel Yagobzadeh Law Firm moved the Court to be relieved as
counsel for Plaintiff Divetta McDaniel. On May 24, 2024, Plaintiff signed and
filed a substitution of attorney, whereby Yagobzadeh Law Firm is to be
substituted out and Plaintiff is to represent himself in pro per. The present
motion is moot and therefore denied.
