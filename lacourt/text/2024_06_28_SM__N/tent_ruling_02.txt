 Case Number:  23SMCV05227    Hearing Date:   June 28, 2024    Dept:  N TENTATIVE RULINGTimothy V. Milner of The Milner Firm’s Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff Robin Robinson is GRANTED.Counsel has asserted a valid reason for withdrawal, i.e., the attorney-client relationship has broken down. (Rules Prof. Conduct, rule 1.16(b)(4).) Counsel has provided the required forms under rule 3.1362 of the California Rules of Court and served the client with the same. There being good cause to grant counsel’s request to withdraw, Timothy V. Milner of The Milner Firm’s Motion to Be Relieved as Counsel for Plaintiff Robin Robinson is GRANTED. This ruling granting counsel’s motion to withdraw shall become effective upon filing of proof service of this ruling upon Plaintiff Robin Robinson.  A change of address will be required for Plaintiff Robin Robinson subject to new counsel entering the action on his behalf.
