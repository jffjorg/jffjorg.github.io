  Case Number:  22NWCV00279    Hearing Date:   April 18, 2024    Dept:  C Shayler v. Mann Investment Group, LLC, Case No.
22NWCV00279
 
This is a disability access case. Plaintiff moves ex parte
to specially set hearings on shortened notice for Plaintiff’s Motion to Deem
Request for Admissions Admitted, Motion to Compel Responses to Request for
Production, Motion to Compel Responses to Special Interrogatories, and Motion
to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories.  
 
The motions are currently set for September 17, 2024, the
first available hearing dates when Plaintiff filed them. Trial is currently set
for May 15, 2024.  Plaintiff requests that the motions be heard before the
April 30 discovery cut-off date.  The Court is unable to accommodate the
request.  
 
The ex parte application is DENIED.  In the
alternative, the motions are ADVANCED to this day and CONTINUED to Thursday, June
6, 2024 at 9:30 a.m. in Dept. SE-C.  Opposition and Reply briefs to be
filed and served in compliance with the new hearing date.  Plaintiff is
not foreclosed from seeking a trial continuance in Dept. SE-F.  
 
Moving party to give notice.
