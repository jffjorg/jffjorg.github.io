The proposed Complaint in Intervention, attached to the
Declaration of Christopher J. Capalbo as Exhibit A is NOT deemed served as of the hearing date. Intervenor BERKLEYNET
for CAROLINA CASUALTY INSURANCE COMPANY is ORDERED
to SERVE its Complaint-in-Intervention within 5 days of the Court’s issuance of
this Order. The Complaint in Intervention will be deemed FILED as of the date
the Court’s issuance of this Order. 
 
Moving Party to give Notice. 
 
No Opposition filed as of April 15, 2024.    
 
Labor Code §3853 makes clear that an employer against whom a
claim for worker’s compensation damages was made may join in an action against
a third party arising out of the incident for which the worker’s compensation
payment was made. 
 
The unopposed motion is GRANTED.
