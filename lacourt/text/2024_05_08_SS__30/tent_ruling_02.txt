 Case Number:  20STCV17991    Hearing Date:   May 8, 2024    Dept:  30 NANETTE PRICE vs CAL- QUAKE CONSTRUCTION, INC., A CORPORATION, et al.TENTATIVEDefendants Cal-Quake Construction, Inc., John Taferner, and Joseph Goldberger’s motion to compel responses to requests for production for documents is DENIED as moot.Defendants’ request for sanctions is DENIED.Moving party is ordered to give notice.DiscussionOn June 20, 2023, Defendant served Request for Production of Documents, Set One, on Plaintiff. (Davis Decl., Exh. A.) Defendant alleges that to date, Plaintiff has failed to provide responses. (Id., ¶ 7.)Plaintiff’s counsel argues that the motion is moot as responses have been served. (Rogari Decl., Exh. 1.)The Court finds the motion is moot as verified responses have been provided.Sanctions are only mandatory in connection with motions to compel responses to request for production when the motion is unsuccessfully opposed. (Code Civ. Proc. § 2031.300(c).) The motion was not unsuccessfully opposed. As such, the request for sanctions is denied.
