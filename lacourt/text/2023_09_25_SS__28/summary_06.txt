The Tentative Ruling

The court has considered the motion filed by the County of Los Angeles, defendant, to compel further responses to demand for production of documents contained in the deposition notice and request for $2,300 in sanctions. The motion was filed on July 6, 2023, and the court has granted the motion.

The court finds that the County of Los Angeles has made adequate efforts to meet and confer with the plaintiff, Jeffrey Volpei, regarding the deficiencies in his document production. The court also finds that the plaintiff has waived any objection to the untimely filing of the motion and the lack of a separate statement.

The court orders the plaintiff to serve written responses, without objections, and all documents in his possession, custody, or control responsive to the County of Los Angeles's requests for production of documents. The court also grants sanctions of $555 to the County of Los Angeles.

The court's ruling is based on California Code of Civil Procedure sections 2025.450, 2031.310, and 2023.030, and California Rules of Court, rule 3.1345.
