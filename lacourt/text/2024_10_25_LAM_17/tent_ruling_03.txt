Defendant’s
demurrer is MOOT. 
 
On 7/30/2024,
in propria persona Plaintiff Aladdin Dinaali filed suit against
Interlaced Social, LLC, Augustin Pulido, and Kenneth Barlow, alleging: (1)
breach of contract; (2) unfair business practices; (3) fraud; (4) fraud; (5)
fraud; (6) negligence; (7) intentional infliction of emotional distress; (8)
negligent infliction of emotional distress; and (9) common counts.  
 
On 9/3/2024,
Defendant Interlaced Social, LLC and Agustin Pulido (collectively, Interlaced
Defendants) demurred to Plaintiff’s Complaint.
 
            On
9/3/2024, Plaintiff filed a first amended complaint (FAC). 
 
            Accordingly,
Defendant’s demurrer is moot. 
 
It is so ordered.
