And here is the English text summary:

This tentative ruling addresses a motion to compel further responses from Plaintiffs Alicia and Robert B. Brown to Defendants' Request for Production of Documents, Set 1. The motion was filed on January 19, 2024, and seeks an order compelling further responses to specific discovery requests. The requests relate to the subject matter of the action, including documents related to the construction defects, investigation costs, expert fees, and consultant fees. The motion also requests an award of sanctions against Plaintiffs and their counsel in the amount of $1,950.

The Court finds that Plaintiffs have failed to justify their objections to the discovery requests and that the requests are related to the subject matter of the action. The Court grants the motion and orders further responses to be served by Plaintiffs within 30 days. The Court also awards sanctions against Plaintiffs and their counsel in the amount of $1,530.

The Court notes that Defendants failed to electronically bookmark the exhibits attached to their motion, as required by the California Rules of Court. Counsel for the parties are warned that failure to comply with this requirement in the future may result in matters being continued or sanctions being imposed.
