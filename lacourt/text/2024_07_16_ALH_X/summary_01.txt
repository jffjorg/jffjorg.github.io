This is a tentative ruling by Judge Joel L. Lofton on a motion to compel deposition filed by Plaintiff Hether Flores against Defendants Trader Joe's Company and B-K La Canada Property, LLC. The Plaintiff seeks to compel Defendant B-K's Person Most Knowledgeable to appear for deposition and produce documents requested within ten days of the Court's order. The Plaintiff also requests monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,802.50. The Court grants the Plaintiff's motion and orders Defendant B-K to appear at a properly noticed deposition and produce the requested documents within ten days. The Court also grants the Plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,802.50, to be paid by Defendant B-K within thirty days of the Court's order. The Court finds that the Plaintiff is entitled to relief under Code of Civil Procedure section 2025.450, subdivision (a), and that the Defendant's failure to comply with the deposition notice constitutes a misuse of the discovery process.
