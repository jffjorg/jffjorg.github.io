            Plaintiff
Madlyn Primack, a minor, filed this medical malpractice action against
Defendants Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, Women’s Care of Beverly Hills, and
Diahnn Campbell, M.D. (collectively, “Defendants”).  Plaintiff moves for a preferential trial date
within six to nine months, per Code of Civil Procedure section 36(b) &
(g).  Defendants join in the motion and
request a trial date of October 28, 2024.
 
            Based upon
the foregoing, the Court orders as follows:
 
            1.         Plaintiffs’ motion for a trial
preference is granted.  The deadline for
this case to proceed to trial is October 31, 2024.  
 
            2.         The Court sets the following dates:
 
                        Final
Status Conference:        October 4, 2024,
at 9:00 a.m.
 
                        Trial:                                       October
14, 2024, at 9:30 a.m.
 
The Court shall hear any motions for summary judgment or
summary adjudication on October 11, 2024, at 8:30 a.m.  The Court finds good cause to hear these
motions less than thirty (30) days before trial under Code of Civil Procedure section
437c(a)(3).  
 
            3.         The Court orders that jury fees shall
be posted within ten (10) days, if they have not been posted already, or the
parties will waive jury.  
 
            4.         The parties shall follow all courtroom
procedures for Department #39.  The
parties shall file all trial documents on or before September 26, 2024.  The parties shall identify all witnesses they
intend to call in their respective cases-in-chief, and identify and produce all
exhibits they intend to introduce in their respective cases-in-chief, on or
before September 26, 2024.
 
            5.         Plaintiff’s counsel shall provide
notice and file proof of such with the Court.
