 Case Number:  23STCV17228    Hearing Date:   January 31, 2024    Dept:  74 Peter Machin v. CRMLS Comliance  [sic]   Motion to Compel Arbitration and  Stay Action    The  court considered the moving papers. Plaintiff did not file an opposition.  BACKGROUND             This action arises from a dispute  over a database that facilitates real estate sales.             On  July 24, 2023, Plaintiff Peter Machin filed a complaint against Defendant  California Regional Multiple Listing Service, Inc.             On  August 24, 2023, Defendant filed this motion to compel arbitration and stay the  action. DISCUSSION             Defendant seeks to compel  arbitration. Defendant bears the burden of producing evidence of a written  agreement that covers the claims in Plaintiff’s complaint. (Rosenthal v.  Great Western Fin. Securities Corp. (1996) 14 Cal.4th 394, 413.) Defendant  has met that burden. (Smetana Decl., ¶ 2, Ex. 1; ¶ 3,  Ex. 2, § XVII; Garcia Decl., ¶ 5; Ex. 1.) Plaintiff has not filed an  opposition to the motion. CONCLUSION                 The court grants Defendant’s motion  to compel arbitration and stays this action pending the arbitration’s  completion. The court orders all of Plaintiff’s causes of action to  arbitration. Defendant  shall give notice.
