 Case Number:  23STCV08453    Hearing Date:   October 17, 2023    Dept:  34  
PLAINTIFF'S
REQUEST FOR ENTRY OF DEFAULT JUDGMENT 
 
 
I.         
BACKGROUND
 
On April 17, 2023, Plaintiff Wells Fargo Bank, National Association
filed its Verified Complaint against Defendants Quickbuys, Inc. and Stacy L.
Adams on causes of action for breach of contract, account stated, money lent,
and breach of guaranty. 
 
On September 1, 2023, by request of Plaintiff, the Clerk’s Office
entered default on Defendants. 
 
On September 21, 2023, Plaintiff filed:
 
(1)      
Judicial
Council Form CIV-100, Request for Court Judgment;
(2)      
Judicial
Council Form JUD-100, Proposed Judgment;
(3)      
Statement
of the Case;
(4)      
Declaration
of William Ocampo in support of Request for Entry of Default Judgment;
(5)      
Declaration
of William Ocampo re Lost Documents;
(6)      
Declaration
of Yvonne Ramirez-Browning re Attorneys Fees; and
(7)      
Proof of
Service.
 
On September 26, 2023, by request of Plaintiff, the Clerk’s Office
dismissed the Doe defendants from the Verified Complaint. 
 
II.      
ANALYSIS
 
The Request for Entry of Default Judgment is GRANTED. The Court AWARDS
default judgment in favor of Plaintiff and against Defendants, jointly and
severally, in the total amount of $68,614.97.
