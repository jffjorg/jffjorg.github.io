Summary:

The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer filed by Defendants Rotten, Inc. dba EATROTTEN.COM and Michael Fisher to the complaint filed by Plaintiff Josh Freydkis. The Court has sustained the demurrer as to the first through fourth causes of action, including claims for harassment, retaliation, failure to take reasonable steps to prevent and/or correct harassment and retaliation, and wrongful discharge in violation of public policy. The Court has overruled the demurrer as to the fifth cause of action, which alleges breach of contract. The Court has allowed the Plaintiff 20 days to amend the complaint.
