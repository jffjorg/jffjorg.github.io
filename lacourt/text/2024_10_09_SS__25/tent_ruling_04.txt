

 
Tentative Ruling:                    Defendant US Auto Leasing
d/b/a Prestige Auto Sales’s Motion to Set Aside Entry of Default and Default
Judgment is GRANTED. 
 
The Default entered on 02/29/2024 and
Default Judgment entered on 05/03/2024 are hereby set aside and vacated.
 
                                                Defendant
is ordered to file its proposed answer within ten (10) days of notice of this Court’s
order.
 
The
Court sets trial in this action for October 7, 2025, at 8:30 a.m. in Department
25 of the Spring Street Courthouse.
 
Discovery
and all other trial related deadlines are to comport with the new trial date.
Parties must comply
with the trial requirements as set forth in the court's Third Amended Standing
Order for Limited Civil Cases (effective February 24, 2020). 
All trial documents
are to be electronically filed at least ten (10) days prior to the trial date. 
Parties should be
prepared to submit a JOINT Trial Readiness Binder / Exhibit Binder, and to
personally appear on the date of trial.
Plaintiff’s Request
for Defendant to pay a penalty for compensatory legal fees and costs is DENIED.
