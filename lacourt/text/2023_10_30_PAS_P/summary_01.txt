**Facts**

* Cadlerock purchased a Citibank business loan from Edith, which was guaranteed by Helen Young.
* Edith defaulted on the loan in October 2020, owing $22,999.79, plus interest and late charges.
* Cadlerock sent correspondence to Edith in an attempt to collect the debt, but Edith disputed it.
* This action was filed in May 2022.

**Disputes**

* Edith filed a cross-complaint against Citibank, alleging that Citibank had wrongfully zeroed out its business checking account.
* Cadlerock argues that it is entitled to attorney fees as the prevailing party on the breach of contract cause of action.

**Intentions**

* The Judge intends to grant Cadlerock's motion for attorney fees and costs.

**Summary**

The Judge grants Cadlerock's motion for attorney fees and costs, awarding $22,225.00 in attorney fees and $2,216.88 in costs. The Judge finds that Cadlerock is the prevailing party on the breach of contract cause of action and is entitled to recover attorney fees and costs under California Civil Code §1717. The Judge also finds that the billing records submitted by Cadlerock's counsel are sufficient to support the request for attorney fees.
