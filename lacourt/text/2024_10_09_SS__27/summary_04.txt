And here is a summary of the Judge's Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has granted the motion by Defendant Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority to deem the content of its Request for Admissions, Set One, admitted. The Request was served on Plaintiff Leon Merritt Sr. on March 11, 2024, and responses were due on April 19, 2024. However, no responses were provided by Plaintiff as of September 4, 2024, when the motion was filed. As a result, the Court finds that Plaintiff waived any objections, including based on privilege or the work product doctrine, and that the content of the Request for Admissions, Set One, is deemed admitted.
