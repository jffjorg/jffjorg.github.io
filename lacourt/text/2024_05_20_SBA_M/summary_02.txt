And here is the English summary:

This tentative ruling concerns a motion to approve a settlement between Keep America Safe and Beautiful, Inc. (Plaintiff) and Suavecito, Inc., et al. (Defendant). The Plaintiff alleges that the Defendant manufactures, packages, distributes, markets, and/or sells consumer products containing DINP and fails to warn consumers in California. The parties have finalized the terms of a settlement, which includes an award of attorneys' fees of $22,500 and a penalty amount of $2,500.00. The Court finds that the warning required by the settlement complies with Chapter 6.6, the Safe Drinking Water and Toxic Enforcement Act of 1986, and that the award of attorneys' fees and penalty amount are reasonable. The Court grants the Motion to Approve Settlement and enters judgment in accordance with the terms of the consent judgment.
