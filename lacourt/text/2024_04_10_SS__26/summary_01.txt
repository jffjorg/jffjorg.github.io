The Court grants the Defendants' motion to deem Plaintiff Xiaofu Zhang a vexatious litigant. The Court orders Plaintiff to post a security in the amount of $5,000 within 30 days of the date of this Order. The Court further orders that Plaintiff is prohibited from filing any new litigation in the courts of the State of California in propria persona without first obtaining leave of the presiding judge or justice of the court where the litigation is proposed to be filed.
