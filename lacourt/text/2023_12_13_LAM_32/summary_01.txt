This is a tentative ruling by a judge on a motion for judgment on the pleadings filed by defendants Schlomo Rechnitz, Boardwalk West Financial Services, LLC, G4 Wellness Group, LLC, and York Healthcare & Wellness Centre, LP against the first and second causes of action in a lawsuit alleging elder abuse and neglect, violation of resident rights, and wrongful death. The lawsuit was filed by Jesus Gonzalez, Carmen Batz, and Olivia Knutson, who claim that Jesus Gonzalez sustained injuries and eventually died at a skilled nursing facility. The judge rules that the complaint fails to state facts sufficient to constitute a legally cognizable claim for elder abuse and neglect, and that the complaint also fails to set forth with particularity the violations of rights supporting recovery under Health and Safety Code section 1430(b). Therefore, the judge grants the defendants' motion for judgment on the pleadings.
