 Case Number:  23STCV19226    Hearing Date:   December 7, 2023    Dept:  26  
 
On the Court’s own motion, the Final Status Conference set
for December 7, 2023 is CONTINUED to 12/11/23 at 8:30 am.
 
On December 6, 2023, Defendant David Kang filed a Notice of Stay
of Proceedings, attaching a Notice of Bankruptcy Case Filing involving Judgment
Debtor David Eun Kang, filed on December 6, 2023.  Here, the Tenant’s filing of bankruptcy automatically
stays the instant UD proceedings.  It
appears to the Court that Plaintiff (Landlord) must must seek leave from
bankruptcy court to lift the stay and resume the instant UD proceedings.  
 
Accordingly, the Court hereby sets an OSC re stay of the action
for December 11, 2023 at 8:30 am.  Any
party who opposes the imposition of an automatic stay effective as of December
6, 2023 must (1) file and serve briefing explaining why this court is not
mandated to stay the action in its entirety pending the resolution of the
Bankruptcy matter or pending the Bankruptcy Court’s granting of a motion to lift
the stay, and (2) appear on December 11, 2023 at 8:30 am and show cause why the
Court should not impose a mandatory automatic stay effective as of December 6,
2023.
 
The Court’s Judicial Assistant shall give electronic notice
to all parties.
