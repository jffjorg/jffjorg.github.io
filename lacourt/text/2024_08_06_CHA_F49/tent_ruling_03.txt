BACKGROUND
This action arises from alleged
personal injuries that Plaintiffs sustained as a result of an automobile
collision with Defendant’s vehicle on September 2, 2022.
 
            On October
12, 2023, Plaintiffs Ana R. De Santiago; Jesus Jacobo; Adrian Barragan, a minor
by and through his Guardian ad Litem (“GAL”) Ana R. De Santiago Garcia;  Diego Alvarado, a minor by and through his
GAL Fabiola Alvarado; and Daniel Alvarado, a minor by and through his GAL Maria
Isabel Alvarado (collectively, “Plaintiffs”) filed their Complaint against
Defendant Frederick M. Reiner (“Reiner” or “Defendant”) and Does 1 to 20,
alleging three causes of action: (1) Motor Vehicle Liability, (2) General Negligence,
and (3) Loss of Consortium. Subsequently, on December 21, 2023, Reiner filed
his Answer to the Complaint.
 
            On July 9,
2024, Plaintiff Maria Isabel Alvarado filed
the instant Petition for Approval of Minor’s Compromise (the “Petition”). 
 
            No opposing
or replying papers have been received by the Court.
