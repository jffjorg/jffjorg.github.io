And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has granted the Defendants' Motion to Set Aside Default and Default Judgment. The Defendants, Yone Aracely Lopez Rios, Jose E. Rodriguez, Tequila B.A.R. General, Inc., and Los Lopez Tequila B.A.R. & Spirits, Inc., argued that their counsel's excusable neglect in failing to file a responsive pleading was the reason for the default. The Judge found that the neglect was excusable and that there is a strong public policy in favor of granting relief and allowing the requesting party their day in court. Therefore, the default and default judgment were set aside.
