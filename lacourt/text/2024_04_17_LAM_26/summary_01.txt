This tentative ruling is about a motion filed by the defendants Content Partners, LLC, CP IEG Holdings LP, and GKF Distribution, LLC to issue a letter of request for the testimony of Yu-Jin Embacher, a resident of Germany. The plaintiff, Library Rights Company (UK) LTD, opposes the motion. The Court denied the motion without prejudice, instructing the moving defendants to give notice within seven days. The Court also noted that the discovery has closed in this case, making it necessary for the moving defendants to first bring a motion to reopen discovery before the Court can hear the instant discovery motion.
