

 
BACKGROUND
 
            On
August 18, 2019, Plaintiff State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company
(“Plaintiff”) filed the complaint against Defendant Carla Beatris Brown and
Does 1 through 40 arising from a property damage subrogation claim, due to an
automobile accident that occurred on or about October 20, 2018. 
 
            On
January 6, 2021, a settlement agreement was reached where the parties agreed to
resolve the above captioned matter for $2,626.41, pursuant to a Stipulation for
Settlement and Order (“Settlement Agreement”).
 
            On July 1,
2024, Plaintiff State Farm Mutual Automobile Insurance Company filed the
instant motion. 
 
            As of August
13, 2024, Plaintiff has not filed an opposition. 
             
                                                MOVING
PARTY POSITION
 
            Plaintiff
moves the Court to enforce the Stipulation for Settlement entered into by
Plaintiff and Defendant Carla Beatris Brown (“Defendant”), pursuant to
California Code of Civil Procedure §§ 664.6 and 1032 and enter judgment against
Defendant in the amount of $9,377.44.
 
OPPOSITION
 
            None. 
 
REPLY
 
            None. 
ANALYSIS
 
I.          Motion to Enforce Settlement
 
A.                                       
Legal Standard 
            California Rule of Court, Rule 3.110(b)
states in pertinent part: “[t]he complaint must be served on all named
defendants and proofs of service on those defendants must be filed with the
court within 60 days after the filing of the complaint. . ..” (Cal. Rule of
Court, Rule 3.110(b).)
1.                 
Notice
            
            Plaintiff’s
Notice lists the incorrect Courthouse address for hearing on Plaintiff’s Motion
to Enforce Settlement. Therefore, the Notice does not comply with Cal. Rules of
Court 3.110(b).
 
III.       Conclusion 
 
            Accordingly,
the Motion to Enforce Settlement is CONTINUED to SEPTEMBER 19, 2024 at 10:00
a.m. in Department 25 of the Spring Street Courthouse, so that Plaintiff can
provide Defendant with proper Notice of Motion to Enforce Settlement Agreement.

 
            Plaintiff
is ordered to give notice of ruling.
