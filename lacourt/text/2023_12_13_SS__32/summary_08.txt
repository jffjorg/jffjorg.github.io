The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Joo Hye Song v. Target Corporation. The Plaintiff, Joo Hye Song, is seeking monetary sanctions against the Defendant, Target Corporation, for failing to timely provide responses to Form Interrogatories and Requests for Production. The Court finds that the Defendant failed to serve timely responses to the discovery requests and that the Plaintiff is entitled to monetary sanctions. The Court grants sanctions in the amount of $2,461.65, which is a reduced amount from the $4,523.30 requested by the Plaintiff. The Court orders the Defendant and its counsel to pay the sanctions within 30 days. The Plaintiff is required to provide notice of the Court's order and file a proof of service of such.
