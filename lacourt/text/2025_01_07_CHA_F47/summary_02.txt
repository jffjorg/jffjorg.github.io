The Judge grants the motion to compel further responses from Defendant Eddie Camargo to Plaintiff Alejandra Jimenez's Form Interrogatories, Set 1. The Judge finds that Defendant Eddie Camargo has failed to justify his objections to the discovery requests and that the factual information provided in his responses is unverified, inadequate, and/or evasive. The Judge also finds that Defendant Eddie Camargo and his former attorney, Khelya M. Okunor, have failed to comply with their discovery obligations and imposes sanctions of $2,660.00 jointly and severally on them in favor of Plaintiffs' counsel, Colleen O'Hara. The Judge orders Defendant Eddie Camargo to serve further verified responses within 30 days.
