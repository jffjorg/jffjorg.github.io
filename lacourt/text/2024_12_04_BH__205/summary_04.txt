Summary:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling in favor of the plaintiff, 3435 Ocean Park, LLC, in its application for a writ of attachment against defendant Heather Palleiko aka Heather Sweeney. The plaintiff is seeking to attach the defendant's property to secure recovery on its claim for breach of a commercial lease agreement. The judge finds that the plaintiff has established the probable validity of its claim and that the attachment is not sought for a purpose other than the recovery on the claim. The amount subject to attachment is $26,974.08, which includes $19,136.58 in expenses attributable to the defendant's breach, $7,267.50 in estimated attorney's fees, and $570.00 in estimated costs. The judge also notes that certain property is exempt from attachment, including an iPhone, a car, a diamond wedding engagement ring, and a retirement account. The plaintiff is required to post an undertaking in the amount of $10,000.00 and to submit a revised proposed right to attach order and order for issuance of writ of attachment after hearing.
