S27 8:30 a.m. October 8, 2024Plaintiff filed this action against Defendants for damages arising out of a slip and fall. Plaintiff filed her complaint on 10/11/23. Defendant filed an answer on 3/27/24. Trial is scheduled for 12/02/24.Defendant moves to continue the trial date, contending (a) the parties are attempting to settle the case, and (b) Plaintiff has ceased responding to communications and discovery. Defendant seeks an order continuing the trial date for 90-120 days.The motion is granted. The 11/22/24 FSC date is advanced to today’s date and continued to Monday, 3/17/25 at 8:30 a.m. in Department S27. The 12/02/24 trial date is advanced to today’s date and continued to Thursday, 3/20/25 at 8:30 a.m.If Plaintiff continues to fail to respond to discovery and communications, Defendant must take affirmative measures to obtain necessary documents and discovery. Plaintiff is admonished that she must fully participate in the litigation and prosecute her case if she does not wish to suffer from adverse discovery or other orders against her.Defendant is ordered to give notice.Parties who intend to submit on this tentative must send an email to the court at gdcdepts27@lacourt.org indicating intention to submit on the tentative as directed by the instructions provided on the court website at www.lacourt.org. If the department does not receive an email indicating the parties are submitting on the tentative and there are no appearances at the hearing, the motion may be placed off calendar. If a party submits on the tentative, the party’s email must include the case number and must identify the party submitting on the tentative. If any party does not submit on the tentative, the party should make arrangements to appear remotely at the hearing on this matter.DATED: October 8, 2024 _____________________________________MARK C.
