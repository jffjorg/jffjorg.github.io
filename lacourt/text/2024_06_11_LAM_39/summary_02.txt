English-text summary:

This tentative ruling is regarding a motion to compel further responses to requests for production of documents (RPD) brought by Plaintiff Saul Gonzalez against Defendant General Motors LLC. The motion seeks to compel Defendant to produce responsive documents regarding alleged defects in vehicles and Defendant's policies and procedures. The court finds that Plaintiff is entitled to discovery regarding the alleged defects in vehicles of the same year, make, and model as Plaintiff's vehicle. The court also orders Defendant to produce responsive documents regarding "service bulletins and/or technical service bulletins" and "the Warranty Administration Manual". The court declines to impose sanctions, finding that Defendant has agreed to produce many of the documents Plaintiff seeks.
