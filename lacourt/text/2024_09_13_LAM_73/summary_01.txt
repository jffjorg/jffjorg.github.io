The Court has issued a tentative ruling in the case of Avery v. Belanger, et al. (22STCV18602). The case involves allegations of elder financial abuse, breach of fiduciary duty, fraud, and violation of Business and Professions Code section 17200. The Plaintiff, Veronica Avery, has filed a motion to compel responses to a request for production of documents from Defendant Only Escrow, Inc. and is seeking monetary sanctions for the Defendant's failure to respond.

The Court has granted the Plaintiff's motion to compel responses, ordering Defendant Only Escrow, Inc. to provide verified responses within 30 days. The Court has also granted the Plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions against Defendant Only Escrow, Inc. in the reduced amount of $1,515.00. The sanctions are to be paid to counsel for the Plaintiff within 30 days of the date of this order.
