This is a tentative ruling by Judge Mel Red Recana in the case of Rexford Industrial – 701 Kingshill, LLC v. Autocom, Inc. et al. (Case No. 20STCV45068). The Judge considered the parties' submissions and ruled that the motion to strike Autocom, Inc.'s Answer and First Amended Cross-Complaint is granted. The motion was filed by Plaintiff Rexford Industrial – 701 Kingshill, LLC and Co-Defendants E-Freight Logistics, LLC, Eshaan Mesghali, and Ali Manale, alleging that Autocom, Inc. lacked the legal capacity to defend itself due to its suspended status in California. The Judge found that the parties had satisfied their meet and confer obligations, but Autocom, Inc. failed to provide supporting documentation showing its good standing status. Therefore, the Judge granted the motion to strike Autocom, Inc.'s Answer and First Amended Cross-Complaint.
