 Case Number:  22STCV16957    Hearing Date:   May 14, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
May 23, 2022, Plaintiff Gianni Ortu (“Plaintiff”),
by his guardian ad litem Nataliya Ortu, filed this
action against Defendants Alba Sierra (“Defendant”) and Does 1-10 for motor
vehicle negligence. 
On
August 2, 2022, the Court appointed Nataliya Ortu to serve as Plaintiff’s
guardian ad litem. 
On
October 10, 2022, Defendant filed an answer. 
On
April 16, 2024, Petitioner Nataliya Ortu (“Petitioner”)
filed a petition to approve the compromise of Plaintiff’s claims. 
On
April 23, 2024, Plaintiff filed a notice of settlement. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
asks the Court to approve the compromise of Plaintiff’s claims. 
DISCUSSION      
Proposed
settlement terms:
          Gross settlement amount:                              $25,000.00
          Total medical expenses:                                 $1,057,299.75
          Total reduction amount:                                 $1,049,662.36
          To be paid/reimbursed from proceeds:           $7,637.39
Attorney’s fees:                                             $6,250.00
Costs:                               
                             $3,772.86 
Net balance:                                                   $7,339.75
          Petitioner has submitted a completed petition with all
required information.  The Court grants
the petition. 
At
Petitioner’s request, the net balance of $7,339.75 is to be paid or delivered
to Petitioner.  
CONCLUSION 
The Court GRANTS the petition for approval of the
compromise of the pending action of Plaintiff Gianni Ortu filed by Petitioner Nataliya Ortu on April 16,
2024.  The net balance of $7,339.75 is to be paid or
delivered to Petitioner Nataliya Ortu. 
The
Court sets an OSC re: distribution of settlement proceeds on July 15, 2024 at
8:30 a.m. in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
The
Court sets an OSC re: dismissal (settlement) on July 15, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. in
Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
          Petitioner is ordered to give notice of the ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of the ruling with the Court within
five days.
