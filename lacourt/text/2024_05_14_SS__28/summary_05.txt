The Court rules that the Plaintiff, Jose Rodriguez, failed to provide timely responses to the Defendant, Byambadorj Tsendayush's, requests for production of documents, special interrogatories, and form interrogatories. The Court grants the Defendant's motion to compel responses to these requests and orders the Plaintiff to provide verified code-compliant responses without objections by June 13, 2024.

The Court also grants the Defendant's motion to compel responses to the requests for admission and orders the Plaintiff to provide verified code-compliant responses without objections by June 13, 2024.

The Court denies the Defendant's motion to deem admitted matters specified in the requests for admission.

The Court awards $480.00 in sanctions against the Plaintiff and his counsel to be paid to the Defendant by June 13, 2024.
