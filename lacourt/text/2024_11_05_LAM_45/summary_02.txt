The Judge has granted the Motion for Charging Order filed by FEM Ventures, LLC (Judgment Creditor) against Eugenio Gonzalez (Judgment Debtor) to satisfy the judgment of $3,279,720.93 entered on June 5, 2024. The judgment was obtained against G2K Holdings, LLC, Eugenio Gonzalez, Monica Gonzalez, and Robert Knohl, jointly and severally. The Judgment Creditor has established that Gonzalez owns an interest in two limited liability companies, Sunflower Apartments, LLC and Avalon Cove Communities, LLC, and has served notice of the motion for charging order on Gonzalez and the LLCs. The Court finds that the Judgment Creditor has complied with the legal requirements for a charging order and grants the motion.
