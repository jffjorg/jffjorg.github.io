This tentative ruling grants the motion to compel responses to the Request for Production of Documents (set one) served on the defendant, Hector Vidal-Ramirez, by the plaintiff, Raul Gutierrez. The plaintiff had served the request on May 31, 2023, but no responses were received. The defendant had promised to provide responses by December 4, 2023, but failed to do so. The court orders the defendant to serve verified responses to the request without objections within ten days. Additionally, the court imposes sanctions in the amount of $250 against the defendant and counsel, payable within 30 days, for the failure to comply with the discovery request.
