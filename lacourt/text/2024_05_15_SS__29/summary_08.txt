And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Court's tentative ruling grants in part and denies in part the Defendants' Pitchess motion. The motion seeks discovery of personnel records of Esparza and Norat, who are LAPD officers. The Court grants the motion as to requests for documents regarding workers' compensation claims made by Norat or Esparza in the five years prior to the incident, and for documents relating to any collective bargaining agreements covering Norat or Esparza. The Court also grants the motion as to requests for correspondence regarding the accident between Norat or Esparza and the City of Los Angeles. The Court denies the motion as to requests for the entire personnel file of Norat and Esparza, and for documents relating to injuries sustained by Norat and Esparza prior to the incident. The Court ORDERS the production of certain documents for in camera review and the attendance of a custodian of records to give testimony.
