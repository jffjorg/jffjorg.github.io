 Case Number:  21STCV45082    Hearing Date:   April 17, 2024    Dept:  30 DAVID LONG vs GUO TAO RUANTENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff, David Long, is DENIED without prejudice. Counsel for Plaintiff’s counsel has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). However, the declaration has outdated hearing dates and thus, does not comply with CRC Rule 3.1362(e). In addition, the proposed order has not been completed starting at item 7. Moreover, there is no proof of service showing the client was served with the moving papers.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
