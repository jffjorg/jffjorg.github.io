The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the plaintiffs' motion to compel further responses to requests for admission or form interrogatories. The motion was filed by Kazem Sanikhatam, through his successor-in-interest Mitra Azizi, and Mitra Azizi against the defendant Persian-American Adult Day Health Care Center, Inc., d/b/a Forever Young Adult Day Health Care Center. The court has granted the motion in part, compelling the defendant to provide further responses to requests for admission numbers 61-68, 74, 80, 86, 111, 115, 118, and 120-121. However, the court has denied the motion in part, finding that the answers to certain requests for admission are not evasive or incomplete. The court has also denied the plaintiffs' alternative request to compel further responses to form interrogatories. Finally, the court has denied the plaintiffs' request for monetary sanctions against the defendant.
