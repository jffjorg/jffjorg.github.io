The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the Motion to Dismiss filed by Defendants Linda Mayne and Stephen S. Mayne. The Motion is to dismiss the complaint filed by Plaintiff Robert G. Furst for lack of standing. The Judge denied the Motion, finding that Plaintiff has standing to bring an elder abuse claim under Welfare and Institutions Code, § 15657.3 as an interested person. The Judge noted that an interested person, as defined in Probate Code, § 48, includes an heir, devisee, child, spouse, creditor, beneficiary, and any other person having a property right in or claim against a trust estate or the estate of a decedent. The Judge also found that Plaintiff's allegations, if treated as true, establish that he is an interested person and has standing to bring the claim.
