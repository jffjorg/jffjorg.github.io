This tentative ruling addresses a motion to compel discovery responses and a request for sanctions filed by the defendant, The Bay Clubs Company, LLC, against the plaintiff, Monique Von Flotow. The defendant seeks to compel the plaintiff to respond to discovery requests that were served in July 2023 and had a deadline of August 20, 2023. The plaintiff failed to respond to these requests and has since requested an extension to reestablish contact with their client. The court grants the defendant's motion to compel and orders the plaintiff to respond to the discovery requests by a certain date. The court also grants the defendant's request for sanctions and orders the plaintiff to pay $1,000 in sanctions to the defendant's counsel, Hawkins Parnell & Young.
