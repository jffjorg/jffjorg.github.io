The tentative ruling is that the Defendant's motions to compel responses to form interrogatories, special interrogatories, and request for production are denied. The Defendant's motion to deem request for admissions, set 1, admitted is also denied. The Defendant's motion to compel vehicle inspection is denied. The Defendant served motions to compel on the Plaintiff, but the Plaintiff did not file an opposition. The Defendant's motion to compel vehicle inspection was not properly served on the deponent. The court noted that objections are a valid response and that a response that only contains objections need not be signed/verified by the party to whom the discovery was directed.
