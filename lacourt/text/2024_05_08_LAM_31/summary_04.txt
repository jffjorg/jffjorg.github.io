And here is the English text summary of the tentative ruling:

This tentative ruling grants the motions to compel Defendant Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC to provide verified, objection-free responses to Plaintiff Christina Gozy's Form Interrogatories, Special Interrogatories, and Request for Production of Documents. The court also deems Plaintiff's Request for Admissions, Set One, admitted against Defendant. The court finds that Defendant's failure to provide timely responses to Plaintiff's discovery requests warrants sanctions. The court imposes sanctions in the sum of $3,153.20 against Defendant's counsel. The court concludes that Defendant's counsel did not advise the Defendant to engage in the conduct resulting in sanctions, but failed to meet their burden to show that they did not counsel the discovery abuse.
