  Case Number:  20STCV15846    Hearing Date:   May 8, 2024    Dept:  57 Plaintiffs have moved for terminating sanctions, or, alternatively, issue, evidentiary, or monetary sanctions against Defendant.  The Court is declining to impose terminating or issue sanctions against Defendant. The asserted discovery misconduct of Defendant on which Plaintiffs' request for terminating or issue sanctions is based does not support the imposition of those extreme sanctions.At the hearing, the Court will ask Plaintiffs' counsel why the asserted misconduct supports the imposition of the less draconian sanctions that Plaintiffs alternatively seek.
