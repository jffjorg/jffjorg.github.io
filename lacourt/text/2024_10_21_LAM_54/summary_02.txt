And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge has issued a tentative ruling on the demurrer to the first amended complaint filed by Pampanga Master Grill, Inc. against Core Value Advisors, LLC, et al. The demurrer was filed by the defendants, who argue that the third cause of action for negligence is barred by the economic loss rule. The judge agrees with the defendants and sustains their demurrer. The judge also grants the defendants' request for judicial notice of the existence of certain documents. The plaintiff is ordered to file and serve a second amended complaint within 30 days of notice of the ruling, and the defendants are ordered to file and serve a response within 30 days thereafter. The judge also allows the plaintiff to amend the third cause of action to fraud. The defendants' request to strike the first amended complaint and answer to cross-complaint is denied, and the defendants are ordered to file a properly noticed motion if they wish to pursue this relief.
