 Case Number:  20STCV37505    Hearing Date:   December 12, 2023    Dept:  28 Having
considered the petitioning papers, the Court rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
On
September 30, 2020, Plaintiffs Aiden Daniels and Sequoia Wiley filed this
action against Defendants Evelin Barragan-Hernandez (“Hernandez”), Manchester
Bargain, Inc. (“Manchester”), and Does 1-25 for motor vehicle negligence,
premises liability and negligent infliction of emotional distress. 
On
February 4, 2022, Manchester filed an answer and a cross-complaint against
Cross-Defendants Hernandez and Roes 1-10 for implied indemnity, apportionment,
contribution and declaratory relief. 
On
November 4, 2022, Plaintiffs filed a notice of settlement. 
On
December 12, 2022, the Court dismissed Cross-Defendants Hernandez and Roes 1-10
from Manchester’s cross-complaint with prejudice at Manchester's request. 
On November
27, 2023, Petitioner Sequoia Wiley (“Petitioner”) filed a petition to approve
the compromise of Plaintiff Aiden Daniels’s action to be heard on November 22,
2023.  The Court continued the hearing to
December 12, 2023. 
No
trial date is currently scheduled. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
Petitioner
asks the Court to approve the compromise of the pending action of Plaintiff
Aiden Daniels. 
DISCUSSION 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed petition with all necessary attachments. 
Petitioner
requests that the money be deposited at Wells Fargo
Bank, 1200 Wilshire Boulevard, Los Angeles, CA 90017.  The Court orders the
net balance so distributed.    
The
Court grants the petition. 

CONCLUSION 
The
Court GRANTS the petition to approve the compromise of the pending action of
Plaintiff Aiden Daniels filed by Petitioner Sequoia Wiley on November 27, 2023.
 The Court
orders that the net balance be placed in an insured account at Wells Fargo
Bank, 1200 Wilshire Boulevard, Los Angeles, CA 90017. 
The
Court sets an OSC Re: Distribution of Net Settlement on February 12, 2024, at
8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
The
Court sets an OSC Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on February 12, 2024, at
8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
