This tentative ruling addresses a motion by Plaintiff Julian Hart to deem Request for Admissions, Set One, admitted and to request monetary sanctions against Defendant Vartan Darbinyan. The motion was filed after Defendant failed to respond to the request for admissions, which was served via email transmission on June 27, 2023. The Court granted the motion, deeming the request for admissions admitted and ordering Defendant and his counsel to pay sanctions of $1,250.00 to Plaintiff within 20 days of the date of the ruling.
