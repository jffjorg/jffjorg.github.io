And here is a summary of the Judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has tentatively ruled that the defendants' demurrer to the first amended complaint is overruled in its entirety, and the defendants' motion to strike the first amended complaint is denied in its entirety. The Judge finds that the plaintiff has adequately alleged the alter ego status of the moving defendants and has alleged facts to support claims for violation of the California Insurance Code section 1871.7 and the California Business and Professions Code section 17200. The Judge also finds that the plaintiff has alleged facts to support a claim for fraud, including that the defendants knowingly employed unlicensed individuals to procure clients and patients. The Judge orders the moving defendants to file an answer to the first amended complaint within 21 days of the ruling.
