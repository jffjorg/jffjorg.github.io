The judge's tentative ruling is in response to a motion for attorney's fees and costs filed by plaintiff Gladys Navarro against the remaining defendants, South Bay Safety Inc. The plaintiff is seeking an award of attorney's fees as the prevailing party under Labor Code section 218.5. The judge is continuing the motion to Friday, August 2, 2024, and directing the plaintiff to serve and file any supplemental evidence supporting its request for attorney's fees by Friday, July 26, 2024. The judge also notes that the plaintiff's counsel has requested an award of $52,425 in attorney's fees, which is based on a total of 83.5 hours of work spent by four attorneys. The judge has concerns about the reasonableness of the fees and is requiring the plaintiff to provide itemized billing records for the work performed by attorneys Shu, Granberry, and Montero. The judge also declines to rule on the award of costs at this time.
