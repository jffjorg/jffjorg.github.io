




            This
is a default on a credit card account. The Complaint alleges cause of action
for (1) breach of contract, and (2.) common counts. 
            
            The
Complaint was filed on April 14, 2023. Default was entered as to Defendant Angelica
Grigoryan (Defendant) on August 17, 2023. 
 
            Plaintiff
Bank of America now moves for default judgment against Defendant. Does 1-10
have been dismissed. The request for entry of default judgment is granted. 
 
Discussion
 
            Plaintiff
seeks default judgment in the amount of $62,528.10, which includes $530.57 in costs.

 
            Plaintiff
submits sufficient evidence to support the amount incurred and owed by Defendant’s
breach of the credit card agreement. (Benton Decl., ¶¶ 1-6.) Further, Plaintiff
substantiates the request for costs in the amount of $530.57. (CIV-100, ¶ 7.)
 
            Plaintiff
is not seeking attorney fees or interest. Plaintiff’s request for entry of default judgment in the
amount of $62,528.10
is granted.
