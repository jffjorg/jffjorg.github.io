TENTATIVE RULING
 
            German Majestic Auto Parts &
Service, Inc.’s Motion to Compel Responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One,
and Request for Sanctions is granted. 
 
Background 
            
            Plaintiff filed the Complaint on March
18, 2024. Plaintiff alleges the following facts. Defendant is wrongfully
possessing Plaintiff’s vehicle. Defendants filed a Cross-Complaint asserting
that Plaintiff failed to pay for services rendered by Defendants.  
 
            Motions to Compel
 
CCP § 2030.290 states: “If a party to whom
interrogatories are directed fails to serve a timely response, the following
rules apply…The party propounding the interrogatories may move for an order
compelling response to the interrogatories.” (CCP § 2030.290(b).)  
 
            Defendant served upon Plaintiff Form
Interrogatories, Set One, on April 30, 2024. (Decl., Stanford Jossen, ¶¶ 3-5.) Defendant
contends that no responses were served to the discovery request. (Id.) 
 
            Defendant’s Motions to Compel
Responses to Form Interrogatories, Set One, and Request for Sanctions is
granted.  Plaintiff is ordered to serve
responses, without objections, within 10 days of this date.
            
            Sanctions
 
Defendant’s request for monetary sanctions is granted.

 
Plaintiff is sanctioned the total amount of $960.00
which was calculated utilizing 2 hours to prepare and appear at $450/hour, plus
a $60 filing fee.   
 
Sanctions are payable within 30 days of this date.
 
Moving party is ordered to give notice of this ruling.
