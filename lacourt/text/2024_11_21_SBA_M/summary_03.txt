This is a tentative ruling by Judge Gary Y. Tanaka in the case of Jalula Roberts v. Torrance Post Acute, LLC, et al., Case Number 24TRCV00364. The case involves claims of negligence, elder abuse, and violation of Health and Safety Code 1430. The plaintiff alleges that they suffered injuries while in the care of the defendants. The defendants served discovery requests on the plaintiff, but the plaintiff failed to respond. The defendants moved to compel responses to the discovery requests and request sanctions. The court grants the defendants' motions to compel responses to the discovery requests and deems the requests for admissions admitted. The court also sanctions the plaintiff and their counsel in the amount of $1,500.00.
