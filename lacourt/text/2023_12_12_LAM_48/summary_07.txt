Judge Hon. Thomas D. Long has issued a tentative ruling granting the motions of S. Mark Taper Foundation Imaging Center, et al. to compel discovery from plaintiff Mehrnaz Hadian. The defendants served a demand for documents, form interrogatories, and special interrogatories on June 21, 2023, but the plaintiff failed to respond in a timely manner. The defendants filed motions to compel responses on November 6, 2023, and the plaintiff did not file an opposition. As a result, the court has granted the motions and ordered the plaintiff to serve verified responses, without objection, to the defendants' discovery requests within 30 days.
