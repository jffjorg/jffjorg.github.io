Summary in English:

This is a tentative ruling by Judge Michael R. Amerian in a case between Angel Paul (Plaintiff) and Calabasas CCRC, LLC (Defendant). The case stems from an alleged incident at Calabasas CCRC, LLC property on October 6, 2023, where the Plaintiff allegedly tripped and fell on defective stairs, resulting in bodily injuries. The Defendant claims that an indemnity clause in an agreement between the parties releases the Defendant from liability. The Plaintiff is seeking to quash a subpoena served by the Defendant on Encino Plaza Surgery Center for the Plaintiff's medical records from October 6, 2013, to present. The Court grants the Plaintiff's Motion to Quash in part, limiting the scope of the subpoena to orthopedic treatment of the Plaintiff from October 6, 2018, to present. The Court also denies the Plaintiff's request for sanctions.
