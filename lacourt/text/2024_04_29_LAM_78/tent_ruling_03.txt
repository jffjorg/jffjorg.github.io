 Case Number:  BC714498    Hearing Date:   April 29, 2024    Dept:  78 Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles Department 78  FRANCISCO RODRIGUEZ,Plaintiff,          vs.LOS ANGELES DODGERS HOLDING COMPANY, et al.,Defendants. Case No.: BC714498 Hearing Date: April 29, 2024 [TENTATIVE] RULING RE: MOTIONS TO APPLY RIGHTS UNDER JUDGMENT TO SATISFACTION OF LIENS FILED BY SUKETU T. PATEL AND GREEN PEAK INVESTMENTS LLC The motion to apply rights under judgment to satisfaction of liens filed scheduled for 4/30/24 at 8:30 a.m. is advanced to 4/29/24 at 8:30 a.m.Creditors Green Peak Investments, LLC and Suketu Patel move to apply Plaintiff’s rights under the judgment in this case to the satisfaction of their liens pursuant to Code Civ. Proc., section 708.470(a). Based on the holding in Brown v. Superior Court (2004) 116 Cal.App.4th 320, both motions are denied without prejudice since the Court is aware of the existence of a claimed attorney’s lien on the judgment proceeds which may be senior to the moving parties’ liens and which may not be satisfied if the moving parties’ liens are satisfied now.  Moving party to provide notice to all parties and to file proof of service of such notice within five court days after the date of this order.DATED: April 29, 2024 ________________________Hon. Jill Feeney Judge of the Superior Court
