Plaintiff’s
motion to compel further responses to her Special Interrogatories (Set One) is
GRANTED. 
            
On 3/11/2024,
Plaintiff Dena Feingold (Plaintiff) filed suit against FCA US, LLC (Defendant),
alleging: (1) breach of express warranty; (2) breach of implied warranty; and
(3) Civil Code section 1793.2(b).
 
On 12/4/2024,
Plaintiff moved to compel further responses to her Special Interrogatories (Set
One).
 
            The
motion is unopposed. 
 
Discussion  
 
            Plaintiff
moved to compel further responses to Special Interrogatories (Set One) Nos. 14,
20, 23, 25, 26, 39, 41, and 43.
 
            The
Court takes Defendant’s non-opposition to be a concession to Plaintiff’s motion
on the merits.
 
            Based
on the foregoing, Plaintiff’s motion to compel further responses to her Special
Interrogatories (Set One) is granted. 
            
It is so ordered.
