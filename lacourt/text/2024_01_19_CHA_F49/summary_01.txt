The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the motion for sanctions filed by Plaintiff Dunn Investment Properties, Inc. against Defendants Charles and Sheila Johnson's counsel, Dominic J. Trutanich and Anthony M. Horiates. The Court grants the motion, finding that Defendants' counsel abused their right to file a motion for reconsideration under Code of Civil Procedure § 1008 and violated the provisions of Code of Civil Procedure § 128.7.
