Here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Court grants Ford Motor Company's motion to compel the deposition of Plaintiff Medardo Sosa and production of documents. Ford properly noticed Sosa of the depositions at least ten days before the deposition dates, and Sosa's counsel refused to provide alternative deposition dates. Sosa did not attend the March 8, 2024 deposition, and Ford attempted to set alternative deposition dates at Sosa's convenience. The Court awards Ford Motor Company $60.00 in monetary sanctions. The Court orders Sosa to appear for a deposition on a mutually agreed upon date within 21 days of the date of this order and orders Sosa and his counsel to pay sanctions in the total amount of $60.00.
