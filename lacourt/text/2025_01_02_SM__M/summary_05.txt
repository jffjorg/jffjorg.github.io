The Court has reviewed the motion for sanctions filed by Plaintiff Ayokanmi Adeyeye against Defendant FCA US LLC and its counsel, Clark Hill LLP. The Plaintiff seeks $10,000.00 in sanctions, alleging that the Defendant and counsel's conduct was in bad faith and solely intended to cause unnecessary delay. The Court has analyzed the evidence presented, including a series of email correspondence between counsel, and finds that the Plaintiff has failed to demonstrate that the Defendant and counsel's conduct was frivolous or intended to delay the action. The Court does not view the conduct as "frivolous" or with "intent to delay" as defined by California Code of Civil Procedure section 128.5. Therefore, the motion for sanctions is denied.
