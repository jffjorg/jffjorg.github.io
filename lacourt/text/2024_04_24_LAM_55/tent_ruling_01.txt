  Case Number:  23STCV18514    Hearing Date:   April 24, 2024    Dept:  55 Plaintiff’s motion for summary judgment is off calendar
because Defendant filed a notice of removal to federal court on 4/21/24. 
“[T]he state court's jurisdiction is suspended when the
defendant seeking removal gives notice to the state court clerk, and it is
reacquired when the district court clerk gives notice to the state court clerk
in the form of a certified copy of the remand order.” Spanair S.A. v.
McDonnell Douglas Corp. (2009) 172 Cal.App.4th 348, 356.
The Court sets a status conference regarding the removal of
the case, and, if necessary, re-setting the hearing on Plaintiff’s MSJ, for May
31, 2024 at 8:30 a.m. in Department 55.
