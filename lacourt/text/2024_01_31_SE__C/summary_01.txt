And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on the Plaintiff's Motion for Sanctions against the Defendant, Office Chairs, Inc. The Plaintiff is seeking terminating sanctions, monetary sanctions, and issue sanctions due to the Defendant's failure to comply with a court order compelling the deposition of Norberto Garcia. The Judge has granted the Motion in part and denied it in part. The Judge has imposed an issue sanction on the Defendant, prohibiting it from opposing the Plaintiff's claims that the Defendant violated Government Code § 12940 and Labor Code § 1198.5 and the Plaintiff was wrongfully terminated. The Judge has also granted the Plaintiff's request for monetary sanctions in the reduced amount of $1,111.65.
