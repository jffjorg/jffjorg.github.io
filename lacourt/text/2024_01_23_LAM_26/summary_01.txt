And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge grants plaintiff's motion to compel defendant's further responses to special interrogatories. The defendant's objections to the special interrogatories are overruled, except for one objection that is sustained. The defendant is ordered to provide further, code-compliant, verified responses without objection to the special interrogatories, set two, nos. 49-54 and 56-61, as limited to store clerks or employees of a similar position hired/terminated by the defendant. The judge also awards sanctions in the amount of $2,068.91 against the defendant and its counsel for their unsuccessful motion to compel and for making evasive responses.
