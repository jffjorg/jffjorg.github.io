And here is a summary in English text:

This tentative ruling denies the motions by Pom Insurance Agency Inc. dba Peace of Mind Wealth Management, Alan Wen, and Angel Wenjun Tang to compel nonparties Lovel Firm, P.C. and Life Insurance Company of the Southwest to comply with their deposition subpoenas. The motions were denied because they failed to comply with the relevant Code of Civil Procedure sections and because the consumer notice requirements were not met. The Court found that the subpoena directed at Lovel Firm, P.C. was not properly served and that the subpoena directed at Life Insurance Company of the Southwest sought consumer records without first serving the statutory notices on the affected consumers. The Court also found that the defendants' argument that the subpoena can be interpreted to only apply to the portion of the subpoena that seeks consumer records was unpersuasive.
