This is an employment action.  Defendant Liberty Utilities (Park Water) Corp. seeks to compel a further response to its request for production no. 6 from Plaintiff Franklin Heldman.RFP no. 6 seeks: “Any and all communications between YOU and any current or former employee of LIBERTY.”The Motion is denied.  The Court finds this request to be overbroad.  The Court declines to award sanctions.Moving party to give notice.If counsel do not submit on the tentative, they are strongly encouraged to appear by LACourtConnect rather than in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
