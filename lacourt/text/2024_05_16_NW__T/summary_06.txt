The tentative ruling grants Plaintiff Financial Services Vehicle Trust's application for a Writ of Possession. The Court waives an undertaking from the Plaintiff. The Defendant, Arpine Oganesyan, is ordered to post an undertaking of $86,000.00 to ensure the re-delivery of the vehicle. The Plaintiff, through its servicer BMW Financial Services NA, LLC, moved for a Writ of Possession against the Defendant, citing the Defendant's breach of the Motor Vehicle Lease Agreement and failure to pay. The Court finds that the Plaintiff has shown their rights to possession and the Defendant has breached the agreement, requiring the Defendant to surrender the vehicle. The Plaintiff submitted evidence of the value of the vehicle, showing that the account balance plus expenses exceeds the value of the vehicle, with no equity existing for the Defendant.
