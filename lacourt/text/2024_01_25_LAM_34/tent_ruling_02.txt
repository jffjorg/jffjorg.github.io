BACKGROUND

Defendant Oh My Crab House LLC from the Complaint. 
 
On June 15,
2023, by request of Plaintiff, the Clerk’s Office entered default on Defendant
Forever Food, Inc. 
 
On June 26,
2023, Plaintiff filed a new Judicial Council Form, CIV-100, Request for Entry
of Default. 
 
On December
19, 2023, Plaintiff filed:
 
(1)       Judicial Council From CIV-100, Request for
Court Judgment;

(7)       Proposed Judgment. 
 
On December
21, 2023, by request of Plaintiff, the Clerk’s Office dismissed without
prejudice the remaining doe defendants from the Complaint. 
 
II.      
ANALYSIS
 
The Court continues this hearing until February 28, 2024 to allow Plaintiff
to file with the Court the wording of his proposed injunction. The wording must
be specific enough to allow the Court to determine if a violation exists and to
provide due process to the defendant prior to the imposition of any sanctions
for violation of such an injunction.
 
 Any such additional pleadings
are to be filed by February 15, 2024.
