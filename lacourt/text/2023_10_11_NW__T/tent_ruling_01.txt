  Case Number:  23VECP00321    Hearing Date:   October 11, 2023    Dept:  T Minitello v
Baldwin Plumbing 23VECP00321
Tentative
ruling: Plaintiff’s request for attorney fees
Granted:
The court finds that the hourly rate of $350 per hour is a reasonable rate for
similar services charged by lawyers in this community and the number of hours
billed at 11 hours is also reasonable and necessary, and awards attorney fees
in the amount of $3,850 in favor of plaintiff Sandy Minitello and against
defendant Baldwin Plumbing, Inc. pursuant to Civil Code section 8488 as the
prevailing party on a petition to expunge lien. 
No opposition was filed to the motion.
