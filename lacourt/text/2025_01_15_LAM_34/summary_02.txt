The Judge has issued a tentative ruling on Plaintiff Champion Echo Park, LLC's Motion for Attorney's Fees and Costs. The Plaintiff filed an unlawful detainer complaint against Defendant Pineapple, Inc. and the court granted the Plaintiff's Motion for Summary Judgment. The Plaintiff is seeking $17,804.91 in attorney's fees and costs, but the Defendant disputes the amount and argues that it is unreasonable.

The Judge finds that the Plaintiff is the prevailing party and is entitled to recover its attorney's fees and costs. The Judge uses the lodestar adjustment method to calculate the fees and reduces the amount to $9,500.00 for 20.00 hours at $475.00 per hour. The Judge also awards $1,647.91 in costs and expenses.

The Judge grants the Plaintiff's Motion for Attorney's Fees and Costs in part, awarding $11,147.91 in total fees and costs. The ruling is subject to change based on any oppositions or replies filed by the parties.
