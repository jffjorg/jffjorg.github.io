  Case Number:  22PSCV00668    Hearing Date:   December 16, 2024    Dept:  O Tentative Ruling
 
DEFENDANT NISSAN NORTH AMERICA, INC.’S MOTION TO
COMPEL ARBITRATION AND STAY PROCEEDINGS is CONTINUED. 
 
Background
 
This is a lemon law case. 
 
On June 30, 2022, Plaintiffs BRIANNA GARCIA and ENRIQUE
GARCIA filed suit.
 
On February 9, 2023, Defendant filed the instant motion.
 
On July 6, 2023, Plaintiff filed its opposition.
 
On July 12, 2023, Defendant filed its Reply. 
 
On July 19, 2023, the court adopted its tentative and
continued the hearing. 
 
On June 17, 2024, the court again continued in light of the
Ford Motor Warranty Cases (2023) 89 Cal.App.5th 1324 (“Ochoa”) being on appeal
to the California Supreme Court.
 
Discussion
 
Like
the prior tentative rulings, the court again continues the hearings as
Plaintiffs rely upon Ochoa which is on appeal. 
 
Conclusion
 
Based
on the foregoing, the motion to compel arbitration is continued.
