  Case Number:  23STCV18933    Hearing Date:   August 19, 2024    Dept:  78  Superior Court of California County of Los Angeles Department 78 ¿ EDGAR LOPEZ, et al., Plaintiff(s), vs. GENERAL MOTORS, LLC, et al., Defendant(s). Case No.: 23STCV18933 Hearing Date: August 19, 2024   [TENTATIVE] ORDER RE: MOTION TO COMPEL FURTHER DIscovery responses  I.  BACKGROUND Plaintiffs Edgar Lopez and Eva Gutierrez (collectively “Plaintiffs”) filed this lemon law action against defendants General Motors, LLC (“GM”), et al. arising from their purchase of a 2022 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 on June 4, 2022. On June 17, 2024, Plaintiffs filed the instant motion to compel GM to provide further responses to Plaintiff’s form interrogatories, set one, as to Nos. 12.1 and 15.1, setting the hearing for August 19, 2024. GM opposes the motion.  II.  DISCUSSION The Court notes that Plaintiffs have filed two other motions to compel further responses, set to be heard for August 20, 2024 and December 5, 2024. The Court has reviewed the briefs and finds good cause to conduct an Informal Discovery Conference (“IDC”) in an effort to informally resolve the discovery dispute at issue, prior to hearing the motions to compel further.  III. CONCLUSION The parties are to meet and confer on a date to participate in the IDC, and Plaintiffs are ordered to reserve and schedule the IDC. Plaintiffs are also ordered to continue the Hearing on Motions to Compel Further Discovery Responses using the online reservation management system, to a date at least 3 weeks after the IDC.  Moving Party is ordered to give notice.  DATED: August 16, 2024 __________________________ Hon.
