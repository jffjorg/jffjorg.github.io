And here is a summary of the tentative ruling in English text:

The court has granted defendants' ex parte application to strike plaintiff's response separate statement in support of his opposition to defendants' motion for summary judgment or, in the alternative, summary adjudication. The court finds that plaintiff's response separate statement does not comply with Code of Civil Procedure section 437c and that many of plaintiff's responses to material facts are not made in good faith, responsive, or material. The court orders plaintiff to file a revised separate statement and opposition memorandum that includes good faith, responsive, and material responses to the stated material facts and citations to evidence in support of his position that the fact is controverted. The court also continues the hearing on defendants' motion for summary judgment or, in the alternative, summary adjudication and orders trial to be continued from January 29, 2025 to April 23, 2025.
