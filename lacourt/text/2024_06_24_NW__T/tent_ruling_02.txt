 Case Number:  23VECV01030    Hearing Date:   June 24, 2024    Dept:  T Hart v
Lasalle 23VECV01030
Tentative
ruling:  Ex parte application by plaintiff for:
1. FOR
AN ORDER ALLOWING DEPOSITIONS TO MOVE FORWARD AND MOTION TO COMPEL RECORDS AT A
DEPOSITION;
2. FOR
AN ORDER PERMITTING THIRD PARTY/WITNESS SUBPOENAS DURING STAY;
3. FOR
AN ORDER TO ADVANCE PLAINTIFF’S MOTION FOR IN CAMERA REVIEW TO JULY 26, 2024;
4. FOR
AN ORDER PERMITTING PLAINTIFF TO RE-FILE HER MOTION FOR SANCTIONS PER THE
COURT’S MAY 24, 2024 ORDER;
5. FOR
AN ORDER THAT LASALLE CORRECT THE RECORD
Denied.  Plaintiff
has failed to make an affirmative factual showing in a
declaration containing competent testimony based on personal knowledge of
irreparable harm, immediate danger, or any other statutory basis for granting
relief ex parte.(CRC 3.1202)  No exigent circumstances have been
shown.  If the relief sought would affect on an opposing party’s
rights, a noticed motion will be required. (See, Sole
Energy Co. v. Hodges (2005) 128 Cal.App.4th 199, 207.)
