**Case Number:** 22STCV40554
**Hearing Date:** October 3, 2023
**Department:** 26

**Tentative Ruling:**

The Judge has reviewed the demurrer and motion to strike filed by Defendants AJJ2020 LLC and Masuda Investments, LLC, and has made the following ruling:

* The demurrer to the first cause of action for breach of contract is OVERRULED.
* The demurrer to the second cause of action for breach of the implied warranty of habitability is OVERRULED.
* The demurrer to the third and fifth causes of action for nuisance and negligence is OVERRULED.
* The demurrer to the fourth cause of action for intentional infliction of emotional distress is OVERRULED.
* The demurrer to the sixth cause of action for violation of Civil Code section 1942.4 is OVERRULED.
* The motion to strike the prayer for punitive damages is DENIED.
* The motion to strike the prayer for attorney's fees is DENIED.

**Request for Judicial Notice:**

The Court grants the request for judicial notice of the Grant Deed for the Subject Property recorded on October 13, 2021.

**Meet and Confer Requirement:**

The Court finds that the parties have fulfilled the meet and confer requirements.

**Conclusion and Order:**

Based on the foregoing, Defendants AJJ2020 LLC and Masuda Investments, LLC's demurrer is OVERRULED. The demurrer to the first cause of action for breach of contract is OVERRULED. The motion to strike the prayer for punitive damages is DENIED. The motion to strike the prayer for attorney's fees is DENIED. The case management conference is continued to November 28, 2023 at 8:30 am. Moving Parties are ordered to give notice and file proof of service of such.

**Signed:** Elaine Lu, Judge of the Superior Court
