This tentative ruling grants EPI Construction, Inc.'s motion to compel a mental examination of Plaintiff Alonso Rivera. The motion is unopposed. The court finds that EPI has shown good cause to take a mental examination of the plaintiff, as the plaintiff is seeking damages for emotional distress related to an accident. The examination will be conducted by Dr. Manuel Saint Martin and will consist of a psychiatric interview and psychological testing. The plaintiff must appear for the examination within 30 days of the order.
