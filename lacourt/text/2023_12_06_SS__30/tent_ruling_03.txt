 Case Number:  21STCV14715    Hearing Date:   December 6, 2023    Dept:  30 Court Order Re: Referral of Non-Personal Injury (PI) 
Action to Department 1 for Review/Reassignment;
The Court having reviewed the legal file determines that the above entitled-action was 
erroneously filed as a Personal Injury ("PI") matter.The Court orders this action referred to the Master Calendar Department, Department 1 for 
review and determination as to whether this case should or should not be reassigned to an 
Independent Calendar ("IC") Court. On the Court's own motion, the Hearing on Demurrer - with Motion to Strike (CCP 430.10) 
scheduled for 12/06/2023 is advanced to this date and vacated (subject to being reset, continued 
or vacated at the direction of the receiving court). (NOTE: If Department 1 determines this matter to be non-PI, all hearings currently set in 
Department 30 of the Spring Street Courthouse are taken off calendar subject to being reset and 
notified by the receiving court.)
Judicial Assistant is directed to give notice to Plaintiff, who upon receipt of this notice, is 
ordered to give notice to all Parties of record.
