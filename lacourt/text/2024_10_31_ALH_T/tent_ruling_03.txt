 Case Number:  21STCV16714    Hearing Date:   October 31, 2024    Dept:  T Motion to Approve Minor’s CompromiseCase Name: Antonio Ramirez, et al. v. Tara Lee BauerMoving Party: Plaintiff Antonio Ramirez Responding Party: N/ATentative Ruling: GRANTBACKGROUNDThis is a personal injury case wherein minor Alexander Ramirez was a passenger in a vehicle driven by his father, Plaintiff Antonio Ramirez. Plaintiffs were traveling through a green light when Defendant Tara Lee Bauer (“Defendant) made a left turn in front of their vehicle, causing the vehicles to collide. Alexander suffered from injuries to his neck, back, left hip, left thigh and left knee, as well as sleep disturbances following the incident, and has since recovered fully from his injuries.On October 1, 2024, Plaintiff filed a petition to approve compromise of a disputed claim for settlement for $10,000.00 with respect to Alexander Ramirez. The petition represents total deductions of $3,915.00 as follows: $2,500.00 in attorney fees and $1,415.00 for medical expenses — leaving a net balance of $6,085.00. (Petition, p. 6.) ANALYSISThe settlement exceeds $5,000 and the payment will therefore be deposited into a blocked account. (Probate Code §§ 3610, 3611(b).)The Minor’s Compromise can be approved. The petition was filed on the MC-350 form. A proposed order form MC-351 was also filed. The petition also complies with CCP § 372; Probate Code §§ 3500, 3600-3613; and Cal. Rules of Court Rules 3.1384, 7.101, 7.950-7.952.RULINGThe Court approves the Petition to Confirm Minor’s Compromise. The Court will also set a hearing to verify the deposit of funds into the blocked account.Moving party to give notice.
