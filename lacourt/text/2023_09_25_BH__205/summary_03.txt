The judge has denied without prejudice the plaintiff's request for default judgment. The plaintiff, Pacific Design Center 1, LLC, is seeking a default judgment against the defendant, Chelsea Carpets, Inc., for a total of $121,004.12. The defendant failed to pay rent and breached a surrender agreement, and the plaintiff is seeking damages, interest, attorneys' fees, and costs. However, the plaintiff failed to comply with the procedural requirements for a default judgment, including providing a basis for the 10% interest and attaching the lease agreement to a declaration in support of the request. The judge has ordered the plaintiff to re-file the request for default judgment with the necessary documentation.
