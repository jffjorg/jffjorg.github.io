**Case Number:** 24STCV21901
**Hearing Date:** December 3, 2024
**Dept:** 55
**NATURE OF PROCEEDINGS:** Hearing on Defendants' Demurrer with Motion to Strike

**TENTATIVE RULING:**

**I. Demurrer**

1. **Alter Ego**: The demurrer is sustained in part and overruled in part. Plaintiff's allegations of alter ego are sufficient to plead liability for Defendants.
2. **Breach of Contract - Tenants in Common Agreement**: The demurrer is overruled. Plaintiff has adequately pleaded a breach of contract claim.
3. **Breach of Contract - Property Management Agreement**: The demurrer is overruled. Plaintiff has adequately pleaded a breach of contract claim.
4. **Breach of Fiduciary Duty**: The demurrer is sustained with leave to amend. Plaintiff's claim for breach of fiduciary duty is incomplete.
5. **Fraud**: The demurrer is sustained with leave to amend. Plaintiff's claim for fraud is incomplete.
6. **Constructive Trust**: The demurrer is overruled. Plaintiff has adequately pleaded a constructive trust claim.
7. **Accounting**: The demurrer is overruled. Plaintiff has adequately pleaded an accounting claim.
8. **Declaratory Relief**: The demurrer is overruled.

**II. Motion to Strike**

1. **Paragraph 13**: Granted. The Court takes judicial notice of the fact that Daniel A. Abrams was not a member of the California bar in 2007 when the TIC Agreement was executed.
2. **Paragraph 14**: Denied.
3. **Paragraph 18**: Denied.
4. **Paragraph 29**: Denied.
5. **Paragraph 36**: Denied.
6-8, 10-14 (Fiduciary Responsibility): Denied.
9 and 14 (Power of Attorney): Denied.

**ANALYSIS**

The Court has reviewed the Complaint, Motion, Opposition, and Reply. The Court finds that Plaintiff has adequately pleaded causes of action for breach of contract, breach of fiduciary duty, constructive trust, and accounting. The Court sustains the demurrer to the fraud claim with leave to amend.

The Court grants the motion to strike Paragraph 13, which alleges that Daniel A. Abrams was a member of the California bar in 2007. The Court denies the motion to strike the remaining paragraphs.

**LEAVE TO AMEND**

Plaintiff is granted leave to amend the Complaint to address the issues raised in the demurrer and motion to strike.
