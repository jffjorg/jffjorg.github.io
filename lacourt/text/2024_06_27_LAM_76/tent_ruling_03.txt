 Case Number:  22STCV26531    Hearing Date:   June 27, 2024    Dept:  76             This is an action to quiet title.
            On January 3, 2024, the Court
entered judgment in favor of Plaintiffs.  
            Defendant Robert Nagle/aka Robert W.
Nagle/HLR Wright Rd, LLC, in pro per brings a motion to dismiss. 
TENTATIVE RULING
            Defendant Robert Nagle/aka Robert W. Nagle/HLR Wright Rd,
LLC[1]
in pro per’s motion to dismiss is DENIED. There is no authority for this motion
after judgment was entered on January 3, 2024.
Pro
per litigants are not entitled to more favorable or lenient treatment than
other litigants or attorneys when it comes to adherence to the California Rules
of Court or Code of Civil Procedure.  (See Harding v. Collazo (1986) 177 Cal.App.3d 1044, 1056; Gamet v. Blanchard (2001) 91 Cal.App.4th
1276, 1284.)
