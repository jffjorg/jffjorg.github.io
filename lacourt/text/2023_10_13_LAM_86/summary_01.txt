**Facts**

* The Site is a 170,000 square foot property located at 641, 705, 717, and 719 East Compton Boulevard in the City of Compton.
* The Site was previously operated by MLOC, which discharged waste into the groundwater.
* Petitioner, REV 973, LLC, acquired the Site in 1998 and has been named as a discharger under the Cleanup and Abatement Order (CAO).
* The Board issued the Final CAO on September 19, 2014, which requires Petitioner to clean up the waste discharges.
* Petitioner filed a petition for writ of mandate to challenge the Final CAO.

**Legal Issues**

* Whether the Board provided adequate notice and opportunity to respond to Petitioner.
* Whether the Board's decision to name Petitioner as a discharger was supported by the evidence.
* Whether the Board's findings were sufficient to support its decision.

**Holding**

* The Board provided adequate notice and opportunity to respond to Petitioner.
* The Board's decision to name Petitioner as a discharger was supported by the evidence.
* The Board's findings were sufficient to support its decision.

**Ruling**

* The petition for writ of mandate is denied.
* The Final CAO is upheld.
