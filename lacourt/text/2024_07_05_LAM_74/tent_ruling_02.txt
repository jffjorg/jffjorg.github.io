 Case Number:  23STCV19069    Hearing Date:   July 5, 2024    Dept:  74 Plaintiff People of the State of
California’s Motion to Compel Defendant Mokhtar Jabli’s Responses to Request
for Production of Documents (Set One).
 
In
October 2023, Plaintiff served Defendant Mokhtar Jabli with Request for
Production of Documents (Set One). (Tejeda-Rodriguez Decl., ¶4, Ex. 1.) On
February 6, 2024, Plaintiff received an email from Defendant Mokhtar Jabli
containing a link to a Google document, which contained a page titled “Requests
for Admission” with 19 one-word responses that appeared to correspond to the 19
RFAs Plaintiff had served on co-Defendant UHL in October of 2023 and a page
titled “Interrogatory” appearing to correspond to Special Interrogatory No. 1
by providing property names and addresses. (Tejeda-Rodriguez Decl., ¶¶10-11,
Exs. 5-6.) However, the document contained no information identifying the
responding party, was unsigned, and was unverified. (Id.) Finally, the
document contained no purported responses to the RFPs at issue. (Id. at
¶12.)
On
March 22, 2024, Plaintiff filed Plaintiff’s Motion to Compel Defendant Mokhtar
Jabli’s Responses to Request for Production (Set One). The motion is unopposed.
The Court orders Defendant Mokhtar Jabli to serve, verified, objection-free
responses to request for production of documents (set one), within 10 days. The
Court orders Defendant to pay Plaintiff’s counsel $360 as discovery sanctions.
Plaintiff
shall give notice.
