The Petitioner, Peachtree Settlement Funding, LLC, has filed a petition to approve the transfer of structured settlement payment rights from the Payee, Sheila Burris. The Payee is seeking to transfer the rights to 192 monthly payments of $13,416.26 each, increasing at 4% annually, beginning on September 30, 2035 and ending on August 30, 2051. In exchange for the transfer, the Payee will receive a lump sum of $500,000.00 from the Petitioner.

The Court has reviewed the procedural and substantive requirements for approving the transfer and finds that they have been met. The Court notes that the Payee has waived her right to receive independent legal or financial advice and has reasonably understood the terms of the transfer agreement.

The Court finds that the transfer is fair, reasonable, and in the best interest of the Payee, considering the following factors: the Payee's financial and economic situation, the terms of the transaction, and whether the Payee still needs the future structured settlement payments. The Court also notes that the Payee has other means of income or support and has not indicated any dissatisfaction with previous transactions involving the transfer of structured settlement payment rights.

Therefore, the Court grants the petition, subject to the Petitioner demonstrating that the amended petition has been served.
