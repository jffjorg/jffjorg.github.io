And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The judge grants plaintiff Malena Gurcinas Nicolini's unopposed motion to confirm settlement. The parties reached a settlement agreement in which Defendant EGL Properties, Incorporated agrees to pay $15,000.00 to settle individual claims and representative PAGA claims. The settlement provides for payment of PAGA penalties and individual payments to Aggrieved Employees, and releases claims for PAGA penalties. The judge finds that the settlement is reasonable and proper, and approves it. The prevailing employee is entitled to reasonable attorneys' fees in this action. The settlement does not provide for attorneys' fees to PAGA Counsel. The judge orders that the parties seek a stay of the action except to effectuate the terms of the settlement.
