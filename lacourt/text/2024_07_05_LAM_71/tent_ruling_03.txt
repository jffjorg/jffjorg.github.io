Defendants AAMC Orange County, Inc. (“AAMC”) and Sandra K.
Gramlich (“Gramlich”) (collectively, “Moving Defendants”) demur to the to the fourth
amended complaint (“4AC”) of Plaintiffs Jonathan S. Powers (“Jon”),
individually and as attorney-in-fact for Phyllis A. Powers (“Phyllis”) and
Jonathan S. Powers Roth IRA, and Henry Kreuter (“Kreuter”) (collectively, “Plaintiffs”).  (Notice of Demurrer, pgs. 1-2.) 
Defendants’ demurrer is moot because the operative pleading
in this action is the Fifth Amended Complaint (“5AC”), filed on June 24, 2024.
Accordingly, Moving Defendants’ demurrer to Plaintiffs’ 4AC
is overruled as moot.
 
Conclusion
Moving Defendants’ demurrer to
Plaintiffs’ 4AC is overruled as moot.
