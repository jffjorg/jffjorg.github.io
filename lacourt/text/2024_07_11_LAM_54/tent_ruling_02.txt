DISCUSSION
 
This is an employment action. Plaintiff Philopatir Rizkalla sued
defendants Craftscape Creations, LLC and Allen Abraham on October, 24, 2023. The
defendants jointly answered on January 16, 2024, represented by their current
counsel, the Tahmazian Law Firm, P.C.
 
Defendant’s counsel moves to be relieved as counsel for Craftscape only.
Counsel declares that the client has stopped paying fees, which is a hardship
on the firm. Counsel has complied with CRC 3.1362. The Court finds there will
be no prejudice to any party.
 
Counsel’s unopposed motion is granted.
