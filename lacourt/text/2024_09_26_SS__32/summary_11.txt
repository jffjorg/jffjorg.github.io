The court has considered the motions for protective orders filed by Defendant United Parcel Service, Inc. regarding special interrogatories and requests for production. The court finds that the Plaintiff has met its burden to justify the number of interrogatories served and denies the motion for a protective order against the special interrogatories. The court also finds that the requests for production are not excessive or intended to harass and denies the motion for a protective order against those requests. The moving party shall provide notice and file a proof of service of the order.
