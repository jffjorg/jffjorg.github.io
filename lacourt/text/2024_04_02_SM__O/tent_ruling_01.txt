TENTATIVE
RULING        
            Cross-Defendant
Advantage Funding Commercial Credit Corp.’s Motion to Set Aside Default on
Cross-Complaint pursuant to CCP § 473(b) is GRANTED. Cross-Defendant provided
enough evidence to show excusable mistake, inadvertence of neglect, the motion
was filed with diligence upon receiving notice of default, and a proposed
answer was filed with the motion. Defendant Cross–Complainant Screamline
Investment Corporation, et al. offered no evidence of prejudice if motion was
granted. 
 
            Defendants/Cross-Complainants
Screamline Investment Corporation, et al.’s Objections are OVERRULED.
