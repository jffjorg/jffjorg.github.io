And here is a summary of the judge's tentative ruling in English text:

The Petitioner, Sun-West Properties and Investments, Inc. dba Sunrise Estates, has filed a petition to declare the mobilehome located at 45131 N. 28th Street E, Space 3, Lancaster, California abandoned. The Petitioner seeks a court order declaring the mobilehome abandoned and awarding damages for rent and other charges outstanding since January 1, 2021.

The Court has reviewed the evidence and finds that the mobilehome has been unoccupied since January 2021, no rent has been paid for the premises, and a reasonable person would believe the mobilehome to be abandoned. Therefore, the Court grants the Petition and declares the mobilehome abandoned.

The Court awards damages to the Petitioner in the amount of $17,019.98 (principal), $2,165.00 (attorney's fees), and $471.29 (costs). The Court also awards accrued rent/storage fees in the amount of $14,822.12.

The proposed judgment filed on March 31, 2024, is signed and entered this date.
