 Case Number:  22STCV20823    Hearing Date:   September 27, 2023    Dept:  30 LESLIE JEAN KANAWAH vs THOMAS JEFFERY PAUL, et al.Motion to Continue TrialRuling: Motion to Continue the Trial Date is granted. Judicial Assistant is to calendar the trial within 180 days, and motion for summary judgment 45-60 days prior to the trial date. Moving party to give notice.Discussion: A party has the right to have a timely filed motion for summary judgment heard. Moving party has not filed a summary judgment motion, let alone timely. The Court finds no good cause to continue the trial on that basis. Good cause exists, however, to continue the trial date for on-going discovery. Given the opposition, the Court finds that opposing side will not be prejudiced.
