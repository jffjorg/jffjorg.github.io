 Case Number:  21STCV04353    Hearing Date:   November 8, 2023    Dept:  30 SIRANOUCHE MELKONIAN vs ELIT PROPERTY MANAGEMENT INC., et al.Motion to Continue the Trial DateTENTATIVEThe motion to continue trial and all related trial dates is GRANTED, in its entirety.  Moving party to give notice.  Judicial Assistant is directed to calendar the trial in 120 days, to a date convenient to Court and counsel, and the final status conference date accordingly.DiscussionThe Defendants move for a continuance of trial and related dates for at least 120 days.Defendants’ counsel testifies that Defendants are not able to bring a noticed motion to continue trial or to conduct Plaintiff’s independent medical examination before the initial deadline for the parties to exchange expert witness information on November 28, 2023. (Sorkin Decl., ¶ 6.) Additionally, Defense counsel testifies the earliest available hearing date for Defendants to bring a noticed motion to trial is December 5, 2023. (Id., ¶ 5.) Further, Defense counsel has attached an email from Plaintiff’s counsel Karo Galajian stating that Plaintiff agreed to a trial continuance. (Id., ¶ 7.)Good cause exists to continue trial because the interests of justice are best served by a continuance, as Defense counsel needs time to conduct IMEs and will not be able to before the expert witness information deadline. Furthermore, Plaintiff agreed to a continuance over email. The motion to continue trial is granted.______________________________________JA’s proposed dates: Trial: 05/21/2024
