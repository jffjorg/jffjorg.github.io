 Case Number:  21STCV41971    Hearing Date:   February 6, 2024    Dept:  30 DEBRA J. CHAVEZ vs UBER TECHNOLOGIES, INC., et al.TENTATIVEThe motion to be relieved as counsel for Plaintiff, Debra J. Chavez, is DENIED without prejudice. Counsel for Plaintiff’s counsel has submitted all of the mandatory judicial council forms (i.e., MC-051, MC-052, and MC-053). However, trial is set in this matter in about two weeks. Plaintiff will not have sufficient time to obtain new representation prior to the trial date and thus, will be severely prejudiced by the granting of this motion at this juncture. Counsel must continue the trial date prior to withdrawing from the case.Moving party is ordered to give notice.
