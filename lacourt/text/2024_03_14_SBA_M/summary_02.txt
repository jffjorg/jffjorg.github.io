Summary:

The tentative ruling addresses the demurrer and motion to strike filed by KLM, Inc., et al. in response to the Second Amended Complaint filed by Irina Jodzio and Derek Jodzio. The Court rules that:

* The demurrer to the Second Amended Complaint is sustained without leave to amend as to the first through sixth causes of action brought by Derek Jodzio, except for the seventh cause of action for Negligent Referral, which is overruled.
* The demurrer to the second cause of action for Fraud is overruled, as Plaintiffs have alleged sufficient specific facts to state a cause of action.
* The motion to strike is denied, as the allegations in the Second Amended Complaint are relevant and properly filed.
* The Court orders KLM, Inc., et al. to file and serve an Answer within 10 days of the ruling, and orders Plaintiffs to give notice of the ruling.
