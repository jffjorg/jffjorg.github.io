TENTATIVE ORDER
 
Plaintiff Shen
Rui’s motion to compel further responses to Request for Production of
Documents, Set One is taken OFF CALENDAR. 
 
Moving party to give notice. 
 
This is a
Song-Beverly action about a 2022 Coachmen Leprechaun 260 DS. Plaintiff moves to
compel further responses to Requests for Production of Documents, Set One,
request numbers 16 through 21. 
 
On August 26, 2024, the Court continued the motion to
November 6, 2024 and ordered counsel to make further efforts to meet and
confer. The Court required counsel to submit a revised joint separate statement
on or before October 25, 2024, if counsel are unable to informally resolve
their discovery disputes. 
 
As of November 1, 2024, counsel have not filed their revised
joint separate statement as directed by the Court. Further, counsel have not
notified the Court that they require additional time to file their revised
joint separate statement nor have they notified the Court of other
circumstances why they have yet to file such. 
 
Accordingly, Plaintiff Shen Rui’s motion to compel further responses to Request for
Production of Documents, Set One is taken OFF CALENDAR.
