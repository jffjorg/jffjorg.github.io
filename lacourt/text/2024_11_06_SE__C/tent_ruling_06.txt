TENTATIVE ORDER
 
NYK Group Americas, Inc.’s Motion for Summary Judgment, set
for hearing on November 6, 2024, at 10:30 AM is taken OFF-CALENDAR.
 
Moving party to give notice. 
 
NYK Group Americas, Inc., erroneously sued as NYK Logistics
(Americas), Inc., moved the Court for summary judgment as to Maria De La Cruz
Cardona Maldonado’s (Plaintiff) causes of action alleged against it. On October
22, 2024, Plaintiff filed a request for dismissal as to the causes of action
against NYK Logistics (Americas), Inc. only. 
 
Pursuant to Plaintiff’s voluntary dismissal of all causes of
action against NYK Logistics (Americas), Inc., the Court takes NYK Group
Americas, Inc.’s motion for summary judgment off-calendar.
