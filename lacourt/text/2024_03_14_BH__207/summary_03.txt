**Significant Statements**

* The Court has wide discretion to allow either party to add or strike the name of a party or correct a mistake in the name of a party or a mistake in any other respect "in furtherance of justice" and "on any terms as may be proper."
* The Court favors resolution of all disputed matters between the parties and, therefore, leave to amend is liberally granted.
* The Court may grant leave to amend the pleadings at any stage of the action, but denial of a motion to amend is rarely justified if the motion is timely made and granting the motion will not prejudice the opposing party.
