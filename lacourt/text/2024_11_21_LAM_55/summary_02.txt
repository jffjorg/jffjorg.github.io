The judge has ruled in favor of 600 Tower, LLC, the defendant, on its three motions. The motions sought to compel the plaintiff, Eja Haynespolke, to respond to discovery requests, including requests for admission, and to deem certain matters admitted. The plaintiff failed to serve responses to the discovery requests and did not file an opposition to the motions. The judge granted the motions to compel and ordered the plaintiff to serve verified responses to the document requests and interrogatories by December 2, 2024. The judge also deemed the requests for admission admitted. Additionally, the judge awarded 600 Tower, LLC $1,650 in discovery sanctions, reduced from the $4,275 sought by the defendant. The hearing on the motions is scheduled for November 21, 2024.
