**Significant Statements**

* The Court finds that the relationship between the parties is governed by the original loan agreement and any subsequent modification thereto, and any cause of action would sound in contract, not in negligence.

* The Court grants the request for a preliminary injunction precluding Defendants from initiating foreclosure proceedings if it is satisfied that Defendants are in breach of their obligations under the modification agreement.
