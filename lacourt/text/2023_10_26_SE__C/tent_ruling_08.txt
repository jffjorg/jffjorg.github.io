 Case Number:  23NWCV02191    Hearing Date:   October 26, 2023    Dept:  C Loan Oak
Fund, LLC v. Gonzalez 23NWCV02191
Defendant's Ex
Parte Application for Temporary Restraining Order, Modification, and
Preliminary Injunction is DENIED without prejudice. Plaintiff's Request
for Judicial Notice is GRANTED. Defendant is seeking to enjoin a
foreclosure sale of the Subject Property scheduled for October 31, 2023. There
is no basis for relief against Plaintiff. Efforts to enjoin the October 31,
2023 foreclosure sale should be directed towards junior lienholder Oregon
Trail. As of the date of this hearing, Oregon Trail has not been named a party
to this action. Opposing Party to give notice.
