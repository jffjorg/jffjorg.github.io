The Judge has granted the Plaintiffs' motions to compel the Defendant's further responses to their request for production of documents and special interrogatories. The Plaintiffs had requested the Defendant identify key witnesses and provide documents related to the Vehicle's defects and the Defendant's warranty and vehicle repurchase policies. The Defendant had provided incomplete and evasive responses, which the Judge deemed insufficient. The Judge ordered the Defendant to provide further responses and to produce documents pursuant to a privilege log. However, the Judge found that some of the requests were moot due to the Defendant's supplemental responses.
