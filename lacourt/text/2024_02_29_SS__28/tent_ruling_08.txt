 Case Number:  22STCV16233    Hearing Date:   February 29, 2024    Dept:  28 Having
reviewed the petition for expedited approval of minor’s compromise, the Court
rules as follows. 
BACKGROUND 
          On May 16, 2022, Plaintiff D.M., by and through her guardian
ad litem Aurora Figueroa Alvizo, filed this action against Defendants Montebello
Unified School District (“District”), Michelle Mendoza (“Mendoza”), and Does
1-25 for negligent supervision, negligent hiring, training, and supervision,
and intentional infliction of emotional distress. 
          On August 8, 2022, the District filed an answer.  On November 16, 2022, Mendoza filed an
answer. 
          On August 18, 2023, Plaintiff filed a notice of settlement. 
          On February 20, 2024, Petitioner Aurora Figueroa Alvizo (“Petitioner”)
filed a petition for expedited approval of a minor’s compromise. 
PETITIONER’S REQUEST 
          Petitioner asks the Court for expedited approval of the compromise
of Claimant D.M.’s action. 
DISCUSSION 
PROPOSED
SETTLEMENT AMOUNT: $25,000.00 
INJURIES:
Plaintiff was diagnosed with a sprain in her right ankle and a fracture of the
scaphoid bone in her left wrist. Plaintiff also asserted emotional distress. 
MEDICAL
EXPENSES: $43,573.66, reduced to $13,332.39 
COSTS:
$1,078.52 
ATTORNEY’S
FEES: $5,000.00, which is 20% of the gross settlement 
BALANCE
OF PROCEEDS: $5,589.09 
Petitioner
has submitted a completed application with all required attachments.  The Court grants the petition. 
Petitioner
requests that the net settlement balance be deposited in an insured account to be
established at Bank of America, located at 3442 Wilshire Boulevard, Los
Angeles, CA 90010.  The Court orders the net balance so
distributed. 
CONCLUSION 
          The Court GRANTS the petition for expedited approval of the
compromise of minor D.M.’s action filed by Petitioner Aurora Figueroa Alvizo on
February 20, 2024. 
          The Court sets an Order to Show Cause Re: Distribution of
Net Settlement on April 25, 2024, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring
Street Courthouse. 
The
Court sets an Order to Show Cause Re: Dismissal of Case (Settlement) on April
25, 2024, at 8:30 a.m., in Department 28 of the Spring Street Courthouse. 
Petitioner
is ordered to give notice of this ruling. 
Petitioner
is ordered to file the proof of service of this ruling with the Court within
five days.
