The Court has issued a tentative ruling on the unopposed motion of Keyes European, LLC (Keyes) for attorney's fees and costs related to its anti-SLAPP motion. The Court has declined to award Keyes attorney's fees incurred prior to February 17, 2023, which were related to Keyes' effort to join the anti-SLAPP motion filed by co-Defendant Rami Gishi. The Court has rejected that effort and Keyes had to file its own anti-SLAPP motion. The Court has awarded Keyes $14,595 in attorney's fees for the period February 17, 2023 to December 31, 2023, and $3,888 for the period January 1, 2024 to the present. The total amount of attorney's fees awarded to Keyes is $18,483. Additionally, Keyes is entitled to $1,073.89 in costs.
