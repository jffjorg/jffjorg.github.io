The judge has granted in part plaintiff Gustavo Rosas's motion for attorney's fees. The plaintiff is entitled to recover $20,697.50 in attorney's fees and $978.65 in costs from defendants Nissan North America, Inc., and Wish Automotive, Inc., d/b/a Nissan of Van Nuys, pursuant to California Civil Code section 1794, subdivision (d). The court found that the plaintiff is entitled to recover attorney's fees and costs because the parties settled the action for $36,000, and the defendants' offer provided for the plaintiff's "attorney's fees and costs reasonably incurred by Plaintiff." The court also found that the plaintiff's attorneys reasonably expended a total of 46.4 hours in connection with the commencement and prosecution of the action at the hourly rates of $600, $525, $250, and $350.
