**Facts**

* Plaintiffs filed a complaint alleging various causes of action related to the condition of their apartment building and alleged discrimination by the defendants.
* The defendants demurred to the Sixth and Seventh causes of action, alleging that they fail to state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action.
* The plaintiffs alleged that the defendants failed to maintain the elevators and pool at the apartment building, and that they were retaliated against for their complaints.
* The plaintiffs also alleged that they requested reasonable accommodations, including extended move-out dates and modifications to the building, but were denied.

**Legal Issues**

* Whether the plaintiffs' allegations state a cause of action for failure to provide reasonable accommodations under FEHA and Unruh Civil Rights Act.
* Whether the defendants' alleged actions were intentional or discriminatory.
* Whether the plaintiffs' requests for reasonable accommodations were made in a timely and sufficient manner.

**Analysis**

* The court will consider the defendants' demurrer to the Sixth and Seventh causes of action and will entertain oral argument as to whether the plaintiffs' allegations state a cause of action for failure to provide reasonable accommodations under FEHA and Unruh Civil Rights Act.
* The court will also consider whether the defendants' alleged actions were intentional or discriminatory.
* The court will review the plaintiffs' allegations and determine whether they have stated a cause of action for the claims alleged.

* "A FEHA claim can be stated without actual discrimination but can be alleged for failure to provide reasonable accommodations." (Auburn Woods, supra, 121 Cal.App.4th at 1592)
* "The legislature sought to make the FEHA 'substantially equivalent' to the Federal Fair Housing Act and its amendments." (Konig v. Fair Employment and Housing Com's, supra, 28 Cal.4th at 749)
* "Courts often look to cases construing the FHA...when interpreting FEHA." (Auburn Woods, supra, 121 Cal.App.4th at 1591)
