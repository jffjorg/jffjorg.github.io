The judge has granted the defendants' motion to compel arbitration and stay court proceedings. The motion was filed by Driftwood Healthcare Center, Driftwood Healthcare & Wellness Center, LLC, and Citrus Wellness Center, LLC, in response to a complaint filed by John Yelusich and Donna Yelusich alleging negligence/professional negligence, elder abuse/willful misconduct, and loss of consortium. The judge also found that Donna Yelusich is bound by the arbitration agreement, despite not signing it herself. The judge stayed the court proceedings pending the outcome of the arbitration.
