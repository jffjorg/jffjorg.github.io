 Case Number:  23LBCP00142    Hearing Date:   July 30, 2024    Dept:  S27 Gabriel Sandoval, attorney for
Charles White, seeks to be relieved as counsel, contending there has been a
deterioration in the attorney-client relationship.  The motion is denied.  Counsel failed to file mandatory judicial
council form MC-052, as required by CRC 3.1362. 
Counsel’s declaration, which is not on mandatory form MC-052, does not
contain all information required by form MC-052.  Counsel failed to fill out the proposed order
he submitted for processing.  Counsel
failed to file proof of service on his client and all other parties who have
appeared in the action.  
 
Counsel is ordered to give
notice.
