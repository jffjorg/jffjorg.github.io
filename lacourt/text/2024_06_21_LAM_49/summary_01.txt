And here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Judge has issued a Tentative Ruling on the demurrer filed by Defendants Will Yancy and Lavon White to the Complaint filed by Plaintiffs The Most Worshipful Sons of Light Grand Lodge Ancient Free and Accepted Masons Jurisdiction of California, Inc. and Queen Adah Grand Chapter, Order of Eastern Star, Inc. The Judge has OVERRULED the demurrer and ordered the Defendants to file an Answer to the Complaint within 21 days of the ruling. The Judge has also ordered the Plaintiffs to give notice, unless waived.

The Judge's ruling is based on the allegations in the Complaint, which seek injunctive relief against the Defendants for allegedly continuing to hold themselves out as members of the Plaintiff corporations. The Judge has concluded that the Complaint states a cause of action and that the Defendants' demurrer fails on several grounds, including lack of standing, collateral estoppel, and misjoinder of parties.
