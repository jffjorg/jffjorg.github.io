FACTUAL
BACKGROUND
 
This is a wrongful termination action. On April 22, 2024,
Plaintiff Christina Geraci filed a complaint against Defendants Torpedo World
Comics, Alex Cooper, and Marz Richards for 1. Wrongful Termination in Violation
of Public Policy; 2. Harassment in Violation of FEHA; 3. Retaliation in
Violation of FEHA; 4. Hostile Work Environment (Gov't Code § 12940, et seq.); 5.
Failure to Take All Reasonable Steps to Prevent Hostile Work Environment, Discrimination
and Retaliation (Gov't Code § 12940, Et Seq.); 6. Commission of Fraudulent or
Unfair Business Acts in Violation of California Business & Professions Code
§ 17200, et seq.; 7. Negligent Supervision; and 8. Intentional Infliction of Emotional
Distress. 
 
TENTATIVE
RULING: 
 
Counsel contends due to a breakdown in attorney-client
communication, Counsel is no longer able to represent Plaintiff. 
 
Counsel has filed forms MC-051 and MC-052. However, Plaintiff has
failed to file mandatory form MC-053. If Counsel can show proof of mailing of
the MC-053 and provide the court with a copy prior to the date of this hearing,
the motion to be relieved will be GRANTED.
