**Tentative Ruling**

The court has denied Plaintiff Careful Consulting, LLC's motion to file its complaint and accompanying exhibits under seal. The court finds that the Plaintiff has not complied with the procedures set forth in California Rules of Court rules 2.550 and 2.551 for lodging a record proposed to be sealed. Specifically, the Plaintiff's motion lacks a supporting declaration and is not accompanied by a factual declaration or affidavit. Additionally, the Plaintiff's request to seal the entirety of its complaint and accompanying exhibits is not narrowly tailored. The court cannot issue an order in compliance with California Rules of Court rules 2.550(d) and (e) absent this information.
