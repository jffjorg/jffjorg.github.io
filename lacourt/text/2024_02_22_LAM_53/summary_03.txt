And here is the summary in English text:

This is a tentative ruling by Judge Robert B. Broadbelt III regarding a petition for approval of the transfer of payment rights. The petition was filed by J.G. Wentworth Originations, LLC, the transferee, and seeks approval of the transfer of 211 monthly payments worth $285,000 from Robert Hufstader, the payee. The court denied the petition without prejudice due to incomplete information and defective service. The court noted that the petition did not include Hufstader's address and telephone number, and that the proofs of service on Hufstader were incomplete and therefore defective. The court also noted that the transferee did not provide all the statutorily required information to Hufstader's previous attorney and that the notice of the hearing on the petition was not served on Hufstader. The court ordered Berkshire Hathaway Life Insurance Company of Nebraska to give notice of this ruling.
