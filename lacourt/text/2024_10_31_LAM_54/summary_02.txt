Here is a summary of the Tentative Ruling in English text:

The Court has issued a Tentative Ruling on the demurrers and motions to strike by defendants. The Court has overruled the demurrer to claim 22 for breach of contract against Skybox, but sustained the demurrers to the remaining claims for breach of contract. The Court has sustained the demurrers to the claims for money had and received against Skybox and the claims for fraud against all defendants. The Court has ordered Plaintiff to file and serve a third amended complaint within 30 days of notice of ruling and defendants to file and serve responses within 30 days thereafter.
