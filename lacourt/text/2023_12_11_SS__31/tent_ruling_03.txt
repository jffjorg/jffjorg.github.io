Seemen, Esq. of Williams and Seemen (“Counsel”), has filed a motion to be relieved as counsel, contending relief is necessary because there has been a total breakdown in the communication between Plaintiff and Counsel to the extent that this Counsel is prevented from effectively representing Plaintiff in prosecuting this action.   The motion is denied without prejudice for the following reasons:  First, Counsel submits only forms MC-051 and MC-052. California Rules Court, Rule 3.1362 provides that the motion must also be accompanied by a proposed order on form MC-053.    Second, Counsel’s proof of service only indicates that Plaintiff and Defendant was served with the notice of motion and motion to be relieved as counsel. Aside from the motion, Counsel must also serve the declaration and proposed order.  (Cal. Rules of Court, rule 3.1362(d).)    Third, if Counsel is unable to serve Plaintiff at a confirmed address, Counsel must also serve the moving papers on the Clerk of the Court- located at Stanley Mosk Courthouse- pursuant to CCP §1011 and California Rules Court, rule 3.1362(d), in addition to Plaintiff and any other parties who have appeared in this action.    Counsel is ordered to give notice.    PLEASE TAKE NOTICE: Parties are encouraged to meet and confer after reading this tentative ruling to see if they can reach an agreement. If a party intends to submit on this tentative ruling,¿the party must send an email to the court at¿sscdept31@lacourt.org¿with the Subject line “SUBMIT” followed by the case number.¿ The body of the email must include the hearing date and time, counsel’s contact information, and the identity of the party submitting.¿¿ Unless¿all¿parties submit by email to this tentative ruling, the parties should arrange to appear remotely (encouraged) or in person for oral argument.¿ You should assume that others may appear at the hearing to argue.¿¿ If the parties neither submit nor appear at hearing, the Court may take the motion off calendar or adopt the tentative ruling as the order of the Court.¿ After the Court has issued a tentative ruling, the Court may prohibit the withdrawal of the subject motion without leave.¿  Dated this 8th day of December 2023      Hon.
