The court has denied Defendant Alpha Beta Company dba Food 4 Less' motion for summary judgment. The motion was based on the argument that Defendant did not have actual or constructive notice of any dangerous condition that caused Plaintiff Maria Barraza to slip and fall on the floor of Defendant's store. The court also noted that Defendant has not presented enough evidence to meet its burden that Plaintiff cannot reasonably obtain needed evidence. The case is set for further hearing.
