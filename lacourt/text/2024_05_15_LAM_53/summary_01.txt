The court has denied the defendant's motion to compel further responses to requests for admission and for sanctions. The defendant, Mulgrew Aircraft Components, Inc., and defendant Mohammad Houshiar, had sought to compel the plaintiff, David Maijala, to provide further responses to certain requests for admission and had requested monetary sanctions in the amount of $1,500. The court denied the motion, finding that some of the requests for admission were not relevant and reasonably calculated to lead to the discovery of admissible evidence and that others sought information protected by the plaintiff's right to privacy. The court also denied the defendant's request for monetary sanctions.
