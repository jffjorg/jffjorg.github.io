English-text summary:

This tentative ruling by Judge Robert B. Broadbelt III grants the defendants' motion to bifurcate the trial, separating the issues of liability for compensatory damages and punitive damages. The court finds that the defendants are entitled to an order bifurcating trial pursuant to Civil Code section 3295, subdivision (d). The trial will be bifurcated into two phases: the first phase will determine liability for compensatory damages and whether the defendants are guilty of malice, oppression, or fraud, and the second phase will determine the amount of punitive damages. The court orders that defendants Los Indios de San Gabriel, Inc. and Andrew Salas will give notice of this ruling.
