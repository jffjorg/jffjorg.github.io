DEPARTMENT 26 LAW AND MOTION RULINGS





1. If you wish to submit on the tentative ruling,
please email the clerk at SMCdept26@lacourt.org (and “cc” all
other parties in the same email) no later than 7:30 am on
the day of the hearing, and please notify all other parties in advance that you
will not be appearing at the hearing.  Include the word "SUBMISSION" in all caps in the
subject line and include your name, contact information, the case number, and
the party you represent in the body of the email. If you submit on the
tentative and elect not to appear at the hearing, the opposing party may
nevertheless appear at the hearing and argue the motion, and the Court may
decide not to adopt the tentative ruling.




2. 
For any motion where no parties submit to the tentative ruling in
advance, and no parties appear at the motion hearing, the Court may elect to
either adopt the tentative ruling or take the motion off calendar, in its
discretion.




3. PLEASE DO NOT USE THIS
EMAIL (SMCdept26@lacourt.org) FOR ANY PURPOSE OTHER THAN TO SUBMIT TO A TENTATIVE
RULING.  The Court will not read or
respond to emails sent to this address for any other purpose.




4. IN ORDER TO IMPLEMENT
PHYSICAL DISTANCING GOING FORWARD AND UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE, THE COURT STRONGLY
ENCOURAGES ALL COUNSEL AND ALL PARTIES TO APPEAR TELEPHONICALLY FOR NON-TRIAL
AND NON-EVIDENTIARY MATTERS.  Thus, until further
notice, Department 26 strongly encourages telephonic appearances for motion
hearings that do not require the presentation of live testimony.

NBCUNIVERSAL MEDIA LLC dba NBCUniversal; MACHETE CORPORATION, d/b/a MACHETE PRODUCTIONS; CAMPING WORLD, INC.;

                        Petitioners,

            v.

 

LYNN E. FELDMAN, ESQUIRE, IN HER CAPACITY AS THE CHAPTER 7 TRUSTEE FOR THE ESTATE OF PRECISE GRAPHIX, LLC,

                        Respondent.

 

  Case No.:  23STCP02830

 

  Hearing Date: September 25, 2023

 

[TENTATIVE] order RE:

Petition to Confirm arbitration award

 

            On July 5, 2022, Lynn E. Feldman, Esquire, in her capacity as the Chapter 7 Trustee for the Estate of Precise Graphix, LLC (“Respondent”) filed a demand for arbitration with JAMS against Petitioners NBCUniversal Media LLC, Machete Corporation, and Camping World, Inc. (collectively “Petitioners”) pursuant to a pre-dispute arbitration agreement.  (Petition ¶ 4, Exh. B.)  Following a multi-week evidentiary hearing on the merits, on May 31, 2023, the JAMS arbitrator – the Honorable Ann I. Jones (Ret.) – issued a written final award denying Respondent’s claims in their entirety and awarding Petitioners approximately $7.1 million in combined attorneys' fees and costs.  (Petition ¶ 6, Exh. A.) 

            On June 13, 2023, Respondent filed a notice of appeal of the Final Award with JAMS.  (Petition ¶ 8.)  On July 17, 2023, in the bankruptcy action pending in the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, entitled In re Precise Graphix, LLC, Case No. 21012663, Respondent and Petitioners entered into a stipulation and order, under which Respondent agreed to withdraw the pending JAMS appeal and agreed to waive any rights to challenge or oppose confirmation of the arbitration award.  (Petition ¶¶ 9-10, Exh. E.)

            On August 29, 2023, the Court granted the parties stipulation – stipulating to the above facts – and stipulating to confirmation of the Final Award and entry of judgment as set forth in the [Proposed] Judgment lodged by Petitioners on August 4, 2023.  (Stipulation filed 8/29/23.)

            The court must confirm the award as made, unless it corrects or vacates the award, or dismisses the proceeding.  (CCP § 1286; Valsan Partners Limited Partnership v. Calcor Space Facility, Inc. (1994) 25 Cal.App.4th 809, 818.)  As the parties have stipulated to the entry of judgment pursuant to the Final Award, the instant petition is GRANTED.

 

CONCLUSION AND ORDER

Based on the foregoing, Petitioners NBCUniversal Media LLC, Machete Corporation, and Camping World, Inc.’s petition to confirm the arbitration award is GRANTED. 

The proposed judgment lodged on August 4, 2023 is signed and hereby incorporated.

Moving Parties are ordered to provide notice of this order and file proof of service of such.
