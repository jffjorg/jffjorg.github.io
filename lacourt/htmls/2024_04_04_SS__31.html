DEPT:  

31 

HEARING DATE: 

04/04/2024 

CASE NAME/NUMBER: 

19STCV24676 SERGIO BARRON, et al. vs VANESSA KIBE 

PETITION TO APPROVE COMPROMISE OF PENDING ACTION OF A MINOR 

  1. Leylanni Barron, Age 15 

  1. Leah Barron, Age 11 

RECOMMENDATION: 

DENY for reasons below. 

 

TENTATIVE¿ 

¿ 

The Court excuses the personal appearance of Claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.¿ 

 

Plaintiffs have agreed to settle their claims against Defendants for the total amount of $5,000, with $1,000 being apportioned to minor claimant Leylanni Barron (“Leylanni”) and $1,000 to minor claimant Leah Barron (“Leah”).  

 

If the settlement is approved for Leylanni, $266.52 will be used for medical expenses, $250 for attorney’s fees, and $196 will be used for costs. The net balance of $287.48 will be delivered to her parent and guardian ad litem, Sergio Barron.  

¿ 

If the settlement is approved for Leah, $139.85 will be used for medical expenses, $250 for attorney’s fees, and $196 will be used for costs. The net balance of $414.15 will be delivered to her parent and guardian ad litem, Sergio Barron.  

 

The petitions filed on January 29, 2024 are denied for the following reasons: 

 

Both petitions have the same defects. No copy of the written attorney fee agreement was provided in Attachment 14a. Additionally, Attachment 18a (identify each party whom the attorney is representing in this matter) is absent.  

 

Lastly, Petitioner must file an Order Approving Compromise of Claim (MC-351) for each minor claimant.  

 

Petitioner must reserve a new date and file a new petition curing the above-noted defects at least 9 court days before the continued hearing date. 

 

Moving party is ordered to give notice. 

Case Number: 23STCV00266    Hearing Date: April 4, 2024    Dept: 31

DEPT:  

31 

HEARING DATE: 

04/04/2024 

CASE NAME/NUMBER: 

23STCV00266 PABLO SANTIAGO TOMAS, et al. vs PREMKUMAR NATARAJAN, et al. 

PETITION TO APPROVE COMPROMISE OF PENDING ACTION OF A MINOR 

PABLO SANTIAGO TOMAS, Age 6 

RECOMMENDATION: 

Deny for the following reasons. 

 

TENTATIVE¿ 

¿ 

The Court excuses the personal appearance of Claimant and the guardian ad litem. Counsel may appear by telephone or video conference call.¿ 

   

Plaintiffs have agreed to settle their claims against Defendants for the total amount of $500,000, with $5,000 being apportioned to Pablo Santiago Tomas (“Claimant”). If the settlement is approved, $1,000 will be used for medical expenses, $1,250 for attorneys’ fees, and $1,113.59 will be used for costs. The net balance of $1,636.41 will be deposited into a blocked account. 

 

The petition filed on January 24, 2024 is denied for the following reasons: 

 

No written attorney fee agreement was provided in Attachment 14a.  

 

Further, in Item 14(b), Petitioner requests “Costs” of $118.59. The Court is unable to determine the difference between this unspecific request for “Costs” and the other item for “Administrative Costs (Flat Fee for Postage, Copies, Client Parking etc.). Petitioner, through counsel, is requested to specifically itemize the type of expenses incurred in order for the Court to be able to determine whether the expenses sought are reasonable.  

 

Additionally, because the attorney is representing another party involved in this matter, Attachment 18a (identifying and explaining the relationship) is required in Attachment 18a.
